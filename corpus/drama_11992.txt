Marcelo a young successful industrialist invites his childhood friends The Cannibals 1977 national champions of little league baseball to a BBQ at his house because he has big news to share with them. They all believe the news is related to his future in politics but what Marcelo has to tell them is far from what they imagine. The news will force each of them to reveal their true self testing their friendship and redefining their concepts about honesty love and loyalty.
