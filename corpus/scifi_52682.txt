Seven years ago aliens made their presences known when a UFO buzzed past an orbiting space shuttle and the world exploded with the news. Seven years later they aliens are all but forgotten as they spend their time exploring the outer planets completely ignoring earth and it's people. Charged with trying to find a new angle on the old news a team of reporters track down a group of quirky enthusiasts they only ones on earth that are still paying attention to the actions on the rim of the solar system and it's a good thing they are. Strange things start happening in space that will change life on earth forever.
