There is an entity in this world greater than the power of the gods- Fate. Knowing this the gods of Mount Olympus used their power to imprison Fate into a mortal body. However Fate has cursed the arrogant the gods and had them trapped in mortal bodies where they are doomed to repeat their mythstakes. Hayden Alexander Reyes is the only one with the power to break the curse and free the gods. He can only do this by "Twisting Fate."
