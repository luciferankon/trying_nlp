A feature documentary following the journeys of Michael Jordan along with his brands rise to global dominance pre-internet and a single mother from Texas who's campaigning against the sports giant to ensure her son is the last person murdered for a pair of Air Jordan shoes. The phenomenon of Michael Jordan and his rise to prominence launched the most recognisable popular and high end sporting brand item on the globe - Air Jordan sneakers. As both stories unfold they're on a course to meet in the present day.
