A fast paced high-energy alternative sports program that appeals to the adrenaline junkie in all of us. It's a spin-off of the award-winning "XSTV" (Xtreme Sport Television) program Fox Sports Net's #1 alternative sports program. Shot on digital video and film "Beach Extreme" is a half-hour magazine style program that focuses on water sports events from around the world. It also incorporates bikini fashion shows and event competitions from the most extreme beach-based sports.
