Lindsay Crouse star and Mamet's former wife talks about Mamet and making the film. Originally Peter Yates was to direct but was unable to and Mamet was then offered the job and Crouse then focuses on Mamet's directing style what it was like to work with him and then her experience acting alongside Joe Mantegna Ricky Jay and others.
