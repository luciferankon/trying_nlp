In the quiet little town of Bunso lives one of the most phenomenal artists Ghana has ever known Samuel Agyei Boateng 85 years. The man who crafted the symbols of Democracy and African Unity in art piece. In 1965 Samuel Agyei Boateng an artist was commissioned by the Ghana Ministry of External Affairs to craft and symbolize the ideals of Democracy and African Unity in an art form. These symbols were nonetheless drowned by Coup d'etats and Politics but today the very foundation of these ideals are still relevant.
