Documentary feature Ten Years of Love is illustrating different issues of young families in 4 European countries: Finland Slovakia Czech Republic and Romania. It presents 5 couples who became parents more than 10 years ago. The longitudinal method captures the changes in their life within 10 years in the 5 years intervals. The movie covers the basic subjects such as: life style social and economic situationwhich gradually shift into dealing with bringing up the children relationships issues crises and finally break-ups and death.
