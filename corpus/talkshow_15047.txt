The candid conversation between Evangelical Kensington Community Church's pastor Steve Andrews and Father John Riccardo pastor of St. Anastasia Roman Catholic Church. Seeking common ground "the priest and the pastor" respectfully examine the similarities between the two great traditions they represent. Topics discussed include: Salvation Holy Eucharist The Virgin Mary Confession The Saints Prayer & Worship Evangelism and Christian Unity. The DVD is complimented by a study guide and book.
