London has one of the biggest and best collections of uncommissioned street art in the world. Local and international artists have decorated the streets of London with creative works using a wide range of mediums from miniature bronze statues to painted murals several levels high. Director and producer Valentina Gordeeva went to Shoreditch to discover amazing outdoor urban art find out what people think about street art and interview two successful street artists who end up having their art in galleries. Film was made for MA course at Anglia Ruskin University.
