Reformed cannibal Bernie Grisso re-discovers his appetite after being used as a pawn in a malicious game started by his neighbor Ashley her rotten group of friends and two sadistic cops. Armed with his legendary family's sword and a ravenous midget guardian Grisso sets out to teach his tormentors a lesson they won't live to forget. Joe Bob Briggs Brinke Stevens Felissa Rose and Julie Strain join the cast in "Evil Ever After" the ultimate fairy tale shocker.
