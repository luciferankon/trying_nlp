This story about a small village in India called 'Veerpur'. A lady called Ammaji is the one who decides that a woman has asserted herself too much and needs to be pushed into a small closed and suffocating room it is Ammaji who decrees that newly born girls would be killed by drowning them in milk and many other such acts. Sia is slowly starting to rebel against the practices implemented by Ammaji that are very anti-women.
