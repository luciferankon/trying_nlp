Happily Ever Faster showcases the Chapel of the Flowers a wedding chapel located in the heart of downtown Las Vegas on the famous Strip. They have five on-site wedding chapels and a complete menu of wedding services including limousine rentals photography and more. Despite catering to upwards of 50 couples a day more than 5000 couples a year the chapel promises a classy and affordable wedding experience. Filled with tears laughter and bridal drama this family-owned business has its own behind-the-scenes challenges which are tackled head on by the owners husband and wife Jason and Holly Myers and their upbeat and zany team of wedding planners.
