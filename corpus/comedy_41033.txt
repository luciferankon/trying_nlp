Summer Breeze centered around the life of a newlywed couple Michael & Eve Wainwrights. Michael & Eve high School sweethearts are finding life married life difficult when an old flame Chris goes out of his way to make their life miserable in an effort to win the love of Eve who he has had a crush on for many years.
