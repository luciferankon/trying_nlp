Ron (Brice McMillon) is a rapper who becomes the victim of flimflam thanks to his record label's duplicitous CEO (Maleik Ramsey). The rat refuses to fork over $1 million in bank royalties for it seems he funneled the cash into drug deals with the Russian mob. So Ron joins forces with his brother a narc and the DEA to set up the mogul. But when the deal finally goes down Ron finds himself on the business end of a pistol.
