A remixed narrative that combines two 'Jake Gyllenhaal' (qv) films (_Donnie Darko (2001)_ (qv) and _Jarhead (2005)_ (qv)) with news footage of President 'Barack Obama' (qv). As the President wins the Nobel Peace Prize Gyllenhaal's character becomes disillusioned with Obama's increasingly pro-war rhetoric escalation of the war in Afghanistan and the failure to withdraw troops from Iraq.
