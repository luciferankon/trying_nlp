The story is about a 20 year old man named Hiroshi struggling to find a job. After many failed job interviews he decides to try and self employ by starting up an Animation Studio. He can draw but can't animate It was too expensive to go to university so he decided to study animation by studying the movements of everything around him. Hiroshi would study how people walk clouds move birds flying cars driving and even the movement of a door opening He would put all this study onto paper and the result is breathtaking.
