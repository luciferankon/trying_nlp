You are the pilot of a spy car on a Top Secret mission deep inside Soviet Russia. Your objective is to find and destroy an newly developed ultimate weapon before it can be deployed. Your car is equipped with offensive and defensive weapons including a force shield heat seeking missiles laser weapons turbo speed booster flame thrower and a new top secret weapon. Powerful enemy vehicles and automatic weapons will try to stop you. You'll need a sharp wit and nerves of steel to survive and complete the mission.
