Follows a family of master appraisers; Kate the daughter Judy her mother and Gloria her grandmother. They take you into their hectic days managing their extremely large antique store just outside of Chicago Illinois and show just how they haggle for every penny they make on a sale. Working together this three-generation appraising team discovers the most immaculate vintage furniture decorative accessories and other pieces of historic art that walks into their store. With quick wits and savvy business sense these ladies barter buy and sell these timeless treasures like it's just another day in the office.
