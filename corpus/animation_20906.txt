In a world where children's bad wishes come to life as mayhem-making villains a secret organization called The Wish Police works 'round the clock to apprehend the evildoers and keep the world safe! Led by an improbable hilarious detective duo - Saul an ex-genie determined to atone for centuries of granting his masters' evil wishes and Kong a wise-cracking monkey and royal pain in the leg - the Wish Police have never met a wish they couldn't wipe out. But everything changes when 12-year-old Randy makes a particularly bad wish under a perfect storm of circumstances unleashing the most powerful wish villain ever seen! Now Saul and Kong must break the rules and team up with Randy to crack the case and save the world! BE CAREFUL WHAT YOU WISH FOR!
