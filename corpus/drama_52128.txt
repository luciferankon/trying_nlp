Vloeksteen is an Afrikaans drama series. The first season of 13 hour-long episodes were written by Hein Eksteen and directed by Regardt van den Berg. "Vloeksteen" is about the experiences of a key group of highly trained and focused deep cover agents and their handlers attached to the South African Police Service's Organized-Crime Unit. A large part of the series is set in the Organized Misdaadtak's complex in a fictional coastal town somewhere along the diamond coast of South Africa.
