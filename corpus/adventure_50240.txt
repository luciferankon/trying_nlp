The story revolves around Ethan a recent college graduate that faces a whopping $250000 student debt that must get paid off within 30 days to a loan shark. While desperately seeking a solution Ethan encounters a ferocious legendary pirate named Banedagger. Banedagger claims that there is a mysterious billion-dollar treasure hidden in the land of Windleheim; however Banedagger cannot do this adventure alone. Ethan agrees to help Banedagger out and they seek to find Windleheim. Upon arriving at Windleheim Ethan discovers that there are no diamonds gold jewels or anything resembling treasure. In fact the concept of money does not even exist. As Ethan explores Windleheim he can't help but wonder what this billion-dollar treasure is.
