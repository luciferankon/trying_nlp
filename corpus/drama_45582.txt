This Christian-themed inspirational drama concerns Jordan (Craig Ellis) a young man at the very top of his game; a successful entrepreneur he launched a business and developed it into a multimillion-dollar empire thus providing himself his best friend Myles (Earl Vernon) and his driver Spencer (Clifton Powell) with the most affluent of lifestyles. Then Jordan receives a shock involving an old haunt from his past which forces him to look inside of himself and strive to be the man God wants him to be.
