High school senior Emma Robbins is unenthused with life in her quiet small beach town. Her relationships with both her alcoholic mother Gwen and boyfriend Sean leave her feeling unfulfilled; so much so that she carries out a secret relationship with a handsome mysterious (and ultimately dangerous) suitor Miles. Emma's interest in Miles grows and she finds herself relying on his attention and passion but when his affections turn to obsession will Emma be blinded by his charm and dedication-or will she realize what she's in for before it's too late?
