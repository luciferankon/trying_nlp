Alistair Reilly is a meter maid whose best days involve ruining yours. When an impossibly optimistic creature named Gus appears whom only he can see Alistair must take his first shot at friendship in a decade. Who Gus ultimately turns out to be changes Alistair forever. MISTER GUS is a comedy about a cynical man and his unfailingly jovial imaginary sidekick in the oddest of odd couple stories that proves you don't have to be a human to be a "Real."
