Rattlesnake Raceway Series is an action-packed series which follows the lives of the Lumsden Motorsports Team through part of their 2006 racing season. The team works hard in and out of their shop to upkeep their cars as they strive to place well in their points championship. The cast of characters ranges from a bouncy cheerleader Chantelle and Wes the underdog who might win his first points race this year to Chris Sr. aka "The Boss" who runs the team with his heart and his might. Watch and see how this band of dedicated racers bring their passion of racing to the screen.
