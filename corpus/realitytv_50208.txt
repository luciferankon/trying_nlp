MTV's new reality show about 7 young men - a entrepreneur (G. Carney) a writer/director/filmmaker(Hodges) record producer (Whitman) a rock star (C.Carney) a actor/cartoon voice/musician (Cipes) an actor(Baker) and party/club promoter (Delgado). This show follows the young men and all the things they go through - good and bad - how they deal with struggling to get fame and what they do once they get it.
