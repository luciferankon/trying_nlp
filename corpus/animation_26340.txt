Based on the on going comic series Atomik Mike from Frozen Beach Studios. A space hauler gets turned into a monkey by an evil warlord set on destroying a solar system to begin the conquest of the Universe. It's up to Mike and his crew to save everyone and hopefully find away to become human again. Atomik Mike has everything; a fast ship a great girlfriend and even a cool nickname. The only thing he doesn't have is his human body because he was transformed into a monkey by his jealous arch-rival Captain Tariq! Now Mike and his loyal crew search for a means to reverse his condition while maintaining his reputation as the fastest cargo hauler in the universe!
