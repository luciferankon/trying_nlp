Promising actress Anjanette Abayari shows her dramatic flair as she tackles two different roles in this back-to-back telecine special. Conservative and radical despondent and playful the real and imagined. Two different roles. Two distinctive approaches One daring actress. In "MURANG ISIP" AJ plays a teacher who quits her job to concentrate on the details of her upcoming wedding. But a failed suicide attempt of a student postpones her wedding and makes her realize what her priorities are. In "DREAM GIRL" Anjanette plays a character in a novel transformed into flesh but only the novel's author can hear and see her. As the writer escapes more and more from the world into hers her personality evolves as well that everything becomes too real for comfort. Where does fantasy end and reality begin?
