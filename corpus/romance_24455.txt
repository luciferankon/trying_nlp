In a wildly unexpected turn of events 20-something friends probe conventions of the heart and toss them to the wind. One of the friends Bear faces a proverbial quandary: What do you do when the one you love is in love with someone else? Eventually they become emotionally entwined and the boundaries of love and friendship between some of them disappear. In the midst of it all they ask: Will any of them find the love they're looking for?
