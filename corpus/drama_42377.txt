Rick Irons (Robert Pierce) moves to Hawaii to live in paradise; surf the gigantic waves. At a party Rick meets Alma (Maureen McCormick) who quickly becomes a love interest. While dating Alma he begins to train for the United States surfing title. As Rick's life is racing to the top on the outside he personally feels like the bottom is falling out on the inside. Up and coming surfer(Rick Irons) meets girl(Maureen McCormick)(Marcia from the Brady Bunch series). The two fall in love and get married. He trains for the surfing championship with the help of a local surf legend. He achieves what he dreamed but is empty inside. A fellow surfer has a shop where Rick works. He also has a local Bible study. In the end Rick discovers what he is missing.
