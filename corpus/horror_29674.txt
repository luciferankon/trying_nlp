Two interwoven horror stories taking place within a 24 hour period as both are investigated by a small town Sheriff of Tacouma County NY. The first story involves the supernatural elements as college friends Madison and Zahira walk home and Madison is mowed down by an SUV and then hacked to death by one of the passengers. Later that night a grief stricken Zahira gets an idea to play with a Ouija board in hopes of contacting her friend. Big mistake as Madison returns from the dead with revenge on her mind... The second story follows Asher the new nanny who just arrived into town as she will soon learn of a pair of serial killing brothers whom are terrorizing all women in their path.
