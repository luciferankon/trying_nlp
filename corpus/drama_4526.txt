A young woman escapes from North Korea and seeks refuge in China before crossing the oceans in the hold of a cargo ship and disembarking in London. Bari becomes a masseuse but she doesn't just heal the body she also brings comfort to the souls of the dead because she has inherited from her beloved grandmother the gift to read the pain of others. Bari finds her way amongst other immigrants finds love in the most unlikely places and suffers set backs that drive her close to the edge.
