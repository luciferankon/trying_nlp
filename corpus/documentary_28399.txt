CHILDREN OF ALL AGES is an amazing three-ringed documentary showcasing the most storied entertainment form in American history: the circus. Ring 1 is Sailor Circus the longest running children's circus in the country. Ring 2 is the world's LARGEST miniature circus an astonishing 3800 square feet tabletop tableau showcasing more than a million pieces. Ring 3 is an exclusive talk with the greatest living circus performers ever to work under the tented Big Top.
