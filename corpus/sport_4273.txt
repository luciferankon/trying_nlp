The first FFC event in the United States will be June 3rd at the Daytona Beach Ocean Center in Daytona Beach Florida. Hitting the US market strong FFC owners have arranged for an amazing fight card for their first American event at the Daytona Beach Ocean Center in Daytona Beach Florida. Former UFC fighter Karo Parisyan is looking to prove that he still has plenty in his tank as he is set to take on Germany's up and comer Arda Ardas. The FFC Welterweight Champ and rising star Laszlo Senyei is set to defend his belt in highly anticipated title defense against Chris Curtis.
