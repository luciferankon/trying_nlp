Vjeruj u ljubav was licensed reality show broadcasted on Croatian RTL television hosted by actor Danijel Ljuboja. The purpose of the show was to connect various people and give them opportunity to start or continue their once broken relationship. The show was not limited only to people who developed romantic feelings toward someone else but also included long lost friends relatives and similar. People who were seeking contact usually would record video message for that special someone. The wanted person would watch the message and decide is she/he willing to establish the connection. The whole procedure was filmed and edited into feature stories as part of the episode. Each episode was consisted of studio part where people would finally meet and edited stories which explained the background of relations presented in the show.
