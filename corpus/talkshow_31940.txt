David Goyer knows how to bring comic book heroes to kicking screaming vengeful life as he did in The Crow: City of Angels the Blade series and Batman Begins. In this wry and surprising dialogue he reveals his tricks of the trade how to hook an actor's ego and why fear can pay the bills. In these in-depth discussions some of today's most successful screenwriters share their work habits methods and inspirations secrets of the trade and eye-opening stories from life in the trenches of the film business. It's powerful knowledge straight from the source.
