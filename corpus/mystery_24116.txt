Hind a 4 year old French girl shows signs of psychosis at school. The family of Middle Eastern - Druze roots believe Hind is a reincarnated soul (Hayat) whose life ended by murder. Social Services decide to take Hind away. The family escape from France to Israel. Investigation into Hind's story results in contact with Rami Hayat's son. Tension grows and positions polarize before Hind reveals who murdered Hayat.
