Four masked Men have just kidnapped Ryan Fletcher the teenage Son of Brian Fletcher; a prominent businessman and multi-millionaire. Their plan is simple; make a video of a battered and bruised Ryan send it to his Father and demand a ransom. Held up at an abandoned warehouse the first part of the plan goes off without a hitch and a ransom demand is sent. However as night falls the Men realize that they are not alone there is a Creature in the warehouse a blood thirsty creature that begins to take them out one at a time. Very soon the Kidnappers and their Victim realize that they will have to work together if they are going to survive the night.
