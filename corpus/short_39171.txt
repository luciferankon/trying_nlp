Terry quits his job and goes on a road trip in attempt to start taking advantage of life. After a few days he ends up in a pleasant little town miles away from home. Instead of staying in the traditional tourist area he opts to stay in the dingy underbelly of the burg. After an altercation outside of a local dive bar Terry is taken by a group of misfits on their drug run as they terrorize him along the way eventually to become integral parts of each others lives.
