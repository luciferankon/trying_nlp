In the eyes of the world the election of Barack Obama as US president was the ultimate proof of a successful integration.  But segregation though prohibited by law makes its return in the USA especially in public schools. In Selma Alabama a high place of the civil rights movement the racial situation is worrying: High schools illustrate the division of the city into two colors.   How do those who once militated for the rights of blacks react to this apartheid that does not say its name?
