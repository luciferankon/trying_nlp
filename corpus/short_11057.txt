When Matt's morning coffee turns into a chance encounter and mid-day romp with the sultry and seductive Selena he begins to think that he's met the girl of his dreams and his luck may be on the upswing. But when Selena reveals that she is actually a messenger of death sent to take him to the other side Matt finds that love at first sight has become fatal attraction. Matt isn't the only one bitten by Cupid's arrow - Selena has an agenda of her own.
