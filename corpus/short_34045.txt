Focus is not an idea. Focus is a paradigm to live by. In order to achieve your goal you have to keep focus. Even if your actions may seem contradictory at times. The hero of our story is trying to keep focus. He is sacrificing himself for and committing to his craft. This 2-day slice of life in the existence of an actor gives an insight to the mindset of 'keeping focus'. It is a short film that plays with the perception of reality. The world of 'Water my friend' unravels in the details and layers of the story.
