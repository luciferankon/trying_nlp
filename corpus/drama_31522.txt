Many years ago Clemencia met and fell in love with Luis Francisco. After a while Luis went to Italy with money inherited from his father.He tried several business but failed. When Clemencia discovered her pregnancy she went to Amada for help but was rejected. She had her baby in the street but when she awoke in the hospital the baby was gone. She fell in love with her doctor the handsome Dr Talamonti and married him and formed a successful business. One day an orphaned young woman comes to her offices asking for a job. Her stepson falls in love with the girl incurring the wrath of his ex girlfriend. Luis Francisco returns having spent all of his money and knowing nothing about Clemencia's baby.
