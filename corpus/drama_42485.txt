Angelena is a popular dancer who by chance meets Cary a talented recluse painter. They are an unlikely couple who discover common ground and fall in love. When obligation and peer pressure convince Angelena to break a solemn promise to Cary there is no turning back. His broken heart never recovers and their true love now questioned will never be. Will Angelena decide to go on knowing that her Broken Promise has taken away the only love she's ever known?
