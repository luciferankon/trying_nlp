Mikey is someone who has had it with life. He feels he will never find his way. While attempting to commit suicide his over-the-top boisterous and recently released from prison Uncle Steve shows up unannounced for a vacation. Mikey is forced to run all around LA with his loud and vulgar Uncle who seems to make embarrassing Mikey his new past time. This off the wall comedy is a fast paced adventure that will leave you laughing at every twist and turn while this unlikely duo struggles to clear Steve's name and add some excitement to Mikey's life.
