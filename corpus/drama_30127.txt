However it sneaks into her inexplicable dissatisfaction and a kind of depression that - with the complicity of disreputable knowledge (in particular the one with Edo Martini) - the door to make use of cocaine.Addiction Laura remain unaware both husband Luigi both the mother and the sister Luisa Serena as long as the woman does not have a car accident in which remains slightly injured along with two little children Julia and Antonio.Laura then comes out of the house by her husband that also prevents from getting in touch with the children.
