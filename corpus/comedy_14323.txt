After the death of Trudy a centenarian some lucrative land with an old house are left at the end of the village. That is convenient for the mayor ('Pavel Kikincuk' (qv)) who have designs on the land and want to build a used car lot there. But Trudy's descendant Adam ('Jan Dolanský' (qv)) decide that he likes the villa and he'll fix it up even though it is supposedly haunted. The plot revolves around a man with a slightly unconventional and ambiguous name Petr Soustal ('Lukás Langmajer' (qv)) who comes to the village to become the priest. He has no idea what's awaiting him or what all he will have to resolve and for this reason a lot of humorous situations arise...
