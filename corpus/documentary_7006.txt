In France at a time when it was inconceivable for a woman to pursue a career in art Berthe Morisot courageously followed her passion and became one of the founding members of the Impressionist art movement. Her male colleagues have become world famous names like Monet Degas Pissarro Sisley and Renoir. But Berthe Morisot has remained relatively unknown. The 'Heart is a Rebel' investigates Morisot's intriguing life and discovers that she is a powerful source of inspiration for young women today.
