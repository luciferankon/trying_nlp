Observe a glimpse into the life of a young man whom has served his country amassed tremendous wealth in a short time and augmented his understanding of the world by venturing to the corners of our planet in search of answers to life's secrets. Or has he? A lost soul Roy Parker wanders aimlessly along an ill-fated path burdened by guilt shame and lost hope. This is a short episode of a man and his self-proclaimed accomplishments fading away at the hands of humanity's greatest weaknesses. Roy is burdened by the pressures of his past. A history full of honor and success or perhaps only an illusion created and destroyed amongst the toxins of his dependencies. But one thing is certain: we have all met him. Roy Parker is 'That Guy.'
