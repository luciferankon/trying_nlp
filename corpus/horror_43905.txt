A Florida family The stepps decide to take a vacation to New Hampshire to visit with family. On their trip they pass through the town of Salem a coastal city in Essex County Massachusetts. Along the way they see everything dedicated to the Witch Trials. With Halloween just a few weeks away they decide that on the way home they will spend a day or two sight seeing everything the town has to offer. Never suspecting the horror and pure evil that is about to be bestowed on them and the town of Salem Massachusetts Margaret Scott is back and she's brought hell with her.
