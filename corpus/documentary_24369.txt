Original artworks and interviews with international artists: Anna Hill/Space Synapse (UK) Kirsten Johannsen (DE) Ayoko Ono (JP) Marko Peljhan (US) Sarah Jane Pell (AU) Bradley Pitts (US) Tim Otto Roth (DE) Eva Schlegal Angelo Vermeulen (BE) and Christian Walvogel (CH): members of the European Space Agency (ESA) Topical Team Arts & Science [ETTAS]. A showcase of projects and artworks which have been implemented by space agencies (ESA NASA JAXA NPO Energia / Roscosmos and CPK I Jurija A. Gagarina) or within a techno-scientific framework (National Science Foundation Swiss Air Force South African National Antarctic Program International Polar Year).
