A documentary on the Zombie Jamboree the 25th Anniversary of Night of the Living Dead convention that took place in 1993. Guests included numerous cast and crew of the Romero Living Dead movies as well as cast from Russo's spin-off series Return of the Living Dead. Other guests included horror icons Kane Hodder Gunnar Hansen and Tom Savini as well as sci-fi/fantasy favorites Adam West Dave Prowse and Suzie Owens among others. Documentary includes interviews with celebrities and fans and includes clips from events of the convention.
