An online documentary which utilizes found recycled and original media to create a new canvas of debate in the realm of documentary creation and 21st Century Feminism. Filmmaker Jodi Nelson takes off on a road trip in both real life and within the virtual sphere to question not only herself but others she encounters along the journey; just what it means to be a feminist in today's global society. Using live interviews traditional documentary techniques transmedia forms and social media platform integration she allows people from around the world to participate in the making of this film.
