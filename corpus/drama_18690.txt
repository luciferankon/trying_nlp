A Young Chap Gautham is on his way to Banglore to stop marriage of his Girlfriend who is getting married to another guy.In route he is kidnapped by John and demands him to narrate his story.On the wedding day of his ex-girlfriend he meets girl Nithya who happens to be a ex-girlfriend of a doctor.Soon friendship blossoms between Gautham and Nithya.As days pass by Gautham falls in love with Nithya and when he is about to propose enters Nithya's Fiancee.More such twists and turns follow till climax.
