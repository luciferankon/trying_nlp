In the unusually whimsical city of Desjardin Kimera -- along with her troupe of friends -- work to uncover the truth concerning the schemes of a mysterious man known only as Mr. Porter. Over the course of the season cleverly named "Kimera Vol. 1" Mr. Porter feeds Kimera what she asks (whether she truly wants to hear it or not) giving her the truth of what happened in the month of January a few years before she was born. Kimera comes face-to-face with painful truths about her father her mother her friends and those around them.
