A muscular war hero (Lance Lyman) was deep undercover in enemy territory trying to recover secret documents on information locating hidden nuclear warheads posing a threat to national security. The mission was simple. Secure the information burn down the base and remain undetected. Everything was fine until our hero was intercepted at the rendezvous point by a very cunning base guard. A soldier called "The Strongarm" then dragged our hero deep inside the enemy stronghold. It seemed that our hero was going to suffer big time. Will he survive this reign of terror? This is where the interrogation begins.
