Rabbi Shmuley Boteach releases his latest yet controversial book titled "Kosher Jesus" and goes head to head in a heated debated with W.S.J. Columnist Bret Stephens and Harvard Professor Noah Feldman in discussing the legacy of Jesus Christ among Jews and Christians. Mayor of Newark Corey Booker opens the discussion with a heartwarming story about his experience in Israel and how he is credited for convincing "America's Rabbi Shmuley" to pen this controversial book "Kosher Jesus". Jane Eisner the editor in chief of "The Forward" was the moderator for this compelling discussion.
