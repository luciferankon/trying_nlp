Elle-Anne is a young beautiful and above all passionate woman. She has a lust and desire for sex that fills every waking hour and even extends into her dreams. Filling her night with scenes of wonderful erotic and sensual pleasure. In order to satisfy her insatiable appetite she sells herself as a highly paid mistress. Elle-Anne has everything she desires until the dreams start to change. The sensual turning to blood chilling visions of pain torture and death. In an effort to understand these nightmare visions she seeks the advice of a psychiatrist. With his help she begins to explore the meaning behind these tortuous images. But even he cannot be prepared for the shocking truth that lies behind them.
