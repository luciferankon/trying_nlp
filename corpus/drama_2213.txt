Lee (Ekow Smith-Asante) a fashion designer is married to Eva (Nikki-Nikoletta Samonas) a nurse. Lee thinks all the ladies love him. Married just three years he always cheats on his wife. When Lee pushes Eva to take a bite of the devil's apple little does he expect he is getting his own back thrown against the wall. From here everything escalates out of control leading to unscrupulous behavior and a few surprises. Put on your armor the battle has just begun. There's no greater fool than a wise man in love. Will Lee and Eva be able to reconcile their marriage?
