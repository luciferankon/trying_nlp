Can an education gained from a not for profit school in the slum of Karachi really change a person? Can it really help a country where 20 million children are out of school? Rabbi Zidni Ilma (Our Lord increase us in knowledge) is a film on a exploratory mission to see what one NGO school network has achieved in the last 2 decades in the polarized nation of Pakistan. From the fishing slums of Karachi to the wheat fields of Punjab the director along with 2 renowned Pakistani photographers take a journey meeting some of the inspiring individuals that will move the audience and give them a deeper understanding of some of the problems facing Pakistan.
