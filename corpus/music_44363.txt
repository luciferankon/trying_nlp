An uncensored look into the life and music of Harlem rap hero Jim Jones and his Dipset Byrdgang. Rappers Mel Matrix Sandman NOE Diplomat Records pres Freeky Zeeky and producer Chink Santana comprise the most dangerous assembly of talent and muscle to emerge from the streets of NY in decades. Their real life stories uncovered in hard-hitting interviews by Angie Martinez weaves gritty realism between musical interludes. This mixed media collage is an enthralling next installment from filmmakers Ethan Higbee and Adam Bhala Lough.
