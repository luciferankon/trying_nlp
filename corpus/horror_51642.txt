Beware The Shadow Man is the story of Tom Miles ( Sean Gallaher ). Who has written a fictional book about LangLoth's real life infamous serial killer James Unger ( James Michael Shoberg ) or as he was called The Shadow Man. By giving words power Tom has unwittingly unleash Unger's dark presences amongst the living again. And it will not rest till has taken everyone Tom holds dear. Including his best friend Jake ( Andreas O'Rourke) and Childhood friend Libby ( Rachel Carey ). Can Tom undo the what he has created or will the Shadow Man become more than just the stuff of nightmares.
