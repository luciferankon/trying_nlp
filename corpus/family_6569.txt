Find a reason to escape from a life in material luxury and escape from stressful existence. Just enjoy the little things in life. She will give us every week some indulging endlessly inspiring and various do it yourself tips and tricks. All this will be combined with different items where Eva (the other presenter) will visit a variety of hot-spots will meet interesting people and where she will remind us on how we can enjoy the little thins in life. Factory of Romance is the ultimate lifestyle program.
