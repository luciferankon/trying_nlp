WHO IS MCAFEE ? In a story made for Hollywood eccentric antivirus pioneer John McAfee found himself at the centre of one of the hottest manhunts in recent history while broadcasting live on social and mainstream media. McAfee was named a "person of interest" but not a suspect by police officials in Belize following the murder of his neighbour Gregory Faull McAfee went on the run with his 20-year-old girlfriend for three weeks before crossing the border to Guatemala. Guatemalan authorities and Interpol detained him soon after entry and fighting against deportation to Belize McAfee was quickly swept away to the United States on a one-way flight to Miami.
