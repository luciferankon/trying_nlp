From behind prison walls SIN BY SILENCE reveals the lives of extraordinary women who advocate for a future free from domestic violence. Inside the California Institution for Women the first inmate-initiated and led group in the U.S. prison system shatters the misconceptions of domestic violence. Against the system and against the odds the women of Convicted Women Against Abuse have risen to expose the stigma of the cycle of domestic violence. Through their stories of terror and hope the viewer can begin to understand the cycle of violence the signs of an abuser and how each and every one of us is responsible for changing the tragedy of domestic violence.
