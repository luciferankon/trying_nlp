Imagine what I could do if I did all that I can. This quote from the 6th century icon Sun Tzu tells you all you need to know about the man. A superior warrior and strategist but also a man fair-minded and principled. Follow the young Sun Tzu through his training with Master Wu to finally become the most feared and revered military man to have ever engaged on the field of battle. His ultimate treatise Art of War has been read throughout millennia by the likes of Napolean Genghis Khan MacArthur and Eisenhower. Sun Tzu's life exemplified the Art of the Warrior.
