In the dry and drought-stricken land on Nowhere praying for rain does not work. But Miguelina who awaits her husband Jose weaves baskets with her skillful hands invites a thirsty and wandering child who loves her into the world of miracles with a drop of tear and from dry earth she makes flowers and plants grow. Others believe her soul is possessed. The time that they intended to burn her among her woven baskets Nature protects her.
