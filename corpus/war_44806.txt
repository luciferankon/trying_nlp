Five german Soldiers are sent on a special mission during 2nd World War. They all are very young and believe in the "great third Reich". Their mission: build a comunication station deep behind enimy's lines. They all are very proud to do their bit for the Fatherland but during the mission they recognize war is horror. After a long march through the Russian winter hard battles and many dead men the decide to return. But Russians soldiers and guerillas are hunting them.
