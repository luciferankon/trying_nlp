LIFE: Fashion Film is about 4 people: 2 handsome men and 2 beautiful women. As the music goes we meet the first woman on a beach finding her way between the rocks to the ocean. Thousands miles from her we see the first man standing up there in the mountains wondering and dreaming about a colorful world. Meanwhile the second woman walks into the forest admiring the beauty of nature. The second man warms up next to a camp fire during these cold winter days. Although they are all so far away from each others they need to be together to find happiness love and life.
