World War II the dark years in Philippine history. This is the story of Tacing (Charo Santos) - a woman involved in different love affairs which resulted in endless trials and tribulations. Angelito her first true love deceived and abandoned her. Instead of accepting her responsibility he paid her enough to change her life. Hesistant with the decision she went to Manila in search of a better life for her child. Fortunately luck is on her side. Nardo Tom Ramon and Angelito were just some of the many men who became part of Tacing's life and played parts in making her accept the reality of womanhood.
