The story takes place in the inner city after sunset. The streets are filled with delinquents as Anthony awaits a phone call. He is stranded without any money and is hatching some pretty stupid ideas about how to get home. Enter our hero Honeylicious. He's tough he's smart he's on the next level and he's ready to do whatever he can to help his new buddy but his motives are anything but clear. Through their bonding experience they start a friendship that will immediately be tested as both of their lives are turned dramatically upside down.
