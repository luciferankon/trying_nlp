A family of shepherds faces the father's death. Three of the 8 fatherless children decide to go on with the tradition of their family. The three shepherds regard life in an optimistic manner and nothing keeps them from loving their families and their sheep. The interviews with them and the images picturing their work in wintertime present the dreams of three young animal breeders as well as the challenges they face when practicing this trade in the context of life modernization in Romania.
