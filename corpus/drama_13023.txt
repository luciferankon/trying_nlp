A Russian family the Turkeltaubs emigrates to the US before the Communist revolution that overthrew the Czar. One of their twin sons Schulke disappears and is believed dead before they leave. After they settle in their new country the family does well--the surviving twin Nicholai becomes a crusading District Attorney. One of the cases he is assigned to prosecute is that of a small-time gangster Jacob Talinef who has killed a former girlfriend of Nicholai's. Further investigation of the case though reveals a shocking secret.
