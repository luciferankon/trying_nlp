When your job is protecting our most precious natural resources in some of the nation's most remote areas there is rarely a dull moment. Show chronicles the lives of conservation officers in America with the 2011 season focusing on the great state of Montana. Watch as these brave men and women defend public lands from poachers and other environmental hazards brave treacherous whitewater rapids and embark on intense snowmobile trails in the remote grizzly bear country. It's all in a day's work for these protectors of the great outdoors.
