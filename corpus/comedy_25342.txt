An idealistic young woman living in Los Angeles strives to be a journalist of substance she ends up working for a gossip network where celebrity and social media culture is worshiped. A half-hour situation comedy in the vein of a 21st Century "Mary Tyler Moore Show" and elements of "The Office" "Unbreakable Kimmy Schmidt" and "30 Rock".
