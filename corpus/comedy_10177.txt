The story of Gwen and Tonton childhood sweethearts born and raised in the province. They promise to love marry and be with each other until death do they part. But they went on separate paths when Gwen left to study in the city. There she made new friends and eventually forgot all about Tonton. When she made a wish to meet the guy who would die for her. Tonton appeared who still eager to marry her. Will they live happily ever after?
