When Tim Cohen travels back in time he feels as though he has hit upon the discovery of the 21st Century: He has found a way to time travel... in a cardboard box. His only problem is that nobody believes him. It also doesn't help that his proof of time travel is iPhone footage that nobody seems to be able to understand. Undeterred Tim resumes his experiment. Continuing to be met with skepticism even more incomprehensible time travel footage and his passion turning into paranoia Tim refuses to heed the calls to do something else with his life. Eventually just when it seems like he's about to give up TIM COHEN'S AMAZING ADVENTURES IN CARDBOARD BOX TIME TRAVEL show they are just getting started.
