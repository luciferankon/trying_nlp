Addicted to medication and illegal drugs; attempting suicide numerous times; sectioned for life - Liesl Alexander could never understand her craving for death and everything negative until she found a curse had been laid on her as a baby to make her - the younger twin - die. She was prayed for in mental hospital and experienced immediate healing from the confusion in her mind. Here she gives her story - an unbelievable turnaround to become a vicar's wife and successful author with a powerful healing ministry to help others.
