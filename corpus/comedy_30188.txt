Set against the backdrop of un-hip Los Angeles fledgling animator Jarrett Larson must find a way to put his failed long-term relationship behind him and go forth into the dating world. But even though he's a loser... he's not alone! Joined by an outcast group of friends and the imaginary byproducts of his own overactive imagination Jarrett is determined to find a steady job a reliable place to live and ultimately true love.
