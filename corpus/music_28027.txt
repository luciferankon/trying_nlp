An exclusive peak into the world of Country Music Superstars. The shows will chronicle the extravagant lives of these modern day cowboy's and girls. With first hand interviews personal accounts and behind the scenes footage each episode will give you a close and personal look into the lives of these idolized mega-musicians. Experience what it really means to be a Country Star in today's world of vanity and excess. From restaurants to yachts to ranches country music has some of the most well-to-do yet down to earth celebrities.... just how much are these stars earning and what are they spending it on? From 75000 dollar a day cruises...to 14 million dollar mansions. Lets just say these country superstars are just making millions and they are spending it too.
