In the summer of 1996 Darlie Routier fell under the shadow of suspicion in the murder of her young sons. The media circled her like sharks and eventually she was sentenced to death-row. 20 years later doubt still remains. Did Darlie do it? In a unique approach to true-crime facts merge with opinions mirroring the true confusion surrounding the haunting case. Through all the speculation and theorizing one thought remains: this was truly an All-American murder.
