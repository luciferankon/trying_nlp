In a game played by the rules one man plays by his own The 26th Man ~ is a compelling character-driven story based on the life of Singing Ed Nottle a career minor league baseball manager who never quite makes it to the major leagues. Ed's life is one of dreams disappointment perseverance passion family and humor personified in his experience of "just falling short" Baseball as the backdrop Ed Nottle has been in professional baseball for over 50 years as a pitcher a coach owner and manager. He has struck out Lee Mayes Eddie Mathews and Hank Aaron in succession. He sang "Fly Me to the Moon" while Billy Martin engaged in one o his bar room fisticuffs and coached over 90 players who went to the Major Legues.
