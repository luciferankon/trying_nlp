In this playful and rollicking swashbuckler drag king pirates take to the high seas on a quest to restore stolen treasures. The gender-skewed adventures begin when Butch a handsome but naive baby butch is compelled by the Queen to seek out and bring back her purlined "Koilos" the source of all pleasures and the key to order and harmony on her island. As an additional inducement the Queen holds out to Butch the promised return of the hostaged Claudia a beautiful and feisty femme who is not about to sit idly by awaiting rescue!. Under the Queen's wrathful gaze tops are bottomed tranny sailors seduce pirate Captains Kings are Mothers and cross-dressing femmes pack a wallop.
