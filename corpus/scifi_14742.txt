The year is 2049 and asteroid mining has created trillionaires but only those willing to gamble will succeed. Hayden is a 27 year old male who is currently studying space geology at University of San Diego for his masters. He has short black hair blue eyes medium build and a strong chin. Due to the robotics industry being fully realized much of the human labor and tasks have been eliminated or became obsolete. Hayden became interested in space geology because of his grandfather and hopes to land a coveted position at Deep Asteroid Industries.
