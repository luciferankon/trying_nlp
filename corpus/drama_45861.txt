The Government secretly Contracted a computer Programmer Charles Ike (Enyinna Nwigwe) to develop a software for the military they called the software "RED". This project took so much of his time that he began to neglect his wife (Baray Mcnwizu) . After a series of sleepless nights for the project and uncountable arguments with his wife he decided to divorce the wife and marry his former sweetheart Brenda (Keira Hewatch) who also is the wife's best friend. Unknown to him the project was not as secret as he thought. He had to decide between Protecting the Secret of The Federal Government and saving The lives of his Wife and Two Kids. His wife was also had to decide to abandon him or stand and fight for the lives of their children.
