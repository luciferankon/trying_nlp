The ROSA: Bravo Division lead by ROSA Agent Tracy Evans in London UK face one of the Worlds greatest terrorist cell organizations "The Doe's" lead by Anonymous. The Doe's are an organization of untraceable assassins who've never been identified by any global government surveillance agencies in the history of the United Nations. Those who were presumed to have unveiled their identities never lived to share their Intel. Agent Evans and her team will now have that very task in which they cannot afford to fail as the future of all humanity may depend on it.
