Different Mothers is based on a true story that happened to Polish movie director Kristofer Kieslowski during the filming of Station 1981 in which some of his footage was nearly used as evidence in a criminal case. Five young men go to a Station to film a documentary about People Waiting. Deciding to finish everything in one day they stay till late in the night. As time passes by each of the characters is influenced in a different way by the events in the station and the accumulated fatigue.
