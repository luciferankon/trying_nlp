Councilman Al Castillo has devised a plan to help him clean up the streets in the process boosting his credibility to be a candidate for the next Mayoral Election. Agent Romell Davis is assigned to take on a undercover case against a street kingpin named Terry Duvalle. Meanwhile the streets have become a war-zone against Rivals Terry Keys and Marquis. Jovan Rich Marquis's little Brother is a rap artist trying to make it out through the music and not follow in his brother footsteps. The battle for the position to Conquer Philadelphia is set in many ways Although the people will also have to Conquer themselves in the process.
