This love story between a Hindu boy Rahul Sharma (Rahul Romy Sharma) and Muslim girl Ghazal Siddiqui (Ambalika Sarkar) invokes the 'Laila-Majnu' track to drive home it's legendary leanings. Unfortunately there's really nothing much to recommend here save for a few lines of stinging thought-provoking dialogue. Rahul and Ghazal perform the Laila-Majnu play in their college and eventually fall in love. But the girl's uneducated successful land shark father Siddiqui (Mukesh Tiwari) is livid about the pairing and puts up hurdles in their way. An NGO basically a website tiled 'Love Guard' started by Arjun - a love-loss survivor hopes to help and protect such couples achieve happily ever after. Rahul and Ghazal seek out his help but will that be enough to withstand the entire onslaught that Siddiqui has in store for them?
