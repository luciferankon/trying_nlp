Once a Garden follows three artists in mourning for a lost friend and a lost New York. Leaving the funeral of Kevin whose wife Sarah died in 9-11 and for whom that loss proves too much Mikey Lou and Nicky seek solace in the public garden Sarah used to tendand in a seemingly bottomless flask of booze. Unable to find comfortthe three menan outsider artist a refugee from television and a self styled "drunken poet and middle aged fraud" scour the bars of Manhattan looking for the city the used to love. Along the way they meet a radical Arab-American Professor Patriotic Teamsters Conga playing Jazz Musicians Republican Bartenders and three beautiful but eccentric women. Their Odyssey climaxes in a long night in a Soho loft complete with Chemical Substances Bathtub Kisses Italian lessons Marmite Poetry and Despair.
