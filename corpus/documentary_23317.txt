She was far away. In her first attempt to be close she was touched by the revolution flame among her generation. Hence she decided not to leave Egypt again. Esraa a 22 years old Egyptian girl carrying her camera and riding her bike while documenting her young colleagues stories. But only one year was enough to change everything she is no longer carrying her camera or riding her bike. She is now instead sitting on a wheelchair. Furthermore most of her colleagues were either imprisoned or passed away.
