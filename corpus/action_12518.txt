On June 6 2016 Volition released a trailer for the next entry in the _Saints Row (2006) (VG)_ (qv) universe Agents of Mayhem. The game takes place after the events of _Saints Row: Gat Out of Hell (2015) (VG)_ (qv) in a futuristic setting. The cinematic announcement trailer shows Persephone Brimstone (a character featured at the end of Gat Out of Hell) leading a new group of Saints known as "M.A.Y.H.E.M." under the Ultor Corporation's payroll. Agents of Mayhem is set for a 2017 release.
