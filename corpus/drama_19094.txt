The tragic story of Rozana who is love with Asyraf. After the death of her elder sister Rozana's father begs her to marry her brother-in-law in order to take care of her nieces. After a lot of soul searching she marries her brother-in-law. One year later she rekindles her relationship with Asyraf. As she is about to elope with Asyraf Rozana's husband meets with an accident. What does she do?
