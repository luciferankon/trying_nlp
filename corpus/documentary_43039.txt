With slumping attendance at the Raceway and advertisers' stickers appearing less on racing cars Darren Rovell takes an inside look at the Business of NASCAR. In this doc you'll find interviews with Brian France of NASCAR driver/owner Tony Stewart visits to the Spint All-Star Race in Charlotte NC and watch Tony Stewart's win at Pocono. You'll also get a peek at the fans of NASCAR both on the track and in a super-fan's basement den.
