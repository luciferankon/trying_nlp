Elemental Justice is a story of two women whose lives scrape against the harsh realities of crime ridden Paris. A young woman gone wrong an older woman who struggles to help her and a ruthless pornographer clash in this story of betrayal and retribution. Shot on the Greek island of Samothraki and the streets of Paris "Elemental Justice" pulls you into the world of Emily and Dulce as they struggle to overcome the evil that is at the heart of the trade in young beautiful women. To survive they are forced to challenge the criminal that wants to exact revenge.
