Isla Gray (Amy Kinder) is a troubled teenage girl struggling through the loneliness and isolation of loosing her family. Once a devout Catholic Isla's turned away from her faith while taking comfort in the company of an excommunicated priest Father Black. Isla's rose colored glasses begin to erode revealing to her the modern day Sodom and Gomorra she lives in. Confronted with this new revelation Father Black and Isla begin on a path that brings their spirituality closer to God but their humanity closer to Heaven's Fallen Angels.
