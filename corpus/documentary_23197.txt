Has Matthew been wasting his life? He's 29 years old with 4 memberships at adult video stores 55 tapes of compiled porn and absolutely nothing to show for it: no girlfriend no ambition only a big stack of porn. Enter RUN RUN IT'S HIM an autobiographical documentary exploring in intimate detail how Matthew's addiction to porn has warped his understanding of women and dominated his personal relationships. Shot over the course of 7 years RUN RUN IT'S HIM follows Matthew as he interviews his parents ex-girlfriends female acquaintances and a fellow porn connoisseur struggling to figure out what went wrong before it's too late.
