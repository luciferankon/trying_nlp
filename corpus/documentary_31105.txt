Hank Williams Jr. is a country musician Southern rocker and the bad-ass offspring of country's first superstar. A performer since he was 8 years old Williams has worked with some of the biggest names in country music including Johnny Cash Jerry Lee Lewis and Charlie Daniels. His rock 'n' roll-meets-country style of music helped him win a Grammy an Emmy and the honor of being the Academy of Country Music's entertainer of the year for three consecutive years. From surviving a 442-foot fall down the side of a mountain to scoring a hit with the NFL this one-hour special reveals the most significant moments in Bocephus' life.
