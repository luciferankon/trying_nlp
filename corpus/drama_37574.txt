Paul and Regina couldn't be any more in love. Yet the two Bostonians are both carrying excess baggage from previous relationships which weigh down their happiness together. Paul and Regina's worlds are further jostled when Paul is set up and arrested for cocaine possession. However an even greater calamity surfaces when authorities discover that Paul is not an American citizen. This fact coupled with the drug charge subject him to deportation. A surprising chain of events leads not only to a tense and meaningful climax but to the revelation of an unexpected betrayal with tragic consequences.
