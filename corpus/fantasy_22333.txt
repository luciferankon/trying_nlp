High in the mountains of Colombia where the Caribbean Sea meets the tropical rain forest a tribe of indigenous elders lives in seclusion. They are the original people of the earth and their job is to warn civilization when the Earth has reached it's Eco-tipping point. Seen through the eyes of teenage Chris the Elders pass on their wisdom and Chris thru fantasy and action sequences earns the knowledge that can save he Earth from Eco-destruction. Adapted from a true story. The film is based on the second book by Author John Lundin who's first book was written in collaboration with His Holiness The Dali Lama.
