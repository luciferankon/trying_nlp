A bus breaks down in the wilderness. Seven elderly womenaverage age 71 and one 27 year old are stranded at a deserted farmhouse. The have only their wits their memories and eventually some roasted frogs' legs to sustain them. Through the long days and nights this remarkable group of strangers share their life stories and exchange intimate thoughts; turning the crisis into a magical time of humor and spirit. Featuring non-professional actors and spontaneous dialogue this memorable film dissolves the barrier between fiction and reality weaving a heartwarming tale of friendship and courage.
