Eleana is married with Giorgos Stavrou a wealthy footballer and businessman who because of his busy program does not notice that he is neglecting his wife. Parallel Eleana starts working as a prostitute at the harbor of Piraeus and finds again love in one of her clients Christos. Christos while falling madly in love with her does not have a clue about her double life as a wealthy wife and at the same time as a prostitute and wants to marry her. Then she starts avoiding him but the life at home is very boring and her husband not willing to understand her problems. Even when she tells him that she is a prostitute he leaves the house due to an urgent meeting leaving her alone with her psychosis growing wild.
