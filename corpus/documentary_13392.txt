To mark the 10-year anniversary of one of America's worst natural disasters Sam Champion Jim Cantore and other meteorologists from The Weather Channel will take viewers on a scientific journey through possible scenarios that could take place if Hurricane Katrina hit 50 years from now. The future-focused one-hour special "Katrina 2065" will utilize cutting-edge weather animations to address the question much of the Gulf Coast still asks itself: Could we endure this again?
