Two sisters face life changing decisions after the unexpected death of their mother from cancer. Will the streetwise drug dealer kingpin wreck havoc on their lives? Go for a wild ride with Tess (Toshia Addison) and Bre' (Lauren Hancock) as they struggle to survive the low-down and money hungry ways of Blue (J-1 The Illinois Boy). Also starring Sherry Thurman as Mom and Sylvester Brown as Tank. Anyone's life can change with a Twist of Fate.
