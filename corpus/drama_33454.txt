Esteban travels Chilean's North along the highway following in Isabel's footsteps. His route takes him through different towns across a desolate and unknown Chile to the very same places where Isabel had been and where he encounters the same people she encountered. But as his adventure unfolds its initial intention fuses together with that which comes up along the path. His journey becomes an end in itself in a spectacle of characters and landscapes that are as unreal as they are real.
