The Story is about a retired 4 yr. Martial Arts champ who been out of his luck when he saves his neighbors little girl from trouble his life would never be the same again. When she convince him to get out of retirement he starts to train again and she join him on his journey. So now he joins the tournament to become a champion again. But when he goes through the tournaments greatest fighter he must now deal with the current champion who threaten him if he doesn't quit he going to hurt someone important in his life. So now he more determined than ever to protect his new friend from anyone. The fight to regain honor starts here. The winner of the tournament will become the New Golden Dragon Champion.
