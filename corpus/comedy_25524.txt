Catch me a Bandit is a Christmas film based on the series call the Socodoodles by Sue Ellen Randolph. When a thief broke into the Socodoodles home on the night before Christmas Eve everyone though that it was Santa Claus. Their cousin Donald plot that they will capture Santa. Doodles just cared about getting back her TV. So on Christmas eve night they waited and captured Santa. Doodles took a Time Medallion from Santa and little did she knew that it was a time transporting device. They went back in time to captured the thief but was in vain. After they returned they described the thief to Santa. It was his old friend now enemy call VOLKLAUS!! So the quest began to capture the evil thief who was trying to give Santa a bad name.
