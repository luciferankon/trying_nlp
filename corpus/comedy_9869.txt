Gregg a father who is newly separated from his wife creates a detective show and casts his entire family in it. The detective agency tackles cases of a mysterious nature from a woman who claims to be an astronaut from another planet to a man who suspects his dog is a clone. One son is on the verge of dropping out of high school; when the other son takes off for LA to try to be an actor the father's advice is 'don't pretend you're not pretending.' It suits him. The mysteries in both his life and his fiction have been about love all along.
