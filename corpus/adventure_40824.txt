When a sickly child predicts the arrival of God in seven days to answer a question for those in attendance and her claim goes viral on the internet a chain reaction of events unfold as many characters rich and poor good and bad believers and non-believers begin a cross country journey to discover the truth of her prophecy. As the pilgrimage closes in on ground zero government forces clash with the people as they enforce road blocks to maintain safety and security tempers flare injuries escalate and lives hang in the balance as characters fight each other and face unspoken truths within themselves as they struggle against all odds to arrive on the appointed day.
