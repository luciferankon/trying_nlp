Steve Briggs (DaneDimension) is a 17 year old from Manhattan. After losing his mother Anna Briggs at the age of 3 he lived a very secluded life with his over protective father Robert. This lead Steve to being the target of bullies in his school life. After discovering he has telekinesis he unleashes his powers on his life long bullies which leads to him almost tearing down the school. Now on the run from police Steve must learn to control his new found powers before he kills anyone else while at the same time learning about his mother's secret past.
