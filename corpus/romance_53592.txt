Is love enough to sustain a relationship or is wealth and status also necessary in a modern-day relationship? Choi Do Kyung (Kwon Oh Joong) has been dating Seo Young Ji (Han Ji Hye) but he breaks up with her because she is from a poor background like him and he is determined to marry a rich woman to better his station in life. Do Kyung has his eyes set on the beautiful Jung Ah Mi (Song Seon Mi) who is a plastic surgeon.
