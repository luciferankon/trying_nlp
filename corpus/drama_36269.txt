This poetic ode of unforgettable characters is a haunting look into the life of a sad and desperate woman during the first great depression. A beautifully flawed yet humbly complicated family meets the daunting challenges of the time. Life takes Leola a newly discovered actress in Hollywood on arduous trips as she must witnesses racism and sympathetically navigate the troubled waters of Tinseltown. Sacrificing all that she believes all that she has and all that she is ending in an unbelievable fatalistic twist.
