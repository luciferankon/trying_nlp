Biopic of the late street brawler and internet sensation turned MMA legend Kevin "Kimbo Slice" Ferguson. Chronicles the life of Kevin Ferguson who went from a bouncer to a YouTube street fighting sensation. Kimbo's internet fame launched his meteoric rise in the MMA and ultimately made him one of the most recognizable names and faces in the sport. Despite his lack of professional training the fighter known as Kimbo continued to be a main event until his untimely death in 2016.
