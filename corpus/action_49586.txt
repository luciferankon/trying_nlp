The Welcoming Attack is an action feature film based around the life of a young African boy who travels to Canada on a 2 month single entry tourist visa. The film follows the lead as a tourist who later decides to reside in Canada illegally beyond his visa. He faces several challenges as an illegal resident who is later forced to dive into a world of drug lords gangsters and hookers. Will he make it in a dangerous world or will he lose his life trying to make things right? Find out soon on 'The Welcoming Attack'.
