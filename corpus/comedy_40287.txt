In this pilot episode Bozana does the old thing and uses every possible subversion such as sabotaging the stove only to avoid cooking dinner. Svetlana is dissatisfied with Rasha's lack of attention towards her and tries to make him notice but he just wants to rest a bit after work and watch his favorite quiz. When Svetlana fails to draw Rasa's attention Bozana decides that she is being mistreated and gives her advice: to split everything in the apartment in half to make Rasa care about their marriage again.
