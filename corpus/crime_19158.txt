Life for Charlie seems to be getting worse. What with his single parent father constantly piling pressure on him to look after his brother and sister so he can concentrate on his banking work. Charlie begins to find it hard to juggle his family school and social life. He decides the best way to get back at his father for making his life a misery would be to rob from his bank... With the help of his friends he assumes all would go well however; Things start to spiral out of control after Charlie meets a girl named Alix. Charlie starts to see that maybe it has done more damage than good.
