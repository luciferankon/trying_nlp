When 9 year-old Lincoln Tapp found himself sitting in a meeting with his parents and school teachers he had no idea that very moment would set into motion a journey that would change his life forever. He was told he had a learning disability and his education options were limited. YoungWild is a compelling new series that breaks the mold. A story of how a young man's battle with adversity leads to an intense passion for bow hunting and a surprising self-discovery. With every new episode Lincoln gets closer to his goal of harvesting all of North America's 29 big game species. Season One is an epic fair-chase adventure that showcases many impressive accomplishments; including becoming the youngest hunter on record to harvest a Stone sheep with a bow.
