In a flurry of excitement a young heir marries his girlfriend and together they begin to live life as 'Adults'. Life does not come as sweet nor blissful as they expected as the they begin to face the harsh realities which further exacerbate due to their immaturity. One of those obstacles is the heir's dreams of a woman whom he lusts for. The dreams begin to come more often and the heir becomes obsessed as he tries to find a method to get into his dreams and find her. His search begins to crumble his world and those around him as he deals with the duality of love and lust.
