Having escaped the terrible ghosts of the past and redressed its wounds Rwanda has long been a peaceful and safe country. Known specifically for the last remaining mountain gorillas Rwanda offers a lot more. A paradise in the midst of Africa with its volcanoes lakes rivers and savanna which shape both the nature and the social life. From Nyungwe Rainforest to Akagera National Park from lions to chimpanzees Muhtesem Ruanda (Remarkable Rwanda) documentary covers all aspects of this beautiful country.
