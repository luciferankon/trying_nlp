Curtiss plagued by his overbearing doppelganger Fredrick C. Alphin goes through his everyday life of butchery yearning to discard his former identity as a naturally gifted businessman for that of a sensitive performance artist. When Arno his 'best friend' returns to their hometown tensions flare; with hilarious results. Curtiss knowing that Arno is just as much a con-artist as he himself is hesitantly allows himself to go back into business with his friend. They decide to launch Curtiss' career as a performance artist in the basement of the butcher shop and pack the house by any means necessary.
