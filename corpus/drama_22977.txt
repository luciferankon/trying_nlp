Sammie Chase grew up in a small town with no money. She worked her whole life to get out of there and become someone. She graduated top of her class at law school. Right after graduation she gets her dream job at one of Los Angeles top law firms. Sammie is moving towards the life of her dreams when Martin Perez comes into the law firm..Martin was seriously injured and lost everything after he was hit by a car. The man driving the car was one of L.A.'s most powerful business men. Will Sammie risk everything to make sure Martin gets the justice he deserves?
