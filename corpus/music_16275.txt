SUZANNA LUBRANO LIVE AT OFF-CORSO Suzanna Lubrano's debut DVD Live at Off-Corso features a stunning live performance. Live at Off-Corso was originally a TV concert which has now been televised in numerous countries around the world including Brazil Portugal and many more. Released on DVD in June 2010 this incredible performance stars a line-up of special guests and dancers including; Dutch saxophonist Candy Dulfer Dutch idol contestant David Goncalves Lantuna the Lamba Zouk Dance team and the Brazilian Zouk Dance team.
