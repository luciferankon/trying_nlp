Shawn Gray has been experiencing dark twisted visions and does not remember much of his past. He sees a girl reoccurring in his hallucinations who he has not met before. Because of his hallucinations he receives treatment from neurologist Dr. Erin Robyn whom Shawn is unsure if he can trust. As Shawn's visions get darker his trust in Robyn and the mysterious girl soon lead Shawn to uncover the dark truth behind his own past. Along the way he is faced with danger and deception. Shawn will have to decipher what is real and what is fantasy.
