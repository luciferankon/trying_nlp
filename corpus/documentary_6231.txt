2 x 1 hour documentary series produced for SBS Australia National Geographic and Granada Media about dinosaur hunters fossil cops shady dealers and the smuggling of prehistoric relics. In 1996 some rare Stegosaur footprints were stolen from an aboriginal sacred site in Broome Western Australia. This documentary attempts to track their whereabouts and also expose the illicit trade in fossils going on around the world. From outback Australia to the 'Badlands' of USA and 'fossil goldfields' of China - this is the world of the 'Dinosaur Dealers'.
