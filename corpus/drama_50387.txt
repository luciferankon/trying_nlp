Mary a mature woman lawyer remains unemployed because of her honesty. Someone like her who believes in the Justice of God seems to have no place in our society but she does not despair and while looking for work writes what she believes matters in a little notebook. She meets two men Manuel a musician who survives as best he can with whom she shares her writings and Santiago an executive director of a record company very committed to protect authors from the disaster of illegal downloading on internet. Both influence her and she is cannot avoid confronting new challenges where once more her integrity will be put to the test.
