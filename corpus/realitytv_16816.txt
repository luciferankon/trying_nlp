A team of students at Full Sail University come together to create the first-ever real world functioning Iron Helmet. Their prototype responds to the name JARVIS and has a select list of commands it's able to perform. With the help of YouTube influencer James Hobson aka the Hacksmith the students were able to successfully integrate the Microsoft HoloLens into a 3D printed helmet. Within the confines of the Hololens JARVIS is able to close the faceplate scan the environment analyze faces in the room and even order pizza for the crew.
