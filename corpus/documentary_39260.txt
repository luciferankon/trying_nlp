Tabatha's Hollywood Hair hosted by renowned stylist Tabatha Coffey is a one-hour documentary that looks back at Hollywood's most iconic hair styles. From Jennifer Aniston's "Rachel" to Jean Harlow's platinum blonde to Justin Bieber's bangs to Farrah Fawcett's feathers - we're analyzing the hairdos that influenced not just Hollywood but people everywhere. This special features exclusive interviews with numerous celebrity stylists & their famous clientele.
