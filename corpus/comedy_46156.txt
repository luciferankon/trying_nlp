Comedy in 3 parts. The first one called "O Padre e a Modelo" tells the story of a young man who in order to conquer his shyness seeks help with an actors' teacher who forces him to dress like a priest. In these clothes he meets a very sexy actress. In the second story a middle-aged man tries to confront his sexual problems in a motel. In the third a man suffering from claustrophobia has to hide inside a closet when his lover's aunt arrives unexpectedly.
