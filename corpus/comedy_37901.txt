Rob (Justin Miles) enjoys a few weeks of single dad freedom while his son is away at Don Landovan's soccer camp but bringing on his boss Dave Roth Jr. (Robert Descant) as a sponsor for his new cooking show is a decision that rocks his kitchen fantasy to its core. To make matters worse his longtime bud Carwash Jeff (Shad Hernandez) seems to be playing both sides. *Cooking for Singles is a sequel to Cooking For Moms which was a sequel to Cooking For Dads.
