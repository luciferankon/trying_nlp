A former child TV star and popular vlogger disappeared without a trace from a hotel room filled with web cams. Two years later a filmmaker created a documentary about her mysterious disappearance featuring interviews with experts and people close to her. The film reviews the shocking footage with experts that many believe to show a paranormal entity. Did a stalker kill young Winter Grae or did she simply quit Hollywood. The investigative effort takes the filmmaker and a TV entertainment reporter to the dark side of fame and the willingness to sell your soul to achieve fame.
