The about is Kashish and her four sisters. Sujal is a handsome boy meets Kashish and introduces her to his sisters. Later they find out that Sujal is the person whom they left behind when she crashed her car. This is the beginning of a serious misunderstanding between Sujal and Kashish. Slowly Sujal and Kashish start a loving relationship but Kashish's life seems to face continual problems with Sujal.
