Ron have you ever danced with a girl? asks a dark-eyed hostess at the strip club where he's celebrating his 30th birthday. "No." How Ron who has cerebral palsy and lives in a group home got to the club and if he gets to dance with a girl is the film's story. Ron is high-spirited as well as in need of help to get through daily life. For his thirtieth the home has a party with hats and party favors but it's not enough for the exuberant and sexually-aware Ron who convinces Martin one of his nurses to take him to the club. A dancer inveigles Martin onto the dance floor so that Ron can be left to the hostess and his own devices his chair and his pent-up joy for life.
