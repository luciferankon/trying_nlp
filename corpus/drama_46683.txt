The show aired on ATN-7 Sydney GTV-9 Melbourne as well as on other affiliated stations throughout Australia. The series ran from 28 May 1960 until 7 October 1962. Presentations were: 28 May 1960 The Grey Nurse Said Nothing 23 July 1960 Thunder on Sycamore Street 18 September 1960 Shadow of a Pale Horse 1 June 1961 The Concert 8 June 1961 Long Distance 19 August 1961 Mystery Of A Hansom Cab 3 June 1962 Suspect 7 July 1962 The One Day of the Year 5 August 1962 Candida 8 September 1962 Manhaul 7 October 1962 Shadow of the Vine
