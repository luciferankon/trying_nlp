Parshuram Paikray (Mihir Das) power full man who doesn't believe in Love Marriage. He has two daughters. His elder daughter Anjana elopes with a guy called Amar form a lower class on the night of her marriage. Parshu's men kidnapped all the friends of Amar. Sanju is one of them. Sanju likes a girl called Sanjana (parijat) who happens to be the younger daughter of Parshu. The rest of the story is all about Sanju elopes with Sanjana and convinces Parshu to marry her.
