Born of sheer necessity Dundeal Entertainment (DDE) is the brainchild of Macc Dundee and a team of close associates who have worked from the ground up to create an independent entertainment powerhouse. Traveling between California Las Vegas The Midwest and Atlanta DDE established connections throughout America's entertainment capitals to secure a network of support while building their brand. As projects began to expand DDE documented various aspects of the independent grind in order to present to a wide audience the necessary tactics principles and methods employed to achieve growth in a demanding industry. The Town Bizzness Series compiles and demonstrates the most essential elements of the daily hustle so that the common man can be readily equipped with the basic tools needed to achieve their dream.
