The latest series brought to Australian screens is the 13-week TVS arts series titled Dreams and Visions. This weekly 30 minute arts program showcases 4 to 5 stories each week on Australian artists and has received a positive reaction in the media mainly due to its universal appeal. It's ability to share the aesthetics of an ensemble of creative professionals which enables viewers to travel vicariously through the artist's mind within a seamless experience. The series is making use of multi-platform opportunities given the extensive source of material and talent within the community. It is expected that Dreams and Visions will receive much sponsorship support throughout the community.
