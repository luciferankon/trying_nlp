The World Of Liu Yuyi is a kaleidoscope of vibrant colors driven by the artist's never-ending passion to paint. Whether celebrating the epic cultural heritage of China or recording important political events of its recent history it is always the desire to capture peace and harmony and the vivid love for life that drives master Yuyi. To date he has painted hundreds of captivating canvases some of them spanning more than 100 meters. He has a talent for capturing celebrity portraits and joyful moments. Join us as we explore the life of Liu Yuyi a master Chinese painter.
