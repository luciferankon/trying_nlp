Somewhere in the remote Greek province Dimos Pournaris a vibrant cafe owner is struggling to attract the girl of his dreams: the village's most beautiful girl. Although this may be true out of the blue a curious coincidence will give him illusions of grandeur and before long from a down-to-earth toiler Dimos will pose as a wealthy and successful big oil company entrepreneur. Undoubtedly this petty scam is designed to gain his sweetheart's attention nevertheless life can sometimes be a bed of roses with a bit of luck and sharp wits.
