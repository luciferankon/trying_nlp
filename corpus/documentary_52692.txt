There was a time a period that lasted about five years between 1977 and 1981 when the city of Bologna had become the center of the world: everything seemed to happen within its old walls it had become a kind of geographical hub of imagination and creativity. Back then many different utopias gave birth to a new "social layer": a revolutionary project made of creativity new languages giving power to imagination the 1977 movement the Drama Arts and Music Studies school Radio Alice the out-of-course students the chained protesters in Piazza Maggiore... There's been a lot of talk about that period but there's a story that's been left out a story only a few know: a story told by the those who have crossed known breathed experienced met and lived even for a few moments the place and the characters of the Warhol-like factory called Traumfabrik.
