REAL Change is an uplifting and star-studded Prime-Time one-hour Network television special that celebrates teachers and individuals who make a difference in American schools today. REAL Change will air on CBS O&O's on April 23rd 2013 two weeks before National Teacher Appreciation week of May 5th. The goal of REAL Change is to promote the ideals and great works of philanthropy and inspire every American to get involved and make a difference. The result will move a nation to "Recognize Explore Adopt and Live Change."
