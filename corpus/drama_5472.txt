It is a story of some human lives whose life got affected due to terrorism. The story depicts the problem from broad socio-political scenario to very individual things. The protagonists both limbs stop working due to the injury of bomb blast. Though bed ridden he cannot hide his biological need and his mother get worried tensed shied by looking at the stain of his nightfall. Will she be able to something going beyond the social barrier for his son? How will she react when the main accused of terrorism managed to get bail under political influence?
