How should Alice tell the woman she loves that she's pregnant after an escapade? And how would Dana react? 24 years old Alice can't tell her girlfriend and increasingly retreats into her shell. The first time in her life she has to make a severe decision and nobody to talk to. In the end Alice finds the strength to take responsibility for her life and talk to Dana. "Vanishing Points" is a film about coming of age the loving relationship of two women and decisions that can change our whole life.
