Brooklyn Experience is an immersive drama that explores the daily lives of a dysfunctional white family as they go through the current US immigration crackdown. Vincent is the head of the family and sole economic support who works as a middle-level administrative employee in an immigration agency in New York. The growing fear of the citizens is the theme that forces the characters to redefine their position before this dilemma. Brooklyn Experience is a 360 fictional feature film recorded in March of 2017. This is the first work-in-progress. Duration: 15min.
