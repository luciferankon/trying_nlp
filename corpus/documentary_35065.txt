One of the biggest and most mysterious hunters of the seas of all times... The Megalodon shark. Before many millions years the up to 20-metres-long shark made the oceans unsafe. Nothing could escape from his gigantic teeth. The Tyrannosaurus Rex was a ruler of the ground the Megalodon a ruler of the seas. Why has this shark become extinct? Has it really become extinct? Proceed with the team of the research vessel Quest on a breathtaking trip in the dark depths of the sea.
