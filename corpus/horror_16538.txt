Dr. George Lusk is an accomplished geneticist with a beautiful wife who adores him. After accepting a coveted award for advances in the field of genetic mutation the doctor has a little too much to drink and decides to drive his wife home impaired. In his inebriated state he causes an auto accident and his wife is killed. In his grieve and knowing it is quite illegal George decides to clone her and bring her back to life. After finding an abandoned late 1800s building in London he tests his theory and clones a preserved corpse that he finds in a walled off section of the old building. He never realizes that he has stumbled upon the remains of most horrific killer of all time 'Jack the Ripper'. The test is a resounding success but the past is about to come back to haunt him. Some people are better left - dead.
