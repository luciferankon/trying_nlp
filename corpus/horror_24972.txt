A remote genetic research modification facility has gone dark. The parent corporation "Mammon" cannot risk international exposure as to the nature of their terrible experiments so they send in a group of in-house mercenaries dubbed "Noble Team" to ascertain what has happened. Noble Team have their orders: 1) Retrieve the facility data core. 2) Rescue any survivors. They are trained by the best and ready for anything... except this. An outbreak of some kind has ravaged the facility staff and has turned them into aggressive primal killing machines. Now the mercenaries are in for the fight of their lives as their mission parameters have changed... Now they're just trying to survive.
