Romance thriller about two star-crossed lovers Chase and Sierra who get reacquainted on Facebook after being apart for approximately 20 years. They plan to meet in Paris on "The Eiffel Tower" at the midnight hour to rekindle what time took away from them so long ago. As Chase questions his sanity he must now follow through by suppressing his darkest fears and mundane subsistence all the while troubled by a glowing red light's constant beckoning of him. Their lust for each other takes every thought and waking moment of their minds although they both know it may spell disaster.
