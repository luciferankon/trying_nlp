In a delicate and humane manner this film touches upon a very serious issue: how to stop fearing death while being in love with life. We talk (and laugh) about this subject with the protagonists of this film modern geniuses and ordinary people from different parts of the world. Tania Khodakivska film ENTICING. SUGARY. BOUNDLESS OR SONGS AND DANCES ABOUT DEATH creates links between the certainty of death and the simplicity of life. A journey that starts as an external observation turns into a personal experience when the Director of Photography of the film dies before the end of the shooting.
