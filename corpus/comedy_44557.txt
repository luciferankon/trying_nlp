A film adaptation of a great theatrical success of the actors Kostas Rigopoulos and Kakia Analyti based on the comedy by Francois Campaux Chérie Noir. A writer is going through a serious crisis since neither his career nor his marriage are going well. His talent and friends seem to have abandoned him. The only one standing by his side is a girl from Africa who has come to Greece to study and in order to make ends meet she works as a maid in his home. Naďve and kind she manages to change the climate that reigns and restore a happy atmosphere to the home.
