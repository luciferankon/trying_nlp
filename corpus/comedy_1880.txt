The comic mishaps of two down-on-their-luck brothers and a suicidal ex-con who literally drops into the brothers' lives when he lands in the back of their truck after leaping off a highway overpass. The three of them find themselves mistaken for contractors at a lavish wedding and soon discover that the bride is being forced to marry her loutish suitor against her will. After the wedding goes sour the house is partially demolished and a stampede breaks out the three men decide to team up as business partners though it's never clear what services they propose to provide.
