Sixteen year old Kip "Kid" Cassidy suffers mild bouts of mild anxiety and depression. Following the accidental death of his close friend Chet Farley he grapples with his own thoughts of death and an unsealed suicide note he had written. On Monday Kip overwhelmed by the sympathy of others at home and at school goes back and fourth between anxiety and disengagement. Memories of his lost friendship are the only thing that seem real the strength of them is uncertain.
