A grandson accomplishes an old grandmother's dream by taking her on a magical journey to the country where her father was born by mere accident of fate. He was the son of Italians and on a trip from his parents he was born in Buenos Aires. The grandson tries to follow the steps from where his great-grandfather lived while his grandmother tries to find his spirit through the streets of Buenos Aires. In the winter of 2009 a grandson realizes an old dream of his grandmother: take her on a journey through the city where his great-grandfather son of Italians was born by coincidence of fate or destiny in a stop before the immigration to Brazil. While his grandmother tries to find the spirit of her father in the magical streets of Buenos Aires he tries to capture the emotion inside her shining blue eyes.
