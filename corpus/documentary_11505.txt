Hsu Su-Chen's death from cancer came at the peak of her artistic career. In her short creative life of 14 years she engaged in cross-disciplinary art such as the multi-layered "Self-Portrait" and collaborated with people in numerous fields as in "Plant-Paradise". This film assembles interviews with Hsu and images of her worldwide exhibitions with footage shot by Hsu herself to tell the extraordinary journey and unique visions of this distinctive artist.
