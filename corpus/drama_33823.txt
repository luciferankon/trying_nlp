Khalil is a mid level hustler who is well on his way to the top but all he wants to do is tie up all loose ends and put the streets in his rear view mirror forever....BUT THAT ALL COMES WITH A PRICE. With the FEDS holding a secret over his head unresolved beef in the streets and a drug connect who refuses to lose his #1 earner Khalil soon learns that it's never easy getting BEYOND THE G.
