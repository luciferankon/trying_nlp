Rage is the story of star-crossed lovers set against the turbulent 1981 Springbok Tour of New Zealand - a time when a small rugby-playing nation made a stand against the cruel and brutal South African Apartheid regime. Carol a beautiful young Maori Police goes undercover and falls in love with Des a charismatic leader of the student protest movement. To save his life she has to reveal her true identity knowing it will destroy their relationship. Their hearts are broken as the nation itself is ripped down the middle over the most noble of ideals - that all human beings are created equal.
