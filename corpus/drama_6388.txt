Fifteen years ago Martin Montana lost his wife. Now he comes across a woman who is identical to Agustina just as she looked like when she died. His obsession for Tania the mysterious woman leads him to a sinister character Mr. Moretti. What Martin ignores is that Moretti is in fact Dr. Aron Mattsson his old professor and rival who came back from the shadows to consummate the perfect revenge against him. Tania on the other hand doesn't know that is that she is in fact Martin's wife and that Moretti turned her. Moretti will learn that love can be an unstoppable force impossible to control.
