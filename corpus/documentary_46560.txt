Documentary follows Jerry Hughes of Hughes Foundation who is HIV Positive and his work in India with an orphanage of HIV/AIDS infected children without parents who have died of the same disease. His work throughout the region combats religious persecution from "White Jesus" preachers who tell those infected they are cursed by God. Fighting the battle daily in Nagpur while also advocating for education love medicine/vaccinations and true compassion with a team of three Indian mates whom he's worked with now for over ten years.
