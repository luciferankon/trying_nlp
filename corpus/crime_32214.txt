An average kid named Street (Cesar Cipher Gabriel) gets the chance of a lifetime when Khalil (Ricardo PRO Rodriguez) an infamous business owner offers him a job that would change his life. The job of the first male assassin in an all female agency. As he becomes the best he makes enemies within the agency but is given his first female target with special instructions. When he meets Deja (Amarie Lee)...he discovers that nothing is as it seems.
