The Nature of Inspiration features intimate interviews with some of Canada's best visual artists who discuss their work and its relationship to the environment. Featuring photographer Edward Burtynsky mixed media artists Marlene Creates Peter von Tiesenhausen sculptor Dempsey Bob and others. The Nature of Inspiration travels from coast to coast documenting not only Canada's best artists but some of its most breathtaking locations. Shot over a period of six years the program is an unprecedented glimpse into the minds of the country's most important artists.
