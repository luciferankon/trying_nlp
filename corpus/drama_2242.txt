Datuk Mustaza's boat gets wrecked while he is out fishing at the South China Sea. Saved by a local fisherman named Sulaiman Datuk Mustaza feels indebted to him and wishes to have their children marry each other. However his son Taufik refuses the idea as he already has a girlfriend named Isabella. Similarly Sulaiman's daughter Salina also rejects the idea as she too has the love of her life Zulkifli. However at the end of the day both of them have no choice but to abide to the wishes of their parents.
