A New York Artist accidentally summons the Ghost of Johnny Cash who helps her find the father she has never met... Carly North is a NYC painter whose love affair with a wanna-be Naked Singing Cowboy and creative life are at a complete standstill. After a visit from the Ghost of Johnny Cash she is propelled on a surreal trip throughout the wilds of Upstate New York to find the father she has never met; and some answers to a few life altering questions. THAT'S WHAT SHE TOLD ME explores the ideas of fantasy infringing on reality and the struggle to decipher what is real and what isn't. Using music an American icon and the lure of the road; this story speaks to anyone who has ever wondered where they really come from and whether we like it or not: the indelible mark of family on our lives.
