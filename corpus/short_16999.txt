Niobrara Jewel of the North proves rich in historical content accessible to students of all ages. This complex river system is explored through a prism of Natural Sciences: geology and geography hydrology biology paleontology and anthropology. It offers History from scientific findings as well as the human record. A number of interviews were taken in the course of filming experts from many persuasions academic and practical.sharing stories and sentiments that will undoubtedly be of interest to historians of the future.
