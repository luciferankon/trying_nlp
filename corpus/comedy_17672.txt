Follow the crazy adventures of the PA-pabol waiter Mario nurse Joseph and the messenger Jess as their friendship keep them together through the tough times in their lives. Will the playboy and homophobic Mario be shocked to find out his father's true identity? Can Joseph handle being a good brother to his five sexy younger sisters? For how long will Jess put up with domineering wife? What long-kept secret will test the brotherly bond of the three?
