Paulo Roberto and Renata are engaged. The pan starts to boil when Paulo Roberto falls in love with Renata's gay brother Roger. Paulo Roberto then faces new challenges since he comes from an aristocratic family. He finally comes out to everyone: his fiancee his mother his whole family. The plot is unique because it deals with a current subject: Gay Marriage in a powerful way and avoiding cliches. This work is dedicated to Rainer Werner Fassbinder.
