As the best selling game of all time Call of Duty: Modern Warfare 3 has succeeded in connecting players in a historic fashion. With its release Activision introduced ELITE a yearlong subscription service giving subscribers discounted access to Maps Contests and Content early and for no additional charge. Friday Night Fights is an Original reality television game show in which 2 teams of 4 go head to head in a best of 3 game competition in the Call of Duty Arena. Winners receive a championship belt and a donation to the Call of Duty Endowment in their name.
