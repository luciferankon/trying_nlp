From the rough streets of Houston to the outskirts of the suburbans Big 'M an 18 year old College student is an avid drug dealer looking for a come up. Faced by tragedy for the majority of his life growing up such as losing his parents to car crash in 2006 he has been left to survive in this cold world all on his own. As a student he is struggling to pay off his rent due to the lack of business in his illicit activities. The streets have him pushing boundaries until he comes across a web drug trafficking empire built within the depths of the unknown side of the internet. Though it all might seem like a dream at first that dream turns into a nightmare that turns his world upside down which reveals the truth about how dark the deep web can become. (2017)
