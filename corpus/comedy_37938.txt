Following a documentary crew filming the effects of the lockout laws on small venues we at first see just another Oxford street pub owned by two men not realizing that the two men are in fact vampires. The vampires whose names are Drake and Francois Dahlvinyer Von Vundakint have moved to Sydney to kill Hugh Jackman for his 2004 movie portrayal of Van Helsing. The series is focused upon the service industry and the comedy of running a business with a spotlight on the night life of Oxford Street Sydney Australia.
