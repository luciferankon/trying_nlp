Shiva ('Ganesh Venkataraman') is an IT graduate who is in love with Archana ('Kalpana Pandit' (qv)) a medical student. Shiva lands up with a big job in USA. However Archana's father throws a challenge before Shiva leaves to USA. He agrees to get her daughter married to him on one condition that he should not call up or try to meet his daughter for the next one year. After a couple years one can see a total change in Shiva. He romances his colleague Maya ('Shibana Shankar') and forgets Archana. But one evening he is suddenly drawn to his past. Thoughts about Archana come up. He rushes to the doctor only to find out that a truth about him which he didn't know so far.
