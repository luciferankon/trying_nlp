More than 200 000 North Korean refugees are living illegally in China in Yanbian area on the border with North Korea. They are hiding from the Chinese police waiting for their departure to Seoul. If they get caught they will be returned to North Korea where death camps and firing squad are waiting for them. Today Mr Park take a great risk for his life to help these refugees escape from China to South Korea. Every mission is like a payback for his own debt the price of his freedom.
