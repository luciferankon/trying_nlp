Iqbal and Sille are best friends and have always lived next door to each other. Their street must undergo urban regeneration and Easelman and The Swine will unluckily be in charge of the project. When Iqbal discovers a chip with magic energy the kids manage to convince Easelman and The Swine to buy the chip so they can reclaim their building. But the villains deceive the children and now Iqbal Sille and the grown-ups in Blĺgĺrdsgade must put in every effort to retrieve the chip and expose the villains' secret plan.
