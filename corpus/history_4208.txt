In an epic story of star-crossed lovers that span many lifetimes and many time periods four people are caught in an endless cycle of intertwined fates. Kang Jung Hwa (Jang Shin Young) is an up-and-coming stage actress who bumps into Dr. Min Ki Beom (Ryu Soo Young) a brilliant neurosurgeon on the subway and is strangely drawn to him. Ki Beom happens to be the brother of Jung Hwa's boyfriend Min Ki Soo (Lee Jong Soo) a fellow actor.
