Goulash once considered a mild mannered Hungarian stew gets massively redefined as it gets a comedic make over with a common man's slant. Fueled by veteran comedian Brandon Gibson "Goulash" gets its new identity by taking questions from its viewers to the streets of America and mining for comedic gold. The search for answers won't take long as Brandon encounters some of the most eclectic unique and hilarious people who are more than happy to help him sum up the questions we all want answered most. Unpredictable television.
