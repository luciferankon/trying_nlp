At the age of 16 the Jason a high school dropout moves in with an adopted family and falls in love with his stepsister. When her older brother discovers their romance he is completely disgusted and ashamed. One chilly November day the three take a motorboat out for a cruise. Jason makes the mistake of being too affectionate with his stepsister in front of the stepbrother who in a fit of anger throws Jason overboard. Hypothermia and panic help fill Jason's lungs with freezing water before the siblings can return to his aid to stop him from drowning. His body is never found. The stepsister never turns her brother in and they report it as an accident. Five years later the stepsister is still in love with Jason and finds herself in a fantasy The Vision of a Ghost where she can attempt to reunite with him once again.
