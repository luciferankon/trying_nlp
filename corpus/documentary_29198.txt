In the past 25 years the town of Paphos has gone from a rather sleepy port on the west coast of Cyprus to a popular destination for tourists and expatriates home to beach hotels and - especially in the past decade - thousands of new villas for Britons and other Europeans. Development in Paphos and its surrounding villages has been intense and often uncontrolled. Many locals struggle to reconcile the new Paphos with the place they once knew - but as they say development "gives people jobs". And meanwhile the newcomers have their own communities...
