Outis Suite. Suite because is a sequence of the most significant adventures of the Homeric hero. Outis Nobody is the name used by Odysseus to fool Polyphemus. On a background of the landscapes of Apulia the film highlights the most fascinating stories of the Laertiades taking in the most emotional aspects as Joyce taught us to read in the Homeric poem. Odysseus here does a journey in the forms of art culture and entertainment creating a relationship between Homeric episodes and Outis ones.
