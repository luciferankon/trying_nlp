UNION SQUARE is a powerful look into the world of homeless heroin addicts that live in UNION SQUARE PARK New York City. Peer into the lives if seven individuals that allowed cameras to follow and capture their trials and tribulations while living and hustling on the streets of New York City UNION SQUARE captures the true and painful essence of drug addiction and seven 20 something year-olds will do to maintain a habit that has trapped them in a vicious cycle. It reveals what they may have all lost and what they may possibly gain while being in the grips on one of the most powerfully addictive drug - HEROIN. Stealing shelters hustling detox and rehab - all is revealed in this in-depth documentary by Stephen J. Szklarski
