Coming Home follows the stories of 6 people how they came to need affordable housing their journey through the process of getting it and how the experience impacted their lives. The concept of the film originated with the Director's quest for understanding of thecomplex topic of affordable housing in our society today. His good friend and fellow musician being the Executive Director of the Society of Hope was the perfect match to co-produce the film. The Society of Hope is a non-profit housing society that provides affordable housing for single parent families and seniors.
