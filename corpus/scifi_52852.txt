Three time fragments ago Captain Zerks Ganymedewski and his ship the Venturi 553 disappeared without a trace. What happened to Zerks and his ship? No one knew until the recent discovery of the Venturi's log capsule - its "black box". As the League of Outstanding Planets begins reviewing the evidence from the log the truth will be revealed. Captain Fantastic? Well Zerks certainly thinks so! However the reality is perhaps just a bit different.
