Kallirroy is a sixteen-year-old girl living in an isolated village around fifties. The young girl is forced by the rules of the closed society not to reveal her secret: she is being regularly raped by her stepfather. Being trapped Kallirroy has accepted her destiny and goes through all these without complaining. She looses her trust in people and she finds shelter in the river near her house. The only person that delights her life is her little sister whom she is trying to protect from her stepfather. For Kallirroy it doesn't matter if this girl is in real danger or not....she only wants to keep this man away.
