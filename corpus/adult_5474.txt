Apocalypse Babylon was first chapter of Charley Crow's intended "Greedlove Trilogy" (the second chapter is Black Angel Falls). Mobster Alejandro Biscotti has a mole in his operation but the mole is the least of his worries as his true adversary is wealthy mind-control researcher Arthur Greedlove. Greedlove has a secret weapon: the sexy zombie assassin Babylon.
