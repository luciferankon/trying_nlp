Thirty two year old married with children Frank is sitting at a bar trying to engage whom he thinks is his long-lost uncle into a conversation about his father the bartender's long deceased brother when strange things are said to him by a male customer in another reality a priest then by a bizarre female customer Mona in another reality a nurse. The "Barman" in fact an ER doctor tries to explain some "cosmic" things to Frank until Frank eventually realizes that his life is slipping by and he is not where he thought he was in this new and original surreal drama which is primed to evolve into a NYC emergency room farce.
