TV Miniseries which recreates the January 9 1964 riots of Panamanian students in the Panama Canal Zone. The riot started after a Panamanian flag was torn during conflict between Panamanian students and Canal Zone Police officers over the right of the Panamanian flag to be flown alongside the U.S. flag. U.S. Army units became involved in suppressing the violence after Canal Zone police were overwhelmed and after three days of fighting about 21 Panamanians and four U.S. soldiers were killed.
