Who's the funniest woman ever to appear on television? From Lucille Ball's physical comedy on I Love Lucy to Tina's Fey's witty dialogue on 30 Rock TV Guide Network is counting down the most comedic ladies who have graced the small screen. Hosted by Will & Grace's Megan Mullally Funniest Women on TV will feature interviews with Betty White and Doris Roberts who made the list of 25. Also making the cut: sitcom stars Julia Louis-Dreyfus Fran Drescher and Patricia Heaton talk show hosts Ellen DeGeneres and Whoopi Goldberg former Friends Jennifer Aniston and Courteney Cox as well as Kathy Griffin and Amy Poehler.
