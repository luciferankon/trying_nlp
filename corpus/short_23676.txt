Sarah Eric and Robert are siblings who run a coffee shop together. Their new employee Amy accuses Robert of sexually assaulting her. Everyone blames Robert without even trying to defuse the situation including his siblings. Robert's world turns into chaos and he does not know how to fix the situation. He seeks a psychic psychiatrist for help. The psychiatrist tells him about an ancient Chinese spell which he can perform by using his own blood. Through the magic he realizes how his siblings try to protect him without letting him worry.
