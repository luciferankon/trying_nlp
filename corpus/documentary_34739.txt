Narrated by actor Benjamin Onyango and told entirely through the voices of Tanzanian-trained scientists and researchers the documentary tells how they are leading the charge for self-determination in regards to conservation and environmental issues based on the cultural and economic needs of the people. They explain in their own words how they are developing solutions to ensure the vitality of the Serengeti and all protected areas will continue for future generations while at the same time improving the socio-economic prospects of their people.
