In this Oscar-winning documentary legendary oceanographer 'Jacques-Yves Cousteau' (qv) explores Continental Shelf Station Two an early-1960s attempt at underwater living located 10 meters below the surface of the Red Sea off the coast of Sudan. Subjected to constant observation and medical experimentation the six men taking part in the experiment have air food water electricity and other life essentials as well as a small two-man submarine that they can use for underwater exploration.
