Saint Gabriel is a city known for its Crime corrupt law enforcement and politicians but most of all for the Occult. Saint Gabriel has a long history of the unexplained but when two private investigators are put onto a case they discover themselves caught in the middle of the Mafia Neo-Nazis and necromancy. Kane and Lawless quickly discover the people closest to them have been hiding secrets but more importantly they discover secrets of their own pasts that can unleash hell on earth while breathing life back into one of the greatest psychopaths in the history of the world.
