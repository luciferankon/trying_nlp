While the film looks at the last 30-year history of Turkey independently on the other hand it also discusses the problems of theatre art the artistic backdrop of new styles and new stage structure. The film puts a numerous theatre groups under the scope tracing their footprints as new theatre groups and companies emerged following up the 1982 Turkish Military Coup D'etat and looking for today to see the transformation of the contemporary generation. The history of this artistic movement is revealed by the witnesses of both sides; the main stream and the alternative wing.
