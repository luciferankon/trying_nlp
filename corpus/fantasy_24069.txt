When a young girl named Gemma Black (Jahannah James) returns to live at her family home after losing her boyfriend and job she is horrified to discover that her mother (Fay Ripley) has become a practicing witch. As Gemma struggles to get her life back on track in a neighbourhood infused with black magic it isn't long before she realises that she may too possess the power to manipulate the dark forces for her own benefit. will Gemma follow her mother and brother into the dark or will she decide to fight back and bring sanity back to her family and neighbourhood?
