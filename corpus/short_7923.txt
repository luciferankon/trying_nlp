Seven Seven Spring/Summer 2016 Campaign and fashion film "In The Deep" shot in Palomino Colombia. Nominations for Fashion Film "In The Deep" include Best Cinematography at the 2016 Mercedes-Benz Bokeh South Africa Fashion Film Festival and Best Cinematography at the 2016 Miami Fashion Film Festival. Official Selections include the Berlin Fashion Film Festival 2016 (Colombian Selection of the Medellin Fashion Film Festival) Canada International Fashion Film Festival 2016 and Fashion Film Festival Chicago 2016.
