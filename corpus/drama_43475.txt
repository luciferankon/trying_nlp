Szánthó Tamás is conducting a concert while his young wife and her secret lover play in the same theatre in the Tragedy of Man. On Christmas Eve the wife Ágnes announces that she is going to leave her husband and their daughter Buksi and will live with Miklós. After the divorce Ágnes and Miklós get married in France and go to Hollywood. However only Ágnes is successful so they move back to Hungary. At home Ágnes is told by a hostile governess that her husband died By now Buksi has grown to Judit and falls in love with her attractive stepfather. Ágnes steps aside and later dies in an accident.
