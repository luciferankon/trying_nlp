Filmmaker Ann Cabano follows and highlights the life and work of humanitarian and Man of the Year Doug Stewart as he dedicates his life to the only night time outreach in the country serving the homeless population 365 days a year for 11 years. Doug Stewart Man of the Year and recognized humanitarian overcomes addiction fights a brain tumor and is faced with the difficult choice of closing the doors on his non-profit sending local government officials in Martinez County California into a declared state of emergency.
