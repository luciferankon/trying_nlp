BJJ NO-MADs fourth full length documentary is about the adventures Mihail Kunchev and Dimitrios Tsitos had while training traveling and having fun in the United Arab Emirates. It features visit at the original ADCC venue interview with the biggest local star Faisal Al Ketbi international superstar Alexander Trans student of Rickson and Machados Roberto Atalla and the incredible life stories of a BJJ cop and a man who almost lost his life because of a BJJ rivalry.
