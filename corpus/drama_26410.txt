What happens when you throw a fish into a shark tank? Ming Xiaoxi ('Pets Tseng' (qv)) is admitted to the elite Kuan Yu University because of her academic achievements and many Kung Fu championship titles. But her humble family background unsophisticated demeanor and awkward appearance put her at direct odds with the polished privileged students who attend the university. When Xiaoxi tries to help someone in need from her hometown she becomes embroiled in the lives of Mu Liubing ('Sam Lin (V)' (qv)) Feng Jianche ('Evan Ma' (qv)) and Dong Haonan ('Kaicheng Xu' (qv)) the big shots on campus.
