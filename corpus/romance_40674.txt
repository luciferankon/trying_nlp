The movie is a continuation of the 1998 drama series "Cinderella" starring Ahmad Idham and Haliza Misbun as Salman and Nurul following the events after the series as the two gets married. In the new movie Ahmad Idham returns in the lead role starring alongside other actors like Eizlan Yusof Atikah Suhaime Faye Kusairi and Cat Farish.
