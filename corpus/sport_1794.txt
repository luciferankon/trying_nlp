Complete coverage of the 2010 Golden Gloves National Tournament of Champions. Golden Gloves has been the premier brand for amateur boxing in the United States since their first tournament in 1923. Since then the Golden Gloves program has led the way in providing opportunities for young boxers to develop personal and athletic skills in and out of the ring. Former Golden Gloves Champions include Joe Louis Sonny Liston Muhammad Ali Joe Frazier George Foreman Sugar Ray Leonard Mike Tyson Evander Holyfield Floyd Mayweather Jr and Oscar De La Hoya.
