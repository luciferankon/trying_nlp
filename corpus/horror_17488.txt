Three months has passed since wife Kera Moore has lost her husband to an violent abduction. The sins of her dark past as a assassin has came to collect more than blood. She discovers that betrayal is a sweeter dish than the evil the Jackals will unleash on her. Kera takes on the assistance of county homicide detective Rose Jones small time gangster Pino Jimenez Soto and the heroics of an chemist from The Freedom Fighters Andy Miller on an all out blood bath of vengeance against The Jackals. How do you kill something that wants to die and can't?
