When Alf reveals to his best friend Jim that his long-term relationship had ended it sparks up an animated discussion about a mutual friend of theirs Will a reputed Lothario perpetually living up the bachelor lifestyle. According to Alf Will is a changed man and going steady but Jim begs to defer convinced that as soon as he learns Alf is single again he will use it to his advantage and try to usher his best friend into his arena of depravity. Both adamant they are right the friends agree on a wager to see if Will would either be tempted by the lure of a beautiful woman or remain faithful to his girlfriend. They decide to set him up with an attractive escort to test his loyalty and then stand back and see which way the pendulum swings.
