Mrs Anatole a superb elephant has just given birth to her son Bob. Reckless and greedy he follows a banana tied to a string. He is caught in the trap and is recruited in a group of trained animals. Mrs Anatole is desperate: "I lost my child!". Auguste the monkey promises her he will find her darling. He travels through the desert and fights a lion a snake and a tiger. Helped by the giraffe he finally finds Bob and brings him back to his mother.
