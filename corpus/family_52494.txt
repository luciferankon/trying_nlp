This interactive point-and-click computer application allowed guests to explore the Walt Disney World Resort circa 1996 (1998 in the Second Edition). Users could take themed tours of the resort play mini-games such as the "Quiz Challenge" view a growing timeline of the resort's history from its opening in 1971 find "Hidden Mickeys" (silhouettes of Mickey Mouse) in images or explore the resort on their own. Nearly every ride hotel water park restaurant shopping option in-park entertainment and backstage facility that could be found at the resort during the time was detailed in the program.
