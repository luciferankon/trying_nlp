Inspired by the classic Twilight Zone t.v. series "Requiem" takes a modern approach to the classic morality tale that the Twilight Zone excelled at. Demented Mind-Altering Scary and Empowering "Requiem" follows a young girl as she tries to make sense of her life and the struggles which some fail to conquer. During her journey she will encounter guides she never knew places yet to be found and lives she will never meet because in the end..."The Story Is Yours To Tell".
