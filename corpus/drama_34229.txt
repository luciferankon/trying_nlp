Agnčs Bonnardet leaves her parents to marry Claude Sironi a painter who becomes famous but loses his talent. Meanwhile Agnes acquires a style of her own as an artist which makes Claude jealous of his young wife. One day he sends one of his own paintings to the Bazar de la Charité a very trendy Paris department store instead of one of his wife's works as ordered. Afraid of her being mad at him he locks her up in the cloak room. A dreadful fire suddenly breaks out and sets the building ablaze.
