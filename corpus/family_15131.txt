At The Poocharelli's follows the daily lives and misadventures of the Poocharelli family of talking dogs. Meet the family - mom Maggie Poocharelli is a poodle who is the boss of the household; Pete that father is a hard-working sensible chocolate lab; Brittany is a sociable teenage Yorkshire terrier; eleven year-old Cody is a trouble-making Jack Russell terrier; five West Highland terrier puppies; Gramps the mixed terrier who is always asleep; and Maggie's cynical sister Evelyne a bulldog.
