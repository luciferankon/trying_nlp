In this special one-hour feature we cover train travel and train-spotting great trains of the world and the future of locomotion. We also look at what people do with trains: they eat on them they sightsee they stowaway they campaign they sleep they murder they highjack and on occasion they sometimes even travel from place to place! We look at how rail opened up the continent particularly the Wild West and mysterios India. A globally appropriate programme filled with interesting trivia and facts The Magical World of Trains will engage all audiences and take them on an entertaining ride!
