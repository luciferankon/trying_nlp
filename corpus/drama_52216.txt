Is there a reason we fall in love with the people we do? 'Passion Love Beauty' is a confronting exploration of the worlds of love sex attraction and all the elements which combine to shape us into the people we are and the people we are compelled to be with. As Garnet Mae explores the nature of his existence and re-examines the paths he has walked we begin to piece together all the weird and wonderful things about life that both drive and defeat us but ultimately make it worth living.
