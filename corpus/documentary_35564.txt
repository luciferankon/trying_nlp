The Giffoni International Film Festival is one of the most well-known children's film festivals in the world. The Giffoni Film Festival which takes place in Giffoni Valle Piana a little village in the surroundings of Salerno started in 1971 when Claudio Gubitosi had the idea of promoting and developing cinema for young people. In this documentary a Hungarian Television's crew interviewed organizers and guests of the festival in 1990. Interviewees include Claudio Gubitosi Tony Esposito Ali Reza Shoja Noori Torgny Anderberg Jeremy Irons Liv Ullmann Bud Spencer Samuel Irons Ferial Behzad Giuliano Gemma and Gezim Quendro.
