A teenager brought up from a low income family finds himself robbing banks to support his mother who has breast cancer and cannot afford treatment considered one of the best he is recruited by the CIA to find a murdering bank robber that has been on the loose for close to 17 years. Being promised money for his mother's treatment in lieu to join he finds himself prisoner just to be taken off the streets with the CIA believing he is the culprit young Aaron must now gain the trust of one CIA agent and find the real murdering bank robber.
