Portrayed during the 2001 Argentinean economic crisis the movie centers on a family that is affected by the political and economical changes of the time. The film works with two perspectives an objective one which narrates two different trips and a subjective one from the point of view of the youngest daughter: Camila. She finds a way to capture the true essence of her family through her handy cam she achieves to break with the everyday routine and take a deeper look inside the relationship between the members of her family and discover deep wounds.
