'Masvingo Glam' is a TV magazine show that showcases the Glitz and Glamour of Masvingo City of Zimbabwe. Candice Masimba presents the opening ceremony of the Masvingo Music Awards live from the red carpet on the season 2 premiere of the show . Masvingo Music Awards 2015 were hosted by Lucky Kunene (Charles Munganasa) B Kay Hunter Miss Tamara Florenco and Miss Pathi at The Charles Austin Theatre in Masvingo Zimbabwe under glitz and glamor.The 2015 awards show was promoted by P & P Global Marketing.
