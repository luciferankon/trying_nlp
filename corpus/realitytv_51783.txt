The Trend is a weekly style series from A-List Content where celebrities and designers discuss emerging fashion trends. From the Cannes Film Festival to The Oscars to New York Fashion Week Coachella and Art Basel in Miami - The Trend covers the world's most fashionable events talking to A-listers about hot new trends. Celebrity journalist Victoria Recano serves as the series host. Canaan Rubin contributes from locations including New York and Europe.
