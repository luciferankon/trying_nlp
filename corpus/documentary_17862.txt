Lost super fan Iain Lee attempts to live out the ultimate dream of any Lost fan- the opportunity to be an extra on the best show on television. Enlisting the help of the cast before approaching the executive producer Iain will do his best to make his dream come true. Full of hilarious moments with the cast and crew this is the perfect show for anyone who has ever dreamed of appearing on Lost. Can Iain really live out his dream or will they remain unfulfilled?
