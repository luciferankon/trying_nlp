Tímamót or Changes in English. An upbeat heartwarming story about Gudjon Sigurbjorn and Steinthor who lived together for decades along with several other inhabitants in the Tjaldanes Institution in a peaceful valley close to Reykjavik. When a decision is made to close down the institution their life takes an unexpected turn and they discover a new side to life and to themselves. Gudjon Sigurbjorn and Steinthor lived and worked together for decades along with several other inhabitants in the Tjaldanes Institution in a peaceful valley close to Reykjavik. When a decision is made to close down the institution they get their own apartment spending money and have to start taking care of themselves for the first time in their lives. A hopeful and humane story about how society's prejudice has changed for the better helping mentally disabled people take control of their own lives.
