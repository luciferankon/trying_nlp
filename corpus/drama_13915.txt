Yu-Tieh's mother drowned while trying to save her. That day Yu-Tieh who was known as a genius child prodigy lost her mother her memory and even forgot how to swim; Hai-Fei is the CEO of a multi-billion dollar corporation. He hides his identity so that he can find out more about the mystery behind Yu-Tieh's lost memory. But somehow people mistook him as a professional part-time worker who seems to be able to do anything; "Sea Horse" Men's Swim Team-the laughing stock of other schools-always seem out of luck and always end up last in swim meets. In the summer of 2016 forces above granted them 2 months to change their fate. Will they be able to overcome severe challenges bail themselves out of their own predicaments and break the waves?
