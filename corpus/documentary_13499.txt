A documentary about the game of football and those who's quality of life has been drastically effected by playing the game. The documentary will seek out former players from the National Football League (some hall of fame) as well as players from all levels of the game and articulate on screen the rise and fall of the human gladiator who was once and still is mesmerized by making it to the big show ( the N.F.L) From suicides depression dementia mind splitting headaches (24/7) domestic violence and suicidal tendencies from former players you will see a darker almost invisible until now side of the game that has taken and changed real lives at all levels forever.
