'Doctor By Heart' is a fictional adaptation of the life of Dr. Romesh Japra a well-known cardiologist in the San Francisco Bay Area and the Founder and Convener of Festival of Globe (FOG) which started as the annual Festival of India 'mela' and Parade 25 years ago. From humble beginnings in Phagwara Punjab India he rose to incredible success as a doctor and community leader in the US. His disciplined mother - a schoolteacher - instilled dedication to academic success and a strong work ethic in him and his siblings. This is an epic journey with people from different backgrounds and cultures.
