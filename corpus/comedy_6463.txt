Due to accident Johnny/Juan (Vic Sotto) is forced by his adoptive parents to leave their province. He moves to Manila together with his younger brother Utoy (Paolo Contis). Johnny is hired to replace Boogey (Rez Cortez) as the driver and bodyguard of Jessica (Christine Jacob). Rage and envy turned Boogey to Mon (Ramon Christopher) and started working against Jessica's family. Johnny must then protect Jessica.
