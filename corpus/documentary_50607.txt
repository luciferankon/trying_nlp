Tower of Song a remarkable story set to an extraordinary soundtrack spotlights the 30 artists currently in the Canadian Music Hall of Fame. Shot on location across Canada Tower of Song places the artists and their music into the context of their homeland as the camera catches the changing panorama of Canada's vast landscape. Canada's very culture and history has been shaped by this same vastness and solitude - the inspiration for the lyrical and musical portraits created and voiced by the country's greatest singers songwriters and musicians.
