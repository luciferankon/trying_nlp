In the Beginning contains two 25-minute episodes. Part 1: "What is the Bible?" How is put together? Kids learn alongside Buck Denver Sunday School Lady Brother Louie and the crew that the Bible tells the story of God and what He's done for us. Part 2: "Who wrote the Bible?" Next Buck and friends learn how God inspired people to write the Bible. They also learn how the first part of Genesis sets the stage for the rest of the Bible - and all of history.
