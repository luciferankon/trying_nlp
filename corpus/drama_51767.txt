This _Out of Africa (1985)_ (qv) style three-part movie deals with a courageous woman who moves to Africa to flee her past. Berlin 1914: When Katharina von Strahlberg discovers that her husband Richard had an affair with her sister-in-law Martha she leaves him after stealing some important plans and photos of East Africa and travels to Dar es Salaam with one of Richard's business partners. In the German colony she learns that nobody wants to employ her because of her husband's power but she draws new courage from meeting Franz Lukas a doctor and Victor March a lively Scotsman...
