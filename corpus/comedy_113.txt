The frustrated Mohan Seeks a eligible groom for his interfering sasu. The sasu that he has inherited in his dowry.the unwanted dowry that takes his life through various levels of hell. So he arranges a swayamwar and story starts happening. To palm of his sasu he finds three bachelors.This story is all about how the sasu gets married and lead to an unexpected ending. Mohan gets married to Nandini but in dowry he inherits his mother in law Lalitabai this unwanted dowry makes his marriage life miserable Mohan and Nandini are unable to get space in the house frustrated with this he arranges her Swayamwar (self selection) and finds three bachelor's who could marry her.
