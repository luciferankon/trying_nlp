A powerful Documentary film that speaks about the rising epidemic of fatherless homes. This is a film about the lack of fathers in the home in African American households how slavery helped contribute to this being passed down through generations single mothers trying to raise young men alone the positive & negative effects of the absent parent the importance of fathers being in the household to be there for their children showing them a positive example of what a man should do & how a man should take care of his home business & how to treat a woman for both his daughters & sons to see.
