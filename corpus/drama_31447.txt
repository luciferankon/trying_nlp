Omar an unstable and lonely teenager lives in a slum in the suburbs of Mostaganem. He is addicted to a famous psychotropic nicknamed Ť Madame Courage ť : Artane tablets very popular among young Algerians for their euphoric effect of invincibility. Omar is a specialist in snatching. That morning he goes downtown to commit his usual crimes. His first prey is a young girl called Selma walking with her girl friends prominently wearing a gold necklace. As he commits his larceny their eyes meet.
