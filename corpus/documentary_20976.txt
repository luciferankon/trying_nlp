The compelling story of Pat Cash's road to victory in the 1987 Wimbledon Men's Single Final. Follows Pat through his recovery from a career-threatening back injury personal drama and incredible determination to defeat Ivan Lendl for the singles crown. Features interviews with Cash doubles aces Paul McNamee and Peter McNamara Cash's closest friends and other tennis luminaries. Includes footage from the major tournaments Cash played in the lead up to All England Tennis Club Men's Single Final.
