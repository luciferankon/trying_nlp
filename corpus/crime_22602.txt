Live PD: Police Patrol is a non-fiction weekend special series that brings viewers an unfiltered look at law enforcement officers in action across America. Each episode highlights the daily life-and-death situations faced by Sheriff's Departments Highway Patrols and local Police Departments in a diverse range of jurisdictions from Connecticut to Arizona. From drugs busts to prostitution high speed pursuits to domestic disputes car theft to gang violence or just minor traffic violations Live PD: Police Patrol offers a transparent look at the daily encounters between police and civilians.
