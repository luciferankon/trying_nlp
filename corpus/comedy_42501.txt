After losing her mom her marriage her dog and her leg 60 year old Imbi Pilt a once famous singer actress and comedian from Australia realizes the only thing left to lose is her mind so she moves to Hollywood to pursue her dreams of stardom. She soon discovers that the loony town of L.A. isn't much different than the animal farm she grew up on in Oz and although her journey proves to be more difficult than expected her out-of-the-box mentality not only separates her from crowd but it also surprisingly enriches the lives of those whom she meets.
