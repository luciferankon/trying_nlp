The documentary features the meeting of Komang Arnawa (the Earth) a famous international body building champion from Bali Indonesia and Cindera Che (the Wind) a professional dancer and choreographer from the USA. They meet in Los Angeles California to create a unique performance-art piece combining Komang's spiritual approach to body building and Cindera's inspiring technique of teaching dance. Their training and discussions become a fascinating journey of two people from two different spheres as each takes the role of both teacher and student. Cindera shares her principles of dance and expression to Komang and Komang imparts the principles of his Balinese spirituality to Cindera. As they combine their strengths to form a beautiful spiritual visual in the form of dance they each learn how life can achieve balance when the solid strength of the Earth meets the moving power of the Wind...
