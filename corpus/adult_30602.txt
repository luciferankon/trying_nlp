When a young woman sees someone entering her vacationing friend's house she convinces her friend that they should investigate before calling the police. Unfortunately the burglar gets the drop on the pair and soon the shapely young women are tightly tied and even more stringently gagged. In the second story a young woman arrives home to see her bedroom in a shambles and her two friends bound and gagged. Before she can release her captive friends the intruder grabs her and leaves her to struggle mutely in another room.
