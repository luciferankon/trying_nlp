In the Van Doorslaer family we are showman from father to son there is no choice in the Blood! This is the story of Kevin he's 18 and he spent his childhood at the fair he wants to be an actor the other showmen tell him he cannot leave the show and her family but Kevin is crazy about cinema and wants to try the American adventure. One day he went against all odds he joined the famous Lee Strasberg Film Institute in New York. Does he become an actor or he will return to the fair with his family?
