Pierre Pierre is proud obnoxious and crass but also deeply misunderstood by everyone except his sweet and loving girlfriend. She knows that he has just become lost in his "Pierre-ness" and that underneath his vulgar facade is a man with a heart determined to make a statement to the world by stealing the Mona Lisa sell it for millions and in the process bring down Western civilization and have enough money to move out of his parent's house. With the police hot on their trail we follow Pierre Pierre and his girlfriend across Europe through chaos and hilarity as they try to find a willing buyer for the Mona Lisa.
