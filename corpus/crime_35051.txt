Anurata a college professor Vidya a journalist Shabana a housewife and Sakhi a college student. When Sakhi slaps a guy who passes lewd comments at her the guy vows to take revenge. He kidnaps Sakhi along with his friends and she is gang raped by them. Further the guy throws acid on her face and all of them run away. Anurata happens to be in the vicinity and she takes Sakhi to the hospital. From then on the movie delves into the trauma faced by Sakhi and points out how even the victim's parents refuse to take a stand and accept defeat. Being a victim of sexual harassment herself Anurata decided to help Sakhi. Helping her is Vidya who is also a victim of molestation at her workplace. But it is Shabana the wife of one of the four culprits who helps the police a lot by revealing the hiding place of her husband.
