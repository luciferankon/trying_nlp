The Geojedo P.O.W camp (prisoner of war camp) was established in 1950 when the Korea war end. All prisoners were Koreans however they were devided by two ideologies - communism and capitalism and came into conflict each other. In 1952 communist prisoners kidnapped General Dodd the head of camp to negotiate their return to north Korea. The Korea-US Combined Forces Command decided to infiltrate special agent Hoseon Kim to rescue general and put down riot. 'STUDIO MACRO GRAPH' produced the incident of abduction that took place at Geojedo P.O.W Camp in 1952 as First Person View (FPV) interactive VR cinematic contents through cinematic direction and the element of interaction with the audience.
