2016: Violence is at an all-time high throughout the city of Richmond. Rival gangs and the local police compete for control of the city massive growing empire of illegal drugs entertainment and sports. The Savwahfair Gentlemen Club though located in the center of this violent city carries a distinguished list of members from fast-moving entrepreneurs and corporate gurus doctors to attorneys judges and of course the ever so courteous crooked chief of police Minx Allison who all have a steak in who gets crowned the new king of the city after the death of it's silent but powerful ruler Jeffery Hollywood Benick.
