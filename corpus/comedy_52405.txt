Set 1 Year After The Events of "Into The Fire" A Feud breaks out amongst Sam's group of Friends. To settle the score Sam initially starts a Prank War to reconnect with his friends. But then an old frenemy of Sam's past comes back into his life to destroy him. This Frenemy makes Sam and His Friends Turn against each other and turns the thing that was supposed to heal their friendship into something gone horribly wrong.
