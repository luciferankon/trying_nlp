Max Russo is an aspiring filmmaker living in Hollywood...Florida. After spending all his money on a film school taught by Steven Seagal he now finds himself living in his sister's garage and working a dead end job selling towels at a gym. Quickly losing faith he soon discovers everyone's fascination with online videos. Rejuvenated Max enlists the help of his best friend Dodge an escort driver who lives with his grandmother and they set out to create the greatest web series ever made. However with no money they end up with a band of misfits. Together they have no shot but they think they do. And that little bit of hope might be just the thing that could bring them success.
