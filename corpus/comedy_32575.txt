On his 30th birthday Carlotta an organizer of weddings discovers that her boyfriend is having an affair. Depressed she doesn't realize that she received magical powers as a gift from his father who she believed was dead. She discovered the possession only when she met the charming Ricardo Valenti the manager that the mother has hired to save the business from bankruptcy.The two become a couple but the sudden discovery of Carlotta's powers pushes Ricardo to marry his former girlfriend. It's up to Emma and Vlad Carlotta's magical parents stop it before it's too late.
