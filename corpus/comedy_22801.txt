Told through the eyes of Hayden a young dreamer whose hope has turned to disillusion we follow the relationship of four friends as they each try to surmount personal and professional hurdles while maintaining their friendship and sanity. As Hayden deals with his recent debacle with love Jared must confront his problems with addiction Mike has a difficult decision to make about revealing his sexual orientation publicly and Danny will choose between career advancement and the trust of her closest friends.
