A man was shot to death when he was about to leave his house. The perpetrator of the crime does not flee hastily instead stays close to the scene convinced of impunity that makes him dependent on fate. Just a few months ago Elías Redondo (José Vicente Moirón) a cold-blooded gunman was an ordinary man financially and professionally well placed. But just a a lapse a random and impulsive event a small theft that he commits turns his life into a pathetic outburst.
