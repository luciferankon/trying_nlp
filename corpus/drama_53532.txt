A group of collage girls go for an excursion under the guidance of two female teachers a teenage girl begins menstruating on an auspicious day a girl on a date get robbed and another girl aspires to be a professional photographer. They all belong to different social strata and at different stages of life yet one thing predominantly governs their lives - an imposition of fear. Fear of darkness fear of unknown fear of crowd fear of space fear of pollution fear of forbidden and fear of sexuality. The fictional stories of coming of age are interrupted by real-life testimonies of domestic violence in the lives of adult women.
