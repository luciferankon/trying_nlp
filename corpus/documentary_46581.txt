Featuring amazing folk music energetic dancing and behind-the-scenes comedy this personal documentary paints a unique portrait about the key artists and musicians behind today's Hungarian Folk Revival Movement. Filmed primarily at the Smithsonian Folklife Festival in Washington D.C. the film is about everything that happened beyond the stage from the dressing room to the hotel and about how this unique revival movement has affected each character's life. A must-see for anyone interested in world music folk dancing and world culture -- and also for those who think folk revival is boring and out-dated.
