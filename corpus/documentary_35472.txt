Is border control between the states a business? 2014 was one of the years most media in the Euro-African borders of Ceuta and Melilla. 'Walled' follows a traveler accompanied his guide dog which seeks to understand how it works the Southern Border. Faced with a journalism that seeks to impact Walled tries to humanize a through conversation with the various actors who inhabit the border: migrants security forces human rights activists and neighbors on both sides of the fence.
