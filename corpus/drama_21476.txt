Biren Dutta is a lawyer. He is unsuccessful in his career and does not have any client. His wife is an alcoholic.The couple has one son and one daughter. Biren Dutta has an extra-marital affair with his only client. Biren's daughter China first falls in love with Ghenti Kaku who is of her father's age and later falls in love with a guy who is involved with Naxalite movement and finally runs away with him without the permission of her parents. Their teenager son Rono comes back from boarding school since their father could not pay his tuition fees. Dutta wants his son to be a barrister but his son wants to become an actor.
