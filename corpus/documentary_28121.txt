Mixuga provides a glimpse of the lives of two transsexual women who could not be more different. One of these women is Laura who is sixty years old and lives in a shelter for the homeless. Although her battle against loneliness is constant she faces life with a great deal of courage - and poetry. Tiara stunningly beautiful and 26 years old wears Prada shoes with heels that measure 12cm. She has Cinderella ambitions.
