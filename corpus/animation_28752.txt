Enjoy these rarely-seen gems from the Amadee J. Van Beuren Studio. Twenty-one classic cartoons from the 1930s are here in the lush;bizarre style that the studio innovated with such characters as Felix the Cat;Molly Moo-Cow and the people from the "Toonerville Trolley" comic strip. A treat for all animation fans!
