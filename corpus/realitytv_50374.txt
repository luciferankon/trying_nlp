Episode I and II of the Ultimate Workshop II are two half hour programs produced by RIVR Media for the DIY network. These two programs detail the construction of the Ultimate Workshop in a backyard. Step by Step information on the building process is given by host Jay Baker . The third episode of the Ultimate Workshop details the transformation of the workshop to a woodworking shop. Episode four details the construction of a greenhouse for the ultimate garden workshop. Episode five shows how to set up and use the ultimate home automotive workshop.
