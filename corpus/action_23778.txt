Ragged Edge is a futurist racing game and its universe is based on Jacques Martel's science fiction book "Bloody Marie". Between two raids on commercial ships Pyrates and Freebooters compete in illegal races to become a legend. Join the most prestigious crews race with the best and your exploits will be told endlessly in all Ragged Edge taverns. Only the best edgers will stay in people's minds. Race in the most epic championships rise yours colours high and re-write history.
