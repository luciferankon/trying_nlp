Political researcher Mae Brussell gives an overview of her 23 years of her work to an audience on the UC Santa Cruz campus in February 1987. She explains that the Axis powers of World War II survived and continued their world-wide fascist campaign overthrowing one country after another. America was overthrown in 1963 when President Kennedy was assassinated. The film is augmented with interviews of Mae's friends excerpts of Mae's radio broadcasts and news clips.
