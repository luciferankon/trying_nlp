A search to the values of life through the people who have survived in an extreme agony Sorokdo is an island of Korea where the scars of the wars are reflected the later confused situation to them the inexplicable suffering to the human being and the injustice in a society extremely concentrated in the economic development. The intention of this documentary is to think about the meaning of the values of human existence approaching to the life of the surviving patients of leprosy in this island.
