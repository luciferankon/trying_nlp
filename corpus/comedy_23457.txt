A family friendly comedy starring Louis Mandylor Mackenzie Firgens w/ Deborah Theaker & Mickey Rooney. Written by Tim Gallagher. Gerald was dropped on his head at birth. At 30 he is living w/ his mother when she suddenly dies. He carries her ashes w/ him in a ceramic doll. When the doll is stolen he enlists the help of Helen but things go awry; he must choose - mother's ashes or Helen's life.
