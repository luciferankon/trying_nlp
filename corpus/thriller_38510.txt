Anya is a thriller about a 16-year old girl's one-day adventure with a suicidal billionaire. When a billionaire decides to travel to the ranch he's never known to kill himself his plans are interrupted by a wild child in the woods. Through their one-day adventure are their lives changed liberated or lost? This high-octane cat & mouse psychological warfare is dialed up to level ten intensity from character tension the pursuit of a deadly mountain lion and the demons from the past that haunt them both.
