Bjarni and his girl friend Crya are coming to help Hatchie and together they're heading out to stop the thread - and to save the world from destruction and chaos once again. "Cultures - Reise nach Nordland" is a real time strategy game coming from Germany game. Nordland is the sequel to the well-known "Cultures 2 - The Gates of Asgard" and tells the story of a brave viking named Bjarni who also has solved the riddles of the secret sun stones of America with his family while he was still very young. Leif Eriksson his father is said to be the first discoverer of America before Christopher Kolumbus. Bjarnis former adventures have led him through Europe to the orient while he solved the secret of a strange vision he had. On his way he made friends with three other brave heroes of different countries where his adventures has led him through. Together they've beaten the dangerous Midgard serpent after many adventures and safe the world from destruction. But soon it was clear that new dangers and adventures were awaiting the four heroes which led them into the regions of Northland.
