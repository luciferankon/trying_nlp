Who is behind the costumes that entertain children and tourists in Times Square? What does it mean to be a superhero? Through interviews with illegal immigrants aspiring actors and the unemployed we explore the underbelly of Times Square. Find out who are these masked characters in the most famous place in the world and the NYC Police Officers who want them off the streets. "Superhustle" is a delirious trip inside the real life of the true superheroes of New York City.
