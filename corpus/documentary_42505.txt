ADRENALIN RUSH is a high intense action packed thrill ride. We will take you from the track to the urban streets as we watch as the 11 infamous members of the Bay areas elite stunt rider group NOTCH RYDERS tear the bay area streets apart with their high-octane illegal sport. You were given a taste in 2003 with Biker Boyz now you are given the real and true story of what it takes to be a premier stunt rider. This raw and vivid documentary takes you through a rollercoaster of ups and downs as these riders risk their lives one stunt at a time in an attempt to put their group on the map. Real crashes; real stunts; real women- fully exposed.
