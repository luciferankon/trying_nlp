A comedy journey from Australia to the beaches and campus of Malibu with 2 Aussie brothers a camel named Mitch and an LA starlet who the boys have longed to meet. Ryan Cobb "Rino" and John "Jonno" mix up a telephone call involving the sale of stud semen from their champion camel. The buyer a wealthy Californian horse stud owner brings the boys to LA and the calamity starts as well as their journey for money and love.
