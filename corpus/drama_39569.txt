The movie is set in Alkud a simple and regular village in Maharashtra having an agrarian economy. When the government conducts a survey at Bardi a barren land adjacent to the village Anna a veteran from the village is called as a witness to survey. On enquiring about the survey Anna gets a vague answer and the communication gap leads him yo believe that his land will be acquired. As a safety measure Anna fences his piece of land spreading rumors that an industrial zone is about to be set-up in the village. All this confusion ends up in people acquiring the land at inflated prices ending all bonds of humanity in the region.
