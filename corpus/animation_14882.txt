On the Ninjago holiday: Day of the Departed the ninja remeber those who have departed. Jay visits his parents at the junkyard; Lloyd and Misako remember Garmadon in the Corridor of Elders; Zane remembers his father in Birchwood Forest and Kai and Nya remember their parents too. Cole unable to fully accept being a ghost visits Sensei Yang as he begins to fade from existence but accidentally releases the spirits of the ninjas' past enemies resulting in an epic fight.
