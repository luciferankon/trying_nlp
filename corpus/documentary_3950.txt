International teen icons Alli Simpson Luke Bilyk Jessica Tyler and Ashley Leggat joined an electric line-up of speakers and entertainers including Fefe Dobson Nikki Yanofsky The Treble Suzie McNeil and The X Factor's Carlos Guevara at the world's largest youth-run event: "Count Me In Toronto". This documentary aired internationally online and took the world by storm. With a reach of over 2.2 million and viewers tuning in from 69 countries across the globe this intimate and empowering documentary event has inspired volunteer action and grassroots movements around the world. Count Me In was designed to inspire community involvement and help connect youth with local volunteer opportunities matching their interests.
