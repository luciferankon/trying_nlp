Five vignettes with beautiful babes in bondage. In the first a cute blonde is tricked into meeting a lovely crook and winds up hogtied and gagged. In the next story an adventuress is grabbed bound muzzled spanked and tormented with ice cubes. In the next tale a innocent woman tries to free herself from the chair to which she's securely roped. In the fourth vignette a woman discovers a former roommate has been captured by a burglar and rather than freeing her gloats while her former friend frantically tries to escape. In the last yarn a woman's former boyfriend angry at being kicked to the curb gets his revenge by stripping her to the waist and leaving secured in a inescapable ball-tie.
