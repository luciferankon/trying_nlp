Beautiful countess of Rudlov disappears in late 1940s. The legend blames the monster the people blame the lagoon. Nearly 50 years later the spirited Illona Rudlov is a struggling ballerina living in New York. When the Velvet Revolution occurs she decides to go to Czechoslovakia to reclaim her ancestors castle. She falls for a handsome hunter (Jan) Paul Hawkins and is haunted by a mysterious creature there.
