For centuries the people of Kanakanavu have kept watch of the crystal-clear Dakanuwa Creek. But the strike of Typhoon Morakot has changed everything. Waves of landslides flooded the Kanakanavu Tribe which forced them to evacuate. Through the year-long effort of Mayaw Biho the director of Dear Rice Wine You Are Defeated revelation is brought to the audience of how the Kanakanavu rebuilt their shelter after Marokot and sought their true identity.
