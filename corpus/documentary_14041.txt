After reaching #1 at Mutual of New York and several other financial service companies Robert J. Smith became totally and permanently disabled as a result of an auto accident. Unable to work Smith interviewed people who have reached #1 in other industries. Sports champions rock stars business people and others who reached #1 status are interviewed. Thanks to #1 people in every industry can learn to develop the habits and strategies of people who have reached the top of their chosen professions. People who become disabled or have to change careers for other reasons can learn that the best is yet to come.
