In this latest installment of Sweetheart Video's critically acclaimed series the passion continues as girls seduce girls into long sensual kisses which lead to the hot intense sex for which this series is recognized. Art dealer Aiden Ashley must contend with temperamental artist Dana Vespoli who refuses to compromise her vision for commerce thus resulting in a compromising situation. Conservative author Alyssa Reece comes face to face with feminist author Sovereign Syre just before both girls are to appear on a talk show leading to a battle of words and of tongues. Makeup artist Jessie Andrews nervously applies makeup on her idol actress Ryan Keely only to discover the actress has feelings for her. Finally Celeste Star finds out that her new roommate Anikka Albrite has a penchant for going through her things.
