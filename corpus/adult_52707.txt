When Sarah Palin is invited to audition for a movie about her life she faces competition from Tina Fey. After an unrelated visit to the set of CSI: Wasilla we catch up with Sarah as she and David Hasselhoff judge on "America's Next Top Governor". Next she interviews master of Illusion Chriss Angel and Levi Johnston on her own show "Simply Sarah". Sarah finally ends up starring in a film for Hustler directed by Axel Braun.
