FOLLOWING ORIGINAL MUSIC WITH TRADITIONAL FOLK MOTIVES OF EASTERN EUROPEAN COUNTRIES PERFORMED BY "TRIO BALKAN STRINGS" WHICH IS CREATED FROM TWO GENERATIONS OF FAMILY STARCEVIC (FATHER ZORAN AND SONS NIKOLA AND ZELJKO) HERE IS A STORY ABOUT AN HUMAN LIVING. BEHIND THE RIVER AS A METAPHOR OF THAT LIVING A HUMAN LIFE GOES BY: THROUGH CHILDHOOD YOUTH MIDDLE AGE OLD AGE...UNTIL A NEW LIFE IS BORN CONTINUING A CHAIN OF THAT LIVING TO UNIVERSAL STORY OF HUMAN KIND AND CIVILISATION.
