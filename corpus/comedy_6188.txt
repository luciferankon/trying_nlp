When the world is in danger superheroes come to rescue humanity. they are stronger faster smarter than ordinary people and are willing to sacrifice themselves for the sake of others. But what would happen if superheroes would not be around and the world's rescue team will be made of selfish irresponsible twenties weirdos? Nerd Swagger Bitch Rocker and Loser go clubbing at a great party called "Apocalypse" but woke up and found themselves in a deserted city with no record of what happened to them at night.
