Lathan Crowe's review of "Darker than Black" Season 2. "Darker than Black: Ryuusei no Gemini" is an anime television series created directed & written by Tensai Okamura & animated by Bones Animation Studio. It is a sequel to "Darker than Black: Kuro no Keiyakusha" & chronologically follows the events that take place after Season 1 & the 4 OVA episodes.
