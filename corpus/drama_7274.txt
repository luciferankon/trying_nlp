A collection of some of the show's most shocking cliffhanger episodes. What will David do when he finds out he's not the twin's real father? How did Beryl Palmer fall down a mineshaft? Will Alison escape after being bitten by a snake hidden in the Hamilton's safe? Who will survive the infamous Woombai shootout? Is Fiona's whiter than white niece Janice pregnant after an unforeseen one night stand? Will Wayne out swim a hungry shark? And will lovable Charlie finally tie the knot in the final ever episode?
