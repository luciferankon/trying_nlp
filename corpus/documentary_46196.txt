Something amazing is going on here. A traditional rite an important symbol an excuse for a family photo op around a newborn - Baptism means a lot of different things to those who experience it. What if there is a lot more happening in Baptism than most people realize? In three engaging sessions the home-edition of Reborn explores the deeply personal and sacred relevancy of Baptism for Parents Godparents and most importantly the Baptized. With a step-by-step explanation of the rite itself including it's profound place in Scripture and Tradition Reborn shows how baptism reveals the heart of God's love for His children.
