Hosted and narrated by Nancy Kerrigan Holiday On Ice: Under the Desert Sky was originally produced in November 2006. World-renowned skaters skate to their favorite Christmas songs on a special rink that was staged at the 5000-seat Dodge Theatre in Phoenix Arizona. The extravaganza includes elves jugglers pairs skaters and a Santa sleigh ride. Featuring two-time Olympic Medalist and World Champion Brian Orser and many more!
