Based on Folklore of Lala hardaul. King Jujhar Singh orders his wife to poison his son like brother on suspicion of having illicit affair. Queen obeys and Prince Hardaul Dies. Sister Queen Kunjavati breaks all relationship with her elder brother Jujhar and Vows that her Younger Brother will come back and Do Bhaat Ceremony in her daughter's marriage . But Do Dead Come Back? This emotional tale tests all belief  Relationships and human Evil Form all together. First Prolific Film made on it went to National Film Awards in 2103
