Panic and fear strike the hearts of the carefree sunbathers in a once peaceful and popular nudist resort in sun-bleached Miami when a mysterious yet menacing intruder manages to find his way in the camp. Inconspicuously taking cover in the thick foliage and hiding effortlessly in the pitch-black and almost shapeless shadows the grotesque and powerful perpetrator will eventually kidnap a helpless woman in the still of the night and after a brief fight he will pitilessly take her life. Could a sex maniac be responsible for the poor woman's gruesome death? As the Miami Police launch a full investigation taking into account a lucky survivor's statement while the killer is still on the loose the terrorised sun-kissed nudist girls will keep on screaming for help during the nights. How could one lure the blood-stained adversary out of his hideaway?
