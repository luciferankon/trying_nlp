TONY CAPONIE" is an uplifting hilarious comedy/drama that takes us through the life of Jimmy Jones "JJ" (Tony Cox). He lost his job his family his health insurance and in need of a kidney transplant leaves Detroit to not be a burden any longer. Life has chewed JJ up and spit him out one too many times. On the brink of suicide things suddenly turnaround when JJ accidentally saves the life of mafia boss Dominic Palumbo who renames his new friend "Tony Caponie". Tony Caponie and his new mentor Dominic Palumbo devise a way to legitimize the mafia families. In order for them to become legit an outsider (Tony Cox) is the key.
