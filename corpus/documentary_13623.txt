Marines of Delta Company 1st Tank Bn lead the 7th Marine Regiment in the fight to "Facilitate Rejime Collapse in Iraq" during Operation Iraqi Freedom. Told through the Lens of Combat Camera Officer B. Sean Fairburn who was ordered to "Shoot the War" in High Definition Fairburn stayed at the edge of the fight bringing a look at this war as never before seen. interviewing the Marines in the Tanks to give their perspective on what was really going on as we pressed through Iraq toward Baghdad. Sgt said "It didn't matter how tough the fight would be for that bridge we all knew the road home was on the other side"
