This film explores the life of Mark Rowswell a Canadian student who went to China to study the languages there. A chance encounter landed him an opportunity to appear on a popular TV show where he dazzled the audience with his command of the language. As a result he has become a popular comedy star in that country and we explore his life in China as he develops a succesful career in the distinctive verbal comedy style of the region. In doing so he has learned more about China and its people in a way he never thought possible for him.
