This documentary asks Bodega owners their staff and their suppliers what their dreams are and what version of the American Dream they are looking to fulfill. During the pursuit of answering these questions we are seeking to understand the relationship between the people behind counter and the people that they are serving non-stop - the ever-growing "hipster" population.
