Since 1982 the Calgary Interfaith Food Bank has provided immediate short-term hunger relief to Albertans who cannot afford to eat. Today the Food Bank has grown into a high-demand $32 million charitable corporation moving millions of pounds of food and helping thousands of people every day. Through five acts and six interweaving story lines Stack It Pack It and Share It takes us inside the logistics of the Food Bank and the powerful personal stories of its client families volunteers and staff.
