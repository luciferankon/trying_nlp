In Bolivia at the end of the 80's Aaron a 9 years old boy lives in a world of delicate balance easily moving from sadness to joy. This world is made of tiny events from which rain - and in the end all forms of water- is the most beloved and mysterious one. Who is calling there? why everything is sad all of a sudden? What kind of nostalgia and what kind of hopes disputes him? The death of a beloved one exasperate this questions till the point of departure.
