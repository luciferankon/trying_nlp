A 21-year-old girl makes her way across an America scarred by the events of September 11 in the hopes of finding her father who left when she was very young. Along the way her adventures some hilarious some tragic some terrifying expose her true nature and perhaps the true nature of the country itself. An epic of small towns and highways JOURNEYMAN follows the adventures of 21-year-old Anna as she travels an America scarred by the events of September 11 in search of her long lost father. Along the way she meets a wide variety of characters from a young boy to an ex-soldier to her would-be step-sister many of whom have themselves met and known Anna's father been affected by him. And through them she learns who he is and perhaps who she is as well. It is a movie of tangents of stories within stories. And Anna's adventures some hilarious some tragic some terrifying expose her true nature and perhaps the true nature of the country itself.
