Athens 1991. The secret organization "Armed Guard" has developed terrorist action. The police are organizing an independent strike team from specially selected shooters headed by Stergios Balatsoukas a tough veteran with muted ideas. Stergios who has lost his wife for many years still lives with her in hallucinations faces big problems in the house with his son and daughter who have grown up and dare to lift his head. His daughter is tied to a marginal young man and Stergios is inhuman and the little one leaves his father's house. But the cop's tragedy peaked shortly as he discovers that his son has revolutionary ideas resulting in a family rupture that breaks his home almost the same time that the time for the final showdown with the organization "Armed Guard ".
