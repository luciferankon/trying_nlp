Scottish program investigating vivisection at Glasgow University and Inverness Research Institute and the secrecy involved with it. Gives some interesting stories of the Research Defence Society maintaining a blacklist of people applying for jobs in such institutions surveillance of peaceful campaigners collusion between hunt thugs and the police and agent provocateurs infiltrating BUAV. Includes Les Ward of Advocates for Animals coverage of the Justice Department Catriona Kirkpatrick of the Hunt Saboteurs Association footage the Animal Liberation Front attack on Edinburgh University the Animal Rights National Index links with the vivisection industry and the John Newbury-Street hoax bomb.
