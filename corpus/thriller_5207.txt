The main star of the story is Gilli (Charmy Kaur) who came to Hyderabad to spend some time with her friends. She was staying in an apartment overlooking the Manorama cafe. One day she was bored and looking at the people in the cafe. As she could lip read she started to test her skill on different peoples. Suddenly she realized that a boy is threatening to commit suicide on phone to his girlfriend. Gilli gets worried and take actions to clear the misunderstanding between these love birds. She went to the cafe to talk to the boy where a strange man Nishan was sitting with a bag which contained a powerful time bomb. The moment Nishan saw Gilli entering the cafe he instantly became attracted to her and suddenly his mission of blowing up the cafe became unimportant. Will the bomb explode or there will be a happy ending?
