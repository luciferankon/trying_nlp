The Ghosting is a horror thriller about internet dating. When Max casually disappears on Emily a girl he's been dating he moves on to his next relationship. But after Emily commits suicide Max's world is turned upside down as he's suddenly erased from the collective memories of his friends and family. Could it be that Emily's vengeful spirit which haunts him night and day is responsible for this phenomenon? Max will have to find out and make amends if he is to undo - The Ghosting.
