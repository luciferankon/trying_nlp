Dixie Mafia is a one-hour special that tells the true story of Mississippi's battles with organized crime. Responsible for dozens of murders and countless other crimes the Dixie Mafia rose to power in Biloxi during the 1960's and their reign of terror lasted decades. Now the Dixie Mafia's torch has been taken up by a new gang equally corrupt and equally dangerous. Dixie Mafia shows the rise and fall of real American gangsters taking viewers into the lives of the crime bosses organization members and law enforcement officers that still battle in America's deep south.
