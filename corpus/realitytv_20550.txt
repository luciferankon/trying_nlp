The Real Estate Adventure of Sandy & Maryse is the story of two women who are motivated by a desire for change. Sandy Niemczak and Maryse Derouin lead regular busy lives but they have reached a point where they need something more - something to jolt them out of their everyday routines. Combining this need for adventure with their love of real estate and home renovation they have found the perfect project to shake up their lives - flipping a house. Over ten half-hour episodes Sandy and Maryse take on every aspect of this challenge of a lifetime: house searching sweating out negotiations renovating decorating and then putting their creation on the market. Will they be able to balance their regular family responsibilities with their dedication to this real estate dream and to each other? Whatever the outcome it's sure to be quite an adventure!
