Press coverage from conference held at Borrelli's of East Meadow to rally together local business owners residents local community groups celebrities and supporters of allowing the people of Nassau County the power to decide on a tax increase in order to raise the 500 million needed to re-build the Nassau Coliseum. Hound Comics celebrity CEO William 'Brimstone' Kucmierowski stepped forward along with the likes of Mike Mottaw (NY Islanders) and musician Mark O' Connell (Taking Back Sunday) in support of saving a true iconic landmark on Long Island.
