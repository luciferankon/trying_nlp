This is a story about Deba an antisocial and Minu who lived alone after murdering her drunken husband. Minu has developed a relation with Khayra also an antisocial and opponent of Deba. One day police raid Deba's area. He escapes and by mistake hides into Minu's room which is in Khayra's area. Minu helps him to hide in her room for ten day.
