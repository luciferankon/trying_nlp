Margaret Sinclair an ordinary girl knew from a tender age to the depths of her soul that the Lord Our God is God indeed. His faithfulness lasts forever and His love and mercy have no end. She pondered Our Lord Jesus divine sacrifice on The Cross which takes away our sins as she knelt in contemplative prayer before the Blessed Sacrament and she found rest for her soul. She was an heroic soul full of love for the great Master and Saviour of mankind and she was devoted to the service of Our Divine Lord.
