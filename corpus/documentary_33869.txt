Without a doubt Berlin established itself as one of the first places to go for emerging creatives from all over the world and stands out clearly within the German art scene. Why of all things is the young Bohemian world settling in this cultural metropolis on the river Spree? What kind of chances and dangers holds this glut in the art market? In twelve short episodes IN THE BELLY OF A WHALE sheds light on varying creatives within Berlin - urban artists graphic designers photographers gallery owners - and tries to capture this unique spirit that is living inside the belly of the whale.
