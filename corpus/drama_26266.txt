A 33 years old charming man who has everything that anybody could desire comes by chance in contact with a peculiar kind of people: a midget two sinister gentlemen and others. Many elements make it clear that these people are deeply involved in satanism and black-magic practices. With them there is a fascinating young lady. As soon as the man meets this young girl he falls in love and he becomes almost unnaturally addicted to her. From that moment he starts to loose everything: the family the social position the money and his mind. As a result he becomes a beggar. Only the viewers can decide if magic forces were at play in his destruction or if it was caused just by love.
