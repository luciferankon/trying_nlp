Tens of thousands of people have UFO sightings however very few of them are officially reported. The 2013 Symposium on UFO's & UAP's is the focal point conference for this documentary dealing with the taboo and profound negative effect sightings have on witnesses. This film documents peoples lives after their sightings as well as evidence provided at the 2013 Symposium where a host of international credentialed speakers will provide evidence and data to the public. Speakers include retired military generals former NASA scientists authors researchers and astrophysicists.
