Lampiăo (known as the King of Cangaço) was finally defeated and killed in the night of June 28 1938. His remaining gang had only eight men and two women left which felt outraged for the unfaithful ambush that was set up to their boss. This band had with it lots of money and gold. Chased by the police the group runs away living a series of conflicts within it. The escape through the rocks is filled with tension friendship resistance violence dreams love and the fear of death. They were living the twilight of the Cangaço...
