From the time James A. Bradley purchased the beach front land until present; Asbury Park's majestic days-the 1970's riots Asburried in Time covers it all. Comprised of interviews from local historians local business owners residents vacationers and other interesting people viewers will learn all about Asbury Park through each decadeoutside of the ever so popular music culture which is touched upon as well. The film broadcasts Asbury Park as a one of a kind beach where everyone from any walk of life can come comfortably and call it home for ever or just one day.
