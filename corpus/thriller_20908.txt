The result of an old CIA mind-control experiment gone bad Felice Quick is on the loose and headed for junior high-school choir teacher Sam Blacker. While the crazy Felice eventually convinces Sam that she's his long-lost and thought-to-be-dead sister she doesn't convince him that the FBI her ex-employer is after Sam for past crimes. Under the pressure of her psychological assaults and mind-games Sam's peaceful afternoon turns into a soul-shattering night. The FBI agents on Felice's trail Kane Patterson and Arlina Romano must face their own grief-filled pasts as they dig for clues to Felice's whereabouts. The threads of child abuse and old murders weave a tapestry of mystery that are brought to light in a shocking conclusion.
