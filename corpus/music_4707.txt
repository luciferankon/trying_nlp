On November 30 2004 the day the Portuguese government collapsed Manuel Joăo Vieira and his satirical rock band Ena Pá 2000 (Holy Cow 2000) celebrated their 20th anniversary with a mega concert at Lisbon's Paradise Garage that included such special guests as Tony Barracuda the raw fish eater; the sexy Miss Suzie Peterson with her theatrical impersonations and Phil Mendrix Portugal's incomparable guitar genius.
