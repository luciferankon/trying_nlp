In 1782 following a sea-storm the Moroccan ambassador's ship is shipwrecked against the coasts of Sicily. As the diplomat does not speak a word of Italian Fra Giuseppe Vela a Maltese brother is requested to watch over him and to serve as an interpreter for him. To tell the truth the monk knows only a few words of Arabic which does not trouble him in the least. Quite the contrary actually: the sly-boots is determined to make the most of the situation...
