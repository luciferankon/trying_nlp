In the 14th largest county in the United States in one of the most liberal areas of the nation the Recession has already forced major program cuts and rendered important social programs obsolete. Faced with another 10% cut in the 2011 budget how will this government decide its new fate? Combining humorous animation with controversial opinions and complex solutions this short documentary shows a government of over 2 million people faced with dire decisions and long-term consequences. In a raw look at local politics find out what local officers are intimately thinking about when they are faced with impossible dichotomies. And facing years of tough economic decision-making to come what can YOU do?
