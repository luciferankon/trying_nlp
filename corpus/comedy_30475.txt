In the past when Gunnar lived in the city he and Erik was always together they were best buddies. But since he moved out to his farm in the country they haven't seen each other but it is now finally time for Erik to visit. He is supposed to stay on the farm over the weekend and already on the bus away from the city and the everyday life he begins to wonder if this really was a good idea. When he comes to the farm he meets a Gunnar that has changed totally. He is no longer the faithful city-metropolitan guy he once was he is transformed into a full-blooded farmer. Erik gets a weekend that he will never forget.
