Sex Drugs and Rock & Roll is all he knows but after Cassius Cole loses everything they wont be enough to save him. Trying to follow his dreams Cassius becomes so wrapped up in his own drug and alcohol filled world that he can not escape. Blinded by his passion he breaks ties with his friends and family to pursue his music career. Jonas Cassius' younger brother wants to be just like Cassius. He looks up to him and wants to a be a Rock Star too. They share the same dream but Cassius can not see just how much Jonas loves him. It's a tug-of-war relationship for Jonas as he fights inner demons and insecurities of his own. Crying out for help Cassius ignores his brothers out reached hands and instead shuts him out. After an accident with Jonas Cassius conceals his guilt with his drug addiction and their relationship breaks it's final thread.
