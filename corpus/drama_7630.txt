Dholi Taaro Dhol Waage is a saga of passion for dhol (Indian Percussion instrument)& love. Raju a dhol player from lower strata of the society meets Mansi daughter of a prosperous Thakur. Raju has inherited the legacy of playing dhol from his father. With his passion for the dhol he wins the heart of his lady love. However they face opposition on grounds of tradition & social status.
