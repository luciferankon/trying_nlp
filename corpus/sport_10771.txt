A Weekly syndicated show involving athletic competitions (e.g. swimming rowing cycling track tennis etc.) involving T.V. Celebrities grouped in teams of 3 men and 3 women each. The teams were made up of cast members of a specific show or sometimes a joint team from the cast of two shows (e.g. Fame&Givemeabreak). There were 3 teams per episode in a tournament format with the top two teams advancing to the next round culminating in a grand finale. Often it aired after midnight on a weekend. The biggest name competitors included: Linda Blair Ted Knight Dick Van Patten Flip Wilson Patty Davis LaVar Burton and Desi Arnaz Jr.
