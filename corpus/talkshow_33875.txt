Incarnation is a 2013 South Korean talk show that is a revamped sequel to the popular talk show "Strong Heart" which aired from 2009 to early 2013. It was hosted by Kang Ho Dong and Lee Seung Gi when it first premiered with actor Lee Dong Wook and MC Shin Dong Yup taking over hosting duties in 2012. In the new season of Incarnation the show will be hosted by Shin Dong Yeop Kim Hee Seon and Yoon Jong Shin. "Incarnation" will feature five to six guests each episode and will take input from viewers on discussion topics.
