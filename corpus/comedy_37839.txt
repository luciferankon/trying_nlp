Written by Christy Gomez and Summer Moran Big Hit Pending is about novice writer and optimist Charlie getting ready to write the next great american musical with her best friend Blaise by her side. Her life is turned upside down when she finds out her crazy ex popstar cousin Persephone inherits the same apartment. The two must put aside their differences and come together as a team to make their show work with the help of Blaise Charlie's dimwitted friend Sonny and his questionable roommate Comrade Mango.
