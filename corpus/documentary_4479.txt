Israel's hot climate and mid-eastern mentality is an unusual setting for this story following the Israeli National Ice Hockey team... It comes as no surprise that they are virtually unknown in their own country as there is only one single skating rink strangely situated on the Lebanese border! Led by a legendary Canadian coach Israel's over-achieving team is comprised of an intriguing group of amateurs from diverse multi-cultural backgrounds... Though they differ in background immigrants from the former Soviet bloc join ex-Israeli's living in North America as they strive for a sense of acceptance & belonging in Israeli society. "Breaking the ice" deals with questions of identity in a quirky manner combing both humor and drama from a personal viewpoint.
