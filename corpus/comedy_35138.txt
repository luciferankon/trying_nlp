Josh a transvestite-in-denial believes he's Tori Amos. Josh and his chronically bleeding Kung-Fu sidekick Larry inadvertently join a transvestite cult known as the "Strict Nine". At war with the Strict Nine are a violent skateboarder cult known only as the V.O.T.S. (or: Victims Of The System) who are obsessed with heavy-metal guitarist Chris Impellitteri. In the midst of this alt-cult war Josh is kidnapped by the V.O.T.S. and in order to rescue him Larry must call upon the unlikeliest team of superheroes on Earth: TEAM BRUTAL!
