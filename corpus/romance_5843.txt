Soleman and Lokman are two brothers quarrel with each other every now and then. One day Soleman separated from his brother leaving all his properties for his nephew Sujon. Soleman's wife gives birth a daughter and dies at birth bed. Soleman rears her daughter Sokhi. Sujon meets Sokhi while stealing wood apple from Sokhi's tree. They meet again and again but no one knows the relationship between them. Finally they learn their relationship and desire to reunite two family.
