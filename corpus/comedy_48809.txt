The movie's main hero is Oskar ('Jirí Machácek' (qv)) a TV weatherman. Like many forty-somethings Oskar tries to ward off his mid-life crisis and his increasingly dissatisfying marriage with a steady stream of affairs. When he manipulates a weather report for the sake of flirting the repercussions are quick to follow: Oskar finds himself jobless and wifeless. His carefree bachelor lifestyle interlaced with lovers of varying ages is not however idyllic.
