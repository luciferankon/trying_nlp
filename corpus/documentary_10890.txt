Meet Colin Carroll Irishman - lawyer - Sumo Wrestler! the Fermoy-based lawyer with one thing on his mind - taking silliness seriously! After recovering from a broken back Colin had a new found respect for the gift of bodily movement. Join Colin as we follow him on his quest to break the World Record for the three-legged marathon at the 2008 Cork City Marathon all the highs and lows of running three legged the unique pacifistic approach to training not to mention trying to find a running partner! And If that's not enough it has Elephant Polo Backwards Running Hornsleding Argentinian Motorbike Adventures Eastern European boybands and most importantly a new way of looking at sport - The Paddy Olympics!
