Weekly talk show/web-series focusing on Nerd/Geek culture Craft Beers/Cocktails etc. Hosts Danny Donovan and Anna Tozzi-Barbay take viewers through the news of the week often times with celebrity guests discussing the ins and outs of Pop Culture. From comic books to films to TV series etc if it affects the Comic/Sci-fi world it gets covered. Geek Chic at it's best.
