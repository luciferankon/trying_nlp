Coming off a nasty attack by bullies that leave both his bike and his day ruined a young boy named Eli has a chance encounter with members of the motorcycle community who gift him with a bandanna and a view into a new world. Years later a socially-awkward teenage Eli still finds himself the victim of ridicule by his peers. Releasing his frustration with a frenzied bike ride out of town he finds himself at the front door of a motorcycle shop. Considering that fate has twice brought him to this world while he was at his lowest Eli takes a job at the shop. In the ensuing years he rebuilds an old motorcycle and at the same time rebuilds himself. Now graced with the confidence he needs and a sweet ride to boot the open road lays out before him full of possibility.
