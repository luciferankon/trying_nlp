Rivalries in the National Hockey League transcend the games on the ice. They're institutions. Showdowns between historic enemies breed both mutual respect and hostility -- no matter where the clubs sit in the standings. NHL Top 10 Rivalries presents an in-depth look into the league's fiercest feuds. Current and former players coaches and NHL historians relive memories and go behind the scenes both on and off the ice of some of the game's unforgettable match-ups. The Rangers and the Islanders The Battle of Alberta The Canadiens and Bruins The Battle of Quebec The Battle of Pennsylvania - plus many more are examined and ranked. Discover where your favorite rivalry falls and let the debates begin.
