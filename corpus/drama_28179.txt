On The Day Of Marriage Of Shanta (Rakhi Gulzar) & Prabin (Suresh Oberoi) The Police Raides The House But They Do Not Find Nabin (Mithun Chakraborty) Who Is Prabin'S Younger Brother. The Reason For Nabin'S Crime Is Not Disclosed. Then Nabin Meets Shanta In The Bus But The Police Attacks The Bus And Nabin Escapes Again. Meantime Prabin Wins The Case Of Their Old Ancestral Property. Nabin Meets Family Friend Suraj (Vijay Arora) And Knows About Shanta'S Delivery Of A Boy.
