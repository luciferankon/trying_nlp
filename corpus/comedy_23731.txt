Have you ever had a second job to make ends meet while trying to get the job of your dreams? This new comedy which is as if Entourage and The Office meets God in a church basement follows the lives of four paid musicians as they work their second job. The thing is their "survival job" is working in a church choir during Lent! Will they ever reach their goals and more importantly will they be forced to fast their personal lives for Lent this year?
