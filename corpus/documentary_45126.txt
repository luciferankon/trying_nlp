Experience Italy is fast paced fun and interesting. A fresh take on traveling through Rome Florence Venice and The Ligurian Coast. With bonus videos on Public Transport-Tips and Tricks Pisa Food and Drink and a trip to Sienna with a local wine producer. The show is very interesting and has some neat hidden facts for example do youn know where the alleged-spot where Michelangelo carved a self portrait on the side of a building? Or why are there locks all over the Ponte Vecchio Bridge? All of this and more with Experience Italy.
