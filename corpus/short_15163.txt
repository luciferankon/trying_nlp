The setting is that of a court with its suite of attendants. A messenger is dispatched to a scientist for an Easter egg and returns with the largest to be procured. At the court the egg is opened and from it is taken a complete outfit of infant's wearing apparel By magic a larger egg is produced and from it appear in groups of two a gathering of ladies exquisitely costumed who dance before the Queen. Finally one lady appears even more beautifully arrayed and in her arms she bears an infant which she presents to the Queen. The cup of joy is now full to the brim and all partake of it freely. The series concludes with a number of fancy dances beautifully executed. Artistic settings and beautiful costumes are a striking feature of this film.
