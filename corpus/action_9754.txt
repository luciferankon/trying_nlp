Dark Claw is a series set High in the African Mountain of Kilimanjaro where there once lay an ancient city of Za' Chagga. The Za'Chagga was led by a young warrior king name Menes also known as Dark Claw. Weeks after king Menes became king his city was ravaged by the Zulu nation lead by Shaka Zulu himself. Now only one za 'chagga remains... Dark Claw!
