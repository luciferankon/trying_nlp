A group of High School students are thrown together to complete an assignment on town folklore. The group decides to prove that once for all the old Miller house deep in the woods is haunted by a nine year old girl whom went missing years ago. What they find was more than they bargained for. Five High School friends embark on a journey deep into the woods to try and find out what happened to a group of students whom went missing a decade ago in hopes to find the Miller House. This home and the family that lived there have been part of a checkered folklore. What this group hopes to find is any clue to what may've happened to the teens and to solve the mystery of Constance the little girl whom went missing from her home. What they find is more than they bargained for.
