Umi is an independent film written by Jamal Hall. It explores the story untold of grandmothers becoming mothers again. From working everyday to making sure their grand kids eat and homework is done. Many of them are taking for granted. The movie focuses on one particular grandmother and her story. A single grandmother raising three grandchildren on her own. From a confused teen that has to decide between the streets or family to a teenage girl that deals with the issues of abortion and while all this is happening "Mimi" the grandmother must keep her family together and still keep the faith. This movie will take you on a spiritual ride from church to the raw streets to the home of this beloved family. Some events in this movie are true and maybe you can relate to the various issues portrayed. Dynomyte Films and Fischer of Men Entertainment bring to you; "Umi".
