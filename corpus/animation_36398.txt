Nina needs to go is a Disney Junior short film that a 4 year old girl named Nina in different settings like the mall or beach. It is a film that a young girl Nina needing to go to the bathroom but she holds it and every time she sees water or some type of running liquid she finally decides she needs to go. Her Nana comes to the rescue and goes through various paths to get to the bathroom. In the end when after using the restroom she says "don't wait to go".
