Daring to tackle Holocaust atrocities from a positive angle this documentary uniquely tells the story from three perspectives. Hungarian Vera was 12 when she was sent to Theresienstadt Czechoslovakia to dig water pipe ditches. Now 85 Vera has a loving family and became a successful Ontario farmer. An Austrian Olympic figure skater Hellmut was conscripted by Hitler and was a POW in British and American camps. He was inducted into Canada's Figure Skating Hall of Fame shortly before his death at age 90 in 2011. Raised by Holocaust survivors Frannie's father rationed their food and pretended to be Catholic. Frannie became a comic and created a successful one-woman show based on her family's story. "Endurance" emphasizes acts of humanitarianism hope and the power of healing.
