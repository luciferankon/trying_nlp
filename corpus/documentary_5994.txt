NASCAR: Unauthorized takes you out of the bleachers and into the driver's seat in the fastest most hair-raising sport in America. Stock-car racing was born on the dusty dirt tracks of North Carolina. It has become the new national pastime that has exploded into a multi-billion dollar industry. NASCAR: Unauthorized will showcase young drivers who risk everything including their lives in pursuit of the checkered flag. Ever wonder what is racing through the mind of a driver when he is at the helm of a 700 horsepower machine? This edge-of-your-seat documentary answers those questions. Race teams are always in hot pursuit to reach the winner's circle. A hand-picked team of automotive experts reveal the trade secrets of stock-car engineering that helps racers grasp the competitive edge on the track. Today's super-speedways are some of the most modern and technically advanced sporting arenas in the world. NASCAR: Unauthorized features every dangerous bend and so close to the cars that you can feel the heat off the hood. NASCAR: Unauthorized is an hour filled with heart-pounding fender-rubbing action using some of the most captivating imagery caught on camera and interviews with stock-car racing's leading experts!
