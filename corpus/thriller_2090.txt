A fashion photographer and three models become unknowingly involved in a series of strange coincidences after the death of a fourth model in an apparent accident. These strange events which are expanded on by a series of narrative flashbacks which develop both plot and characters include two other accidents and a senseless robbery. These lead the photographer to return to the one place that links these people and events a beach in Portugal. Once there he and one of the models are kidnapped by a group of desperate drug smugglers keen to retrieve photographs they think caught them in a drugs shipment transfer. Only the deep sense of guilt of a woman caught up in these events saves them.
