This story follows the journey of 2 dancers who are best friends in Los Angeles one of which has been diagnosed with stage 4 breast cancer. The film explores what it is like for a friend lover companion spouse or family member to experience a loved-one's tragic cancer diagnosis. It is a powerful testament about the preciousness of life and the depths and lengths we go as humans to preserve it. The film also brings to the surface the true meaning of life and one's quest and search for their true purpose.
