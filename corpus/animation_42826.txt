Aathichudi Kathaigal is all about the way of Living. Their lives a Family consists of A Father A Mother and two Children. The Daughter Name is Pooja and the son name is Teja. On their daily way of Living They are facing so many Hurdles Happiness Disappointments and Problems in their family. On Behalf of that That Family will teach us on how to live our lives happily.And also each and every poem has been turned into a story so that everybody can able to witness the meaning of life.
