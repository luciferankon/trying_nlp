Luis "Trikz" Da Silva aka the World's Best Ball Handler presents his exclusive freestyle basketball form which took the world by storm inspiring a new generation of basketball fans and revolutionizing the game of basketball to millions across the world. We see advanced streetball training including the freestyle tricks he performed in the 2001 Nike Freestyle ad campaign and moves he developed while traveling across the globe with the Harlem Wizards and while promoting NBA superstar Stephon Marbury's "Starbury" clothing line. Trikz is a positive youth advocate and prolific actor who inspires kids as well as their families through his public performances motivational speaking engagements and ongoing community involvement.
