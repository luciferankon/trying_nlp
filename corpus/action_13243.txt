This action-thriller that tells the story of Darin who is haunted by a past life. His negative lifestyle changes once he falls for a young lady from his village named Nara. Soon the couple migrate to KL city to leave behind Darin's indecent past and to start a new life. They are blessed with a bright and intelligent son Saufi. Alas their family's happiness doesn't last long with the arrival of Somchai an old colleague who has returned to cause unrest at Darin's workplace.
