Gemma and Connor are two fairly typical university students. Their future together looks certain until a change of plans breaks up the relationship. Both move on and Gemma gets involved with playboy criminal Justin. Things seem to be going well until Justin is found murdered. Although completely innocent Gemma is charged with the crime. Barrister James persuades her to plead guilty to manslaughter. This she does and is given a two year sentence. As she emerges from prison Connor is waiting for her.
