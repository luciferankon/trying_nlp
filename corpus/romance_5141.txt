Saaya is the story of Sudha and Kamya. Sudha is quiet fragile & incredibly shy with a simplicity and beauty that takes one's breath away and on the other hand Kamiya bursts with a vibrant zest for life full of fun pranks & an exuberance that borders on waywardness. Awkward circumstances bring the two together in an inseparable bond of friendship. They are always there for each other supporting each other through rough times. And yet through all this there is an undercurrent of jealousy and one-upmanship. The story moves on where circumstances begin to tear the two close friends apart - bit by bit. Life almost seems to be playing a cruel game with the two friends as slowly fate and chance conspire to make them the worst of enemies. One will eventually be destroyed. Which one will it be? Will they come back together or will they remain enemies forever?
