Wealthy businessman Annasaheb a widower owns a Papadam factory in the city. Simpleton Bharat works as a manager in his factory. Bharat is in love with a girl called Meeta. Bharat has taken a loan from Sanjubhai a money lender and is tired of paying the interest. One day Meeta happens to meet Annasaheb who takes a liking for her. The story revolves around how the hero's struggle to manage all this chaos and friend Annasaheb to get aside from his love.
