A new program stylized for multiple market(s) Marine Media World is geared to expose the yachting/boating lifestyle to television audiences worldwide while bringing news event and boat racing coverage to the screen from locations rarely covered. With an eye toward edgy content a modern shooting style and some of the world's most beautiful and interesting locations Marine Media World is a unique first-of-its-kind program that offers both existing yacht owners and dreamers alike an up-close and personal look at some of the world's most beautiful and exotic yachting destinations.
