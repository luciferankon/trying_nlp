It's 1983 and Brad Roberts is the best high school quarterback in the country. He dates the sexiest girl in town and is extremely popular but Brad is not satisfied. He wants to prove that he's more than just a hot-shot quarterback; Brad tries to turn his heavy metal band into the first ever boy band and they reject him. So Brad the jock teams up with a stoner and an ex-figure skater and a couple of reject break-dancers from the Bronx to make their mark in this sweet film.
