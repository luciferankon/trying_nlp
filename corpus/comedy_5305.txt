Armaan Pratap Singh who returns from the USA throws a major party at his house with nothing more than a bid to get back with his ex-girlfriend Shreya who he has'nt even written to or talked to in a long time. This movie is about four people/groups of people who attend trying to attend or just happen to be there at the time namely Shreya and her friends a Jat gym instructor Karan a musician and a bunch of teenagers who just can't seem to locate the place and get into a lot of trouble with the locals.
