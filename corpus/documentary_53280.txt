KROQ was arguably the most influential radio station in the world -- Being the first to play bands like The Clash U2 Depeche Mode No Doubt Beck Imagine Dragons and a laundry list of other A List bands. KROQ also started Loveline which ran for 32 years. Kevin and Bean the morning show spawned the careers of Jimmy Kimmel Adam Carolla Kennedy and Chris Hardwick. Along the way -- sex drugs and rock and roll reigned supreme -- Stay tuned for more KROQ.
