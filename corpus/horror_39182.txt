Starring Niharica Raizada Prashantt Guptha Ashrut Jain Gaurav Paswala Gaurav Kothari Disha Kapoor and Darshan M and cinematography by Sathya Hegde who is a prominent cinematographer in Kannada film industry and has done movies like Duniya Jackie Myna. The film tells the story of a group of youngsters whose mountain trek goes awfully wrong. This is one of the first Hindi horror films in the found footage genre The film tells the story of six friends whose mountain trek goes awfully wrong. They encounter bizarre incidents during their mountain trek. These incidents are recorded in a video recorder. The story is presented as they discovered video recording left behind by characters.
