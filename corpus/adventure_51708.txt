The Story: Gold Nuggets Bear Spray... Film Locations? Vancouver filmmaker buddies Dalton and Stewart find themselves in Canada's Yukon in search of gold treasure to fund their next film project. Old Uncle Logan has left some cryptic clues behind on a map leading our heroes on a gold nugget speckled trek through the diverse northern terrain. Through snow sun mountains lakes tundra locations and historic gold rush cities - they find themselves inspired by Yukon's natural wonders and eventful past. While trekking through sand dunes sleeping under stars riding a 100-plus-year-old train marveling at black granite summits and enduring a freak bear spray accident - they discover something much more valuable than gold: a treasure trove of film locations as diverse as the Yukon seasons. Will they be seduced by Yukon Magic?
