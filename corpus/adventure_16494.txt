Davey Hughes Untamed follows the extraordinary journeys of New Zealander Davey Hughes. From Africa to Alaska Australia to Norway each episode sees Davey - aka the Swazi man - follow in the footsteps of the great explorers and forge new paths of his own offering viewers a unique insight into some of the worlds' amazing cultures. A family man businessman adventurer and conservationist Davey Hughes is the man behind the highly successful Swazi outdoor clothing label; he's a passionate conservationist and he sure can tell a tale or two. Each episode sees Davey face new challenges and some incredibly unique circumstances as he hunts tracks and spends time with the local characters of his destinations. His exploits include a visit to Norway's Doomsday Vault; getting up close and personal with a Kodiak Brown Bear; and participating in New Zealand's epic multi-sport challenge the Coast to Coast. Watch Davey Hughes Untamed and come along for the ride.
