Childhood friends Derrick and Rilow have been trapping in Atlanta for nearly 20 years being supplied by the plug Mook however Derrick has aspirations of being the man in charge. Unbeknownst to Derrick Mook finds out. Thirsty for revenge Mook enlists none other than Rilow to kill his best friend with the reward of a place in Mook's organization.
