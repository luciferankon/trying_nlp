Seeking to leave his dark past behind Christian Figo settles down in Los Angeles to try living a normal life. That is until his old comrade Marcus returns. Manipulated by Marcus's Uncle (Baggat) while under the influence of mind altering drugs and discovering his girlfriend Aminah is giving up on him Figo finds himself caught in a web of prostitution murder sex and deception. Will he survive the journey or lose himself in the process?
