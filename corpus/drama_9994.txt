Ashland is the story of Ash Slavin a lonely and depressed teen secluded from the world ignored by his parents abandoned by his friends and betrayed by his loved one. Ash begins to see his blurry life become blind as we follow him through his last day of living. From the bizarre and hurtful incidents that make up his day to the shocking and disturbing final hour Ash will bare his heart and soul and be revealed before his last breath has left.
