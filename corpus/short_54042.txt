Meet Joe Everman a man who wants to be a musician first and a celebrity second. When Joe's band Lost My Appetite get their demo heard by record executive Clive they get an offer to have one of the songs featured on a movie soundtrack. Joe has to figure out what it is he wants: Artistic Integrity? Fame at all costs? Flavor of the week? With the support of his girlfriend Tina who herself is a struggling actress he comes to the right decision.
