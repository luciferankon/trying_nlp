Welcome to the jungle home of Kid Fitness. This fun entertaining and educational video will encourage your children to exercise and they won't even realize they are. Kid Fitness has invited some children from the town to visit him and his jungle friends for the day. As each of his jungle friends come by and interact with the children they share some fun facts and then teach the children to move like them the "exercise." Zebra Elephant Gorilla Bunny Kangaroo Alligator Snake Duck and more all come by to visit and meet the kids. Original music and delightful animations will have your child watching this entertaining video over and over again! Designed for ages 18 months through 8 years old everything about this video has been custom tailored to create the perfect atmosphere for your child to exercise and have fun all at the same time.
