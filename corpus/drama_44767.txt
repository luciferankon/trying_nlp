A drama about a girl who's about to commit suicide by jumping off a building roof when she's saved by a party boy who'd just come up for a smoke. He's the worst person imaginable to be her savior and the ensuing conversation between them where they bring out the best and worst in each other as he tries to convince her to live is the film. Tragic story of an unlikely Savior party boy Alistair who pulls suicidal Mia from a roof's edge. He's everything she hates and the only person who can convince her life is worth living. Two people one roof real time. Can anyone be saved?
