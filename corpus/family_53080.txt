Baron is reluctant to move from Jakarta to Balikpapan. In the new town he chooses to play with his remote-controlled car instead of making new friends. Despite his behavior Baron is chosen to represent his school in a boy-scout camp with his schoolmate the overtly optimistic Rusdi. Together with other friends the fire expert Anton and the temperamental Aldi and a strong girl they meet in a camp named Sindai they stumble into an unexpected adventure as Rusdi and Anton are kidnapped by illegal loggers. Now it's up to Baron Aldi and Sindai to rescue their friends in this fun and thrilling adventure film for the whole family in the Lebaran holiday!
