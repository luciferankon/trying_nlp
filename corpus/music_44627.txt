Ep.1 - "Enemy At the Garden Gate" - SYNOPSIS We are welcomed into the world of three separate strangers connected by one theme - human connection. Soon the cracks start to show as their internal conflict with love loneliness and a looming darkness. Ep.2 - "China Cup" - SYNOPSIS Our familiar strangers have become hindered by their own anguish beginning to slide deeper into darkness. Disconnect from themselves and their world hopefully they don't lose themselves completely. Ep.3 - "Lever by my side" - SYNOPSIS Battling for understanding place and resolve where might our strangers find comfort and connection in their darkest hours. Ep.4 - "Whispering Tree" - SYNOPSIS We witness the potential for new beginnings in loss vulnerability and relationships both new and old.
