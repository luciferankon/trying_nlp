In August 2013 36 teams traveled from around the world to compete for the chance to be crowned as world champions. The community raised over $20000 to send the best teams from their respective regions to battle it out over the 3 day event. The teams competing included; America's High Rollers Gaming Australia's Team Immunity as well as Europe's Epsilon eSports Team Cooler Master (formerly Broder) and Team Infused.
