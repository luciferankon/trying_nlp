BARN9: Chariots on Fire depicts the true story of the tragic barn fire on Wednesday March 15 2016 in Barn9 at the South Florida Trotting Center. Twelve horses perished that day but eleven survived. BARN9: Chariots on Fire not only details the events surrounding the fire but it also provides insight into the need for new technology that will prevent barn fires and catastrophic disasters like this from happening again. In addition BARN9: Chariots on Fire focuses on training techniques that can be implemented by the First Responders and Fire Fighters to better educate them in handling this kind of disaster when it comes to saving horses/livestock and dealing with their temperaments and size.
