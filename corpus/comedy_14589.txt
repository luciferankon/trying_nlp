Rosie just can't seem to get it together. None of her multitude of jobs have panned out and her family is quick to remind her "She's a hopeless screw-up." But her fate changes the day she enrolls in Sushi Academy. From an epic struggle to convince the sushi master to train a loud-mouthed red-headed woman to her showing off her skills in Tokyo to writing a sushi cookbook for Americans it is the lessons of the time-honored fundamentals of becoming a sushi chef where Rosie finally finds herself. Oh but she still totally screws up along the way.
