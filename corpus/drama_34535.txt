Innocence is no defense... In the city of Belfast in1941 where the men have all gone off to war lived a mill-girl named Belle who could tell you the story of a movie better than the screen itself. Into her life strides Gene a handsome Irish-American student stranded by the war who falls in love with Belle and her gift of telling the pictures: her Scarlett O'Hara he says is better than Vivien Leigh's. But in this city of anxiety poverty and rebel fire Belle is Protestant Gene is Catholic therefore she is Juliet he is Romeo - and tragedy soon rides in.
