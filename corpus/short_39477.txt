Jeff is about to become the father of twin girls. He has to move. When he starts to pack up after ten years in a Manhattan apartment his skeletons come out of the closet - literally. Jeff's collection of bones ephemera and "the stuff of everyday life" will change the way you see the world - and Jeff. Is the stuff in his closet art anthropology or obsession? His observations on nature maggots detritus and design form an inspiring ecological world-view.
