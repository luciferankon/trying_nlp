Beyond Breathing is a heartfelt true story of Margarete Cassalina's loss of her 13 year old daughter Jena to the fatal genetic disease Cystic Fibrosis. Her journey takes you from unfathomable heartache to love and understanding of life's realities. Through Margarete's journey she learns that life lessons come from her children and the beauty of living and the power of love. In the span of one year she learns to go from depression and dependency to inner strength and the realization that love never ends illustrating that there are truly no coincidences in life.
