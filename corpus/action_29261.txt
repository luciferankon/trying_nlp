Hector Riviera (Hector Echavarria) is a man with a past. Once a member of a lethal Special Forces unit in the Falkland War he now lives an ordinary life with his wife and son. Suddenly his peaceful existence is shattered when he discovers that his best friend Tomas has been brutally murdered by a mysterious hit man known as the Reaper (Ryan Watson). But when Riviera rushes to the side of his dying friend the police target him as Tomas' killer. Now Riviera must stay one step ahead of the law as he tries to prove his innocence.
