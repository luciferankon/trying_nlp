The Alpha Youth Series is a brand new interactive series exploring life faith and meaning. Re-imagined for today's youth culture each episode looks at a different question around faith and is designed to create conversation. It's for young people with no Christian or church background. The Alpha Youth Series will all be available for free online. After the success of the first Alpha Youth Film Series in 2013 the new Alpha Youth Series has been updated rewritten and redesigned for a global audience. It has been filmed in Vancouver London Paris Rome Israel and many other incredible locations.
