Mane Karakas came to a little town on the sea shore to dig a water hole for watering system. He tried to brighten up town's sleepy atmosphere. Local journalist didn't understood Karakas well so he published news about new popular melody festival in town. When it came to signing the contract Mane explains it is all a big misunderstanding. But singers and orchestras arrived. Music is everywhere but Mane goes elsewhere to research oil.
