Raja and Mahesh are lost brothers Raja makes his living by conning people and Mahesh is a Doctor who tends to forget things when Raja goes to steal in Mahesh house he is shocked to see that both look the same later Raja and Mahesh decides to exchange their lives with each other. A comedy of errors the film has lookalikes Raja and Mahesh (Bharat Jadhav) both living opposite lives. Raja is a poor person and a thief who sells stolen goods at Chor Bazaar Mumbai. Mahesh is a rich doctor but a very forgetful person. Pallavi is in love with Mahesh but he doesn't bother much with her. Once Raju goes to break in and steal at Mahesh's place but gets a shock of his life seeing his lookalike Mahesh. Will they meet each other? What will happen next?
