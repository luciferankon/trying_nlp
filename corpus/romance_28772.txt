A lot of time has passed since Igor and Misha saw each other. Back in youth they used to be best friends: went to school together spent their free time together and both shared one love... They met Inna at college. The cheerful girl was accompanied by her devoted gentlemen everywhere. Unexpectedly the relationships of this unseparable trio had turned into a love triangle. Jealousy threatened their friendship. Igor and Misha came to decision: to leave their love aside in the name of a true friendship. However nobody ever asked Inna about her decision...
