Paris in the 1920's what happens when a journalist decides to help a woman with a cursed husband? What happens when a tragic accident brings an omen on a successful artist? What happens when a soft man slowly falls into murderous madness? This novel is a macabre story about a perverted scheme where the puppet master is the Devil himself. In this swirling and grotesque tale gruesome crime meets occultism light meets darkness luxury meets depravity. This tale where encounters are never innocent...from serial killers to magicians...will bring you to the edge of sanity.
