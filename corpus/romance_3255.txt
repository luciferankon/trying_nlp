In a little rural town in Paraguay lives Virgilia a young street *chipa vendor who is in love with Walter her closest childhood friend but Virgilia fears she will loose her friend if she tells him how she really feels. *Chipa: A type of small baked cheese-flavored rolls a popular snack and breakfast food in Paraguay. It is quite cheap and often sold from streetside stands and on buses by vendors carrying a large basket with the warm chipa wrapped in a cloth.
