Love is Blind. This is a story between two cousins that were best friends all of their lives. Throughout the story Trina (girl cousin) finds new love and Jason (the husband) starts to make his move. Montez (male cousin) feels throughout their marriage the husband is trying to get him out of the picture. While Jason does a good job getting rid of Montez and Trina on his side Montez randomly finds a secret about Jason that he tries to reveal to his cousin by the end of this Love Cycle.
