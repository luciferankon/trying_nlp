Written and produced by Minerva Vier and Debbie Jaffe Wingin' It is a dark comedy about the misadventures of two alienated and desperate women on the brink of nervous breakdowns. Deb is a neurotic socialite hitting the skids. She could have been one of the stars of The Real Housewives of Beverly Hills if only her ex-fiancee did not dump her right before their perfect wedding. Min is an emotional mess of an artist whose suicidal tendencies have flared up since her ex-boyfriend a Charlie Manson wannabe left her for his doped-up groupies. Bonding over their misery Deb and Min find a mutual shoulder to cry on and embark on a roller-coaster adventure together in their quest to heal their broken hearts.
