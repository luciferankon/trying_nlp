Bergen op Zoom. A city in the Netherlands near the Belgian border. For generations the Van Dort family fishes here for anchovy with traditional methods dating from 1612. Only in the moths May June and July they can catch the fish which secures their existence year after year. But this years catch is worse than ever before. Will the family survive this bad year?
