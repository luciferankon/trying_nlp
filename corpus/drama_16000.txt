Sweetwater is the story of two young women whom join forces to find a cure for cancer but soon realize their lives may be in danger if they expose what they discover. Focusing on cancer the film plays part political/corporate thriller interspersed with elements of fractured friendship redemption and family bonds. Sweetwater suggests that while a cure is possible or already exists the powers that be (medical pharmaceutical charitable) find it in their financial interests not to reveal the solution. Would you risk your own life to save the lives of millions? Our hero's would...
