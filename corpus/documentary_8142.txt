Interweaving portraits of the works of Ettore Ximenez Luiz Brizzolara Felicia Leirner and other sculptors the documentary features the amazing sculptures spread in the city of Săo Paulo and Campos do Jordăo in Brazil and the huge and difficult work of those who struggle to make them "survive" in interviews with technicians and researchers of the teams of the Department of Conservation and Heritage of Săo Paulo the State Art Gallery Civil Guard officers private restorers and more.
