1000CC Raw Thrill is a Sport-bike Lifestyle motorcycle show hosted by long time Motorcycle Industry insider Greg White and Erin Bates of AMA Supercross fame. 1000cc Raw Thrill introduces and entertains the audience about the world of Sport-bike riding and all that it has to offer. The show features in-studio interviews from some of the industry's top experts and riders tech tips the latest in sport-bike technology stories from around the United States and more.
