Sara Jane wife of a wealthy farmer in 1912 is blameless upright and beloved in her rural town of Kilkenny Georgia. Over a short span after the arrival of a stranger Kane and his two companions she loses her husband children cattle and her land becomes infertile. The town turns against her fearing she has done some evil to deserve this fate. As she fights the torment from Kane the town's relentless ridicule and the hovering bankers threatening to strip away her home and land she reaffirms her drive to overcome this devastation and perseveres as no one could have imagined. When she finds herself on the brink of losing it all her path is revealed. She remains true to her faith overcoming deceit and the lure of the stranger who is determined to win a bet with God.
