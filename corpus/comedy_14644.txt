Every night Ambroise is bored. And to kill boredom he calls randomly. His calls take us to the heart of a game of poker with friends where the comic and the tragic are tutelage in a loft where seduction no longer operates under shelter bus refuge souls in search. But Ambroise sometimes meets a woman alone with a listening ear. Seduction in manipulation he then leads the conversation on the ground of intimacy. It disturbs but intrigues it offers the promise of an extraordinary adventure. As a final strategist he always manipulates and touches the most sensitive point. In this field he always wins .
