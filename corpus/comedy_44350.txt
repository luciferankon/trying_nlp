The Retributioners is a comedic loosely scripted web series presented from the perspective of a vindictive woman. Stephanie is a happily married accomplished woman who feels that she has not yet proved herself to the world. She decides to contact past boyfriends former friends teachers skinny people people who stole her taxi etc. to seek retribution. Each episode will feature a new guest star. The result: a really funny witty sarcastic uncomfortable confrontation with many possible surprises and endings.
