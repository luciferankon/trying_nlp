Mouse is a thirteen year old British Pakistani boy with a brilliant mind but an unfortunately small stature which often brings him unwanted attention from the schoolyard bullies. Thankfully he's got his best mate Luke by his side as well as the love and affection of his tight knit family. But when circumstances leave Mouse alone and in charge he's forced to rely on his intelligence to defend himself and his family's honour - without arousing the suspicions of the world around him.
