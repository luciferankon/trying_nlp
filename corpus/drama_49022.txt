Mountain girl Trigger Hicks a fierce loner equally handy with a rock or a prayer is in danger of having her faith-healing mistaken for witchcraft by the neighbors. She shows a vulnerable side when John Stafford a philandering engineer working on a new dam is attracted to her. Matters come to a head when Trigger "rescues" an abused baby from its parents and seeks help from the engineers. Trigger Hicks a young faith-healing mountain girl with a drunk father who lives her life by bible saying she carries in her pocket is treated as a witch by the local folk. And the two young engineers working on the local dam become interested in Trigger and her troubles.
