Chain - is a story about three friends. Two of them have been friends since childhood. They both serve in "the hot spot" there they meet Stepan who joined their company. Back from the army the friends decide to open their own autoservice center. After that a chain of events happens in their life and it would have a great influence on their relations. Every hero has its skeleton in the cupboard and their stories are inseparably connected with past and future like the links of one chain...
