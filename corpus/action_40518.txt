While super-hero movies are the sensation of today truth is they have been a major recurring genre starting from the 30's serials well into to the 60's pop and 70's Grindhouse eras. While Hollywood presented us with Blaxsploitation variations and historical heroes Europe and Asia turned to costumed vigilantes of all kinds to attract audiences of all ages. Insane super-heroes cheesy spies mythological warriors Kung-Fu masters this compilation has it all.
