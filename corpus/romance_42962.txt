A young gay man and a self-professed straight hustler are holed up in a cheap motel room. The young man wants to get laid. The hustler needs a place to hide. As they snort coke smoke crack and get to know each other a sexual cat and mouse game is played and the line between who's using whom - and for what exactly -- is blurred. During a passionate moment however a kiss - an undeniable physical and sexual act - between the men brings their true desires to a boil and major clash.
