Old-world values are challenged by new-world technology when a café manager strikes up the unlikeliest of friendships with a co-worker's young daughter. Archaic traditions and new technology collide when a close friendship emerges between Frankie a thirty-something bachelor and Alessia a much younger woman who is frustrated with the rooted Italian ways of her ever-present family. Tension stirs and complications arise as they both work at the Montreal café owned by Alessia's Family. The story builds as their connection grows around clandestine messaging while their smartphones become the key to keeping their relationship a secret. Meanwhile they deal with the constant scrutiny of their friends and family and inevitably keep finding themselves in dangerous situations.
