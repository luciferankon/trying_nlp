KVL the Knight of Valor and Leadership is the notorious lacrosse fraternity at Royce College. Kicked off-campus they are now unsupervised and out-of-control. Guy Foreman ignores their reputation because he sees KVL as his ticket onto the party scene. He enlists the help of several friends to get him on the lacrosse team and into the frat. But once inside pledging KVL proves to be crazier than he ever imagined.
