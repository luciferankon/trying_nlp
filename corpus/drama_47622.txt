Death's Door is a medical drama based on the book At Death's Door and the true-life story of Sebastian Sepulveda M.D. Death's Door tells the story of an end-of-life-care doctor Dr. Mark Corbin. Dr. Corbin's entire career has been dealing with the terminally ill and dying. The deaths slowly take their toll on everyone around: first on Dr. Corbin's staff then his family and even himself.
