Just when Spider-Man thought it was safe to relax he's summoned by the famous Colonel Nick Fury of S.H.I.E.L.D. Fury has had his eye on Spider-Man for quite some time and now he's being recruited to help take on a world wide threat unlike anything they've ever seen. Spider-Man will journey to the far corners of the earth teaming up with a host of super hero friends and some of his most famous enemies to ultimately bring down an Evil Mastermind who threatens to change their world forever.
