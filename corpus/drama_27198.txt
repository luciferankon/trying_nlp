The USA Secret Service office assigned to protect the USA President hires a female agent for unlimited protection duties equivalent in danger and physical demands to work traditionally carried on my male US Secret Service workers over previous history. The new female agent is noticed by other staffers who don't agree she should have been hired. She encounters challenges and problems in the course of her work and gets help and also has trouble from unexpected people and situations. This TV movie was a pilot for a planned TV series which wasn't "picked up" and used after it's completion.
