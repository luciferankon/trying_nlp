Barristers. They are young educated soft on the inside hard where it counts. Don t let the wigs fool you when you stand alone in court when your life is on the line these are the people you want between you and the jury. The North Square chambers are finding their feet fighting for a large piece of the criminal justice business in Leeds. Working for his barristers or maybe the other way around is their manipulative chief clerk. Ruthless obsessive with a razor sharp mind and a cosh-like sensibility Peter McLeish loves the business and the business loves him.
