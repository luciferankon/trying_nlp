Skip Frazee a legend in the audio/sound business with 55 years of experience speaks with Andy Chiles Director at North Lake College's Video Technology division. Frazee was Chiles mentor years ago and this intimate talk is very informative. Frazee is a member of SMPTE AES IATSE and ACFEI. Frazee has won a "Grammy" and an "Emmy" and answers the questions of the North Lake College students during this talk. Frazee now owns Sound One and Forensic Sound and has much to offer concerning digital and the new domain.
