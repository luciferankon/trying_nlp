A veteran at home and adrift Joe seeks to turn his war memories into hip-hop lyrics with the reluctant help of Abdul a teen down the street. They swap rhymes and stories Joe eager to use Abdul's songwriting talents to help exorcise his demons Abdul slowly appreciating that Joe's first-hand experience with violence makes him a legit storyteller. When Joe's life unravels with the discovery of his affair with his girlfriend's sister music is about all Joe has left. When Abdul goes too far trying to be real Joe - now a newly commissioned police officer - must choose between his hip-hop collaboration and keeping the job he loves.
