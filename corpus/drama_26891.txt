A prisoner of war doctor Bertram has just been repatriated .He learns that his fiancee was killed in a bombing.When he meets a young girl who is exactly like her he cannot believe his eyes.Her name is Hilda : Bertram's fiancee got married when he was reported missing and she is her own daughter. The doctor is a gynecologist;Hilda is a model and she is wooed by a bad boy Kurt .Whereas the doctor falls for an attractive young widowHilda gets jealous and becomes an easy prey for Kurt.
