Portraits of Emotion is a feature length documentary film that follows over the course of four years the life of 14-year-old Jonathan Lerman an artistic prodigy who is diagnosed with autism a lifelong developmental disability that affects learning communication and social interaction. While capable of drawing astonishing portraits that capture the nuances of human emotion Jonathan is unable to effectively verbally communicate his feelings.
