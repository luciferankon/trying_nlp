Mel Sobel is a shameless philanderer and con artist who is partners in a successful retail operation known to Southern California TV viewers as The Mattress Guys. Unbeknownst to the Mattress Guy he is a double for the FBI's most wanted fugitive the Imam Abdul Muhammed Zaleeka. Mel's compulsive lying and chicanery plunge him into a web of further deceit and complications when the search for the Imam expands to Homeland Security and the CIA. Not to mention a few dozen women he has seduced and abandoned on his mattresses.
