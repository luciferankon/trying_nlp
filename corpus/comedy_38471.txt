Late night talk show battles explode as a controversial host is brought to a declining network to save the 11:30 hour. The staff and executives must adapt to his unorthodox methods of working behind the scenes before each show and the guests and crew get crazier and crazier as they are thrown into a "Zoolander" world of eccentric and baffling egos in this parody of current headlines in the battle for "King of Late Night".
