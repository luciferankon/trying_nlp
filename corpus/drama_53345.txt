Two teenage sisters Katherine (Kitty) and Christine (Teeny) Burrows are from the Mennonite religious sect that restrains from influences of contemporary society. Their grandmother has recently passed away & they are given the task of driving her body to another Mennonite community to be buried. As they travel throughout the southern states with her body in the bed of a pickup truck they meet a cast of colorful characters along the way and discover the new world through a vacant and modern landscape.
