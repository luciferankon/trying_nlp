Joe is the head of an itinerant combine crew working the harvests against rival crew boss Alperson. Joe's buddy Jim joins the crew with startup money. Farmer's niece Fay falls for Joe. He puts her off. To get back she marries Jim whom she prods into high-grading the grain (skimming off some for private sale). The last payment on Joe's machinery is due just as he discover's what his buddy has been doing. Joe Madigan is the boss of a harvesting crew which includes his buddies Jim Davs and "Kink" that follow the wheat-harvesting from the panhandle of Texas north through Oklahoma Kansas Nebraska Colorado and into the Dakotas each season. On their first farm-stop the farmer's niece Fay Rankin falls hard for Joe but he isn't interested in anything but reaping wheat. So she plies her wiles on Jim and joins as Joe and his crew move their combines northward. While keeping her eye on Joe and letting him know she is available she influences Jim to start skimming wheat from the customers so there will be some extra money to keep her in the style she desires to become accustomed to. When Jim is caught high-grading his buddies flee with him across the state line. There Fay tells Jim she only married him in order to be close to Joe and Jim and Joe have a fight about it until they both conclude she isn't worth fight over and they go off together and leave her standing alone in Nebraska.
