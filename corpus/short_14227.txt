The story of this psychological horror movie is based on a terrifying encounter between Robert C. Hansen and Kitty Larson that took place in 1981. Two years later Hansen confessed to the murder of 17 women. Robert is portrayed as a restless soul with a insatiable desire to remain absolute control. Being in control excites him. With every new adventure he promises himself to bring the woman back home safely but he doesn't realize that his obsessive mind demands the same emotional excitement he experienced the first time he killed a woman. And that means taking risks. He chooses Lisa. From the moment that Robert and Lisa arrive on his hunting ground Robert walks the delicate line between having control and loosing it. The first signs of violence on Lisa awakens the spirits of his former victims. They come to her aid to make his blood run could.
