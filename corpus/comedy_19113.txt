Louis Stainval a humble car washer at Mr. and Mrs. Grillot's garage hits the lottery jackpot. It is the end of hardboiled egg time for him. One day he helps a stranger who has just tried to kill himself for the umpteenth time. This man Grandvivier is a mediocre painter whose work is depressingly worthless. But the artist has produced a unique masterpiece at least in Louis'eyes his daughter Lucie. Falling in love with her at first sight Louis soon decides to please his future father-in-law and works out a scheme. He invents the character of Van Patten a rich buyer who has developed a burning passion for Grandvivier's art and poses as a go-between between the two men. Visit after visit Louis buys a picture in Van Patten's name which he immediately throws into the river...
