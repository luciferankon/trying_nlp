In the age of manscaping metrosexuals and grooming products galore - what does it mean to be a man? Oscar nominee 'Morgan Spurlock' (qv) and executive producers Ben Silverman 'Will Arnett' (qv) and 'Jason Bateman' (qv) present a delightfully entertaining doc featuring candid interviews from 'Will Arnett' (qv) 'Jason Bateman' (qv) 'Paul Rudd (I)' (qv) 'Zach Galifianakis' (qv) and everyday people weighing in on everything from the obsession with facial hair to body dysmorphic disorder.
