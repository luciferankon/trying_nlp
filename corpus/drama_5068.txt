Cristina a young emerging photographer dreams to do a reportage on Famagusta on the "Ghost-town" where all access is prohibited. Despite the difficulties in finding a new job and her personal life Cristina helps her friend Marina to overcome her own "fear to face life" that kept her on a bed for a long time. Cristina's ways to cheer her friend up will help Marina improve her health condition. As Marina improves Cristina is always more determined to follow her dreams no matter what.
