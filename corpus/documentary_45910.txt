Ben Stewart and Dan Stewart creators of Esoteric Agenda and Kymatica focus the essence of this film around the journey through transitional and groundbreaking events in a human's life. At a major juncture in his life rob in the page family began down a road less traveled to free himself from fear government control and reliance on public energy and food supply and shared his journey that led him off the grid. The purpose of this film is not to give legal advice nor is it to simply educate but rather as inspirational fuel. To become more of what we already are.
