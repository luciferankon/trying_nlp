Mark Rabbetts Changing Britain. is a hard hitting documentary four part mini-series which follows Mark Rabbetts a self-made millionaire who after personal tragedy of losing two older brothers and a failed marriage tuned to drugs for comfort. The series picks up on Marks story after he managed to beat his ketamine dug addiction.Mark now believes that he is a prophet from God here to change the world starting with Britain. Mark believes that his father "God" has had enough of the world's wicked ways and plans to make changes to our society for the better. Mark has given thousands of pounds of his own money away to people he believes are more deserving and in need. Asked how he will make such changes Mark says he plans to become the Prime Minister and run the country under God's will and law.
