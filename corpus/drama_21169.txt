David Wilde is a concert violist on the brink of worldwide success. Obsessed with his career and music he has ignored all parts of life from family to friends. But when he has a stroke and as a result loses the ability to play the viola he is forced to find out who he truly is apart from music. He must face the parts of his life that he has ignored. Now he must make a choice between his new found relationship with his family or his old obsession with music and career.
