Cleo a research chemist accidentally creates a substance that turns Jacqueline her boss into a bi-polar bondage fiend - first she ropes and gags the astonished Cleo then begs submissively to be tied and silenced then becomes a raging dominatrix again. Cleo's efforts to find an antidote are curtailed when animal rights terrorists attack the researchers' laboratory and leave the hapless scientists in a desperate predicament.
