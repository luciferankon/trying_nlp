The 365 docobites team travelled through 5 continents 30 countries and 70 cities to make 365 short documentaries over 365 days. They released a new short documentary each and every day for a year featuring a new story from a stranger they met on the streets of the cities of the world. A series that celebrates humanity in all it's diversity and invites viewers to come along the journey with them and get to know the Strangers of the world.
