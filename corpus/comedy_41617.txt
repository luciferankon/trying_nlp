The School of rock faces the loss of its funding from an overly uptight philanthropist who just doesn't know how to rock out while a growing zombie threat eats away at students and teachers alike. The story kicks off when a time machine crashes into a chemical research facility in Hamilton NJ. When the kids get a chance to open up for the band Fosterchild The School of Rock students and faculty have to fight their way through every hardship to get to the concert night. Will they make it?
