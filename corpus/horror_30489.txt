A young child Nick Johnson was the only surviving victim of a horrible crime. His entire family was viciously murdered. Some say he was responsible for the homicide and that sinister forces influenced him. Some say that he was left alone by the killers. He was institutionalized at a mental asylum for over 20 years. Since then his family home the scene of the horrendous crimes had become a grim gathering place for teenagers who would attend parties there on Halloween night. With a lack of evidence and because of state requirements Nick is released and prepares to return to his family home. And an unsuspecting group of young people are planning to party in his house on the scariest night of the year.
