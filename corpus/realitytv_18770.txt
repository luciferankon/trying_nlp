Follows three teams of America's best relic hunters competing head-to-head-to-head in a race armed with metal detectors to discover the most valuable artifacts in a location from forts to historical landmarks. Once on site the teams must use all their resources and hustle to ensure their victory - and paycheck. At the end of the hunt our modern day treasure hunters will have their finds evaluated to learn more about the piece of history they've uncovered and the price tag that goes with it.
