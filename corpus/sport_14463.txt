In The Game - High School Sports features high school athletes in the southern region of Georgia; showcasing their skill determination & teamwork throughout a season. ITG gives an inside look at a different team each season showing the hard work from both the athletes and the coach. The show also features a "Player Profile" in which Mark Dykes the host chooses an athlete every week that has shown outstanding performances either athletically or personally. In The Game - High School Sports is currently on WTXL & WSWG (ABC & CBS affiliates) and also showcases on Mediacom On Demand.
