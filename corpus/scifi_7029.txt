E.A.R.T.H. Force is an American action adventure television series starring Gil Gerard. The series aired 3 episodes on CBS from September 16 1990 to September 29 1990 before being canceled due to low ratings.[1] The show is about an elite group the Earth Alert Research Tactical Headquarters (E.A.R.T.H.) that was brought together by a dying millionaire to prevent environmental disasters around the world.
