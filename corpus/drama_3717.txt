When Hollywood said NO she said YES and guerrilla filmmaker Migdia Chinea with her whatever-it-takes attitude took on the arduous task of making the poignant period piece WHEN IT RAINS. Two years in the making Migdia set the stage to carve out a piece of iconic Los Angeles post sexual revolution history. Her neo noir film revolves around actress/photographer Miranda Maar her lovers her friends and their aspirations during two weeks of the worst rain storm in Hollywood 1972.
