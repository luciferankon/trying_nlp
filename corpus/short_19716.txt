Binary is a documentary about the virtual world and relationships that arise from it and how man operated human relations behind a mask. The documentary focuses on men and his way of relating with each other. Today humans have lost the need to interact face to face. Now there is a new way to talk meet and interact much faster and instantly. The phenomenon of social networking has found a solution to a latent need in people: exhibitionism and the need to disseminate opinions and tastes. It is the need to express to the world be important and influential.
