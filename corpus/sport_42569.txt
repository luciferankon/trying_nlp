Award-winning sports broadcasterTony Leodora brings viewers on visual golf trips to the most beautiful destinations across the country and around the world. The golf and lifestyle show -- after airing exclusively online for two years -- features the historic Shawnee Inn and Golf Resort in the Pocono Mountains of Pennsylvania. The popular Philadelphia and New York tourist destination is featured in this first episode for NBC Universal Comcast SportsNet and The Comcast Network based in Philadelphia.
