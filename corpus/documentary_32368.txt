A former Elite model reveals how the worlds of fashion advertising and celebrity impact teens and young women. Who sets today's standards for beauty and how are these standards affecting our girls and society? Who is responsible? Are there ways this can be changed? If so who can/will change it? Startling interviews with fashion editors from Teen Vogue and ELLE magazine. Intimate interviews with top agents designers models and many more. An important issue covered is the sexualization of young girls in the media/advertising. Why are young girls obsessed with their looks and being skinny? Who sets the standards for beauty? Why do they set the bar so high? Can it be changed? The documentary explores the influence and pressure media has on young girls and women. Shocking interviews with editors from ELLE & Teen Vogue magazines along with heart touching commentary from children. Pparents teachers and body image experts lend their voices to this eye-opening film.
