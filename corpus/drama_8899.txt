JUNE 6 is an episode-film consisting of five distinctively different film-films each separately titled as well as produced and directed by five highly revered film Japanese film directors. Each of the five films contributed concerns a tragedy which either occurred on the 6th of June in history - or - as in Muroga Atsushi's episode entitled "Kill Pigs" - a sci-fi glimpse of an apocalyptic tragedy inspired by the likes or John Carpenter's "They Live" and Ridley Scott's "Bladerunner".
