ON THE EDGE is an award-winning series of 29 short verite films all originated in HDTV that explore with sensitivity and wit the various subcultures in New York and Los Angeles. Shorts from the series have been honored at numerous international film festivals including the Sundance Film Festival New York Film Festival and many others. Titles include "Rave On" the first HD-originated film to be nominated at Sundance; "Outside Society" about the homeless community that resided in New York City's Amtrak Tunnel; "The 14 Words" about neo-Nazis in Southern California and others.
