This unique insightful drama offers an intense view of prison life and the lives of those connected to the outside world. It is a prison wherein the lives of male and female inmates constantly cross one another for better or for worse. The lives of those within the prison as well as those profoundly connected to them offer shattering stories. This film now completing final negotiations will move to production early 2017. It will be a pilot for a series dealing with the intense drama of male/female prisoners and the working staff within the prisons. Life inside and outside the prison will fuel profound powerful stories about the controversial working of prisons today and those who undertake those incredibly challenging professions.
