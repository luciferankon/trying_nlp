The first of its kind this is a film of courage beauty wisdom and hope. It tells the story of the Findhorn Foundation Community through the eyes of the people. This 70 minute documentary features the voices of residents fellows and visitors to the Foundation over the past 52 years. The inventive weaving between colorful images and words takes you on a journey from the inception of the Foundation in 1962 and follows the various stages of its transformation to the present date. The film includes rare archive footage and a beautiful and exclusive interview with Dorothy McLean one of the founders.
