AS THE CALL SO THE ECHO tells the story of an American surgeon who volunteers in a poverty stricken hospital in central Vietnam. When a woman with a deadly cancer is presented to him he must struggle to save her life fighting to overcome a language barrier limited equipment and his deepest fears.
