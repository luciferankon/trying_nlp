Sarah Mitchell a loyal and loving young wife has just become a widow. Her husband's suicide is shrouded in mystery and Sarah is forced to grieve her husband without understanding the truth about his death. A year later Sarah's friends Kim and Michelle plan a weekend at a remote cabin for some laughs to help her recover. Unbeknownst to Sarah Kim and Michelle's husbands Jeff and Joey have invited Scott Walton. Scott and Sarah have met in the past but their meeting did not end on good terms. As expected sparks fly when they come face to face at the cabin. Sarah is determined to avoid Scott if at all possible. But as strange and horrific things start happening at the cabin Sarah is forced to face the mystery of her husband's death. Friendships and relationships are tested and Sarah soon learns that Scott might be one of the few people she can trust. The characters and story are inspired by true events.
