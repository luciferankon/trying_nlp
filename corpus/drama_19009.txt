Nicole Garcia's film shows a family through an adult man's figure and his weekdays. The main character is one of the sons of an old and ill man who is in hospital nowadays. The father has 2 other sons too. Jean Paul visits his father all day. After he divorced his life is unexciting and troubled. He has a lot of dark suits a good car a big house but he has some debts and his private life touch bottom. He has to call his brothers and ask them to help ...
