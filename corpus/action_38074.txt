Dattu a gangster enters the gang of Ali bhai and starts to work for him. Meanwhile he falls for a girl named Anjali who also falls for him. But as she realizes that Dattu is a gangster she asks him to quit these violent activities for her love. But Dattu himself refuses and says that he loves her and can't fulfill her wish. But love continues between them. Meanwhile Ali bhai gets kidnapped by the police but the gang rescues him by threatening the officer. They feel relieved but what they don't realize is that there's an undercover cop in their gang - they have no idea who he could be. No one has an idea about this mystery and the cop's motive. But when they come to know who's the cop they are left shocked. To find out who's the cop and how does he fulfill his responsibility watch this action packed film Porki.
