Four young Americans travel to Europe to work as counsellors for a summer camp there. Before the kids arrive however a rage-inducing demonic disease spread rapidly from animal to person resulting in the counsellors desperately trying to escape the illness. However the camp is in the wilderness and with the phones out and cars wrecked how are they going to get to safety? Four American summer camp councellors get more than they bargained for in an old manor in Spain when a synthetic water-born fungal disease turns them into savage killers in the Spanish wilderness they are without contact and without help to make matters worse they aren't alone.
