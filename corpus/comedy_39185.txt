When an intrepid journalist CRAIG HORWITZ meets up with M a mysterious whistle-blower from the Mayonnaise cartel he is taken on a historical journey through a seedy condimental underworld. Horwitz starts to fully comprehend the stranglehold Big Mayo has on modern American politics revealing a much larger truth to his investigation: It's no longer about what they're doing to our body. It's about what they're doing to our country!
