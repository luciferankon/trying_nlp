The harrowing story delivered in fully immersive 4-Dimensional format of a 9th century Chinese merchant vessel stranded at sea while attempting to transport an ornate wedding gift from the Chinese Emperor to the Sultanate of Oman. Weathering a fierce south pacific typhoon the ship journeys on valiantly attempting to avoid a labyrinth of rocky formations as it is constantly buffeted by waves. Disaster strikes as the ship crashes separating a proud father from his only son. Audience members are treated to cold blasts and water sprays among many other stunning effects as they join the adventure and learn the fate of these two lost souls.
