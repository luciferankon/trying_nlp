A gambler decides to play one last game before he turns over a new leaf. However during the game one of the players accuses him of cheating. Suddenly the lights go out shots are fired and when the lights come back on one of the players is dead. The gambler is accused of the killing. He didn't do it but has to find out who did and why he was framed for it. When house dealer Jack O'Diamonds wins a ranch deed the owner accuses him of cheating and when the lights go out the owner is shot. Jack thought he fired at the owner's gun and doesn't know if he killed him. Planning to return the deed he goes to the ranch and becomes foreman and is attracted to the daughter. But then the real killer tells the daughter that Jack killed her father.
