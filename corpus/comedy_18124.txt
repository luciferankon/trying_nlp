Scheming agents sketchy directors and cringe-worthy auditions are only the beginning of actress Cheryl Texiera's hilarious journey in pursuit of her dream. Loosely based on her real-life experiences Cheryl shares a very tongue-in-cheek point of view on the state of today's entertainment industry with her witty dialogue Broadway/Vaudeville song and dance numbers and an array of colorful characters you won't soon forget. She sings she dances she acts - but she can't get cast. Unabashed unapologetic and unquestionably hilarious Confessions of a Bitter(sweet) Actress takes its audience on a journey inside Cheryl Texiera's world as she navigates the ironically tumultuous waters of the very-fickle entertainment industry.
