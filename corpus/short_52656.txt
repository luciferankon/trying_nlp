In collaboration with the Black Out for Human Rights Collective UR Media presents "Pixán: The Ghost of Góngora". An experimental Poetic Essay honoring Luis Góngora Pat a homeless undocumented Mayan immigrant who was murdered by the San Francisco Police Department on April 7th 2016.
