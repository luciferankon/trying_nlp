Air Racer is the true story of husband-and-wife team Jon and Patricia Sharp who risk everything to create the most advanced airplane ever made for air racing. Their bold new design the Nemesis NXT is a two-seat propeller-driven airplane that can fly as fast as a jet airliner. The Sharps hope to revolutionize air racing as they test fly their machine for the first time and brave fierce competition from some of the best pilots in the world at the Reno National Championship Air Races.
