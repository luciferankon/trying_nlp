Five men trapped in the basement vault of an office building share visions with each other of their demise. Stories revolve around vampires bodily dismemberment east Indian mysticism an insurance scam and an artist who kills by painting his victims' deaths. Based on the top horror comic's of the 1950's-The Vault of Horror was based on one of E.C.Comic's 3 Top titles.The other two include world famous Tales Of The Crypt"also made into a similar movie" and The Haunt Of Fear.This movie has 5 short stories simular to the comic book format.
