A conciliate young man entangles himself with an assertive young woman. After a passionate fling he thinks he found the woman he can bring home to mother she thinks she's found herself a scapegoat. As the young man is dragged into her murderous plan the duo try to cover up their tracks. Soon their perfect execution turns to chaos as the ones closest to them are put in danger by someone or something that's seeking revenge. In a race against time their patience wilt as they're constantly antagonized by the stalking of an unknown stranger.
