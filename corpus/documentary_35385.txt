In the best traditions of the classic Pathe Gaumont and British Movietone newsreels we present a selection of events from a historic eventful and sometimes very difficult year for the 2nd Battalion The Royal Regiment Of Fusiliers and its proud ancestor XXth The Lancashire Fusiliers. Featuring parades speeches and exclusive interviews with a variety of dignitaries soldiers (serving and veterans) and officers this film is a must for anyone and everyone with an interest or connection to the regiment.
