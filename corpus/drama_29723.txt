The Governor and Lady Bo Luo are in the midst of protecting their homeland from hostile neighboring provinces. However their biggest battles of all seem to come from within their tranquil walls. Loyalties honour and courage are tested not just in those closest to them but between the rulers themselves. With seemingly no end to the brutality and betrayals a cryptic plot of destruction ensues between the rulers and their foes. Watch to see whose trust truly runs the deepest amidst the temptations of lust power and ambition to rule The Wild Orchards.
