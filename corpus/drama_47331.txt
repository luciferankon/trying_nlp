Zorg (Mark Wijsman) an 8 year old boy is a very sensitive and intelligent boy. One day his father who is a hard-working stressful man suffers from a heart attack just after he had a phone call with his boss Boshart. The father is brought to a hospital and meanwhile Zorg invites the boss to his home for dinner and prepares together with his 80-year old neighbor who seems to have magic powers a meal with rats maggots and other dirty things.
