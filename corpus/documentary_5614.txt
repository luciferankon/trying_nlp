With Photographers Kapil Arn Fiona Clark Bruce Connew Marti Friedlander Gil Hanly Robert (Tom) Hutchins John Miller and Ans Westra this documentary looks at their photo images and the events and social unrest that took place in New Zealand from the 1950's up to the bombing of the Rainbow Warrior. Issues of homelessness and housing in New Zealand the right to protest regarding Maori land and coastline the French Nuclear tests in the Pacific and the Anti-Apartheid Springbok Tour events of 1981 the funeral of Norman Kirk finding democracy in South Africa and observing workers in China; street-photographers of importance.
