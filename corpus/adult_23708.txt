While reading a book about a serial killer a lovely redhead speaks his name thereby summoning the spirit. The ghost turns out to have a penchant for bondage and quickly binds and gags the woman who becomes even more frightened when he reveals her fate though he disappears before he follows through on his threats. The spook reappears with alarming frequency each time binding the woman more tightly and threatens to bring his visitations with the woman to a fatal end. Will the buxom bondagette escape and find a way to break the curse?
