Alexander Way a secondary school teacher with a fractured personal life finds himself attempting to rescue a woman from a violent sexual attack. Alexander finds himself overpowered beaten & taken to hospital. Jesiah Jarvus a famous British musician who is frequently touring has lost his goal in life. Having not spoken with his father in over 20 years troubling news offers them the chance to reconcile & move forward in both their lives. Theodore Smith attempts to bury the sadness that threatens to engulf his childhood upon hearing the news that his parents will be separating. Elethea Conall a newly qualified nurse continues to look after her patients whilst attempting to escape from her past. What do they all have in common?
