A travel adventure show hosted by British born extreme athlete Squash Falconer. Squash is an extreme athlete and world traveler with a knack for instantaneous friendships and a penitent for extreme adventure. For Squash these aren't mutually exclusive concepts. At any given moment she is making new friends that inevitably lead to her next adventure. In each episode Squash Falconer trains and competes in a bizarre physical challenge from a unique place in the world. The challenge is the hook and entry point into the exploration of new places food and culture told through the eyes of Squash as she interacts with her environment. Audiences will love watching Squash hearing her thoughts in travel-diary style narration all with a dash of humor that Squash brings to the table amongst her diversity of extreme talents.
