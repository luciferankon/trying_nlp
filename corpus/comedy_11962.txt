Another Castle is an original web series that follows two best geek friends TJ and Barrett as they pursue their passions in nerdom. TJ is a play along channel superstar who has just gotten her first opportunity to take her gamer profile to the next level with the help of Barrett a struggling artist who is trying to find herself through her work and a team of bad ass diverse geek women. The series alternates between a fantasy comic book world and reality featuring two original female super heroes. Through quick wit and a plethora of geek-filled references we follow the girls through love and loss career challenges and successes and the many challenges women face in a male-dominated subculture.
