Above burning the sun is a feature film that narrates a story by means of documentary as well as fictional images. Juana a young woman in her twenties sets out on a backpacking trip to the northwest of Argentina. Her aim is to find some places and people that appear in a series of photographs that she carries with her. As the adventure goes by Juana finds out that the deepest meaning of the trip was but the simple fact that she has travelled through the experience itself.
