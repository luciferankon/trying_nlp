Dave McCue a stay-at-home father of three sets of twins and Kip Kessler a Christian comedy greeting card maker form a comedy improv troupe. After holding auditions to add to the troupe they begin performing for live audiences. However chemistry in the group doesn't exist and it hasn't occured to anyone that they don't really have talent. This mockumentary follows the pair as they try to make their mark in the world of improv comedy culminating in a tournament at the DC Improv.
