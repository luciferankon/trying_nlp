Gyonshi is a flashback to the creature movies of the 1980's. It's a story about two college kids Jimmy (Adrian Zaw) and Matt (Chris Beard) and how they get tangled up in releasing an ancient Chinese vampire a Gyonshi. As the night grows long and the deaths surmount the kids must put a stop to the Gyonshi. Unknown to them the Gyonshi is changing and all the ancient Chinese techniques are failing.
