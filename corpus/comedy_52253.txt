Most people probably think the life of an online video blogger is pretty straightforward stuff: sit in front of a webcam and talk about pointless crap. It's not though. Just like in real life there's a sinister underbelly of social media a dark side that can drive anyone mad. Especially when you get as many followers as Paul gets and especially when you have as many haters as Paul has. It truly is just like real life; the strong survive the weak get exploited and if you go and mess with the natural order of things well let's just say you don't want to be that guy.
