Ed Loomis is a man on the streets trying to find his way through life looking for a new opportunity to redeem himself in society. Until one evening he finds himself conrnered and in a dire stuation for his life. Not believing it he is killed and brought back as a Vampire. This is a story of "transition" from life to the undead and its trials and tribulations. Follow Ed and his fellow clan members as they confront the challenges that come within their shadowed society. We all live we all die only the unfortunate transition.
