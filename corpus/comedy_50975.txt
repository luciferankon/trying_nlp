A young writer named Seth (Alex Gasbarro) sets out to find a Film Producer to Help Him Bring His ideas to the Big Screen. Instead He Finds Producer Andy Fineman (Mark F. Ott) who has his own Ideas about what a Film should be. On their Very First Meeting Andy "Convinces" Seth to "Keep an open Mind" and he and his assistant are soon suggesting a whirlwind of changes to the script. Small "Adjustments" to script setting. Dialogue and even the Plot itself. Not the least of which is replacing the Female lead with a beautiful Young Girl.
