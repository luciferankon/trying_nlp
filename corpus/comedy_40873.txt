Cancelled after only one episode was telecast this sitcom created to capitalize on the success of "National Lampoon's Animal House" was to chronicle the antics at Baxter College a former all-girls school which recently converted to co-education. Brewster House was a dormitory populated by a number of young men and women notably shy Tucker preppy Doug nutty Gobo wholesome Elizabeth and sexy Sandi. Mrs. Selby was the looney house-mother.
