Jamaican Gold is the longest running Reggae club in California. This spectacular film recorded from July 2006 to Summer 2007 features Reggae and Dancehall Superstars Beenie Man Elephant Man Damian Marley Cham Lady Saw Luciano Spragga Benz Jr Reid Richie Spice Cecile and Mad Cobra. Witness the artists as never before in all their splendor performing at the legendary Jamaican Gold club
