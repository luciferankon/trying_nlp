The Simple lives of Chandu & Nandu two street-smart extortionists change into a roller coaster ride when they steal a 201 carat 'Moghul diamond' and loose it only to be found by a friendly duo Ajay & Vijay who must somehow arrange 100000 rupees which they loose on a 'teen-patti' card match to save their lives from their boss Diljaan Kolgate who is fondly called Boss D.K. Faltu Utpatang Chutpati Kahani is a light hearted time-pass film a crime-comedy-thriller that gives a glimpse of the fast paced and ever-changing Mumbai lifestyle through a looking glass from the other side.
