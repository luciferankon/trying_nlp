To Change Our Future We Must First Change Our Past First. In 2240 Disorder and chaos dominate our society fuelled by fear and absolute control by the global united syndicate. There is a small number of people that call themselves the resistance who believe that the world that we have destroyed it can be reversed and we can give ourselves a second chance to start over and change the course of history by going back in time. The resistance has found ancient blueprints of a time displacement machine designed to send people back and forward through time but in the wrong hands this can have catastrophic consequences.
