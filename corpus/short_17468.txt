Feminist theory is one of the ways to keep questioning everything that's taken for granted natural eternal and unchangeable while at the same time examining how such constructions narrow the perception of reality. Inspired by many years of engagement at BeFem Milica and Natasa decide to take their cell phones and hear the voices of different women in order to learn how they feel but also how they fight against existing gender roles. Inspired by a pair of stockings Jodie decides to share something about herself.
