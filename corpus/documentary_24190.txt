89 Steps: A Chapter of Living Los Sures In the late 1950s Marta's mother found refuge for her family in Williamsburg after leaving her village in Puerto Rico and enduring homelessness and hunger elsewhere in New York. When Marta became a single mother she fought hard to stay in Los Sures. Now struggling to afford the rapidly gentrifying neighborhood Marta must decide whether to stay or go. 89 Steps is an interactive experience that visits Los Sures. As the viewer explores Marta's voiceover reacts providing guidance descriptions and anecdotes. The project offers a deeper understanding of the pressures and incentives that force individuals to give up their homes and longstanding communities.
