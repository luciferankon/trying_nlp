Psychological Thriller about the volatile way people are communicating over the Internet. While looking for freedom people stumble upon an illusion created by the online experience in a world without limitations visceral and anonymous. Others tortured by anxiety and anger want to inflict their own torment into as many as possible in order to experience the only form of control over their redemption. An ambulance is the symbolic vehicle that transports people between this illusion and the sharpness of real life. "Watching from the corners of the world spiders may imprison gullible prey by pooling them into delicate webs through electrostatic attraction. It is the same energy the world wide web is feeding upon to wrap together abusers and victims"
