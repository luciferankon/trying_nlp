Leyla (42) a lawyer and a poet takes the long-distance train to attend her high school reunion dinner. On the train she meets Canan (21) a young nursing student in distress. As the conversation develops Leyla learns that Canan is travelling to assist with the suicide of a paralysed man Yavuz (45) who wants to die. At the end of the long over- night trip surrounded by the stories of people both on and off the train Leyla decides to accompany Canan on her difficult visit.
