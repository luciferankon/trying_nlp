In the aftermath of 9/11 the U.S. government institutes a secret think tank featuring the most creative minds in science and entertainment that is tasked with dreaming up man-made disaster scenarios and their possible solutions; because the hypothetical ideas are deemed extremely dangerous the list is sealed and the program shut down; but when a catastrophe occurs that's ripped from the pages of the missing doomsday book the team is brought back years later to prevent the disasters of their own making.
