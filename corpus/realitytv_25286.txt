Trinny Woodall and Susannah Constantine are two British fashion advisors presenters and authors. They are best known for presenting the BBC television series 'What Not to Wear' and 'Trinny & Susannah Undress...' on ITV. Since 2012 Trinny & Susannah have toured Norway and done "total makeover" on Norwegian women. The Norwegian TV-show is called 'Trinny & Susannah - oppdrag Norge'. The first season started on channel FEM (part of Discovery Networks Norway) in 2012. The show became very popular and they have now aired four seasons. In the latest series Norwegian celebrities is being restyled.
