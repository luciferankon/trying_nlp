Zeyno a hospital nurse in Amsterdam finds out she is pregnant by her first love. She daydreams of her wonderful life to be with the love of her life and unborn child is unaware of the surprises life is about to throw her way. A continent away the powerful Akbas family has a young daughter in need of a bone marrow transplant and will do anything to get it. Watch how the fates of these two family's intertwine in this heart wrenching Turkish drama.
