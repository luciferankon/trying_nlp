Stop Pepper Palmer is a comedy about three black guys from Utah who are the only black people from Utah who stumble upon a new black woman who has come to town. They vie for her attention but feel they aren't 'black enough' to get her so they hire someone from out of town Cleveland to teach them how to be 'blacker.' But when he arrives from Cleveland they realize he's not quite what he claims to be and that the man they hired to help them is now the man they must stop.
