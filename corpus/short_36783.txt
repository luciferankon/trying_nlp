Set in the fictional Northern world based upon the Paleo-Eskimo invasions and turf wars over Inuit and early Thule territory 'Throat of Cold' tells the tale of 20 year old Nayeli and her brother who were lost in the beast-ridden tundra as children and stolen by the fearsome Ijiraq - soulless creatures that devour the memories of anyone who approaches. Devoted to finding her brother and restoring their place in the village Nayeli embarks on a great quest to find and bargain with the all powerful Sedna goddess of the Sea.
