Bhaarat wakes up in an empty world. Void of any companion Bhaarat reminisces the story of his life. Living a freewheeling life with his best friend Champak Bhaarat is the heir and grandson of industrialist Mrs. Desai. Bhaarat meets Kisha both then become each other's first love. But Bhaarat struggles to prove his love to Kisha who has a strong faith in the kingdom of stars.
