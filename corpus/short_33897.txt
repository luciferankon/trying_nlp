This eastern style art film is about a middle-class man who brings a prostitute back to his condominium. The woman he brings is an amateur who hates being a prostitute and is only doing it to support her family. The man is attracted to this beautiful woman. He treasures beautiful things and therefore doesn't want to rush his evening with her. As soon as the woman takes off her clothes the man realizes that the woman has recently given birth. He falls into an emotional struggle whether or not to help the woman or use her for what she is offering.
