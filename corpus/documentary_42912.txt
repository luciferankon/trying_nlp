Visit Greece Promotional Videos were produced for EOT - VISIT GREECE the Greek National Tourism Organization. A series of 17 thematic short documentaries presenting the beauty of Greek Nature the Cuisine of Greece the Museums it's History Culture. and other various thematic natural and cultural values of Greece. Filmed during 2013 in locations such as Athens Mykonos Santorini Corfu Zakynthos Crete Halkidiki Symi Halki Patmos Hios Skyros Amorgos and the Peloponnese Region. All Documentry Videos were produced for the "EOT - Visit Greece" Web Portal and other Social Media Campaign of the Greek National Tourism Organization.
