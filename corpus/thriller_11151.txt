Bratislava Czechoslovakia. Secret agent Paul Grumbach seems to live in paradise all his dollars make up for a good living. Then Czechoslovakian secret service begins to show acute interest in him. Suddenly he is in the middle of an intrigue and confronted with a beautiful woman who turns out to be a Czech secret agent. As if things are not complicated enough both fall in love with each other. Paul is on his own now. Will he be smart enough to survive this deadly game?
