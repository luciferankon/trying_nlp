In 1982 Iowan suburban housewife Joyce Jackson (Roseanne) is an obsessed fan of the late Elvis Presley. She's used the so-called King of Rock and Roll's inspiration to turn her small house into a wacky shrine. Convinced that Elvis is still alive five years after his death Joyce concocts a plan to set off and find and meet him so she enlists the help of her estranged husband Jack (Tom Arnold).
