Unrestrained intense evocative rock music with Patti Smith Group in a recording from the Stockholm Concert Hall October 3 1976. Including Patti Smith's highly personal interpretation of "Land" the Rolling Stones song "Time is on my side" Lou Reed's "Pale Blue Eyes" and the PSG original "Redondo Beach". Patti Smith former journalist artist and poet and rock artist discusses with Lennart Wretlind in an introduction her approach to rock music.
