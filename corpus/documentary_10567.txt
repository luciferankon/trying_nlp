A documentary looking at the issues to do with itinerant migration from Africa to Italy. It mainly focuses upon the 2011 Florence racial attacks upon African migrants working in the markets of Florence. Through the eyes of survivors Mor Sougou and Cheikh Mbengue Italian attitudes toward migration are scrutinised. Also Senegalese griot Mohamed Ba reflects upon his own experiences of integration in the aftermath of a knife attack in his adopted home city of Milan.
