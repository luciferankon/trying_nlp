Four down on their luck comedian's are forced to begin touring small comedy clubs again after spending years in arena's all hell breaks loose when a bank CEO extorts them into robbing banks in the cities that they are performing in. Comedian Carrot Top has some big decisions to make as he and his three fellow comedy buddies look for a way to get out from beneath the thumb of a sinister CEO who is stealing TARP funds while hoping the robberies that he is forcing the comics to commit make up for those losses before the feds get wind of what he's been doing.
