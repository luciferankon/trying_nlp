Darnell backslides from his born-again phase and joins a secret society of entertainment execs that seek to monopolize publishing rights to all recorded music. Darnell must turn out young naive entertainers and aspiring executives to affiliate for fame. He must convince them to pledge to his entertainment frat in exchange for fame. Two artist age war against Darnell and his major label to retain publishing rights and are in jeopardy of losing their lives.
