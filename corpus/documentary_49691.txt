The average wedding in North America costs $30000 but if you're Indian in Canada that cost is $100000. Little India Big Business explores the growth of big fat Indian weddings in Canada over the last decade. It reveals the pressures that young South Asians face when planning their weddings. The documentary highlights how lucrative these celebrations are for vendors the lengths they go to ensure their clients needs are met and the cost of this commercialization on the sanctity of marriage.
