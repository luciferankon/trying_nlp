A vivid reimagining of the classic The Wizard of Oz Wicked spotlights the untold stories of Oz's most famous (or infamous) characters namely the Wicked Witch of the West and her unlikely friend Glinda the Good Witch. The show follows green-skinned star Elphaba from birth to college and through the life-changing events which eventually label her "wicked" introducing spoiled rich girl Glinda local prince and heartthrob Fiyero and even the Wizard of Oz himself a troubled man very unlike the one you may remember.
