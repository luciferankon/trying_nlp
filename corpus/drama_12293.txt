Recently there has been a brutal killing of two drug pushers named Rocky and Dre. These killings has Lamont looking deeper at his life and where it is heading. Lamont and his friends Corey and Miguel decide to rob two Jamaican drug dealers named Gully and Biggas. They work for an Italian gangster named Mr. Dicaro. What they thought was going to be an easy money getting scheme leaves them in danger of losing their lives. Lamont stumbles upon a street vendor name Taharka who opens Lamont's eyes to the world around him. This encounter has the potential to save Lamont's life.
