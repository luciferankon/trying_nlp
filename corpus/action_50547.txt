The world is trembling under the fear of an Internet virus that is threatening to destroy any nation's military system. Tarma and Eri are busy developing a vaccine in order to stop the virus from spreading across the Internet. Marco and Fio have been called in to fight a terrorist organization known as Amadeus the group which is responsible for the Internet virus. They are joined by newcomers Nadia and Trevor and together these four are the only ones who can stop the virus and destroy the Amadeus terrorist group before the entire Internet and military computer system is wiped out.
