3 Seconds 'til Glory is the stories of several wrestlers and their coach's trials as they struggle to find their place. These stories are based on real life events. Thomas Stark a biology student teacher is recruited by the school to run the wrestling program. Their intension is to scrap the program in order to use the money for other programs. What they don't know is you can't always judge a book by its cover. Thomas Stark is a former NCAA Division 1 wrestling champion. Despite his battle with the school's bureaucracy he inspires the team to strive for more see the best in themselves and what it means to be the light to others.
