Hikari is a young girl who though congenitally blind has nightly dreams about a young man named Horu. She falls deeply in love with the idea of this man and the brilliant shining world he inhabits. Unable to realize those yearnings she struggles each day. One day Hikari decides to visit a fortune-teller who is said to be able to erase people's memories. The procedure succeeds and her memories are effectively removed. Nevertheless she keeps having the same dreams at night.
