She - The Movie is an upcoming feature Bollywood film produced by Nikhil Chandwani. It portrays the outlook of a village girl who has high dreams in life but due to poverty she chooses to enter into flesh trade business due to want of money to survive and meet her household expenses as her husband played by Ravi Bhushan Bhartiya (Montu) is a small time fisherman who finds difficulty to run and maintain a household expenses. Sundari works in a hotel but the greed of money makes her greed grow day by day and ends up into a prostitution racket. But the story has a crisp turn when a prostitute though is referred as a heartless witch falls in love with a wildlife photographer.
