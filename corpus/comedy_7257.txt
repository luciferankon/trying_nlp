Hollywood Acting Studio follows Professor LaFrange a failed Hollywood hopeful and his attempts to teach "blockbuster cinema acting" to his community college acting class. LaFrange teaches his class the secrets to auditioning working on a green screen working with a child actor and even filming a sex scene using summer blockbuster titles as his source material. Using a slew of unorthodox and even abusive techniques LaFrange prepares his class for the cold unforgiving world of Hollywood.
