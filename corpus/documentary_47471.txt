'The River' is a work in progress of Soom movement a contemporary dance company based in Seoul Korea. Working on the dance performance the dramaturg tries to give dancers inspiration to enhance their improvisation. Training with body awareness contact improvisation imagination fabulation poetic approach collaborating with other artists and many things come into their world. The dancers try to keep their inner attention as well as external attention. And they explore the river flowing through their movement to ask where they are reaching out by making this film.
