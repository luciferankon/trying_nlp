In this all-new edition of "E! Investigates" series host and investigative journalist Laura Ling sits down with six women who have faced unspeakable tragedies and seemingly insurmountable odds yet lived to tell their stories. Kidnapping; a horrifying fall; sexual abuse by a teacher; attempted murder and a cancer death sentence. These stories are featured along with an exclusive interview with Sarah Shourd one of three young American hikers falsely accused of espionage and imprisoned in Iran.
