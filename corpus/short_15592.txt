July 1951. Harry Lynch a mathematical genius and physicist working on the ongoing and top secret Manhattan Project in Los Alamos New Mexico discovers a fatal flaw in the chemical combination of one of the testing devices for the Atomic bomb. While at a briefing in Washington D.C. he scrambles to the telephone to warn the testing physicist however he fails; leading to the deaths of thousands in the testing zone and beyond. Now 17 years later Harry is still dealing with the deaths of thousands as he believes he is solely responsible for the devastation. Could he have been wrong all these years?
