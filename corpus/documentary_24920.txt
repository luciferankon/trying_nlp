Interview with Classical music legends Julius Rudel Gunther Schuller Sheldon Harnick and Frederica von Stade along with glimpses into the workings of one of the oldest Opera festivals in the USA along with an insight into the history of Central City once the center of the gold rush in Colorado and now a gambling mecca and the largest historical restoration in Colorado all lead to the full performance of a compact gem of a perfect American Chamber Opera... The Face On The Barroom Floor composed by Henry Mollicone with a libretto by John Bowman.
