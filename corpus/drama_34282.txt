Maren a young girl is the sole survivor of the Black Death in her Norwegian village. Using instincts folklore luck and the clairvoyant powers granted her by being born with a "Victory Cap" Maren survives on her own waiting for other people to discover her plight. Painstaking recreations of medieval customs and settings dominate the film. I Jostedalen pĺ 1300-tallet lever folk isolert. Likevel kommer svartedauen hit.
