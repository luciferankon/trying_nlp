A family struggles with the news of a cleft lip and palate diagnosis (also known as CLP) in their unborn daughter. Their obvious concerns are met with equal if not unexpected challenges. As we follow Emily and her parents on an emotional journey of adaptation medical understanding social issues and professional biased - Two Sides demonstrates the joy of successful treatments the skill and dedication of medical professionals and teams as well as the commitment of cleft charities and support groups in the UK. An engaging and realistic story of human courage strength empathy combined with unconditional love and support.
