Francisco is a selfish has-been actor pigeonholed by a popular TV character he played years ago. When he finds out that Laura his ex-wife is about to remarry and take their daughter Alicia to live abroad Francisco manipulates his own mother his agent his only friend (and number one fan) and even his daughter herself to put a plan in motion to stop the wedding and get his family back while unwillingly facing his inner demons and learning that in order to grow up you don't need to be a superhero.
