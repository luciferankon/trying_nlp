Seventy years after the end of World War II the "invasion" of Berlin by Soviet troops is remembered through old photos and real testimonies. Berlin today is still an open-air building yard a charming capital and secretly not very German always busy picking up structural and psychological debris; with the long and terrible passage of the Wall to the massive reception non-EU people that produced ghettos. The pictures taken in 2015 will talk about this in Berlin developing a visual story inevitably caught between past and future; temporally tridimensional sequences.
