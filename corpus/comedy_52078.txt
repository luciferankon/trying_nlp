School spirit has never been lower at Marvin Gardens High School so when a new principal is scheduled to take over everyone is hoping for change. What they get instead is conman George Thurlow. After causing a car wreck that injures the new principal George and his sidekick Madge run from the law by posing as the new principal and his secretary respectively in a case of stolen identity. Taking on their new roles the criminals begin to double-talk and dodge their way past pushy school board members a nosy security guard overactive students and the other manic people in the school building in this riotous plot-twisting tale.
