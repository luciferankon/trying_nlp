A 12-part documentary about the history of Sweden beginning at the end of the latest ice age (some 15000 years ago) and reaching into today. It is part of a larger enterprise in which the Historiska museet (Museum of History) in Stockholm runs an exhibition in 2010-2011 about the history of Sweden from the year 1000 to today and the book company Norstedts publishes a series of eight books on the history of Sweden from 13000 BC to today of which publishing began in 2009.
