Ben Jason has found a lost gold mine. When Morgan learns this from Wyatt he and his henchman Rocky chase down Jason and kill him. Banning and sidekick Rafferty arrive on the scene only to be arrested and jailed for the murder. They escape from jail and now have to find the real killers to clear their name. On their way to Rimrock Bob Banning and Chito Rafferty aid an elderly prospector Ben Jason whose wagon has broken down. He gives them a nugget and asks them to meet his niece Judy in Rimrock and tell her he is on the way. They do so and then sell the nugget to Matt Wyatt the local assayer. The latter recognizes the nugget as having come from the famous "Lost Dutchman" mine and decides that Jason must have re-discovered the mine. He schemes with gambler Anse Morgan and his henchman Rocky to ambush Jason. They do so murder the old man and take his map. Bob and Chito arrive to late to help Jason but in time to make the sheriff think they are the culprits who did the killing. Judy thinks so also. They have to find a way to clear themselves but they first must get out of jail.
