From the Academy Award nominated directors of Troublesome Creek Steven Ascher & Jeanne Jordan comes a new documentary filmSo Much So Fast. A black-humored cliffhanger of romance guerrilla science and the redefinition of time So Much So Fast unfolds like a nonfiction novel. Stephen Heywood finds out he has ALS. His brother Jamie becomes obsessed with finding a cure. And the woman who's falling in love with Stephen has a decision to make.
