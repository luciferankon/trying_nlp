Based on a true story Bianca: Tell Us Who Did This to You is adapted from the best-selling book of the same name. It tells the story of a woman whose search for love is derailed due to years of physical mental and self-inflicted abuse. As a child Bianca's mother betrayed her after learning she was molested by members of her own church. During her teenage years Bianca's constant physical altercations with her stepfather eventually left her pregnant and defeated. As an adult Bianca left home hoping for a better life with her husband however the life she ended up with was one she never would have expected. This is a very emotional and powerful story that is sure to give a voice to those who have always struggled to be heard.
