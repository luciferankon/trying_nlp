The whole gang are back and as close as ever. They decide to get even closer by spending the summer together at a beach house. They decide to hold the biggest party ever to be seen even if the preparation doesn't always go to plan. Especially when Stifler Finch and Jim become more close to each other than they ever want to be and when Jim mistakes super glue for lubricant. After their first year at college the guys reunite for another summer of fun. Jim continues his quest for sexual independence by seeking the help of his old prom date Michele after an unexpected call from Nadia who plans to visit Jim. Meanwhile Kev and Vicky find themselves in an awkward situation after having broken up for a year. Oz must deal with a long distance relationship when Heather heads off to France to study abroad. Old feuds die hard as the ever-so-horny Stifler harbors his hatred toward Finch who is practicing the Hindu practice of Tantra.
