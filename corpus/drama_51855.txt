Nature Boy is the story of Nate Fox a former pro tennis player who finds himself washed up and disconnected in his home town at age 33. Nate has no cell phone no vehicle no direction a handful of local friends. He splits his time between the local dive a bartender named Pam's apartment and the public tennis courts where he hangs with his ailing tennis coach Ray Barrette. When Coach's condition takes a turn for the worse Nate is forced to evaluate his relationships and the direction of his life. Nature Boy is an independent drama set in Palo Alto California.
