Filmmaker Jeta Amata is saddled with the responsibility of changing the narrative in the Niger Delta from "violence pays". He selects 7 individuals from 3 different Nigerian states; Bayelsa Delta and Rivers to make them into filmmakers within few days.The 21 trainees intensively train on filmmaking conflict resolution business and brand management. The trainee's duties are to make films that show they can solve conflicts in their communities without resorting to violence.
