Cliff Barackman isn't your average bigfooter. When Cliff is not Co-Hosting the Discovery Network - Animal Planet's hit show 'Finding Bigfoot' you'll find him on vacation - looking for sasquatch. Cliff may not have a helicopter or a dozen crew members but he is the world's foremost bigfoot field researcher and he has the gear the skills and his wacky camera buddy Craig Flipy. Together Cliff and Craig take us on a genuine bigfoot roadtrip adventure. From the deep wilderness of the Pacific Northwest to the Idaho State University bigfoot laboratory of Dr. Jeff Meldrum get ready to make all new discoveries and uncover new evidence on the bigfooting adventure of a lifetime. New cast data new testimonials and never before seen photos interviews and genuine bigfoot field research are all scheduled stops on this Bigfoot Roadtrip.
