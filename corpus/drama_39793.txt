Eva is a Jewish literature teacher at a high school in Bordeaux France after the Second World War. During the war her whole family was sent to the German extermination camps by French collaborators. Eva survived by chance. Then by chance a new girl enters her class a girl who resembles Eva's dead daughter. Eva dares hope that perhaps one member of her family may have survived the holocaust. 1958 Eva is a fifty years old woman. During World War II she lost her daughter and her granddaughter Sarah who was still a baby and all her family who has been raided in 1943. Since the war Eva is trying to rebuild herself. She is now teaching French. One day she recognizes among one of her student's father the militiamen responsible for the raid and she realizes that Sarah is alive.
