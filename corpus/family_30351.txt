Hop on board the Macster Boat with Mac and the McPuppets! In this second series of the Learning Tree children's EDUtainment Mac along with his McPuppet friends take children on an incredibly fun and engaging boat journey through American History with travel to such places as our nations capitol Fort McHenry National Historical Park and Monument Baltimore Harbor Jamestown Settlement in Williamsburg and much more. Mac and his McPuppet friends bring the fun of learning to every child and the child-at-heart!
