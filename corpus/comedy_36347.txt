Sachin and Mahesh are two graduates looking for a job as their parents have told them that they wont get any support from them after college. One day they save two female friends Kavita and Madhu from some low life goons. The two men quickly fall for the beautiful women and are eager to impress them. Still unable to find a job Sachin turns himself into an old man to teach music and ends up teaching Kavita. Mahesh starts teaching female dance classes where Madhu is a student. Both men do not reveal their true identity. The story takes a twist when Kavita's aunt falls in love with Sachin thinking he is a old man and Madhu's father falls in love with Mahesh thinking he is a female.
