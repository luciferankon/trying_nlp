CRAVE: an Original Series by Aneesh Sheth tells the story of Maggie an actress trying to make her mark in the business and her secret being transgender is her greatest asset or her biggest liability on any given day. She's lucky to have the support and love of her two best friends Bobbi a gay man stuck in an abusive relationship and Karen a military wife stuck in a loveless marriage when her husband Mark returns from a deployment in Afghanistan. And just when things couldn't get any harder Maggie's estranged sister shows up pregnant seeking support from Maggie the sibling she abandoned years ago.
