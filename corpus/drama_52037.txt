Jimmy Angel a convicted drug trafficker has just received a second lease on life. His plan? To re-enter the fast paced dangerous world of illegal substances with his cohorts: Johnny Vain and Tommy Land. Together again the trio will look to revolutionize the market with a highly explosive new drug: Crystal Meth. Standing in their way are a nosey cop rival trafficking gangs and figures of Angel's past. But as the plot begins to unfold Jimmy must make the ultimate choice: wealth or happiness? Is Angel willing to give up a world he knows a world he controls...to be in a world he longs for? Or will the thrill of fortune women and fame draw him deeper into a fate unknown?
