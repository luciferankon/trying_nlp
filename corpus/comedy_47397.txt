A failed town in Northern Wisconsin. A corrupt mayor. A bumbling sheriff. A failed childhood actor. A drunk Amish guy and angered salvage yard owner. Plus a fast junkyard car make for a unusual slapstick comedy about 2 drunks that work at Rutts Auto Salvage who are trying to save the place from being taken over by Mayor Carbunkle. In the town of Salvedge there are no GOOD GUYS only BAD GUYS. Choose your villains wisely! NR not rated. Adult language and lots of drinking.
