When sports photographer Paul is temporarily confined to a wheelchair after a collision on field with a linebacker he thinks he will go crazy from boredom - until he catches a glimpse of the wife living in Apartment C across the way. With a parade of men coming in and out of her bedroom and her shades wide open Paul has a non-stop sex show right outside his window! Things start to heat up when Paul gets out his camera takes some sexy shots - and leads the Wife From Apartment C right to his own doorstep!
