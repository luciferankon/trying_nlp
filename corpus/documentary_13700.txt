Story about a mountain village in Japan. The village is in serious aging and under-populated situation like the other local village in Japan. The film is focusing on process of change and stage as a knot where various actors including a filmmaker interact with each other. While the whole film as well as respective long takes can be grasped as a process of inevitable change of the traditional village located on the global world it would be understood as a process of discovering precious memories as well as lively voices of respective individuals recalled through dialog with the filmmaker.
