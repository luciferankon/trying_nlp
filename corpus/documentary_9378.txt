This critically-acclaimed film pays tribute to many great Italian-American athletes coaches and executives who have had a profound and lasting impact on American sports history. Special guests include: YOGI BERRA MARY LOU RETTON MIKE PIAZZA JOE TORRE DIANA TAURASI MARY CARILLO VINNY PAZ ANGELO DUNDEE LOU CARNESECCA JOHN FRANCO JERRY COLANGELO ROBERT LOGGIA PAT COOPER and many more.
