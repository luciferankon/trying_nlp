A six-part documentary mixes movie clips behind the scenes elements and interviews. We hear from cast and crew on the development of the books and the first one's adaptation for the screen character design early pre-production locations sets and creating a fantasy universe. From there we go through casting and performances elements from the shoot such as dealing with the elements and working with animals stunts and fight scenes and various effects. In addition the program examines costumes and makeup logistical challenges photographic choices creating Saphira and looking ahead.
