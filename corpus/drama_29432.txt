7 New York City friends are at a turning point in their lives pushing the boundaries of their dreams potential and sexuality. They've spent their 20's looking for the perfect partner career and lifestyle. Now as they approach 30 they are questioning their choices and beginning to challenge the societal norms they have been conditioned to believe. Marcus Jasmine Hayley Jacob Melanie Jason and Sandra are the embodiment of Gen Y - young smart confident and open minded. They are now questioning what "growing up" really means to each of them while searching for themselves and true love- whatever form that may take.
