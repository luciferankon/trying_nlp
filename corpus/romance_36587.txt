Usually the double role stories are about lost & found parent with a look alike child or imposter/look alike characters. PhotoCopy is an intense Romantic drama where identical twin sisters who have the same likings from childhood end up liking and loving the same boy. As a twist in tale the boy does not realise till the end that he is actually romancing two different sisters. Set against College backdrop & extensively shot in Lavasa first for any Marathi Film PhotoCopy with an ACE team is directed by the debutant director national award winner writer & ad film maker Vijay Maurya.
