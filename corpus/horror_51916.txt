This film is about a priest an ordained exorcist and former missionary who pastors a college town church. He blesses a dorm house believed to be invaded by dark spirits and by doing so unleashing a war between him and an ancient demon Lord. On the other side of the story is a woman suffering under demonic oppression for her life as a witch and the sad possession of a 15 year old girl whom the priest Father Clancy must deliver her from an unnamed demon. Father Clancy must fight for the souls and survival of women. Can he win or be destroyed by the very evil that has been unleashed by the use of demonology and the occult. Faith against doubt light against darkness. A promise of a new day in the midst of madness.
