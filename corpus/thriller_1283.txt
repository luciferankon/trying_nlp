As his father lies dying Ziggy returns to help his family business from going under in the small town he fought so hard to escape. But facing demons he thought long left behind will be more complicated than he thought after restarting a romantic relationship with Gio a troubled addict with mounting debts. A friendship with Ray a nightclub bouncer with his own checkered past is the one bright spot in his new life. Things take a deadly turn however with the arrival of a mysterious stranger hellbent on revenge and destroying anything Ziggy holds dear.
