A documentary leaning hybrid series that highlights ordinary citizens with extraordinary talents and passions and pairs them with local businesses and other community members; providing them with opportunities they never would have had if it hadn't knocked on their door. The show has a "buy local stay local" and "pay it forward" foundation. Participants/recipients featured in each segment are uniquely matched with a business(es) and members in their local communities for their "opportunity" which aligns with the purpose and/or mission of the community member or establishment.
