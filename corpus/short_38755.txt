September 1939. Reichsfuhrer Heinrich Himmler "Hitler's Right Hand" oversees his Einsatzgruppen forces' relentless and lethal sweep through Poland. On a routine inspection of the unit's functionality Himmler discovers an unexpected flaw in his perfect scheme to conquer Europe: humanity. Touched by a young soldier's struggle to perform his gruesome duties Himmler formulates a deadly new answer to "The Jewish Question" to preserve his execution squads and ensure they remain Decent Men.
