Two brothers thought they knew exactly what they wanted out of life but got a second chance at life that they both did not expect. Ho Li-Wei ('Chieh-kai Shiou' (qv)) was perfectly happy settling down with his beautiful but narcissistic wife Ko Hsin-Yi ('Pink Yang' (qv)) until a final unbearable last temper tantrum caused him to leave her for good. Ho Li-Fang ('Kunda Hsieh' (qv)) enjoyed his carefree unfettered life but a car accident changes everything for him.
