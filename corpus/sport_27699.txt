Samantha Marchewa Darcy Williamson Theresa Walters Jennifer Lee and Brittany Capon decided to raise money for Dog Tales Rescue & Sanctuary. They are all wrestling fans. So they decided to put on a wrestling show and donate All Proceeds to the shelter. All of the superstars appearing in this video have volunteered their time their bodies to help raise money for the shelter. Over $3500 was raised by the end of the show!
