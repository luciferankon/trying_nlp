The film revolves around a family of Jewel. She is a college student and likes independence. She has a very caring mother who fears everything jewel do and a loving father. She hates her mother who criticize for everything. Meanwhile her family friend falls in love with her. Soon she met a guy through chat and he makes her to leave her worries and encourages her. She began to love her mother and brother. Thus the family feels happy. She fell in love with the chat friend and film ends with whom she decides to marry. Great family story.
