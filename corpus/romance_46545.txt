Ivan Santos (Lester Marte) lost his heartthrob status after he had broken with his junior high campus hot babe girlfriend and daughter of one of the shareholders of their school Amorsolo Academy. His world changes as he meets a schoolmate Kristine Navarro (Xyryl) youth aid leader of Club 800. Kristine shows Ivan a different world one could possibly create and deliberately live in despite of their young age with attached peer pressure of high school life.
