We spend so much time caught up in our daily routines that it's important we bust loose every now and again relax de-stress and do something different. Enter Lonely Planet's Stressbuster: showcasing inspirational short breaks that offer a new perspective on life. Whether you're looking for spiritual solace or natural highs a warm inner glow or a burst of adrenalin to blow the cobwebs away intrepid presenters Dominic Bonuccelli and Anita Kapoor will brave high seas and rugged canyons to find it for you.
