When Suriya (Poppy) was very young she was raped by a boy of a local politician. She killed that boy and flee to the city. In the city she establishes a gang of women who fight for the rights of the girls in the city. Wherever there is a woman in distress they went there and help her out of the distress. Mishkatur Rahman (Shakib Khan) comes to the city to find a job and meets Suriya. After first meeting Suriya liked Mishkat very much and later falls in love with him. Mishkat also started love her back. They begin to work together to uproot evil from the society and restore peace.
