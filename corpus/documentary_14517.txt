Money-Driven Medicine is an overview to tackle the problems with our 2.6 trillion dollar health care system. Going beyond the health insurance reform to discuss future problems that might occur while trying to continue having superior care while still maintaining low expenses and unwanted values in a safe manner. Alex Gibney an award-winning producer who was encouraged by Money Driven Medicine: The Real Reason Health Care Costs So Much Maggie Mahar's much-admired book produces this documentary. The film delivers the important information needed by merging the opinions from both doctors and patients to inform its viewers how to attack the not unsolved challenges health care has on the United States.
