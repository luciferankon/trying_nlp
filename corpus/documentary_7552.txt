In 2011 group of people have formed a charity-organisation in Oulu Finland. They have raised money and donations and they plan to drive two vans filled with the donations from Finland to the Gambia. They start their trip from Puolanka and hit the road on a foggy cold October morning.. Plan is to give the donations and also the vans to the local people in Gambia to help their income and in top of that to build a nursery and music-studio there. The movie covers 6 month period of time.
