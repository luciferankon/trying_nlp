Zhao is twenty-five years old. She has gone with her boyfriend Martín to a house on the beach where her love story will unfold. She is writing some memoirs about her life. It was adopted in China by a Spanish family. Her happy childhood is suddenly truncated with the death of her adoptive parents. Zhao begins to lock himself in his own world and aims at Chinese classes. Divided between her memories and her love story with Martín she has to make a decision.
