A young man whose life revolves around basketball must give up what he loves the most to repent for a horrible tragedy. Tuan Chen-Feng ('Hsing-shu Yen' (qv)) a talented high school basketball player is on the way to the most important competition of his life when he is involved in a car accident that paralyzes Angel ('Michelle Lee-Wen Lin' (qv)) who happens to be the younger sister of his biggest rival Iceman ('Yi-Fei Chen' (qv)).
