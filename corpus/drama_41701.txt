An Italian film adaptation of D.H. Lawrence's classic erotic novel. After a crippling injury leaves her husband impotent Lady Chatterly is torn between her love for her husband and her physical desires. With her husband's consent she seeks out other means of fulfilling her needs. Loosely based on D.H. Lawrence's Lady Chatterly's Lover. A woman and her crippled husband live in an expensive villa in Italy. When an old acquaintance of her husband's shows up just released from prison he is given a job as a gardener/handyman around the estate. Although she is initially suspicious of this newcomer her interest in him gradually grows.
