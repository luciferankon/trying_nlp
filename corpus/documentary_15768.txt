Determined to confront their conflicted pasts the descendants of both Nazis and Holocaust survivors undergo several years worth of intense workshops in Berlin. Gaining insight into how the descendants of both victims and aggressors suffer today the workshop process - leading up to a climactic train ride to Auschwitz - reveals the emotional wreckage left behind from the Holocaust. With tension beginning to form between the Jewish and German participants does confronting an incomprehensible past heal the present?
