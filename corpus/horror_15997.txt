In the town of Santonio Bay three babies are born on October 31st the same night as the local police kill three Cult members during a human sacrifice ritual designed to bring about reincarnation. 13 years later the reincarnated cult members are now teenagers. Their devoted parents are blissfully unaware that on Halloween night the teenagers plan to make them the next human sacrifice to the dark God of Samhain. But a former police detective knows all about the deadly plan. Will the parents believe him and turn against their own children? Or will The Reborn turn Santonio Bay into a town where no adult is safe?
