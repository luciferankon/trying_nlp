Orange County Choppers: American Made is a lifestyle and build series that features Paul Teutul Sr. and his world-renowned crew from Orange County Choppers and Rusty Coones from "Sons Of Anarchy." Audiences will get an intimate glimpse inside the exciting worlds of Paul Sr. and his new friend Rusty's adrenaline fueled lifestyles. Watch these American icons as they create and build the most extreme custom themed bikes boats and other unique one of kind machines on the planet for their celebrity and interesting clients.
