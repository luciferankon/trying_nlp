When AJ and Liz are assigned to work in a small desert town their friendship is far from stable. Matters become more complicated when they meet another outsider in the town Daniel who is looking for his missing friend. When Daniel too disappears AJ and Liz are caught in a quickly unfolding mystery. The locals including a diner owner and the town's only cop are far from concerned about the missing persons in their area. Packed with suspense No Precedence was made entirely in Eastern-Idaho utilizing local talent.
