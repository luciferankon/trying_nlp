A Moment Can Last Forever is a romantic comedy about the humor and coincidence in life even in life's darkest hour. The death of a loved one and a crippling accident set two people on a quest that unites them in a common very personal interest. Robbie's special relationship with her mother inspires her to seek the most beautiful sunset in the world while Dale's early retirement gives him the opportunity to pursue his life-long goal of photographing the most beautiful sunset in the world. Despite the well-intentioned comedic antics of Robbie's best friends Robbie and Dale meet unexpectedly and pursue the most beautiful sunset in the world and fall in love along the journey.
