Ingrid Segerstedt Wiberg has been mocked ridiculed and criticized. She has even been called a traitor. Her "crime" is an irrepressible commitment to freedom whether it applied refugee issues peace issues or women's issues. Ingrid Segerstedt Wiberg's life from the early 1900s to the present portrayed in a film that is both a personal documentary and a live image of the Swedish twentieth century history.
