Following the underhanded murder of Pancho Villa on the outskirts of Parral Chihuahua  the whole city mourned his death. The wake for the revolutionary hero was held by his closest collaborators. Standing out among the mourners were the four women with whom Villa was having intimate relationships at the time of his death. As expected the tension between the women grows and intensifies culminating in a dramatic surprise ending of. An intimate and human portrait of the Centaur of the North.
