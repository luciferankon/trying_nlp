Antonio Diaz embarks on a seven day journey in which he will try to meet one of his all-time pop idols Mr. Nick Mason the drummer of the rock band 'Pink Floyd'. To do so he will test the six degrees of separation theory which states that anyone in the world is connected to anyone else by no more than 5 people. In order to get the people he meets to help him in his quest he will use his mastery of magic to impress them.
