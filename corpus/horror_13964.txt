A beautiful Russian woman named Olga is sent by her father on a mission. To transport a deadly virus: A virus that brings the dead to life and looking for flesh to eat. Stolen from the Russian Laboratory by Dr. Pavlova with the interest of further research for an antidote. From the Southwest desert "Land of Enchantment" once a peaceful town. It is now infected with the epidemic that is growing out of control... There is a powerful antidote: "love" the only cure that can save Zombiequerque... But It is being kept a secret...Why?
