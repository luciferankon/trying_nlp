Newly arrived settlers are attacked by local ranch owner Ben Blazer and his men. Kent Taggart sees his parents killed by Blazer's son whom he tracks home. Taggart kills the boy in front of Blazer himself dying of a wound who then puts a price of $5000 on Taggart's head an offer enthusiastically taken up by ruthless killer Jason. Taggart has no option but to head out into hostile Apache country followed by Jason and other no-goods.
