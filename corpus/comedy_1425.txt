This romantic comedy presents a story of two women twenty-year-old Laura ('Zuzana Kanócz' (qv)) an editor at a woman's weekly and her widowed mother a translator-interpreter named Jana (('Simona Stasová' (qv)). The two of them tirelessly seek Mr. Right. Having once lived through an intense relationship with a 'typical' Czech man Jana intentionally avoids Czech men. After several unsuccessful attempts Laura falls in love with Oliver ('Marek Vasut' (qv)) a forty year old who works as an ad agency idea man. Little does she suspect that twenty years ago Oliver was Jana's true love...
