Shortly after Ace Queens' assassination David Anderson has gone missing. While in his absence Chalkface's army enters Alpinoma and takes control of it. The citizens of Alpinoma go through pain and despair as they learn of what may happen to David if they don't follow Chalkface's rule. During this tragic moment Bailee Scarlett goes searching for a long lost loved one as a brave girl by the name of Jasmine Davis followed by an agile professor James Beck deliberately fight to save their city and friends.
