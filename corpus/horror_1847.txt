In 2012 a group of paranormal researchers lead by Emily Strand (Whitney Anderson) set forth on their last ghost hunt in one of America's most haunted hospitals in the community of Boyle Heights Los Angeles California. They never expected the haunt of a lifetime. This collective of paranormal researchers whose focus was to help explain the paranormal by holding educational and informative community events and sharing 3 years of ongoing paranormal research at one of the most haunted location in the Unites States. Some of their lives would tragically come to an end at the Linda Vista Hospital.
