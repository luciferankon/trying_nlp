Menateus is an abbreviatory combination of german words with the meaning "Man - Nature - Technology". It is the official video game which has been produced for the EXPO2000 World Exhibition in Hannover Germany. The game is placed in the near future on planet earth and you have to stop the fog of indifference to save the planet from pollution. In doing this you have to travel through time and space. Visit the "Cyberdome" the palace of elements and the pyramids of mystic. Your object is to find all puzzle pieces in order to finish this game.
