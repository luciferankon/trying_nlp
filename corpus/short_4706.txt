A lab mouse has a never-ending hunger due to the side effects of a cancer treatment. Dr. Tribune also has a never-ending hunger but his is due to greed and lust. He monitors the mouse fixated on curing disease. Yet all the while he overlooks his own lust which is itself a disease...putting everything he built at risk. Dr. Tribune is contacted by a former lover who was also a student of his. What she asks him to do could very easily destroy his marriage and his career.
