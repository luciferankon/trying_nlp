Bez grada i bez zakona is a riveting documentary about the media manipulation surrounding The Serbian Orthodox Church during the Milosevic reign. The documentary centers on the effort of director Nedeljko Despotovic and writer Bogdan Zlatic who were hired to produce the annual celebration of St. Sava Day on the Belgrade Faculty of Theology. Soon enough they encountered different attampts at political manipulation from Milosevic's tycoons most notably the Braca Karic Corporation. This film documents their struggle to put their vision across and it compiles the wisdom of contemporary Serbian intellectuals who tried to protect Serbian heritage from Milosevic's reach.
