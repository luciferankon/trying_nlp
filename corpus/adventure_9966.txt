Marcus Numidius ('Ettore Manni (I)' (qv)) a Roman tribune sent to Armenia to put down a gladiators' revolt captures the rebels' popular leader Aselepius ('Georges Marchal' (qv)). Princess Amira ('Gianna Maria Canale' (qv)) with ambitions of being Queen and jealous of Asclepius' popularity plans his death in the arena by substituting a lion for his human opponent.
