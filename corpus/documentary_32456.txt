The Daily is a documentary about newspaper people. Those who press the shoe leather to a murder scene watch the trek of a hurricane and explain why your cable bill will be going up this month. This film explores the importance of daily journalists and their survival in a changing medium. Reporters and editors of the Winston-Salem Journal continue to serve their community even as coworkers and friends disappear from the newsroom. A portrait of people who hunt down the events and moments that impacts our lives giving context to the world around us. While certain elements of the film deal with hard truths this is a celebration of daily news; sometimes sad at times surprising always real.
