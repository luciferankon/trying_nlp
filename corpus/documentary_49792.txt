This classic documentary series Between the Wars: The Road to Pearl Harbor 1919-1941 follows the events that inevitably sparked World War II capturing the drama the excitement and the juxtapositions of these crucial years between the end of World War I and Pearl Harbor. Former CBS News correspondent and commentator Eric Sevareid one of the world's most respected figures in journalism presents this extraordinary series which features stunning original newsreels soundtracks and rare archival footage.
