Two brothers from a background of crime murder and drugs. Grown up not knowing right from wrong born in a suburban council estate of Derby. Crime is all they know the streets become there home but no matter how much bad they did their parents were always there with that little bit of hope and faith they could change for the better. A mothers love made them realize that enough is enough both having so much respect for their mother they decide to go legit and settle down. They are not young anymore and cant live the way they have forever. Years later on a cold winters night the brothers witness the murder of their parents over the phone resulting in their old ways they swear to avenge the death of their parents by any means necessary. Resulting in a path of deceit betrayal and destruction. However the brothers' investigation results into something a little deeper than they expected. How far would you go?
