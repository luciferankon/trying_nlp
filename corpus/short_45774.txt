A few days in the lives of Armelle and Matéo. At first sight Matéo is only Armelle's son: isn't he only ten years old? But his mother can neither read nor write; all she can do is sign at the bottom of documents she does not understand. So Matéo is more than her kiddie : he helps her protects her is her accomplice. Thanks to Matéo Armelle can hide her handicap. One day though she gets a job offer...
