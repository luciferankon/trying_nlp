A feature documentary about a Rock/Progressive/Pop Punk Band named Forever Came Calling over five years in the making. "If you're going to try go all the way. Otherwise don't even start." From self diagnosed depression to exploring a lifestyle / fulfilling dreams that only music can provide this film traverses the highs and lows of touring full time. These guys strive to surround themselves with like minded people and for 10 years strong they've given life their damnedest to make good on promises to friends all across the states.
