Nina and Thomas a young couple in their thirties are expecting a baby. They live in a small community and are in the process of renovating their house which is situated on a lake outside the village. Thomas works at a gas station and gets help with the renovation from Nina's brother Sigge who works at the town bakery. One day an Estonian couple come to the gas station. Their meeting with Thomas and Sigge starts off well but ends badly changing and ruining lives in the process.
