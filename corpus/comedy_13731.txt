Episode 1 Alice meets "The Actress" for the first time. Episode 2 "Jars of Sh*t" Alice is given her first task and it's a really bad one. Episode 3 "Gold Face". The Actress demands Alice go to her house to put her incredibly expensive eye cream in the fridge but Alice gets carried away by all the fancy products. Episode 4 'Alice Steps off Elton John". Alice meets some other Hollywood assistants and gets a real glimpse into the world of being a slave to fame. Episode 5 "Alice has Phone Sex" The Actress and her fiance Daerrick also an actor struggle to make their sex life work while he's away filming. Good thing The Actress has Alice on speed dial. Episode 6 "Alice has a Fan" Alice gets recognized at a restaurant and The Actress does not. Are The Actress and Alice all that different? Assistance is about Alice a nice girl new to LA following her dreams taking a part time job as an assistant to The Actress a not-as-famous-as-she-thinks-she-is actor who quickly turns Alice's life into a nightmare. And as it turns out Hollywood can be a real bitch.
