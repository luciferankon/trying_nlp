What is the price we pay to love someone - deeply? What is the cost of betrayal? Betrayal by a neighbor - betrayal by a wife? In The Bronx New York there is a price. In The Bronx the 'old neighborhood' - there are laws - codes men live by. When he believes love is slipping away Frank Radasta receives help from an unlikely source. Frank fights for what he believes in...no matter what the price.
