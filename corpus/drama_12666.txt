Thirty year old Sybille still lives with her parents in a boring suburb. She's not a beauty still a virgin and so shy that even in her bedroom she first puts on her night dress before removing her underwear. On her way to work she reads cheap romantic novels. When one day she meets handsome Victor on the bus it looks as if her romantic fantasies are about to come true. Against her expectations their feelings are mutual. In no time he moves in with her and her parents. Then his real nature becomes clear...
