Bruno Desantis is a popular TV showman but Sofia his wife has left him. Bruno is still in love with her and tries to call her. Unfortunately or fortunately his telephone is out of order and he is forced to go out seeking for one. He ends in a house where he finds three women: Vera Simona and Betty. The three are so moved by his distress to decide to cure him to nourish him and in the end to love him. Bruno begins to live like a Sultan in his harem. But all the dreams come to an end and he finds himself alone again with his telephone still out of order and he is forced again to go out and to meet other women... who knows?
