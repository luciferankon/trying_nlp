Leadership of Argentine radical and violent organization 'Montoneros' makes this propaganda film interviewing their main leader Mario Firmenich during his exile in Paris (three years before the organization's dissolution). Both Firmenich and the voice over of a fictional character which is supposed to be a militant of Montoneros analyze the 20th Century Argentine History the first of them rationally the second one more emotionally. Plus Firmenich describes the present situation and the tactical needs for his organization.
