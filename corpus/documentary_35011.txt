In his debut documentary Papa Weifeng reveals his true colors as a Chinese rainbow father. Raised in an era when open talks about sex were taboo Papa Weifeng exposes the sexual cultural wasteland surrounding him and pokes fun at the distorted mentalities he encounters in Chinese society. The film shines a bright light on him and other rainbow parents. Together they form a close-knit group of everyday heroes taking the lead in demanding equal rights for their homosexual children. Resolutely striving for happiness they break down old concepts and contribute to the creation of a flourishing world embracing diversity.
