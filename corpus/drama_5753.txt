Lost In Oxyland is based on true events about a high school middle linebacker star who played semi pro football with potential of making the NFL. Dylan Jennings truly had it all a beautiful wife great career nice house cars and most importantly GOD. Dylan had a big secret he was trying to keep and it finally caught up to him; his prescription drug addiction. Things soon unravel to complete madness entering a world he never knew existed. His addiction will take him to places above and beyond his own comprehension. Join us in the true life crime thriller titled "Lost In Oxyland".
