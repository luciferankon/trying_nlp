That is the year is a 12 minute documentary about football fans in Ankara. They are known as holigans. But they are interesting because they have more fans than even the biggest football teams in Turkey such as Galatasaray and Fenerbahçe. Ankaragücü was in 2rd league in Turkey during the shoot of documentary. But in the and they have been promoted. The documentary focuses on ''How the fans effected this success?''.
