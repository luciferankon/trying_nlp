Known primarily as one of Brodus Clay's Funkadactyls Naomi surprised the entire WWE Diva roster by emerging the winner of the pre-show Battle Royal. Does she have what it takes to unseat Eve whose impressive Divas title reign is approaching the 100 day mark? The high flying tandem of Rey Mysterio and Sin Cara tries to soar through the tag team ranks for a shot at the Titles held by Team Hell No. Standing in their way is the dangerous team of the intellectual Damien Sandow and the newly mustachioed Cody Rhodes.
