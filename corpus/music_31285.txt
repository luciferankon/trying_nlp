DEAN MARTIN: ENCORE features Dino at the top of his form singing many of his greatest most-enduring songs at the height of his popularity. Handsome charming equally at home in heavy dramas or slapstick comedies Dean Martin was above all one of the most-popular and most-imitated singers of the twentieth century and this fabulous DVD is ample proof of his remarkable versatility. No fan of pop music - or the Rat Pack - should miss out on this great collection!
