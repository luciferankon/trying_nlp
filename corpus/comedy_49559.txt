A widow goes to Italy from South Africa to find out whether or not her late husband's five nephews are worthy of his inheritance. She poses as the lawyer who wrote the will and lets the real lawyer pass for herself. This way she will be able to discover the five men's vices and faults. Michelino Cesaretto Alfonso Giuseppe and Piero Pasti are brothers. Each of them has a negative trait: Michelino is notorious for his jealousy for his wife Clara; Alfonso is penniless but he calls himself count as he married a countess now dead; Giuseppe is a miser; Cesaretto is lazy and Piero is a womanizer. When they receive a wire announcing the arrival of the widow of a wretched uncle emigrated to South Africa they think they have to lodge an old and poor aunt...
