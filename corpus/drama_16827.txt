Sam Ach Romain and Emma are pure products of the techno generation. They live at 200 miles per hour and vibrate at the rate of House Music. Of return of Bali their old dude and mentor Tod is warning of the end of their problems. Impatient to find him they go to the Full Moon Party the biggest techno evening of the summer. The club is crammed the DJ makes the pressure rise bodies touch and the party is going up. Only problem: Tod is untraceable.
