Jang Soo-jin is tricked into returning to South Korea by her father who wants an heir for his corporation. At the same time Kim Yong-nam a water bottle delivery man assumes a large debt to get some friends out of trouble. Jang Soo-jin and Kim Yong-nam end up using each other to try to solve their problems. Jang Soo-jin must pass off Kim Yong-nam as a Korean elite worthy of marriage to get her father to back off. and so the story begins.
