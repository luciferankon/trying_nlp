Ryan Black's fear becomes reality when he accidentally discovers his unique ability to travel across parallel dimensional planes. An ability viewed by many as taboo and dangerous. People with this ability are hunted down and captured or eliminated by an opposing force of operatives led by the autocratic Chancellor who seeks to maintain what he considers a dimensional balance. The Chancellor and his men pursue Ryan as they view him and others like him as a potential threat to mankind. Ryan must quickly master his new found abilities and uncover the truth behind the Chancellor's real objective before they find him first.
