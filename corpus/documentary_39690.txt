We all know how important it is to preserve and protect our fragile natural environment. With ever-greater and faster population growth and development staring us in the face it's more critical now than ever to live in harmony with the environment by preserving clean air conserving water and dealing with issues like recycling and open spaces. Huell Howser highlights what innovative and creative Californians are doing to solve environmental challenges and provides viewers with knowledge and practical ideas that they can implement in their daily lives.
