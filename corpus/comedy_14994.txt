The Village Idiot cleans up after the horses in the Village and takes us on a tour to the local restaurant plays a game of heads or tales and orders his favorite ice cream flavors. It all goes wrong luckily he calls his friend Dave to share in his troubles. Vidiots is a comedy comprised of four separate vignettes. The Village Idiots goes to the restaurant Heads or tales Village Idiots goes to the ice cream store and news stand. Each episode ending with a call to his friend Dave recapping the show.
