Inspired by the novel 'Pamietnik Wendy' ('Wendy's Diary') by Ena Kielska 'Nerve' is a psychological horror film set in a dying city in Central Europe concerning a madness-inducing book written by a factory girl human trafficking the sigils of a bird-headed goddess and a Ukrainian cinematographer in a white van looking for a woman who has dug herself out of a shallow grave. First part in a trilogy: 'Nerve' 'Noise God' & 'Laughing Men'.
