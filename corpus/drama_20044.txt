Marie leads a calm and happy life withe her boyfriend in a nice suburban neighborhood. After a night out in a bar with her colleagues she wakes up dizzy in a cab with no memories of the night. What could Marie say to her boyfriend Richard when he suspects her of cheating on him? What if Antoine a simple auto parts delivery man has the key to this enigma. Antoine and Marie traces a troubling portrait of two people's destinies forever united by one dramatic event... Her rape.
