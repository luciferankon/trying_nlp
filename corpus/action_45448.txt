Nines Ranks is a no-nonsense female drug dealer in Philadelphia. With the help of her sister Tee and female Gangsta Grrlz she bails out of prison Ninea plans to make a mint in the drug game. Unfortunately in this male dominated business Ninea is constantly tested but comes through with flying colors...or should I say... bullets. On the other side of town is Dion... her competition. The two are constantly pushing the lines to gain more territory. After Dion is murdered by an ambitious underling Ninea refuses a deal from the new boss to join their businesses together. Harsh words between the two fuel the battle to see who will be left standing to run both neighborhoods.
