Film "Patranq" ("Illusion") is about a man living in the world of his sick imagination. In the film "Khosq" ("Speech") the pilots are afraid to take off a plane after awful statements on the board about the possible exploding. Late passenger manages to relieve all of the fear with happy cheerful words. Film "Gortzoghutyun" ("Action") is about a strange village where the hero is visited by strange visions that could only be seen by people of special spiritual sight.
