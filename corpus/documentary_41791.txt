Beijing Girl: Made in China is a candid thought provoking insider's view of Beijing which includes background history from the opium wars through the cultural revolution to present day. Phabey wang explores her hometown of Beijing with a new perspective now that she lives in Los Angeles. Beijing Girl is a touching informative view of the city both literal and figuratively via striking shots about town and heartwarming interviews with four generations of Beijing relatives who take us on the emotional and real journey that is their lives.
