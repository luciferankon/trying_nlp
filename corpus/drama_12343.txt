A dramatic comedy about a young African American man named Ron who is determined to save a business that was handed down to him by his late uncle. After receiving news that the neighborhood including his center will be deconstructed it is up to him to save his friends and community meanwhile dealing with personal issues of his own and the community that is in turmoil due to the many police shootings that have occurred.
