Jasmine Harris is a high school senior getting ready to attend college at John Hopkins University. She has the looks the brains and soon the man. Everything is working out for her until a decision turns her life upside down forever. How far will one go to persevere? How would you feel being an honor student; a talented athlete with the pick of any school or scholarship? You would feel pretty amazing wouldn't you? Now picture that amazing feeling being snatched away after ONE decision that alters many lives. How would you feel then?
