Ruben grow up with his grandmother since his father died and his mother decided to marry again. His childhood was unhappy and he blamed his mother for his misery. Later out of need for money he becomes a hired gun for a syndicate. Despite all these he still tries to keep a personal conviction against illegal drugs. So when he learns that his cousin and wife are into drugs he decides to liquidate the members of the drug syndicate but he gets caught. And this starts another hapless chapter in his life...
