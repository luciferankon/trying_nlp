Los Angeles mid-eighties a decadent city in free-fall. Several morally challenged characters mostly interrelated intersect in dangerous and sometime lethal combination of bad decisions failed plans and vacuous life styles. These stylish group of wealthy twenty-something's spend most of their free time clubbing dancing screwing popping and sniffing their way down a destructive path of incessant drug use deceit violence deviant sex and murder. Privilege has an ugly price.
