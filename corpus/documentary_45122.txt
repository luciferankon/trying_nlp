On March 25th 1990 The Happy Land Social Club in Bronx NY was filled with people having a good time. Music dancing and laughter filled the place. At approximately 3:30am a fire would break out in the hallway which led to the only known exit of the unlicensed establishment. That night 87 people would die...but that was just part of the story. After more than 20 years the whole story unfolds.
