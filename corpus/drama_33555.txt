The lovely Beba Marturano recently graduate with a degree in architecture. She has always lived good her only suffering has been the lost of her father Tomas Marturano who died in a suspicious car accident. Beba grew up hating Mara Marturano and Brando Leon. Beba meets a young hard worker named Urbano Duran. They fall in love and marry. But their happiness is short when in an accident Beba loses her memory and ends up in the house of Brando Leon. During her amnesia she falls in love with Brando. Now Urbano does everything to make Beba remember him their love and life.
