The security guard Luther Bentley begins hearing things one night at his warehouse and charges out with a flashlight and his gun to investigate. It turns out that he is hallucinating and delirious and eventually he collapses in front of the warehouse though not before calling Emergency Services. It turns out he's been taking medicine for a cold but that he also has heart medication. While Luther recuperates in the hospital he learns how to avoid mixing drugs. A security guard at a warehouse Luther Bentley accidentally mixes his cold medication with his prescription medication and falls victim to the resulting side effects. After waking up in the hospital he learns how to avoid mixing drugs in the future.
