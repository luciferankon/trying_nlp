Amy a young traveler with fragile identity is rescued by Lobsang a Tibetan lama who takes her under his wing and leads her in a spiritual journey into the depth of a cave where the young woman will meditate during seven days. Their beliefs will be put to test while Lobsang and Amy will confront their ideas and try to untie the knots of resistance that hinder their spiritual progression. Inspired by a true story Amy and Lobsang's account depicts a cultural encounter that will forever transform this western woman and the Tibetan Lama. A window on Tibetan Buddhism Lobsang exposes the psychological transformation of a young lady helped by a Tibetan Lama who seeks to keep control of this most novel and unexpected relationship.
