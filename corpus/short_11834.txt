On a distant planet of red sky and multiple moons a lone wanderer Bobby Young gets caught up in the affairs of its crazed inhabitants. On his travels he attempts to survive the perils of a mercenary machine gunner and chaotic potion swigging green haired scientist but an encounter with a lone cartoon bird leads Bobby to a wise old man who convinces him to help out a young girl. With the moon man raising darkness across the world Bobby must face his fears and save the girl from the evil warriors.
