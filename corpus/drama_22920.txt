A plastic collector named Sahir refuses to pass judgment on fate when his son is wounded and the outbreak of war proves him right. Young Bouba is given the task of answering the question "What is the greatest part of all knowledge?" His search leads him to love. And Sonam sets out across the Himalayas to sell his dzo but the journey proves useful despite the fact that he doesn't sell the animal. All three stories are presented in the form of traditional songs which intermingle in this engaging on-screen narrative. The characters are always seeking after something thus enabling the movie to grip the viewer's attention. This voyage of discovery through Rajasthan Mali and Ladakh contains three songs three stories and myriad timeless wisdom. A dynamic film based on oral tales with lovely characters which celebrates the narrative art form and the type of human simplicity that offers sound advice for our everyday lives.
