In the waning days of the Soviet Union a Russian military officer orders the release of an experimental chemical weapon even going so far as to murder one of her fellow officers to see that it is unleashed. The weapon is soon loose turning those who are exposed to it into flesh-eating zombies. A young man hears and sees this unfolding disaster on the TV and radio. Society soon disintegrates and the protagonist sets out for a safe havenalong with three of his friends. The allies travel across the continent battling masses of zombies. However they quickly find that the deadliest threats are the human ones. They discover a secret that may be vitally important but they survive long enough to get it to someone who can use it for good?
