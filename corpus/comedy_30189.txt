This is a comedy portraying the antics of three brothers - Paaji Rajveer and Ravi who get involved in a situation which exposes the truth of Dera Babas and the events happening in their ashrams in Punjab. A hilarious packed with punch lines and comical situations this film is a humorous expose of the self styled Godmen who make their living by duping innocent people and carry on illegal and harmful activities within their ashram all under the garb of religion. Motor Mitraan Di talks about the values of family loyalty love and cautions people against being cheated by unscrupulous and corrupt Godmen who have ruined countless lives. But Says everything with a smile or a laugh..
