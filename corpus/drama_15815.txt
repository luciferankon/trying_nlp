Kashinath Sawant a State Transport bus conductor lives with his family in a small village. One day family are wrongly slammed with a huge electricity bill. They feel trapped by the circumstances and humiliated by the indifferent bureaucracy. The Right to Information Act accidentally crosses Kashinath's path. With the support of his driver friend Saiyyed and under the guidance of activist Dr. Durga Kashinath his family regains their honor.
