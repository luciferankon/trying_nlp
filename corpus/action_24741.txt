Young Jewish girl brutalized by Nazi's 1938 flees Germany to the US with surviving family to live and work in American Doctor's home. She returns to Hitler's aggression in North Africa as a Nurse. Falls in love with older married American Doctor CO of Hospital. Follows him to South Africa comes face to face with the SS who murdered her family is raped but has her revenge by killing him. Passing at 102 she never spoke of her heritage for fear of retribution nor of her unrequited love.
