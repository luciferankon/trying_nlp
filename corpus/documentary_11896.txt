Ravenna vs. Kent. Adjacent communities with intense pride in their winning football traditions. The first game was played in 1911. In 2014 after 100 games the record is Kent 49 wins Ravenna 48 wins and 3 ties. "The 100th Game" reviews the 103 year timeline of this legendary rivalry and contains plays from the 100th game. Sharing 60 years of memories from the rivalry are head coaches Tom Campana John Nemec Jim Lunardi and first year head coach Tim King.
