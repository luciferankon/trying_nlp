The Arbuckle Simpson Aquifer has sustained much of South-Central Oklahoma as a source of drinking water for approximately 150000 residents. For generations the Arbuckle Simpson has served as the source of municipal and rural water districts water for agricultural use recreational use as well as robust economic development. The membership of The Citizens for the Protection of the Arbuckle Simpson Aquifer (CPASA) is made up of of citizens from throughout Oklahoma and other states who came together in response to threats to the aquifer.
