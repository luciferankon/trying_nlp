Detroit Promo TV is a techno based show where artists talk about their motivations and life choices while emphasizing the importance of dance music in an industry dominated by rap music. Detroit Promo TV also shows the positive side of Detroit by showcasing local business art and everything Positive In Detroit! Detroit Promo TV's mission is to create content that entertains educates and inspires it's viewers. Detroit Promo TV offers programming that expands the mind entertains the soul and fulfills the music void.
