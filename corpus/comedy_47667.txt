Ramblings of dancing Rebels in Berlin is the first feature film produced by HolyRock Studios. Starring David Zavodny the movie tells the story of a man very little people seem to notice as he experiences all the facets of what constitutes May 1 in the Kreuzberg area of Berlin. Bounced around by the impulsions of his friends and the rambunctious spirit of the city he lives in he is witness to the day's blandest and most surreal moments experiencing Berlin's reputation under all its seams through music riots parties and unexpected acquaintances.
