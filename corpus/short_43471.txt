Open 'til 3 is the name of the film and the restaurant where numerous stories intersect resulting in an evening no one will soon forget. A waitress Angela meanders from table to table while she carefully and systematically creates an explosion of emotion involving everyone simultaneously. Tonight Angela subtlety alters the lives of a militant vegan some rowdy youths a mysterious older man a compulsive gambler an honest cook and many others. Angela reverses the servant roles in this restaurant and by the end of the film has her customers right where she wants them. Follow 13 people through this comedic exploration as they ask the question " Who is serving whom?"
