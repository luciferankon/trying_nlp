Cattleman Benson finds Mildred and her brother George living in one of his cabins and their sheep are on his land. Attracted to Mildred he not only lets her stay he deeds part of his land to her. This leads to trouble with the other cattlemen. Remade in 1934 as "The Sundown Trail" with Wally Wales by Robert Emmett Tansey a 25-minute "featurette" that served to prove that less is better an attribute seldom used in connection with any Robert Tansey offering.Yakima Canuttthe star of "Canyon Hawks" also shows up in the remake as an uncredited stuntman. Cattleman Jack Benson finds Mildred Manning and her brother George living in one of the line-shacks on his ranch and their grizzly sheep grazing away at his grass. Smitten with Mildred he not only lets them stay he even deeds this part of his ranch to them. This leads to trouble with other cattleman and also with an outlaw gang known as the Canyon Hawks led by Steve Knowles. Actually the $500 reward poster posted by Sheriff Jackson identifies the gang as "The Hawks."
