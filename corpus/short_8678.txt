A singer is sent abroad by her husband to study. In order to maintain her in Paris he impoverishes himself. When she returns she secures an engagement in the opera meeting with great success and has her head turned by the flattering attentions of representatives of society forgetting the man whose sacrifices enabled her to be thus successful. One night a fire breaks out in the theater in which she is rescued by her faithful husband whose heroic act wins for him the return of his wife's old affection.
