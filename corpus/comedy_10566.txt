Madhav Bhatt is a simple bank clerk living with his wife Seema who is a staunch devotee of one Anna Maharaj. Their only daughter Ashoo is married to Babanrao Thorat an auto rickshaw driver who is always at loggerheads with Madhav. Vatsala a poor woman who makes a living selling Vada's always starts her sales from Madhav's house. Unable to resist his urge to have meat he requests Vatsala to bring him some hot spicy chicken in the night as his wife would be coming late. A simple urge soon snowballs into a series of mishaps and misunderstandings.
