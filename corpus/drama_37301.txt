Italy1915:In the Alberti family's castle live the count's widow and her elder sonEnrico slightly crippled who seems to be at one with the Austrianswho occupy and dominate the land.The youngest sonFranco a talented piano playerperforms concerts all over the country.He is also one of the leaders of the resistance fighters.Polish opera singer Elisabeth is his devoted collaborator as well as his mistress.But during a visit to the castle he falls in love with an Austrian lieutenant 's niece and wardAnne andin spite of his brother's disapproval and jealousy  marries her.Then he joins the army who fights the Austrians.
