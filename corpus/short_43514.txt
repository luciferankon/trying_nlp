A bible study goes helter-skelter when pill-poppin' Crown and Coke swillin' Johnnie (Gloria Loring) shows up unannounced at the home of her daughter Joy (Melanie Holmes). Within minutes Johnnie is using her affiliation with Mary Kay to sell black-market Botox to Joy's well-to-do church pals. Joy must intervene before her own secrets are laid bare and her world toppled.
