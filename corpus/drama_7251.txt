Seamus an an aging boxer suffering from the brain damaging condition of CTE. Despite warnings for a neurologist Seamus continues to pursue boxing because it is all he knows and all he is. His pursuit for his identity jeopardizes his life and his marriage. Seamus is further manipulated by his coach and lifelong friend Bruce who sees Seamus as little more than a cash cow. Inspired by the short film "The Gatekeeper" and the short story "The Gatekeeper" "The Pugilist is a deeper examination of the dangers of CTE in athletes as well as the need athletes have to inscribe their identity through their sport. "The pugilist" is an adaptation of the novel "The Pugilist" by Jeffrey Kelley currently in pre-media.
