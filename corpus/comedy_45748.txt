Shalom TV series follows four singles two men and two women who first meet through an online Jewish dating service. Trailed by a reality documentary crew the twentysomethings/thirtysomethings seek advice from friends family and even their faith to navigate the choppy relationship waters. Series creator head writer and director Sloan Copeland blends humor and heart into story lines that explore the difficulties and rewards men and women in their 20s and early 30s encounter when they plunge into the dating scene.
