Stars avid bicyclist Terry Taylor who has lived his life on "two wheels" since 1944. Follow Terry's adventures on his 2016 40th anniversary of Bikecentennial retracing his 1976 path beginning in Astoria Oregon and ending in Virginia. It's about passion fortitude and determination proving that at 78 past multiple bike accidents knee replacements broken bones and broken relationships Terry will persevere and his life-long joy of the bicycle will triumph over any challenges along the way.
