Julie Ufema decided to make a movie not a short - not a teaser but a feature length film. To bring this dream to life having no knowledge of filmmaking outside of acting she solicited the help of those who did and admittedly some who didn't. Wake Me When You're Dead will reveal the good the bad and the bloody behind the seemingly unsurmountable task of making an award winning feature length film with little more than a ton of moxie a great location and group of people willing to do just about anything to make it happen.
