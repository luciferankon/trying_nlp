Daniela and Paolo decide to take a vacation in Vang Vieng in Laos practicing tubing rafting and having fun. After an impromptu rave on the river followed by downhill tubing the two get Lost in Laos. Having also lost their belongings they begin to wander around a different Laos to the one they pictured. In Italy the parents become alarmed meanwhile Daniela and Paolo embark on a revelatory journey becoming the guests of a small village where they receive care hospitality and friendship.
