Dev sells his family coffee estate as a statement underlining his renunciation of the world. When his mother dies heartbroken he vows to prove worthy of her before scattering her ashes little realizing that that involves confronting the world he shunned and his turbulent past. Having forced his mom to sell their ancestral coffee estate in spite of her reluctance Dev feels extremely bad when she dies heartbroken that he preserves her ashes and vows to get the estate back to prove his worth. However complications arise when he finds that the current owner of the estate is his former-lover Anika who is now married and living at the estate with her exuberant husband.
