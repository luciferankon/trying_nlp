Late in the 21st century a 5 year bloody war engulfed the world...afterwards mankind struggled to rebuild. An evil power rose from the ashes bent on the homogenization of mankind. Controlled by fear the world's decimated population is kept in underground silos. Vowing to never allow for an uprising by the underclass The Organization has begun the task of creating an ultimate unfeeling and invincible army. Armed with Chromosome Fusion technology The Organization has sanctioned a series of Ultimate Fighting competitions. The goal of these battles being to harness the genes of the winners to be used to create the Ultimate Fighter a fighter who's DNA will be duplicated over and over the create the perfect fighting force.
