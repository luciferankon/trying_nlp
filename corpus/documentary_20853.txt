Voice Not Violence explores issues concerning youth violence and nonviolence. The 42-minute video program is narrated by Jason Alexander star of the NBC comedy series "Seinfeld". With interviews of over 30 diverse teenagers the share thoughts on what youth of today are thinking about when it comes to the issues of violence and what they are doing to create a non-violent environment in their communities. Some of the questions that are addressed in the program include "What is Violence ?" "How do you deal with anger ?" "Have you ever been involved with physical verbal and/or visual violence... and how did you deal with it ?" and "What is non-violence ?". Some of the youth projects highlighted in the video include a self defense class peer mediation Teen Court a low-rider bicycle club and the Youth Environmental Summit.
