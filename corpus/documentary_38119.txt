Emanuelle hosts this peculiar sexploitation Mondo film that looks at several examples of bizarre sexual behavior. Opening with a sexual mystic ceremony it moves on to views of modern night club including a woman trying to seduce a mule into bestiality. An unorthodox penile enlargement operation is shown plus lesbian sex championships. Outside of the modern world there is footage of a boar slaughtering in New Guinea interspersed with scenes of sex. Like many Mondo films most of this footage is staged but still provides a fascinating view into Italian exploitation cinema.
