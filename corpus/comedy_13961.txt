Comedian Felipe Esparza in his second 1-hour special - this time on HBO. This time Felipe's material gets more personal and focuses on his experience growing up as an immigrant in the United States while still being very much an American. We hear more stories of the environment that created the man - gangs drugs growing up in the projects etc. He also talks about being a teen father a horrible one at that as well as his marriage to his long-time wife and business partner who happens to be white.
