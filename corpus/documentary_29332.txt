Into the Blue explores the world-renowned Bluecoats Drum & Bugle Corps' 2011 season and their dynamic & unique Program "Brave New World" featuring the music of Radiohead Michael Daugherty and John Mackey. From the first notes of their audition camps through the final steps of their performance at Lucas Oil Stadium in Indianapolis Indiana for the 2011 Drum Corps International World Championships join the members and staff of the Canton Ohio corps as they experience the triumphs and tribulations of their competitive season.
