Viral Victim follows the unforeseen 10 seconds that altered Jordan Peisner's life forever. Jordan a 14- year old boy is sucker punched by another teenage boy leaving him unconscious who then shared the incident on snapchat making it go viral. The director and producers begin their journey exposing the story behind this vicious crime following Jordan's condition to how the parents have been trying to find the silver lining and turn a horrible experience into a positive change in society by creating the Jordan Strong Movement and Jordan's Law.
