Nimrod's kingdom is about to face its greatest challenger in a young boy named Abraham. While little is known of Abraham's childhood in the bible the Midrash presents a mighty tale of this courageous boy who stands toe to toe against the most powerful ruler of the ancient world. For the ancient city of Ur to the lush hills of Canaan Abraham's journey is filled with adventure danger and plenty of laughs while inspiring you. This is fun entertainment the whole family will enjoy!
