MotoGP Tonight is BT Sport's brand new weekly MotoGP show - presented by BT Sport's Craig Doyle and former Olympian and European and Commonwealth games 400 metres champion and biker Iwan Thomas This is a studio-based show for bike fans featuring the best racing action from the weekend special guests from the paddock and a host of motorcycling fun and games for the audience to take part in. Craig and Iwan will also be joined by an array of guests from the motorbike world.
