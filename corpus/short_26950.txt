When Ben Evans arranges an interview at an Equestrian Center his 14 year old daughter Jorgie must prove herself not only to the daunting hunter-jumper trainer Susana but to Susana's riding students the very same scornful self-important It Girls who make Jorgie's life miserable at school. Rising to the Trainer's challenge Jorgie matches wits with Stella a cagey old show horse who puts the girl through her paces. Her efforts draw the attention of handsome 16 year old Zando further inflaming the It Girls animosity.
