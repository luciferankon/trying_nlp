Lena a strong independent Greek woman 5 years after knowing about her heart-brother's dissapearance travels to Africa with her rich fiancée by her side trying to find out what really happened. Firstly she meets her brother's partner. A mysterious guy named Zatelis also Greek married to an even more mysterious (masai-origin) african woman named Randa. Lena is attracted to Zatelis's dark character more and more and she decides to extend her staying in Africa to get inspired for a book she is writing. Only that her fiancée and Randa won't just let it go..
