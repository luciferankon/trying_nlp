In the midst of a planetary crisis for food and fuel a vast ecological cataclysm has spawned massive beasts unlike anything ever witnessed. Displaced from atop the food-chain humankind struggles to stave off extinction in the wake of the monstrous rampage. Amidst the ashes of the world's largest cities search and rescue teams wade into the ruins in hopes of locating children orphaned by the cataclysm. But not all the remaining humans think alike and in a world dominated by monsters man is still the most dangerous animal of all - seek shelter.
