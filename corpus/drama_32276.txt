In order to better their lifestyle the husband of a Goa-based family which consists of his wife and three young children decides to travel abroad in a boat. His family bid a tearful farewell and hope that he returns home soon. Shortly thereafter the family will be devastated when they get the news that the boat has sunk in a storm leaving no survivors. The wife destitute and distressed loses everything. Unable to bear to see her children starve she decides to marry her husband's wealthy friend - a male she dislikes. The marriage takes place and the family re-locate to live in their palatial home. Years later the children have grown up with their step-father - little knowing that their biological father is still alive - but refuses to re-enter their lives.
