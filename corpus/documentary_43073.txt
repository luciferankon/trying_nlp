Why is the Black Woman Angry?" is not your typical documentary....but it's not your typical mockumentary either. What it IS is a funny yet insightful & informed look into the mind of the 2012 American's perception of the Black woman. WHO are we? WHAT do we look like? WHEN did this stereotype become our defining characteristic? WHY have we become complacent with being looked at as ONE type? WHERE is our place and our voice in 2012? In this raw and in-the-moment style of film you will come along with Farelle and Kiya on their journey as they discover answers to these questions and uncover some surprising truths about themselves!! You will laugh you may cry you will be moved and affected you will learn you will discuss you may disagree....but above all.....you will QUESTION. And questions are ALWAYS the start of a journey to personal TRUTH.
