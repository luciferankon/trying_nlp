A tough talking chicken from the future goes back in time to change the out come of the 1995 women's All State basketball championship. While there he meets his own grandmother and falls madly in love. Unfortunately as he soon finds out love has been out lawed by the Evil tyrant McPickles a hard nosed duck billed playpus. Can he save the day and nail his own grandmother before the clock strikes 3:47pm and the time/space portal collapses in on itself creating a rift in time that forever renders him William Shatner? You must watch to find out! 3D glasses recommended!!!
