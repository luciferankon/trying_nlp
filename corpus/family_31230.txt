Thomas lights up the tracks in a magical Christmas collection! Climb aboard for adventures and cheer as your favorite engines prepare for a festive celebration. Help Thomas find the grandest tree on Sodor and then join the Logging Locos as they sing the Misty Island Snow Song in the hope of a winter wonderland. As the snowflakes fall Gordon Mavis and Percy will take you to the Steamworks for a special surprise party. 'Tis the season for holiday magic with Thomas and Friends! Features the episodes: Let it Snow Surprise Surprise Percy the Snowman and Tree Trouble. The UK release features the episodes: The Christmas Tree Express Ho Ho Snowman Salty's Surprise Emily's Winter Party Special Percy the Snowman and Tree Trouble.
