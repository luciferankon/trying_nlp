A found footage film tells the story of a sound technician and film graduate who take on the case of the McKinnely's house unsolved haunting's. Sound technician and sceptic Scott Deveroe aims to put the homes rumour's to rest by gathering accurate audio readings using a theory he has been researching called Infrasound. Accompanied by film graduate Maia Davies their experience in the McKinnely home is documented over the course of four days. But when the two investigators over stay their welcome the project takes a frightening downfall as they are tormented by an evil spirit who doesn't like to play fair.
