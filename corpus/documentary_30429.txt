The brave story of a Cystic Fibrosis sufferer as she awaits a double lung transplant. Kirstie Mills has always known that her life would be short. Born with Cystic Fibrosis she was put onto the transplant waiting list in March 2011 and told that without new lungs she could be dead within 6 months. As her health rapidly declined Kirstie had to decide whether to rest and wait for the lung transplant or to live her life however long it would last. This film follows Kirstie's extraordinary experience of living on the transplant waiting list and the realities of constant pain and endless medication. During her journey Kirstie is rushed to hospital just three days before the wedding- will she make it down the aisle? And more importantly will she get the transplant she so desperately needs?
