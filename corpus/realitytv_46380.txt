A real married couple is blindfolded and dropped in remote regions to read the clues solve the mystery and make their best guess for which way to go. They don't always agree and the stakes are a lot higher than simply making a wrong turn it may be days of suffering and miles of struggling through the jungle if they are wrong. They must also use all their skills to read the sings like the plants animals winds stars and more to navigate back to civilization. They are given minimal gear a knife 1 high tech gadget 1 feminine gadget and some other seemingly worthless items and it is up to them to figure out how to make the most of it as they must do whatever it takes to survive in the wild along the way and hopefully survive their marriage too!
