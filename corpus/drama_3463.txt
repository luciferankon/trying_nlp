Jason's newest case takes him to a small town in Alabama to convince a small successful import company to sellout to a large New York Investment firm. During his investigation Jason discovers that the reasons behind the small company's success during such a struggling time in American economics may not be legitimate and there are certain people involved who are willing to kill to keep their business practices secret. Jason "Ghost" Campbell is a very successful corporate hit man for hire. His job requires him to convince unwilling Fortune 500 or small business owners to do things that they would not do on their own. Jason uses all means of convincing both legal and illegal.
