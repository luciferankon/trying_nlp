A new action horror film by writer/director Kelly D. Weaver. In the vein of Blue Exorcist Black Lagoon and a little bit of Supernatural. A group of hunters known as "The Night Shift" are funded by the government sets out to take on all cases dealing with the creatures of the night. Vampires Werewolves Demons and Succubus rule Crescent City. It's up to the Night Shift to take the city back.
