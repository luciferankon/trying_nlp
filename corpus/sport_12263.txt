A legendary event in Japan PRIDE FC matches the world's fiercest fighters in competitions of strength and honor featuring an intense mix of combative sports such as wrestling boxing jiu-jitsu karate judo kickboxing and others. Originating in 1997 PRIDE FC has quickly earned a reputation for creating a fight experience that trumps most rock concerts with its mind-blowing action and theatrical and cultural spectacle. Boasting an enormous fan following PRIDE events consistently sell out arenas around the world of up to 74000 seats.
