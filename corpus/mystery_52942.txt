Love comes in all forms and guises but when a strange and beautiful young gypsy [Leah] arrives in the middle of the night at a country house in Oxfordshire the only resident's [Rachel] deepest desires are awakened and unleashed. But all is far from normal. The local community appear disturbed and frightened by this new arrival in their safe and quiet community. A series of unexpected accidents and incidents are blamed on the gypsy and her uncanny powers. Is she a demonic presence? Does she have a gift? What secrets do the villagers protect? A strange tale of sexual lust affirmation of love and smoldering desire against a backdrop of horror hatred and mystery.
