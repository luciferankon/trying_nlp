Almost There is the story of Theo a mentally disabled young man who is subject to the prejudice and taunts of a small town and a big bully. Katherine is an independent confident young girl her future ahead of her who won't stand to see the big attack the small. Jake is quietly moving through life with his friends and Katherine. The strong independent girl he's loved as long as he can remember. The story of one love one friendship and a whole town's loss will have you questioning the fairness and the fragility of life and shows the hardship faced by those left behind. Almost there is a story of life loss and unspoken love that will test you and leave you with a sense of hope.
