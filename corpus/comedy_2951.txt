With more Taiwanese students studying at Japanese universities in recent years Japanese teachers need to better understand these students. When Onizuka (AKIRA) is invited for a one-week teaching exchange program at a Taiwanese high school he is eager for the experience and takes his friend Ryuuji (Yuu Shirota) with him. On their first day there Onizuka and Ryuuji meet Saejima (Yusuke Yamamoto) a local legend.
