A depressed and lonely computer technician is tortured by the complete lack of support in his current relationship. He turns his attention to a phone sex operator for his required emotional well being. The two of them develop a fast friendship and begin to care for each other greatly. Discussing life with their best friends at a common coffee shop leads to a needed slip up. Great care must be taken to not break company rules but a chance meeting may change the rules. An angry girlfriend disgruntled parents religion and then decisions that can change all of their futures forever.
