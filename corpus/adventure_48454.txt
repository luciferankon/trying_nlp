Mujoe and the Hige Huge Bandits fed up with their plans for universal domination being squelched by Bomberman unleash their most desperate plan yet; they intend to pilot the jet powered "Dark Star" straight into Planet Bomber and utterly destroy it. With only 24 hours until the Dark Star strikes Bomberman and MAX with assistance from the Jetters make landfall on the Dark Star in order to shut down the large jets and stop it dead in its tracks...
