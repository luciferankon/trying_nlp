A documentary about the on-going conflict on the island of Cyprus between the Greek-Cypriots and the Turkish-Cypriots. American journalist Varnavas Zagaris travels to his homeland to explore the topic and in the process talks with Greek-Cypriot and Turkish-Cypriot citizens and officials as well as with the UN and various ambassadors. An informative and objective view on the conflict and the on-going issue of the missing persons is presented.
