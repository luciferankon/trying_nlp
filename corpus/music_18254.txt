Video Inspires by Nelson Scott If you've enjoyed some of the music made popular in the 90's and early 2000's then you've experienced the skills of Nelson B. Scott. He helped create the signature sounds for a number of popular artists. He mastered the bass guitar trombone and keyboard as a member of Robert Newton and Lotusfete band based in South Carolina. Scott holds degrees from the University of South Carolina and Allen University. Although Scott has a soft side that led him to work in the field of child protective services for ten years he served as a law enforcement officer near Atlanta GA. His latest endeavors in the entertainment industry led him to create and host Video Inspires a video music countdown program featuring Christian gospel and inspirational music genres based on Billboard's top 100 rankings.
