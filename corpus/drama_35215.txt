Kyle Flanagan (Robert Mitchel Owenby) has no job no home and no family. The only thing keeping him grounded is his relationship with his boyfriend Casey Brandt (Nick Izzo). But when their relationship grows more serious and he begins to fall for a woman in his group therapy sessions Kyle must make a decision. Does he continue to lead on his boyfriend or reveal the truth: he is not actually gay. From award-winning writer/director Braeden Orr comes the first web series from Single Serving Pictures. Starring Robert Mitchel Owenby Nick Izzo Christina Leidel and Amy McCoy. Produced by Braeden Orr and Meaghan Davis in conjunction with the SCAD-Atlanta Film & Television Graduate Program.
