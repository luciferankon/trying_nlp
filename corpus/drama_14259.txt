One fateful day Natali Garcia is going about her business when she meets an attractive young man. Due to a culmination of events; she never learns of his true name before it is too late and he is gone from her life. Expecting never to see this man again Natali starts to forget about him simply naming him Spencer so she can remember this fateful day. Five years later Natali is married successful and wealthy. However despite her apparent "perfection" in life she still yearns to once again meet Spencer. What transpires is a slow downward spiral into obsession. She sees Spencer on street corners in shop windows and even at work. As her sanity slowly starts to unravel Natali realizes she must face her obsession before it tears her life apart from the inside.
