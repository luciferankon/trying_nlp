A coming-of-age comedy that follows D. Ray Morton a small-town have-not who has grown up to be a conspicuous big-city socialite. But after a human resource audit reveals an overstated educational background Ray loses his job his gaudy paycheck and in turn his identity. In an attempt to salvage his career and self-worth Ray returns home to feebly check the boxes on this embellished resume. While there Ray comes to find out that his obituary not his resume will be the true measure of his worth.
