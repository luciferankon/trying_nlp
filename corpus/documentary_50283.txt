The Odyssey is a documentary about the importance of outdoor education for students. The film follows a grade 7/8 class from a Montessori school in Canada on their outdoor expedition pursuing knowledge about the world around them their peers and themselves. Student film maker Brynn Weigelt has a passion for film and the great outdoors. When she was attending Kanata Montessori School Brynn also had the opportunity to experience the odyssey trip and she states that it changed her life for the better. Brynn embarked on this journey yet again to share this once in a lifetime experience with the world.
