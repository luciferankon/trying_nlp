Virudhagiri is a 2010 Indian Tamil action film written and directed by Vijayakanth making his directorial debut besides playing the title character as well.The film co-starring Madhuri Itagi Arun Pandian and Mansoor Ali Khan among others released on 10 December 2010. The film is a remake of the English movie Taken.
