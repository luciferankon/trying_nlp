Mr. Peach a foster dad is fed up with the wild tales he is hearing about portals pixies and witches. As two magical women try to convince him the children are not lying a cat Semaphore Sam comes through the Bank Street portal right before his eyes. We follow Sam through the years with live action Aesop's Fables and find out why he keeps coming back and why he is a hero! Illustrations by Gretchen Ersham tell the final story about how Sam and Boy saved the passengers on Northern Pacific.
