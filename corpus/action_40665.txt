Long before he became the "Ghost of Sparta" Kratos was leader of the Spartan army. Nearing defeat at the hands of his enemies Kratos begged Ares the God of War to spare his life. Before Ares would save Kratos he needed to make sure that the young Spartan was worthy enough to be saved. Join Kratos in his very first 8-bit adventure to prove himself worthy to Ares.
