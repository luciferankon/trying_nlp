Alex a 25 year old kid at heart stills plays with action figures. Little does he know the action figures are actually alive. They kidnap the love of his life an cheap toy of a girl from a cartoon who is not alive. They hold her for ransom and tell Alex to stop playing with them saying he's too old to play with toys and to instead donate them. After giving it much thought Alex decides to donate them. As he's getting ready to get a box to put the toys in he catches the toys have betrayed him. Distraught by what he has seen the toys chase Alex and try to kill him. Alex must do whatever he can do survive.
