When a long police operation goes badly wrong resulting in the death of an undercover officer D.S. William Lawrence thinks that disgraced FBI Agent Nikki Fry can shed much needed light on one of their prime suspects club owner Melvin Anderson. Despite his superior's misgivings Fry is seconded to London to help tie Anderson into criminal operations on both sides of the Atlantic. Given Fry's obsessive nature will she turn out to be more of a hindrance than a help and who is really pulling the strings in the investigation?
