1987 Cardiff. A local newsagent is brutally attacked in his garden in Canton a suburb of Cardiff. Five days later he died from his injuries. A brutal police investigation followed. Three men were wrongly convicted of his murder. Michael O'Brien always protested His innocence this is the story of his fight to prove it to the system to the prisoners and to the corrupt police officers who framed him. Based on The Death of Justice: Guilty Until Proven Innocent by Michael O'Brien and Greg Lewis
