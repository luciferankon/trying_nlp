The Deal is a 2017 indie film that exalts the business deals on Wall Street. AJ Cross and Michael Hernandez star as mentor and protege in a game of high stakes and greed that define Wall Street. The rich and extravagant world of Wall Street is fully revealed when ruthless businessmen vie for power and control. But when things go too far the latest deal-makers find out that they might have asked for more than they bargained for.
