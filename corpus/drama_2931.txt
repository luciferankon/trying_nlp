Moonbeams is the coming of age story of Dave (Peter Richard) a young boy whose family is falling apart. After discovering his father is having an affair he begins having conversations with the moon. Voiced by veteran radio and television actor Art Gilmore (Dragnet Highway Patrol Adam 12). Castulo Guerra (The Mexican The Usual Suspects Terminator 2 Alias) and Mary Joan Negro (The Practice NYPD Blue) are the boy's psychologist and teacher who are both determined to uncover the truth about his imaginary friend. When Dave's family and friends think he is losing his mind mysterious events happen that change the lives of Dave and those around him forever.
