The inspiring true story of "Brundibar" an original opera which was performed by the children of Terezin a holding camp in Nazi-occupied Terezin Czech Republic. Terezin housed some of Europe's best musicians painters singers and actors who continued to practice their art in order to survive. Brundibar has since delighted and inspired audiences in 15 languages and 30 countries who marvel at how it exemplifies the power of art to transcend human suffering.
