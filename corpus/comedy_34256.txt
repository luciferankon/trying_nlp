A mad scientist Dr. Frankenweenie was bored and horny on a Saturday night so he decided to create himself a hermaphrodite. He named her "Dmentia Divinyl". The creature escaped and became a domesticated prostitute in a small town called "Pennsy-Tucky where she encounters various bizarre and dysfunctional people. Dmentia's psychologist Professor Dr. Scotty informs her to write her life events in a journal which she narrates as we explore a world of side show freaks and mental disorders. Will Dmentia ever become a mother? An actress? Or meet her dream man? You must watch and see for yourself as we explore her dark and twisted world in "Memoirs of Dmentia."
