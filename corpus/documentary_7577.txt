In the mean streets of Lisbon live some rather peculiar characters. Mário is special: former boxer ex-convict occasional gigolo and tourist guide he truly is the king of his neighborhood. Known and respected by all he his greeted by taxi drivers restaurant owners and policeman alike as he strolls through Rua de Santo Antăo an ancient pedestrian street deep in the center of Lisbon where the theatre's glamour mixes with the drifters.
