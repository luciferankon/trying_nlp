Thomas Hansen was one of the most uncompromising voices in Norwegian music. Haunted by the terrifying effects of mental illness his music mirrored his life candid chaotic and tinged with a sense of child- like wonder at the world around him. It was also painfully honest in documenting the fear and violence that ran throughout his life. Despite the trail of destruction that followed him in his wake Thomas Hansen engendered an unlikely humanity and devotion in those closest to him and it is through their memories and intimate archive recordings that this strange and uniquely poignant story is finally told.
