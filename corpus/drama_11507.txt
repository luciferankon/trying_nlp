The documentary Secrets of Tantra: Magic in Practice from Asia searches for the quintessence of ancient and brand-new conclusions. Eastern and Western mystics approach from different sides to the question of cause and effect consciousness and matter mind and body spirituality and love. Interviews with Indian and Tibetan masters or lamas represent a wide spectrum of insight. The film also includes an insight to the basic concept of sexual tantric practice for the first time. A fictional narrative level will show how western people typically deal with mind and body professional and private life pressure to perform and passion. Using illustrative dialogue and powerful imagery it will tell about daily life of a man and a woman Sam and Judy culminating in the collapse of their world view opening their mind for a new way of love inspired by tantric awareness.
