Rob Gordon doesn't know who he is or what he wants. He struggles to find himself in his relationship with his girlfriend Julie slowly aided along the way by his friend Rachael. A struggling photography major Rob meets his arch-rival Collin Briggs making Rob wonder whether he has what it takes in this competitive field but more importantly does he have what it takes to make the right decisions and changes in his life; prompting us to all ask how much have you changed in the year past?
