A story of a teenage girl in search of a miracle. An orphan left in the care of her aunt and uncle 'Dee' has struggled to fit in. After a brush with the law 'Dee' along with the help of her best friend 'Tee' decides to head for Emerald Texas to meet a radio pastor who promises miracles. Along the way she finds friends strength and empowerment... all from within. A story of a young girl in search of a miracle.
