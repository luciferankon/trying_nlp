Documents Tors' rise from studio screenwriter to anti-violence and animal rights maverick to Zoologist and Adventurer to leading producer of successful family films and programs which marked a specific Tors' style which reflected his sense of mission that traded sex and violence in film and TV for wholesome entertainment that also dramatically argued the case for wildlife conservation compassion for all animals and a wide range of humanistic causes. It is reasonable to assume that throughout the world TV viewers of all ages have seen at least one or more of the hundreds of TV shows and movies that Ivan Tors wrote produced and/or directed. At the peak of his fame and for years after the name Ivan Tors was associated with animals science drama and adventure.
