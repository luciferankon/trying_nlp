A father attempts to reconnect with his six-year-old daughter in the wake of his wife's sudden and tragic death. Traumatized the young girl retreats from her family only to find solace in a new and peculiar little friend who enters her life. As gruesome and untimely accidents leave neighbors and family members dead and with mounting evidence leading detectives to suspect the young girl her father must race to uncover the real culprit and save them both.
