The forbidden path. That's what the locals called it. Rumours are it is haunted. Anyone who goes does not return. So when Jack decides he will be the one to test the rumours by going there alone at night little does he realise he will come face to face with.....Ghost. After a narrow escape he recounts the events the next day. But when his best friend Summer insists on going back there Jack finds his harrowing experience was just the tip of the iceberg that follows.
