On primary basis the project deals with the scarcity of clean drinking water and sanitation facility at the micro level which will give a general idea of the circumstances at the macro level. From the very beginning documentary focuses on a particular family and shows the challenging circumstances in which they are trying to make a living. Their hygiene conditions the provision of clean drinking water and sanitation facilities their miserable lives their plight. Considering this intense situation where there's an absolute lack of provision of necessities Interviews will be conducted with some of the government officials and the concerned departments in relation to the subject matter trying to show the total absence of interest to curb this problem.
