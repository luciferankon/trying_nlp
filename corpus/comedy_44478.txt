MAZINYO DOT Q depicts the complications of a relationship begun in cyberspace continued in Gugulethu and ending who knows where! Charles quickly discovers that he is a fish out of water in a South African township. And his relationship doesn't go as smoothly as he had hoped - especially when the vain self-involved Thembi barely acknowledges his existence! Qondi has to convince her sister to go along with the deception to keep Charles around while simultaneously trying to find a way to show Charles that it is in fact Qondi who is the perfect woman for him.
