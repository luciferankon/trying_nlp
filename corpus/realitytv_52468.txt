The show features unusual collections of five collectors. Anthony Toth collects airline memorabilia and has been working on his replica of a first-class Pan Am cabin for over 20 years. Bob Grove (a.k.a. Barf Bag Bob) has been collecting airline barf bags since 1967. Audra Kunkle discusses her fascination and collection of the macabre. Many items can be seen at her store "Loved To Death". Musicians Richard and Flo Newman discuss their collection of "Neat Junk" with special focus on their signed toilet paper samples from around the world some of which are signed by celebrities. Artists Richard and Judith Lang collect found plastic trash gathered from Kehoe Beach and assemble it into amazing art installations to raise awareness about plastic pollution.
