After the plague that wiped out most of mankind a pilot who calls himself Hig his dog Jasper and a survivalist named Bangley reside in and near a small municipal airport in Colorado. Running perimeter checks with his 1950's era Cessna "The Beast" Hig hears a squawk on the air radio leading him down a rabbit hole that only loneliness and the need for loving contact with another being can drag him into. What Hig discovers outside of his safety zone and inside of the perimeter is both parts frightening and soothing to his damaged soul.
