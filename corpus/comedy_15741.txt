After a happily married man's wife sheds over 100 pounds his life gets turned upside down when she decides to pursue a long-time secret passion -- ACTING! To his surprise and dismay not only does his beautiful wife land a role in a huge blockbuster film she's also going to perform a steamy LOVE SCENE with one of the most eligible bachelor in Hollywood with a reputation of wooing his leading ladies. Fearful of losing his wife to a Hollywood hunk he's determined to stop at nothing to keep his wife from performing the dreaded love scene even if it means using a disguise sneaking into the set and sabotaging the production.
