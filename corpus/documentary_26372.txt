Leaving Europe to look for Akiko Jean-Noël discovers her city the exotic Tokyo. A documentary on everyday life in Tokyo its KABUKI theater its nostalgic TAKENOKOZOKU dances its thousands of suit-and-tie executives its geishas its Western style marriages its tranquil green areas its pulsating nightlife... Yet the commentary which deliberately often strays away from the images throws into question the objectivity of the documentary film. Here what we see is inseparable from the experience of its young maker as he cast a loving eye on a people and its culture.
