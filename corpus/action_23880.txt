Jordan Parker is a wife first and a mother of two boys not that she would choose one over the other. She has a secret that could interrupt the life she has come to know. They say what you do in the dark will come to the light. Jordan's past will come back with Vengeance and leave her to make a choice. At a young age Jordan became a part of a Top Secret agency. So she knows how to keep a secret but don't let her pretty face fool you. Jordan Parker is a weapon in her own right; she will do whatever it takes to protect her family. Will she be pushed to the edge?
