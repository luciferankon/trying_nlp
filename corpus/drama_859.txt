They are enemies in their professional lives. Chiang Chih-Heng ('Melvin Sia' (qv)) is a former mercenary who now is known as "The Bodyguard." He is the owner of Krisis a security consultancy company that protects public figures who are chased by the paparazzi. Ting Jo-Chin ('Wei-Ting Huang' (qv)) is nicknamed the "Variety Queen" for her dogged ability to chase down celebrity gossip exclusives as a tabloid journalist for "V-Focus."
