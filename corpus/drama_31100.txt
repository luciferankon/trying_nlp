'Come Back To Sorrento' is the romantic tale of a music teacher with a mysterious past who finds more than he bargained for when he travels to a small town in Ohio to teach music. In his unique way he comes to love a housewife seeking a long forgotten dream. "COME BACK TO SORRENTO" is the charming story of a music teacher with a mysterious past who must reluctantly return from his enchanting European travels to accept a teaching position in rural Ohio in the 1930's. Decker is pleasantly surprised to find a kindred spirit in a lovely housewife there who seems to share in his many artistic interests and pursuits. Inspired by each other they become caught up in their fantasies until they are forced to finally face the reality of their own bare authentic truths.
