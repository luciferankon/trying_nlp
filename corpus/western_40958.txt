When Joe Kirk the Indian Agent is murdered His adopted son Tom Kirk heads out after the killer. Finding gambler Honest John with his father's ring Tom arrests him. But Honest John found the ring on the floor when Crandall the real killer played in his card game. Crandall kills Honest John to keep him silent. But Tom now finds the clue that will lead him to the killer. The Navajo Kid (Bob Steele) whose real name is Tom Kirk but he doesn't know it as he was separated from his family when a young boy was raised by the Indians until he was adopted by Indian Agent Pinky Cole (George Morrel). When his adopted father is killed by a gang led by Matt Crandall (Stanley Blystone) and Honest John Grogan (I. Stanford Jolley) the Kid sets out to track them down. In doing so he also finds his real father.
