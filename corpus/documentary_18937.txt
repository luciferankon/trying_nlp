Afrika Unzipped documents the trials and rewards for American expatriate Alan Donovan as he shepherds his stunning models acrobats dancers and musicians through 9 grueling performances for the African Heritage Festival Millennium Tour as they rock the runways from Nairobi to Mombasa and into the Y2K New Year. Includes notable interviews with Uhuru Kenyatta then president of the Kenya Tourism Board now president of Kenya. Also included are interviews with then American Ambassador Johnnie Carson and Dr. Tom Mboya head of the World Bank STI Unit and Kenya Ministry of Health. The Festival was sponsored by the Kenya Tourism Board Kenya Health Ministry and the World Bank to raise awareness for prevention of HIV AIDS.
