Evie Shandra Bailey and Mia are four secondary school friends who learn to work together escape real life and protect themselves and the magical realm of their Live-Action Roleplay from the evil Troll King. Their task? To protect the Oracle Stone an ancient magic relic of great power guarded by Queen Valmoria Penteghast. How can they hope to succeed against the Troll King and his army? Will they reach Queen Valmoria's tower in time? What power does the Oracle Stone truly hold? Find out in Homebrew: the Webseries.
