It's London's Second Season in Brighton England. Prince Regent's exotic taste reigns but it's the humble talent of a French maid that sets the upper class ablaze. Emile's secret gift becomes a highly coveted asset among the rich and powerful scandalous and discreet but she isn't the only one cloaking a penetrative gift among the elite. A mysterious interloper threatens to expose her social intrigues until he discovers a hidden key to their futures and his own destiny.
