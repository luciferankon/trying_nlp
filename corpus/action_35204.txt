When a revolutionary compound called Kronos is released in the middle of Birmingham overnight; flesh-eating killers roam the streets. Four Survivors form an uneasy alliance in order to stay alive. But to survive what will come they must build trust between themselves form alliances with enemies and fight the threats that they will face all while unraveling the truth behind Kronos before they are killed captured...or doomed to become a mindless horror. "When the end of days approaches who will you trust to fight by your side?"
