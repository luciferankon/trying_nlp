Tony is interrupted from a session with his lover to hear his men kidnapped three rich daughters and one of their roommates. The roommate then seduces the first kidnapper while one daughter and her buxom sister seduce the second one. When their rich uncle and aunt refuse to pay Tony lets the young women go but they decide to help him get revenge. The other daughter thus seduces the visiting state lawyer while the roommate seduces the uncle. Now seduced the state lawyer is sent to seduce the estranged older aunt. With the husband and wife therefore both occupied the daughter can grab their money.
