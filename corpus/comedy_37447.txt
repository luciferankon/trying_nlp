Film Trix invades the American Film Market on the quest for distribution. Trixie gets the inside scoop on how to market sell and distribute independent films without getting screwed (in a bad way)! Features exclusive interviews with such greats as Martin Landau Lou Diamond Phillips Edward James Olmos Ron Jeremy and a Bum Fights reject.
