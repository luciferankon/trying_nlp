Theme parks across America compete to build the tallest fastest most thrilling roller coasters ever conceived. Tour fourteen of the most exciting roller coasters in the nation. Includes vital statistics such as speed length G-forces number of inversions and unique one-of-a-kind features for each coaster. Featured coasters: Viper and Batman - The Ride (Six Flags Magic Mountain) -- The Texas Giant (Six Flags Over Texas) -- Magnum XL-200 Mean Streak and Raptor (Cedar Point) -- Cyclone (Astroland Coney Island) -- The Beast (Paramount King's Island) -- Top Gun (Paramount's Great America) -- Kumba (Busch Gardens Tampa) -- Drachen Fire and Big Bad Wolf (Busch Gardens Williamsburg) -- Thunderbolt and The Steel Phantom (Kennywood).
