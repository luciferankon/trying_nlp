A young woman in love suspects her man is being unfaithful and decides to take matters into her own hands to reveal his infidelities. She composes a plan to secretly introduce her lying spouse to two beautiful women to see if he can be trusted. Just as she holds out hope that maybe she is wrong the tiger reveals his stripes and takes the bait. All the while he thinks he has it all figured out indulging in a life he could only dream of. His world comes crashing down when his secret life is exposed.
