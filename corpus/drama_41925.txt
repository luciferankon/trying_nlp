Welcome to Danville; Small Town USA. On the surface all is right but we all have our secrets and Danville has its share. As our story unfolds Ray Mathews discovers the long-lost diary of Betsy Cooper which opens up the sordid past of this small town. The answers to the puzzles laid forth in the diary however are missing and only Jeremiah knows Danville's secret. The answers found by Sheriff Matt Chandler only lead to more riddles. Caught up in in this tiny burgs' twisted past is a young man struggling to shed the trappings of the hierarchical society of Danville. Josh Webster is secretly eying Ben Daigle's prearranged girlfriend Millie Hansen. But as normal as things may seem all of their lives will be turned upside down when they discover Jeremiah's Secret.
