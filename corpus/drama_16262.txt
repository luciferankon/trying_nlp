A young passionate musician named Ana escapes to her music when her life begins to fall apart. But she goes too far and begins to ignore the people in her life. She gets lost in her passion. Meanwhile a troubled young man named Danny does whatever it takes whether it's legal or not to support him and his mom. He gets lost in the harsh reality with no will to dream and no time for passions. When their lives hit rock bottom a homeless high school drop out named Charlie pops into both of their lives and turns it around for the better while both of them are simultaneously changing his.
