Ms. Gamache is an actress originally from Costa Rica and has been acting since she was a child in Film and Theatre. Her vibrant positive energy is a vital part of the experience of the show and has left guests wanting to come back for more. Some of Ms. Gamache's most prominent guests include Joe Franklin Marilyn Sokol (actress Sex and the City) John Michael Bolger (actor Public Enemies) Teddy Atlas (ESPN) Sebastian Silva (Director The Maid) and musical guests including Maud in Cahoots.
