Eunuch Alley is a Bollywood-Noir about gangsters journalists eunuchs mothers and castration. It follows the adventures of a journalist as he encounters eunuchs his mother and famous criminal Charles Sobhraj. This surreal film is set in India but was shot in Philadelphia using a multi-racial caste. Using elements of Bombay film and Noir this film rollicks its way from chases to musical numbers to lush flashbacks.
