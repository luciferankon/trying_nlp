The in-depth story of famous vallenato star Kalleth Morales who died tragically in a automobile accident in 2005 at the peak of his career. Kaleth was born into the "Morales dinasty" of musicians his father Miguel Morales is a well known singer / composer. Kaleth is known as the king of the new wave of Vallenato. He modernized the genre and became one of the most successful composers of his time. The documentary also exposes one of Colombia's and latin america most known musical and cultural movements; vallenato the magic the mysticism and the artists (old school and contemporary). To this day; Kalleth has a huge fan base and new artists that follow his path including his younger brothers "The K Morales"
