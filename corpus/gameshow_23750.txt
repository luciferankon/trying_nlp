Danish version of the TV-format "Trash or Treasure" a big money game show where it pays to know what's valuable and what's not. The show's antique expert has traveled around the country and found 8 collectibles ranging in value from 500 dollars up to a staggering 100.000 dollars. In each show a couple competing together faces the task of eliminating these objects one by one winning the value of the last collectible standing. Will their instincts and the expert's advice send them home with a piece of trash or a valuable piece of treasure?
