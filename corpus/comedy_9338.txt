Marvin and Rayleene Atlas are a teenage married couple who are going to college and are spending their first week together as a married couple. They are learning about themselves as well as each other in their first week together. They are greeted by their next door neighbors Ben and Becky Donaldson who are the more experienced married couple who know more about campus life then the Atlas's. The four of them try to figure life out as college students as well as life as a married couple as well as themselves and each other.
