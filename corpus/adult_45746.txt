A detective pursuing an international adventuress finds stolen plans hidden in her home and escapes leaving the countess gagged and tied to a chair. The Countess turns the tables on the detective when she and her henchman capture the detective and force her to reveal her secrets when tormented with a vibrator attached to her nether regions. The henchman then reveals his true colors and binds both women placing them in a deadly trap. In a second story another detective and her pretty assistant are pursued by foul villains and find themselves in dire peril.
