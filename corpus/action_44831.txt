Welcome to War Clash - the first true mobile RTS game. Unleash the power of unique faction heroes and lead your armies to victory in head-to-head and 2v2 battles! Discover a world suffused by the powers of magic and chaos transforming skilled warriors into immortal heroes and bringing the great civilizations to the brink of war. Choose your race and prepare to expand into contested territory for in this war only one side will survive. Lead a variety of fantastic troops into battle and choose your favorite hero - a unique unit whose deadly powers can make all the difference in the right hands. On the ever-changing battlefields of War Clash superior strategy is the key to victory.
