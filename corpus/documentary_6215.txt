This feature-length documentary gives fans of "Field of Dreams" a look behind the scenes at the making of the classic film about a farmer in Iowa who receives a calling from the spirits baseball's past. Included with the film's DVD release it features interviews with director Phil Alden Robinson stars Kevin Costner and James Earl Jones and many other members of the cast and crew who share their experiences on the set and give viewers a glimpse of the work that went into bringing the project to completion.
