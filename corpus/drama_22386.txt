In the early hours of March 24th 2002 Wendy Chandler awoke to a stranger taking off his clothes. He held her down raped her and fled. At the time of the rape Wendy was an award-winning filmmaker. Within months she was barely able to eat unable to work scarcely able to function a person. Despite her ordeal Wendy came to realise she was one of the lucky ones. One of the Lucky Ones tells the story of Wendy's assault and its aftermath.
