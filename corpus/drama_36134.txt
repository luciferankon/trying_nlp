René and his two artist friends lead a meager but careless life in a Parisian small apartment their main worry being to avoid the housekeeper. Whenever they get some money they call more friends in and celebrate. This is how he meets beautiful but fragile Denise who wants to be a singer as himself and they fall in love. Yet when she finds out her real condition she takes a drastic decision which will determine their fates. La Bohčme arias and more.
