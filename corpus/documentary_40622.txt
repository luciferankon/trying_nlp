Seventeen Indians are sentenced to death for murdering one man in UAE. They would end up like statistics but for their families who get local media and Human Rights to pressure both governments into fair trial. Shadowing them collecting proof of innocence on camera takes us into the unseen world of migrant abuse which starts from Punjab but seals a workers fate in Arab Emirates. Shadowing the trial through reveals how migrants struggle to survive between a corrupt and a conservative nation in juxtapose is the ability of everyday people to take on governments in the pursuit of freedom
