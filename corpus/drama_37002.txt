Sean arrives at Ken and Lila's house expecting to play poker with the boys but Ken informs him that the other guys couldn't make it. Lila suggests inviting her friend Christy over so that the evening is not wasted and so that Sean and Christy can get to know each other. The four of them spend the evening playing strip poker and telling erotic stories about people they know which just might be the inducement that the shy guy Sean needs to make a move on Christy.
