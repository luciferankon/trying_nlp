Based on Sue Hooser's book 'Butterflies Buses and a Guardian Angel' this heartwarming family movie follows the story of young Sue as she becomes a teacher in a small New Mexico town and develops a true heart to help the troubled mostly poverty-stricken students in her care. With the help of her community she develops a program called "Suspreno" 'Super Spiritual Revolution Now an outreach program to teach the children about the life-changing love of Christ.
