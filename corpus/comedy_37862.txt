Door No. 1 a first-of-its-kind live-action choose your own adventure VR comedy series in which the viewer is the lead character. The first episode Ten Year takes place at a high school class reunion where you will make choices to navigate through former flings and longtime rivals. Get high with the janitor climb the gym rope on more time or confess your love to the girl of your dreams. In Door No. 1 you get one last chance to prove you are not who they remember.
