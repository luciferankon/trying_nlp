It's the beginning of the summer. What better place for children to enjoy their pastime of being young than at the playground. The playground is a place where kids can have their imaginations run wild. Children go swimming play on the sliding boards and hang on the monkey bars. Yet there is one problem. The local bully and his goons! They harass tease and persistently antagonize all of the kids having fun. However four kids are determined to making the playground a safe haven and take a stand against the biggest bully of them all at the playground.
