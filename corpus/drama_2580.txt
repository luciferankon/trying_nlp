A neurosurgeon is thrown out of the medical profession after he performs a daring but unsuccessful surgery. He flees to Alaska where his plane crashes in the frozen wilderness. Dr.John Mandre (Edmund Lowe) brain specialist is barred from the medical profession when he performs a delicate operation and he is accused of letting the patient die because of his affection for the man's wife. Formerly a war pilot he enters the airplane ferrying service in Alaska and on a return flight he crashes in the Klondike. He now finds himself confronted with the same type of operation as he had done before unsuccessfully.
