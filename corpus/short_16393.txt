A young woman has cut herself off from the world. In isolation Samantha recalls vivid memories of the past. As she reminisces about joyous moments she cannot escape the haunting of a tragic event. In her self-imposed state of solitude Samantha is plagued with grief and continues to distance herself from loved-ones including her best friend. Life is not without tragedy: Is one able to separate suffering from happiness? Ultimately do we decide on what moments we remember? What memories do we desire to forget? Through isolation despair and the good intentions of her best friend Samantha must decide how she is going to get on with her life.
