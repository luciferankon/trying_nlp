Whether KT and Rio are hosting clients or exploring new territories on their own KT records each adventure in his personal diary. He challenges us all to give thought to the things that are important. Based from a small operation along the Texas coast & Nebraska KT is ably assisted by his Australian-born female assistant Friday who plans coordinates and tantalizes guests and viewers with her Aussie perspective of outdoor adventure! Each exploit is highlighted with thrill-seeking travel to the destination and the inevitable challenges in hunting and fishing. And along the way KT will proudly reveal the unique geographic and historic details that make each quest so memorable.
