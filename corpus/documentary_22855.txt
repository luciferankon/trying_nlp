'Amhrán na bhFiann' marked the 100-year anniversary of the writing of the Irish National Anthem with a question: does this old-style anthem the words unknown to many still have relevance in today's Ireland? Paddy Casey George Hook Fintan O'Toole Micheál O'Suilleabháin and many others discuss the anthem and its possible alternatives in a documentary presented by Harry McGee political editor of the Irish Examiner.
