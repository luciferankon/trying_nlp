After husband and wife team Adam and Evia start to experience a string of financial downfalls due to Adam losing his corporate job they are approached by Evia's boss Shay who gives them a 5 million dollar offer in exchange for one weekend alone with Adam for her 50th birthday. Evia turns down the offer without question but when her struggles seemingly become larger than her faith Adam begins to consider trading his soul for the money. Like most forbidden deals it's not long before the offer begins to feel a lot like dancing with the devil.
