The Laďtes tribe lives on the site of "Plaça de Sant Jaume" today the square at the heart of Barcelona's old city. Their world is narrow and limited: they don't want to expand their horizons and they cling to old beliefs and superstitions. The Lacets are their neighbours a tribe made up of Laďtes who do not accept the murky mystifications of the Laďtes' witch-doctor. Laďtes and Lacets must overcome years of distrust and incomprehension and unite their forces to face a new and imminent danger: the Barbarites...
