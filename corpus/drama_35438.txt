70-year old Simone leaves her retirement home in the city and travels across the country to visit her 36-year old daughter Michčle at her summer cottage for an annual one-week visit. Michčle is a potter who lives with her husband Pierre and two children. There is no absence of love in this family but communication problems there are a plenty. Simone and Michčle have always had a strained relationship and neither can bring themselves to say what's on her mind. Everyone strives for a more open exchange of feelings - black and white inserts reveal what each character really thinks.
