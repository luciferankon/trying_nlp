Sannata is a queer adaptation of the one act play named Sonata by renowned Marathi playwright Mahesh Elkunchwar. The story of Sannata revolves around three gay bottoms Zameer Manoj and Rosa who live a non-conformist unconventional queer life free and outrageous but also lacking lonely and not at peace with themselves. The action takes place in a simple but tastefully furnished drawing room at one night. Three friends share their innermost thoughts; they fight and hurt each other yet find a deep sense of bonding to survive the struggle for happiness love and contentment.
