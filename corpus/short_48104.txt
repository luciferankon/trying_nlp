This 65 minute DVD is an unbelievable collection of never-before-seen footage starring rock legends Guided By Voices. This collection includes some never-released videos including "Weed King" Bob and Demos early studio clips backstage footage Bob's high school movie "Wild People" featuring "Lucifer's Aching Revolver" plus an early live performance from 1992. Recorded in Dolby Digital and narrated by Geo.
