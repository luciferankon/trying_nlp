Bondage and discipline by Mistress Be Be LeBadd and her three slaves Darla Crane (Sydney) Angela Faith (Paige) and Ja Lynn. Playtime begins with Be Be punishing Darla and when Angela rides Darla one too many times Be Be decides it's her turn. However Be Be also decides that Darla should be the one wielding the whip since she was the target of Angela's taunts. Darla begins hesitantly but soon warms up to the idea. Next both Be Be and Darla mete out extended punishments to Angela and Ja. The turnabout comes when Darla decides that it's Be Be's turn to be on the other end of the whip.
