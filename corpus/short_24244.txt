Private eye and former New York City detective Guy Parker catches wind of a murder that's supposed to happen at midnight at the Jersey Shore where he's set up shop. A period Neo-Film-Noir piece set in the early post WWII period the story takes place over a four hour period in seven acts/scenes all at night. Intrigue unfolds as Parker's associates old friends and dark underworld characters emerge one-by-one to slowly reveal this complex mystery to a startling conclusion. Shot in black and white the piece intends to emulate and respect the Film-Noir murder-mystery genre.
