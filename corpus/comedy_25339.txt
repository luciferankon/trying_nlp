The story is about the Royal Deshmukh family (Nirmiti Samant) with a history of adoption as the women in their family have failed to conceive for seven generations. Here comes in Dr. Shriram (Makrand Anaspure) full of innovative home remedies for the couple (Sanjay Narvekar and Puja Naik) who refuses to settle for adoption and is hell bent to have their own child. What follows is a hilarious comedy ending with the first born male child in 8th generation of this family.
