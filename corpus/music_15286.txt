Adele Live in London with Matt Lauer (or simply Adele Live in London) is a television special starring Adele originally set to premiere June 5 2012 8 P.M. EST on NBC although this was changed to Sunday June 3 2012 though. [1] According to a statement from Sony Music Entertainment Adele sits down for an exclusive interview with newscaster Matt Lauer to talk about the incredible success of her record-breaking album 21 and how it has changed her life. [2] It included performances from her successful concert film Live at the Royal Albert Hall.
