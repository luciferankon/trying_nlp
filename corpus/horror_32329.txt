The week leading up to Halloween is usually an exciting time for anyone but this year for Charmaine and Kira it's turning into a nightmare. While researching the towns history on witch hangings Charmaine and Kira discover an old book by the gravestone of one of the towns most famous witches (Elda Morgan). The girls takes the book and read aloud an entry in the back which brings forth the 400 year old witch. Each day the witches hauntings become more and more prominent to point of physical attacks Charmaine and Kira must find a way to stop her before Halloween or the witch may just walk the earth once again.
