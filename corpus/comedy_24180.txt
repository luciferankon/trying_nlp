Pierre is a workaholic company manager born with a silver spoon in his mouth. He is a widower who jealously guards the domestic equilibrium he has rebuilt with his three very possessive daughters. Florence is a divorcee of modest means who has just lost her job and has three children of her own to feed. Exuberant and candid she is cheerful by nature. They were definitely not cut out to meet. But this summer on a beach in Southern France the impossible happens...
