Our two adversaries are appointed captains of the cheer squad. Their fighting is tearing the squad apart giving the advantage to their arch rivals from another school. Gibby has to step up and teach the captains that the squad comes first. Rivals Katie and Alicia battle to be captain of the cheer squad. To their surprise they both are co-captains. Their fighting tears the squad apart allowing their school's rivals to gain the upper hand as they try to show them up at cheer camp football game and dance. Gibby saves the day by showing Katie and Alicia what leadership is all about as she rallies the squad to beat their rivals. In desperation Their rivals monkey-nap Gibby so that they can win Regionals.
