The Adventure of Ronald and Edgar revolves around two high functioning developmentally disabled besties who finally leave the facility for the Disabled to start a new life. Ronald Walker (very tall) is a complete optimist. His glass is always full really full like the fullest. His ambition is to be an entrepreneur and the best roommate in the whole wide world. Edgar Garcia (not tall at all) is highly competitive and a complete pessimists. He has a tremendous work ethic but is afraid to take risks. He spouts affirmations to give him courage. They are every day heroes running skipping and trudging their way through life thank god they have humor. They just keep on truckin'. The Adventures of Ronald and Edgar is the so called "disabled" people who help the so called "abled" people live their life.
