In the wake of the Medgar Evers assassination disillusioned ambulance driver Patrick Rawlings is recruited as an FBI informant within the increasingly dangerous United Klan of America. Klansman Gene Haywood and his trusted second-in-command Garth Dillahunt deal with a mole within their Klavern. Haywood's wife Bev struggles with the impending one-year anniversary of her eldest son's death. FBI handler Dean Waller receives some alarming information.
