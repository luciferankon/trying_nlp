'Thission Cinema' is a poetic documentary about an old open air cinema situated in the area of Acropolis in Athens where one feels the energy that flows through time and connects our lives to the stories we watch the past to the present the cinema to life. 'Thission Cinema of Athens' is a poetic documentary about an old open air cinema in Athens which despite being situated in the extremely touristy area of Acropolis stubbornly defies modernity and remains strangely timeless. It is not nostalgia one feels here for the old days nor admiration for the antiquities around but a visceral sense of the energy that flows through time and connects our lives to the stories we watch the past to the present the cinema to life.'
