Chronicles the misadventures of four friends as they make their way thorough today's slightly askew dating scene. Tom the voice of the group finds himself dumped by his girl and fired by his job. He has to make the choice to either sit around and mope forever or to get on with life. Mike the moral center of the gang is about to get married. He finds himself asking if he really wants to do this or has he just convinced himself he should? Clyde the ladies' man of the four meets a woman he cannot easily seduce. How far is he willing to bend the truth to get what he wants? Jerry who's never been popular with women finds himself caught between two of them. He also finds himself skirting the border of propriety legality and common decency. LIARS a Romantic Comedy about how modern men lie to others - and to themselves.
