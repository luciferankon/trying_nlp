The Spell Directed by Indira Cesarine and Phantasmic TV takes you through a visual journey of Surrealist inspiration as two heavenly muses are caught in a web of the other worldly desires and remain only in your dreams. The film features Beata Grabowska and Jessica Bertoncello with make-up by Bernadine Bibiano and hair by Rutger. Featured photography stills by Indira Cesarine cinematography by Phantasmic TV edited by Phatasmic TV with creative direction casting and production by Indira Cesarine.
