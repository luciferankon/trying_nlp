Six country girls raised the good ol' fashion way. Working on the farm. All six adopted by longtime farm owner William Pale when his wife was unable to have children. William's wife had passed away years ago and made him promise that he would raise these girls into great admirable healthy and respectable country women. Respecting his wife's wishes William did. Cell phones laptops and items of modern technology have never been introduced to them. The entire region seems to have started being bought up by the State. Modern stores and Sports bars are chasing all the farmers out. The girls lives take a fatal turn for the worst when the youngest sister kills a city boy in self defense. Into the mountains the sisters flee evading the army of individuals seeking revenge to kill them. They will use every bit of their knowledge of the land and survival skills via horseback to outsmart the modern technology and survive another day until they can be found innocent.
