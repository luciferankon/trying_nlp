Story centers on fast rising Assistant U.S. Attorney Charlie Beckham as his seemingly perfect world comes crashing down around him on the eve of his biggest case when he discovers his brother presumed dead for 13 years may not only be alive but may also be directly involved with the criminal organization he's in the process of putting away. Now he must unravel the conspiracy surrounding his brother's disappearance; avoid the hit that's been put on his head and discover who has been feeding the organization info from the inside - all before the sun comes up.
