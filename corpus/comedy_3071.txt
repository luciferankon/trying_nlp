Willy Jenco has served his time of 23 years for a crime he did not commit. He reunites with Anthony Nutello an ex-crime boss he protected and did the time for. Grateful Mr. Nutello rewards Jenco with cash and a home but soon comes to realize things are not as simple as they seem. Forced back into tying up some loose ends for the ex mob boss Jenco is picked up by the police and delivered to his Uncle who is now a police Chief. He must work for both sides to have any chance of a normal life while encountering some less than normal characters to help him get there.
