Sisters Margo and Karen along with best friend 'Chelle were full figured bootylicious and proud of it - they loved hard laughed out loud and lived life by the code "the more the woman the 'More to Love.'" That is until Margo returns from Chicago with not just a new job but a new body to go along with it. Armed with a new image no man is safe not even Terence Karen's Bar Exam study partner and secret crush. Believing that a full figured woman could never land a man like Terrence Karen moves out of the way and Margo and Terrence get engaged. However on Karen's 30th birthday Terrence professes his love for her. After sharing a night of passion Karen must decide whether to fight for the man she loves or watch him walk down the aisle with Margo.
