Most film and photography on Tibet is from "The West" or "Western Eye" looking in. This film 'Voices in Exile' by Tibetan and first time director Tenzin Wangden Andrugtsang presents the Tibetan Exile Community from the rarely seen Tibetan point of view. Never before seen footage from The Tibetan Exile Community includes many interviews and accounts of life in exile. Shot in black and white and colorand with English Voice Over.
