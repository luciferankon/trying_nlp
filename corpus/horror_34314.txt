Successful young Rose is not the ordinary next-door photographer who shoots nature or portraits of happy brides. Instead inconspicuous Rose is a hardened bondage specialist a keen connoisseur of debasement and dehumanisation but most of all a pitiless infernal executioner for profit. In other words Rose is in constant need of unwary and innocently eager models to entice them in her den with the intention to devour them physically and mentally. But Rose's latest quarry Freya isn't willing to go down without a fight. What will it take to break her will?
