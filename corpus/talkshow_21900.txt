BRUNCH which is on the Q Television Network is the first LIVE morning daily talk show created specifically for the LGBT and gay-friendly progressive community. This upbeat and fast paced Morning show is part talk show and part sitcom. The hosts are Honey Labrador of Queer Eye for the Straight Girl and Scott Withers live in lofts across the hall from one another. Brunch is eclectic and touches on all sorts of topics from cooking to politics along with having many interesting guests BRUNCH will run the gamut of issues and interests that are important to the diverse LGBT community.
