A how-to and inspirational DVD video on green building featuring hybrid straw bale and adobe house design and construction green building materials for the home passive solar design rainwater cisterns earth plasters photovoltaics aesthetics and more. Follow the construction of one house from start to finish and learn how natural building materials like straw and mud can work together with high-tech elements like a photovoltaic system to create a home that is both beautiful and energy-efficient.
