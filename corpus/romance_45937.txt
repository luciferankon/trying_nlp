The handsome Ali meets Leyla at the airport it turns out that Ali is working for Leyla's father. Leyla wants to be close to Ali and becomes his secretary. She prevents Ali's girl friends talking to him. They marry but Ali's ex-lovers give him no rest. Leyla gets jealous. She has a plan and uses their neighbor Abdul Osman pretending she is attracted to him yet she tells Osman that she just wants to make her husband jealous. Osman tells Ali that Leyla isn't with him though Leyla tells the opposite! Ali confronts Leyla with Osman and everything turns out to be fake. Ali promises her not to cheat on her and all the problems get settled.
