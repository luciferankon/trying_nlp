Spawning the period from 1967 to 2010 the series is about Lulwah who as a young girl has a relationship with a rich reckless man. After becoming pregnant her family forces her to have an abortion. For the remainder of her life Lulwah becomes a servant to her brothers and a caretaker to their children. The brothers do every bad thing to abuse terrorize and take advantage of her including trying to sell her organs while alive!.
