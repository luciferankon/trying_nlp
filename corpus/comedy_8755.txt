Nicole has a nice house good job and loving husband...and they're starting to drive her crazy. When the death of a long lost friend results in her being tasked with traveling to Hollywood to make the deceased's terrible screenplay into a movie Nicole discovers an opportunity for a romantic adventure and the chance to do something she's never done before...knowingly make a bad decision that can't possibly end well. Ultimately Nicole will have to decide if she's willing to risk the life she always wanted to live the way she never has.
