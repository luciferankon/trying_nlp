Jack Wiley saves Inez Almado when her team runs away. Later Jack gets into a fight helping an Almado servant and is jailed. To repay jack Inez has him broken out and made a guest at her ranch. When Inez is abducted Jack is on hand and takes off after the kidnapers. A war veteran returns to his ranch. Shortly after arriving there he happens upon a girl trapped in a runaway carriage and saves her. Later he finds himself falsely accused of a murder and must work with the girl and her father to prove his innocence.
