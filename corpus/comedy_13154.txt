American Idol meets Last Comic Standing. Stand up & Fight is a reality based one-hour stand-up comedy competition show that pits comic against comic hoping to be crowned King of the Comedy Ring. Each comic has two minutes to impress a live audience and three celebrity judges who will critique and judge them by holding up numbered score cards (Think: Dancing with the Stars) The comic with the highest number stays in the competition until beaten by another comic. Ten comics Five rounds. Two minutes each.
