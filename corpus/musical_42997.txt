1985. The street craze for break-dancing sweeps the world and reaches Indonesia where young people seem to practice it everywhere all the time. Mr. Gondo hates it and forbids his daughter Chicha to hang out with her friends a group of break-dancers. On the other hand Mr. Indra tries to understand the youth interest in break-dance. When holidays come Chicha and her friend Rico plan to perform break-dance in order to rise funds for social purposes. Despite Mr. Gondo forbidding her to do so Chicha secretly participates in the break-dance show which proves to be very successful. Witnessing the obvious positive results of Chicha and her young friend's creativity grumpy Mr. Gondo will eventually realize his mistake. Happy end.
