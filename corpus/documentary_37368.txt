Two Danish filmmakers went on a journey to find the story behind the bestselling organist Klaus Wunderlich. This adventure led them to England Germany and Denmark where they were introduced to a whole new society - the organ world. Klaus Wunderlich was a German electronic organist who sold more than 20 millions records. He interpreted genres like classical music operas and pop hits instrumentally on electronic organs like Hammond and Wersi. He was known for being a brilliant musician and producer. And a bit eccentric.
