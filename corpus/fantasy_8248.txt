Capodezero Films is pleased to bring you "Neptune" the groundbreaking feature by award-winning independent filmmaker Anthony Marchitiello. This post-apocalyptic nature film set in a nightmarish future not too far off is chilling look at a species on the verge of extinction...man. After the failure of civilization the scattered remnants of humanity eke out a meager existence far from the cities while the natural world thrives around them. A crate of souring milk sets in motion a cycle of treachery and violence culminating in a vicious showdown. Filmed on location on the Northern Californian coast Neptune is a work of meditative brutality and mutant genius.
