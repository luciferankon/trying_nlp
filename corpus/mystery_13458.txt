'Love you forever Donna Dell' a story of fame sex murder and rock and roll set against a 1950's pre-Beatle mania era a Britain full of rumbling music clubs fast cars and long country roads. A young French orphan crippled by a bout of polio dreams of stardom and escape making it her life long goal to meet her idol the infamous DONNA DELL. Little does she know her life's about to change forever when Donna Dell's abrupt murder thrusts her into the spotlight to take over the role of the most famous pop star of them all. Tim Burton meets the Cohen brothers; 'Love You Forever Donna Dell' follows the quirkiest group of characters you've ever met through a musically driven ethereal world that challenges the boundaries of fame and identity told through the eyes of a young girl who's wanted nothing more than to just... not disappear.
