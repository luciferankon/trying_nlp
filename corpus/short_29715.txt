A character study that follows the exploits of Bob his brother Ernie their cousin Gerry and their friend Roy: young urban Latinos who aren't hoodlums or cops; who occasionally drink a beer or smoke a joint but aren't hardcore junkies; unemployed because of hard times not because they're slackers or deadbeats. Frustrated by the lack of opportunities available and unable to see any future in their grim surroundings the only thing they have to look forward to are Friday nights when they get together have a few beers and chill out. One of these Friday nights friendships are tested hard choices present themselves and they realize there are no easy solutions.
