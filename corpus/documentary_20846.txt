William Patrick Patterson founder/director of The Gurdjieff Legacy Foundation author of nine books on Gurdjieff's teaching of The Fourth Way and the director/writer/narrator of the award-winning documentary film trilogy The Life & Significance of G. I. Gurdjieff undertakes a 21-day pilgrimage to visit Mr. Gurdjieff's father's grave in Gyumri Armenia imparting the great wisdom of his father guiding influences (geographic religious and spiritual) and circumstances that led to the formation of his strong character and the key figures in the dissemination of this esoteric teaching of spiritual self-transformation.
