Creators and co-hosts Jessica Golden and Matt Boren take a fresh approach to the genre as they converse with comedians actors artists musicians chefs authors designers social media influencers and a variety of other tastemakers and thought leaders. Don't Steal aims to make you laugh connect think a little differently and greet your day with a full heart. Golden and Boren have an infectious energy that evokes authentically entertaining conversation with their diverse array of engaging guests.
