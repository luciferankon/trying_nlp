Crystal Thorn an African American Professor of Literature married to a retired NBA player must choose between supporting the movement in her position as the faculty advisor to the Black Students Matter (BSM) and supporting her son an NCAA star. Meanwhile dad Donald Thorn is furious that she even thinks she has a choice. Crystal Thorn a bi-racial Professor of Literature and Donald Thorn a retired NBA player are grooming three sons for the NCAA and NBA during socially turbulent times while the family constantly faces moral professional and personal dilemmas.
