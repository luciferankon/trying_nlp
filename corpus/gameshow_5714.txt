Each contestant starts with $5 which they can take and run or they can bet it on the next question. If they answer correctly they can then bet on the next question. Some of the prizes were gags others legit. If they answer five questions correctly they get to face the jackpot question worth 1000 bars of Sweetheart soap and $1000. Actor Don Ameche hosts. Sponsor is Nestle's Nescafe coffee.
