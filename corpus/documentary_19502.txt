Second World War and Korean Veterans meet at a local air show to be honored and to reflect on their individual war experiences. The smells and the thrills of their surroundingshelp to bring out profound stories that are heart wrenching and moving. With archival footage of the era and current day air force technology combined the viewer takes a journey with these men and women that is not soon forgotten. Their collective courage and desire to fight for freedom risking their very lifes is respectfully presented in this documentary that was selected for the New York Independent Film Festival in 2002.
