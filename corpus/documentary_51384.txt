You need courage to choose to be a migrant. You need strength and determination to fight your own fears and face your own limits. Deciding to leave Italy and move to Australia - today - is not a choice dictated by an economic need. It is mostly a choice that comes from an inner need. Five young Italians talk about their 'Down Under' lives showing their own strengths and weaknesses in a journey that is primarily towards the discovery of themselves. A period in Australia that has been long forgotten. The Cronulla Riots were a series of racial riots targeted at the Australian Lebanese community beginning in December 2005. This film upholds a dark humor whilst tackling this serious topic
