Following a 2-year stint in jail Kaska Czajkowska (Marie Carrour) is finally free. She has a dark past: drugs smuggling and organized crime. Kaska is staying at her sister's Marta (Orina Krajewska) who is single and leads a stable life working at a corporation. The sisters have an agreement: Kaska swears to never return behind bars Marta is meant to help her achieve this goal. However rebuilding a life is not an easy task. Kaska must face soulless officials old friends from the underworld and finally - herself and her own weaknesses. Despite her best intentions she makes dramatic mistakes.
