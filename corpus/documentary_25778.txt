BETTER SAFE THAN SORRY is an incredible grouping of 8 original public safety shorts from the 1950s and 1960s. We have lovingly restored these little cinematic gems and offer them to you for the very first time on DVD. This collection to certain to delight. The short subjects presented on this DVD are: PASSING...AND BEING PASSED THE CAR AHEAD INKY AND BLINKY OTTO ASKS A RIDDLE GET A GRIP ON YOURSELF 2 STEPS TO SAFETY MOTORCYCLE SAFETY AND COURTESY IN TRAFFIC and FOR SAFETY'S SAKE.
