A chilling eerie portrayal of the mechanisms of the mind of a serial killer masterfully acted and directed. Security cameras record the last days of Tila's life in prison. His past is revealed through his writings foreboding literary renderings of a tortured mind. Reminiscent of The Silence of the Lambs a female journalist regularly visits him to unveil Tila's secrets. Violence contradictions and conflicting points of view will shape this crime story into an in-depth portrait of the mind of a murderer. Based on a true story.
