The focus of the video is to illustrate the characteristics and spirit of the Richmond Cultural Centre: the hub of community gathering and the centre of creativity and communication The video has a clear story line. It shows how a person's life is transformed by the Richmond Cultural Centre. The facilities and activities held at the cultural centre will be presented clearly and artistically through the character's journey inside and outside of the place. The message behind the transformation of the character is to tell the public that Richmond Cultural Centre is a place where people's mind and life are enriched.
