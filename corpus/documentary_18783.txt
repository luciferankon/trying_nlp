The making and breaking of a feature film. Bradley Creanzo and Luis Cortes two different visions one production. Watch how a production crumbles destroying a friendship. Just like a train wreck you can't help but watch. Witness Creanzo's struggle to maintain his sanity as he tries to keep the cast and production going while diving deeper into financial debt whereas Cortes will do anything to separate divide and destroy a collaborative effort putting the cast in jeopardy to the brink of losing their jobs and the film canceled. Cortes is bent on self-destructing the production which is "Case at Midnight".
