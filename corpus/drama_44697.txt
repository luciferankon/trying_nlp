One day with my father is the story of a man who lost his father 8 years ago. After a heart surgery that went wrong he had to take the toughest decision of his life. The decision between life and death. Since then he lives with the weight of his decision. That was the worst day of his life. Unable to mourn his lost he struggles in his personnal and professional life. The only thing that could save him from falling and losing everything would be... To spend one day with his father.
