Bad & the Birdieman is part of the innovative 5 film documentary series 'Reimagine Rio' by the acclaimed Brazilian filmmaker Katia Lund (City of God) and the first-time director Lili Fialho. These films celebrate the courageous individuals passionate leaders local innovators and resilient communities of the favelas and marginalized neighborhoods of Rio. The five films together are much greater than the sum of the individual films and shatter stereotypes and reconstruct our collective vision of the possible.
