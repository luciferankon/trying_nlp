Explores the phenomenon of the "Jerusalem Syndrome" also referred to as "Jerusalem Fever". There is a palpable sense of the faithful experiencing God's existence in Jerusalem more than any other place on earth. There are other sacred cities or places but none have the power of Jerusalem to dominate the imagination and the hearts and minds of millions. In fact Jerusalem's inhabitants believe that the power of Jerusalem drives some people crazy. "Holy Madness" follows events within the tourist police station where most of the tourist-pilgrims who have commited affrays due to religious zeal arrive before being hospitalised or deported or both.
