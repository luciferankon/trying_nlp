Survivor meets "Alone" meets "The Hunger Games" in this real-time social media driven reality-TV show that sets skilled outdoor enthusiasts to compete against each other in the areas of wilderness survival hunting and social media audience engagement. Scenic locations around the world provide a dramatic back-drop for adverse situations that bring out the best and the worst physical and mental attributes of each Competitor. In order to thrive Competitors need to rely on their on-screen charisma and social media know-how to solicit sponsored items "lifelinks" that audiences can send in real-time to those who leverage the highest audience engagement. Audiences can watch the host work with a panel of relevant experts to establish parameters for the competitors and to initiate socially-relevant discussions surrounding land conservation and wildlife management.
