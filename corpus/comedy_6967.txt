A life in show biz. Everyone wants to be in it. The glamor. The fame. The money. The excitement. The adoring public. The trouble-free life. But what about the rejections? The failures? The fear? The day-to-day existence as an artist who can't get any work? Or gives up trying? What happens when the approaching impending doom of the age of forty further reveals to you just how much you haven't done in your life?
