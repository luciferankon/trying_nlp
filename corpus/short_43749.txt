LOST & FOUND a short documentary intimately follows three senior women as they cope with the loss of their life partners and attempt to find new meaning in their lives. Anna Torpy age 90 starts dating for the first time in 50 years; Peggy Padilla age 84 and a recent widow finds solace through dance therapy; and Trudy Sivick age 91 finds lasting companionship in her senior home. Woven together with in-depth interviews observational scenes and archival family photos LOST & FOUND offers a profound portrait of senior widowhood exploring the delicate question of what happens after losing that person you thought you could never live without.
