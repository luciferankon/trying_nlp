In a dreamlike atmosphere over 30 Spanish and Latin American celebrities talk about the state of human rights today and the achievements and shortcomings of the Universal Declaration of Human Rights as it turns 60. The film also pays tribute to Eleanor Roosevelt one of the leading advocates of the Universal Declaration of Human Rights. Stars who took part include - Ana Belén Sara Baras Gabino Diego Fernando Guillen Cuervo Gracia Querejeta Fernando Tejero Bollaín Icíar Carmen Posadas.
