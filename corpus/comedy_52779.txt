Oh Katherine! is a simple & unique comedy that takes place during some convoluted uncertain vintage time in the past. The story surrounds a "damsel in distress" - like character named Katherine and her day to day dilemmas as she struggles to find employment marriage and self-fulfillment. She relies on her network of friends to help her out: JAN her level-headed best friend & confident; RICHARD her beau of 4 years who has YET to propose to her; and PEACHES Her pet cat who is a stuffed animal.
