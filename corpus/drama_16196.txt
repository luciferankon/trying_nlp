A conflicted young man is released from prison after doing time for a crime he didn't commit then his attempt at a fresh start goes horribly wrong as he plots his revenge having discovered his best friend is secretly seeing his ex-girlfriend who he's still in love with. Mikey is released from a two year prison term bitter about taking the wrap for a crime he didn't commit. He's struggling to turn his life around but addiction anger and dissatisfaction keep getting in the way. Then his jealousy takes over when he discovers JD his best friend is secretly seeing Trish the one love of his life. He thinks he's got it all figured out but nothing can prepare him for the real secret she is holding. When revenge becomes his motivator nothing goes the way he's planned and the real question is "Who is crossing who?"
