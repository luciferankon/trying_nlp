Award winning Icelandic bass player and songwriter Tomas R. Einarsson is already well known in his home country mostly for his latin music. In THE LATIN VIKING we get to know the man behind the double bass and the music and how it came about that this country boy from Western Iceland embarked on a musical journey that took him all the way to Havana Cuba to finally return home to the sub arctic valley of his childhood bringing back a hot and colorful latin feast.
