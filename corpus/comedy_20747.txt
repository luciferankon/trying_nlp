The Sock is frustrated that his career as a super villain has yet to take off and is forced to find other means of supporting himself. Between demeaning jobs foiled get-rich-quick schemes and an annoyingly chipper roommate The Sock finds himself sinking into depression. Eventually with new-found determination to pursue his dreams of arch-villainy he takes his roommate and his girlfriend hostage holding them for ransom in a final showdown that does not go exactly according to plan.
