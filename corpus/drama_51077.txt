What will a young man do when his mother has an inflexible rule that all of her children must marry before the age of 30? Wan Sheng-Jen ('Chris Wang (III)' (qv)) faces the wrath of his mother if he doesn't meet her marriage deadline. As a result he is determined to find a "little angel" from his childhood but he doesn't know the girl's name or any other information that could help track her down.
