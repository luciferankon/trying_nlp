What's it like to be homeless? This film gathers together a collection of stories direct from those who - through circumstance - have wound up living on the streets. From riding the buses to dodging rats and coping with ill health find out about just some of the challenges rough sleepers face each day and those they overcome with the help of The Passage. This film was first screened at the Royal Festival Hall on 9 October 2014 the eve of World Homeless Day as part of the annual fundraiser A Night Under the Stars in aid of The Passage charity.
