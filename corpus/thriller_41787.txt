Arjun (Anup Revanna) and his two friends make a living out of conning people for a few thousands and are quite good at it. When Arjun falls for a girl he agrees to his friend's plan of stepping up to nail a big con job so that he can help her financially. The con job sets off a chain of events that lead to a riveting cat-and-mouse game with a heist at its fulcrum.
