Jacob a 17 year old homeless teen lives on the streets and has the ability to see the future. He is constantly faced with an ethical battle that shows his true character as a hero. Even though society does not give him more than a passing glance and a rude comment he still has the strong desire to save them from themselves through the visions that he has. At his side is his foster sister Bailey 16 whom Jacob was fostered with before he was on the streets. And Ronnie 16 the hopeless drug addict and bad influence on Bailey that Jacob finds himself continually saving. A new threat has come to the city and it is up to Jacob Bailey and Ronnie to stop them. Along the way they meet up with a powerful ally with incredible strength which brings them to ask the question are there others with special gifts out there.
