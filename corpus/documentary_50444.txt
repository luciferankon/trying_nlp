The documentary gives a very personal insight into Conchita's way of fulfilling her dreams and her ambitious aim of taking the next step: winning a Grammy. Be it the recording of her new album international performances of Conchita at concerts talk shows gay prides around the world the famous Golden Globes or the debut at the legendary Cabaret "Crazy Horse" in Paris. "Conchita - Unstoppable" shows how the "Queen of Austria" manages to reconcile her tight schedule and thereby always remains faithful to herself and her convictions.
