June 2016 saw the much anticipated return of guitar legend Ritchie Blackmore to his rock roots with three remarkable shows in Europe. The first two shows in Loreley and BietigheimGermany were captured on camera and the result is "Ritchie Blackmore's Rainbow: Memories in Rock". Showcasing tracks from his Deep Purple and Rainbow catalogue Ritchie reinvents classics such as "Highway Star" "Man On The Silver Mountain" "Mistreated" "Spotlight Kid" "Black Night" and "Stargazer" closing the show with the timeless "Smoke On The Water".
