The search for Ireland's next style star. 10 aspiring stylists designers and fashion buyers battle it out a senior creative role with Aurora Fashions and a design contract with ByCaprice lingerie. Tasks ranged from window dressing to personal styling advertorials to catwalk shows photo shoots to dress design. Part advertiser-funded sponsors included Bulmers Light Special K Lipton Tea Scotch Hall Shopping Centre Arnotts and the Daily Mail's You Magazine.
