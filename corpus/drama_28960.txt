Stepbrothers who follow separate paths and live in different worlds work hard to succeed and find peace within. Dillon fights to save the company he has worked for since adolescence from falling into the hands of a crooked developer. His struggle with Union thugs pales in comparison to the war he wages against personal demons that threaten to unravel him. Tommy is on the fast track to corporate glory and has spent the last several years making all the right moves while aligning himself with all the right people until one faithful day he meets the woman of his dreams who could prove to be his demise.
