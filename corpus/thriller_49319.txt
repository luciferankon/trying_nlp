Cliff (Blake Bahner) a marginally successful writer of a paperback detective series has found the perfect setting for murder. He is using an adult club for swingers as a backdrop for his questionable characters to commit their lurid crimes. While reluctantly researching the seedy swing scene he and his cosmopolitan wife Elaine (Krista Lane) meet Daryll (Rick Savage) and Nancy (Christine Moore); an outgoing unusually charming couple. They unwittingly become caught up in a criminal scheme when they get involved with an adult club for swingers and they have a difficult time for understanding what is happening to them.
