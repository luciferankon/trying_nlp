A young woman investigates when she sees a strange car parked outside her friends' house. She quickly finds the two women bound and gagged in the basement. After an abortive rescue the masked goon has three buxom babes securely tied to play with. In the other story a woman decides to divorce her husband who is connected with the mob thinking she'll obtain a handsome divorce settlement. Unfortunately her husband quickly shows why it's not a good thing to trifle with an organized crime figure as his wife and her best friend are bound and gagged and headed for a date with the fishes.
