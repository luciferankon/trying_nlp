Carolo and Benito are two losers with a loose screw who share their passion for cinema. They've written a script for Benito to direct and Carolo to act but they also need a leading actress so they approach their muse Amanda Castro a famous star whose career is starting to go downhill because of her drug problems and bitter character. She takes them for whackos (which indeed they are a bit) and refuses to listen to them. So then driven by their determination the two men kidnap her and take her to a remote house in the woods where filming will take place in the hope that her initial hostility will turn to friendship once she gets to know them and their project...
