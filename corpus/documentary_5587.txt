Models of Achievement is a compelling series that explores the success and aspirations of 20 Australians who changed their lives through higher education. Interviewees tell their stories in their own words many from disadvantaged backgrounds originally arriving in Australia as refugees many uncertain of their career passion. The forum-style Models of Achievement Special Launch Episode moderated by Annette Shun Wah involves participants in a Q &A discussion on their educational journey. Models of Achievement proves it's never too late to change your life.
