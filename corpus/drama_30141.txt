College swimmer Chad Billingsley is his middle class family's pride and joy. After a moody phase of scared denial he owns up to father attorney Roger that Rosalie Frank the provocatively dressed waitress who attended his frat's last party and is missing since had sex with five of them. Roger does his utmost to prevent the potential statutory rape case ruining his son's future but confides in his moralistic wife refuge house worker Karen who instead of supporting the boys haunts them like Rosalie's mother Inez with multiple tragic results. Drama about a respectable middle class family whose lives begin to fall apart when the parents come to suspect that son Chad may have been involved in a gang-rape.
