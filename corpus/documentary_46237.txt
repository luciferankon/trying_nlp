From the over-the-top wedding of Kim Kardashian and Kris Humphries to the fairy tale wedding of Kate Middleton and Prince William and even the surprisingly traditional wedding of Courteney Cox and David Arquette TV Guide Network relives the "Most Memorable Celebrity Weddings." With a panel of entertainment journalists as well as stylist and event planner to the stars Sharon Sachs the one-hour special takes you inside the most exclusive celebrity weddings.
