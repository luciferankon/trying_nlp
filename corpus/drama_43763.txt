What would you be willing to give up your life for? Some believe that the ultimate sacrifice is to lay down your life for a loved one but what if that loved one wish you didn't exist. David and his Cal share a common bond and that bond is both of them desire a relationship with each other. The only problem that they have is that life has placed a wedge of bitterness and a unforgiveness between them. David in an attempt to recompense have given up everything that he own in order for Cal to be a success.
