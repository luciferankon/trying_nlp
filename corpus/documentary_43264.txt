The true story about the only regiment in the world where all of the combat pilots were women. They were around twenty when they volunteered. They learnt how to fly in the night and how to load the plane with bombs- and how to bomb German troops. They made even more combat missions than their male colleagues. The Germans called them Night Witches. The pilots navigators mechanics technicians all were women. They had Ten Commandments. The first was: Be proud you are a woman.
