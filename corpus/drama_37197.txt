As two late teen's - a recently paroled hustler trying to get out of poverty (Ro Mack) and a privileged wannabe gangster trying to get in (Dante Alexander) - connect they learn how much they need each other and that the line between legal and illegal isn't always well-defined. Brilliantly directed by Lamont Stephens IN DICE WE TRUST is an urban drama about two young men from opposite sides of the track who make their names from their involvement in an underground gambling ring.
