When the girl of his high school dreams moves in next door Nick Peters is determined not to shy away for a second time. But before he can declare his love he discovers she is waiting for her boyfriend to propose. Her boyfriend however is "in the closet" and has no real intention of proposing. So Nick decides to lure him out and accidentally finds himself entangled in a comedic love triangle. Nick soon realizes to win the girl of his dreams he may have to make out with the man of hers. "Self-Delusion and Other Obstacles" is a sweet romantic comedy that will leave audiences pleasantly surprised.
