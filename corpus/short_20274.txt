The story of childhood friends Brian and Vic who after spending some time apart deal with the tensions of sexual orientation after they attempt to renew their friendship. At the beginning it seems that Vic's sexuality will not be a problem but after the two friends hang out in a local bar Brian realizes his hometown is not as tolerant as he is. The couple is faced with family and social concerns which goes from the argumentative to the violent. As the main characters try to mingle with the conservative town they soon find themselves looked upon by a small town resistant to change. This film explores homophobia and violence in small town USA this time from the 'straight' point of view.
