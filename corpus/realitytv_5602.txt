This 8-part series is hosted by Peter Elliott and raises awareness of the importance of architecture and design. 'The Art of the Architect' follows the journey of local architects as they travel around the country of New Zealand creating and transforming impressive designs. Each individual episode goes in depth on how the architect creates their specific design and the creative process that they undergo. The audience are able to see and understand the challenges for both the client and the architect including the likes of sticking to a budget whilst meeting the clients expectations.
