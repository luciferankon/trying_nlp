Nothing's more important to Emily (Anna Marie Dobbins) than to graduate from high school and head off to college. But everything changes during a night out with friends when Emily is attacked and raped. Forced to deal with the attack on her own things become more difficult when she learns she's pregnant. With her future now on hold she must figure out how to take care of her new child as well as deal with being a rape victim. But the biggest challenge will come in court where she will fight to keep her daughter.
