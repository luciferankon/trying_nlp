A young movie star Scotty Moran discovers that at the age of just fifteen not only is his career as a leading man is over but that he has now money. He agents explains to him that he has paid money to the Ronald Reagan retirement home and if he "retires" it would help facilitate his comeback. Scotty is way but agrees. The faded stars of Hollywood who live in the home initially welcome his with open arms but when he turns out to be a monster - a brat they decide to kill him. The methods they use are "murders" that they explored in their careers on the big screen.
