Wherever You Go chronicles the adventures -or rather uproarious misadventures- of Manny a taxi driver that right after almost driving over a drunken young lady of a clear dazzling beauty April decides to take advantage of the circumstances and plans out a detailed scheme to allure her and win her heart by impersonating a charming businessman. His quite con-artistic plan get twisted when this risky amorous conquest will gradually lead him to truly fall in love with that same girl who he once intended to have as a toy affair.
