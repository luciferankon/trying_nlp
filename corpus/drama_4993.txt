This is about a woman who could not find her only true love. Rosa Gomez. She was a woman with so much courage to find her love. After her performance in singing a man asked her for a moment and so they went to a resturant where they learned they just had love at first sight. They had many differences and lifestyle. Rosa loved the streets while her fiancé loved fine dining. Her fiancé learned her ways until Rosa's friends' mother was behind all her time together trying to force her daughter and soon to be her son in law to get married. Rosa only had a child and soon found out many things she didn't know. She soon found a dancing career to care for her baby. Luckily her friend was behind her all the way being helpful.
