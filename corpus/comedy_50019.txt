Patxo Escobar thought that he was a successful guy. He was the editor in chief of an important magazine in his country (Colombia) but now he has been fired and his life has changed dramatically. He's suffering from panic attacks. He feels like a looser. He has lost faith. But one day when he was talking to his 8 years old daughter on a bench at the park she had an amazing idea: "Daddy what if you start making videos on YouTube?". Those words were a revelation...
