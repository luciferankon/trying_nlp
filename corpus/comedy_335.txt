To save their home from foreclosure two TV producers (Oscar and Brett) bet on races plan a robbery and consult with the mob. Framed through a behind the scenes look at community television eight film school grads a small time mob boss and a full-time Muppet desperately try to save their home and their way of life by making "TV: the Movie". Fusing documentary footage with fictional scenes "TV: the Movie" invites its viewers on a deconstructive journey through reality and illusion.
