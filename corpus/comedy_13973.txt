Delivering the Goods is a "follow your dreams" comedy about Steve a first generation Italian-American wishing to become a head chef against his father's blue-collar judgment. Unfortunately Steve doesn't have a culinary degree so his dream quickly hits a brick wall and in order to pay the bills he takes a job with a package delivery service. Initially embarrassed by his new 9-to-5 existence Steve soon learns there is more than one way to pursue your dreams and the answers to life's toughest questions are found when you stop looking for them.
