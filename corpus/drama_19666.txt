Ian and Cullen brothers who have been estranged largely from Ian's problems with addiction travel to their family beach house to reconnect before Cullen is to be married. When Cullen asks Ian to be his best man Ian invites down old friends to give his brother a real bachelor party inviting substances that put their relationship to test. Two estranged brothers travel to their family vacation home for a weekend alone. At pivotal junctures in their lives they hope to recapture a once cherished bond.
