Doctor Wen Er Du is an experienced Mongolian doctor whose expertise in Mongolian medicine has helped him cure countless patients. However as he ages he finds it increasingly difficult coping with the workload. Hao Ri Wa a medical school graduate knocks on his door hoping to be taken under his wing. But Wen constantly makes it difficult for Hao. Eventually Hao impresses Wen with his perseverance hard work and kindheartedness. When Wen finally decides to accept Hao as his student a mysterious patient's appearance changes the old doctor's mind...
