Tahir Shah an anti-hero among explorers is looking for the most famous lost city in history. Personally he loathes the jungle it's insects and savages. Tahir's collaboration with his jungle guide Richard Fowler ends dramatically and Tahir sets his hopes on Eduardo Huamani. Eduardo knows Pancho an Indian who in his youth he says saw the ruins with his own eyes. Together the three of them head off deep into Madre de Dios the misty rainforest of Peru in search of the legendary golden Inca city Paititi - the House of the Tiger King.
