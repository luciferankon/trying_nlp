The McCandles ranch is run over by a gang of cutthroats led by the evil John Fain. They kidnap little Jacob McCandles and hold him for a million dollar ransom. There is only one man who is brave enough and smart enough to bring him back and that man is Big Jake. Big Jake McCandles is on the trail on bandits in this action drama that stretches from Texas to Mexico. It's 1909 and the Old West is giving way to modern times. When the outlaw gang led bu vicious John Fain raids Jake's ranch and kidnaps his 8-year-old grandson Jake's wife whom he hasn't seen in 18 years sends for her husband to rescue the boy. While the law gives chase in rickety automobiles Jake saddles up with an Indian scout a faithful dog and a box of money. But paying ransom isn't Jake's idea of good old frontier justice.
