Chen You Ming an ordinary indifferently-looking man lives with his only daughter and he is struggling to get a primary school's permission for his young daughter. Six years ago Chen You Ming lost his wife in a car accident and he lost his job as a narcotics agent. Since then he runs a small grocery store to make a living but life is tough on him. When he knows that the driver Ma Wen is early released form jail he determines to find out the truth of his wife's death.
