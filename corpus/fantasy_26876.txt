This eight episode mini-series uncovers all of the mysteries from the cult-classic adventure game DARKSTAR: The Interactive Movie. Narrated by Peter Graves (Mission: Impossible/Stalag 13) and starring Clive Robertson (Sunset Beach/StarHunter) and the entire original cast of Mystery Science Theater 3000 these eight episodes walk the audience through the entire cinematic animated and epic story of DARKSTAR without having to actually play the game. The game was written and produced more like a feature film and this series takes the viewer from bow to stern aboard the doomed Starship Westwick down to explore an alien planet and finally to the end of the story. This series also reveals all of the secrets of the game and shows all of the many "death sequences".
