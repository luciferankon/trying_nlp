In Nottingham three young men prepare for a contest in the early days of the Northern Soul scene. At the club hosting the contest the owner - Mr. Lance - takes bets on who will win and he tries to fix the result by sending two goons to break the legs of local hero Jake Bancroft one of the contestants a good-looking guy whom Lance's bird fancies. The goons do their dirty work on a side street while the contest gets under way first with Spike and then with the favorite Tony. Will Lance's dastardly plan succeed?
