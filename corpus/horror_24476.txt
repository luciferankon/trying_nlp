It is a love story that unfolds between a 26-year-old researcher and surgeon Reyna Malik at a Mumbai hospital and her oncologist beau Shekhar amidst mysterious and dark occurrences in her house. Reyna has just moved to Mumbai after taking a short career break. She is very eager to get back to work - to surgery her first love. But because of a trauma she endured before the break the hospital is not sure if she is ready to take up new assignments.
