The story takes place in a Lisu Village near the Nu Riverwhich in the border among Yun Nan Tibet and Burma. Di ALu in love with Mu Pa's sister Ji Ni is banned from pursuing her by Lisu tradition. Mu Pa is arrested after cutting down a national protected plant. Mu Pa's father tries to marry Ji Ni to A Da to settle Mu Pa's case. Ji Ni dressed in a traditional Lisu wedding outfit disappears into a foggy mountain on her wedding day.
