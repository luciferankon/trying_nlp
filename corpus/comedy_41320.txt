A chicken farmer from middle America wins a vacation for having the "Best Chicken". When Millie arrives at the new-age retreat she meets lifestyle guru Angelica Lovecraft and her reality TV show producers. Before she knows it she has accepted their lucrative offer and they arrive at her farm for a week of reality TV programming. While Millie is awfully excited about the show her husband is much less enthusiastic the producers are getting antsy and Angelica is getting dramatic. They're going to turn Millie's life and her chicken farm upside down.
