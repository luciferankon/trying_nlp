Space 139 is a modern film noir thriller about a night clerk for a storage facility located in the desert who also moonlights as a private investigator for the local town Sheriff. Then one day a mysterious woman walks into his office wanting to lease a space. It is then the murders begin and he finds himself being followed by strange men in dark suits. Caught between her suspicious behavior and beauty a dangerous cocktail that spirals out of control places him between two opposing forces that only see him as a pawn in their deadly game.
