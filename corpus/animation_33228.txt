The 'Bird of Steel' movie is based on the satirical 'immature audience' underground comix series supplement as seen in filmmaker/cartoonist: Tex Watt's infamous "Space Chick & Nympho: Vampire Warrior Party Girl Comix". A 'motion graphic-comic book' tale about a feathery bird-child refugee from outer space that is sent to Earth; and is found to have extra-ordinary powers ( in the 1970s era North America)!
