A game-changing new interactive format and provocative television event designed to kick-start a new national conversation about one of the most divisive issues in Australia today: climate change. Witness the journey of mutual discovery for former conservative senator and committed climate skeptic Nick Minchin alongside founder of the Australian Youth Climate Coalition Anna Rose to see if they can change each other's minds. At home and online the national audience joins in leading to a cliff-hanger decision. Have we shifted the national mood? Have we changed people's minds?
