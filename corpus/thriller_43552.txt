INTO THE BLACK is a found footage movie that follows a couple Sarah and Randy on a journey from Los Angeles to India while they shoot their travel show #SPONTANEOUS. Despite a warning from the locals to avoid the sacred cave temple the crew visits the site awakening the mythological god to curse them on their journey. After Randy was bit by a snake at the cave strange occurrences start happening to the crew. Eventually leading to a member of the crew's disappearance. With death staring them in the face they must come to terms with their actions and try and escape their dreaded fate.
