It's a story portraying Pandu's struggle to maintain peace in his family. Pandu is happily married to Kamala. However because Kamala could not give him a child Pandu decides to marry another woman Malkin. Trouble begins for Pandu for sharing time between the two demanding wives who pretend to be one above the other. Kamala and Malki fight for their rights making Pandu hilariously sandwiched in this battle. What follow is a great comedy and funny one liners..
