Dean returns to his roots in Toronto to revitalize his acting career - but all his agent can drum up are some less than spectacular roles. With an industry-realistic tone and style in the vein of CURB YOUR ENTHUSIASM EPISODES and EXTRAS we see the humble realities of Canadian Showbiz exposed through the lens of American Tabloid Culture. Dean has to expertly navigate his past and reputation in order to re-brand himself while staying relevant and on the wagon.
