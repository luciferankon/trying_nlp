A collection of five Undertaker matches from 1994. Undertaker-Underfaker is from SummerSlam 94. Undertaker-Kwang is from Monday Night Raw. Undertaker-Yokozuna is from Survivor Series 94. Undertaker-Neidhart is from WWF Superstars of Wrestling. Undertaker-Lawler is a Coliseum Video exclusive from a Wrestling Challenge taping.
