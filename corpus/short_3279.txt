It's evening and she is standing happily at a work table in her kitchen perhaps making a cake while the radio finishes a song for young lovers. Then the 7 o'clock news begins with a report on an arrest in Israel. As she proceeds through a couple of kitchen accidents - dropping a bowl pulling a smoking pan from the oven and waving the smoke out the window of her fourth-floor flat - she realizes that the newscaster is informing listeners of her cooking disasters. She decides to lower the kitchen blind....
