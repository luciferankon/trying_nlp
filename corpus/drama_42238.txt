A wild journey through the dark S&M underworld. Salene a struggling Hollywood writer with a haunting past gets drawn into the world of S&M as she is researching a novel about a dominatrix. Georgia Brown a native hometown friend arrives for a visit. She is soon seduced into a forbidden affair with Salene. When a murder occurs in an underground club Salene is prime suspect. While the shock of the murder accusation brings Salene back to the mainstream roles are reversed and Georgia takes on the role of the dominatrix.
