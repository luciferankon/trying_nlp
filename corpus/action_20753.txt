This documentary will uncover the true story behind the difficult birth (and near death) of the ambitious American Dream Car: Fisker Karma. Hundreds of people in Southern California worked day and night to create a project aimed at changing the status quo of green automotive technology. Developed by the best engineers Fisker's technology had been realized with shorter time constraints and less budget compared to other established companies. Yet even with its innovative developments the US media presented it as a failure in technology. But the Fisker Story is not just about one-sided media coverage-one where rival car companies pay bloggers and sponsor journalists. Suppliers dealers and engineers were all forced to watch the destruction of their American dream as the results of their years of work was thrown under the bus. Why?
