Mahatirtha Kalighat is a devotional and historic representation of the institutionalizing of the Temple of Kalight in West Bengal for the Mother Goddess Kali. It shows the story of two devotees of the Goddess Atmaram and Brahmananda and their resolute dedication in the praise and worship of Goddess Kali. It also portrays the truth of worship and prayers that it was not meant to be restricted to the rich and upper class as the times have contaminated so now. And also gives a glimpse of all of Mother Goddess's various images of Sati Saraswati Chandi Kali and others.
