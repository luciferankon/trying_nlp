Runeseekers 2 is sequel of Runeseekers 1 simple point and click adventure type game. Find the hidden items created by Hyptosis for Escape Games 24. You gather items and give them to spirits or use them to help them to make them happy. Help Rufus make all of the nature spirits happy again. Search and find what their hearts desire and bring peace to the natural world again. Search for hidden objects and then find who wants each of them.
