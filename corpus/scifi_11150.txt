Paulina the Thumpers eco-warrior discovers the the glorious city on the Atlantic Ocean she was joyfully anticipating has been perverted by a corporate kiratsu. With only days left to avert ecological destruction the college student goes undercover to steal the hidden ecological impact study that proves the Kiratsu is willing to accept a cost effective but inevitable ecological disaster. Paulina is captured on the Thetis mid-ocean construction site. When Maili and the Thumpers rush in to rescue her it begins a battle with the Violators in which the destruction of the oceans is at stake.
