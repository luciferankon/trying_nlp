Harvey and Matthew Sparks are in trouble. 10 years ago they drove down a hopeful road marked Hollywood but now they find themselves in the cul-de-sac of despair. Out of work out of luck and out of ideas the Sparks brothers have hit rock bottom. But when their English grandfather the late great Sir Buster Sparks bequeaths them his country mansion Sundown House they see a way out. Fly to England sell the house and build a new life with the money. However when they arrive they find the house mysteriously occupied. As they become acquainted with their house guests they soon realise that nothing is as it seems. And Harvey is harbouring a dark secret that will put all their lives in jeopardy...
