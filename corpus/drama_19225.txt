The film is a devotional film in praise of Maa Ashapura Madhwali who is very much worshiped in the Kurch region of Gujarat. Jaya Seal looses her mother and she finds solace at the feet of Devi. A tycoon Raj Singh Verma comes to find peace in the temple. He meets Jaya and they fall in love and get married. Problems arise when Jaya is not able to bear a child but her problems are solved with the blessings of Devi.
