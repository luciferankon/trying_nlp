Dr. James Fadiman shares a "Transpersonal Conversation" about the earliest days of transpersonal psychology taking psilocybin with Ram Dass (AKA: Dr. Richard Alpert) and the founding of the Institute of Transpersonal Psychology along with many other stories. Dr. Fadiman was an original founder of the Institute of Transpersonal Psychology and the 2nd President of the Association for Transpersonal Psychology. He has written several books and given lectures across the globe. Books that he has authored or co-authored include: Health for the Whole Person: A Complete Guide to Holistic Medicine Unlimit Your Life : Setting & Getting Goals and Exploring Madness: Experience Theory and Research.
