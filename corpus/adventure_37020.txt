Dangerous Diva a cat-suited criminal bests three lovely ladies while achieving her criminal aims. In the first story she surprises a beautiful brunette while searching a room full of junk for a secret treasure and leaves the poor girl naked gagged and tied to a chair. In the next story she learns of gold-digger trying to make time with the husband of a close friend. When the woman declines to leave the man alone Diva grabs her gags her with duct tape strips off her clothes then proceeds to fondle and fuss over her naked captive. In the last story Diva learns of a blonde blackmailer who discovers a notebook full of juicy tidbits. Before her adversary can convert her knowledge to cash Diva steals the documents and leaves the angry woman gagged and hogtied on a bed.
