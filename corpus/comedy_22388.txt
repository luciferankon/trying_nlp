A musically handicapped young man who turns his back on his heritage and left his strict mariachi family to fulfill his dream to become a famous rock star. After years without success he and his equally inept band members might be able to pull it out of thin air. Rick redefines his passion with the not-so-subtle prodding from his employer and learns to "Air" so he can compete in the US Championship and against his life long nemesis Todd who is fast becoming an Air legend.
