About the Imagery (Samantha Krukowski) liquid skin lava over quiet rocks fast sheets | bubbles down | painting water 'till Triple planes converge 'neath almost waves About the Music (Bruce Pennycook) Consistent Trickle__Shrill then Still__Quiet and Even. Pushing Pulse Pulling Back__Catch and Release__Call and Response. The constant rhythms of the clear water flowing and twisting around the rocks and pebbles inspired a steady pulsed music that shifts with the kind of irregular regularity of the water. On top of this I added sets of sounds that sparkle and glisten and add relief to the relentless yet somehow soothing pace.
