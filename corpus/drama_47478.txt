It's a story of Samantha who sets out to reunite her mother with her family. They disown her when she disobeys them and gets married to an American. She is born and lived all her life in the US but her family values are deeply rooted into the Indian System. Over the years Samantha's mum falls ill and wishes to reunite with her family and thus Samantha takes it upon herself to bring the family together. She enters her grandparents' house and convinces them to accept their daughter. In the process she falls in love with her maternal uncle's son Surya.
