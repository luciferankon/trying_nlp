Lita is a twenty-something young woman who is bright smart articulate. However the avoidance of the memory of the rape she endured several years earlier has her in the throes of self-abusive behavior. She eats and drinks too much. She has sex indiscriminately. Then she meets Abel a bookstore clerk with a love of mystical poetry and big women. She wants desperately to love this man but she first must decide to love herself.
