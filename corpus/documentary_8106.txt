The Crazy Artist features the life and times of modern master: George Draghici as a crazy artist known as "King Ghici" who embarks on a journey to enhance the art viewing experience as he paints live with a jam band in front of a live audience. He paints to the music as it inspires him. King Ghici not only faces the common inner struggles of an artist when he loses inspiration he also lives in a comical power struggle between his wife and muse and an unconventional entourage with his manager chauffeur bodyguard and dog.
