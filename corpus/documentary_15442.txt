A profound insight into history of Yugoslav cinema through censorship perspective. How did famous anti-communist movies from Yugoslav time succeeded in being made and what consequences did they had to bear? Film contains original interviews with most important dissident filmmakers from communist time including Dusan Makavejev Zelimir Zilnik and Lazar Stojanovic. Through the conversation with Yugoslav film authors and excepts from their films this documentary tells a story of a film phenomenon and censorship and its focus is in fact a painful epoch of Yugoslav film called a Black Wave. The film tells a great Ťthrillerť story of the ideological madness which characterized the totalitarian psychology.
