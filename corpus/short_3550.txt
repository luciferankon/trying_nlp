Applicants submitted 8-10 minute videos and were chosen from a field of 87 entries that spanned 22 nations and 18 states within the US. Jurors consisted of Kalamazoo College faculty staff and students along with regional social justice leaders evaluated each entry; all applications were reviewed by three jurors. Each finalist will be awarded $1000 and brought to Kalamazoo College Oct. 9-11 where they will present their video and make a case for their projects to an audience consisting of a second jury other finalists Kalamazoo College campus members invited guests and the general public. One project will receive the $25000 Global Prize at the end of the weekend gathering."
