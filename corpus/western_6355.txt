A rancher comes home and finds that his son has been murdered and his daughter kidnapped by a bandit gang. He hires a professional tracker with a reputation for finding his quarry to help him find the gang and rescue his daughter. A rancher returns to his home to find his son dead and his daughter missing. Before dying his son tells him that they were attacked by troopers. He then organizes a posse and sends for his friend a Marshall who's the best tracker there is. But his friend sends his Deputy and protégé a Black man. And the first thing he reveals is that his daughter was not taken by troopers but by Indians but the man steadfastly maintains that he believes it was a trooper. Eventually he proves them wrong. And they go on with the Deputy leading the way.
