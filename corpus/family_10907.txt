Two sisters like all of us are engulfed in a sea of decision making. After her father's job transfer Jeannette a senior in high school is hoisted from her comfortable life in the city to the country. Will she accept this change or will she resist her new circumstances? Her older sister Bridget sets off on her college journey. Will she ace her first year of college or will she crumble under pressure? The decisions these sisters make have a dramatic impact on their lives and the lives of those around them.
