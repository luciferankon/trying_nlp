Fray Elliott's group of best friends - who were like his family - have to pick up the pieces of all that he left behind and face the music together. If they can handle it that is. In the meanwhile they try to figure out if their friendships are even worth saving. This fresh new series will take audience members on a journey with people they can relate to. These people aren't afraid to be themselves for better or for worse. They all have their highs and their lows.
