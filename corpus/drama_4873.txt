Maurie Monnier a poor young American sculptor in Paris marries Clarice a gold-digging model who later abandons him. When Maurie's wealthy father dies Maurie returns to the US but his mother and brother won't have anything to do with him because of his poverty. At the end of his rope he's about to kill himself when he meets Hope a beautiful young girl who inspires him. Just when things are looking up for Maurie and his new love Hope who should show up but his gold-digging wife Clarice.
