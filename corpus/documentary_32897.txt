The Search for Sheila NaGeira The Legendary Princess of Newfoundland will explore the origins of the popular Newfoundland folk legend surrounding Sheila NaGeira. Sheila is said to be an Irish princess from the time of Queen Elizabeth I. Many people in Newfoundland accept this legend as history while others insist it is pure folklore however it is possible that a plausible explanation might exist that could marry the two points of view and create a more important truth one that explains not only the origins of the Sheila legend but also explains the generational evolutions that have often re-shape the history to meet the changing needs of the changing times.
