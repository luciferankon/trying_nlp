Ambrosia flowers thought she was just your average girl. But life does not work out the way she expected. Her husband is tragically murdered and her life spirals out-of-control. Moving back home with mom reveals details about her identity which she is not quite ready to hear. Ambrosia discovers these truths while her mother is pressuring her into a new relationship a strange man is stalking her her hormones are going wild from her pregnancy along with battling the social stigma of being unwed mother and her best friend is too self-absorbed to be of any help. In the end she discovers a truth that gives her peace That tragedy is all around her but so is love.
