Alex Liddell is a misfit an outcast. At school she is the constant victim to Macy Robertson's cruel pranks. One day Alex can't take it anymore and leaves. But it's when she runs away that her fate is sealed. Alex is killed and wakes up in Rogana. Here is where she meets odd characters who must help her realize what has happened. But not all is good in this world. The evil ruler named Rogana as well is a demon that thrives off of pain. Alex must not only come to terms with what has happened but must also face this demon and save her new friends.
