Chickie Bryce a young stenographer has a dream of marrying a millionaire. Until then though she's content with carrying on with Barry a lawyer from a nearby office. The relationship soon becomes serious but one day Chickie meets an actual millionaire Jake who sweeps her off her feet. Barry tries to win her back but his efforts are stymied by Ila a girl who is in love with Barry and doesn't want him to get back with Chickie.
