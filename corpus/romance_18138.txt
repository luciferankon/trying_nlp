Kulsoom (Noor) is engaged to Sarwar Shah (Babar Butt) but she is deeply in love with Zill-e-Shah (Shan). Later in an encounter Zill-Shah (Shan) kills Sarwar (Babar Ali) therefore sent to Jail. As Zill-e-Shah returns from jail he finds Kulsoom (Noor) married to Abid Shah (Safqat Cheema) the elder brother of Sarwar (Babar Ali). She is not comfortable so is Zill-e-Shah. In his sought to forget his past Zill-e-Shah adopts alcohol and company of courtesans. There he meets Sitara (Saima) a dancer and they fall in love.
