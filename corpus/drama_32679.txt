Chief Adikali Momoh III is the ruthless leader of Ropolon Village were only men are allowed to rule and girls do not go to school. He is married with eighteen wives and none of them had the good fortune to bear the crown prince.Aminata the daughter of Pa Kelfala the village drunk is a fifteen year old ambitious young girl who wants more to life than what the village can offer.Kidnapped and forced into an relationship with Chief Adikali Aminata eventually escapes from the misery and backwardness of Ropolon to the city in search of her dreams.Did she make it? FIND OUT!
