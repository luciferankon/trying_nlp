We follow our unlikely hero Roswell Stevens through his sojourn as a Mormon pioneer in the early years of this new American faith. Roswell Stevens joined the church in Canada and is the only individual to immigrate to the U.S. and take part in the exodus from Nauvoo serve in the Mormon Battalion cross the plains in the 1847 Vanguard company with Brigham Young and while an old man volunteer as a member of the Hole-In-The-Rock expedition. There is a lot of drama action heartache and tears as we witness this fascinating and untold story of the Mormon saga thru the eyes of this faithful servant.
