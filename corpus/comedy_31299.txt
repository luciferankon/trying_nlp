On the road to success three friends (Richard Kirk and Donovan) have dreams of becoming big-time party promoters in a city that never sleeps where the weather is always nice and the women are always beautiful. Reaching the top and achieving their goals will prove challenging. But with social media vying for their attention the guys embellish their lifestyles and troll those whom they envy. Will they survive the flashy and fast life of South Florida or will they crack under pressure?
