Third sequel of collecting documentary views of various human communities - after One Year (a film about the life of old people in the villages and secluded dwellings of Hornácko) and God's Quarrz (a year in Northern Bohemia) this time the two directors and two photographers (Jindrich Streit Marie Zachovalová) set off to look at four Romany families living in a Romany settlement (Slovakia) in a village (Hungary) in an industrial area (Poland) and in a suburban community (Czech Republic).
