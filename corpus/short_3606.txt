Red Sneakers is a short film about nine-year-old Cassie and her little brother Tommy who go through a series of after-school babysitters leaving their frazzled working mom with few options. They end up at the apartment of Mrs. Feinstein a cold rigid woman who goes to great lengths to keep her spoiled son separate from the other kids. However she's met her match in Cassie who finds a way to subvert her control and payback the shoddy treatment!
