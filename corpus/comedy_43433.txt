Newhaven Port Football Club nicknamed The Chicken are struggling in the lower echelons of semi-professional football. American Buisnessman and Chairman Benedict Power has surprised the fans staff and players alike by appointing eccentric ex-player and club caretaker Dougie Christmas to take charge of this weeks game. Dougie's love for town and club is unsurpassed but will this be enough to win the seasons biggest cup tie? As the match looms we meet a wide range of the club's characters ; club captain and should-be manager Steve Miller team hard man Spud and his sidekick Tom tannoy announcer and bigot Joe Malloy blind physio Keith Funnell and Peter Philpott the club's self appointed number one fan. Will the club make it through. Will the players take to Dougie's unusual management style and is Benedict Power all he appears to be?
