An unsophisticated dreamer named Gerald accepts $1500 front money from a criminal known as Bleach to smuggle black market cigarettes from Mexico. Soon thereafter Gerald learns he has become the victim of Identity theft. Overwhelmed by his ill fate he uses the front money to maintain ownership of his house leaving Bleach hot on his trail looking to recover his cash. Meanwhile a master of Identity theft named Reign disguises himself as a homeless man while retrieving financial documents from people's trash. He is living the high life in the Hollywood Hills addicted to crystal meth. His most recent victim Gerald is now spiraling into the depths of humiliation and alcoholism. Gerald is eventually rendered homeless which sets he and Reign on a collision course as both men struggle for redemption in this surreal tale of crime and punishment
