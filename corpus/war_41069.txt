This is a story of a regular man becoming a hero. Andrey Petrovich Starikov a chemistry teacher nicknamed "Charlie" lives with his wife and mother in an old house. This house is inhabited by people of many nations. Among them there are the Russians the Ukrainians the Jews the Tartars and the Armenians. But when the Nazis enter the city the civilians's lives change. To save his family and friends "Charlie" has to work as a translator for the Nazis...
