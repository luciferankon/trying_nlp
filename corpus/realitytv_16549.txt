The Ridgeways follows Erica Ridgeway-Cook a former national ballroom dance champion along with her enterprising parents Ron and Jennifer. While many of the family's adventures revolve around their real estate businesses many more happen during their daily lives. Each story unfolds as mom daughter and their accomplices have one mischievous plan after another to surprise both Ron and Erica's husband Garrett. To even the score Ron and Garrett have a few fun surprises of their own for the ladies. Whether it's knocking down homes rescuing crazy dogs biking up mountains in the hot California sun or hijacking fishing boats around the bay each rendezvous with the Ridgeways keeps viewers wanting to know: "what happens next with this family?" From tense confrontations within the business to funny family moments each show has viewers wondering what predicaments the Ridgeways are getting into now. Each episode of The Ridgeways will highlight the chemistry and personalities of all the different family members (and their crew) as they both manage their business and the family challenges that crop up.
