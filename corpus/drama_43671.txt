When Xiao Bo's father who had a sex change and wants to be called mom is dying he insists on having the taste of Xiao's milk (sperm) when he dies so he gives him head. Soon after he meets his best friend Nana for the first time after his sex change now a beautiful woman. Other acquaintances are getting sex changes while Xiao copes with his loss. Nana becomes despondent at the prospect of never finding the right man.
