Sunao ni Narenakute is a story about the blossoming friendship of five young people who were brought together by Twitter and their journey to finding honesty with each other and with themselves. Nakajima looks up to his father who was a war photographer but can only get a job as an assistant for gravure magazines. Mizuno is a provisional high school teacher currently on probation. Nishimura is Mizuno's best friend whose boyfriend has been avoiding her since she discovered she was pregnant. Ichihara works at a magazine where he is being blackmailed into sexual favours by his chief editor. Park on Twitter takes on the persona of a doctor when he is actually working at a company that sells medical equipment to disinterested doctors and at the same time trying to take care of his younger sister.
