The stern instructor at a top modeling school insists that two of her less capable students stay after school for some additional tutoring. She starts their remedial training by forcing the young would-be models to pose while bound and blindfolded. When the winsome lasses tire of their treatment and protest their teacher mutes their arguments with thick cleave gags. Once bound and helpless their cruel mentor contacts a dealer in bizarre garments to see if he might interested in acquiring their unwilling services.
