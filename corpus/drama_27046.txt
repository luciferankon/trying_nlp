Yulia and her pregnant sister Katya witness the robbery of collector car. In the shootout bandits kill all the collectors. They do not notice sisters but Katya gets wounded by a stray bullet and dies in hospital. Her baby daughter survived and was adopted by Yulia. After the robbery one of bandits nicknamed Robust gets into a car crush which killed the wife and daughter of Rescue Service Captain Andrey. One year later the leader of bandits gets caught. Yulia who has recognized him is going to speak in court as main witness. Accomplices of the leader try to kill Julia but she was saved by Andrey who happened to be near. From this moment he will help this woman. Having found a new purpose in life Andrey will soon realize that Julia and her little daughter became his new family.
