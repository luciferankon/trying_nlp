Grateful Nation is a unique and compelling outdoor adventure series that goes behind the scenes and into the hunting fields with American disabled veterans. This original unscripted program takes viewers inside the minds of wounded combat veterans and returns them to their traditional American hunting heritage. The majority of narrative for each episode comes from the organic conversation between the featured Vet and host in the context of the hunt. Hosted by former Army Airborne Ranger Tim Abell this series will keep you captivated by the brave soldiers and the stories they will hold with themselves for a lifetime.
