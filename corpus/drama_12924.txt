Police officer Miki fears he might be gravely ill but cannot accept the reality. He alienates his family believing he is protecting them. He has several crises at work distracting him. Animal recently released from prison is blackmailing the police chief Hector Rubio. They have history that is coming back to haunt them. Diablo is in a power struggle with his father Animal over the future of the family criminal activities and the loyalties of the motor bike gang.
