While all couples of the world present each other with tender words of love our heroes take surprises of fate. Popular rock star is desperate to finish the song for Guy Ritchie's movie and finally meets his muse for so many years. Meanwhile shy gynecologist in between patients inspection falls in love with a fatal beauty. Love is the price in their own security. Close to midnight the city does not sleep. Especially the students of the first course! On this night they staged a large-scale party timed to coincide with Saint Valentine's Day.
