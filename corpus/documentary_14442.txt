The Directors are an award-winning electrifying and unparalleled 11-piece Show band led by vocalists Tony Picciuto and Randi Katz. The Directors Showband made a thrilling career and became the 2012 recipients for Best Canadian Event Entertainer at the Canadian Special Events. They have appeared in the golden globe and 7 genie award winning Oscar nominated film Barney's Version starring Paul Giamatti and Dustin Hoffman. Also to their credit have appeared alongside the cast of The HBO series The Sopranos in the 2012 Nickelodeon film Nicky Deuce. This highly entertaining documentary was shot at different times between 2009 and 2013 and features an exciting showbiz path of the Montreal's most sought after showband.
