Could there be another Earth out there in the vastness of the Cosmos or are we indeed alone in the Universe? This question paired with our curiosity regarding the existence or not of any alien life forms in Space has been filling our minds with expectation for as long as we are humans. Is now the time that we actually have a chance at finding the answer to this riddle? Scientists all over the world in search of answers have discovered that of the 50 billion solar stars and systems that exist in our galaxy one in five should have planets warm enough capable of sustaining life so there must be out there a suitable exoplanet that we can call home. With scientists believing that we are living in the Golden Era in the discovery of exoplanets is the greatest discovery in the history of mankind finally within our grasp?
