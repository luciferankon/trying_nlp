Féodor Lakasse is a John Blunt. Féodor Lakasse is married to Hanna who can hardly put up with him. Féodor Lakasse used to be a taxi driver but following some traffic offense he was forced to change jobs. He now drives an ambulance. An ambulance that carries Gabriel a ten year old boy who after being mistreated by his unworthy parents has become silent to the world. Féodor and Gabriel two autistic beings one driving the other to various institutions and hospitals. And unexpectedly two withdrawn creatures who open up to each other...
