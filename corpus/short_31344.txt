It's the last days of a hot summer and the brothers Oliver and Manuel are experiencing the last days in the house of their childhood. While Manuel is talking about leaving everything behind and moving to Berlin Oliver silently awaits their mother to come back and to take care of their situation. Instead Gaëlle arrives - a friend from the past. Oliver wants to revive old memories but Gaëlle prefers to get carried away by Manuels lightheartedness. The relationship of the two brothers is about to be put to the test.
