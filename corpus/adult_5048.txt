For his first assignment a reporter interviews an older madam who shares her prostitutes' stories. Her first would-be prostitute was casually offered money from a limousine riding businessman. Back in the present a wife and a husband join a man and a woman in "the party room". The madam reveals her prostitute Sonya saved the married couple by being like a surrogate. The madam also recalls how sending client Harry to an oil treatment with prostitute Babbit fixed his boredom with sex. The sexually inexperienced reporter gets a little nervous and the madam playfully seduces him. Now ready he joins the "the party room" while she calls his editor to thank him. It turns out the editor keeps her off the headlines and she turns his naive reporters into men.
