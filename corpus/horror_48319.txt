A young mother Victoria "Vic" McQueen discovers she has the power to unlock portals to other dimensions. As she delves further into this hidden universe she encounters many other "inscape travelers" - some benevolent and some who have been corrupted by their power. When Vic encounters Charlie Manx an immortal who stays that way by feeding off of the souls of children she makes a sworn enemy of Manx who kidnaps her son in retaliation. Now Vic must travel through hell and back to reclaim her child while fighting to keep her family and her sanity together.
