Damocles NV is a company where you can hire people to deliver unpleasant messages or shut someone up who shouldn't be talking. Benjamin a newcomer in the city promptly gets promoted to director after finding a suitcase full of gold on the street and brings it back to the office. But is Damocles really as innocent as it seems? If so why does it keep files on anyone who has ever made a mistake in his life? And what happens when Benjamin discovers a file on Nadia the girl he likes but is almost too afraid to talk to?
