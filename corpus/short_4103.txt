Narrates the bizarre experiences of two young men on a quest. In the crowded streets of a market place on a typical day two young men are on a quest. As we realize what they are looking for it becomes apparent that it is not an ordinary day for these men. Yet in many ways it is not so different from any other day in our lives. "Amguleechaalitham (Manipulated by Fingers)" is about the times we live in - with an abundance of choices endless desires and the irrepressible urge to satisfy them at any cost. But the decisions we make are they really our own?
