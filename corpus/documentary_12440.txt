It is a great uncertainty to leave comfort steady pay and normality to wander. Yet there is a great freedom that comes with a full tank of gas an open road and an open mind. On December 7th 2014 Forrest Mankins and Garrett Danz packed up a 30 year old Landcruiser that was saved from a junkyard and left their lives behind for a life on the road. They did this simply for the sake of experiencing life to the fullest and earning an unbelievable story to tell. Whilst covering 15000+ miles in 5 months they met many unique individuals gained corporate sponsorship and asked probably the utmost incredible question anyone could ever inquire of themselves "What makes you feel alive?".
