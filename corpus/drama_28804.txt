Inside the clock is a coming of age drama/thriller film produced by Birmingham's award winning independent production house Rotten Park Road. The story embarks upon two teenage friends Max & Jack who are every day lads from Northern England trying to make a little bit extra money so being selling Cocaine for a local drug lord Frankie. Upon their first sell Jack gets really drunk & stupidly decides to hide 10 grams of cocaine in an old alarm clock then misplaces it without realising until its too late. Their quest is to find the drugs before Frankie finds out they went missing otherwise their easy get rich schemes & livelihood are in ruins. But will their search bring them what they set out to find or even more complications?
