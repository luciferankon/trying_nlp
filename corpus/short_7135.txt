We've all heard that laughter is the best medicine and though we don't recommend you try to bottle it a daily dose can be a surprisingly effective supplement to a person's physical mental and social well-being with no harmful side effects. Laughter has the physiological benefits that counter the harmful effects of stress in the body as well as improving a person's quality of life. Whether it is laughing at a joke or attending a laughter club people are laughing for their health and laughing for laughter's sake.
