Design Superstar portrays the life and work of a creative visionary who dominated the New York and Los Angeles design scene in the 1970s and 80s. The consummate gentleman Angelo created glamorous interiors for prominent figures such as Ralph Lauren Donald Trump and Diana Ross and maintained a grand lifestyle until his untimely death at age 50. Throughout his life and career he reminds us that interiors have a visceral impact on behavior and that the home is the stage set for life.
