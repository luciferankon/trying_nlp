The documentary gives the viewer a look inside Armin's life. From his parental house to the private jets. It brings you from empty hotel rooms to the heat of the moment as 100000 clubbers await their hero. From the preparations of his 'Mirage' world tour and the phone call that will tell him if he'll keep his number one DJ Mag Top 100 position. 'A Year With Armin van Buuren' is a documentary of an important time in the life of the DJ and reveals his perfectionism and huge passion for music.
