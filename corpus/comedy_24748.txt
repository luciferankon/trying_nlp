College buddies Shawna Lanelle and Kayla are all grown up now living in an apartment together and hanging' with the fellas Damian Bryce and Russell. The six friends-Shawna the shy sweetie Lanelle the rambunctious queen Kayla the calm kitten Damian the distinguished diplomat Bryce the arrogant aristocrat and Russell the allusive observer-keep it real about each other's dating endeavors with the opposite sex. Their escapades take them from the woes of Internet and speed dating to deciphering a co-worker's intentions to breaking down the "ex"-factor. With each experience everyone learns a little more about themselves and each other. They will also learn if what they are looking for has been in front of them all along.
