The ambitious young Ina Littmann is investigative journalist for the TV talk show "Eye in Eye". Her current subject is Henry Kupfer who wrote a bestseller about a psychopathic killer after he was 8 years in prison for manslaughter himself. As an entry for the show Ina plans to use a current series of brutal murders among prostitutes. When Ina meets Kupfer she is despised and fascinated at the same time. Soon she's convinced that Kupfer not only writes about murders but commits them himself. She smells a smash hit and prepares to prove him guilty on the show... Before best-selling author Henry Kupfer was jailed for having committed a murder now he wants to promote his new book on a talkshow not the least because a serial killer is on the loose. TV-journalist Ina succumbs to Kupfer's enigmatic charisma while she suspects him at the same time to be the serial killer. By developing their exploiting relationship mass media's addiction to sensationalism and greediness for high ratings is unmasked.
