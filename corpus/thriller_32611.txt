HURRICANE AARON is a psychological thriller set in the deep South modern day. Aaron a mid-20s army veteran returns home from his tour of duty for Christmas coinciding with his brother's winter break from law school. Days later the brother's girlfriend dies in a mysterious accident for which Aaron is blamed and imprisoned. Over a decade later when Aaron is paroled he moves in with his brother who was disabled by the same accident that killed the girlfriend years ago. As Aaron learns the truth about his brother's role in the mysterious accident that led to his incarceration he faces a choice with fatal consequences.
