Starting Up brings to you the story of a young Silicon Valley techie who is back in India on an unexpected work leave. He shifts to stay at his newly wed wife's apartment who had been preparing to join him in US. At this unexpected juncture of their life begins the story that traces the awkwardness and intimacy of marriages arranged in today's ambitious millennial generation. The story is about discovery and hope of a generation of young India who are wary yet confident of creating new opportunities.
