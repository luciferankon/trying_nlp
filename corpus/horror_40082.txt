Les Walsh unknowingly leads his team of paranormal investigators to an out of the way house for what he thinks is their dream investigation. As the investigation starts he begins to notice their are a lot of strange things going on in this house. The Investigation takes a turn for the worse and the team is forced to leave their Lead investigator Les Walsh (Frank Powers) alone inside to continue to document whats going on. The investigation takes a fatal turn and leaves their dream investigation looking more like a nightmare!
