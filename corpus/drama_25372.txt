Sarah Denise and Toni have been friends since they were in elementary school together. In between soccer matches band practice and dance recitals they had dreamed about what their future might look like. Going to school falling in love marriage kids- they had everything mapped out. Now in their thirties things are not as rosy as they had imagined. Faced with incredible challenges at work and at home they have to find a way to get up after life has kicked them in the teeth.
