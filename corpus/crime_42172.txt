With a constant dose of rap-filled street energy caustic humor and raw dialogue Partners For Life creates an engaging film with subtle messages about todays society. The film explores the influence the media and environmental surroundings have on todays youth. Partners For Life chronicles the life and relationship of best friends Brad Oswald and Tommy Dusalt as they aspire to become the crime lords of the city. Brutally entertaining with gritty images intense characters and an eye for urban transgression.
