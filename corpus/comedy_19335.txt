Billy Mack has been an actor since childhood. An actor that left Hollywood and traveled the world saving animals in need including endangered species. But Billy missed acting. So he returned years later to Hollywood and along with his companion Boo soon realized that he needed to rescue another endangered species... his acting career. Billy and Boo's adventures encounter different twists and turns as Billy tries to get back into "the business". Funny experiences surprise characters and stars all add to the fun and funny adventures in The Trouble with Billy.
