A six-part film documentary series consisting of archival video interviews with the distinguished actors directors writers composers and special effects mavens who were special guests at the FANEX Film Conventions over a period of 19 years. The interviews along with film clips movie trailers and commentary by well-known film historians authors and critics takes on the impressive task of examining the history of the horror fantasy and science fiction films from the groundbreaking silent 1920s through the Golden Age of Universal the Silver Age of Hammer and up through the 1980s and beyond. The series has a true running time of over 8 hours of fully produced video content perfect for television. Available for Worldwide Televised Distribution.
