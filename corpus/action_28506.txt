After a major nuclear war happens millions and millions have lost their lives. One government takes over the rule of the whole planet. Men women and children are put in prison camps . One man R.J. Scott a news reporter goes around filming what he sees to show the world what is going on. He is on a two day journey to a head prison camp. The camp has information. If he can get his hands on this info and hand it over to the alliance he can help to overthrow the government.
