It's been a year since Bo-young became a divorcée. She is tired and even the most trivial conflict is hard for her to endure. She picks fights with a postman with friends with her indifferent daughter. One day Bo-young leaves her daughter to give a lecture at an institute. There she rooms with Cheong-nam who is another divorcée and they sharing stories that were never told. When Bo-young returns to her father's house her disabled father unexpectedly gives her words of encouragement and for the first time in a year she prepares breakfast for her daughter.
