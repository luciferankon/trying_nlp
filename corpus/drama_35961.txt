Adapted for screen by Milica Djukic. This is the first drama structure (with defined screenplay cast etc) ever on the work of an Iconic Serbian poet Branko Miljkovic. The screenplay casts a different light on the iconic work image and unexplained death of Branko Miljkovic who died at the age of 27. Brank falls in love with Mila Andjelic also a poet and a translator. We watch this love story unravel with another iconic figure and poet Oskar Davico who gets involved in this love triangle being Branko Miljkovic's protector beneficiary and somehow rival at the same time.
