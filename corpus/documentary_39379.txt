Jazz Legends Live. is a series of live performances from contemporary jazz artists recorded all over the world in a variety of venues - intimate American jazz clubs open air jazz festivals and concert halls throughout Europe. However what is consistent throughout the series is the wonderful music the quality enthusiasm of the performing jazz legends and the appreciation of the worldwide audiences.
