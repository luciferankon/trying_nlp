The fate of the country rests in the hands of a group of teenagers. Bai Ze (Chen Xiang) is the emperor's loyal subject who is sent to search for the five mystical stars that can restore stability and peace to the tumultuous times. During his travels he meets four very different teenagers who join him on his quest: troublemaker Zhan Xiong Fei (Leo Wu) chef Yuan Shuai (Deng Shao Wen) mysterious A Ji Ya (Zhang Yu Xi) and upstart princess Zi Su (Zheng He Hui Zi).
