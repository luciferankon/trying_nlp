Many beautiful office workers are trapped in bondage peril in this series of vignettes. In the first tale a beautiful realtor is gagged and tied to a post when she tries to correct back rent from a deadbeat. In the next story a con-woman gets the drop on a nosy reporter and leaves her tied muzzled and stuffed in a box. Later at a government agency a new employee is forced to undergo an unusual loyalty test. The next tale tells of two beautiful officer workers who are abducted by a ruthless kidnapper only to discover that their company refuses to pay the ransom. Next an eager new employee is gagged and hogtied as part of her new company's orientation program - or does the man who trussed her really work in the security department? Finally two beautiful women run afoul of an enraged spouse is furious when she discovers her husband's infidelity.
