This Five-Piece features: "James Byrkit: Sao Feng's Map" (6:18) "Crash McCreery: The Cursed Crew" (5:23) "Rick Heinrichs: Singapore" (5:13) "Penny Rose: Teague's Costume" (3:37) and "Kris Peck: The Code Book" (5:20). We hear from Heinrichs Rose property master Kris Peck conceptual consultant James Byrkit creature designer Crash McCreery. Each looks at various production specifics. The shows cover the design and implementation of Sao Feng's map the creation of Davy Jones and his freaky shipmates the Singapore set Captain Teague's costume and the making of the Pirates Code book.
