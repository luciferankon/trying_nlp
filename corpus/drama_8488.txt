Zoe is a young Parisian who is 9 months pregnant already scheduled to give birth in a cesarean procedure in a nearby hospital. In a whim of a moment she packs a suitcase and drives to her grandmother in the French countryside. She decides to give birth naturally with the help of her grandmother by her side. Joining her in the upcoming days are her boyfriend her mother and her mother's boyfriend. All are concerned about Zoe and her situation as the 9 months mark has long passed and the baby still won't come out. As time goes by haunting memories visit the family.
