Each episode involved the main character wishing to find their other half (soul mate). The basic rule is to show that the participants cannot be married. In one episode the main character becomes a young woman in the next episode - a young man. In each episode audience can see new participants. Main character must choose a companion of his proposed show participants. Venue show -limousine. Host offers to the main character to choose from the pictures of the first three participants in the show. Other participants are waiting in a restaurant or bar near the limousine. Three of the first participants taking seats in the limo with the main character. The MC in a conversation on any topic with the participants can replace one or all participants to the other or others. Until the end of the show he has to make his choice and leave in a limo only one participant. Prize winners of the episode becomes a night walk on the Vancouver limousine and a romantic dinner for two at one of the restaurants in town.
