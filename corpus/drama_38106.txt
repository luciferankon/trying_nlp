In Puerto Vallarta Silvestre has to fight with the pain of his own loneliness. His fragile temperament and strong personality are altered day by day and the arrival of his brother Jorge with his partner Eva at the port is one more element that excites and distress his moment of life. From a meeting in a restaurant of these people will sprout a new and unexpected situation the unforeseen; at sea dropouts on a beach and a night crazed by excess alcohol and drugs.
