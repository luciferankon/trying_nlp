After a violent robbery the British/American film student Gabe decides to abandon his studies Shortly afterwards he receives a link to a video on the Internet showing a masked man in Amsterdam who is beating up a car thief. Inspired by this expression of justice he tries to convince a film crew to accompany him to Amsterdam to capture this masked hero on film. After they arrive in the Dutch capital the crime fighter proves untraceable. During the last night of their stay there the team makes one more attempt to locate the mysterious man by luring this vigilant out of hiding.
