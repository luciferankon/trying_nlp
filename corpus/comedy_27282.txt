Fed up with their dead end lives in Cleveland a scheming filmmaker an eccentric musician and a delusional male model move to Los Angeles for a fresh start on life. Chasing flashy scenes from The Entourage and glamorous images they've seen in the movies the guys envision a lifestyle filled with money women and success. But once in the City of Angels the three clueless friends soon discover the sobering reality of life at the bottom of the L.A. food chain. The Cleveland Crew may never make it in L.A... but they're going to have one hell of a good time trying.
