Amey and Priyanshi meet at a club and decide to have a one night stand. While Priyanshi is battling a massive breakup from her puppy love Amey has his own baggage which he is trying to get away from. No names no numbers and no emotions are the rules of the night but unfortunately they have "No place" as well. It is the story of two strangers who will understand life and a bit about themselves at a deeper level in a night filled with adventure as they search for a place for the instant gratification of their problem - Quickie.
