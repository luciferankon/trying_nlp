Detached from his son's life because of an eight years jail sentence The Forgotten Father is a story of one man's desire to reconnect with his son and the struggles he faced with his brother his son's mother while trying to reconnect with his son. Although he now has a completely different outlook on life during his incarceration his fight just began while wanting to assume his responsibilities. At the end he realized that the journey to fatherhood is more difficult then he had ever assumed.
