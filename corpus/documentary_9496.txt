A criminal court judge starts a running club on L.A.'s notorious skid row. The club's members comprised of homeless drug addicts a recovering single mom and an paroled murderer consider themselves among those least likely to succeed. As they train together to run in international marathons they learn to dream big as they are re-acquainted with their own dignity.
