Honk For Peace follows the 2003 anti-war movement starting in New York City and culminates with the historic 2006 Senate race between Joe Lieberman and Ned Lamont in Connecticut. In the Democratic primary Ned Lamont a political newcomer ran an anti-war campaign beating Joe Lieberman the 18 year incumbent and former Vice Presidential nominee. As a result Joe was forced to run as an Independent in the general election and defend his Pro-war ideals.
