Directed by Damian Apunte and Adam Fiske Preppers follows two roommates Allison and Ben having to face life death love and survival during a doomsday chemical strike. With the air tainted outside they are holed up in their apartment trying to survive while challenging each other's patience fears values and dreams. Allison is the 'prepper' while Ben is carefree in life which forces these dueling characters to try to find a way to work together. As they fight to survive they are challenged with a rekindled romance all while their impending fate forces them to question everything.
