All the staff of a luxury hotel in Manga del Mar Menor is nervous about the arrival of Don Juan a potentate who has reserved most of the hotel for himself and his entourage as he gives out impressive tips. Don Juan has been preceded by Rosita an ex-folkloric who enlivens waiting and boredom flirting with every neighbor's son who discovers his eagle sight. Installed in the hotel Don Juan who besides money has a crazy hobby for women chases every girl who enters his radius of action giving constant skids. Rosita who is in the reserve has a reprisal program in which the hotel's waiters and bellmen happily collaborate. Fights scares and persecutions follow to the exhilarating and unexpected end.
