Something Unorthodox is a short film about Carol Rosen a 35 year old Jewish wife and mother who reaches a place in her life where she feels the need to rebel. Years of suppressed questions and doubt about her religion marriage and life are brought to the forefront after she learns of the adulterous behavior of her husband Simon a 39 year old notarized prosecutor. He's caught in an internal office investigation linking him to prostitution solicitation. With trust shattered and faith shook Carol yearns to delve into her fantasies of an unorthodox life. She finds a pathway to this experience through a friend and coworker Travis Gill an attractive 35 year old black man. It's a story that challenges temptations beliefs and one's own morality.
