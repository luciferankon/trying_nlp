From YouTube to TV! THE DUDE PERFECT SHOW follows the 5 best friends known as Dude Perfect as they cook up their next idea for another crazy video. Operating from their headquarters in Dallas Texas these friends have the job every kid dreams of. The crew consisting of bearded captain Tyler Toney Tall guy Cody Jones Garrett Hilbert aka "The Purple Hoser" and twins Coby and Cory Cotton take the extreme wherever they go. Wether it's a archery kart battle or a trick shoot session with Chris Paul and Aaron Rodgers one thing's for sure when you hang with the Dudes anything can happen!
