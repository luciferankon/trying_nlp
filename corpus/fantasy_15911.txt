In a world where mortals exist lives one guy who can see the world for what it really is. His name is Zayn and he sees both the mortals and the immortals. His friend Chloe's Dad is a terrorist and he seeks to take the life of his daughter and the lives of many others. With the immortal ghosts to help him he sent out men to kill his daughter and her friends but Zayn was determine to stop him but was it too late?
