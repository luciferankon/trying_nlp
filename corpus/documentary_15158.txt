Kamal Joumblatt recounts the major events of his life until his assassination on the 16th of March 1977. The film takes us on a global journey from Lebanon all the way to India following the footsteps of Kamal Joumblatt the Founder of the PSP (Progressive Socialist Party) the Leader the Rebel the Reformist the Thinker the Poet the spiritualist... Kamal Joumblatt (1917-1977) becomes the last witness... of his own life.
