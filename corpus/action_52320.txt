July 1981 British cities are rioting. Paul a passionate 2-Tone fan is days away from his 18th birthday but his future is put in jeopardy as a night of uncontrollable violence and small town revenge escalates into a full blown riot. The brutal SPG and skinheads are closing in old town loyalties start to fracture as Paul and his gang find themselves isolated at the wrong end of town. The streets are a battle-ground as Mods Punks Rude Boys and Casuals fight for the soul of British youth.
