A young man SABIK (24) gets thrown out of a club. Angry and hurt he sits opposite the club waiting for his friends to come out. Sabik gets upset and decides to walk away from this place. In a city unknown to him he now wanders into the night. At first he feels naked without any of his material belongings. But when he has a few encounters with strangers on the street his anger slowly transcends into some kind of spiritual awakening. Which makes him reflect on the choices he made in the past.
