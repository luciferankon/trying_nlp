A missionary and his wife are killed by natives when they accidentally discover their golden temple but his daughter is spared and is raised by the tribe. Fifteen years later she seeks vengeance for her parents' murders and joins an expedition searching for the same golden temple. A tribe of Amazons guarding a temple build on top of a gold mine murder an explorer and his wife who discover the location. Their young daughter escapes and is raised by local tribesmen. Now 15 years later she sets out to avenge her parents' murder.
