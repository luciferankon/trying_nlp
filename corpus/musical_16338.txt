La Magia de los 60 (The Magic of the 60's) is a musical series which recreate important moments from the wonderful decade of the 60's. This series includes live performances and recordings of the best musical groups and soloists from those years. The performances were recorded in natural scenarios and at Cubana de Television Miami Studios [us]. The Magic of the 60's also shows amazing choreographs to create an environment that takes us back to that time. The program provides an opportunity to relive unforgettable moments with musicians from Latin American Spanish and other nationalities.
