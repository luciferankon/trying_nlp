This 110-minute show includes film clips from 20 rare and seldom-seen TV shows from the 1950s and 60s: "Sir Francis Drake" "Dragnet" "William Tell" "The New Adventures of Charlie Chan" "Captain Gallant" "Adventures of the Scarlet Pimpernel" "Adventures of Robin Hood" "Mr. and Mrs. North" "Adventures of Sir Lancelot" "Decoy" "Ramar of the Jungle" "Rocky Jones Space Ranger" "Adventures of Long John Silver" "Front Page Detectives" "Errol Flynn Theatre" "Federal Men" "Captain David Grief" "Biff Baker USA" "Dangerous Assignment" "African Patrol".
