A secretary is robbed by a masked thug and securely bound and ballgagged by the burglar before he makes his escape. The media calls the criminal "The Ballgag Bandit" and soon the city's woman are terrorized by the thought they could be his next victim. When Loren's home is invaded by the thug she recognizes his voice to be that of the janitor in the building where she works. The burglar decides to make it unpleasant for the woman who identified him to the police and she's quickly tied gagged and awaiting her fate at the hands of the unhinged criminal. In the second story a buxom blonde is stripping gagged and bound in a naked frogtie by the thief who broke into her home.
