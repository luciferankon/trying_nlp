This hilarious mockumentary follows the rise and fall of fictional 80's rock stars Arnell and Jarnell who at that time were considered to be the most irreverent and irresistible forces in rock-and-roll. Arnell and Jarnell's close friends talent representatives and love interests reminisce about the duo's crazy and drama filled lives. The two lived a famous lifestyle comprised of sex drugs and rock-and-roll until their unexpected disappearance.
