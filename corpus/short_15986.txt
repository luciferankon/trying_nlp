7 year old Rebecca lives in a large and creepy house in which she believes monsters are living under her bed. After close inspection of her bedroom floor and taking heed of her mother's reassurance she gathers the courage to walk down her dark and gloomy hallway in search of a glass of water. However still shaken by her nightmare she imagines monster's shadows on the wall but quickly dismisses them and regains the courage to return unhindered to her bedroom.
