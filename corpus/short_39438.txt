In 1998 Dr. James Thomson perfected a method to grow human Embryonic Stem Cells in a laboratory environment. His discovery sparked a new debate in what historically has been a long line of moral dilemmas initiated by scientific advancements. Lines in the Sand provides the viewer with an unbiased presentation of facts regarding Stem Cell research. This documentary addresses the nature of Stem Cells their purpose origin and future use in advanced medical treatments. Can health care truly be transformed through the use of Stem Cells? Up until now this question has been answered from the heart not from an understanding of facts. Lines in the Sand seeks to reverse this trend and provide individuals the information they need to decide for themselves free of coercion and spin.
