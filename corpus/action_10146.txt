JAKE BOLT wins a championship street fight. The only problem is his mafia boss had instructed him to lose. Going on the run Bolt visits his estranged foster brother HENRY in San Francisco. Henry reluctantly takes him in but finds their old feud is far from extinguished. Then Bolt falls for SONYA - Henry's ex-girlfriend. As the mafia closes in Bolt Henry and Sonya must band together to fend off the mafia - while trying not to kill each other in the process.
