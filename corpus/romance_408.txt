Right after graduation Ben and girlfriend Emma embark on a trip to Thailand. It is not long before they come to blows and part ways. Ben is propelled to explore the exotic surroundings of the paradise island of Koh Samet. There he meets mysterious local Fon a local masseuse on the beach. As Ben and Fon come closer we see what happens when two radically different worlds collide. Is this a summer romance or an encounter that will change their lives forever? Can they overcome their cultural differences and stay together or will Ben leave this fantasy and return to the real world back home?
