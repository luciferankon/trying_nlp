Valley of Susa will be transformed for the next 20 years in the open-air building site of a high speed train connecting Turin and Lyon (TAV). NOTAV is the popular opposing movement and the valley is a confrontation between two opposite way of conceiving and living social relations economic activity culture politics relationship with the land. Men and women have decided to continue to live in their own land devoting their existence to the daily fight for together life and future. A red thread brings their story together woven like an epic tale: the eternal fight of good against evil.
