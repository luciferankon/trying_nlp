Aamir (Viddharth) a college professor in Sikkim befriends Rizwan a student who is targeted by other students because he is a Muslim and makes him stay in his own place. We get to know about Aamir's family and his Hindu lover Meenakshi (Hardika). However their love affair comes out in the open and that is why Aamir is in Chennai. However upon his arrival at the airport he is met by mysterious helmet-wearing men who give a phone and order him to speak. A mysterious man tells him that he has kidnapped his family who he will let live if Aamir does the tasks he sets him. Having no other alternative Aamir accepts this order which leads him into a situation from where there is no backing out. Can Aamir who has become a puppet in the hands of a shady operator find courage within to shape his own destiny?
