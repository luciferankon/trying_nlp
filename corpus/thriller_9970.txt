For years Damien's father has been in the slave trading business until one day he gets caught. Facing a lifetime in prison he miraculously gets a second chance at life. To free his father Damien picks up where his dad left off in the slave trade to pay his father's bail. Faced with a looming deadline and no other option Damien resorts to selling his best friend Lynda to the slave trade. Lynda's mother appoints Detective Mathew to solve the case and find Lynda. With Matthew tailing closely Damien will do anything to save his father's life and avoid prosecution.
