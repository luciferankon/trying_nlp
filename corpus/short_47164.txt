This a story of a modern Magus struggling with his thoughts and his desire to be a successful person. He enters his magical room filled with creepy and distorted things and declares his intentions. He shouts his intentions into the winds and the 4 corners of his magical room it echoes and echoes into the fabric of the universe and sets in his contentment. Seeing his future inside his mind he then starts his journey into a chaos ritual. A chaos ritual gathering occurs and wanders into the wilderness. He gathers elements of earth fire  water and air and mixes it inside a divine bowl. He chants unknown languages and calls upon the spirit of the dead and the transcended He then ignites it with fire in preparation for his magical climax. He conjures the universe and stares into the symbol turning him mad. After the ritual he realized his mistake and falls asleep into the abyss.
