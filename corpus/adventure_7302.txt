8th Wonder of the World is part of the widely popular RTS game series "Cultures" originating from Germany and the successor of the well-known and successful "Cultures 2 - The Gates of Asgard" and "Northland". A few years after the happenings in Northland Bjarni our viking hero and his brave comrades return to their homes after their last adventures and are living happily together with their families. Life was peaceful again but after some time evil rises again to bring hatred anger and death to the world. Our heroes must set out again to stop the evil forces from taking over the world. A new adventure unfolds before the eyes of Bjarni and his friends. "Cultures - Das Achte Weltwunder" plays a couple of years after the last installment of the Cultures Game Series "Reise Nach Nordland". The heroes around the famous viking Bjarni traveled back to their homes after their last adventure and returned to their everyday lives. But one day evil and darkness try to take over the world once again. First no one believes the warnings of Bjarni and his friends because they felt something bad might happen. Soon hatred war famine and evil are spreading amongst them and slowly parts of their world collapse under the weight of evil. A new adventure begins.
