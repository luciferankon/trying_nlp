Two Goth Emo teen lovers have their Halloween night ruined by a gang of drunken bullies. Teasing leads to destruction. Destruction leads to rage. Rage leads to murder. . . and murder leads to a last vow of bloody revenge in this campy fun-filled ride with jumps bumps screams and laughs in this cheesy homage to genre stereotypes. Starring Conrad Ricamora from ABC's "How To Get Away With Murder."
