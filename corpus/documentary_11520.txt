Keeping kosher is only one way in which many of the Jews in Texas maintain their Jewish identities while living amongst a community that is overwhelmingly Christian. Whether one keeps kosher or not however does not matter as the Kosher Chili Cook-Off remains a unique and exciting event that serves to unite the whole Community - Reform Conservative Orthodox unaffiliated...and even non-Jews. Meet some of the colorful people charitable organizations and amazing leaders comprising the Dallas Jewish Community as Wernick combines humorous interviews with smart narrative in documenting Tiferet Israel Congregation's 14th Annual Kosher Chili Cook-Off.
