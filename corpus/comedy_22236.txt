Widowed and with a young son in his charge García works as a publicity agent in a razor blade factory. One day taking advantage of the cycling tour to Spain García puts into practice a new and risky system of advertising. But Mr. Raúl the factory manager does not like the idea and dismisses him. From then on García will make a living in the most varied jobs: barber shoeshine or guide of the Prado's Museum a difficult situation for his former boss to accept the relationship he keeps secret with his daughter Conchita.
