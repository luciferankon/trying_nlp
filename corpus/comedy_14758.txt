Jovan Ilic is a former professional football player. His successful career is over now assumes the role of husband. Jovan trying to find a job and trying to justify himself. His wife Ana is a marketing expert. She's ambitious attractive and educated woman. But they live with Ana's grandmother. Grandmother is grumpy and boring. She regularly organizes intrigue for them and daily talks about great-grandchildren. There are and two neighbors Gula and Mika. Gula is well- read bureaucratic-minded and cynic. Mika tries to be a seducer. The action of Kazneni Prostor is set in Belgrade. After the bombing the country is in transition. This is the life of ordinary people.
