With a USB Headset/Microphone in your possession this game will let you choose from over 35 songs made popular by various top artists to sing. As you sing in the game you will gain the amount of points depending on each lyrics phrases you sing. If you can keep it up with the rhythm you are sure to gain a big score at the end of each song earning either a Gold or a Platinum Record and unlocking various rewards at the conclusion of each songs. However if you perform very poorly with each lyrics phrase chances are you will possibly get booed off the stage.
