Adam is a lost soul. He has lost his girlfriend Amy his plum office job and has hooked up with the wrong sort of people. Dee works for "The Big Man" and is the kind of person you don't want to meet but somehow in our life we all do. Adam must take Dee to retrieve a mysterious package for "The Big Man" that Adam had been left with. On their way to collect the package Adam must avoid the people he loves and also deal with the imposing Dee. For Adam the package will have long lasting consequences that he can't even begin to comprehend yet.
