Toonerville was a popular newspaper cartoon from 1908 to1955 that was adapted into an animated cartoon series in 1936 by Van BeurenStudios. It also is known as "Toonerville Folks" or "The Toonerville Trolley That Meets All the Trains." A central character in both the original comic strip and animated series is the "Toonerville Trolley that met all the trains" that arrive in a small town.
