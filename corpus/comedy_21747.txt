Don Luis a wealthy industrialist is very happy because he has been kidnapped by a family of fraudsters led by an agronomist with the pretensions of an international delinquent. The reason is that upon being abducted the visit of some auditors who have discovered the numerous and serious frauds carried out in their business is delayed. To make matters worse don Luis convinces the naive kidnappers to destroy the accounting books and compromising documents. Finally these scammed scammers won't even get their own cell to have the luxury and comforts that don Luis has in his.
