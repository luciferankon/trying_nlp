Each season is filled with laughs as Larry struggles to gain control of his life and find the love of his dreams. Despite his best efforts the monsters are always there to mess everything up. Among the monsters are a Mummy whom everyone hates for no particular reason a Wolfman who's afraid of everything and a scheming Vampire who carries the brunt of Larry's hatred. Larry's only ray of light? His loving sister who is always looking out for him and the only one not afraid to kick a little monster ass if necessary.
