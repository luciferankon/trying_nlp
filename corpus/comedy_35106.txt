M (Yossawat Sittiwong) a young man who wish to have a better life has decided to go to India for pilgrimage at the four main Holy sites of Buddha: Lumbini where he was born; Sarnath where he delivered his first teaching Bodh Gaya where he Enlightened and Kusinara where he attained nirvana. But to complete what he wish for he have to take another journey in his mind to face the origin of suffer himself.
