Demonic possession or delirium either way Alex Murphey (Jacqueline Lovell) suffers. In a desperate attempt to seek closure over her husband's death Alex travels over seas to Amsterdam with her baby Ashley in hopes of finding tranquility. When Brother Wolf (Edward Nyahay) a traveling monk crosses paths with Alex at the shrine of "Our Lady of All Nations" God's mysticism manifests forcing Alex to confront her demons.
