Crafted closely from the civil war affected areas in the Northern Province of Sri Lanka its about a group of fearless Salesians of Don Bosco who rescued and transformed the lives of children who were caught in the crossfire. It is a fascinating journey into the heart of a true Salesian of Don Bosco. Some of these interviews are rare and have been documented for the first time. This documentary was extensively shot in Kilinochchi Mullivaikkal Puthukkudiyiruppu (Mullaitivu) Mankulam Negombo Colombo Uswetakeiyawa India (Chennai) and USA (California).
