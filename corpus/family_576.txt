The series run for 26 weeks and it was composed of 26 shows of 24 min. each. In the shows Bruno Pischiutta interviewed personalities of Toronto's Fashion Industry: designers creators store owners tailors and seamstresses models etc. The goal of the show was to bring to the two hundred thousand Italian homes in Toronto Ontario the best that the fashion industry had to offer. Children fashion bridal fashion men and women fashion formal and casual was presented weekly with great success. The name of the show was Telemoda which translates in English as Fashion TV. Later the show was imitated several times in Canada and overseas.
