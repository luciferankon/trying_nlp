The first round of the 2013 Big Ten Men's Golf Championships hosted by French Lick Resort is underway at the Pete Dye Course in French Lick Ind with the second round following at 1 p.m ET. Saturday's third round begins at 9 a.m. while the final round is slated for 8 a.m. on Sunday. Nine of the top 20 finishers in the 2012 Big Ten Championships return for this year's event including five of the top 10. Illinois returns two top-20 finishers from a year ago as Thomas Pieters (T-5th) and Brian Campbell (T-15) look to help the Illini earn their fifth-straight Big Ten title.
