Bam a troubled hit-man rescues Jenny an Orphan Girl who's spent her life as an entertainment commodity for a cruel gangster named Big Daddy. Bam and Jenny become best friends. He teaches her the tricks to his trade until one day she becomes too dangerous for anymore hit-man lessons and instead turns to ballet. Bam decides to retire from killing and goes on a new leave for a clean conscious. One day during Jenny's ballet lessons a caravan of Big Daddy's gangsters in prop cop cars corner Bam into a shoot out in the middle of the street. Jenny is ripped out of his life. Bam survives then let's just say he comes out of retirement to get his best friend back.
