Inspired by rumors circulating in a tight-knit suburban community Montreal Swap Club is a thoughtful funny and relatable story of Val Matthews the under-appreciated mom loyal friend and committed wife. Underneath Val's nervous laughter and raunchy sense of humor is an unhappy unfulfilled 39 year-old in an ailing marriage that craves more between the sheets before she hits the big 4-0. Montreal Swap Club follows Val over the course of one year while trying to keep her marriage afloat after joining a secret sex club that permits her and her husband David to have sex with other married couples.
