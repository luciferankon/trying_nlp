Some time in the summer Pete Connor and Aymz graduate from university only to realise that a recession hit Britain has become a barren wasteland for work. Forced to live of Jobseekers Allowance cheap cider pot noodles and joined by Trent Petes semi-special-trolley-boy-friend they find an abundance of stupid strange awkward and bad situations that will leave you feeling molested... But in a good way.
