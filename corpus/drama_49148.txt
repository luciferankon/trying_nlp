James the supervisor of an agricultural colony is in love with Hélčne who is unfortunately meant for another. At Hélčne's engagement party James has an argument with her fiancé. When the latter gets mysteriously killed James is arrested and condemned to twenty years of hard labor. Hélčne is convinced James is innocent but she falls under the control of a gold digger who has set up a dummy company with the money stolen from James. The swindler is finally arrested and Hélčne and James are reunited.
