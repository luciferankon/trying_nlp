It may be a sign of social evolution or devolution for others but these days men are removing all traces of their primate origins. And we're not just talking shoulders and backs. Men are eliminating hair from their chest arms legs hands and more often down under. "Manscaping" in short has gone mainstream. Long & Short of Body Hair" is a documentary that uncovers the where the how and the why of male body hair along with a growing counter-culture movement taking pride not shame in their furry manes.
