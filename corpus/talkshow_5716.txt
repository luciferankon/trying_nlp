_"Verbotene Liebe" (1995)_ (qv) Star 'Andreas Stenschke' (qv) surprises a 15-year old startled fan as he comes onto the studio stage. The young girl who on the show had been talking with Andreas on the phone because he said he unfortunately couldn't come to the studio because he was too busy working now gets to fulfill her great wish and dream to meet him personally in front of the cameras.
