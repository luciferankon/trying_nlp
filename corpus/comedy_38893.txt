Rory Coveney Jaime Ramsdell Kevin Brunelle Greg Roussin and David Wallace star in this explosive stylish dark comedy described as a movie for couples that just don't have that kind of chemistry. Directors Uke Doiron and Kevin Brunelle launch their first feature film about Jack and his whack job friends who plan and scheme horrible ways to kill his recent ex girlfriend Toni. Jack's friends uncover the ugly truth about Toni's true sexuality a full blown lesbian who had been leading Jack on for over a year. Death is the only closure in this relationship and furious to do so they concoct a fool proof plan to rid Toni of the world while being tracked by the towns most deadliest police officer! The movie is full of off beat characters catchy one liners and twisted events. Will the boys succeed with their plan or face the strong arm of the law?
