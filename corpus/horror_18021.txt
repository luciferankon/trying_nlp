Laurel Hastings is a young news reporter who uncovers the identity of a serial killer that has murdered one hundred people by reading the minds of his victims. Once the killer goes after Laurel she goes into hiding and becomes a private investigator. Her first assignment is a young women name Jordan Bell who communicate with evil spirits while she is sleep walking. During the investigation Laurel finds out that the evil spirits are the killer's past victims and now Laurel and Jordan must team up to fight the serial killer who can read the minds of his victims.
