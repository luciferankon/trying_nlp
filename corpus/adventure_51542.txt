Yaz a Russian internet celebrity whose real name is Viktor Goncharenko travels around the country and looks for unusual even extreme recipes. His mixer is a Kamaz truck his pancakes are 2 meters in diameter he cooks a bathtubful of food at a time. There is no culinary experiment that he has not tried to reproduce. Whatever he cooks he finishes the meal to the last crumb so he always wins in the battle: Yaz vs. Food.
