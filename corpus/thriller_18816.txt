Augury is a police based crime thriller set in London but with the cinematic look feel and tone of Seven Silence of the Lambs and Zodiac. The web series follows detectives Harris and Grant as they attempt to track down a sadistic serial killer that has murdered and mutilated five men over the last two years. The gruesome nature of the crimes have shaken the city and the public are demanding arrests be made. But with the killer promising to strike again in the next 48 hours will the detectives - with the aid of cyber expert Hannah Treadwell - be able to crack the killer's code in time to save the latest victim?
