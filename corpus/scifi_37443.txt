In the near future East Los Angeles is an island a ghetto sealed off from the world with a ten-story concrete wall around it built by a morally and financially broke United States government that built it to contain the escalating Mexican cartel violence in the city. Now one man (Reckless) and his makeshift band of ex-criminal deputies must fight a war in the streets not only to protect the citizens on the "Bad Story" side of the wall but find a way to bring the wall down.
