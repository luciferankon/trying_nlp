Mr. and Mrs. Dr. Frank have it all: three beautiful children a cozy home and Frank's wealthy income as the top local optometrist but even as a skilled eye Dr. there are things Frank cannot see like his wife's drinking problem his secretary's sexual advances towards him his oldest son's drug experimentation and the eerie events throughout the night that terrorize his daughter. Is it a ghost? Is it the scotch? Is it an alien abduction so often mentioned on T.V.? What goes on in the minds of this suburban town's league of physicians while their wives stay at home like house pets?
