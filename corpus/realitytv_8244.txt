Road Trippp follows Casey Veggies Joshton Peas and Anwar Carrots on their journey from hanging out on Fairfax Ave in Los Angeles to running their own fashion and music empire. In the 6th and final episode the Peas & Carrots crew head back home to LA for the last show of their PNCXX Tour. While home Casey Josh and Anwar check in on their Fairfax Ave clothing store and Casey battles nerves as he prepares to give the biggest performance of his life.
