Frank Coleman is a Vietnam veteran dying from cancer brought on by exposure to the defoliant chemical Agent Orange which he turns to Maude DeVictor a Veterans Administration benefits counselor who teams up with Coleman to fight a lopsided batted against the bureaucratic system for its cover up of the possible dangers of Agent Orange. Vietnam veteran Frank Coleman is suffering with cancer after his exposure to the poisonous chemical Agent Orange. To publicize the chemical's dangerous effects Frank enlists the help of Maude DeVictor a Veterans Administration counselor. Despite the cover-up surrounding Agent Orange which threatens to silence Frank and Maude the partners refuse to back down until the government guarantees full compensation for afflicted veterans.
