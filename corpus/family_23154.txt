Kamal and Swati are happily married couple residing in Suburban kolkata for the last six months. They were forced to shift out of Kolkata due to financial crisis. Kamal is a clerk at a private company and commutes to work on a local train. They are a typical middle class couple. Suddenly the rhythm of their life begins to break. In the beginning they were irritated; gradually it developed into a sense of helplessness which culminated into a fear psychosis. Ultimately insecurity crept into Kamal's mind. In the mean time Kamal faces a financial crisis owing a huge loan left over by his late father during the construction of the house. Kamal confides his helplessness to his colleague Pranab Kamal is in dilemma' what will he do ?
