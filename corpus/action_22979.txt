Cole Ledbetter a brilliant but crooked accountant formed the Fellowship to steal half of Weyback council's budget and launder Russian Mafia money before disappearing. Now he's back 'To steal the other half' he suggests to the Night Club singer he beds. But the Fellowship want their revenge and the Russians want their laundry while a Special Branch female undercover officer waits to pick up the pieces. Crooked accountant and council leader conspire with others as 'The Fellowship' to get rich administering the town's regeneration scheme. Over-reaching their ambition they launder Russian Mafia money and fail to return the criminally obtained now laundered fortune.
