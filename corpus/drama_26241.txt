What if Jesus came to earth as a college student? Who would follow Him? who would be healed by Him? Who would crucify Him? A modern day version of a timeless story this film follows Desiree a young journalism major in desperate need of healing as she investigates a student who rumor claims as the Messiah. Named Immanuel this student has reported healed sickness brought happiness and inspired hope in the student body. After spending just a few days with him Desiree begins to realize that sometimes rumors aren't just rumors.
