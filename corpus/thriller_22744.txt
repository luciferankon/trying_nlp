AVUS: (Latin) Grandfather. In the future humankind has discovered how to travel forward in time and receive messages back from those sent forward. Through this process it is discovered the world is going to come to a catastrophic end. Now equipped with newly discovered ability of how to travel back in time one man is sent back in time as a last resort to stop the end at its origin point. Along the way he will discover lies secrets and betrayal and a terrible question: Can you bypass the Grandfather Paradox? Can you change the past?
