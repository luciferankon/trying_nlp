This 'fish out of water' situation comedy tells the story of Woodrow W. Spoon a chef making his way to the city with hopes of having his own cooking show. Woody Is ambitious has an open mind and the physique of a five-foot tall wooden spoon. Woody's adventures start with his first encounters with the humans of the species. In kind he is the first talking spoon they'll ever meet. Woody's character originated in The Lazy Persons Guide to Cooking and the weekly newspaper column 'Cooking With Woody' both by show creator Ronald Kolman.
