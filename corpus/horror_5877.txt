Tom and Vernon stumble into an abandoned building for shelter entering a hellish world of shadows and nightmares that would change their lives forever. Trapped and psychologically tortured by an unseen forces the brothers descend into a vortex of madness dominated and controlled by demon succubi and paranormal entities for one sole purpose...to re-awaken the Anti Christ. Some say evil is skin deep yet the forbidden ancient texts say that true evil is embedded into the soul of the world itself.
