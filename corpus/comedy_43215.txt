FREE 'N CHANGE is the name of a counter-culture second-hand shop founded by Adam in the spirit of Los Indignados. Monetary currency is banned in the Trade 'n Change warehouse and replaced by barter: the exchange of goods or services through a currency called the "Euron" a customized note based on time. One Euron is equivalent to one hour of an activity whatever that activity may be - - Adam (40) - an anti-globalization activist an idealist and an eccentric - makes the acquaintance of Anna (40) an attractive and intrepid political journalist on the rise. Adam still quite immature collects short-term relationships. Anna is investigating a government minister in the eye of the media and is living with her female companion a high-spirited feminist activist. Their friction will make sparks fly.
