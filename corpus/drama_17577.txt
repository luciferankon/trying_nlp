After having an incredibly colorful and pleasant dream Antonina wakes up and realizes that her entire life was a fake. She looses interest in her children and husband a reputable psychiatrist who is still trying to save her. One of his patients Arkadi a talented songwriter tells him the biblical story of Lot whose wife "became a pillar of salt." Apparently some patients at this mental institution are very bright and honest but they are locked up behind bars alluding to situation in Russia today...
