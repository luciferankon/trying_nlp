Ranked among the world's most popular tourist destinations Thailand is a breathtaking paradise not to be missed. Blessed with a diverse variety this ancient mystical land has so much to share with its visitors. Often called a "feast for the senses" Thailand is an amazing mix of sights sounds smells and much more. Join host Christina Cindrich as she discovers the best this Kingdom has to offer on a private guided tour with the luxury travel company Abercrombie & Kent. From exotic markets and 5 star luxury in the buzzing capital of Bangkok the revered temples and incredible wildlife of Chiang Mai to the stunning natural beauty and sexy vibrant nightlife on the world-renowned beaches of Phuket. A country that welcomes everyone with a smile you'll quickly discover why it's one of the most magical destinations on Earth.
