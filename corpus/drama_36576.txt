Lost Angel is a compelling drama that follows Samantha a small town Christian girl and her two friends Nick and Amy three aspiring actors who move to the glamorous world of Hollywood. As they struggle to become successful their lives take dramatically different paths. Sam tastes success early and becomes lost in a world of drugs sex money and fame. While Nick and Amy struggle with their own demons they are forced to watch as Sam loses her faith and turns what was once a burgeoning career into a life of hopeless despair. Lost Angel looks inside the harsh "reality" of life in L.A. and the toll it can take on one's character friendships and core beliefs.
