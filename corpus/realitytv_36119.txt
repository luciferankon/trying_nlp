Travelogue Show Co-Hosts Lenny Rosenberg and Adesa Swan travel across the country interviewing unique and quirky restaurant owners that welcomes us into their kitchen while we create extravagant dishes to help improve the restaurant. Lenny Rosenberg the eccentric restaurateur who flipped over 50 restaurants and Adesa Swan who has worked with Lenny side by side designing restaurants is the top in her field. Co-Hosts Lenny Rosenberg and Adesa Swan in the first segment visit the famous Sweet lady Jane bakery and restaurant on Montana Ave in Santa Monica California and cook up their favorite cake. In the second segment they go into Ziggy Gruber from the "The Deli Man" movie and mix it up in his kitchen "Kenny and Ziggy's" in Houston.
