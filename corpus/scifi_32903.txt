In 2055 at a time when humans have transcended biology a young astronomer sets out on a journey of self discovery. Following the death of her father Ariadne leaves behind her small town life to find the city and take "The Test" - a government service that promises true love by matching young adults with their perfect life partners. Along the way she meets and falls for Benjamin a star-gazer who challenges her core beliefs and ideas. Together they journey through a strange new world and uncover the truths behind the rapidly-evolving concept of the singularity.
