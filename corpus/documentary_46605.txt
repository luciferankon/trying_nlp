At first glance they have everything - families jobs... And yet the descendants of an unique people cannot dismiss the family memory about their slain roots. What are the present human dimensions of a 1915 political act? The film is a momentary picture of the emotional state of the Armenians in present day Bulgaria. Their stories are the balance-sheet of human and family bases. They sound topical and uncompromising in the context of the international events of the last months.
