Eighth annual "peoples choice" awards show from Danish broadcaster TV2 Zulu which awards "The Zulu Warrior" in 14 categories covering Movies Music Television Radio Commercials and Sports. Denmarks largest awards show includes appearances and performances by the hottest and most current Danish celebrities and artists. Nominees are chosen by a select jury of experts in each of the six arts. The winners are announced on the night following a nationwide public online vote starting one month prior to the show.
