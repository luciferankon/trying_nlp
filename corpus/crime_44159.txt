Inspired by a real life British-Italian gangster who redefines Mafia. An unlikely childhood friendship between a shy Michael Vizzini and an adventurous London lad Paul Adams is formed on the harsh streets of 70's London. As the two naive youths look out for one another like brothers they become embroiled in a world of sex drugs and crime. Hot headed Paul craves excitement and riches. Michael quieter more naturally gifted attempts to look out for them both. A criminal enterprise tears their friendship apart and separates them for the next fifteen years. Fifteen years later their two worlds collide over a storm ridden London they must come together once more to battle the same dangerous foe... "fate has two sides to its coin" 'Dark Clouds Of London' tells the epic story of one man's rise and fall as he attempts to redefine Mafia.
