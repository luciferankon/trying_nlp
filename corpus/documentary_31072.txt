The lives of four girls in the complicated Israeli reality. Sarah a Bedouin girl dreams of becoming a doctor. Yarden wants to take care of her animals but a wave of terror urges her to move to the Netherlands. Orthodox Mevasseret promises to build a Zionist religious home -while traumatic disengagement from Gaza is in the background  Noga though very fearful wants to try everything . A touching story of a 17 year follow-up. - do dreams come true?
