'Cardboard Roof' takes a glance at the mundane and humble daily routine of two foreign laborers working as cardboard collectors in the UAE. Conceived by Shahin Yazdani the producer of award-winning short films and documentaries such as 'Rabbit Hole' 'The Old Palm' 'Nations & Tribes and 'Easy on Love 'Cardboard Roof' sheds some light on the lives of its two out-of-place characters and enables the audience to gain insight into their emotional states and habits.
