A film about the first woman in the world of Senegalese wrestling the promoter of the fights and politician Ndeye Ndiaye Tyson and two young Senegalese men: Modou and Thiam. Despite being illiterateTyson has become famous and is now a role model for lots of her compatriots. Currently Tyson travels a lot and is treated with a great recognition and respect. Modou and Thiam want to change their lives through to the wresting. They compete because each of them hopes to get noticed by the famous promoter one day become a master of Senegalese wrestling and win the fight for better life.
