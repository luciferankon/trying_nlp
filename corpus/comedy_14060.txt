A 10 part anarchic spoof cookery/comedy show set in a filthy flat in Cardiff where hapless would-be TV presenter Simon Adams attempts to present a cookery show aided by his bizarre sidekick Joe Ely and hampered by 'cowboy' builder Sean Bowen and an assortment of odd characters. Produced by Aspect Television for BBC Choice. Aired on BBC Choice in the Summer & Autumn of 2000.
