Ever wonder what America is thinking? What better way to find out than to enlist two brash Latino hosts to check the pulse of America one meal at a time. Radio host Neil Saavedra who produces "The Jesus Christ Show" on Los Angeles radio station KFI AM 640 and Idalis De Leon a sassy celeb who got her start as an MTV VJ host Breakfast Lunch and Dinner a show that interrupts good meals for even better conversation. These two passionate Latino hosts serve up hot-button issues like prisoner rights abortion and gay marriage to restaurant patrons at their favorite eateries.
