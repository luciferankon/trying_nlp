Top acting coach Issic Munrow is getting increasingly worried about falling attendance and is striving to save his studio. Nigel who runs the studio's agency seems to have no money worries even though he has not booked any of the students jobs for some time. Alex and Chad students of the studio keep coming up with failing ideas to make themselves famous cultivating in writing directing and staring in their own film called 'A Very Long Journey Home to Mummy'. Issic discovers his nemesis from drama school Franklyn Ross is the reason behind some of his studio's problems as he has opened a modern all thrills acting studio called 'The Actors Playground' Finding this out Issic attempts everything to save his studio including trying to re-launch his stage carrier and calling the much feared American coach Kelly Badsong to take some classes at his studio who subsequently tries to take over the studio herself.
