In the remote and forgotten villages of Eastern India separated from civilization by a vast plain Dai is delivering a baby exactly as her ancestors would hundreds of years ago. She is a midwife and in this region that is to be a representative of an ancient and mysterious art often employing methods unthinkable to us in the West. Our heroines two ancient and delightful midwives are never short of a saucy joke - or a wise word. Humorous and uplifting beautifully shot and poignant an intimate glimpse into a disappearing world.
