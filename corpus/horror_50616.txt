Entertainer Levi Williams has it all except for the girl of his dreams. He finally one day meets his soulmate only to lose her to an untimely murder right after proposing to her. His only wish is to have his beloved Jasmine back in his life & is willing to do whatever it takes to make that happen no matter what the cost. Levi's dream of happiness with the woman he loves soon becomes his worst nightmare as an grave evil rises from an Dark Unrest. He soon learns it is very important to "Be careful what you wish for".
