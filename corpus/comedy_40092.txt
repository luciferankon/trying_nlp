#hotmessmoves is a series of #twebisodes that chronicle two typical 20-something single girls in the city. It was written by Ashley Skidmore and Lyle Friedman who also happen to be the show's main characters. They're totally relatable...in the worst way. This will not be over quickly. Ashley and Lyle started writing #hotmessmoves as a way to process their feelings about life. They dedicate each episode to 20-something hot messes in New York City and all over the world.
