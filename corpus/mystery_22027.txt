Director Sohanlal shot into fame in 2000 with the TV Movie 'Pedakom/The Box' adapted from a short story by Guy de Maupassant. The film was telecast on Asianet as a Thiruvonam day special film on September 10 2000. 'Pedakom/The Box' brought critical acclaim and several awards including the Kerala State Film Critics Association's Award for best debut Director.
