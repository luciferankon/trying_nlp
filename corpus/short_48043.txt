No one wanted to be there that night but everyone there had a reason to stay. That's what all of the strangers felt as they looked around in suspicion. All of them received letters promising that they would be blackmailed if they didn't show up and moments after arriving it looked as if their mysterious host was making good on that promise. In less than an hour this group of eclectic characters will have to find a way to work together to unravel this mystery or else.
