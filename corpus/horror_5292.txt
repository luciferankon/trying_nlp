Dylan Gregory is a new widower and an overprotective single father. A car runs over his daughter at the bus stop after the driver dies at the wheel. Dylan falls into a depressive fit of rage causing trouble for the town he's lived in for more than 20 years. One night while Dylan's daughter fights for her life in a chemical induced coma her boyfriend sneaks a drunk girl into her bedroom. Dylan catches the two having sex and kills them both. A message from a family friend inspires Dylan to bury the bodies of the two in various places on his ever-growing property. Many eyes are on Dylan due to his family crisis. Though we all bury our mistakes somewhere Dylan can't hide a secret this big forever.
