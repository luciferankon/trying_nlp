Bob vs. Society chronicles a few days in the life of a misanthrope named Bob. Bob is a washed-up energy trader who now ekes out a living in New York City by working in a comic book store. Naturally Bob's marriage with his young wife Kate has deteriorated to its final stages. Bob likes to amuse himself by bothering customers strangers and Kate's friends. He also likes to get his drink on. However Bob is about to find out what happens when society fights back.
