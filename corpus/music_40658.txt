The film presents this very special mentality using the Neapolitan music and its famous songs. A musical discovery of the sights and sounds of the city of Naples and its people. Naples city under the volcano city of contradictions: beauty and ugliness joy and desperation are found side by side throughout its history. Everything that makes Naples so fascinating is reflected in its music! The camera does not linger on details of picturesque tourist sights but throws itself literally into the bustling traffic and street markets to meet the Neapolitans going about their daily life and strife!
