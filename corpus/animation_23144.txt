When the KING of BREMEN in 18th century decides he must have the greatest band ever to perform at his daughter's wedding his trusty WIZARD CREGG builds a contraption that can search across space and time for just such a prize. Unfortunately he ends up with DAVE BRISTOL from 2016 the world's worst singer. WIZARD CREGG then makes another attempt and snares aging rock group who were transformed into farm animals due to a glitch in his portal. Now the musicians trapped in the bodies of domestic livestock led by DAVE must figure out how to perform for the PRINCESS' wedding to the evil DUKE HENRI so they can return to their former normal lives. The only hitch? DAVE falls in love with the PRINCESS.
