Deb Cabral The DeClutter Coach helps individuals declutter their lives and houses. Deb also works with organizations from Non-Profits to Police Departments to Businesses and more. Each show follows Deb and her team in a different situation and helps them organize their environment. She has done closets and offices and basements and storage buildings. There have been as of January 20 2017 139 episodes have aired in 17 TV markets spanning 16 states.
