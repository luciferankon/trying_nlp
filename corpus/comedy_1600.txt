Prepare to relive your college years through the eyes of these eager new freshmen: Jeremy the quick-witted smartass Taylor the competitive one-upper Fisher the socially awkward newbie Blake the dumb-but-cocky playboy and Ots the impulsive functional alcoholic. Together they will run the gauntlet of college milestones and antics - from the intolerably unnecessary lectures of Orientation Week to the bittersweet crescendo of graduation day.
