Disconnected focuses on our present day dependency on technology and how it has left us with little reason to think for ourselves. It features a wide array of relevant topics today ranging from the unseen dangers and benefits of devices such as smart phones televisions tablets and more. It will also showcase the significant technical progress we have made in such a short period of time and how it has affected our ability to interact with one another as human beings. Ultimately the film aspires to send a universal message reminding the public that sometimes we have to disconnect in order to reconnect with life.
