A documentary about Edo Maajka a hip hop star from Bosnia and Herzegovina. This documentary follows Edo Maajka from his childhood in Brcko Bosnia to the outskirts of Croatia then to Zagreb then back to Bosnia in Tuzla where he began his career. The documentary crew also follows Edo Maajka on the road to Novi Pazar where he will be holding the first hip hop concert there as well as footage from the concert it self.
