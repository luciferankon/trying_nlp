After hearing of their fathers mysterious death the five privileged Mosier sisters are forced to return home to claim their share of the family inheritance. With an ironclad will Charles Mosier the Patriarch of the family grants his heirs the fortune necessary to protect their financial future. But after losing her identity as her fathers keeper oldest daughter Kathryn seizes the opportunity to unleash decades of pent up hostility and resentment. But Kathryn isn't the only one with something to prove. Each member of the family has a price to pay and as the weekend unfolds they find it can't be paid in cash.
