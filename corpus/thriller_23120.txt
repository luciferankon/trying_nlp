Stained Glass Psyche is a psychological horror film about a husband and wife who have a young daughter that is experiencing deep seeded issues with fear particularly a fear of the house's cellar. Unable to help they take their daughter to a psychologist to try to gain insight as to why she is so frightened of the cellar. The psychologist administers a series of tests and the girl's answers to these tests are the basis of the individual short films. In the end it remains to be seen if anything can help the girl.
