Two companies. One goal - to be the only name in sports-entertainment. This 20-episode WWE Network series delivers the in-depth stories of both Vince McMahon's WWE and Ted Turner's WCW as they battle head to head in a primetime power struggle that forever changed the face of sports entertainment. Part two delves into the shock value of WWE's Attitude Era and how the boundary pushing antics of DX and others raised the bar in the Monday Night War as WWE began to fight back.
