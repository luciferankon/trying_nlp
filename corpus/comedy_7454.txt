Vaibhav and Rasika a happily married couple and well settled in life accept each other's opposite nature in marriage. Vaibhav is fond of children whereas Rasika too concerned with her looks is against getting pregnant. There is a hole in their lives...the joy of being a parent. Vaibhav cannot accept this from her wife and hence their relationship starts suffering. Over a period of time Vaibhav cannot understand the changes occurring with his body to which Vaibhav's friend identifies these changes as symptoms of pregnancy. Can you ever imagine a man becoming pregnant? The laws of nature are in question.
