In this live show Malevolent Creation gives a homicidal performance of U.S. death metal classics in Sydney Australia. The lineup of guitarists Phil Fasciana and Marco Martell drummer Gus Rios bassist Jason Blachowicz and vocalist Brett Hoffman is tight as hell and downright rabid. Filmed on the March 27 2009 one of the group's Doomsday X world tour the performance is the centerpiece of Death from Down Under. This is the first professionally shot DVD for Malevolent Creation. It was filmed in Australia by Hardline Media. A 4 camera shoot with extensive editing and live interviews throught the performance. Excellent Stereo mix makes you one with the show. PLAY IT LOUD!!
