Children's series that encourages kids and their families to become involved with the community doing good deeds for others. In the premiere episode the "K2K" kids (Keanu Tristan Jordan David Sammi Nick and Gracie) help children with special needs at the physical therapy facility Carousel Ranch in Canyon Country CA. With the guidance of adult mentors Jackie and Mandy Ellen the kids clean up the ranch paint and redecorate the club house while having fun and learning about others.
