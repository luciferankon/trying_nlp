Buon Fresco Foundations - Fresco Cartoon DVD introduces the student to the foundational principles and techniques of preparation and use of the CARTOON (preparatory full scale drawing) for True (Buon) Fresco Painting. Topics illustrated include the step-by-step process and principals of creating of the cartoon for fresco filmed in real time without omissions. Student will be able to see exactly how long each step takes and what tools and methods each requires. iLia Anossov (fresco) guides the student by thorough and clear demonstration of the foundations of fresco cartoon relevant to any size fresco as well as classic murals and architectural trompe l'oeils.
