Two brothers in their early 20s one black one white each the other's keeper since their family was torn apart by a decade old tragedy. Neville is a comedian struggling with his comedy and his brother Matthew is a boxer consumed with the pleasure and pain of his skin. Neville becomes enchanted with Niko a beguiling young singer who becomes entangled with him on his journey of self-discovery. With the support of their long-time friend Julian and Sister Sarah a nun with a past Neville and Matthew come to understand love in all its forms.
