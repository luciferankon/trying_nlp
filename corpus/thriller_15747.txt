Jake Darcy a down on his luck Private detective takes on a job looking for a missing girl. Jake stumbles upon a murder scene and rescues a femme fatale. Drawn into a deadly intrigue Jake is pursued by a psychotic killer with a vendetta. In South of Heaven Private Detective Jake Darcy is unwittingly thrown down a path of mystery and deception. When called on by Portland local Isabella to find her missing sister Jake comes up empty-handed. When he pays a visit to the house across the street he discovers a woman naked and tortured and near death. Thinking he's solved his missing person's case Jake sets out to find her true identity and the person responsible for this crime. But he quickly realizes that he isn't the only one interested in this girl and becomes entwined in something even more sinister. Soon he's caught in a web of lies and murder as a host of mysterious players move in to fulfill their own agendas. South of Heaven is classic film noir gritty and dark leaving you guessing until the very end.
