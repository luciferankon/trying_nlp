In the tradition of great classic serial killer films such as "American Psycho" and "Zodiac" the thriller "Innocent Killer" promises mystery excitement and of course the ultimate battle between good and evil. A modern city is hit with a string of continuing inexplicable murders of seemingly random women from different cultural and social-economic backgrounds. The audience journeys with the murderer as he carries out his deadly deeds. We also follow the dedicated police force which will not let go of every possible lead. What happens when the murderer meets the last standing policewoman on his trail will leave audiences in shock! "Innocent Killer" is a tribute in style to the masterpiece thriller "Dressed to Kill" by the master of the genre Brian De Palma.
