Three buxom waitresses for a well-known restaurant chain are nabbed by crook when they open the restaurant in the morning. While the three shapely ladies struggle to free themselves from the cruelly tight ropes and gags the thug proceeds with his criminal enterprise. Two other women have their home invaded by a man who's on the lam. The ladies are roped and ball-gagged to prevent them from calling the police until the intruder leaves their home.
