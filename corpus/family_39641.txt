Like many children Kazem is dreaming of becoming a famous actor. When a production company is looking for a teen actor to play the main role in a film his chance to realize his dream appears imminent. They audition and screen test a lot of teenagers and at long last the director finally shortlists Kazem and Farhad. But then he can't make up his mind. One thing however is sure: only one of them will get the part. Kazem is ready to do whatever it takes but his family is far from pleased with his plans. And when his father has to go to the hospital Kazem's dream of movie stardom seems seriously imperiled.
