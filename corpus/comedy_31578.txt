The story of a man questioning the ethic and morals in society. When a serious condition threatens his life he begins questioning life and expresses his opinions in actions without holding back He realizes that he kept scheduling his happiness and avoided discovering the beauties of life. He finally decides to create a to do list before his list that includes becoming closer to his Mother (Aysegul Atik) telling his Father (Zihni Goktay) how much he means to him and treating his wife (Yasemin Conka) like when they first met telling his high school crush that he loved her and telling his wife the truth of his Birth date. Mufit goes on the long-awaited holiday with his daughter (Merve Hazer) and wife and explains that the formula of joy in life is expressing what is inside of you.
