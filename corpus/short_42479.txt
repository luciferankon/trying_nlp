Three outcasts from the quaint village of Old Sodbury set out on an epic adventure to save the beautiful Amelia (Julia Aks) from the neighboring merciless Moorgouls. Together Aldus (Justin Elias) a squire boy and the laughing stock of Old Sodbury Hazel (Sam Gurnick) an intense weapon wielding lesbian and Beazley (Emilio Rossal) a potions master who hasn't mastered his potions must overcome their shortcomings and rescue their beloved Amelia.
