The end of a relationship can be more devastating for some people than others. Wen He (Wen He) falls on the more-devastated side of the spectrum after his girlfriend breaks up with him. In a post-breakup haze Wen He has a hard time getting over his heartbreak and functioning in his daily life. The only time he feels like he's still living is at 11 p.m. each night when he lights up the lighter that his ex-girlfriend gave him.
