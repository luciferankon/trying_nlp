A documentary portrait of the filmmaker 'Harmony Korine' (qv) during the production of his third feature film _Mister Lonely (2007)_ (qv). Shot on location in Scotland Paris and Panama each chapter presents the production process and the causal day-to-day absurdities of the actor/impersonators featured in Mister Lonely. Throughout the process as Harmony reveals his thoughts on the film he also reveals a part of himself.
