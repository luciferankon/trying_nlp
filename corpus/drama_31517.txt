The Heart warming story of 14 year-old Hayden Clark who is violently bullied in school and on social media. Hayden is bullied because of her brooding looks her struggle with bulimia and her love of the music that made Memphis the City of the Music Greats like Elvis Presley Johnny Cash BB King Roy Orbison or so many more. To make matters worse Hayden does not have much support at home as her mother also struggles with her alcoholism and depression a result of a dark secret she has been hiding from Hayden and herself. With the help of an unlikely ally in an old time blues singer Uncle Joe Turner Hayden fights to reclaim her self worth sense of dignity and purpose and her dream of becoming a singer.
