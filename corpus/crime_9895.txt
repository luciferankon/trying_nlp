The story of two hired hit men who are forced to work on Christmas Day. Anthony a family man who is tired of the life he leads desperately tries to convince his friend and partner Marcus that his life needs to change. Caught between the woman he loves Danelle and the intimidating mob boss Don Silio who dictates his every move Anthony must confront his present in order to save his future. Unfortunate events lead to disaster when power overcomes friendship and loyalty reigns above all.
