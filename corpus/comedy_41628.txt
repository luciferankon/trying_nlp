When they are framed for an act of vandalism in their church Traz and his altar boy pals are plunged head first into a summer full of church-cleaning repent uncertainty... and ULTIMATE FRISBEE! Forced to work as a team with a nerd a fat kid a lonely goth a foreigner and the ever-miffed Jason Traz faces the ULTIMATE challenge of bringing together this rag-tag group of wacky characters to overcome their rivals the evil Yacht Club team. Hilarity and hijinks await in this ULTIMATE sendup to the classic 80's sports movie!
