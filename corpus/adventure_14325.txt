Flushy Fish is an action-adventure VR game that was first released for Gear VR on August 3rd 2016. According to the official website the game is planned to be released for all the popular VR platforms in the near future. In the game you'll take a role of a cute smiling cartoonish fish called Flushy. You need to use the different colored jelly cannons to travel across the beautiful vivid fantasy world landscape solving puzzles and take flushy to his well-deserved freedom. Flushy Fish features 30 unique and challenging levels. In your journey you'll need to collect lost coins and start in order to finally challenge some of the world's legendary dragons. The game was designed to appeal to all ages and made for comfortable gameplay experience.
