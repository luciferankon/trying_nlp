Join Simon Cox author of the international best-selling "Illuminating Angels & Demons" as he and eleven additional noted authorities take you on an in depth journey to uncover a plan for world domination that dates back to the early foundations of Christian history. This investigative documentary based on Dan Brown's book Angels and Demons seeks to uncover any possible truth behind the theories posed in the story. Is the Vatican secretly embroiled in an ancient war with the Illuminati or is the story posed by brown pure fiction?
