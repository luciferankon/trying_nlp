17-year-old Martina loves circus life as well as her father. But she does not want to work like he does at the Perch because she is not dizzy. Christina is much better suited for this. Quite apart from the fact that Martina does not want to be an artist of second class she loves black panthers and her dancer Dittrich. But Dittrich says no irrevocably. How can he know that Martina decides to practice on her own with Satan the youngest and wildest panther?
