A delusional college graduate comes back home to stop the wedding of his high school sweetheart and ends up in a bizarre love triangle between his beautiful but melancholy memories of the past and a clumsy but down to earth schoolteacher who fights for his affections but when he has a one night stand with the schoolteacher and leaves her to go to the wedding where he finds the bride with a case of the jitters he must ultimately decide between the security of his past or the possibilities of his future.
