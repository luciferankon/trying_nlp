The final installment of this four-part series begins with golf's third Great Triumvirate of Arnold Palmer Gary Player and Jack Nicklaus who bring new mastery to a sport that has become a multi-billion dollar world-wide obsession. New names like Lee Trevino and Tom Watson face a rising threat from stars from Europe Japan and Australia. This final chapter leads us from the Golden Bear's 1962 US Open victory to John Daly's improbable triumph in the 1991 PGA Tournament while identifying promising future stars like 11 year-old Tiger Woods.
