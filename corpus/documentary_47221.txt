A documentary based on the original play by Euripides Medea now set in the streets of present day Athens through a breathtaking detective manner. Throughout the investigation we experience conversations with theoreticians directors & actors casting sessions rehearsals but most importantly awe-inspiring interviews with people on the street. The purpose of this adventure is to look for Jason Medea's legendary husband consider who he would be represented by in today's world what went wrong and why he did not accomplish his initial goals.
