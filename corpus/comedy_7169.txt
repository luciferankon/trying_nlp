Faem a upcountry student come to study at a university in Bangkok. Faem has joined Preeda's group which has Eed (a fake fortune teller) as a (love) counselor. After freshmen party Faem fall in-love with a beautiful freshman Ribbin. The relationship is going well until Triruk a popular magazine model become the latest gig. Now it's time to choose the gig or is this is just a game of love?
