A documentary/narrative focused on the connectivity between art and skateboarding. Six distinct artists express ideas of individuality limitation and knowledge bestowed from a constantly adapting culture. The story of two symmetrical beings controlled by a powerful entity parallel the documentary helping guide the narrative. Both characters strive to find purpose in life later to discover mirrors answer their most desired question; What is my love and passion? Five entities all connected. A symbolic juxtaposed experience leaving the viewer reflective of fine detail and purpose.
