Everything in town appears calm placid lovely. But Woyzeck a rifleman assigned as an orderly hears voices -- the times are out of joint at least in his cosmos. To his captain Woyzeck is a comic marvel: ignorant but courageous full of energy to little purpose. To a local doctor Woyzeck is a curiosity the object of cruel study. Woyzeck 40 has a young wife Marie and a small child. He dotes on them but Marie even though she has periods of guilt and remorse carries on affairs and flirtations. When the captain lets drop broad hints of Woyzeck's being a cuckold his inner demons and the voices of the spheres take over. Will madness bring action? Of what sort? Franz Woyzeck is a hapless hopeless soldier alone and powerless in society assaulted from all sides by forces he cannot control. Abused and tortured both physically and psychologically by commanding officers doctors and his unfaithful wife Marie Woyzeck struggles to hold on to his humanity and his fragile sanity.
