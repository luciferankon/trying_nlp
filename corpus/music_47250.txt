Did you hear that roar? That was the sound of a whole lotta rock blasting from the walls of Much HQ courtesy of Audioslave! Chris Cornell Tom Morello Brad Wilk and Tim Commerford hit the floor for an unbelievable set of tunes like "Cochise" and "Doesn't Remind Me". Then these founding members of two of rock's greatest acts - Soundgarden and Rage Against The Machine - sat down with Devon to discuss their past present and future.
