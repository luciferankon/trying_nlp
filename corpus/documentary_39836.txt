The documentary represents a video diary of the film-making process. In 2008 director Merab Kokochashvili made House of Joy - a film dedicated to the XIX century Tbilisi and the first Georgian musical film of Qeto and Kote. In Search of Qeto and Kote describes the process of creating a film. It also contains unique video materials in the shape of interviews with director Vakhtang Tabliashvili and artist Ioseb Sumbatashvili.
