American hard rock group Kiss is a lifestyle a religion and a global enterprise. This is the story of an unlikely success. How to hold on to a dream that you become one of the world's biggest bands - twice. It is also a unique insight into what happened when everything looked down. About those who fell who died and the guitarist who disappeared. Kiss formed in New York City in January 1973 by Paul Stanley Gene Simmons Peter Criss and Ace Frehley. As America's #1 Gold Record Award winning group of all time and that's in every category Kiss can easily be named one of rock's most influential bands. The Rock N Roll Hall of Famers have released 44 albums and sold more than 100 million albums worldwide. Honored by their peers Kiss received the illustrious Ascap Founders Award in 2015.
