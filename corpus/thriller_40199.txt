Struggling to find rent money Kira and Katrina are referred by their friend Joey to Jake Stevens of No-Mutts.com. The girls are invited out to a house at Parris Lake for a live webcam audition. Kira and Katrina arrive and are greeted by someone they believe to be Jake. After some sexual indiscretions and frightening requests the girls begin to realize something is wrong. But it's too late! With scores of viewers glued to their screens they watch as Kira and Katrina are raped and sadistically tortured until their bodies struggle for their final breath. Special Agent Jensen and local Deputy Thomas assess the scene and soon realize this is no ordinary triple homicide and Deputy Hatchet does not fit the typical profile.
