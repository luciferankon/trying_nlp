In the year 2038 a war between the "Economic Military Alliance" and the "Great Empire" has broken out! Choose the side you want to fight for. You have to solve numerous missions like infiltrations reconnaissances and stealth combat. You're going to save hostages and you have to take over different territories by shooting down your enemy. You will use the most powerful military vehicles and aircrafts that the Special Forcec possess like high-tech tanks helicopters jeeps amphibious vehicles and so on. Solve over 25 missions to preserve world peace.
