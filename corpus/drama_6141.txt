In the cut-throat real estate world of Los Angeles Jon a young man in the shadows of his older successful brother uses any means possible to impress his father and prove to him that he is a capable businessman and worthy of his father's love. Faced with hard times in the real estate market Jon must find a creative way to bring business into the company. In doing so he finds himself in bed with the wrong group of people which may ultimately ruin his chance to prove himself to his father and family. Jon Kazden is a real estate agent working in the family business. As the black sheep of the family  his brother Matthew and cousin Danny can seem to do no wrong in the eyes of his father he can do nothing right. In an attempt to prove everyone wrong about his lack of potential and intelligence he takes drastic action to secure a deal he believes will take him from the bottom to the top of the real estate game.
