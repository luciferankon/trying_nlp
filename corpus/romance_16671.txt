Years ago a fight estranged Thaddeus Hartington from his son David. Gravely ill Thaddeus refuses a risky aneurysm surgery until he has tried making up with David. Now his doctor's heiress Kate Spellman sets out to find David. He runs a bookshop without business sense but with a passion for rare editions and a deep mistrusting grudge so she hides her purpose fearing the mission hopeless. The key to his heart is devotion his own doted pre-teen son Jeffrey who takes a liking to Kate first a a riding instructor and soon starts plotting to fix the adults.
