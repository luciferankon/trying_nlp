Honoring gay-themed motion pictures with a special awards presentation ceremony and gala in Hollywood each year the International Gay Film Awards (known as the Glitter Awards) offer a one-of-a-kind glimpse into the heart of the gay entertainment industry. Join host Miles Swain and Vicky Wagner stars Ryan Green of "Queer as Folk" comedian Jason Stuart for a full night of glamour fame shocks and surprises at the 2005 awards ceremony.
