Outlaws took $30000 from the Adams Bank but one of them double crossed everyone and hid the money in old Fort Dodge Nevada. As old Fort Dodge is out of his jurisdiction Rocky takes a vacation there and finds that everything is owned by old Skeeter who knows nothing about the hidden money. But the Pike gang is also in town looking for the money and Rocky wants the money and the head bandit. When settlers come to town he devises a plan to catch all the bad ones and get the money back for the Adams Bank.
