MR. BECHERA (Anil Kapoor) is a shy printer and since the passing of his wife a single father to his infant son--but his quiet life is shattered by the schemes of Dr. Dayanand (the one-and-only Anupam Kher) a medical man with techniques so unorthodox they border on complete lunacy. Dr. Dayanand has a beautiful amnesiac patient named "Asha" (Sridevi) and he wants Bechera to pretend to be her husband and the father of her child. The two couldn't be more mismatched yet as time passes they begin to rely on each other. The lies of the doctor however begin spiraling out of control with both humorous and poignant results. Widower Anand Verma is the father to an infant and also the owner of a printing press. He is approached by the unorthodox Dr. Dayanand who has an assignment for him namely to masquerade as the husband of one of his amnesiac female patient Asha. Anand does agree to carry out this task until Asha regains her memory and both starts to get acquainted with each other and they eventually fall in love. They then decides to get marry but on the day of the wedding the couple are approach by a man named Ajay who is claiming that Asha is his wife Anita - leaving the couple in a dilemma. Known for his unorthodox methods of treatment for his patients Dr. Daya Anand prescribes a "husband" for an amnesiac woman who he has named Asha. The prescribed "husband" is a widower Anand Verma with a son. Anand reluctantly agrees to pose as the husband until such time as Asha's memory returns. Both live together and get used to the same lifestyle. Several months go by and she remains amnesiac and both decide to marry. That's when a young man named Ajay comes into their lives claims that he is the betrothed of Asha whose real name is Anita and now wants to marry her. Will this jolt her memory back? If not will she accept Ajay as her husband?
