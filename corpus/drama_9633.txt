When Brett Cornell learns that the mother of a child he is alleged to have Fathered 5 years prior has died in an accident he heads south to try and take custody of the daughter that he never knew. Brett takes to desperate measures to make it in time to claim the young girl and prove that he will be a suitable Father. The consequences of his actions follow him however putting his life in danger and potentially ruining his chance to save the child from foster care by raising doubts in the mind of his case worker whom he has become romantically involved with.
