In the small suburban hood of Nukaville New Jersey lives the story of young Jerry Rizzo a 15 year old who can't get enough of horror films. One day after a fatal mistake from one of the bullies at school turns Jerry's path into a brutal serial killer. It just so happens they're having a scary movie week of non-stop horror horror horror on the television while Jerry is killing everyone in his way. Who will survive? Will this be his victims last Movie Night?
