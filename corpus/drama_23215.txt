Robert Gordon (Leon Janney) a sheltered 18-year-old youth reared in a Catholic school believes he has a vocation for the priesthood. He is taken to live with his father in his palatial Florida home. There he falls in love with his step-sister Patricia Morrow (Eleanor Hunt) ten years his senior. He runs away and joins the R.A.F. Shot down in battle he is rescued and taken to a monastery where he renounces the world to study for the priesthood. Story starts with Richard (Dick) Gordon at a Catholic boys school. He gets called home to attend the wedding of his father and step-mother. He meets his new step-sister Pat while there. After the wedding he wants to return to the school but instead goes to college. Dick is also a skilled seaman and pilot. He becomes much more "worldly" while at college. When he learns he will never have the love of the woman he falls for he runs away and joins the air force. After getting shot down while recovering he returns to visit Father O'Malley at the school who has always been someone Dick has looked up to.
