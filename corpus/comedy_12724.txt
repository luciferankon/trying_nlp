Trying to avoid a gang conspiring to assassinate him wealthy businessman Stuart Granger ( Herbert Rawlinson ) persuade his unsuspecting cousin Jack Burton ( Herbert Rawlinson ) to impersonate him. Jack survives several attempts on his life gradually realizes what is happening he soon falls in love with Mercia Solano ( Barbara Bedford ) the leader of the gang. Meanwhile Stuart is killed and Mercia is happy when she learn Jack's real identity.
