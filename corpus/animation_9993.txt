The center characters of the film are two kids-a boy and a girl a donkey and a mobile phone. These kids embark on a journey to save their Mother and village during their journey various problems arises most of which get solved with the help of 'speaking' Donkey and magical Mobile. In this film foolish donkey conveys messages-satire to mankind.The story and 3-D effect in the movie makes the film more entertaining which will not only enthrall children but also the adult audience. In the story there are few surprises which will be disclosed at appropriate time. It's not only the Donkey which is surprisingly speaking but their mobile phone that plays an important key role..time to time it displays some messages helping them surmounting the hurdles...In itself it's a suspense that who is sending messages on the mobile phone and how the Donkey got speaking power.
