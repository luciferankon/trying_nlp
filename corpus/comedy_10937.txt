Meet two girls Melaina and Sara. The only thing they share in common is a delinquency on their lease and a flair for never giving up on their dreams! Behind on rent behind on bills and behind on life Melaina answers an ad that promises to be an answer to their prayers: "Female masseuses needed. No experience necessary!" Sara has her doubts but Melaina convinces her that everything will be OK! Things go from bad to worse to way worse after their first client gets too handsy! Now faced with hiding the body or calling the cops Melaina makes the decision for both of them. Nothing will ever be the same again!
