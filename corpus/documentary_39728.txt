The Fraternity of Tiberiade in Belgium is a new catholic monastic family which brings together some forty young brothers and sisters who share a life punctuated by prayer study and manual labor close to nature and animals in the spirit of St. Francis. The history of the Fraternity started in a little wood where its founder brother Marc came to build huts when he was a little boy 50 years ago. Today it is a spiritual oasis must for thousands of young people and families in Belgium. The movie follow the everyday life of the brothers and sisters.
