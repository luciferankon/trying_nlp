In this documentary Mika Tsutsumi a bestseller author explains the nature of Patient Protection and Affordable Care Act (Obamacare). She analyzes various problems on the US liberal health care system and inequality among US citizens in a corporatist society. She is a Japanese author and social activist known for her political analyzes and criticism of corporate globalization. She is best known for Corporate Poor Superstate America a book that went on to become an international bestseller and Sinking Superpower America  a critical analysis of the history of neoliberal economics. Her latest book is Sinking Superpower America an Amazon non-fiction bestseller.
