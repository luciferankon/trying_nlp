A satirical tv show where Kees van Kooten and Wim de Bie portrayed several characters. In several sketches all kinds of social and political issues where addressed. Most popular characters were Jacobse & Van Es and the mayor of the small village Juinen Hans van der Vaart and his assistant Tjolk Hekking. Other characters were Walter de Rochebrune de vieze man and Cor Van Der Laak among others.
