The financially "not doing so well" Royals family becomes millionaires in just a day! The beginning... One upon a...night financially challenged but optimistic and kindhearted Markos Royal rescues desperate megalokyria Eugenia Paleologos from suicide. He invites her over to cool her down where she meets his family the "too original" Royals. Markos is a widower with four children around their teens a very special sister and above all Yuli. Yuli is Markos's sister in law and the typical over concerning Greek mother taking too much care of them all. Especially Markos since she does have an untold crash over him... Eugenia being grateful to Markos rewards him by giving him her former job as Chairman of her international cosmetics company! The Royals effect... What happens when business ignorant Markos Royal suddenly becomes chairman of a powerful group? How does Emilios Kanellopoulos the "dark" vice president who waited years to become Caliph in place of the caliph respond to Marko's threat? How would their lives be affected? Which women would fall deeply jealous and which would prove to be simply irresistible? Which teens will fall in love with whom? Can you resist the Royals? Just meet them!
