Actor and Daily Show Correspondent Aasif Mandvi is taken on a mouthwatering tour of New York City's Indian restaurants and related businesses by culinary legend Madhur Jaffrey ranging from SoHo to Murray (Curry) Hill to Jackson Heights Queens. At the end of the tour Madhur Jaffrey introduces Aasif Mandvi to Chef Akhtar Nawab who shares his kitchen secrets and helps Aasif prepare an Indian feast for his family and friends.
