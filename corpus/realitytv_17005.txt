Traces the origins of the U.S. Army All-American Bowl from its humble beginnings in Dallas Texas 11 years ago to its current stature as the premiere high school football all-star event. Includes an examination of the Army's role in football over the decades as well as just what makes high school football such a central part of the American landscape. The special also features in-depth coverage of this year's crop of All-Americans including a preview of the 2012 U.S. Army All-American Bowl.
