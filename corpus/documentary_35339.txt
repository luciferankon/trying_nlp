Since bursting out of Seattle with 1991's multi-platinum selling Ten Pearl Jam have become one of the finest rock bands in the world today. Eddie Vedder Co. set about reconstructing their sound while campaigning against the more unjust aspects of the music industry. After 20 years Pearl Jam refuse to rest on their laurels constantly exploring new musical directions while remaining as fiercely loyal to their fans as ever. Interviews with the band contributions from their closest allies and from the finest rock writers rare and classic performance footage and many other features make this the finest documentary in existence on this most iconic of rock acts.
