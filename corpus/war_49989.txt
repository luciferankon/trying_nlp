Uka is an old Albanian who lives in the mountains on the border of Kosovo and Albania. As an honorable man he must deal with his son who befriended Italian fascists during WW2. The film depicts events in Kosovo during WW2 where the son of the great wolf from Prokletije Dzahid is being misled and deceived the Italians indicating his partisans killed brothers .. moves to bleed retaliation that will eventually cost him his head.
