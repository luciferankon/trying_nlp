The intimate story of Susan Keeney a cancer survivor who embarked on a cross country road trip to meet various women of different ages and backgrounds who were also cancer survivors. Susan the founder of Cancervive a non-profit organization providing support and guidance for people who have beaten cancer was driven by her pressing desire to find out what if anything these women had in common that helped them survive. Her journey lead to many discoveries both personal and universal that helped her find meaning and purpose through the cancer experience.
