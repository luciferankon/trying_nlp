What does it mean to be born a criminal? To be called that all your life? What does it do to your psyche? Your outlook on the world? The paths you choose in life? Can you choose at all? And if you turn these questions around they are no less intricate. What does such labeling do to us? How does it drive behavior policies and attitudes? Do such tribes serve as scapegoats allowing us to overlook the damage caused by far more serious crime in our midst? If so just what kind of society are we building for ourselves?
