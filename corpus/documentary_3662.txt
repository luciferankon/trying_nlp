With his professional and personal life in turmoil Jimmy's life takes an unexpected turn as the transformative teachings of an antique book help him realize how his thoughts are in fact shaping his world. Insightful documentary interviews with renowned authors speakers and ministers are used throughout the film to highlight the powerful message of the book - that our thoughts are the creative force in our lives.
