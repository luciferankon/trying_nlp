Follow host Lord Walhorn Foechuckle the Shadowdancer and his apprentice Leviathan on an intellectual conquest to interview the finest academic prodigies of the modern day wizard world (or so he thinks!). Each episode contains one very special guest to accompany the renowned Shadow Dancer himself for a one on one interview filled with whimsical anecdotes and LOL's (lots of laughs). Featuring historical discussions followed by a profound investigative look at the newest techniques used in modern day wizard-magic.
