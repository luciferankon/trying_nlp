Casino Ester and Alberto have been friends since childhood. Alberto and Casiano are both in love with Ester. Alberto sends Casiano on a dangerous quest to deliver diamonds to a man on the carrabean. He plans to get rid of him in order to keep Ester. His plan works Alberto convinced Ester to marry him And accepts her even though she is pregnant with Casiano's child. After years of being her support system Ester finally agrees to marry Alberto. Years later Casiano returns to claim back the love of his life and all that belongs to him.
