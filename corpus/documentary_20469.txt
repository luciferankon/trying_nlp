Monterey Shore Diving is video all about SCUBA diving along the beautiful coat of Monterey California. This program features: fourteen of Monterey's most popular shore diving sites four well attended annual diving events a look at the Monterey Bay Marine Sanctuary diver support services boat diving non-diving activities and much much more. Segments are hosted by local diving experts well experienced in the waters off of Monterey; with aerial land map diagram and underwater perspectives. If you are and experienced diver thinking about becoming a diver or if you're an ocean lover desiring to get a feel for Monterey's unique marine biodiversity then you should look for the very informative and beautiful program: Monterey Shore Diving.
