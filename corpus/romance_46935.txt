A modern day Romeo and Juliet adventure about a young woman Sirena with dreams of becoming an Olympic swimmer who experiences her first love with a boy from the 'wrong' side of the tracks. Her father and his insidious girlfriend Minerva do everything in their power to keep the young lovers apart... Based partially on the Little Mermaid a rich fifteen year old girl with a flair for swimming rebels when her father is about to marry a woman that can only be described as "the evil sea witch". In order to see a new boyfriend that no one approves of she makes a deal with her future step-mother. As all her problems begin to drown her she must make choices about her dreams and family.
