Is it possible to experience total alienation in a country full of people who look like you? Lee Young Ho (Jeong Eun Woo) is a youg man who is from a high-class family in North Korea but he crosses over to South Korea to complete a "mission." When he gets into a car accident he finds that he is stuck living in this new country. Can he navigate his new life of alienation in the South? "Stranger" is a 2013 South Korean drama.
