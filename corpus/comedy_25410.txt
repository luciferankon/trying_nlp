Cross-cultural comedy series inspired by the fish-out-of-water mishaps of Japanese-American filmmaker Atsushi Ogata. With a cowboy hat Japanese "yukata" (casual summer garment) and rapid-fire tongue Atsushi stars as Yukata Cowboy drifting across the U.S. Japan and Europe. Everywhere he goes he tries to fit in but the more he fits in the more he is mistaken for someone else. Amidst all his accommodations to perplexing newness he struggles to find his own voice.
