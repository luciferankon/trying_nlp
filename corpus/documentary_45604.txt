In Sweden the popularity of buying and selling things at flea markets is constantly rising Who are they? Why do they buy things that they don't need? What are they selling and why? Many find flea markets as an easy way to earn some money. Instead of throwing away stuff that can be used clothes electronics furniture toys books they try to sell them. Some of the things that we get tired of can be reused and resold it's a win-win situation. The ecological argument is also important. Instead of buying and producing even more stuff we can reuse many things that are in good working order thus saving the energy and material it takes to produce a new item...
