Episode 1 'Mumbai Dreams' : A city that lives life on a fast track a city that sells dreams a city that gives a roller coaster life to millions a city where people either come to 'live' or live to go to... Standing at the threshold of this city Saloni decides to embrace an all-new life. Pulled and beaten down by her very own better half she picks up whatever has remained of her holds her daughter Anjali's hand and moves to this city that may seem a labyrinth from outside but is a dreamy trail for all those who dare to live their dreams!
