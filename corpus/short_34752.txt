A heartfelt story of 3 separate family struggles. Noah moose's parents are having a difficult time working on their marriage and raising their 4 years old son with Autism. Frank and her young single-mother Lucy are taking on the world alone since Franki was diagnosed with Leukaemia; and after loosing their Mother and moving in with their estranged Father Ben and Jacob haven't had an easy growing up. The boys are looking their worst nightmare in the face. But how are they all connected?
