While taking out the garbage Ted is abducted bound and gagged and thrown into the trunk of Bobby's car. Once driven to a remote location Ted is forced to dig his own grave while his former lifelong friend stands watch. Using various techniques Ted tries to remind Bobby of what they used to be and how it doesn't have to end like this. Blinded by jealousy and feeling betrayed by his friend Bobby's mind is made up he has his orders and plans on following through with them. Unfortunately Bobby doesn't follow his orders to a T and ends up putting himself in danger.
