Radio star Dave Rickards receives an offer to jump from KGB-FM San Diego to satellite radio for 5 years $500 million - on one condition. That he ditch his long-time partner Chainsaw. Dave learns from Bob Costas and Bob Saget that there's a reason "Seriously..Satellite Radio" does NOT want Chainsaw and it has to do with a broken romance from the past. As Dave ponders his decision me must contend with his personal loyalty to Chainsaw versus unspeakable riches.
