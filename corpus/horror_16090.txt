The sun rose on the campsite shedding light on the hellish carnage. A young woman's lifeless body was slumped against a tree slashed open. Her husband lies near by in a mutilated heap. The fire flickers revealing the charred remains of their unborn child. Who would commit such an atrocity? The police are clueless but someone knows the truth. Fueled by a savage lust for revenge and a brutal appetite for death they set out to find the killer in a blood drenched quest for retribution.
