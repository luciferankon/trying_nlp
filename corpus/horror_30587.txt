Farah and Rika are sisters who inherited their late mothers house after her sudden death. With no where else to go they are forced to move into their late mothers place. Rika on the other hand objects to the move however Farah ignores her sisters plea. Having heard countless rumors about the house being haunted and called "rumah kentang" (trans: potato house) Farah tries her best to sell it but to no avail. Rika however feeling uneasy since the move was suddenly attacked when she was home alone. Farah regrets not listening to her sister and decides to listen to stories about the houses past. What hidden agenda lies within the walls of their late mothers house? Farah soon unravels why the house is called what is it; Rumah Kentang.
