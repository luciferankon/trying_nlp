Stambeno pitanje (The Housing Issue) is documentary TV series created by Croatian well known author tv creator and journalist Silvana Mendjusic. TV series is produced by Studio dim for Croatian Radiotelevision. Through eight episodes the author follows how politics wars revolution transition bureaucracy and administration affected the fate of the old and the new owners of apartments and houses. Shortly it is a story about our homes. In court the land registers it is only cadastral plot but for us each of these episodes is a little history of our country. This is a series about "little people" and large systems that have conspired against them.
