Kris and Silvia meet by chance in a small town. She is a daughter of a businessman he is an average boy who lives in the countryside. They fall in love. Instigated by the strong feelings Kris decides to move to Silvia's home town the capital of the country Sofia. Infelicitous accident provoked by the dark past of Silvia's father puts a strain on their relationship. This moment will change Kris and Silvia's life forever.
