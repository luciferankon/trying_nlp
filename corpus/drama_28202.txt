CASES is a drama series following the lives of five students each from various walks of life as they embark on a three year journey through law school. Unexpectedly friendships quickly form love interests kindle and the personalities of the group begin to shed their cocoons. Along the way we learn of their societal statuses idiosyncrasies financial woes and ultimately their hunger for success. As each week passes and the students learn something new opportunities begin to unfold. Each week we follow the group as they toe the line of unlawful practice and simple goodwill. As respective wealth accumulate for each of the members suspicion engrosses the group. Animosity builds from within and trouble is right around the corner waiting to catch a member slipping. The rise to the top is always a slow process but the downfall is swift. With trouble brewing will the group rise to the occasion or divide and fall to their respective demises?
