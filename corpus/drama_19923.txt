Among his friends Sasho is famous for "killing a wolf". Milka a good-hearted girl falls in love with the heroic boy. They get married and have a baby girl. In order for Sasho to continue his architecture studies Milka starts to work in a factory. Sasho gives up his studies and he gets involved with a gang of swindlers. He is ready to make his "big hit". After the failure the militia catches him. Te beautiful story is at its end.
