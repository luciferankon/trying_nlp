Hrvatski Top Model was licensed Reality show that was aired on Croatian RTL Television for two separate seasons. Show is based on internationally acclaimed "Next Top Model" series. Host of the first season was famous TV star Tatjana Juric while the second season was hosted by successful Croatian model Vanja Rupena. In each season certain number of young female candidates would enter the show and compete throughout season participating in various challenges based on modeling and fashion industry. Usually every episode would end with elimination catwalk where one or more girls was kicked out of the show. The final episode was aired live where the jury would decide whom from the finalists is the winner of the season. This girl would win lucrative contract with one of the best model agencies and chance to make career as model.
