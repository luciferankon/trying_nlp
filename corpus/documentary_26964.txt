HIV-Negative Gay Men in the AIDS Epidemic is a documentary that examines how the impact of AIDS affects HIV-negative gay men's ability to sustain HIV risk reduction practices and stay negative. Only Human features outspoken HIV-negative men of diverse ages cultural backgrounds and sexual experiences who candidly share their stories of desire loss and survival. Woven through these interviews is a dramatic narrative of one gay Latino youth's struggle to deal responsibly with his health as he explores his sexuality and first relationships.
