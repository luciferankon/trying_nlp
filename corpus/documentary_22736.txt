The series takes its audience around the globe as cutthroat crime cartels with tentacles that reach impoverished countries stop at nothing to keep their multi-billion dollar sex-slave trade alive. As terrorists and criminals operate across international frontiers a global operation is under way; the collaboration between countries in the field of law enforcement and non governmental agencies has expanded to tackle the threats posed by these networks. DS Special Agent Samantha Morgan takes the lead in organizing the Global Human Trafficking Task Force in LA. Not only does she begins to unravel a world very few people talk about she comes face to face with a daughter she has not seen in 24 years.
