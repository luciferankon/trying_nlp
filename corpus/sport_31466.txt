Everyone can Arm Wrestle. New Season new Champions. The WAL - Based in Chicago the World Armwrestling League is the largest and fastest growing professional arm-wrestling league in the world featuring intense matches a high-energy and up-close fan experience and the sport's top superstars. We are an inclusive league dedicated to introducing the sport to new arm-wrestlers and encouraging participation at every level and we believe everyone has the ability to develop the strength technique and strategy it takes to compete in "The Pit." as everyday men compete for the title of WAL Champion.
