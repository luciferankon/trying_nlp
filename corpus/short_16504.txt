When a woman takes a walk ordinary signs in her environment begin to make her doubt the decision she's made to abandon her children. The third film from the Backyard Movies collective is also the directorial debut of Mosley film regular Gideon Seaman. Mosley and Seaman collaborated on the story using all non-actors and shot this cinema verite style over the course of a cloudy day in Arlington Texas. Starring real-life husband and wife Janice and Danny Bishop.
