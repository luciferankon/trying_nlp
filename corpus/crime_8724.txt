No one in the party knew him . Was he a gate crasher what was his motive? And biggest question is how he ended up dead. In a party full of people a gate crasher was murdered. No one saw who did it . Are they all lying to cover up something big? DSP Nirmal Pandey finds himself in the middle of a brewing conspiracy. He now not only needs to find out who did it but also how it was done. Exploring the labyrinth of complicated urban relationships Supari tells a story of long lost love lost friendshipsbetrayal and heart warming loyalty .
