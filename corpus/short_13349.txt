Live action immersive full dome show in 5kx5k resolution about the North and its inhabitants. Experience a real POV husky ride through the snowy forests of Lapland visit the reindeer herds living in the tundra and get to know how they survive in the extreme cold where no trees can grow. The expedition to the North is experienced through the eyes of a little boy guided by his grandfather on search for an arctic fox that appears only at nights.
