A mysterious murder in the small sleepy town of Evergreen Tennessee sets in a motion a chain of events that will force two unlikely allies to work together. The reason? To solve a mystery that has been brewing in the town for many generations...and one that could continue to plague its citizens for years to come. Join detective Cal Hayes (Austin Olive) and private investigator Nicole James (Tanesha Franklin-White) as they search for the answer to the series' most pressing question: Who is THE STRANGER?
