Nick Blackheart has escaped being tortured for over 100 year's by his evil brother Zarek. Time has passed and its 2012. Zarek sends the vampire Cezar along with 2 henchman to capture him again and end the ancient family feud once and for all. The decision Nick Blackheart made over 100 years ago has cost him his wife son and his freedom. As he escapes the torture his brother Zarek has inflicted upon him Nick knows the only way to reclaim his life is to find the Silver Clutch.
