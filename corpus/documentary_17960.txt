The NGO "Sports Around the World"'s program covers several African countries: Cameroon Swaziland Tanzania and Rwanda. Since the association was founded they've built multiple basketball courts and launched a number of sports programs in villages in Cameroon in the Arusha region of Tanzania in the village of Mahamba in Swaziland and now in Rwanda.
