The story of Tam's World centers around a college-aged daughter Tam and the strained relationship with her father Ray. Undercover government agents are also looking for the stolen computer chip but a gang of hackers led by Grekov have discovered it's hiding location first. Grekov and her gang kidnap everyone in the museum in an effort to find the chip. Tam and Ray happen on this event and must use their wits to try to capture the hacker gang and free the hostages.
