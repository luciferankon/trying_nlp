A young bride is murdered on her honeymoon and now she is back from the dead to exact bloody revenge on a world that killed her perfect life. The plot centres around the band ASH as they tour America and as band members start to drop and the celebrity cameos rise Agent Bones (Chris Martin) and Agent Ford (Jonny Buckland) are called in to find the supernatural serial killer before she strikes again. Featuring cameos (and deaths) from a multitude of musicians and celebrities along the way.
