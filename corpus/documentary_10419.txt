The music of Wolfgang Amadeus Mozart has amazed and entertained people for over 250 years...but who was the man behind the music? What inspired him to compose the musical masterpieces that immortalized him as one of the greatest composers the world has ever known? Mozart Decoded takes an in-depth look at Mozart's tumultuous and often difficult life from his beginnings as a musical prodigy through his rise to stardom and membership with the secretive Freemasons and ultimately to his mysterious and untimely death or murder at the age of 35.
