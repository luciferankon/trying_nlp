Lilith follows the story of young woman whom after the brutal and tragic death of her family is left with an uncaring foster family and surrounded by bullies and tough life dilemmas. After a incident at her school causing her tormentors to retaliate they summoned the wrath of an ancient creature more powerful and more terrifying than any of them could imagine. What was once an innocent girl is now a terrible force of evil that seeks to destroy everything and leave nothing and no one alive in it's wake. Can no one stop this unrelenting monster or is this small town and it's residents forced to have to defend themselves against an evil so terrifying so vicious and so blood thirsty that even Death itself shakes in it's presence?
