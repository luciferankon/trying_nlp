Why do we only appreciate something when we're suddenly in danger of losing it? We've all said "I love you" to someone who's dying or already gone when it was already too late. Our eyes are opened to what is truly important in our lives only when it's gone. Eddie McWaters promises to never let that happen again. He vows to live life to the fullest to pursue all of his unaccomplished dreams and try his best to convince others to not wait until it's too late to appreciate all that they have either.
