A voyage through the imaginings of Canadian artist Edmund Alleyn Who was the man I called father? A painter an artist a secretive soul. After ten years of filmmaking in both documentary film and fiction Jennifer Alleyn takes up the challenge any artist must face: seeking one's origins. Inheriting her father's studio she finds herself drawn to the intimate space still exuding the artist's imagination. She decides to bring the camera inside. In a point-of-view documentary the same child that spent hours observing the artist at work now challenges her secretive father and embarks in turn on a quest for answers.
