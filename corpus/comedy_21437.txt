Fourteen-year-old Nikki Maxwell just moved to Westchester New York and is dealing with the social pressure of a hundred other super rich kids who all have lives waaaay cooler than her's at her new school Westchester Country Day. Meanwhile she has no friends a crush on a really hot guy and other problems along the way. Including a spoiled bratty sister. Will Nikki survive? Series written by Rachel Renee Russell this book (and movie) is sure to thrill the lives of teens and tweens.
