In this tale of beach exploits in an industrial seaside town the sights and sounds of music and people dancing beach side emanate as Crab Shack Sammie unofficial king of the beach and all around "rad" guy proclaims it another perfect beach day. Crab Shack Sammie gets one of his employees to wear a new hot dog costume to help promote his hot dog stand. He believes that the hot dog costume is a good luck charm for the beach. As Caspar arrives late to the beach party because of his uncontrollable bad luck he discovers he has lost his one true love Ruby to his nemesis Little Big Mike. An unknown thief steals the hot dog suit and bad luck descends upon the beach. Caspar realizes he must act quickly to get his girl friend back and find the hot dog suit so good luck will return to the beach.
