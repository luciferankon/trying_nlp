The top twelve names in the black entertainment industry go head-to-head in a no limit $50000.00 prize pool Texas Hold 'Em Poker tournament where proceeds benefit the charity of their choice. Hailing from the movie TV music and sports industries journey with these movers and shakers as they show the audience what it really mans to "act a fool" but keep a chill poker face in the weekend-long face off. The Prize money of the final tables goes to the winner's charity of choice. Unlike other poker tournaments there is no poker bracelet for the winner but the sponsors (A luxurious watch seller and a poker jewelry store) added some "bling" of their own.
