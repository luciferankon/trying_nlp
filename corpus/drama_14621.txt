Welcome to Fresno is a film about the journey of a Male Nurse called Ron and his life in Fresno. Through Ron's journey we look at the human condition faith life love and hope. Set in the 90's with an electric musical soundtrack - the film is a 'love letter' to the era. As a male nurse Ron is taking advantage of all the women around him and competing for them with the doctors. Ron is diagnosed with a fatal cancer and begins to change and changes the lives of the women around him. Ron leaves a legacy of compassion and love.
