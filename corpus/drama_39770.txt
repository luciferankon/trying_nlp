The merciless struggle between the two great masters of ElSalakhana (Cattle Slaughtery and Butchery); Master Negm (Adel Adham) and Master Abu Halaqa (Mohamed Reda) to hold control on ElSalakhana in Godfather's style meanwhile a love story starts between Alaa (Samir Sabry) the younger son of Abu Halaqa and Safaa (Laila Hamada) the only daughter of Negm which could hold the only hope of ending this bloody war for good.
