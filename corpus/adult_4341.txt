After learning her boyfriend is on the run from the law the shapely brunette decides to have nothing to do with him - but her 'ex' won't take no for an answer even if he has to chloroform bind and gag the woman to keep her. In the second story a blonde is thoroughly cowed when a burglar breaks into her house and steals the contents of the sage - but worse is still to come. Bound to a chair and ball-gagged she could only wait in silence as human bait for her boyfriend who is in serious trouble with the mob.
