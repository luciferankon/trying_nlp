Through an experimental session of attempted mass hypnosis a unique triptych comprised of three autonomous yet inextricable acts unfold in front our very eyes with the use of an unobtrusive and subconscious video-transmission. Life-long dreams celluloid pleasures irrepressible aspirations Ingmar Bergman and three babies later are all intertwined with the camera's reality. Concentrate hard enough and you may lose your subconscious.
