The story is set in the summer of 1985 in a small river town in the Central Valley of Northern California. Jimmy spends his time alone fixing bikes in his backyard shed until a new kid Sammy comes to town and draws Jimmy out of his shell. One day Jimmy sees the town bully Steve mistreat the girl next door Madeline whom Jimmy has admired for years. Jimmy is encouraged by his new friend to put on his deceased father's cherished clothing and to wear it like a uniform into battle with the bullies creating a new sense of confidence and a new standing in his community.
