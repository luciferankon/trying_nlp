Justin and Lola rekindle their relationship one night camping in the woods after a 6 month hiatus. They look good together but have never officially been. On the brink of a new beginning their car cell phone and wallets are stolen. After a day of searching and no sign of civilization in sight they find help in a sole hiker Chad. They learn of a devastating earthquake that hit the coast of California blocking all communication and connections to their families back home. With nowhere to go and none to call they must confide in Chad who turns out to be a thrill seeking psychopath. Lola and Justin must play along with Chad's games to stay alive in hopes of returning home to find their families and start what could be the beginning of a life long relationship.
