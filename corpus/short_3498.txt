A sobering look back at Tallahassee's most infamous hospital: Sunland. A place where patients checked in but rarely checked out. It was once a treatment center for those afflicted by tuberculosis but then was closed and reopened as a center for the mentally disabled. What appeared on the surface to be as happy a place as its name suggested Sunland quickly evolved into a hospital of horrors. Following a series of funding problems and law suits the hospital was shut down but the horrors carried on into the haunted corners of the abandoned building. Welcome to Sunland explores this history and offers an insider's look into the true atrocities of Tallahassee's most notorious sanitarium for the bizarre and paranormal.
