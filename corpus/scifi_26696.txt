The Alien is a web-series about an alien being who accidentally lands in a local lake while on a mysterious mission to save the human race and our planet from disaster. A scientific experiment the ITER project soon to be conducted will destroy the Earth and everyone on it . This creature can take the form of anyone he chooses and has powers of concentration far beyond a human. He has studied our culture for many years and believes the human race deserves to be saved. He interacts with some members of the community who are convinced they should help him achieve his mission although some are suspicious of his real intentions.
