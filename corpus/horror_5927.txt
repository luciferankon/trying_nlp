When Etain wakes up on a Scottish island with a group friends all who have short term memory-loss she cannot shake the feeling that something far worse is afoot. Her suspicions are realized the more the group search the small deserted island. The few houses are empty and the clocks have stopped but they are not alone - something is intent on hunting them down. As their memory begins to come back they realize that the island is the least of their worries and that together they may hold the key to the darkest of all secrets. There are some places in the world man was never meant to go.
