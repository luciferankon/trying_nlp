Mahesh  a stage Artiste receives a telegram of his father's last hours on the stage but he refuses to leave the play half way and goes home after the show is over. His father takes a promise from his son that after his death he would try for his sister's marriage in a big reputed family. His friend Jago finds a prince named Sunil son of Sardar Gajraj Singh Ghansu. He also finds out that Sunil is a bachelor. In order to find appropriate attire for the proposal he steals a king's costume from a theatre. Thus starts a roller coaster ride of drama.
