A coming of age character driven drama about an adopted teenage girl's troubled family relationships. While on a family day out Violet meets her estranged birth mother. This fleeting but shattering moment permeates the lives of the girl her birth mother and her adoptive family. Painful memories resurface relationships become strained and lives spiral dangerously out of control until the two mothers try to return stability to the life of their daughter. A coming of age drama about the interwoven and unravelling lives of teenage daughter Violet her adoptive Mum and her birth Mum. When Violet and her adoptive mum visit a small Scottish town one summer they unexpectedly run into Violet's estranged birth-mum. The mothers clash as they try to keep a connection with their rebellious daughter who struggles with the conflict of having both mums back in her life.
