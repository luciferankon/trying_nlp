Refugee crisis 2015. A young man wanted to make himself useful and started a wave of empathy. He mobilized lots of people for his project and founded an association. During one single month they collected and sorted eight tons of material donations collected financial donations for the transportation and also for the nutrition of refugees and finally transported these donations to the Croatian-Hungarian border where thousands of hungry freezing and traumatized people were crossing an illegal border crossing point although officially. This film reports this project from the idea to its implementation and grapples with the question of solidarity and social awareness.
