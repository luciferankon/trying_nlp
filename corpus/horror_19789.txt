In one day everything has changed. After a nightmarish sleep John (Shane Nelson) arrives late to work again and loses his job. Attempting to keep this a secret from his girlfriend Laurie-who wants only the best for him-John tries to find comfort in friends and in solitude but is unable due to an omnipresent and alluring masked man following him. Twisting reality and nightmares together the masked man pushes John to the edge in this dreamlike thriller which leaves audiences wondering what is The Red Dream?
