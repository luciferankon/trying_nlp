Pavan's mother Vaishali has been murdered by Mishti. Pavan used to stay with his mother Vaishali and daughter Gudiya. He hires Mishti as a servant. Vaishali becomes suspicious about Mishti. Someone mixes sleeping pills in Mishti's tea and she faints. Pavan admits her in the hospital. The officials from the hospital inform Pavan that Mishti is missing. The next day she returns home. Mishti murders Vaishali and this gets recorded in Gudiya's mobile phone. The police start the investigation and find out that Mishti's twin sister killed Vaishali for money.
