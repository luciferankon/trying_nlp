One of the most important works of 20th century Swiss architecture: Freudenberg cantonal school. Jacques Schader (born 1917) has created an internationally acclaimed masterpiece of enormous visual power forging a meaningful link between an educational postualate and an architectural formulation. openness transparency scope. A detailed tour on film (DVD) provides a clear account of the building's complex quality and includes conversations with the architect and experts. The accompanying book contains the planning documents and links the media in a new way.
