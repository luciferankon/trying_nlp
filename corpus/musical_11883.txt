Music 36 is an old Pub in 1923 in Toronto which is struggling through the prohibition years. Follow a group of people and the pub as they travel through the years together. The cast ages as the pub and the city of Toronto changes with the times travelling through the war years the 50's 60's and70's right up to modern times. Regulars drift away some pass on some move away while others just lose interest. They all come back for the party because they all know that Music 36 always ends with a bang! Entwined in this original story are 23 musical numbers from 19 musicals performed at OLT since 1994.
