Voukoum is a creole word from the French West Indies islands meaning a kind of mess and riot. Voukoum is also the name of a cultural movement from the Guadeloupe islands based in a popular district in the city of Basse Terre. Voukoum is a noisy and massive movement an original way for people Ť from the street ť to express themselves and claim their identity. The film is a try about civic and cultural resistance body and soul expression through music dance and historical memories.
