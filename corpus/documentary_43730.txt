An introduction to Mokichi Okada's nature farming system which is a Japanese organic style of farming which developed in the 1930s. Food and agriculture directly impact our health. Discover the science of "Living Soil" which produces healthy crops on a farm managed by Kazuo Suka a "natural farmer" who succeeded in building "living soil". This is a priceless insight to the possibility of natural farming in contrast to modern agricultural systems based on industrial systems of conventional farming. It also reveals problems of modern agriculture and lessons learned from them.
