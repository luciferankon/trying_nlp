Germany shortly after its reunification: Western capitalism seeks its profit in former East Germany the one way or another. There is Ada Fenske on the island of Ruegen. She seems to be just an lovely old grandma. Suddenly everybody seems to be interested in buying her little house. There is M.A.D. agent Zwirner who has official order to buy it but it is not as easy as he thought. Ada Fenske quickly realizes the odds and ends of the new system.
