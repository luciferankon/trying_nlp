Two teams of children play against each other in 3 games to gain points. In each episode there are 3 other games The points destine the teams' starting positions at the pyramid the pyramid has got 10 notches in which three players of each team have to put a stick on which they hike up. The points are the starting position for the teams' first player e.g. if team blue has gained 5 and team red has gained 3 points team blue's first player starts at 5 team red's first player starts at 3. There are little prices for the winners. The whole game is watched by the referee Robertus Maximus.
