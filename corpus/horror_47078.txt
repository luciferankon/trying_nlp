Psychology student Erin (Christiane Imdahl) has had enough with her studies. Frustrated she and her friends Julie (Pia de Buhr) and Danny (Christian Stock) decide to spend a relaxing weekend in the woods far from their everyday lives. Gathering up several more friends the group heads into the forest - but their carefree weekend quickly goes downhill. When an unexpected stranger arrives their fun turns into a nightmarish race for survival. Alone bloodied and hunted - the group's friendship is put to the test... and secrets from their black pasts have begun to darken the present.
