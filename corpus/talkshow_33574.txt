In the tradition of no budget rock documentaries like Heavy Metal Parking Lot now comes Brenda Fest: A Slutibration. Join Eddie Rotten and the former cast of Zombie Life TV as they celebrate Austin LGBT Icon Brenda Dickerson's birthday with a music fest at Kick Butt Coffee featuring the musical talents of: Stella Sonic New Age Love Toxic Water Evil Flying Chickens and Torque Order. A Beautiful Celebration of the Live Music Scene in Austin Texas. Documentary Shot and Edited by Kevin from the Other Dimension and his magical iPhone 7.
