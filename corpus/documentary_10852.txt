The story of the early years of the Holy Family is revealed through contemporary scientific discoveries and startling new evidence! This documentary chronicles the journey of the Holy Family to Bethlehem and beyond. Witness the birth of Christ and then follow Mary Joseph and the newborn Jesus as they are forced to flee into Egypt to escape Herod's murderous decree. Real historical sites in Israel Egypt and Jordan are the backdrop of this timeless tale of miracles and mystery. Visit actual sites in the Holy Land where astounding events still occur in the present day; the same places where Jesus performed miracles 2000 years ago. These and other mysteries are thoroughly examined through biblical and apocryphal writings of the times historical accounts archaeological research and legends myths and fables that have passed down from generation to generation. Noted scientists archaeologists historians astronomers and religious scholars present modern evidence supporting the events of the birth and early childhood of Jesus.
