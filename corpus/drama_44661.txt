Annie Randolph's life falls into shambles when she receives devastating news. She must deal with the fact that the father of her child has walked out on them her premature baby is on the verge of death and she will never be able to conceive again. Annie slowly rebuilds her world meeting the man of her dreams John(Doug Gatten)and eventually finds herself befriending a rugged teenage girl on the run Dora Lee (Melinda Reyneir) and her abused brother Danny (Colton Burnsworth) on what will begin a new chapter in her life. Annie takes on the hardest challenge she has ever faced to turn a child with the characteristics of a wild animal into a human being through the power of love. Based on the true story "The Miracle of Danny" by author Barbara A. Clemens.
