Rocky Star was a 1950's radio serial. Stephen Fearnley Stephen Harrop and Andrew Trauchi have taken the this 600 minute radio serial and created a 100 minute television series with live actors using the original soundtrack dubbed in. Rocky Star is about the space adventures of Rocky Star and his assistant Mitch Morgan as they battle Zog and Cos. The series length was made up of 20 x 5 minute episodes. Episodes often have the cast breaking into song! The episodes were screened as segments of Eat Carpet (1993).
