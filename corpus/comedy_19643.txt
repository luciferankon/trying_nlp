Young African American Male by the name of Adam he's ambitious and drive becomes the manager of this face paced high scale coffee shop in Beverly Hills. Adam will soon learn the hardships of trying to be successful in a thriving market where Starbucks and Coffee Bean will be his strong competitors. Adam and his team are doing what they can to prove that they have what it takes to stay in business. But what Adam doesn't know is that his ethnicity will make it difficult due to the location to keep things running smoothly.
