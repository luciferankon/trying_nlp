From 'The Return of the Living Dead' to 'Weekend at Bernie's' character actor Don Calfa is one of the most recognisable faces on TV and the big screens of the 1970s 80s and 90s. With a wealth of experiences and stories Don is a true LEGEND in the film industry. 'That's a Cut?' is a tribute to the man himself featuring candid interviews with Don about his long and glorious career supported by his friends and co-stars.
