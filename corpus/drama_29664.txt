An unflinching raw look at friendship love and fate stay with me. is award winning writer-director Jason Hooper's debut feature film. Set against the backdrop of a rising cult "the liners" who revel in public suicide the picture follows the intertwined emotional paths of childhood friends Ridgley Smith (Kurt Finney) Paige Madison (Vanessa Ray) and Lincoln Colby (Mike Knab) as they grasp to find meaning and salvation in a world gone insane. The Interplay of tension probes into the world of not only "the liners" but asks the audience to contemplate risking all to achieve your dreams in a primarily unromantic callous world.
