This story revolves around Martin who from the outside seems to have everything. Martin is a young intelligent affluent professional with nothing seemingly wrong in his life externally yet all his internal conflicts leave him paralyzed from obtaining true happiness. He suffers from social anxiety; which disables him from engagement with peers distances him from friends and most painful of all seeking out his love interest. Life begins to come completely unravel and shatter a flyer with the words Start Living Today" presents itself in helping Martin re-emerge from the brink of total breakdown. Just when we see he begins to live the insurmountable battle within him leads him to his demise with no one to help."
