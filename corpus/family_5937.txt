When two children adopt a pug from the local rescue shelter- little did they know just how much Margaret the rescue Pug could teach them. Welcome to Pug Parenting -- a heartwarming series about the fun funny and true life lessons which can be learned from a pug. Margaret become far more than just another hungry dog -- she shows her new family how to take a step back relax enjoy what matters and make the most from life. It is both comedic and endearing- and ultimately serves to encourage other families to adopt from rescue shelters.
