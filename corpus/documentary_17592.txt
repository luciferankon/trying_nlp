It's Not Fair is an immersion in a school playground of Paris. In the middle of the immense energy that generate 100 children from 3 to 5 years the attentive camera follows some characters in their daily essential concern : to play ! In this microcosm we discover how the child learns by the game the rules of what will be all its life its social and personal relationships. How to create to understand and to respect or to cheat with the rules of the game ? How to behave with the others and to become friend or not ? The child is the revealing surface of our buried memories. He sends back to us as a mirror the image of a contemporary society rich in its cultural and social diversity. With an impact so social as intimate the movie is the reflection of an intense collective and emotional life which we the adults largely forgot...
