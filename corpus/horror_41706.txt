In 1936 the Gallegos family owners of the Bethlehem Hotel ritual murderers and devout followers of an ancient demon were lynched by the townspeople of Belen New Mexico. 80 years later a small group of paranormal investigators will be the first to research the hotel and the souls of over 200 victims. Nikki the group's lead medium immediately becomes confused by the overwhelming amount of supernatural energy and a hidden ancient evil. Slowly the ancient evil begins to reveal itself. But there is another evil a closer evil waiting in the hotel. Waiting for its own moment to strike.
