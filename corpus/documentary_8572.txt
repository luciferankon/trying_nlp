A documentary about a young girl who was kidnapped sexually assaulted and brutally beaten by her father's friend. This terrible act took place 10 years ago just two months after Elizabeth Smart was taken. It was the worst child kidnapping sexual assault and attempted homicide in Utah's history. Hear this young woman's story in her own words recounting the hardships she has overcome. Learn about her dreams of college and the hope of having a family of her own someday. Hear how this tragic compelling story has been overlooked by the media for the last ten years Come and meet this remarkable courageous young woman; an inspiration to all that meet her.
