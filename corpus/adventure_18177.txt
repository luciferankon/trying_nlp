In order to save his home Johnny Swade together with his friends Tim Abby Kelly and Ariel are on a riveting journey to expose the secret dealings of a tyrant Henry Wilmont. In pursuit of these students to prevent Wilmont's dealings exposed are his henchmen. Throughout this gripping adventure we will learn a great deal about these five characters through their friendships aspirations and heroics. Set in Melbourne Johnny Swade is a feel good Aussie adventure drama-comedy film about family friendships responsibilities and with lots of laugh the whole family can enjoy.
