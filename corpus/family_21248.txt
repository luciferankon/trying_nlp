WE ARE NEW YORK: WANY is a series of dramas about the lives of working class immigrants who come from all over the world to make New York their home. It's created by the Mayor's Office of Immigrant Affairs and the City University of New York with the purpose of helping adult immigrants for whom English is a second language to improve their understanding of English by watching the series and also to learn about their rights to city and community services. The topics deal with worker rights immigration rights the struggle to make ends meet in New York City food justice dealing with family issues early childhood education and others.
