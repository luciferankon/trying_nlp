Cape Town Soup. Four portraits of Cape Town residents. A staged documentary filled with Capetonian stories and dance about a melting pot of cultures race and habits of poverty and wealth. How do people get along fifteen years after the abolition of apartheid? What's it like to live together in a society in which your place of birth and the color of your skin still determine your future? Can you break away from your own expectations? A film about the great personal challenges of life but also about the smaller ones: which haircut suits me and what do I wear?
