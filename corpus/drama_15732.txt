Rachel Levesque a street smart no nonsense bad ass chick is Windwill Town's most decorated gambler. She is the only undefeated player in Windwill Town; but a mysterious man from her past by the name of Mortimore Henry looks to manipulate her into losing a big poker tournament. He threatens to reveal a dark secret about her past if she doesn't bend to his demands. In order to keep her reputation safe Rachel seeks the help of Tyrone Briggs a man who equally matches Rachel's street smarts and bad ass attitude to murder Mortimore.
