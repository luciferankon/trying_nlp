Alfie Placktis a small town blue-collar kid turned flamboyant entrepreneur with a style all his own. From his first humble falafel stand at age 15 to multi-million dollar real estate developments he barrels down paths that only a true entrepreneur can stomach. This fresh playful format mixes a pair of young millennials with seasoned business pros and sends the audience around the globe to meet colorful entrepreneurs with the goal to finance their projects.
