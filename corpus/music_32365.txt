Discipline Live 1995 presents Detroit's progressive rock legends during their most theatrical period. This special edition (DVD only) includes an extra hour of live Discipline band footage spanning the group's early years from shows in 1988 1992 1997 and a rare 1998 performance caught on video of the 20-minute song suite Into the Dream.
