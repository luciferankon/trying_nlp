Resilient is an action driven feature with US government asset Adam Lancaster (Stephen Preston) being assigned to terminate Redwood (Jonathan Pienaar) a notorious drug lord and mastermind Crime Boss (Niel Herman). The mission for our Hero takes a turn when a little 7 year old girl becomes a target for our ruthless killer. The suspense and action builds up even more when Lancaster's wife McKenzie and now adopted daughter are being taken hostage by Redwood and his team of thugs. LANCASTER faces betrayal from his late wife Victoria Lancaster (Anchel Bosque de Madera) who's father is the Crime boss (Borislav) and Hamilton (Jena Waldron) a corrupt US government official. Adam is counted on to save the day and live up to his reputation as a true American Hero trough all the conflict of betrayal.
