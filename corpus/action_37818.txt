10 years ago Jeff Bogard Southtown's greatest martial artist entered the King of Fighters tournament. He beat every one of Southtown's best fighters and won. But the crime boss Geese Howard interrupted his winning ceremony and demanded that he fights Bogard. In the midst of the furious fight Geese kills Bogard without any remorse. 10 years later Bogard's sons Terry and Ady and their friend Joe Higashi entered the KOF tourney to swear vengeance against Geese for killing Bogard. Will they avenge Bogard...or will they lose?
