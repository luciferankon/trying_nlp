From construction worker to travel show hostMy Top 10 follows Tara Guacher as she explores one of the most fascinating destinations in the world Iceland. Tara landed her dream job after winning Travel and Escape's national search to find their next travel personality. In this one hour television special Tara counts down her favourite experiences in the country that has become one of the most traveled spots of 2013. Glacier hiking Snorleking and Ice climbing are are all on Tara's Top 10 things to do in Iceland.
