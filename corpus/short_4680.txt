Ricky begs his best friend John to fly out to LA without giving a reason - only that he is in a bad way. Once arriving John discovers a small-time drug dealer locked in a dog cage. Solving the problem is harder than expected when the police arrive and things go awry. In the end their friendship is tested not just by the situation at hand but also by John's relationship with Ricky's girlfriend. Chase sequences a love story and plot twists.
