In the film debut for Texas A&M football player John Kimbrough and the third remake of this Zane Grey novelMajor McNeill ('William Farnum' (qv)) of the Texas Rangers in an effort to break up and stop a gang of cattle rustlers sends Texas Ranger Buck Duane ('John Kimbrough (I)' (qv))into Exceter County Texas to take over. Buck meets Barbara Lonstretch ('Sheila Ryan (I)' (qv)) who has just been robbed by a pair of the outlawsas she was returning home from the East.
