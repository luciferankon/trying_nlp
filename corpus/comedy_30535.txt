Since they were kids Adam and Thomas have been best friends and ghost hunters. But now in their 20s their passion for investigating the paranormal is proving unsustainable. Thomas takes a desk job in another city. But Adam unwilling to give up on his dream hires Dirk a local ghost tour guide and con man to help him start a ghost hunting business. Adam and Dirk embark on a series of embarrassing and disturbing investigations in an effort to try and make a buck. Along the way they prove that real life can be more bizarre than anything paranormal.
