Poised to become one of the most successful screenwriting teams in Hollywood Alex Kurtzman and Roberto Orci are certainly in striking position. A team who became just that after meeting in high school they have frequently collaborated with writer-director J.J. Abrams and director Michael Bay. As a team their screenwriting credits include The Island The Legend of Zorro Mission: Impossible III and the upcoming Star Trek XI. In these in-depth discussions some of today's most successful screenwriters share their work habits methods and inspirations secrets of the trade and eye-opening stories from life in the trenches of the film business. It's powerful knowledge straight from the source.
