Hurricane Katrina caused the greatest mass migration since the dust bowl and while leaving only took a day rebuilding a life in a new place takes much much longer. The short documentary Transplant: New Orleans to Texas chronicles two such uprooted women: one who is mentally and physically challenged and the other her caretaker through the state of Louisiana. After evacuating New Orleans to escape Hurricane Katrina both struggle to form a new life in a small town in Texas. While Vonda the caretaker and her children are having a hard time adjusting they are learning a lot from Tanya her client who has overcome all challenges to pursue selling Avon products door-to-door. Tanya who is without a family of her own is learning to be a part of Vonda's family. Both learn to redefine their definitions of family and home.
