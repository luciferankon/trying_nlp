This new twist on the ever-popular matchmaking format takes the notion of "blind date" to its literal extreme as Aussie guys and girls have various liaisons in pitch-black conditions over several days. Will they fall for each other? And if so will they still want to pursue a relationship when they each know what the other looks like? Hosted by celebrity model and 2008's Miss Universe Australia Laura Dundovic Dating In The Dark combines cheeky voyeurism with irresistible insights into how human relationships really work.
