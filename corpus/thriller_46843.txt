Mercy is like most teenage girls boy fever fights with her parents hates doing her homework but instead of sleepovers she spends her free time hunting and killing wild animals in the woods. For 19 years Mercy has been raised in isolation by her ex-military parents who are trying to stay off the grid for the crimes of their past. But they couldn't run forever and Mercy finds herself alone for the first time in Los Angeles where life is dark menacing and unforgiving.
