A filmed account of the 28th Eucharistic Congress held in Chicago June 20th to 24th in 1926. A pilgrimage of 750000 people journey to the Seminary of Saint Mary of the Lake in Mundelein Illinois for the great pageant of the Eucharistic Procession. Cardinal Bonzano is shown officiating at the high mass at an altar erected before the Church of the Immaculate Conception with 11 other cardinals in attendance. Children's day is celebrated in Soldier Field with a children's choir of 62000.
