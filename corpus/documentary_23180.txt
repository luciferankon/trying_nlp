Leon a 15 year old is part of the cosplay community teenagers who dress up as characters from comics and Japanese Anime meet and role-play. It is a subculture which developed in Japan and has since spread worldwide. Leon with his masculine facial features was born as Shiri a girl but at the age of 12 he began to wonder whether living his life as a girl was really what he wanted. As the movie progresses and leon becomes a well known and appreciated cosplayer in his community he begins to unfold and take off his real significant costume- he is a boy trapped in the body of a girl.
