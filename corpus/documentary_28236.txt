Twenty years after Diana's death this major new documentary rewrites many of the myths of her legacy. Rather than portraying Diana as unhappy at war with the Royal family and herself; and suffering a tragically death - we discover an exceptional woman who changed the future of the Royal family forever. With a cast who knew Diana intimately including Ken Wharfe Diana's long term Protection Officer her personal trainer ballet teacher spiritual healer and personal friends Diana - The Woman Inside highlights Diana as a woman and mother rather than just a tragic icon.
