SHIN is a Japanese pianist living in London. He decides to travel to Europe planning to compose a new piece of music. He arrives to Croatia and a local family suggests him to visit Rovinj. He begins to record ideas and his emotions into a voice-recorder. However he gradually becomes frustrated and lonely in the alienating situation. All he has managed to record during his trip is the conversation with his own self. Beautiful scenery and sounds around SHIN take his thoughts back in time when he lived in Japan. His memories and present merge together in Rovinj. SHIN's internal journey begins and takes him deeper and deeper into own self. He departs from Rovinj but the town remains unaware of his spiritual journey.
