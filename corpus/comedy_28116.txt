Two suspended 1970's fashion wearing crazy funky police officer's "Eddie Snooze" and his brother and partner "Woody Splinter" start their own Cat Daddy private investigation company. Since being suspended by their captain "Leroy Brooks" crime has arise within that month such as TheftKidnapping & the Rise of the new drug "Coco Dream". While most of the police department are corrupted who can be trusted? . Can Eddie and Woody crack down the bad guys and bring justice to the Tri-State area before spreading all over?
