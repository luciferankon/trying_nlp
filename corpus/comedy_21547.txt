Garry Schmitz has a very small problem that's having a deep impact on his love life. When he goes to the local clinic and is diagnosed with MicroPenis things quickly go from bad to worse. Desperate for a cure and about to lose hope Garry attempts a dangerous (and hilarious) solution with near disastrous consequences but his old friend and physician penitologist Dr. Alexander Roth will go to any lengths to make sure his patient has a happy ending.
