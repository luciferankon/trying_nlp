Max Jordan a hot-shot talent agent from Akron Ohio is ready to hit the big time by establishing his own agency in LA. Because he is consumed with his desire to succeed Max has grown into a distant self-involved man. He is willing to sacrifice anything including his relationship with his fiancee in order to advance his career. Max's life soon takes an unexpected turn however when after seeing a news report about a hit-and-run involving a child he fears he may be the one responsible. In an effort to right his wrong Max decides to aid in the boy's recovery. A paternal bond forms between Max and the boy who is an orphan. Through this experience Max rediscovers the aspects of life that truly matter. As the police continue to search for the hit-and-run driver Max struggles with a difficult decision; surrender his freedom and turn himself in or remain silent and surrender his integrity.
