Agree to Disagree is a documentary that takes a look at life through the eyes of 5 African-American young men who have maintained their tight bond & kinship from childhood to adults. The men embarked on a cross country journey to witness the inauguration of our 44th President & the first African American to hold the office Barack Hussein Obama. Along the way the friends who all have very different occupations & personalities; confront discuss debate and argue over a number of tough issues. Whether it's politics religion health sex racism economics etc. no stone is left unturned no stereotype is off limits and no topic is taboo. This experience will challenge not only their friendships but their beliefs & ideals as individuals. By the end of the long journey life as they know it will be changed forever.
