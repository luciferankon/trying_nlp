This is the most definitive film ever done on Yogi's life and career because Yogi himself tells the story. Featured are never-before-seen photos from the Berra family as well as incredible color footage from the great Yankee teams of the 1950s. Special guests include: MICKEY MANTLE GEORGE STEINBRENNER DEREK JETER WILLIE MAYS TINO MARTINEZ CARL ERSKINE DUKE SNIDER and many more.
