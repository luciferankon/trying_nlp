Dana is an isolated young woman who comes to learn of an outbreak of 'Disconosolatus' in the city which requires mandatory evacuations medication and quarantine for those infected. The symptoms of this bizarre infection coincidentally resemble that of Dana's general depressive state. As the lines between her dreamworld and reality become blurred Dana plunges into a mystical subconscious world riddled with poetry. This surreal journey ultimately leads to the critical moment of Dana's personal awakening.
