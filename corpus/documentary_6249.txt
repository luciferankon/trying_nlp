The Greatest Fashion Icons in Film will celebrate the fifteen most legendary fashion icons in cinema but will do it with a surprising contemporary twist. Not only will it focus upon the great fashion moments scenes and screen personalities it will also celebrate and reveal which of today's cinematic stars best exemplify our fashion legends both in sense of style and screen personality. Indeed we all recognize such fashion icons as Marilyn Monroe Audrey Hepburn and Cary Grant but who among our contemporary stars best personify their fashion frisson and mystique?
