Dian (Erra Fazira) is concerned about her son as he has grown silent and developed a strange habit of hiding. She restricts him from moving away from her after a stranger informs her that enclosed places pose a danger to him. His prediction comes true when Adam goes missing and is nowhere to be found. Everyone is unaware about his death. However the son comes back to his mother longing for her love and also seeking revenge against every person who hurts her.
