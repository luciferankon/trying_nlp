In 1916 the Spanish Ship called "Principe de Asturias" sunk on the coast of Brazil under mysterious conditions carrying more than 1200 immigrants and 8 tons of gold. Many of the immigrants died that night and their bodies washed across 300 miles of shore. The gold disappeared. Rumurous explanations try to elucidate the mystery of what happened that night and after the years that sealed the ship under the ocean.
