This full-length comedy features Belle the main character who spends far too much time engrossed in her hand-held smart phone device. So much so in fact that she has been sued fined busted and ultimately forced to hire a lawyer to keep her out of jail. Her latest incident caused the court to haul her back in once again. She knows she might be facing jail time. Her attempt to stay out of the slammer: That is where this story begins and where Belle's life takes a bizarre turn.There is an important lesson for everyone in this film: There are heavy penalties for smart phone users who do stupid things because of them. And some pretty interesting consequences for those who break the law-or are there?
