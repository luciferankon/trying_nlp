The second film by Brian Edward Doyle. After the release of Bernard T. Ward's Popcorn Bag of Terror Brian Edward Doyle is back with his next film "Camp and Die!" which is another horror movie filled with blood and guts. This story is about a group of four best friends from high school who don't get to see each other much any more now. They plan a get together to go camping for the weekend in the woods. Little do they know our campers are camping in the same woods that Del and Milo Rollins live in. Who are two crazed psychotic hermits who take kindly to anyone invading there territory. What will happen when Del and Milo cross path with the campers? All you should expect is that things are going to get bloody.
