Set on the backdrop of a village the story begins with Iriya who is a grave digger and he has the rare power of seeing the future. One day he sees Siddha in his dream and believes that the old aged Gowda of the Big House is dead. He makes his preparation but is shocked to hear from the caretaker that no death has taken place at the house. Later Rudri wife of Iriya sees a dream that Siddha is visiting the village and everyone is excited. However Siddha doesn't turn up and this shatters the couple. They begin to doubt if they have lost that rare power. What happens from there forms the rest of the story.
