'Hope' a documentary about how the biggest sport in the world - football can break down barriers and give disenfranchised youth 'hope' through the lens of the UN charity 'Football For Peace'. It will look at how football can give young people the opportunity to understand and communicate with others and help communities to find common ground and dialogue to unite in peace. From trafficked women in Cambodia who founded the women's national football team to street children in Pakistan meeting Pele the documentary will follow some of the most famous footballers in the world inspiring young men and women throughout the world. It will show the results that have been made by dedicated sports men and women working with these young people who need help finding a direction in their life.
