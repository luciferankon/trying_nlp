Promotional documentary television special filmed at the London East End Docklands area and River Thames for the filming of the opening boat chase for _World Is Not Enough The (1999)_. The documentary examines the role of the second unit and examines in technical detail the staging of the boat chase sequence including construction problems and difficulties scheduling weather conditions testing stunts safety speed distance landings floatation injury gadgets explosions rescues rehearsals filming and shooting on water.
