Denise Wohrer is a normal average woman living a normal life in Portland Oregon; a normal person with a few distinguishing quirks - did we mention she routinely eats people? Particularly ex boyfriends. Haunted by the ghost of Jack her meat-head bodybuilding ex turned meat-in-her-freezer and guided by the wisdom of her therapist Meredith (a woman of mystery who just might have a few quirks of her own that are more than Denise bargained for) tune in to Denise Wohrer's journey balancing her career as a math professor and her staunch vegetarian ethics with her secret life is a serial killer with an insatiable taste for human flesh.
