Delia is a young woman who survived a river-flood ten years ago among her mother and her two sons. The have been forced to live in a poor neighborhood of Caracas. She works hard trying to rebuilt their lives but she soon discovers that her sons William (17) and Santiago (11) are exposed to the violent and dangerous environment they live in. And Raiza the grandmother is the living image of hopelessness. The four characters will show us that the hope of reconstruction is possible because the force of love despite all keep them united.
