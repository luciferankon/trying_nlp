Three short stories: In 'Voyeur' an employee at a hotel exploits his position of trust by spying on rooms through a network of screens. In 'Blessed Transgression' a couple who have divorced run into each other in a park. They embark on a sex game involving two complete strangers reigniting their old passion in a new way. 'Quattro' is set on the eve of the new millennium: New Year's Eve 1999. To celebrate the beginning of a new century two men and a woman have a wild night of passion together in a hotel room.
