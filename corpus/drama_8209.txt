After five years away in Africa young engineer Bob Schneider comes back to his hometown for a short time.He wants to find again Andrée a girl he used to be in love with.He meets Karin who lives in the same boarding-home as Andrée.Since Bob 's departure Andrée has been prey to Korbanke  a pimp and a drug trafficker :she has a child whose father is none other than Bob.Korbanke mistakes Andrée's former lover for a policeman and lures him into a trap.
