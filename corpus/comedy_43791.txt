Ever wondered how video game mechanics and concepts such as a zombie hoard in Left 4 Dead or the banana peel in Mariokart would work in the real world? This is a show that will test these mechanics and concepts using what ever means nescesary even if it means risking the two test subject's physical wellbeing and mental state. Burnie Burns and Griffon Ramsey have fun doing "science" experiments that they created from concepts in video games. Guinea pigs Gus Sorola and Geoff Ramsey go through rigorus training regiments to test these out. Sometimes they learn something
