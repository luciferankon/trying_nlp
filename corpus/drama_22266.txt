Claire a young artist from Liverpool arrives in Seville in 1968 after following her husband Manuel she stubbles across a country living under a fascist dictatorship an extreme catholic family and a society with two roles for women: mother and wife. In this oppressive environment Claire meets Ana a lesbian airline stewardess for Iberia where she will begin an intimate relationship and will be introduced to the artistic and bohemian underworld of Seville. "She loves you" is a tale of love passion personal discovery and a fight for freedom.
