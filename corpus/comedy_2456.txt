Four years removed from his glory days as Mt. Nutt High School basketball's most esteemed Fighting Gentleman Walsh "Vanilla Float" Perry (Sean Chuthers) is in no rush to relinquish his fading celebrity status nor his claim to fame: a district record 3023 career points. His marriage-hungry girlfriend Jen Jr. (Natalie Coleman) however is much more interested in Walsh's future than his past...preferably as her husband. Watch as Walsh battles his way through a dose of responsibility as the former all-star struggles to keep the "Vanilla Float" alive.
