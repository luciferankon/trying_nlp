Ramesh and Malti have made it big in their life after lot of struggle their only son Samir a college student has never struggled in life for anything.Samir wants to live life like a free bird and doesn't want his parents to interfere in his life as he feels his parents also did the same in their younger age.Ramesh and Malti scold him and tell him that they did party in their times but they were responsible towards their duties.Later due to his way of lifestyle Samir is caught in drug raid.
