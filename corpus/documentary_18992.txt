The true story of one of rock-n-roll's most influential groups. Includes more than 20 live performances from The Band's body of work including: The Weight The Night They Drove Old Dixie Down Up On Cripple Creek and Stagefright. The documentary follows The Band from their days as a backing group for rockabilly star Ronnie Hawkins to playing with Bob Dylan in 1966 when he changed to a rock artist from their landmark first album Music From Big Pink to the group's initial breakup commemorated in The Last Waltz and their comeback in 1992.
