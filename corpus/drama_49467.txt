A peasant boy from the rough caucasian steppe whose relatives in Privolnje die of hunger and suffer imprisonment by Stalin comes after WWII to Moscow without even speaking proper Russian - and rises to become the last emperor of the Soviet-Union end the Cold War free the world and save humanity from nuclear extinction. During the coup of 1991 his life is at stake - and all of his achievements. Will the plotters turn back the clock of history - will the Cold War come back or will Gorbachev find a way to make a Cold War impossible forever?
