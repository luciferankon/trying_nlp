The first film of an Ethiopian director from Israel speaks mostly Amharic and reveals the Ethiopian community through the eyes of Meseganio Tadela aged 74 who is a hard and nervous man. After he loses his wife he sets out on a journey that leads him through his children's homes. He comes to realize that he belongs to a rapidly disappearing variety that believes in retaining the Jewish Ethiopian culture. The harsh reality hits him in the face and he tries to survive according to his own ways. Meseganio Tadela 74 is a hard obstinate and nervous man who immigrated to Israel from Ethiopia 28 years ago with his family. He comes to realize that he belongs to a rapidly disappearing class that believes in retaining Ethiopian culture.
