One Night for ONE DROP is a completely original one night only production featuring artists from each of the 8 Las Vegas Cirque du Soleil productions alongside renowned guest performer John Legend. The inaugural event in 2013 was produced and created by Krista Monson. In 2014 Mukhtar O.S. Mukhtar produced and created the second edition. After another resounding success One Night for ONE DROP was back in 2015 and once more produced and created by Mukhtar O.S. Mukhtar. This third edition was held on Friday March 20 2015 at The Beatles LOVE Theatre at The Mirage.
