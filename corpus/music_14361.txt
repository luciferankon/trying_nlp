A concert film about Grammy Award-winning Tedeschi Trucks Band's 2016 show at the historic Fox Theatre in Oakland California. The film features live performances of the band's original material as well as covers of The Beatles Leonard Cohen Bob Dylan and Derek and The Dominos. Interspersed with backstage footage and interviews from bandleaders Derek Trucks and Susan Tedeschi (conducted by comedian/podcaster Marc Maron and music-critic David Fricke) the film offers a glimpse into life on the road with this 12-piece ensemble.
