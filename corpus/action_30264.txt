With a focus on promoting faith and trust in the Afghan Security Forces this thirteen-part drama series centers on an elite police unit tasked with combating major crime and corruption at all levels of society. Eagle Four was fast-paced contemporary and addressed some of the most significant security issues facing Afghanistan. The fact that the team performed their duties with integrity honesty and strict adherence to the Rule of Law countered universally held notions of the Afghan Police while reinforcing the on-going professionalization of the security forces.
