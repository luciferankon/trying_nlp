Two young men Jerry and Omar separately enter quarter-life crises. Jerry a Starbucks employee and roller-blading-vagabond is recovering from a breakup; Omar a closeted homosexual struggles to liberate himself. A chance meeting at a bookstore results in a connection between the two strangers over burgers cigarettes and Skittles. In an attempt to find meaning in their lives the two set out to make a documentary - called "White Wall" - about the significance of dreams and the people that follow them.
