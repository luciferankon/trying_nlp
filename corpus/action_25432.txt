Alex is a modern day cupid a singular operative who joined a mysterious global organization with the goals of helping the world rediscover romance. Her clinical execution and near perfect timing award her regular Ethereum payments. She works fast and she works alone until one day her bosses throw her a partner named Theo. He's a bad communicator a fast driver and frenetically impulsive. As she realizes their connection is real she starts to question the true interests of the very organization they work for and the meaning of their jobs.
