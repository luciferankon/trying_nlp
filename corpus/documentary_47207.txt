Sledhead is a documentary about a remarkable team and even more remarkable men. It's the story about Team Canada's Sledge Hockey Team and their quest to become the World Champion. It's a gripping doc an up-close look at this tough sport - the players battle their opponents with bone-breaking intensity on the ice in titanium sleds striving to score with unrelenting intensity. It's also about men from a wide variety of backgrounds with amazing stories to tell. For all it's little wonder they are determined to win at all costs.
