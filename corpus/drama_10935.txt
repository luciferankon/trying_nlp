The characters are connected through a series of intertwined relationships that have the collective group questioning decisions they've made in their lives and the possibilities of changing their circumstances; be it an unhealthy relationship unfulfilled career move prescription dependence or sexual indulgence. It's the choices that one has to make to take that leap and turn the tables on their current condition or play it safe and succumb/submerge and let things stay as they are and remain unsatisfied. These life pressures create emotional sensations that take each character to the threshold of unraveling changing everything in their life as they know it.
