Armando Quiroga is the wealthiest and most powerful man in Villa Ruiseńor. He has built a lovely home with his aristocrat wife Estela and their children. Quiroga has achieved all of his dreams but his life is about to be shaken by his own actions. While he partakes in a forbidden affair his daughter Maria Elsa has fallen in love with the son of the housemaid challenging the most important rule of their time: the division of social classes. And the arrival of Reynaldo Suarez the new priest in Villa Ruiseńor means more trouble for Armando. Maria Elsa quickly becomes seduced by this priest's charm unable to avoid the romance that begins escalating between them. This priest will find himself stuck at a crossroads: keeping his faith or succumbing to a woman's love.
