ALL THE WRONG PLACES follows Sean Will and Cotter three childhood friends who for the first time find themselves simultaneously single. They make a pact to "seek and serve" the women of LA dating their way from Culver City to the Valley in hopes of finding their perfect matches. Whether it's drift racing golf carts or pretending to be homeless the boys always begin their weekends together plotting and planning their next conquests. In the end successful or not they all meet up to bowl in a senior league with their octogenarian ace in the hole Dolores.
