Two men embark on a Quixotic Journey to find the infamous J.H. a.k.a Jessica Hanlan. As Alex a hardworking heroic protagonist longs to lose his virginity. Jesse a strong-holded temperamental man wants to be the change Alex wishes to seek. Through a night of toils and amoral acts each man finds themselves on the long and winding road in one long farcical plot that is the epitome of each waiting for godot.
