After a horrible breakup JACK a poor ordinary man gets a job to take care of his rich uncle's house. Jack hosts a party in the house and gets the attention from a sexy girl ROSE. They have sex in the house. To impress Rose Jack shows off his horrible drawing skill. The next day they try to reprise the classic scene from Tatanic but both fall into the cold water. Jack reveals that he is not rich at all. Rose reveals that she is just a gold digger. Because neither of them can swim they have to wait for help in the cold water and fight for the only dinghy.
