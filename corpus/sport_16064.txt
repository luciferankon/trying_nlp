Severe Racing makes it a trifecta with the release of their new film Severe Offroad 3 Trophy Trucks Buggies Moto X Bikes Quads and UTV's battle it out across Americas toughest terrains. Get up close to the action with us and get blown away by the roar of 100 MPH+ vehicles headed right at you. The best drivers and riders in Offroad put it on the line for you.
