In this tongue-in-cheek science fiction movie the eclectic crew of misfit Astronauts is nearing their destination. Realizing that the crew is showing the onset of insanity Hetfield the ship's artificially intelligent computer tries to salvage the situation by becoming more human. Learning that humans use instincts and hunches Hetfield decides to try a hunch of his own and secretly changes course to answer a distress call unwittingly causing the crew to be infected by an alien life-force.
