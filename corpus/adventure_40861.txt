Among the immense and lush hills of the Ardennes scattered by ancient castles a group of teenagers is about to be dragged into a thrilling and mysterious adventure. Four Belgian boys one Frenchman one German and a pretty Canadian girl on vacation are hunting like a game for the treasure lost by the Templars but soon find themselves involved in mysterious inexplicable events. In the silence of the Hautes Fagnes in fact impressive and sinister ruins of the unnamed Castle rise. Around this historical relic that dates back to the feudal times the boys will make continuous and surprising discoveries increasingly inexplicable in caves and underground. But everything is so disturbing and it is not clear what actually lies in the castle. Finally the young scouts with courage cunning and audacity will help the police solve the mystery. The story is inspired by a similar fact that happened in those years in Belgium.
