ODD ONES OUT documents four years in the courageous lives of three transgender teens Naomi David and Tot as they transition into adulthood and face life-altering events. Family intolerance abuse homelessness sex work violence and harassment in their schools and community are only the backdrop for a dramatic story of acceptance and rejection identity and change. It's a coming of age story about trying to fit in while fighting against society's rigid boundaries. With Naomi David and Tot you will reconsider the definitions of male and female and then discard them as you grow closer to three teenagers who just want to grow up.
