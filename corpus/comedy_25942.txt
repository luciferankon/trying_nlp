In the world of casting in New York City Susie Goldstein is your go-to person. For over twenty years she has delivered star driven projects with the support of her quirky staff despite the big personalities of the actors and their chaotic lives. This comedic series gives a behind-the-scenes look at the business on both sides of the casting table. CASTING is the story of "The Wonder Years" rip-off TV pilot written by the young screen writer Rebecca Spielberg; the fictional niece of the famed director. The pilot is directed by the "famous" Trojan Commercial Director ex U2 cover band member Jay Meijer. Goldstein accepts the challenge to cast the show trying to beat all odds in order to make the project a success.
