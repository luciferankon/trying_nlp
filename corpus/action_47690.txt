What can a group of idealists do to stop rampant political corruption during the Ming Dynasty? When an evil minister comes into power he sends Ye Hong Ying (Jiao En Jun) a trained killer to go after Gu Ren Yuan (Yang Zhi Wen) and her family for being active in a movement to end government corruption. Yang Dong Qin (Qi Fang) is a dancer who also is politically active.
