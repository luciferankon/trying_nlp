Old man Wan enjoys boasting about his adventurous days as a gangster to anyone who would listen in the teahouse. His son Hue a strong-willed boy is in love with Chew Hung the daugther of Wan's blood brother Chuen. But things turn sour when Chuen promises her to the son of a powerful politician. To force the lovers to separate Chuen gives a loan to Wan with a condition attached. The young people decided to elope to Taipei but soon Chuen and his gang of hoodlum have tracked them down...
