Fantasy and imagination make life in a modern city super exciting for Alex and his family. Magical stories invented by Mom about what happens on her way home inspire Alex to create his own amazing real world games and adventures. The parents of 5-year-old Alex try to make his life in the modern city as interesting as possible. Mom makes up stories about incredible adventures that happened to her on her way home weaving magical characters and incredible situations into the canvas of ordinary urban stories. Alex loves his Mom's stories and they always inspired him to invent plenty of the new games for his friends. In the evening the whole family gathers together and discuss their adventures of the day.
