Hong Kong year 2035 H has just arrived in Hong Kong on business. His life suddenly takes a dark turn when a stranger furtively slips a mysterious object in his pocket and dies in his arms. The businessman will later learn from Ylvie - a young woman as sexy as she is dangerous - that the mysterious object called 'S.O.L.I.I.D' is a cure for a terrible threat to humanity. H and Ylvie have to find a way to activate the S.O.L.I.I.D. GOD made the world in 7 days; they have 7 hours to save it!
