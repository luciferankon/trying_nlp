In a world without hope. On a boulevard of broken dreams. Four heroes will rise to seize their destiny. In the high stakes world of Hollywood Boulevard Costumed Impersonation being the best is a way of life and staying the best a walk on the razors edge. When the fat kid playing the Incredible Hulk goes missing four loose cannons will band together on a journey that will take them into the heart of evil or at least Rancho Cucamonga. But time is not on their side. As a six o'clock audition looms will these characters lose themselves in their alter egos?
