A present-day thriller about a man with a needle in his brain. This needle poisons his mind and tortures him with morbid thoughts and an urge to kill. When he finally commits a murder there is a witness who is drawn into a deadly game of cat and mouse which will come to a climax in an abandoned fish factory. Policeman Göran Ĺhlund is on sick leave when he quite by accident discovers the dead body of a woman under a bridge. In his spare time he is investigating ads in the yellow paper and discovers that one person hustler Chris Lipinski is placing ads daily. In Lipinski's shop Ĺhlund steals a laptop computer on which he finds photos of mutilated women that only the murderer could have taken. Several women have been murdered and mutilated by a killer that the police are looking for and this is the lead that Ĺhlund needs to solve the case
