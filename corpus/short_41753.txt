Thinking of Remodeling? This one of a kind DVD is designed to peel back the curtain and provide a behind the scenes look (with real life footage-not scripted TV footage) at what you will be embarking on with the remodel process and give insight on how to make your project a smooth success. It captures the inconvenience and mess that TV renovation programs merely skim over.The narration throughout offers opinions and suggestions that will help anyone about to embark on a remodel to be more informed.
