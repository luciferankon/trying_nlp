Gohin Baluchor is a love story between a boy and a girl living in a village very near a river. One day a piece of land (Chor) rises from the water. This piece of land was once submerged in the river. The feud between two villages over the ownership of the risen land and the fate of the star crossed lovers will unfold in the film Gohin Baluchor. The story will mirror the longing desires determined struggles hope and frustration personal interest big or little sorrows and happiness of the villagers.
