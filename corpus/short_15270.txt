This public service short is presented by the Variety Club of Northern California. 'Glenn Ford' introduces his wife 'Eleanor Powell (I)' (qv) who asks moviegoers to contribute to the Variety Club's efforts to help blind babies and their families. As an offshoot of her Christian based television series _"The Faith of Our Children" (1953)_ (qv) former dancer 'Eleanor Powell (I)' (qv) who is introduced by her actor husband 'Glenn Ford (I)' (qv) appeals for public financial support for the Variety Club of Northern California's work in caring for the several hundred blind babies in the area. The club has a staff of eight doctors who specialize in this field providing both information to parents and direct medical care.
