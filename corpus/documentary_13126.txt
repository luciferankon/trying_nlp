In our modern world most services are oriented to a minority conforming what we call the tip of the population pyramid (only one sixth of the world's population) ignoring 5 billion people that constitute the base of the pyramid a sector with a very low income and many necessities. Entrepreneur Ricardo Salinas believes it's time for this model to evolve. "It's impossible to ignore millions of people any further. We must find a way to satisfy their necessities and find a way to integrate them into a productive lifestyle". This documentary includes testimonies by people who have enjoyed access to financial tools which have enabled them to transform their lifestyles and opened the doors to a future of hope.
