In a hollow deep in the back woods of Kentucky there is an unassuming grave of a young woman who died at the young age of twenty-two. Folklore tells that a beautiful and intelligent woman with long flowing black hair is buried here on this peaceful spot of the woods. However folklore also tells of a more sinister secret about this seemly innocent young woman and the circumstances that surrounds her death as an evil witch named Leah Smock. The story of 'Leah Smock' the only witch burned alive in the United States is being explored by an amateur filmmaker played by Jeff Armstrong with the help of her descendant a Paranormal Investigative team and other experts in the field.
