A divorce hearing is taking place in a courtroom. The witnesses are talking about the family of Dobri who for years travels from site to site. After the trial Dobri goes back to the construction site. Because the transformers for the electricity have not arrived the specialized electric installation team of Dobri will screw in light bulbs in the cafeteria. This becomes the reason for the discussion on the piled up problems due to the poor organization of the construction site. Dobri must resolve his life issue - to stay with his brigade or to go back to his family.
