Airing on The Inspiration Networks i-Lifetv Network and internationally on INI the Everyday Woman television show features best friends Barbara and Becky as they share the struggles and stories of women who've done extraordinary things. This mother-daughter team and their guests will inspire and entertain you and help you develop practical ways to survive life's daily pressures and thrive in your home health and relationships. The Community of Friends Special launches this on-line community and magazine that will carry on the mandate of Everyday Woman.
