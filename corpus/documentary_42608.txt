The film exposes in an original way a portrait of the ancient inhabitants of the Romanian territory claimed in a certain way that ancestors of the Romaniansthe ones called the immortals by the antiquity writers. "DRACO-the stone faces" is a cinematic journey into ancient Dacia a meeting with Zalmoxis forgotten deity of the Getae a series of questions controversy and images revealed word-by-word from Latin and Greek texts of contemporary writers. An authentic portrait carved in stone by the greatest Roman empire artists. A fascinating visual journey in ancient times.
