In THE IMMACULATE MISCONCEPTION we meet Marry Flanagan (Drapper) a good girl. Marry Flanagan goes to the gynecologist to get the pill for a friend in exchange for protection from school bullies. All of a sudden Marry Flanagan is pregnant. But How? She Claims she has been visited by Saint Bernadette and the holy Virgin Mother. So her pregnancy must be a miracle - it must be the second coming - right here in Jersey City! When word gets out the faithful seek her. The media descend on her and her boyfriend Joey who believes her and loves her despite the circumstances.
