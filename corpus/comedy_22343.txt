To boost the dwindling sales of the magazine where she is an intern Alice 23 comes up with a nationwide competition to adopt Victor 85 and alone in the world. Guillaume his wife Sylvie and their kids win the contest and the cash that comes with it. They welcome Victor into their home despite Sylvie's initial reluctance. The children love their new "grandpa" Guillaume's mother-in-law flirts with him and Sylvie is too busy having an affair with the magazine's editor-in-chief to care that Victor is spreading chaos in her household. Meanwhile Alice uncovers Victor's past and realizes that she may have unwittingly let a wolf into the chicken coop.
