Louis now has sole responsibility for his nine-month old daughter. That's unfortunate - because his only concerns are being at the Sports Bar for happy-hour having enough weed to smoke and trying desperately to get any woman unlucky enough to meet him to come back to his hideous house. Angry at first that his lifestyle has been compromised by this unwelcome responsibility Louis slowly comes to realize something very important... Babies can be used to get sympathy money and girls! But even Louis cannot be devoid of all fatherly feeling. Can he? Louis' life is about to change in ways he could not possibly imagine.
