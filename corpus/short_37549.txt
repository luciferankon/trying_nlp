Christ is Coming is a special extended part of the films "The New World Order: Art Thriller Christ Rituals" "The Hidden Messages of World War 3" "Red Dreams" "Christ is Coming" and "Christ Premonitions." All films musicals aim to the 2nd Coming of Jesus Christ and the battle between the Anti-Christ and Christ. "Christ is Coming" features the song "Christ Technologies" from pop/rock album Omer Pasha Music (A Canadian Tribute).
