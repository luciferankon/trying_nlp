In this sneak preview for the American Damsel Network's upcoming season the lovely hostess provides clips of upcoming action. Several scenes from the "American Superheroine Stories" series are shown with buxom heroines are placed in deadly peril by their wily foes. A scene from a forthcoming episode from "Action Nurses" where a captive turns the tables on a well-endowed nurse and relieves her of her costume is shown. Several segments of "Go Hostagetts" featuring cheerleaders in dire peril are aired. In "Miss Shay's Daring Wild West Adventures" a blonde cowgirl is securely roped silenced with a bandanna and left behind a helpless struggling captive. The preview winds up with a scene of the buxom hostess securely bound and ball-gagged.
