A story of a sweet and innocent girl named Kammal (Kirti Gaikwad) who is brought up by three bar dancers Rama (Pratima Kazmi) Shabbo (Zahida Parveen) and Rita. They bring up Kammal with lot of care and affection and keep her away from the harsh truths of life. The story takes a major twist when a rich young boy named Manav (Sandeep Baswana) falls in love with Kammal but his family opposes their relationship on knowing Kammal's background.
