1946: Agathocles Cypriotis a World War II leftist partisan exiles himself to the island of Milos where he takes up the position of managing director at the minerals company "Varytini". 1956: With his first camera he films the inhabitants of the island and their activities on 8mm colour film. 2006: The films' "stars" watch the footage for the first time and talk about past times and of the misfortunate disposal of Agathocles Cypriotis their benefactor who remained a legend in Milos.
