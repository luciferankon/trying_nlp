Each episode features Kleinfeld Bridal's fashion director and star of TLC'S hit series 'Say Yes to the Dress' Randy Fenoli as he dishes out his top ten tips on a variety of bridal related categories. Whether it's picking out the perfect dress for your body choosing a dress to complement your venue or theme or deciding who and what to bring to your bridal appointment Randy takes on a number of topics as he shares his bridal expertise with viewers.
