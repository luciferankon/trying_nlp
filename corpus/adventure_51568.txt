A father upset with his kids constantly playing video games tells them a real life adventure story they will never forget. The father's story takes them on a journey into his own childhood where he and his friend Col discover a 150 year old Australian mystery - the identity of the blacksmith who made Ned Kelly's armour. They learn that a 5th suit helmet was made and hidden somewhere in the Ironbark Forest and they set off on an adventure to find it. Along the way they battle tiger snakes a rowdy tip owner and discover an abandoned train carriage full of illegal merchandise. With two angry goods traffickers on their tails they must use their wits along with their homemade billycart to escape. But little do they know that someone who has been following them in secret is about to change their fate forever.
