This documentary follows the Minnesota State University Moorhead Dragon Basketball team and their run at a championship title. An NCAA Division II roster helped rewrite the record books after making it to the Elite 8 for the first time a national tournament for the top schools in the country. The film captures every moment of the legendary season: the locker room monologues the pre-game pressure the buzzer beaters and offers a glimpse at how a sports program is more than just wins and losses. It's the story of how a team bonded together to make history.
