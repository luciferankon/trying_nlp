Robots have managed to finally take over the world and have created a colony where human Undesirables can be contained. Humans in the colony are only allowed out on leads to work at the colony as slave labor and are allowed to participate in procreation activities purely for regeneration of the labor force. Manetic's Inc runs the colony where men are contained. Run and managed by Robots this colony treats Undesirables separated by the segregation regime as breeding captives to generate human slaves.
