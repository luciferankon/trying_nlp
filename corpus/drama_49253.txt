Lorenzo a handsome first-year professor in an isolated Italian village falls under the spell of his most beguiling pupil a dark-haired starry-eyed 12-year-old named Duilio. As Lorenzo's relationship with his heartless girlfriend deteriorates he finds himself walking the fields with his loving student. When their idyllic friendship is questioned by Duilio's suspicious stepmother Lorenzo is brought to a crisis of conscience.
