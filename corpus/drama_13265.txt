In despair the young and beautiful Electra crashes her car on the day of her mother's wedding and as a result she is rushed to the hospital on the verge of death. However during her recovery Electra reflecting on the past she will concoct the ultimate disguise for revenge focusing on a desirable yet ruthless man in search for easy money. Soon the determined Electra will leave the hospital with only one thing on her mind: retribution. So she will begin to weave her unimaginable and fiendishly deadly trap.
