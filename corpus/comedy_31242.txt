MIS-FITS is an off beat comedy about "Sam" a self-proclaimed "necro-detective" who looks for other peoples' lost dead relatives and in the process - finds maybe love definitely death and some resurrection. Perverse unpredictable and in the tone of HAROLD AND MAUDE MIS-FITS holds all our sworn beliefs hostage to a revelation that we must be able to laugh at our human imperfections. Rich in metaphor and music MIS-FITS takes you on a charming joy ride.
