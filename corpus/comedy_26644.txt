This movie is the sequel of _Io Chiara e lo Scuro (1982)_. Francesco forgot billiards because now he lives with Chiara: "Billiards is jealous he explains if you love a woman you can pass your time on the green table". She is a musician and she would like to become famous. Francesco is different: he found in her the reason of his life. But when she accepts a job far from home Francesco comes back to billiards: he will meet again Chiara in Casablanca Morocco during a World Championship and in the magic of a mysterious bar called Ricks...
