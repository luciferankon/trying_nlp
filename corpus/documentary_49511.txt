A documentary about the life and career of British actor 'Frank Williams (I)' (qv). Williams recalls many anecdotes from his work on stage and screen as well as re-visiting the original locations used for the classic BBC sitcom Dad's Army. As well as featuring never-before-seen photos of _"Dad's Army" (1968)_ (qv) the documentary also provides a detailed look at his career as a playwright and his plans for the future.
