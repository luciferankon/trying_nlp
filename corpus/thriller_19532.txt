Allan goes hiking in the forest with his wife Samantha and she vanishes mysteriously. Alex Allan's drug-addicted anorexic sister-in-law moves in to find out what happened to Samantha. Meanwhile Allan keeps getting threatening phone calls from a mysterious woman so suffers from paranoia. A group of friends travel to a remote location for an intimate wedding. Unfortunately for them the romantic getaway turns into a nightmare as one by one the wedding party is stalked and killed. Leaving those alive to wonder who is after them and who will be next.
