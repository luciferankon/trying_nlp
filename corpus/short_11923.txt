Two Trumpet Players one Trumpet one The Little Princes Lunch-Box. Only one can get out of this confrontation alive. A duel between Art and Crime which Tromp a dreamer Trumpet Player is approached by AL Fame an opportunistic Trumpet Player asking if he can play for a while. Tromp waits for his Trumpet back holding AL Famas The Little Prince Lunch-Box. Does AL Fame resist the temptation? This short film is a free adaptation of an excerpt of the poem Fame's Penny-Trumpet of Lewis Carrol.
