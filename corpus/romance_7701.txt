When destiny unites during highschool these two teenagers who come from two very different backgrounds that is when a wave of craziness begins. Lorna the daughter of a really famous artist brought up in a luxurious environment where she always had everything falls in love with Goni a boy who lives in the suburban part of Tirana. He is a boy with the big dream of becoming a movie director but at the same time he has to work two jobs in order to maintain his family. A teenage flirt which turns into a passionate love full of adventures becomes the main cause of all the family contradictions and conflicts of a destroyed society and full of problems.
