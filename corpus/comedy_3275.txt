Garage Daze is the story of a former high school garage band who now find themselves in their late twenties early thirties and their lives are not turning out the way they dreamed. Lucas the only member of the band who is still living in their hometown has had enough of his life as a high school janitor. After deciding that the last moment of true happiness he experienced was when he had a band Lucas stages a fake 11 year class reunion. Lucas hopes to persuade his former band mates to restart the band. He makes a fatal error when he mistakenly invites the entire class instead of just his band mates. Lucas and friends must now face demons not only from their current lives but from the past as well. The drama and antics swell until it reaches it's pinnacle during a local battle of the bands. Will the band have it's encore or will they discover that the past is the past for a reason?
