Beyond The Headlines: Michael Jackson will give viewers of the Lifetime movie Searching for Neverland a chance to hear the story behind what made The King of Pop the icon that he was. Michael Jackson's career is unrivaled to this day by any other artist and his album Thriller remains the top selling album of all time 35 years after its release. This one-hour documentary includes never before seen footage of the Michael himself as well as interviews with those who knew him best from his sister La Toya Jackson and parents Joe and Katherine to his long-time friends Frank Cascio and famed choreographer Debbie Allen; from his bodyguards Bill Whitfield and Javon Beard to his personal makeup artist Karen Faye. Their combined stories will shed light on Michael's incredible rise to superstardom the way he handled the media's portrayal of him and his lifelong dream of becoming a father.
