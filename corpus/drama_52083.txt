Parsa is a story about a young man from Iran who travels to Australia to earn money for his father. His father is Bankrupt and his money was taken by scammers. With the help of Emrah his Turkish friend Parsa manages to settle down in Australia despite so many differences between his country and Australia. Parsa strugglesto find a job he falls in love and he faces so many challenges throughout his stay in Australia. He is conflicted between his love and missing his family. The movie shows that love conquers all and never dies and how the human spirit can triumph over adversity.
