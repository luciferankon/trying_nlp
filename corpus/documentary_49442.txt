Troy Andrews has been using all forms of drugs all his life but has become addicted to crack cocaine for the past 8 years. He tells the story of his struggle with drugs and what his family and girlfriend have gone through because of his drug use. Also telling the story of their loved ones using drugs are Micaiah Erhardt Chris Tozzo Cindy Harney and Tamara Gilman. This film was made in hopes of educating people about drugs and what they do to families and the people that us them.
