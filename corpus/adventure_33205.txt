Years ago there was a darkness that covered the land. The previous king your grandfather chose his eldest son to go and defeat it. Now the current king your father has called upon you to defeat the darkness that he was forced to defeat years ago. You are chosen for the kindness in your heart and the selflessness you show to all around you. Your brothers are in danger of becoming apart of the darkness. It sees their weaknesses and knows that with even one of them it is unstoppable. You must get stronger to defeat the darkness and save the kingdom and your family. Will you let history repeat itself? Or will you change it for the betterment of the future?
