For most of us life is little more than a daily routine; obtaining groceries and basic needs from the supermarket following news and world events on TV listening to our favorite music through headphone devices and coming across strange and eccentric people on our occasional travels. Yet life's driving forces are personal will and competency; our insight and self- determination become the deciding factors which will ultimately change the direction of our lives. And so the ultimate question becomes; will we set foot on the less travelled road of unusual experiences?
