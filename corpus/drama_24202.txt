When the love of your life comes back to you suddenly after struggling to move on with life you find yourself not exactly letting go of your past when it returns to you. Being in love with someone comes with a price in this case that price is passion with no regards of who and what maybe in the way of that. Kimberly and Taylor were college sweethearts when they fell madly in love with each other. Both women found themselves at odds when Kimberly moved away to take on her father's restaurant food chain business. As they find themselves reconnecting they discover their love has no boundaries and unapologetic.
