A rebellious teen threatened with expulsion from school is offered an alternative: an experimental program in which she would counsel troubled children. Initially rebelling from the concept she finds that her involvement lets her confront her own angers and eventually her family including a loving grandmother. With the help of a new friend she eventually turns away from her trouble-making boyfriend and makes a new life.
