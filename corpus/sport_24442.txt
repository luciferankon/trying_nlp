Total Carnage is arguably the best Muay Thai promotion ever held in Australia. In the headline event "The Elbow Master" Nathan Corbett takes on the star out of France Stephane "Ippo" Susperregui in defense of the WKN World heavyweight title. Furthermore Frank Giorgi squares off against Tobias Alexandersson of Sweden. The contest is for a vacant WKN Welterweight title. Total Carnage is produced by Cross Promotions. The promoter of the event is Ed Zouroudis. The championship supervisor is Parviz Iskenderov representing the World Kickboxing Network. The event aired on Fox Sports and is available on YouTube.
