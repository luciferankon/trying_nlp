Take a look at the life of music-industry sensation Sam Smith. Follow his path to stardom from his crucial formative years - to current international fame. Hear the story of how his failed romances that led to his universally adored ballads. Featuring the Tracks: "Time Won't Wait" "Momentarily Mine" "A Little Melancholy" "All This Madness" and "Out of our Heads". A new documentary on the rise to fame of Sam Smith - the British singer-songwriter with the unforgettable voice who has taken the music world by storm and recorded the new Bond theme song for 'Spectre'. Featuring music from his first album recordings before he signed to Universal this is the true story of his incredible rise to fame which Sam described as a Dream Come True.
