This beautifully presented documentary captures the excitement of Western Australia's biggest hotrod and custom car spectacular- 'MOTORVATION'. Held at the Quit Motorplex annually over 25000 spectators flock to see the event along with automotive entrant enthusiasts from around Australia. Presented on the DVD documentary is all the action from cars girls driving events dyno pavilion car judging audio competitions and of course the world famous burnouts including displays from the World Burnout Champion - Gary Myers. The DVD is packed with loads of bonus material in addition to the main feature including in-car camera burnouts full un-cut burnouts out-takes & a huge exclusive photo gallery.
