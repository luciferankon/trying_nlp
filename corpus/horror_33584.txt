The small Mid-western town of Rockford is still haunted by the series of bizarre killings. The voluptuous tempestuous vigilante Dr. Sylvia Trumbull is salivating revenge from the mysterious killer who has been terrorizing delectable women for years. Consequently The Pumpkinman has her as perfect mouthwatering prey along with a few others (hot teenyboppers and sexy hookers) as the showdown begins on Halloween.
