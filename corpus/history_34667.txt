LUPEO is based on true events of January 12 2010 the memorial of the tragic earthquake in Haiti. After receiving the tragic news of the 7.0 earthquakes the Lenor's attempt to connect with their loved ones in Port-au-Prince has taken more than a toll on them. With prayers patience and the help a survived family member they were able to locate some of the missing ones. The condition in which they were found will need more then just love and money to overcome this experience.
