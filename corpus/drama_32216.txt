Dhinveynuge Hithamaigaa is a story of two families. Naashidh is a carefree young man meets Nisha and falls in love and almost instantly asks her to marry him. Naheez is a single father who is looking after his daughter he lost his wife due to complications while delivering. In the meantime Naashidh meets an accident and is bed ridden and soon decides to divorce his wife Nisha because he has lost hope of ever walking. Soon Nisha who doesn't have any family is forced to find a job In the same time Naheez is in search of a helper to be with his daughter. Nisha come to know of this through a friend and when they meet Naheez went for a shock because she resembled his dead wife.
