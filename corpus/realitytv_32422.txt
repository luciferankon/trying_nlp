24 episodes filmed in a variety of locations - over 100 in all - and the highlights edited into themed shows; OAP clubbers fancy dress parties foam parties eccentrics piercings body art games sex school disco valentines Halloween. A very quick-paced sojourn through the annals of what is a unique culture's national pastime. People who have never been to the UK and believe the Brits to be a reserved or uptight bunch will find themselves shocked to see the regular antics of a nation adept in the art of party. Also the first series to feature the start of "The Dilemma Question"; a variation on the familiar phrase 'Hoson's Choice' where in this instance especially either choice is particularly unpleasant.
