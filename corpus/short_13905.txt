Narrated by Diane Lane. A Cinematic Celebration of our beautiful world featuring culture destinations and picturesque moments. Includes quotes from Nellie Bly who in 1889 courageously traveled around the world in 72 days - beating the previous record of 80 days. She did so despite resistance having been told that only a man could accomplish such a feat. Substance Over Hype Ritz-Carlton Reserve and Marriott Content Studio set out to create a tone poem that would inspire exploration contemplation and joy.
