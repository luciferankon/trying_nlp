May 15 1897 - Howard Lake MN Tom Boxell and his wife Lydia met a gruesome death - Both were struck repeatedly by an intruder - with the ax from the family woodpile. Ten people were accused of this horrible incident - sadly and perhaps unbelievably no one was ever convicted! This is the chilling tale of how the Boxell family and the community of Howard Lake coped with the accusations investigations and trials of this unthinkable crime - told through the collective voices of the Boxell siblings and the residents of Howard Lake.
