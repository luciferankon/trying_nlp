Documentary based on the Maria Catroppa story in which she was subjected to domestic abuse violence and ultimately her death by way of 126 stab wounds. This chilling real story takes a hard and honest look at the life of a warm and kind mother grand mother and contributing member of society. The re-enactments police and corner evidence as well as supporting testimonies help to lay the ground work bringing awareness to the Domestic Violence subject and sound the alarm for a call to action to bring change.
