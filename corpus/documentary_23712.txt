One Korea 3D (OK3D) is the first modern documentary to tell the story of both North and South Korea from an objective historical and cultural point of view to inspire peace for both Koreas. The documentary will culminate in one of the greatest peace concerts the world has ever seen where both countries will be brought together to celebrate the concept of One Korea. More than just a documentary One Korea 3D is an essential mission to promote understanding and peace between the two Koreas and the rest of the world through music and documentary.
