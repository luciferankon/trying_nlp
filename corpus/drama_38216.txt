One year after losing her husband and unborn child in a car wreck 24 year old ANGELA moves away to a small Texas town. There she meets 8 year old ELLIE who is being abused by her father BOBBY RAY. Unable to get any help from the authorities Angela beats Bobby Ray senseless and runs off with Ellie. On the road evading the police the two are helped by some and hindered by others as a maternal bond grows between them. Eventually captured Ellie is returned home and Angela to jail leading to a shocking and devastating end that is sure to leave the audience in stunned silence and as the title suggests emotionally Empty.
