We Intend To Cause Havoc is a feature length documentary on the life and times of Emmanuel "Jagari" Chanda lead singer of the most popular Zambian rock band of the 1970's WITCH. At the peak of their popularity the band often needed police to keep fans at bay while Jagari whose name is an Africanisation of Mick Jagger's riled up crowds by stage diving from balconies and dancing manically to the WITCH's blend of Psychedelic Rock and African rhythms. Today a man in his 60's Jagari spends his time mining gemstones in Zambia hoping to strike it rich - until very recently his music just a nostalgic memory of youth.
