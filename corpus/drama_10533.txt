This story is about an honest police officer Amit Sen and his wife Seema. Seema has to kill a smuggler Changez in self defence but she is not able to tell anything to Amit. Changez's boss Shamsher Singh harasses her for the diamonds that Changez has accidentally dropped in her house but she has no clue about. In due course Seema is arrested. Finally Amit catches Shamsher Singh and proves Seema's innocence at the court.
