A university student's home unexpectedly becomes epicenter for international students. Han Yuri (Park Ji Young) is a college student whose family suddenly faces a crisis when her father runs off with another woman. To be able to continue to live in the family's large home Yuri's mother decides to take in international students as boarders. These students from all over the world have different customs clashing cultures and aren't accustomed to eating Korean food which make it difficult for Yuri her mother and brother.
