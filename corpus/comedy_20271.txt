Return Home is the story of Jonathan Barker who returns to his home of Melancholy Falls NJ after five years away. An unknown entity beckons him back East in order to unravel the mystery of what his home town has become...or has always been. Reunited with his best friends Buddy and Ami Jonathan must discover the secrets of Melancholy Falls and why he was told to return home. Return Home is a serialized audio drama done in the style of radio shows of ages past. It is fully produced in stereo sound to make you feel like you are part of the action. Though you can listen however you'd like it's recommended you do so with headphones. Alone. In a darkened room.
