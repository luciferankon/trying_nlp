In Conwy Castle: Medieval Masterpiece we awaken this sleeping giant reconstructing every corner to show the brilliance of its design - from the sumptuous quarters intended for Edward's wife Queen Eleanor to the narrow passageways between the kitchen and the Great Hall. We also take a tour of the medieval town walls to gain a new perspective on Conwy Castle. Conwy Castle a World Heritage Site is a magnificent fortress in North Wales. Built by James Saint George for King Edward I it has towered over the medieval town of Conwy ever since.
