Two superstar LA cops are thrown a curve ball when their Captain sends them to Lagos Nigeria with a white 'facilitator' to join the new bi-lateral 'Crime Investigation Squad' (CIS). The pair are also reluctantly teamed up with two local hero cops from the streets of Las Gidi aka Lagos a beautiful criminal psychologist and a canine crime fighter named 'Bingo'! With a power cut or two thrown into the mix this outrageous comedy follows this unconventional team as they struggle to get along with each other while attempting to solve a local murder and bring down the notorious Lagosian gangster known as 'The Tax Man'!
