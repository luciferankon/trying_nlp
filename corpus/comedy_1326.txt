A movie about two brothers from Brooklyn. Travis and Bryant. One is a successful business man and the other is a not so successful street hustler. As with most odd couples one is always getting into trouble. The other has to come and bail him out. When a hustle on a local thug named Tank backfires all hell breaks loose. It's up to Travis and Bryant to work together to solve their latest dilemma and in the process they gain respect for each other's life.
