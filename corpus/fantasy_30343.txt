From the world of 'Tyal the Daughter of Flames' comes the epic saga 'The Blessed the Cursed and the Forgotten One'. Thousands of years ago wizard decided to capture all of magic into one stone. The Daughter of Flames and her seven warriors defeated him but the stone was lost and magic never returned. Till today. In the last decades magic made a sudden return bringing chaos with it. Three people were born with special abilities meant to restore balance and fix the instabilities of magic. This is where the story begins.
