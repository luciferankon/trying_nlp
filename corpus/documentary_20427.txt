Approximately 2000 summer cinemas have existed in Turkish Cinema's last century. From all of the summer cinemas which people from that time enjoyed the most in summer only three remain active. Tarik Vardar and his friend struggle to keep the summer cinema culture alive despite all of its difficulties. Despite his old age Tarik Vardar who struggles to keep cinema alive who alongside Güntay Tasçi who quit mechanical engineering in order to be in cinema and the one of the oldest machinists in Turkish Cinema and a 1972 model sinema machine Elif Cinema and its inhabitants are told. The documentary is about a group of cinema lovers trying to keep the last summer cinema alive.
