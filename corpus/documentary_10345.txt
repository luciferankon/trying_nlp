According to studies of the London School of Economics the happiest people in the world live in Bangladesh one of the poorest countries in the world. With this background the documentary shows the life of four inhabitants of Dacca the capital city: Hassan a youngster who tries to get a job in the local cinema industry; Kutti who abandoned her job as a maid to become a worker; Milli a successful entrepreneur who divides her life between London and Dacca; and finally Russel a boy that drives rikishas and dreams of becoming an actor.
