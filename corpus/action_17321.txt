The year 2636 - The world has undergone many cycles each reborn from each apocalypse. For now this future reality has settled back into the times of the old west. Firing weapons have been extinct for many hundreds of years and crime is rampant in this dystopian future. Stitch a 'Hunter' and expert swordsman survives by hunting down the criminals wanted by the law. Gunpowder is suddenly re-invented and the Hunters have now become the hunted as Stitch's past and future collide. Who has developed this old but new technology and who is now pulling the strings in this post apocalyptic world? It has not only become a fight for survival but a race to stop to the end of the world......again.
