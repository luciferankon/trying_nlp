On trial for the death of her children Lilith Manes contests her innocence as her case draws Casey Anthony-style coverage. When she claims the shapeshifting Chupacabra killed her children the media labels her a modern day embodiment of the Mexican myth La Lorona "The Weeping Woman." Manes hires Mexican luchadore El Guey "The Dude" to take care of business but will he be able to vindicate Manes or will the case hang in the balance of truth lies legends and myths?
