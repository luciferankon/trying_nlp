Terry Willis grew up like many youths with dreams of fast cars fine women and fat pockets. Then just like that opportunity came knocking on his door. After being arrested with 2 ki's of cocaine Terry is offered freedom and fast cash or 15 years in prison. Which would you choose? It doesn't take long for greed to rear its ugly head causing Terry to make a costly side deal that puts him in over his head. After Juana his childhood friends makes Terry a proud new father Terry realizes that there's more to life than material things but it's too late now he's in too deep with no way out....He's a Mark.
