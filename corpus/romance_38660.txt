Each year in June Bavarian housemother Elke Richter visits family in Halle in the GDR. There she meets family friend Gregor Pohl an equally married carpenter and starts a secret affair. After her family cease the annual visits due to the husband's promotion in the communist regime the adulterous couple arranges to meet on 'spearte' holidays as in Prague. After Gorbachev's glasnost leads to the fall of the Iron Curtain wall everything changes but Gregor chooses to emigrate to Canada.
