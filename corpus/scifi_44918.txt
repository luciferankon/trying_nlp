When down-on-his-luck reporter Andy Moore stumbles on the story of his career he quickly realizes that what everyone believes to be a visitation from another world is in fact the beginning of The End. The thrilling episodes will keep viewer enthralled from week to week. COMES THE END... is in the desirable Science Fiction genre. The story begins in the years know as the Last Days of the End Times. Andy and Stephanie are swept up in this thrilling account of two people going through these times. They encounter circumstances beyond human imagination as they do battle with forces of evil. The story is delivered in a sixteen part episodic style. Thus fulfilling two seasons of adventure. Every episode leaves the viewer hanging with the desire for more.
