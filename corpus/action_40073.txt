Arjun is a young man who will always defend his family even at the risk of his own life. As Arjun and his twin sister Meenakshi are finishing their college education Meenakshi reveals that she and classmate Uday have expressed their love for each other. However Uday's parents plan to marry him to an heiress. Nevertheless Arjun vows to arrange the marriage for his sister and her beloved. Soon however Arjun isn't just fighting for his twin's happiness but for her life! When Meenakshi becomes the target of her murderous in-laws Arjun is forced to take up the machete and go to battle.
