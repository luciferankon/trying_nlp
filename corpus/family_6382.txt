Padmavati / Paddu (Hebah Patel) is the adored daughter of Raghava Rao (Rao Ramesh). A Prophesier prophesied Raghava Rao that if he refuses to accept any of her daughter's wish then she will leave him. As a result from child he fulfilled all her wishes and never opposed to her any wishes. When Paddu grew up his father select a guy for her to marry. But she said that she will choose her own husband and if only if she failed to find a suitable match for herself then she will marry the person that her father suggested for her. As always Raghava Rao agrees and Paddu begins her search.
