In this film/ music essay hybrid by Marcus Cotten the average day is under examination with the intervention of a little bit of imagination. Through melody  images color and daily natural sound The Globe As A Graph constructs a look at the connecting world we live in. Filmed in 25 non-linear sections The Globe As A Graph examines subjects such as : language growth time man and celebration ; color business city ocean discovery and light. The music composed and performed by Cotten was created simultaneously with the film. The Globe As A Graph employs a system to create an emotional compass for the viewer using 3 primary colors : Red for dark outlook Yellow for uncertainty and Blue for optimism.
