Russian jewellery art has more than 2000 years of fascinating history. Accurate delicate scrupulous job of the masters of Old and Imperial Russia search of inspiration for the new ideas during modern time discoveries and disappointments - all that has left indelible mark in the world art culture. Prosperity decline and renaissance... In the documentary "The Path of Inspiration" you will learn how the approach to the jewellery art has been changing in Russia since the beginning till now through the events which caused its oblivion and its new revival.
