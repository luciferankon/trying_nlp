Riley and Scott have been trying to make a great Emory-centric comedy series. They haven't had much luck and are looking for something to give their show the spark that it needs. The fledgling writers decide that a cameo by Dooley could take a show to the next level but there's one problem Dooley's arrogant agent. Just when Riley and Scott have given up hope yet again a surprise appearance by another great Emory figure may cause their fortunes to change.
