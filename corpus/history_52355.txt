Raja Harishchandra is a great king of Ayodhya. He is staunch follower of Satya Dharma. He adheres to path of truth no matter what. His strength to abide by truth is put to test by great Rishi Vishwamitra. He is destined to lose everything including his kingdom wife child even his independence. Will Raja Harishchandra stick to truth and his duties or give up? It is a story of tussle between two great tapasvis Raja Harishchandra and Maharshi Vishwamitra.
