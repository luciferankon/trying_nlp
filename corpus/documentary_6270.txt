In 2010 as part of the official celebrations for the Bicentenary of the Mexican Independence the Mexican Federal Government produced a sports festival with the approval of the International Olympic Committee: The Bicentennial Olympic Festival. A two day event which took place in October 9th and 10th of 2010. This documentary explores the 'making of' the festival from the perspective of the athletes and the production staff of the Festival from the pre production and planning of the event to the culmination of it.
