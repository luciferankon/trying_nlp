Comedian/filmmaker Camille Solari spends the last year interviewing the legendary musical icon James Brown before his untimely death on Christmas 2006. The documentary includes interviews with his band members and many celebrity musicians who have been influenced by James Brown including Patti Labelle Chuck D (Public Enemy) Doug E Fresh Emcee Lyte Bobby Brown Fergie Wil.I.Am.
