A cello case becomes an instrument of terror for a photographer convinced he murdered his cheating wife. Chinese film star Ge You (Butterfly Smile) is the schizophrenic haunted by the case which he may have used to dispose of his wife's corpse. The lines between the subconscious and conscious the dead and the living horrifically blur in this intense atmospheric psychological thriller. Drenched with suspense and black humor SUFFOCATION's twisted nightmare journey through the emotional strangulation of madness will make you gasp!
