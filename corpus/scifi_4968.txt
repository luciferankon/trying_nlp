Project Mammoth: Awakening is a live-action web series from award-winning filmmaker Nathan Hale set in the high-tech world of the Domers from the popular comic Project Mammoth: Dying Light. Michael Bongara a lead security analyst from the Houston dome has been conscripted into the dome which houses the world's brightest scientists. These scientists are part of a top-secret project known as Project Mammoth and they're on the verge of restoring the earth to it's former glory - if the Naturalists don't blow it up first.
