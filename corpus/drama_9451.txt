39-year-old Jacob an upcoming musician has locked himself up with his music and Nescafé in his two bedroom Vesterbro-flat. One day Jacob's best friend Sřren moves in with him due to Sřren's relationship being 'on stand-by' and this is a turning point in Jacob's life. Time goes by with the boys giving each other a hard time and soon Sřren is moving out again. Once again Jacob is on his own alone in the flat. But this time he's ready to open up to his art and to love.
