Aspiring models in a Soho photo studio aiming for the centerfold are getting molested tortured and killed by a mysterious perpetrator who hides its identity by wearing a cloth bag over its head. A number of suspects are being investigated by the police. Could it be the phony talent agent with the casting couch? Or the photographer himself? Or perhaps the Bible thumping old aunt enraged over her niece's plans to expose her commodities to the world? Lots of unexpected nudity intimate situations and beautiful actresses parading in this ultra low budget cult/horror video.
