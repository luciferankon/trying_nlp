Follow Amelia's world a YouTube and Instagram celebrity exploited online and blackmailed to continually expose herself. Her life falls apart as she is mercilessly bullied online and in real life by classmates from her refusal to continue with the demands of her blackmailer. Learn the dangers that lurk behind the keyboard find out how one moment defined a young girls life spreading to encompass the lives of her family and friends and her final call for help. Explore the murky world of cappers and how nothing online stays out of the "real" world. Inspired by true events around well known cases of cyber bullying such as Ryan Halligan Megan Meier Jessica Logan Tyler Clementi and Amanda Todd.
