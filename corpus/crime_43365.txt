Former centerfold Ella Arkins returns to her hometown Detroit Michigan to have a go at teaching science forensics at Gross Point High School. After coming up short chasing her dreams in LA she takes away a brutal drug addiction on heroine. This come back tale meets dark redemption meets investigative lucidity. Ella fights to take her life back in her early 30's she must face a dark cloud of depression and Obsessive Compulsive Disorder while putting the pieces of her life back together.
