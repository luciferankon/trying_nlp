It began with the beat of the drum. With the beat came a voice for those who without one. From this voice came a movement. Overcoming the odds the originators of hip hop took their music from block parties of New York City streets to world wide radio waves. During the early years the music and message reached new heights by exploring humanity politics and street life while keeping it real and having fun. But what ever happened to hip hop? Currently the most pervasive music worldwide its roots have been forgotten its message perverted. With hip hop in the spotlight it's time to put it back on track. This documentary presents views from hip hop founders contributors and artists in an attempt to return its audience to the four principles: Peace Love Unity and Having Fun.
