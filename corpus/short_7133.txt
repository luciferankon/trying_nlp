The story centers around Sophie and Josephine and their grieving process over the recently deceased Tristan on the day of his funeral. Both struggle with the life-altering event in different ways but ultimately lean on each other for support. On the day of the postponed memorial service for Tristan both women find themselves face-to-face with their inner demons. Dealing with Tristan explores the levels of the grieving process as we watch Sophie and Joe come to terms with their loss.
