A Harvard student trying to get into one of the most exclusive and only the elite final clubs on the campus. Christopher Morris gambles his $100000 dollar Harvard tuition for a chance at a membership but during this high-stakes game of poker he soon realizes that maybe being a member of the club isn't all as cut out as he had hoped. He starts to think back about his grandfather who taught him poker and upon winning the tournament is faced with the decision does he turn over his winnings for a seat or does he use the money to help his family and more importantly his grandfather.
