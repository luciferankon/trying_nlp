'Encountered on Saffron Agenda?'; is based on investigative documentation of encounters of Sameer Khan Pathan (22 October 2002) Sadik Jamal (13 January 2003) Ishrat Jahan-Javed Seikh (16 June 2004) and Shorabuddin Seikh (26 November 2005) all happened in Gujarat India. Those killed in them were said to have on a mission to kill the then Chief Minister of Gujarat Narendra Modi (now India's Prime Minister) who had allegedly organised the 2002 genocide of Muslims in the state after Godhra train burning incident. Film tries to find out the truth behind Police stories and politics of encounters in Gujarat.
