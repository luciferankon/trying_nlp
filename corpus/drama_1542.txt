Willie Francis Must Die Again is a feature film and a true story about a 16 year old African-American boy in Louisiana who was wrongfully charged with the murder of a Caucasian man and sentenced to the electric chair in 1946 However - The electrocution was not successful and Willie did not die. A young attorney Bertrand took on Willies case and brought it all the way to the Supreme court Willie was denied justice and executed a 2nd time.
