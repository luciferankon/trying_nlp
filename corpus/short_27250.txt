A powerful and hilarious mockumentary about our culture's obsession with gender self-promotion and the Internet Little Faggot of Jesus tells the story of one man's quest for fame love and self-fulfillment. Mari Conita DeJesus (literal translation: Little Faggot of Jesus) tells the story of a Struggling female impersonator with a brilliant strategy for remaking him/herself-into a household icon: before an audience of millions of fascinated Internet users Mari Conita DeJesus is going to have a sex change Operation.
