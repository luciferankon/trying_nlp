His trance is broken by the arrival of the barman's boyfriend who is also young and pretty but obviously more confident than the naive young Johan. At first he's amazed at their sensuous kissing across the bar and then he's jealous. Johan contemplates leaving when a gorgeous hustler walks in. Johan not knowing he's a hustler decides to stay to see if he can attract his attention... What follows is a chain of desire as Johan experiences real passion for the first time and eight gorgeous guys join him in a chain reaction of heated passions.
