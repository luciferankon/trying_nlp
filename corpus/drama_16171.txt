There are two kinds of world - the one we dream about and the one we're in. The Kodra's live somewhere In Between. This candidly intimate story follows the dizzying effects of dislocation loss of identity unrequited dreams foolish pride and bad choices. The richly complex characters and finely woven plot evoke moments of humour and high spirits touching romantic love and fierce family loyalty to underscore the harsh realities of their life. With passports in hand and money in their pocket the idealistic Kodra family embarks on a new life in Canada-a life packed with unrealistic dreams. Upon arrival they set out to conquer but are completely unprepared for the series of ill-fated events that follow. Willful and inflexible to alternative ways of thinking their life begins to spiral out of control.
