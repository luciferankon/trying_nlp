A sci-fi romantic comedy which follows Clark (Evan Gaustad) a late 20s slacker that has reached a level of complacency in life and work. He especially thinks he's set when he meets LISA (Brigitte Hagerman) a fellow comic book geek who encourages him to skip an important meeting at work and quit trying so hard. Just then Clark meets another girl - Kelly (Cheryl Texiera) who makes some pretty shocking claims about her origins and forces him to make big decisions in life and work. Clark will have to decide who is his future girlfriend.
