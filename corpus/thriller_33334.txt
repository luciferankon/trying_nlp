The Shaws need an escape. So Ben takes his wife Penny up to his mountain house for a weekend getaway in the vast Appalachian mountains. After a drunken night Ben awakens sometime the next morning...only not with his wife by his side...and not in his house. He must put clues together as to what happened the night before all while adapting and overcoming strange obstacles that wait before him in the beautifully harsh Appalachian mountain range.
