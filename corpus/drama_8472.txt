A True-life story of the complete deliverance from life's battles. Linda ('Kristen Quintrall' (qv)) the daughter of a wealthy family is left broken by spiritual forces beyond her control. While working at a night club Linda meets Steve ('Michael Madsen') an aspiring writer Steve who in his search for the "Meaning of Life" gets pulled into a world of drugs and spiritual darkness propagated by Katrine ('Jane Santos') the nightclub owner. Linda begins to see beyond Katrine's world with the knowledge she receives from a total stranger. With a fearless attitude she conquers the darkness in her own life and unknowingly changes the course of many lives along the way. A fearless spiritual awakening that shows forth the goodness of God in a dark and perverse world.
