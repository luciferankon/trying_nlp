Al Barnum is an Afro-American man who grew up in the slums of Philadelphia. First working as a teacher he later began driving luxury limousines and within a few years built up his own successful company. After driving limousines for over 15 years he discovered a new goal. He started helping kids who are in a similar situation as he was stay on the right way. Experience one day in the life of this wonderful example of the American Dream come true in a fascinating documentary.
