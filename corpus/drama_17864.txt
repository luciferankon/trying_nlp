Headstrong teen Angel Rodriguez channels her hopes for her future into her talent for painting. But when Miguel Badalin - from the notorious graffiti crew Reyes Del Norte - opens her eyes to the underground world of graffiti she's blown away by the aerosol art. Soon she's running with Miguel's crew pushing her skills to the limit and beginning to emerge as the artist she always dreamed she could be. But Angel must find a way to avoid Miguel's dangerous charm and focus on what she cares about most before the artist inside of her can truly break free.
