A group of students start their college experience with a "Frat Night" initiation in a haunted hotel known for murders and mayhem since the turn of the century. Beautiful thrill seekers encounter more than they bargained for when harmless pranks and sexy endeavors erupt in a murderous spree. In this five-star hotel unsuspecting youngsters are joined by a ghostly apparition two escaped felons from the most notorious prison and a ruthless bloodthirsty murderer. As these worlds collide hopeful survivors face an unbearable choice: escape fight back or play along. Gorgeous coeds strive to make it through the "Frat Night" in one piece facing an initiation from hell that just might thrill them to death.
