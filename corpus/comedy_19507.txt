Dude Where's my Chutzpah?" is a comedic web series which follows Jessie on her adventure to discover what it means to be Jewish. From the underground Israeli club scene to the front of the picket line to the depths of the Kabbalah Jessie soon discovers the plethora of ways to experience Judaism. In addition to her hilarious adventures she also taps into the spiritual realm and learns how her soul can flourish within the tangible world. Watch as Judaism shows Jessie that the journey is the destination and that every answer is just a question in disguise.
