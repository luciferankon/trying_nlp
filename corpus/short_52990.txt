25 Missed Calls is an urban take on the myth of Sisyphus set in Brooklyn. Dom an alcoholic reaching the peak of crisis has been drinking all night. It's early morning and the bartender has had enough. Thrown from the bar Dom finds himself bruised bloody and alone when he sees that he's missed 25 calls from his girlfriend. This is the story of one man's struggle between a dark and dirty reality and a sensual mysterious dream as he desperately tries to get back home.
