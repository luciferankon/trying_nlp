Top Gun: Hard Lock is a 2012 video game released for the PS3 Xbox 360 and PC by developer 505 Games and published by Paramount Digital Entertainment. It was released on March 13 2012 in the United States and April 5 2012 in Europe. It features Maverick Iceman Merlin and Jester as supporting characters 14 missions and planes. It also involves new characters a young pilot named Spider who is similar to Maverick his wingman Deadeye and Fury who is similar to both Iceman and Charlie as both an rival and a love interest for Spider.
