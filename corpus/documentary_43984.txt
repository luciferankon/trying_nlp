Everybody has a dream but few actually live them. This documentary highlights the creative and mental processes and the many layers of persistence and determination needed to live your dreams. It showcases filmmaker Andy Swan's body of work before and with AREA 9 Productions as well as features interviews with some of the industry's top filmmakers and actors talking about their growth experiences in the business and how Andy's drive and determination has allowed him to live his dreams. (Featuring Joe Mantegna Paget Brewster AJ Cook Matthew Gray GublerShemar Moore & Kirsten Vangness)
