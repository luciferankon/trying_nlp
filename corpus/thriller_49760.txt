Gumbo is a found footage film that takes place primarily in an unnamed town in Mississippi. It revolves round the journey of documentarian James Gloster and his reunion with his younger brother Adam. The film takes place after James unexpectedly leaves the filming of the third season of his public access show. He seeks out Adam in an attempt to show what his viewers what home life is like. Once he arrives with his cameraman they find out that something isn't quite right at the residence of Adam Gloster.
