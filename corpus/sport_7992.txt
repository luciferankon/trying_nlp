They Call Me Termite The Movie is an inspiring tale based on a true story about Maurice "Termite" Watkins a boxer from Texas. Maurice "Termite" Watkins was a middle aged former amateur and professional boxer who volunteered to go to Iraq in 2003 to kill bugs. In Iraq to serve his country he accepted the challenge "a one-in-a-million chance" to get an Iraqi boxer into the Athens Olympiad. An unlikely hero in war-ravaged Iraq he and his team faced and overcame incredible obstacles while training and attempting to qualify for the Olympics.
