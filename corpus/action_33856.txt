As book story written in the 1940 the story of the Monkey King and the quest to find the kingdom in the west that later became a series in 1986. In the year 2000 came the movie as a tribute the story that has been going since the 80s and 90s. Li Po Lung return as the Monkey King to finally find their kingdom in the west. As his allies he go through his journey with Pigsy and the human Monk.
