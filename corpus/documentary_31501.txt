A documentary that focuses on how the Israeli Occupation affects the Palestinian population of today. The story is told by Christian-Palestinians to include the Mayor of Bethlehem Mrs.Vera Baboun. Their interviews shed light on a different perspective of the conflict that we don't hear about in the American media. Come and witness the Christmas celebration and hopeful spirit of those in the walled city of Bethlehem. This film conveys a message of peace along with an inclusive solution to the Israeli/Palestinian conflict.
