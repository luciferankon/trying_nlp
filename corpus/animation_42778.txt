After arriving in Hollywood to pursue their illustrious career of 'making it' in Hollywood Belvy Winchester E-Dog and Phillip hear that the 'W' is the HOTTEST place to stay in town so... they post up behind the 'W' of the Hollywood sign and embark on their adventure. Broadcasting his morning radio show 'WFLY' from atop the city Belvy and his three friends engage with the Hollywood elite (and not-so-elite) in their daily struggle of climbing that slippery ladder to success and we see Tinsel Town from a place that only a 'bird trying to make it big' can... and where it's a lot easier to take a dump on things...
