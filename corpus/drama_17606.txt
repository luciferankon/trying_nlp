Lucia's father is dead her mother is in a mental hospital and she's living with her aunt. Now she's been kicked out of school-again. Making her way through the world with only a book a zippo lighter a pocketful of stolen licorice and the striking intelligence that she tries to hide Lucia spends her days riding the bus to visit her mother. But when she discovers that her new school has a secret Arson Club she's willing to do anything to be a part of it. As Lucia's fascination with the Arson Club grows her story becomes one of misguided friendship and ultimately destruction.
