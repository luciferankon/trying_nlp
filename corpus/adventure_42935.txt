The Keen brothers JACK & TOBY are "invited" by the Merlinite Wizard MERLIN MONROE to help rid "Lower Olliestan" of the insidious villain PHARAOH CLEOPATRICK IX. They must struggle to survive in the last place on Earth where Magick rules until they can defeat the Pharaoh in Wizardly Combat. To do this one of them must become a wizard while the other deals with the multitude of weird characters that populate this fantastic land. They meet CEDRIC the feral boy KING HOOZON 1st. the glamorous zombie girl GUENEVERE OF THE GRAVE and an obnoxious pixie among others. Witness the final battle in the darkest depths of the foul Pit of Snothgor.
