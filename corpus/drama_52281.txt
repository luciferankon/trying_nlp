3AM Films and Misfits of Film present Texoma. Rancher Clint Forest's girlfriend Kat has dumped him again. She's fed up with his easy going nature and inattentiveness. Clint's mind is elsewhere. At his ex-wife's insistence he's looking into the suspicious death of an old friend. Clint's problems are compounded by rival rancher Nick Grandfield who wants Clint's land. The windy plains of Texoma are filled with clues for one death and are ready to be the place for murder.
