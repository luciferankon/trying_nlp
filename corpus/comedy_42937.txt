Jodi Collins a poised expert Realtor was supposed to have a reality show where she fixes and sells homes. But to appeal to a "wider demographic" Jodi now has to work with her brother Jasper a renowned medium and her longtime rival and fix up houses on the market... that are also haunted. With her show turned from open houses to haunted houses will Jodi be able to share the spotlight with Jasper and his team of ghost hunters? Or will this deal go dead?
