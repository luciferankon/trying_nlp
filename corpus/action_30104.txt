When demolition expert Bard Clemens joins a scientific expedition on the beautiful uninhabited island of San Crispin he has no inkling that the project is destined for disaster when it unknowingly stumbles upon a secret Soviet military operation code-named "Delos." The expedition is attacked by a ruthless assassination squad and the survivors begin a desperate fight to stay alive! With no weapons and no help in sight Bard Deni Trion the beautiful leader of the doomed expedition together with Luis Vasquez are pitted against a team of cold-blooded killer commandos with one deadly objective: to protect operation "Delos" no matter how many lives it costs
