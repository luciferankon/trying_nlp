With a refreshing original new take on the format "Jocking Around" is the first in a series of entertaining documentaries from Elite Sports Documentaries Inc. The best athletes in the world both active and retired ranging from Gold Medal Olympians to World Series Champions recount their personal favorite humorous anecdotes from their careers. These candid stories offer an insightful and entertaining glimpse into the otherwise private world of some of the world's greatest and most celebrated athletes.
