Ever dream of quitting your day job and taking the trip of a lifetime? Ever wish you could ditch the guidebook and go straight to where the locals go? For each episode of This Is My City Tim and Thomas find a local who is willing to show them what life is really like in Lisbon Beirut Beijing and five other global metropolises - Over the course of a madcap weekend they film everything that transpires no matter how dangerous bizarre or borderline illegal. This Is My City is a show about what cities mean to the people who live in them brought to you in the most personal way. It's not about seeing the sights; it's about seeing the soul.
