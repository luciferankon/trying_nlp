A sequel to Abduction (2017). The galactic empire is under increased threat from the alien greys. They dispatch Thorson to locate the aliens lost ship on earth. But the Hive Queen has returned and is hell bent on inseminating humans to create a hybrid race of alien/humans. A group of ex-abductees are also hunting for the lost ship intent upon revenge. Ultimately each party clash in a horror comedy following on from the style and mayhem of Abduction (2017).
