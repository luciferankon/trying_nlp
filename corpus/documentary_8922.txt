Humayun Azad Bohumatrik Jotirmoy is an interview with Humayun Azad (1947-2004) who was a Bangladeshi author poet scholar and linguist. He wrote more than seventy titles. He is regarded and honored as the most powerful and influential writer in the history of modern Bengali literature. His writings against religious fundamentalism received both positive and negative reviews. He was threatened and attacked by Islamist fundamentalist groups for his writings.
