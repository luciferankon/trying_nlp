EVE Online is a massive multiplayer online game which is set in a world of galactic magnitude and governed by a hyper capitalistic economy. Your aim is to establish yourself as a major mover and shaker trusted by your friends and respected by your enemies. EVE Online is now a single online game world with more than 5000 solar systems connected by a series of jump gates. You can be almost anything you want to be from pirate to military leader to CEO of your own corporation.
