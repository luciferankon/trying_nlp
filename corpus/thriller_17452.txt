Katie and Guy take a sea cruise in their sailboat and discover a couple on a wrecked boat victims of a brutal attack by their crew. They take Eliza and Nick on board. The new boarders appear nervous about returning to Honolulu and when the boat's engines die and other little incidents occur Katie and Guy start wondering whether the shipwrecked couple are what they appear to be. A middle aged couple go sailing in the Pacific in an attempt to save their marriage. They come across a motionless boat which has a starving man and woman aboard. They have no option but to let them board their own sailboat unaware that they happen to be rather dangerous.
