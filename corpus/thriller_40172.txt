The Berbatovs are Nottinghams most successful crime family but with rival gang led by Jackson Reid closing in on one side and the police on the other their time may have come to pass. When 'The Six' Stefan Berbatovs trusted captains are slaughtered he must attempt to escape whilst he can leading his wife and son across a city fraught with danger.
