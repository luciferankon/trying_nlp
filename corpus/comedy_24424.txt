Carson was born with triangle hands thus his friends calling him "Carson Trianglehands" He worked at a burger restaurant before he quit. He struggles to work which leads him to losing one of his hands. He becomes the best cello player and becomes world famous. After he quit that he goes to the birth defect hospital his doctor puts an orange in a microwave and then Carson gets his triangle hands back. He goes back to the burger restaurant to become the world's best chef.
