2 SHOTS OF TEQUILA tells the story of an emotional and extraordinary encounter between Dom and Avi two homeless men on an average day in the capital of the American dream Los Angeles. The film is inspired by the reality of the over 500000 people living on the streets of America. AVI once a very powerful executive who dreamed of one day becoming a painter; DOM homeless by choice has been carrying a strong burden since he began his new lifestyle 17 years ago. These two unlikely heroes embark on a journey to find a bottle of Don Julio Blanco Tequila to warm up their hearts and at the same time all of ours. AVI and DOM develop a powerful story about life and their love for it. The surprising path of AVI and DOM is a humorous reflection of the infinite capacity of humans to adapt and survive.
