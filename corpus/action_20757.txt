The year is 3587 conflict spans the stars. Huge robot armies are locked in an enduring stalemate. At the center is a team of elite mercenaries fighting for the spoils of war: The Awesomenauts. Pitting teams of three against one another Awesomenauts will thrust players into the shoes of genetically re-engineered cowboys alien frog-rappers crazed Russian labmonkeys and many more unique characters.
