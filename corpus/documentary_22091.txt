WELLNESS FOR LIFE is a television series that explores the world of health from western naturopathic and eastern philosophies. Created by Ashley Gracile and produced by GPI Content Corporation Wellness for Life provides treatment options for ailments that we all suffer from occasionally - like headaches cough and colds to more serious illnesses like cancer WELLNESS FOR LIFE deals with each of them from the perspective of these three philosophies - providing a forum which gives the viewer a balanced approach to treatment options. There are so different types of treatments and health care options available to viewers WELLNESS FOR LIFE wades through all the research experts and philosophies to show the viewer real options for their symptoms.
