Nancy Scorggs the daughter of the owner of a once famous but now struggling hotel hatches a plan to draw in new customers. She picks up Peter Alstyne a young man following his doctor's orders of a strict diet and a relaxing vacation at the train station and convinces him--using the philosophy of Christian Science--to disregard his doctor's orders and stay at their hotel and eat all he wants. Soon he and Nancy fall in love and the hotel begins to pick up business again. But soon Peter receives a letter that changes everything for both he and Nancy.
