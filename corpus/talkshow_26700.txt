Every episode has a Style Theme that best represents that Style Icon And our gorgeous host - international designer - Pria Kataaria Puri makes them reveal their style secrets in a fun-filled episode. Featuring for the first time on Indian television Grey Goose Born Stylish engages iconic stars of the high fashion world - like the legendary Jean Paul Gaultier the iconic Fendi scion Ilaria Venturini Fendi Anna Zegna of the Ermenegildo Zegna family among others Massimiliano Giornetti from Salvatore Ferragamo - in a special chat around their work around India and around Bollywood.
