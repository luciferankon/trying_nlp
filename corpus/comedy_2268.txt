The Epic Concept is the jam band to watch and Rachel an inexperienced filmmaker wants to document their rise to stardom. She attends a 3-day jam band festival bringing her buttoned-up boyfriend Evan along to run sound. When Rachel's camera and equipment goes missing Rachel tries to make the most of their weekend and enjoy all the music--and recreational drugs--the festival has to offer. Meanwhile Evan is on a mission to infiltrate the jam band scene he despises and find the missing camera. Complicating things is the presence of Rachel's ex-boyfriend Mike a non-stop party animal bent on winning her back. A deadpan comic tale about the hazards of judging others by their appearances and the impossibility of recapturing the past.
