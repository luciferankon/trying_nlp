'Melvyn Bragg' (qv) presents the first in-depth look at the science-fiction adventure series _"Doctor Who" (1963)_ (qv). The documentary includes clips of stories featuring all of the first four Doctors interviews current Doctor 'Tom Baker (I)' (qv) and producer 'Philip Hinchcliffe' (qv) hears the views of fans of the series of all ages and goes behind the scenes of the most recent serial _"Doctor Who" (1963) {The Talons of Weng-Chiang: Part One (#14.21)}_ (qv).
