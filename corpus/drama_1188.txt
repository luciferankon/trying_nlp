When their son Noel died it is as if Albert (Albert Martinez) and Sara (Liezel Martinez) died with him. Their happy home became an empty mausoleum. But their uneasy tranquility is about to be disturbed by Ariel (John Pratts) a family friend's precocious son. Ariel is to stay with them for several weeks while his parents are finalizing their divorce. Will Ariel's arrival finally by the impetus that forces Albert and Sara to confront their pain re-examine their lives and finally accept their son's death and move on?
