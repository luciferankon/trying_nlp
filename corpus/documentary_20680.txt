Three Jewish Orthodox immigrant families from varying socioeconomic backgrounds were interviewed for this program; chiefly the fathers of these families: Rabbi Max D. Raiskin (immigrant from the US) David Azran (Immigrant from Morocco) and Binyamin Moshiashvili (Immigrant from Georgia). The documentary follows their struggles with adjusting to a new environment and culture a new country and displays their differing opinions on immigration absorption society religion and politics.
