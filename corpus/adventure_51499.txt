Monroe's Mother had always been much better at starting things then finishing them. When she died she left over 300 canvases not one of them finished. Along with Monroe who felt pretty unfinished himself. The Orphanage allowed him to keep only one painting. And so he chose the Unfinished Swan" that had always been his mothers favorite. But that night he woke up to find that the Swan had disappeared. So he took his mothers Silver Brush and followed the footprints of the Swan to a small door he hadn't seen before..." From there Monroe enters a different world that was once ruled by a king. he finds out more and more of what happened to this once great kingdom... And its King...
