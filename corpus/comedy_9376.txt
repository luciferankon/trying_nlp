Two slackers accidentally get control of a top-secret drug and are pursued by HITMEN. They hide out on a bus filled with a FRATERNITY (K.O.K - Kappa Omega Kappa) and a SORORITY (T.I.T.S - Theta Ipsolon Theta Sorority) headed to a Ski Resort for a Winter Carnival. In disguise in DRAG they get mistaken for a couple of female Ski Instructors on the mountain and comedy ensues. "Snowbunnies" is a romantic-comedy/action film with the backdrop of extreme skiing and snowboarding.
