This documentary explores the making of the 1972 cult film "STANLEY". Features exclusive interviews with director William Grefe' screenwriter Gary Crutcher star Chris Robinson and co-star Steve Alaimo as well as rare "behind the scenes" photographs and 16mm film footage (from the director personal archives). For the first time in over 35 years you will learn the shocking story behind the making of this drive-in classic from those who experienced it first hand.
