Thomas Caine; a successful real estate developer husband and the father of two. Thomas is a family man who when not at work loves spending time with his family. Thomas wife Anna a beautiful fashion designer mother and devoted wife worships the ground her family walks on. The perfect suburban family. Thomas who started as a laborer worked his way up the ladder and formed his own company; Caine Properities along with his brother Marcus Caine a well-known Psychotherapist who specializes in Peculiar Human Behaviors. Business is going good then as time goes on Marcus notices how Thomas begins to become withdrawn from his family yet just puts it on the latest property Thomas has acquired. The property which is located sixty miles away keeps Thomas busy or does it?
