A personal emotional look into 25 plus years of the San Antonio punk rock metal and goth scene through the eyes of Veronica (the filmmaker/AKA Ronnie Stich) as she returns to her hometown after 15 years and interviews musicians and artists she knew or had a connection to. Since moving away and becoming a novelist Veronica discovers many changes in the music scene she was once a part of. It is a return that also includes self-discovery hardship and unexpected pain.
