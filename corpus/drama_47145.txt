Kichhhu Sanlap Kichhu Pralap is a 1999 Indian Bengali drama film directed and produced by Ashoke Viswanathan starting Ashoke Viswanathan Sanjiban Guha and Nandini Ghosal in the lead roles. The screenplay was written by Ashoke Viswanathan collaboratively with Shankar Bhaterjee Prhalad Chattopadhay and Mrinmoy Nandi.
