This two-DVD set contains a condensed version of the Jewish High Holy Day services. Each DVD is divided into two 1-hour parts. One DVD includes a Rosh Hashanah evening service and a Rosh Hashanah morning service. The other DVD includes a Kol Nidre service and Yom Kippur day services including a morning service a memorial service and a concluding service. The services were staged and filmed at Temple Sinai in Washington D.C. in 2008 with the participation of Rabbi Mindy A. Portnoy Cantor Laura T. Croen and the Temple Sinai Choir. Temple Sinai is a Reform Jewish congregation affiliated with the Union for Reform Judaism (URJ).
