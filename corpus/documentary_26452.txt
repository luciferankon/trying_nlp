Nawal Al Sadaawi 85 psychiatrist writer and activist has inspired women from all over the Middle East. When the outcome of the Arab Spring didn't include women in politics or other decisive positions female resistance starts to grow in Cairo. Nouran Salah 24 pursues emancipation in a bold and charitable way: A group of women on bicycles adventure into poor neighborhoods and distribute food - a way of taking down barriers. Or Mahienour el-Massry 30 a human rights lawyer several time imprisoned under Mubarak and Sissi finally released she takes on a new case. The past of Nawal al Sadaawi Egypt's most famous Rebel reveals these contemporary stories of these young and courageous women.
