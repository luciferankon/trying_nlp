Patricia Clarkson was a flourishing young artist bursting with creativity. After years of work she had completed a trilogy of sci-fi novels only to burn them in the name of religion. Nearly forty years later her son Cole Tanner stumbles upon a pen and paper copy of the first novel. It is accompanied by a journal and hundreds of illustrations. After years of a delicate and strained relationship with his motherCole finally has the key to the answers he desperately seeks.
