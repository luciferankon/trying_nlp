A family torn apart by violence. The rage of a drunken husband Lou ignites a series of events leading to his beautiful wife Mary leaving him at the advise of her guardian angel Saint Germain and his eventual suicide by an overdose of pills. In the afterlife Lou now known by his spiritual name "Lucas" must pay penance for his wrongdoings. In his journey through the afterlife he meets a mystical character The Hollywood Angel who helps him understand the source of his rage. His soul is cleansed through the power of an ancient mystical vibration "The Violet Flame" and renewed ready to be born again.
