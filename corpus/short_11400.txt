Olivia is a teenage girl with a unique problem she is totally bald due to alopecia. She is bullied and teased in school. One day while shopping Olivia is discovered by Fashion Designer Nikki Blaine and Fashion Photographer Jay Gambino. They are intrigued by her unique look and hire her as a model. She goes on to be a famous actress and celebrity. While arriving on the red carpet for the premiere of her movie she encounters the same bullies who were mean to her in school. Olivia discovers that you can overcome any challenge.
