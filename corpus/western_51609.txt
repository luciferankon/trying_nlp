Konga the favorite wild horse of rancher Yance Calhoun leads a stampede into the wheat field of Jordan Hadley and the old feud between horse-breeder and wheat farmer flares anew. Hadley's daughter Judith and her sweetheart Steve Calhoun Yance's son bind Konga's wounds from the stampede. Hearing a rumor that Hadley has shot Konga Yance confronts him and in the ensuing altercation kills him in self-defense. Yance is sentenced to prison but Judith the dead man's daughter fights for his pardon.
