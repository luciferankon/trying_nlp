Changing Our Minds: Clear Thoughts on Depression Drugs and Alternative Paths to a Healthy Mind is a new documentary film which answers the pharmaceutical industry's media blitz. Living Life Films' "Changing Our Minds" questions society's values while revealing interesting and disturbing truths about psychiatry and the drug industry. Scientists counselors spiritual leaders and affected individuals address the symptoms causes and treatment of depression including trauma and self esteem issues; medical problems that present symptoms of depression; the history and current knowledge of drug therapy and current talk therapies such as cognitive behavioral therapy philosophical counseling meditation and other helpful practices while sharing their compelling stories of struggle discovery and catharsis.
