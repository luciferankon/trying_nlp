This brazenly over-the-top dark comedy portrays a passionate but disparate group of friends and their individual struggles for love and validation. Sally Christy and Joanie leave New York City for sunny southern California in search of their fame and fortune. Too fun loving to get jobs they pool their money for a tiny studio in which they share a king size mattress. From here they negotiate the minefields of modern life: relationships identity crises responsibilities and the right shade of nail polish. But does moving to a new city mean moving away from their problems and living happily ever after? Find out when you enter this blackly comic maelstrom of narcotics libido and confusion. The naked half truth about LA revealed as three beautiful bedmates explore a playful wonderland where adventure and romance hide the nightmare underneath.
