Growing up my father has always been an inspiration to me and this film chronicles my journey to discover the true stories of his past that led to his imaginative and inspiring fundamentals of raising 3 girls and 1 boy. I didn't realize when I began this intrinsic quest how dark the rabbit hole could get. Wall St. in the 80's hitch hiking across the United States and kid napping filling only a portion of the rear view mirror he calls his memories this tale brings to to life the power of free will imagination love determination the most basic of humanistic traits the fight for survival.
