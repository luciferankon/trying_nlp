A young interior designer goes to his native place.The absence of a hospital there shocks him. He them embarks on a quest to get basic medical facilities to his village.The residents of Mashem in Goa are god-fearing people who are afraid to go against the word of a Godman in the village. Such is the fear in them that they fail to speak up even when they witness money being collected in the name of God and being stacked in the hermitage locker.
