Lenny C Rocks the Mic is a documentary short about 76-year old Denver poet rabble-rouser and tireless humanitarian Lenny Chernila. Brazen and brilliant proud of his activist and Socialist heritage Lenny tore up the open-mic scene in Denver for 20 years on subjects ranging from politics to women's rights to his arch nemesis 'kill culture'. He could also wax beautifully poetic about love art and the merits of "just being nice". His riveting and incendiary performances at countless open mics rallies and concerts inspired a vast following of artists and poets of all ages making him an oft-quoted local legend. Lenny passed away a week before the premiere screening of this film leaving a void in the Denver poetry scene yet an extraordinary voice that inspired countless young poets and artists.
