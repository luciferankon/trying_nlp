A thin line between love and duty: what will overweight the fate's scale? This film is not about Ukrainian revolution. The directors don't name any places or streets. The main focus is that conflict can occur anywhere in the world. Anna meets Alexander - a cryptic handsome intellectual. They fall in love at the Pergolesi concert and their relationship grows deeper rapidly. However the events occurring in the country are not so romantic : the protests on the city's main square foretell the beginning of a revolution. The government enters increased security measures. The protagonists have yet to discover their roles in this political game: Anna becomes an activist of the revolution and Alexander becomes the special unit's captain...
