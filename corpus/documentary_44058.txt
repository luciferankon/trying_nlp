The Young Gods is one of the most influential European bands of the last 25 years. The outcome of the collaboration of a classically trained guitar player and a computer nerd is a fusion of classical music French chanson and punk. Their first LP was rewarded as "album of the year 1987" in the UK and quickly summoned a bunch of followers spanning various European countries as well as Brazil the United States and Australia. "Lonely Pioneers" is a film about extraordinary musicians and their passion for music.
