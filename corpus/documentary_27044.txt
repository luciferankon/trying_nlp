The Garcia brothers are two famous Bolivian sculptors who since childhood have traveled a long journey in search of their artistic fulfillment. Now they embark on a new journey; the most spectacular and risky. Joined by 20 of their best assistants they will travel 2200 kilometers from Cochabamba (Bolivia) to La Rioja (Argentina) where they will deliver their latest assignment: a 35 meters sculpture of controversial hero Facundo Quiroga.
