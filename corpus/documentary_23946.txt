The Untold Story of F-105 Pilots and their Dedicated Ground Crews. Forgotten F-105 Warriors is a documentary film that will capture the personal stories of these heroes - these warriors - for this generation and the next. Almost 50 years have passed since these warriors fought a war that resulted in the loss of so many. Now is the time to get these warriors and their families in front of the camera to preserve their thoughts and feelings from those days.
