In a strange and disturbing world Kwesi a fearless humanoid gangster is forced to settle a large debt that he has with the leader of the mutant mafia Ian Demone. Before fleeing the city Kwesi in order to pay off his debt is given a contract to exterminate one of Demone's henchmen Sonny Londa a small time rogue mafia boss. It is in this setting of perverse decadence that Kwesi discovers the mysterious and colorful nightclub 'Dawm'. And with him he brings Myaranda a high-class prostitute who decides to leave everything behind to follow him in his adventure. And thus begins the puzzle-like story set in an unreal world that basculates between dreams and reality. Kwesi must find the door that leads out of the labyrinth of this obscure world.
