When Timawa meets Delgado "When Timawa meets Delgado" is an unusual story that revolves around the equally-unconventional lives Jun Delgado and Ruben Timawa and their only way to reach their American dreams: to be nurses. A self-proclaimed filmmaker whose parents and siblings are already in America  Delgado decides to enroll in Nursing after breaking up with his nagging girlfriend. Timawa on the other hand is a gay literature teacher and a Palanca award-winning writer who also decides to enroll in nursing when the object of his desire went to America . When they meet at the College of Nursing dean's office worlds of possibilities open.
