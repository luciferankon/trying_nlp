Luis Gutierrez a high school baseball phenom on course for a career in the Majors has everything going for him until he shows up in the wrong "hood" at the wrong time. Tim Reynolds an African-American man in the prime of his life has a promising career as a Major League pitcher until his bad choices catch up to him and he's diagnosed with a deadly disease. Despite age and cultural differences the two meet by chance and an unlikely friendship forms. Together they restore their broken spirits discover their inner strength and overcome life's obstacles by embracing a new future and new dreams.
