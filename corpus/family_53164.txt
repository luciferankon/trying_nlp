Bopanna a native of Coorg region in Karnataka India returns home after 15 years of active military duty in Indian army to lead a retired life. Due to lack of proper rehabilitation of the soldiers ailing father parched agriculture land changing work dynamics among his peers loss of childhood love marital and financial issues of sisters Bopanna is faced with the task of solving them. Shot in the backdrop of Western Ghats of India this movie provides a visual treat with its scenic beauty melodious background score and a very intense family drama with a strong social message.
