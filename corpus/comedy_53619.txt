After Don a modest I.T. employee is kicked out and dumped by his girlfriend Allison he's forced to find a new place to live. After Paxton a former child prodigy turned wealthy wild man discovers he's being cut off from his wealth he's forced to look for a new means of income. When both of their problems come up during their weekly Dungeons and Dragons game Paxton invites Don to move in with him for a "reasonable" fee.
