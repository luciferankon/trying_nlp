The film take place in Tehran 1990s and is the story of a 6 years old boy Shahab who still can not talk and because of that is being teased by every one. Even in his family they care for his bigger brother more than him and only his mother is defending him. He runs away from his family and meets an elderly couple who take him to their home. Their behavior change him a lot but he is back to his home once again. Until his grandmother is come to stay with them for some time.
