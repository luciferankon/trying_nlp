NFL Top 10 is a high-octane perspective that provides analysis of some of the most intriguing subjects in the NFL creating and debating a top ten list for each category. Each episode counts down from No. 10 to the top ranking in each category. NFL Top 10 examines ten items in a specified weekly category with interviews and archival video and audio footage as illustration. Among the subjects examined are - Top 10 Draft Trades - Top 10 Comeback Games - Top 10 Football Myths - and Top 10 Most Fun Teams.
