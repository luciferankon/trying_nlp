The misadventures of Spinner and Sox who find themselves down on their luck...charming as they are dumb the hazards of becoming a bikini dancer grow ever more apparent as the story unfolds. Episode introduces Spinner and Sox to two dangerous girls from work (Roxxy Cotton and Speed Demon): Spinner and Sox desperate to fit in find themselves learning no good deed goes unpunished or without antibiotics when they give the girls a ride to 'The Reptilians' after party. Sex drugs and rock & roll turn them into hard partying groupies and soon they are faced with the err of their ways when their cosmetician Svetlana gives them the heave-ho. Will the girls face their demons?
