In "House of 1000 Corpses" two young couples take a misguided tour onto the back roads of America in search of a local legend known as Dr. Satan. Lost and stranded they are set upon by a bizarre family of psychotics. Murder cannibalism and satanic rituals are just a few of the 1000+ horrors that await. Jerry Bill Mary and Denise are two couples out on the road in hopes of writing a book on offbeat roadside attractions. When the four meet Captain Spaulding they learn of the legend of Dr. Satan. When the four take off in search of finding the tree that Dr. Satan was hung from they pick up hitchhiker Baby. When their car breaks down Baby invites them into her house. This is where the two couples meet the Firefly family a murderous clan of bizarre people. As the family attacks the unsuspecting couples Jerry and his friends try to survive the house of 1000 corpses and it's dark secrets.
