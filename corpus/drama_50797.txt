The day before the wedding takes place Asmara gets a harsh reality that her lover Dewa had an affair with her work colleague Anita . Although Dewa begs to hold the marriage Asthma has already broken heart. Moreover the affair makes Anita pregnant. In Beijing Asma meets Zhongwen a handsome man who introduces the love legend of Ashima the beautiful daughter of Yunan.
