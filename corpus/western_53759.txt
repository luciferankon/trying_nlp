To pay off his gambling debts to Keeler Babe Rivers sells his and his brother Buck's cattle and then joins Keeler's gang. After the gang pulls off a stage holdup Keeler takes the gold and double-crosses them sending the Sheriff to their hideout. When Babe gets caught and the lynch mob forms Buck sets out to free his brother. Reece Rivers ('Buck Jones (I)' (qv))is the older brother of Babe Rivers ('Pat J. O'Brien' (qv) a young man whose gambling debts place him in the power of unscrupulous men and who later joins their outlaw band. When they rob a stagecoach Reece takes the trail of the bandits primarily to rescue his brother but to also wreak vengeance on the others in the gang.
