A thousand years after the fall of a space-faring empire a young princess named Tahira travels the far reaches of her barren kingdom alone but for her trusty horse Iba. When word reaches her of an army wielding the devastating technologies of the past she finds herself thrust into the most challenging night of her life as she fights to become the leader her people desperately need. Experience the epic coming of age story of a young woman told in near-real-time across a 12-hour period in this tactical turn-based adventure.
