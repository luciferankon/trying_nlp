A is an actress and she starred in a film two years ago but ended in failure. The role of B that A played in the film has always been with her. However B has been the biggest obstacle to her audition. In order to get this opportunity she has to make B disappear forever. However A is trapped in pain after B is killed. At this time another 'actor' C appears and then A realizes that she is nothing but a role in the film while C is the real actor. And A plans to use C to kill the director so as to change her fate in the play.
