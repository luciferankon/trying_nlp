Partnering with a new generation of voices from Hollywood to Washington D.C. and delivering entirely online with strategic partners Dead Reckoning features intelligent entertaining and relevant Christian conversations on religion economics lifestyle politics art and culture. The podcast recording is streamed live in front of a Facebook audience and is presently occurring.
