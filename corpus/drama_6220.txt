Sins of South Beach is the story of sex drugs violence corruption murder and the making of Miami Beach. Based on the Book of the same name written by Alex Dauod Sins of South Beach reveals the city's renaissance and the crime and corruption with that fueled it. Daoud who served as Miami Beach's Commissioner and Mayor from 1979 to 1991 is a story with first-hand details how as an idealistic young lawyer he was seduced by money power and sex and how that seduction led to the moral decay that nearly drove him insane A time when "cocaine cowboys' slaughtered each other in drug wars and the criminal refugees from the Mariel Boatlift many released by Fidel Castrol from his worst jails and insane asylums terrorized the population of Miami Beach.
