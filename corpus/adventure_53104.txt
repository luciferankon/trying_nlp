The year is 1820 at the time of the Bourbon Restauration. Louis XVIII is king of France. Napoleon is dying on the island of Saint Helena. Peace has been restored in Europe and order prevails in France. Or nearly so... In Bayonne it is not quite true yet: the customs directorate for example worries over smuggling operations between France and Spain. To thwart it the former customs officers will be replaced by tough Catalan soldiers and Lieutenant Carradoux has decided to strengthen the border posts. All that is no big deal for Gorri who has found the response hiding contraband horses among those of the escort of the bishop of Pamplona in visit to Bayonne...
