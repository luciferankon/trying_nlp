Tough-as-nails former stuntman and world record breaker Ackerman O'Neil convinces his estranged daughter to let him take his mute withdrawn grandson Omelette under his wing for a summer. Omelette may not be able to speak but Ackerman believes that he's destined to break a world record and he's determined to figure out which one. During the two months they spend together Ackerman reveals a nurturing side his daughter never knew and Omelette discovers his true potential.
