Rough Land is the story of the people who are not afraid of facing themselves and things they have done in the past. They have had a series of incidents which are influencing their lives today - People from three different generations are united to define the meaning of holding together and be human again. They prove only love and tenderness are the reasons behind the survival and unity of mankind. Real samples of the characters can be found all around the world these days. They are the people who never forget helping others and be kind despite the bumpy road of life.
