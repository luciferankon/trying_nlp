A young orphaned pirate is freed from the evil Armada's prison and escapes to Skull Island. There they meet Captain Avery who gives them their first ship. After a while the young pirate finds a piece of the legendary map to El Dorado an ancient city of gold. But the evil Armada also wants this map to conquer the Spiral and rebuild it with no place for pirates! The Armada is a military made up of and lead by clockwork puppets who's puppeteers lost control of them without knowing it. The Armada threatens the whole Spiral and not to mention the young pirate's very way of life. It is up to you the young pirate to find the legendary map to El Dorado and defeat the Armada once and for all.
