Comprehensive in its approach Yoga to Awaken the Chakras helps you work with the subtle energies of the body leaving you feeling both calmed and rejuvenated. Beginning with a brief easy-to-understand explanation of the chakras and the many benefits of working with them this routine then moves into a step-by-step series of yoga postures designed to help you awaken and direct the energies of each chakra. You'll be guided through postures special exercises and mini-meditations that will help you develop an awareness of the chakras and their potential for self-transformation.
