For years Tony and Leo have been a happy couple. They had always talked about having a child... someday. So when a sickly infant is abandoned at a local hospital Tony sees it as a sign. Suddenly Leo is confronted with making good on his promise to start a family; he must face his deep-seeded fears of being a father or risk losing Tony altogether.
