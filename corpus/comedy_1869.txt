Mitsos the restless and mischievous son of a matchmaking fortune-teller after following his late mother's advice he decides to maintain an independent matchmaking agency struggling however to make ends meet along with his childhood friend Spinos. As a result Mitsos in order to succeed and satisfy his clientele's whims by keeping his identity secret he even disguises himself depending on the circumstance. Unexpectedly this is an infallible tactic nevertheless just when everything seemed to be going well Mitsos has a chance encounter with the charming Eve and his heart begins skipping a beat.
