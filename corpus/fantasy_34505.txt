The Snake Queen returns in this bizarre horror fantasy. In this sequel to "Nyi Blorong" (1982 aka "The Snake Queen") Brian a man who believes that money and power will win over the love of his girlfriend. While fleeing through the woods (after attempting rape his now-ex-girlfriend) he stumbles upon the Snake Queen who promises him great wealth if he does her bidding. However the evil hungry snake woman who wants revenge against the Snake Queen convinces Brian to trick the Queen by sticking a magic pin in her neck during love making turning her into a snake!
