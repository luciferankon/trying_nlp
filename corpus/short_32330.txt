Voices of History is a short film from Kirkpatrick & Kinslow Productions. The film profiles pivotal points in Tulsa's history set against the backdrop of Tulsa's historic Council Oak Tree. To help tell the story Producer Russ Kirkpatrick worked closely with Tulsa Historical Society Executive Director Michelle Place and writers Bond Love and Michael Wallis for the narrative that was beautifully put to film by Director Bunee Tomlinson & Director of Photography Sam Calvin.
