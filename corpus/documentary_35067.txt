'Children of Chile' is a first-hand investigative documentary film on the adoption process in the 1980's which includes the 'Children of Silence' and illegal adoptions as well as the personal journey of Veronica Alejandra Contreras Sanchez who was adopted at age 7. Veronica will be traveling back to Chile in the search for answers and visit her biological mother for the first in over 20 years while also discussing the Chilean adoption system and what allowed for so many babies to be stolen.
