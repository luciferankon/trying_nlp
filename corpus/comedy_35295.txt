Lots of laughs when odd couple meets odd couple in 'LOVEITIS'... At first a bad bet. Then a case of mistaken identity by 'Styx and the Boys' resulting in the tremendous hospital pummeling of aristocrat prosecutor Stephen Wells pairing him with hard rock guitarist 'The Love Machine' asleep one bed over. The Machine in an effort to make good for the hospital mishap embarks on an effort to restore Stevie's happiness. Their paths cross with odd couple sisters Kimberley Ann and Cindy ironically in the same position with Kim trying to cheer up her sister after a failed marriage and suicide attempt. Machine and Kim play cupid trying to restore Stevie and Cindy's happiness with a curve ball requiring an unorthodox remedy at the end
