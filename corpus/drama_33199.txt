The Pea Shooter The Pea Shooter is a dramatic comedy that follows the day in the life of two kids Taylor and Rae who in spite of their differences in religion and their economic social status are still able to form a friendship. Starring Brennon Olsen (Taylor) and Hannah Trauscht (Rae). The film is written and directed by Joseph T. Spence and executive produced by Julian Lennon and Dawn M. Cernak; produced by J David Parrett and Roy Triplet.
