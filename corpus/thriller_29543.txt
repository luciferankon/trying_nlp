Bike Week 2013 has begun and the party is in full motion. A tour of Main Street Daytona Beach and then off to the World Famous Broken Spoke Saloon. just like any other BikeWeek its full of custom bikes hot babes and bikini contests free moonshine and live concerts on the main stage. But something is triggered and the Rally Stalker is on the prowl. No one is safe and no one is discovered missing until the clean up crew arrives the next morning.
