Matt Vineyard is a small town kid who is at the brink of becoming exactly what he wanted in life a successful actor writer and director. Life is good. He's engaged to a beautiful Hollywood socialite and is about to make it onto the A-List. Still something is missing. He receives an invitation to return to his hometown as the graduation speaker at his Alma mater. A surprise visit from a familiar face soon throws his whole world upside down and sends him on down a path of self-discovery.
