The film is a psycho-thriller based on a travel experience that includes a lot of thrilling events and incidents. It features three contradicting characters a cop a psycho and a driver that are headed to different destinations on this journey. The lives of a mad serial killer an ACP who is just hours from his marriage and an unemployed youngster tasked with delivering a serviced vintage Contessa car to a superstitious MLA intersect under coincidental circumstances leading to a classic cat & mouse tale!
