Living in the same house as strangers (or even friends) can be difficult with each person liking to do things their own special way. With no mother to help run the house cleaning washing and general house hold cores seldom get done. That is where Share House comes in this educational lifestyle program teaches people how to pick and choose the right roommate create a gourmet dinner out of fish sticks and two minute noodles organize a housework schedule that is sure to keep everyone happy plus much more. Amy Johnno Mel and Skip give you you all the tips and tricks do's and don't about living in a share house.
