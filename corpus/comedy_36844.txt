It's a show about spies in Nashville. Agents. Dean McClane and Mac Robertson are CIA agents at the top of their game when their identities are published by Wikileaks and their covers blown. Understanding that the agent's reputations make them too volatile to simply cut loose the Agency decides to hide them in plain site by sending them to Nashville Tennessee until the heat dies down and they can be released back into civilian life. Now stuck in an strange place home to stranger people Dean and Mac must find a way to make their own fortunes and learn how to live in a world where they're especially not special. Aiding in their exploits is CIA handler Destiny Diamond neighbor/bail bondsman Dallas Jacobs and Landlady Madam Szardos.
