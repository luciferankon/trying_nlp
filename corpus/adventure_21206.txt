Lightning McQueen helps out Doc Hudson when he opens up a racing academy in Radiator Springs in order to prepare his students for the Race-O-Rama competition. Lightning McQueen also discovers that Chick Hicks has also opened a racing academy where he teaches his students to not race honorably when they crash two of Doc Hudson's students during the intro race. Now Lightning McQueen must race against Chick Hicks and his students to keep the Race-O-Rama competition honorable. After Chick Hicks is annoyed of losing to Lightning many times he recruits 3 new racers: Candice Stinger and El Machismo! Not only that but he also plans to attack Radiator Springs and shut down the Doc Hudson Racing Academy! Now it is up to Lightning to stop Chick from tearing down the Doc Hudson Racing Academy.
