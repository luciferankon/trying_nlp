Set in Austin TX "Stumblers" tells the story of two local musicians who must overcome their laid-back lifestyle to expose a scandal overtaking the music industry. After losing their big gig at SXSW to a high school bully (DJ DOOMSDAY) Willy and Josh discover their old classmate is using technology to manipulate his fans and even kidnapping booking agents in his search for fame. What started as just a concert will pull two boys down a rabbit hole bigger than Willie Nelson's stash and only they can expose the truth to save their city and their futures.
