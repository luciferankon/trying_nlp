This film is about the history of Rensselaerville however the story is much larger than the town. It begins with Stephen van Rensselaer III the "Last Patroon" and continues with the significance of the Anti Rent War. The film will encompass these topics and use Rensselaerville as a pure example of Stephen van Rensselaer's influence and the impact of the Anti Rent War on a small community.
