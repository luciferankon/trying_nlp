Sucheta Chapekar is a senior Bharatanatyam exponent and her contribution to Bharatanatyam has been recognized by several cultural institutions all over the world. Along with the firm base of Bharatnatyam technique and conceptual knowledge that came through her Guru's teachings it was Sucheta's liberal approach towards her art-form that transformed her into a revolutionary artist. She pioneered the blend of Bharatnatyam with Hindustani Classical Music in her creation "Nrityaganga". This film captures her overall approach towards life and dance as a medium of expression.
