No Equal: The Trials of America's Female Soldier is a documentary that provides a compelling and intricate portrait of the historic evolution and contemporary challenges facing women in the US military. Through candid testimonials and archival news footage No Equal examines the undermining role of congress military motherhood and the hot-topic issue of women serving in front-line combat roles to weave an intricate portrait of the female soldier. Still faced by gender inequality the film aims to inspire social and political awareness around a group that goes largely overlooked.
