"Land of Winds" - is a creative documentary movie telling the story about nearly extinct Curonian-Latvian community mainly the members of old generation. The film reveals little-known fragments of the history of a small Lithuanian seaside town named Sventoji highlighting its multi-cultural dimensions connections with the Latvian culture and Baltic heritage. Herewith it talks about the relationship between human and his own and foreign culture nation and native place. Lithuanian-Latvian borderline - the space where two different countries people and their traditions meet each other.
