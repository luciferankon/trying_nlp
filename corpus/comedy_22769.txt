Stan Hall is back with his latest documentary this time he is tackling the subject of meth. There are surprises around every corner and Stan never knows what will happen next. Sit back and enter the world of meth as seen through the eyes of Stan who had no choice but to move back home to northeast Indiana when he was left penniless after a legal dispute. There is always imminent danger lurking when dealing with meth... will Stan survive his trek into the underground world of meth or will he meet his demise? One thing is certain... never count Stan Hall out.
