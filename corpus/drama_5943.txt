The setting is 90's in Vietnam. Lien is a traditional pure and simple Vietnamese girl who has a dream of a happy peaceful life. The opening described a series of traumatic events that happened to Lien when she was a young child. Her mother passed away her father married the second times to a horrible wicked woman. The evil stepmother who is always unconscionably buffeted her step-daughter tried to sell Lien to a pander. In a hopeless effort to get out of the lost and mourned reality Lien met up with the Master. Under his tutelage Lien retrieved hope and regained the pipe dream she'd thought that will never has any chance of being fulfilled. At the end Lien finally enlightened the answer: "Patience is a bridge to overcome suffering."
