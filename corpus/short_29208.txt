Naive Catholic boy Josh 20 finds his life turned upside down when he falls in love with Monika 19 a dynamic infectiously joyful pole dancer. Josh begins working on the campaign staff of a candidate for Mayor and is shocked to discover that 'Monika' is actually a multiple personality of Miranda the candidate's unpleasant acid-tongued daughter. Josh takes a leap far out of his comfort zone in order to rescue Monika/Miranda from a sure to be unhappy arranged marriage.
