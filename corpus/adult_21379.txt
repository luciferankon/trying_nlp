Out of work and out of luck Alexa considers herself fortunate when her Uncle Jack asks her to housesit for him. When Uncle Jack's ex-wife accuses the pretty blonde of being the golddigger who broke up their marriage and leaves the girl bound and gagged naked and helpless Alexa begins to think that maybe she wasn't so lucky after all. She's becomes sure of it when Uncle Jack's current main squeeze drops by the house and accuses her of moving into her territory. Poor Alexa is quickly stripped cleave-gagged and hogtied and quite unable to free herself.
