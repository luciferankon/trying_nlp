All Phenomena are fleeting. "Shikisoku Zekuu" this is one of the Buddhist doctrines; everything changes at every moment. Therefore what you see and believe doesn't exist. We are tiny and powerless in relation to the universe. A coming of age story of two sorrowful and confused Japanese women who find solace in each others' company. Tatsuki the main character decides to eradicate her sorrow with the help of her friend Shinobu an accomplished tattoo artist. Both of the women undergo a spiritual metamorphosis through the expression of the art of "Shisei" (Tattoo).
