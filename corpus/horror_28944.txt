After her motocross racing mullet sporting boyfriend is killed Brittney finds herself haunted by visions of her dead lover. These visions are brushed off at first and she is encouraged to move on but the nightmares simply won't end. Meanwhile she takes off to a cabin in the woods and encounters a camp counselor who yells at everyone and hip hop dancers who prefer a rural stage. When she takes on a new lover her ex rises from the grave and begins to terrorize everyone through a strange nightmare link. This leads to bloodshed a guy bouncing off a queen sized mattress and a broken condom. But is her dead boyfriend really riding his dirt bike through death's door or has Brittney just lost her mind from looking that horrible music box?
