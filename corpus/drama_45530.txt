An ambitious and successful young woman by any means Sienna's life comes to an urgent halt when faced with a life threatening diagnosis. She must now face what appears to be the most difficult decision of her life while finding small triumph amidst tragedy. How do we define life? Is it the number of days lived or how we live them? When we cannot speak for ourselves who speaks for us? This is a female driven production with a story and message that all can relate to. End of life and terminal illness has affected most of us. What happens at the core of our lives love and family in our darkest hours.
