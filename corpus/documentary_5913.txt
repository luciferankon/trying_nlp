This documentary film was made by high school student of Gunma Kokusai Academy JAPAN. The four of students did everything from pre-production interview shooting editing and all post production works. And they got an award from the Jury Dir Nobuhiro Suwa at our school film festival. And I Hiroyuki Oda was in charge of this filmmaking class at the school. However our class policy is that teachers don't help too much . Teachers let students think about how they can make their original films for themselves. There film theme is about Brazilian community in Japan.
