Agnes MacDonnell (Greta Scacchi) a strong and self-confident Englishwoman in her forties owns a large estate on an island off the west coast of Ireland. When she begins a passionate but dangerous affair with twenty-something James Lynchehaun (Daniel Craig) her new estate manager Agnes wages a desperate struggle to dominate this charismatic but destructive man. A tale of passion and violence in the Victorian era LOVE and RAGE is based on the novel by James Carney.
