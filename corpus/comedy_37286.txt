After being transferred to post as night court commissioner Charlie Murphy presides over a case involving his abusive childhood foster care guardian Vera Jenkins played by Marla Gibbs. Assistant DA Chadwick Morgan (Dan Lawler) faces off against coworker friend public defender Valerie (Emily Morris) as Chad prepares for his high-school reunion with the release of his complication-riddled first book while also defending against romantic advances from his high school bully Cathy Dugan (Margaux Susi). Court stenographer Rihanna Wu (Katherine Lee) navigates the court landscape and involves herself in others' relationships including that of court clerk Angela Torres (Elizzabeth "ZZ" Prado) and a former fling delivery boy Cesar.
