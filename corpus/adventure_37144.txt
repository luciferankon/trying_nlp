Set just before the outbreak of the Second World War this is the story of a British test pilot who is caught up in a scheme to steal plans of a top-secret aircraft working for an unnamed enemy. Made in 1939 and released in March 1939 (UK) by the time it was in a general release war had been declared. After a lawsuit initiated by Ernest Hemingway over copyright infringement was settled the film in an abridged form was premiered in 1940 in the US.
