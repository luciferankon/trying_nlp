In this Flemish comedy format stand-up comedian/ actor Han Coucke and his mate Wim Coenen play persiflage versions of various BV's (which in Dutch stands for Bekende Vlamingen i.e. 'famous Flemings') such as politicians Yves Leterme (then Flemish regional Premier later Belgian federal Prime Minister) Willy Claes (former socialist party leader various cabinet posts and shortly secretary-general of NATO) TV presenter Sergio singer Helmut Lotti and cyclist Johan Museeuw.
