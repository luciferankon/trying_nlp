****WINNER BEST FILM AWARD at the 2014 Cause Film Festival**** In Australia bats are one of the most misunderstood and persecuted groups of wildlife. However many people are unaware of the critical ecological roles that bats play in the environment. 'Hanging in the Balance' provides a fascinating insight into the lives of these incredible creatures dispels the most common myths and misconceptions and showcases some of the passionate and dedicated people fighting for their protection.
