Guy is sitting at his keyboard trying to write a musical. His girlfriend Karen enters and asks him to play what he's composed. He does so and she points out that he has just played the Jurassic Park theme song. Guy is frustrated and says all his ideas are ridiculous. Karen tells him all musicals are ridiculous and they discuss the silly plot lines of many musicals. She leaves and he pictures a Jurassic Park musical but quickly decides to go back to the drawing board.
