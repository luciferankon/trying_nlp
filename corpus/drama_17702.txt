Ambitious gifted impoverished novelist Angus Frasier journeys from Port Chalmers New Zealand to Edinburg Scotland and throughout the world on the HMR Olympic in the mid-1920s to discover his noble Scottish lineage and prove himself worthy to marry Duchess Abigail Hargraves. Frasier's efforts are opposed at every turn by Abigail's father Duke Jacob Hargraves and Baron Lathian "Laches" Rutherford who wants Abigail as his trophy wife.
