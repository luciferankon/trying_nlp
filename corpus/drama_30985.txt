Jason is an advertising executive whose life is out of control - his wife has left he is broke and his business is about to go under. He sees a chance to make his fortune by promoting a scheme to raise money for a little girl who needs a heart transplant. Bowler looses his money due to the actions of Haywood as his accountant to recoup these funds Hayward says he has some "investors" and can set up a fund so they can both skim some money off the top. The resultant actions run the full gamit of human emotions including a charming part for a young lass waiting for a transplant.
