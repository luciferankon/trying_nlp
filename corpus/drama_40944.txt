In a small town in Northern Michigan two undercover street detectives are assigned to a case unlike ANY other. One is an over the edge cop with an itchy trigger finger who VOWS to put away one of the most RUTHLESS criminals back behind bars. When Police Chief Kurt Williams (Josiah Johnson) gets a fax from an out of state prison with Keith Donner's (Jerry Christin) personal where abouts he sends his two finest cops (Matt Kwiatkowski and Josh Hill) to bring Donner to justice. Suspense hits hard around EVERY corner in this fast paced drama with an ending NOT to be missed.
