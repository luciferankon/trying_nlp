Nationally respected constitutional law expert Michael Farris leads the audience on a riveting journey through the history and the theory behind the U.S. Constitution. With engaging visuals stunning photos engrossing motion graphics and great sound effects and music Constitutional Literacy draws in the viewer to an enjoyment of history. The series emphasizes a return to constitutionally limited government as Farris discusses where America originated how it's constitutional interpretation has morphed and the best way to insure a free and prosperous society based upon original constitutional principles.
