This made-for-tv documentary treats fantasy and adventure fans to a behind-the-scenes look at the making of Peter Jackson's three-film adaptation of J.R.R. Tolkien's Lord of the Rings. Includes archive footage of Tolkien discussing his work along with interviews with director Jackson and the rest of the cast and crew of the film who share their experiences from working on the project as well as discuss the special efforts that went into bringing this massive work to completion.
