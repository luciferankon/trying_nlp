Join the cast and crew of theCRASHERS as they travel the USA crashing conventions on behalf of their eccentric boss Francois who's mission is to present an unauthorized look at the inside of conventions and the towns that host them. Have fun as theCRASHERS team tries to stay one step ahead of security as they pose as a team of journalists and bloggers while getting the inside scoop on some of the worlds biggest events including CES Magic and more!
