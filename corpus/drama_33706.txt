Grant means Great. at 18 Grant has yet to find the greatness in his life. His parents marriage is eroding before his eyes. His mother (Linda Kash) unable to cope with the recent passing of her mother tries to numb the pain. His father (Stuart Hughes) struggling to make ends meet desperately tries to hold the family together. Left feeling like he's on the outside looking in at his own family; Grant needs to make the decision to be the man in the family or the 18 year old that still can't quite figure out how to say hello to the pretty girl.
