Welcome to the world's worst estate agency - staffed by the apathetic the incompetent and a seven-foot blue monstrosity known only as House. When manager Paul leaves abruptly in suspicious circumstances letting agent Alexander Bamforth-Hamilton sees the vacant position as his unassailable birthright. But Flossy Brown his desk buddy and sworn enemy has her sights set on the same job. New supervisor Ted Cheer is tasked with deciding between them. But as the battle for the position descends into outright warfare only one candidate can emerge victorious.
