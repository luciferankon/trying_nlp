Galaxy Superstar is a talent audition show discovering Indonesian talents who are totally into K-pop and dream of becoming a star like K-pop idol groups. The selected finalists of the survival-type audition show travel to Korea to undergo rigorous training system that has produced wildly popular Korean pop stars. The 6 finalists of 3 months of training in Korea were formed into a girl group S.O.S (abbreviation of Sensation of Stage).
