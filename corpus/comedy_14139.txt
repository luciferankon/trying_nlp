Dutch 'zapcabaret': various sketches by De Vliegende Panters with in every episode another suject. But in each episode you can see the story of "The Turbo Twins (Party-band with a message)". Twins Peter Pascal and Pierke Smulder have their own party-band. They perform especially in houses for the elderly and bars. Peter and Pascal want to go professional. They're buying a new keyboard and they perform almost every evening. But one morning the mentally challenged Pierke seems to have a burn out and won't be able to perform for a while.
