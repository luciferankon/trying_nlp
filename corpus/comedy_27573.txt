High ambitions for the screen turn into an action-packed adventure for Slater Malone when his hired stunt double Harry Houston shows up at the wrong location. Harry ends up blowing the cover of CIA agent Marley who is in the middle of a terrorist investigation. Harry and Marley have to partner up to survive the life-threatening situation. Armed with Harry's awkwardness and modern day street smarts and Marley's no nonsense attitude they have to fight their way through a twisted scheme with big guns and bigger wits.
