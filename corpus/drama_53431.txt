Based on the true story of Dr. Misambo Asabi and his family. The Democratic Republic of Congo is oppressed by rebel armies who kill rape kidnap and plunder wherever they can. A surgeon is captured by a rebel army and forced to work for them leaving his family behind. Two of his daughters are missing and his wife and five other children are forced to flee to Kenya where they are still unsafe. Realizing that God's promised land lies beyond his years spent in the wilderness Dr. Asabi applies for asylum to move to the U.S. Meanwhile the United Nations requests Dr. Asabi to testify against the rebels.
