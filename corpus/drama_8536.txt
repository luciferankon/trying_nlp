Sometimes you can't fight a battle until it becomes personal. Israel an inner city street kid quickly finds this out as he embarks on an unexpected journey to find himself. When he first meets Jason an overly enthusiastic shelter worker he has no idea how this one man will change his life forever. As Israel battles his own demons his story becomes intricately intertwined with the journey of Ethan and Emily a pair of runaways from their heroin addicted prostitute mother. Israel and Ethan become involved with a drug smuggling gang ring and things seem to be going fairly well for them. But will all three be able to navigate this volatile environment and make it out alive?
