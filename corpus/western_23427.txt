Colonel Beldan runs a Wild West show. When Beldan's star attraction switches to his unscrupulous competitor Vicarino the young Bob Mason takes the opportunity to take over the job with his horse 'Tarzan'. Mason falls in love Beldans daughter Sally. When Mason also proves to be successful with his performances Vicarino decides to sabotage his rival. Vicarino orders to steal some cars of Beldan. Mason however tries to fiend back the cars for the upcoming performance.
