Dissatisfied with her options in the world of striving a young deaf woman Iris decides to give up on yearning to connect. Instead she floats along working at a greenhouse embracing her silent solitude and learning to be happy with less. Everything is going just fine until she meets a precocious musician named Celeste. Beautifully shot on Prince Edward lsland Singing to Myself is an intimate gaze into the complexity and ease of female friendship.
