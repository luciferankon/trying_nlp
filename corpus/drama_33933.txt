A true depiction of Urban Life in San Francisco's bay area Holliday Entertainment present 'Big Things Happen In The Bay'. Filled with an array of characters including hot shot lawyer Michael (Andre Convington of Def Comedy Jam) and starring Katt Williams and Laura Hays. It is a mix of controversy daredevil stunts steamy love scenes and scandalous characters. Featuring exclusive footage from around the Bay check out Big Things Happen now and get in on one of best-kept secrets in the Independent movie world.
