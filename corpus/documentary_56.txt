Marshall Nord is a 49-year-old executive Father husband and amateur endurance athlete. He has 30 years of multi-sport experience behind him having run marathons Iron Mans triathlons you name it. But with the big 5-0 looming he is desperate to achieve something truly epic. And what is more epic than taking on the toughest ultra-endurance cycling event in the world? The Race Across America is a 3070 mile bicycle race from the West to East of the USA that must be completed in under 12 days. With a small support crew made up of family and friends but not a minute of RAAM experience between them the odds are stacked against him. Marshall is determined to push beyond in order to achieve his goal however no matter what obstacles the race rules or road can throw in his way.
