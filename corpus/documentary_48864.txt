After achieving the incredible feat of being the youngest person to sail solo non-stop & unassisted around the world in 2010 Jessica thought it was time for another challenge - Skippering the youngest crew ever in the iconic Sydney to Hobart race. The crew of 11 had an average age of just 19 the youngest crew in the history of the race. The documentary goes though the crews journey of the preparation setbacks and success they achieved as a team in one of the most dangerous and challenging races in the world. The documentary was narrated by Alan Jones and was broadcasted on Channel 9.
