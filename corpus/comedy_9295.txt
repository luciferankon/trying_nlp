Black comedy set in Usti nad Labem a town in northern Bohemia Czech Republic. The story revolves around the relationship between two new lecturers at a local university - a Russian and an American. Tension starts when the two men are forced to live together in one small dorm room. The two men could not be less alike. They quickly develop a disliking for each other. They conflict over everything from ketchup to women. It is as if another Cold War has broken out with the battleground being their little tiny dorm room.
