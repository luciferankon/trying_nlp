Australia. In the western suburbs of Sydney. Chloe a stubborn young woman and her sister Louise have grown up without their mother. After a brutal attack her life is changed and suddenly Chloe's life flips upside down. From medical bills to looking after her family Chloe decides to leave town and visit the east the expensive eastern suburbs of Sydney. There she makes friends and falls for a stranger but whom can you trust in the life style of a snob?
