Set in the stunning lake paradise of Kelowna BC. Known to the west coast as the ultimate vacation area with massive lakes very warm temperatures fruit trees and a booming wine industry. Cassidy and Gerald DeVeer both come from a long line of builders. Following in the footsteps of parents and grandparents Cassidy and Gerald build stunning custom properties under the name of 3rd Generation. On top of running a successful building business Cassidy helps her parents and unique older brother with their flooring store. She also coaches sports while raising 2 young children - And she is only 28.
