An impoverished single parent Robert Lim (Jericho Rosales) spends his free time with his young son Brian (Bugoy Carino) turning his back just for a minute he loses the little boy. Surveillance videos reveal the child being led away by an older man. The father's anguished search begins. When the police fail to locate his child he plows through the back streets of Manila unearthing the dark facets of human trafficking determined to find his son at all costs.
