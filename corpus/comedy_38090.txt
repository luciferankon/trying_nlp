Dr. Laurie Hill is a pediatrician and loving family woman who puts up with boorish self-centered sexist men during her job from her supervisor Dr. Kramer to co-worker Dr. Wiseman. Laurie's home life has her dealing with her husband Jeff a freelance writer spoils their five-year-old son Leo while Laurie's only friends are her female employees whom are Nancy MacIntyre the resident nurse and Beverly the receptionist.
