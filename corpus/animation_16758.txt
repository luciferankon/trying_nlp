Julius Jr. is an animated preschool series based on the Paul Frank family of characters. Julius Jr. is a funky monkey with a penchant for invention. Together with his best pals Worry Bear Sheree Clancy and Ping they build a playhouse out of a simple cardboard box. But when they walk inside to their surprise and delight they discover that ordinary objects magically come to life and amazing adventures are just a door away. Julius Jr. proves every day that the best inventions are the ones that help your friends.
