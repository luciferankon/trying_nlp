Inside the Marx Brother is a look at the personal and professional lives of Groucho Harpo Chico Zeppo and Gummo Marx featuring classic clips from such Marx Brothers favorites as "Animal Crackers" "Monkey Business" "Duck Soup" "A Night at the Opera" and "A Day at the Races." Also included are rare newsreels TV clips home movies and interviews with Groucho Chico Groucho's son Arthur and "Room Service" co-star Ann Miller.
