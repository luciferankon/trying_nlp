The story revolves around a woman who sells milk for her livelihood. She marries an affluent man but when the groom's mother finds out about his son's marriage to her she tries creating conspiracies. She influences her son to marry another girl but fails every time. Eventually the groom's mother kills the bride. She puts false allegations against her son's friend for her death. The movie takes a new turn when the ghost of the bride takes revenge on her death.
