Samantha Noble is a high-profile magazine editor. While investigating a career-making cover story about a mysterious billionaire she finds herself accused of murder. As she tries to clear her name she uncovers information that leads to more murders and more unanswered questions. Samantha is then forced to question her own place in the shark-eat-shark world of ambition and power where truth is ever elusive enemies wear a friend's face and the stakes are nothing less than life or death.
