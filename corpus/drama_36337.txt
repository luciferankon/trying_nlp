In the future (1921) an alliance of several foreign countries plot to attack the US. American officials coming to the realization that the country is basically defenseless offer $1000000 to anyone who can come up with a weapon to defeat the invaders. Winthrop Clavering a writer and inventor hears of the reward and tells his friend Bartholomew Thompson a scientist and inventor who has been working on developing flying torpedo. However enemy agents have also heard about Thompson's project and set out to kill him and steal his plans.
