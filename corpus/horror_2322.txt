At the young age of 18 Ellie Landrum narrowly escaped the hands of Colin Miller who would go on to become the legend of Carpenter Falls and earn the moniker of The Wicked One. For nearly 30 years Ellie has lived a life consumed by her experience and trying to get over her dark past. But when darkness descends upon Carpenter Falls and The Wicked One returns Ellie must face off against her worst fears if she hopes to save her family.
