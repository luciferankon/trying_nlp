Hedge clearance the replacement of old pollard willows with metal poles the destruction of ancient orchards and intensive use of pesticides and chemical fertilizers: all of this has led to a decrease in the population of the Little Owl. But to turn around this phenomenon and bring life back to our countryside again not much is needed. The film is an invitation to discover this delightful small bird of prey evolving in her habitat as well as the fauna which populates her 'realm'.
