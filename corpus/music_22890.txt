Discover the fire that burns in the rock-n-roll hearts of Joe Getty & The Dead Flowers. With little fanfare and no glamor discover what drives this collective of talent to plow through sets in local establishments and promote their debut CD "Low Expectations". Tear the Whole Thing Down delivers a full live performance by the band at Folsom California's legendary Powerhouse Pub while taking you behind-the-scenes to learn about the band and it's members in their own words. From good old' Americana-Stonesy rock (Post St. Blues)to introspective ballads of addiction (Losing Everything) the Dead Flowers use their music and personalities to entertain their cult fan-ship in the Sacramento area.
