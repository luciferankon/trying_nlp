Shades of Darkness is the story of a young woman named Bridget Avers Anne C. Trebilcock who returns to her home town of Autumn Hills to discover a threatening Entity John Maczko attempting to wipe out the entire population. Aware that she has a tie to the town through a past life Bridget begins to put together the pieces of a much larger puzzle revealing a horrific course through history that only she has the knowledge to stop. As Bridget confronts the Entity its massing strength threatens to overtake her and infect the townspeople with its hateful plague. Her two worlds collide blurring the lines between the past and present. She soon realizes that the powerful force the Entity embodies will destroy her and the people of Autumn Hills if she cannot stop it's destructive reign.
