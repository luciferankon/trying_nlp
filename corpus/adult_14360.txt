Going to college means that farm girl Wendy Phillips must leave her boyfriend Jeb after a literal roll in the hay of course. At college Wendy meets coeds Paige Catty and Elaine and their boyfriends Phil Danny and Bruner. The boys' hobby of secret videotaping comes in handy when Dean Plumm extorts sex from Wendy. Blackmail gives the frat boys license to party hearty and they do. Wendy receives a "Dear Joan" letter from Jeb so she's free too.
