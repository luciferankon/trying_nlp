Acclaimed documentary series Footnotes to History presents an in-depth look at the intense and mysterious Russian avant-garde theater director Anton Luvkin. From his early days acting under Stanislavsky to his questionable legacy Luvkin's historical life was marked for probable extinction. Join scholar (and devout Luvkinite) Martin Bigby Ph.D. as he leads us through Luvkin's personal journey - one marked by discovery dishonor and occasional disrobing.
