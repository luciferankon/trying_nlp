A year before Chaz Dowdell's "The Chute" Cooper Ray of Delta Tau Phi gets ready for one of the infamous DTF pledge initiation party's for the new freshmen. while getting ready Cooper Ray decides to show DTF's frat leader RJ a thesis from Coopers twin brother Teddy rogers ray. Cooper and RJ read the thesis and while reading it find out a dark twisted story that happens on their rival fraternity Beta Alpha Nu fraternity house.
