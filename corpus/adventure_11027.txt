Johana is absolutely in her element while adventuring-- she is daring confident and an excellent shot. Alex meanwhile can't seem to find his footing. The pair sets out treasure hunting when a battle with a mysterious figure goes awry. They must set their days of treasure hunting aside to try and reverse the battle's lingering curse running into friends and foes as they trek across this fantastical world. First they head to a Saloon then to a Victorian mansion - Their findings eventually lead them to a place from which no adventurer has ever returned.
