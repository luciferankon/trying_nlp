This episode focuses on the musical work of Johnny Mercer and is presented as a rehearsal for a performance of a musical revue called "Words by Johnny Mercer". Song and dance numbers are presented to such all-time Mercer favorites as "Arthur Murray Taught Me Dancing in a Hurry" "Dream" "Fools Rush In" "G.I. Jive" "Jeepers Creepers" "Pardon My Southern Accent" "Skylark" and "That Old Black Magic".
