Ever since Kokoro has stopped dreaming while sleeping she can't smile. Lilou does all she can to help her friend and bring both the dreams and the smile back to Kokoro's life. Kokoro suddenly disappears. Lilou wants to find her so she embarks on an unusual journey during which a dream-book and a video-game will serve her as guides. The second picture by Izuru Kumasaki is an extraordinary surrealistic children's tale for adults.
