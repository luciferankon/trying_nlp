Plot: Tara Delvecchio (Olivia Virkler) is starting her second semester at Collins University her father who recently passed was a Italian mobster who owed a Mexican drug dealer named Martino Gomez (Elvis Rosario) money before he passed during this new semester Tara bumps into Martino and now he is after her meanwhile other characters like Jake Stookley & Rock Boy (Sean Iacobbo Steve Robinson & Ramy Hammoud) try to impress a girl named Olivia Valentine (Pelinsu Mese) they try to get her high and have a gang bang but find themselves in the middle of helping Tara & her father's former right hand man Vito (Brian Kronsberg) face Martino.
