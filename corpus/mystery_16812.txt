The Right Eye story begins with a girl waking up in a hotel room with no recollection of her memory all she has are five unidentified items left in the room. She has to discover the backstory behind each clue plus their correlation to one another. And she only has 24 hours to solve the mystery; at the same time she has to find out who she is how she got involved in this incident. Through the course of her pursuit she encounters a series of unexplainable events and a group of mysterious individuals. She eventually uncovers a missing link between various historical events: The Great 1906 San Francisco Earthquake the secret behind Harry Houdini's final magic act early history of Tiffany & Co. history of the Wells Fargo Bank during the Old West period and origin of the Winchester Rifle Company. The revelation of connection between these historical events creates a ripple effect that forces history to rewrite itself.
