Lance Stewart. Preston Sterling. Yancy Kaminski. Chad Steele. Shaun Meadows. Individually they are five young visionaries who share love for music and dreams of superstardom. Together they form a blossoming artistic unit of refreshing youth coupled with deep emotional resonance - so deep they haven't even come up with a name for themselves yet. Nonetheless this cohesive collection of high school harmonics is poised to dominate the boy band scene over the coming year.
