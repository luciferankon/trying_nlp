BEAST is based on a true story ripped from the headlines about an teen-aged African American all-star football player who initiates a relationship with a shy introverted Caucasian girl. In the beginning he is a sweetcharming and caring boyfriend but quickly turns into a controlling abusive beast.She becomes afraid to leave the relationship. She is forced to move away. Drama heightens when he begins to date a popular confident African American girl. His patterns of abuse intensifies as rage begot violence begot murder.
