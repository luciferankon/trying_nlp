Three compelling stories collide at the local high school and soon the underbelly of the Internet is exposed with shocking impact. 15 year old JADA is trying to find her place in the fragmented world of her parent's divorce. She seems to find an understanding ear with her on line relationship with Jeremiah or does she? The answer tears apart her life. JULIAN teaches high school students with personal dreams of a better life for his wife and baby daughter. Unfortunately his young wife LISA is restless and finds an outlet on the Internet leading to devastating results. DEREK'S family has all the outside appearances of suburban perfection but dark ugly secrets hide just below the surface. A friend's addiction soon becomes the catalysis to break apart the twisted lies. The Internet the world's information highway and fantasy freeway.
