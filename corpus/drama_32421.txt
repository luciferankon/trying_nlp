Hidden in a small village in early 19th century New England a tale of desire obsession and scandal. Human relationships are tested tragedies faced and journeys begun all centered around the immortal question: who is allowed to love? Inspired by the life and poetry of Emily Dickinson. Directed by Academy Award nominee and Emmy winner ELLEN KURAS (Eternal Sunshine of the Spotless Mind The Betrayal: Nerakhoon A Little Chaos Ozark Legion). A co-production with M.Y.R.A. Entertainment Europe.
