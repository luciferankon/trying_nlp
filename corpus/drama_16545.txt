Vorotily is a saga about the generation born in the USSR. As they grew up the country collapsed and a new state emerged. Four friends - Vsevolod Nikolai Dmitry and Mikhail - have started their businesses during college. They created legitimate financial schemes while constantly clashing with the existing government. A few years later friends become real business sharks while maintaining their friendship dignity and decency. On the eve of the 1998 Russian financial crisis the four friends set off to conquer the capital...
