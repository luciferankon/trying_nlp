Op 'Til You Drop is a television pilot where a pair of fashion 'tragics' are taken op-shopping and made-over with second-hand bargains and treasures. Our style king and queen former world champion roller skater and fashion designer Jayson Sutcliffe and award-winning Australian pin-up and vintage enthusiast Ms Lily White will transform the tragic and show them what op-shopping is all about: a fun treasure hunt promoting sustainability re-usability and experimentation all on a budget.
