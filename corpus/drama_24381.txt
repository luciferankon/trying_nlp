Based on The Divine Comedy Dante tells of the poet of the same name's travels through Hell Purgatory and Paradise in search of his recently deceased true love Beatrice. Before he is redeemed by his love for her he learns the meaning of evil sin damnation and forgiveness through a series of unforgettable experiences and encounters.
