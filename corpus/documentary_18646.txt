One of the most popular and influential female RB groups of all time The Pointer Sisters combined the influences of jazz soul gospel scat pop and sometimes even country music into a winning amalgam. They continue to perform their enduring and new songs that maintain their devoted following this concert was recorded in April of 2004 at the Alberta Bair Theatre in Billings Montana. It features the most recent lineup consisting of Ruth Anita and Ruth's daughter Issa and the group plays some of their most enduring hits. The set list includes Fairytale Chain Of Fools Yes We Can and I'm So Excited among many others.
