The planet's natural ecosystems and regenerating bio-capacity are being severely degraded and as a result this compromises the ability of the planet to sustain life. Forests fisheries oceans rangeland fresh water systems and other natural ecosystems are all threatened while many are on the verge of collapse. Water land and air are getting increasingly polluted water tables are falling soil erosion is leading to desertification global warming is well underway and species are dying out 1000 times faster than their natural rate of extinction.
