This intense book of courage written by Marita Patos is coming to the big screen with the finest in talent. Her story of her dreams of living in America and her fight for freedom  even when taken as a political prisoner is a haunting and very timely story. Marita and her husband made drastic attempts to cross the wall but were captured. Undaunted they faced imprisonment torture and brutal living conditions. Marita even within the prison walls spoke of freedom and hope. AKA "Trapped Behind the Iron Curtain."
