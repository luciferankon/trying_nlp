According to one of the oldest books in the Bible ancient people lived not just several decades but several centuries. Is there any truth to this story? Could it be that the secret to this amazing longevity is about to be rediscovered? And might the search for Noah's Ark hold the key? The answers may be concealed in one of the most inaccessible and hostile locations in the world. High atop a mountain in Eastern Turkey is a 5000-year old wooden ship. Could it be Noah's Ark? Might modern satellite evidence confirm the ark is not only real but is still visible today? And how might this astonishing artifact lead us to uncover "The Longevity Secret?"
