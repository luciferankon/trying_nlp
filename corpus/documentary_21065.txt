Born from the depths of the Great Depression and nurtured by the New Deal of the 1930s P.J. Jacobs still continues to be a venue of quality education to this day. This movie celebrates the fact that during one of the most crucial and challenging eras of Stevens Point Wisconsin and United States history the community had the courage wisdom and forward-thinking perspective to build an architecturally timeless and visually compelling public school building that has withstood the test of time.
