It is a story about a bank manager who has been falsely accused on stealing and embezzeling five lakh rupees and after being released from jail he learnt that his colleagues have stolen the money and his mother had died in freak accident and his lover left him heartbroken. He left the city and faked his death and he turned into a Sikh industrialist with the help of an old wealthy zamindar and took revenge against the colleagues who ruined his life and after that he found his love
