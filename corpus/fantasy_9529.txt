Ruka Izumo just your average university girl who dresses up in the current fashion trends listens to popular music and watches the typical TV shows finds a mysterious love letter at a library when she opens a book of ancient poems (Manyoshu- a collection of 8th century ancient Japanese poems). As the days pass she finds the mysterious exchange between the two lovers captivating. Later she discovers about the real secret of these letters. Enjoy a love story that treads down a slightly older road of romance.
