Petru a drilling engineer living in a community of oil industry workers has a conflict. On the one hand he is about to get married to his pregnant girlfriend Laura and on the other he is involved with the wife of one of his colleagues Sonia who suffers an accident at work. Petru's new life seems to be starting while Sonia feels hers is ending. Although he feels indebted to her he also feels responsible towards his unborn child. Laura finds out about his relationship with Sonia unleashing a series of events. Petru and Sonia end up seeking comfort in each other's arms. Both suffer irreversible damage as they desperately attempt to survive.
