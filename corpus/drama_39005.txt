Raj (Gurunandan) is a wealthy man an ideal student a doting son who has everything one could ever want. He has the perfect life yet all this perfection is superficial. He longs for something. Will the new boy in campus (Snehith Gowda) be able to help him find solace? Will Raj be able to rekindle his romance with his high school sweetheart Prarthana (Sharanya)? Will Rajs life be complete?
