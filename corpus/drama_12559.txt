Brianna Johnson accompanied by her best friend Emily visits a night club where two con men were waiting for them. The information exchanged during casual conversation with these two characters will lead to a painful episode in their young lives. The following day Brianna becomes a kidnapped victim taken by a gang and held prisoner in a local whorehouse where a familiar face by chance comes to see her but he's not there to rescue the young lady having just swallowed some sexual stimulants.
