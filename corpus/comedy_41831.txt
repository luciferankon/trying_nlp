Heart-warming romantic comedy is about Tilek who is secretly in love with Kanykei a college star. Tilek decides to become Kanykei's best friend and win her trust. Then he starts getting rid of all Kanykei's suitors by gossiping about them. One day Kanykei meets Rasul who seems to be an ideal guy. Now Tilek is determined to find Rasul's skeleton in the closet.
