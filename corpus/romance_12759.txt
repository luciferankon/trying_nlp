Manju's mother was very strictly disciplined. One day Manju listen singing form Nirmal and She fell in love. Nirmal went to Manju's mother and said to her mother that he wanted to sing the song of Manju. But Manju's mother refused his proposal. Everyday Manju went to Nirmal's sister house for sewing. Their love story built up there. Manju's mother stopped her from sewing. When Manju's mother knows of everything then Nirmal and Manju decided to marry.
