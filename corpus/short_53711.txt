Forever Ahead is a short film written and directed by Sanjeev Sharma. The Armed Forces personify strength righteousness and 'service before self ' who distinguish themselves as a group of brave men who give up all they have to stay in isolated border posts and guard the security and security of the Nation. Also amidst the changing internal and external dynamics the Armed Forces have adapted to a plethora of roles once thought extremely different from their agenda. The film 'Forever Ahead' is a short insightful and absorbing visual which tries to highlight the overwhelming dedication and commitment of the Armed Forces towards the Nation & Countrymen. The film depicts the beautiful affiliation connect and bonding between the Army and the common man and conveys the message that no matter what the Armed Forces are always there to ensure the safety and integrity of the Nation and its people.
