The Projection Room is a mind-bending thriller that illustrates the arduous battle Cole undergoes in his mind as he struggles to define what is real and what is merely a projection of his subconscious. Cole plucks an unlabeled package out of the mail containing a heart-shaped locket intended for his wife Laila; he resists confronting her about the affair suppressing his nightmare until it comes out in a barrage of projections where in them he is physically hurting Laila.
