American Movie Classics produced this original salute to those celebrities who worked to improve the morale of the soldiers in World War II. Hosted by Tony Randall the one hour presentation included many clips from the news USO shows Armed Forces Radio US Savings Bonds and more. Interviews were conducted with some of those involved in the efforts including Bob Hope Frances Langford Esther Williams Dorothy Lamour Roddy McDowell Eddie Bracken Anne Jeffries and Maxine Andrews.
