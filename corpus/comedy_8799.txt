Forever is a long time practically an eternity when you think about it. Don't. It will do your head in. Watch this show instead. Arj Barker is Australia's adopted son of comedy. Having frequented our shores for the past 7 years he has built up a fan base of loyal followers. A regular occurrence on Australia's TV screens; on panel shows such as Good News Week Spicks and Specks the 7pm Project and as Dave on hit show Flight of the Concords. Recorded in front of a capacity crowd at Sydney's beautiful State Theatre.
