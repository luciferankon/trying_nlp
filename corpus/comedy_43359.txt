A delicious combo of romantic-comedy and social satire the film meanders through the journey of a 'gift' yearning to be unwrapped. A beautifully wrapped gift is caught in an uncaring society of party-goers who have passed it on to others without unwrapping it its journey will be complete only when it fulfil its purpose. Combining 2D animation with quirky characters (actors) The Gift Of...takes the audience on a hilarious journey unravelling the hypocrisies of the society.
