Profiles six competitors in the 2004 Annual Whistler's Convention held in Louisburg North Carolina. We hear them whistle and they and others talk about whistling as a way to relax cheer up entertain express emotion and enjoy life. Each talks about how others see their hobby. Joe is older competing after a ten years break. Chris uses interesting sounds to spice up his performances. Graham stays with the melody as does Sandra. Steve is smooth a multi-year champion. Geert is new having come with his wife from the Netherlands for the competition. First the prelims then the finals. Clips from films illustrate the qualities of whistling.
