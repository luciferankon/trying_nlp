After the 1973 coup that deposed Allende and brought Pinochet to power in Chile the former members of his cabinet are imprisoned on Dawson Island the world's southernmost concentration camp. Veteran filmmaker Miguel Littin follows the ordeal of these men who are determined to survive and provide history with their testimony.
