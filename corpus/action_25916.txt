Recoil is an action driven feature with US government asset Adam Lancaster (Stephen Preston) being assigned to terminate Redwood (Jonathan Pienaar) a notorious drug lord at the ready to massacre 3000 innocent villagers for his oil lines in the Sudan. The mission for our Hero takes a turn when a little 7 year old girl becomes a target for our ruthless killer. The suspense and action builds up even more when Lancaster's wife and now adopted daughter are being taken hostage by Redwood and his team of thugs. LANCASTER faces betrayal from Hamilton (Jena Waldron) a corrupt US government official. Adam is counted on to save the day and live up to his reputation as a true American Hero.
