The life in a rural countryside is boring and monotonous. Every single day is predictable. The movie depicts lives of random inhabitants of the village. Here is the young woman Tania who is wasting her youth on everyday routine: household - the hospital job - the eccentric mother in constant search for love. The local surgeon Bulba cannot choose between his wife and a mistress; Iraida Fedorovna who cannot let her dead husband go. And here is the young doctor Timur - intelligent nice man who made Tania feel beloved but he also has his own problems. The seemingly calm peace in the province faraway is getting confronted with the deep emotions of every single character. Their insight world is full of passion and tensity. Every single story is a whole microcosm of drama and human desire to know oneself.
