Featuring flashback footage and live interviews with two stars of 70s prime time soap opera "Number 96" Elaine Lee and Jeff Kevin. Steve Bedwell and Number 96 historian Ian McLean discuss fan reactions to the show. Comedian Vince Sorrenti conducts a tour of Moncur Flats at 83 Moncur Street Woollahra the actual location of the Number 96 building seen in the show's credits.
