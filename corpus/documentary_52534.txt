Box Art - A Gaming Documentary looks to probe all eras of gaming across PC and home consoles. We'll connect with the artists discover their process how box art has evolved the role of said cover art and even look into related pieces like game manuals strategy guides and gaming magazines. From big questions like "when does something become art and/or collectible?" to "does art have to by tangible to be art?" we hope to hit all corners of the canvas with this film.
