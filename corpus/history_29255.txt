The mystic Rumi and his mentor Shams Tabrizi find one another along their respective journeys. they instruct activate and awaken the parts of one another as yet undiscovered They mutually assist one another in cultivating a deeper connection of and love of the Divine. But the path is not strewn with roses alone. Many thorns appear as well- jealousy envy betrayal and murder. How will these deeply spiritual beings deal with the harshness and cruelty of this world while also having a profound relationship and understanding of its sublime and Divine presence?
