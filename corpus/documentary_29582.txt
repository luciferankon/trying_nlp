Shaynna Blaze takes us on an exciting journey of discovery through the design capitals of Milan Venice and New York as she seeks inspiration for her own iconic lighting range. She shows us to some of the world's most innovative and cutting edge designs and meets with experts in the field who share their take on the latest trends. Digging beneath the surface Shaynna uncovers some unexpected delights that help her develop her ideas as she continues her journey towards the creation and marketing of her lights. But time is not on her side and this adventure has many bumps in the road...
