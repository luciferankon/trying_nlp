For years urban legends have warned about an isolated Southern town where the locals ran-off their police department and outsiders fear to tread. To blues musician Big Mike Griffin Iron City seemed like the perfect subject for a song. However first he must find out for himself if the legends are true. Join Big Mike and his guide a cigar-chomping ex-Marine named Jason Neese as they ride their Harleys through Tennessee's backwoods into a place that even cops fear. You'll meet a collection of unforgettable characters as you witness the creation of a blues song about the most notorious town in the South. Welcome to Iron City. Watch your back.
