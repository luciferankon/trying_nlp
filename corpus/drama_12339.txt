Damian is a 45 year old lawyer married with two daughters who undergoing a deep crisis finds refuge in an abandoned building down town. One night Damian meets Estela a local transvestite who takes advantage of the privacy of the building to satisfy her clients. Form that moment on a strange relationship between them starts. Apparently Damian and Estela Seem yo be quite different but they are not. Through Estela's eyes Damian begins to look at things from a different point of view to know himself deeply and to start making a decision that will affect the rest of his life.
