Over 50000 dogs and cats were left behind in New Orleans in the aftermath of Hurricane Katrina as FEMA required that all animals be left behind in the mandatory evacuation.This forced separation created America's first-ever major animal rescue.A dedicated and compassionate group of volunteer rescuers and animal welfare groups from around the world risked their lives to sledgehammer down doors and brave toxic floodwaters in a truly heroic effort to save nearly 10000 animals.Hurricane Heroes is a film about hope and survival in the face of one of the worst natural disasters in American history.It will renew your faith in the American spirit.
