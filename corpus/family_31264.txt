Abbie Walker knew from the beginning that solving mysteries was her destiny. Little did she know however that a secret government agency watching her every move -- waiting for the day when they could bring her into their fold. What they didn't count on was another young adversary... planning Abbie's demise. From the discovery of a secret underground office to the forming of her detective agency Timepiece will take you on a roller coaster ride through time as Abbie and her friends race to put right what her evil nemesis has altered somewhere in time.
