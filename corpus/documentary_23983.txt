Straight from the Streets The Wordz Project is a gritty behind-the-scenes slice of life look at the stories from hip-hops true Godfathers and Poets - Bone Thugs-n-Harmony Pat's Justice Lil' Drawz The Last Poets Mr. Mix and Yo Yo - as they find spiritual guidance and the true meaning of the words of Christ. Featuring visionary Michael Beckwith (The Secret) who with his words of wisdom echoes the spiritual experiences expressed by these infamous founders of the hip-hop movement that you won't find on MTV or BET.
