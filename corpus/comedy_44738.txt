A local housewife Mary Fitzpatrick suddenly appears to die shortly after making love to her fisherman husband Finn. The whole community turns out for Mary's wake along with her four children. During Father Fennell's eulogy he splashes holy water on Mary's body and to everybody's surprise she suddenly opens her eyes and sits up - alive! From that moment on all hell breaks loose as . . . her priest tries to make a saint out of her . . . her doctor faces a malpractice charge . . . her husband's ex-girlfriend wants a religious experience . . . her children think she has brain damage . .. . . her unexpected pregnancy is heralded as The Second Coming . . . the lingerie factory owners who employed her want her to endorse their product and the church is ready to excommunicate her. Mary had a quiet dull life . . . until she died. Whoever said dying is easy?
