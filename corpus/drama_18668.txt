Corey and Nicole Langford are seemingly a perfect couple. He a promising medical student at the top of his class and she owner of Chicago's top natural hair salon. Everything about their relationship was ideal expect their sex life. Determined to make every aspect of their new marriage impeccable Nicole grants her husband his fantasy a three-some with his sexy classmate Whitney; But only if they can also have one with her handsome co-worker Trevor. How will these two outsiders ruin this perfect union? And who will love and sex destroy in the end?
