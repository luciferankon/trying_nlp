An architect is driving his woman who is a publisher to the airport and he tells that he intends to move to her house and his office in her basement to save money; however she tells that she will leave him since she has met another man. They are surprised by a storm and they stay trapped in her apartment. They find a cat in a hole in front of the apartment and the man rescues the animal. Soon their neighbors arrive in the apartment seeking for the cat but the animal is hidden. Meanwhile they prepare dinner together.
