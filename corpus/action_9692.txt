When Benigno (Raul Zaragosa) decides to become a priest as a fulfillment of his mother's dying wish his pregnant girlfriend Maribel (Jennifer Dimla) commits suicide. In retaliation Maribel's father the powerful mining magnate Don Benito (Bob Soler) orders Benigno's family massacred. Benigno would have perished too if not for his timely rescue by the local maiden Urduja (Melanie Marquez). Urduja is also Don Benito's enemy over a land dispute involving the cultural minority's ancestral lands. It promises to be a bloody fight to the end as Urduja and Benigno join their hands against Don Benito and his son Dennis (Julio Diaz).
