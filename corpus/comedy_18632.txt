Fátima and Sueli are good friends. Both independent and single the two share an apartment in Méier a district in the suburbs of Rio de Janeiro and work in a shop that rents and sells bridal gowns for wedding ceremonies in Copacabana Djalma Brides. Every day they deal with the dreams of many women also hoping to find their own Prince Charming but the task does not seem to be easy. After all the love life of the two is surrounded by confusion.
