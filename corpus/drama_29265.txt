Struggling artist Anna Larsen's mother has never understood her. So when Anna returns home to Minnesota to help care for her ailing mom she brings years of family baggage with her. But the rediscovery of a magical mailbox from her childhood and the search to solve its mystery forces Anna to confront the fact that maybe she's the one who doesn't understand. DRAGONFLY is an independent feature film about homecoming hurt and healing for a Minnesota family divided by divorce and illness. It's about how we see the world who we are in the world and how those two things don't always line up. And it's a little bit of a mystery.
