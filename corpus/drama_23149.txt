While still in film school Diego (Dida Andrade) stood out as one of the most promising students. However arriving at the job market he can not establish himself as a filmmaker being forced to film wedding ceremonies. While struggling to make his first film and be recognized as a director he tries to survive his greatest fear and object of desire: women. He still has to face his complicated relationship with women which awaken in him the desire and the fear. He has a platonic passion for a young librarian girl but does not know how to approach her.
