A realistic glimpse of the Filipino youth in the gulf of Manila's urban gullies that tackles socially-related issues surrounding the intersecting lives of marginalized ordinary Manile≈Ños. Presented in a staggered vista of correlated events the movie explores the day-to-day survival of the characters and their broken dreams pent-up anger and abnormal sexuality. It conveys its message powerfully in a daring and unapologetic way-- using street lingo to it's fullest. The story unfolds one night when Rey the docile and cowardly teenage student is mauled as he passes by a drinking spree of street toughies: Pogie Jopet Kahoy and Taba.
