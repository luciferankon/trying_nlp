'Waterfalls of the Southern Highlands' features the best of the many hundreds of specular waterfalls in the Southern Mountains of Western North Carolina Eastern Tennessee and the northern mountains of Georgia South Carolina and Alabama. (Yep Alabama has mountains!) Episode #1: 'Some Carolina Mountain Falls' shows eight falls from the 'Land of Waterfalls' in Western North Carolina's Transylvania Jackson and Macon counties. These falls include the most popular waterfall in the Southeast Looking Glass Falls; Toxaway Falls; the very beautiful Silver Run Falls; Whitewater Falls the highest waterfall in Eastern America; Satulah Falls; the ethereal Bridal Veil Falls; Dry Falls which are anything but dry; and Cullasaja Falls. View the thunder up close and personal.
