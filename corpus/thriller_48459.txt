In the search for the truth about the Fate of her grandfather Julie (Nina Corradini) faces the past. Julie is driven by questionable evidence of a dangerous game to understand the past of her family. She and her best friend Nicole are on the best way to unravel the mystery of her grandfather. But suddenly everything is different. As she pursues she involuntarily meets the two strange guys Johnny and Richie. Soon it turns out that the four could not imagine what actually comes to them.
