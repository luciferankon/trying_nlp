Every May in glittering Las Vegas the world's best poker players pay up to $10000 of their own money to compete in the ultimate card game . . . the World Series of Poker. POKER KINGS follows the high stakes journey of five players over 12 months in their quest to win poker's Holy Grail and its $2.5 million first prize. Featuring: Phil Hellmuth Jr. Simon Trumper Garry Bush Rob Varkonyi and Carlo Citrone.
