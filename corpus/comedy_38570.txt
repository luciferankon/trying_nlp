Shuvo (Dev) works as a collection agent of ICICI in Kolkata and leads a happy life along with his friends and his father Dibakar Burman (Mithun Chakraborty) who wishes to see Shuvo married to a girl soon. Once Shuvo rushes to a Hospital where his friend is admitted as he tried to commit suicide by drinking nail polish solution and asks the reason for it. Then he describes about the traits of Maria (Koel Mallick) and her uncle (Kharaj Mukherjee). The duo have a habit of cheating gullible young men including his friend and Shuvo decides to teach them a lesson. Herogiri is a remake of the Telugu hit Balupu. The story is about the heroine and her uncle who specialize in taking unsuspecting guys for a ride in the name of love. The hero (Dev) decides to give them a taste of their own medicine for duping his friend. But Dev himself is trying to run away from his notorious past which catches up eventually.
