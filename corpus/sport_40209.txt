Mr. Olympia is one of the most significant and well-known international bodybuilding competitions held under the auspices of the International Federation of Bodybuilding (IFBB) from September 26 to 29 2013 in Las Vegas  Nevada USA. It was the 49th on the account competition of "Mr. Olympia". The prize fund was 675000 dollars. Under the new rules of the IFBB only winners of professional tournaments for the competitive 2012 were accepted for the competition but there were exceptions for 5 bodybuilders who received the right to compete on the points scored in the qualification. The winner was the American bodybuilder Phil Heath having won this title for the third time.
