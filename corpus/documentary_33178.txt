For the better part of 50 years Rebekah has been trying to recover from the traumatic events that shaped her life and behaviour. "Love Rebekah" takes you into the intimacy of Rebekah's journey of love relationships and her mental ill health. Hoping to humanize mental illness she shares intimate moments of her everyday life: thoughts feelings emotions that allow you to witness the pain and the stigma of this often misunderstood affliction. With the WHO estimating that mental health problems affect 1 in 4 people in the world Rebekah's story is as important as ever.
