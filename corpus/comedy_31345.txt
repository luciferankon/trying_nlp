Do time with four losers in a crumbling prison surrounded by lunatics terrorists and criminal masterminds - and that's just the guards! Flatrock is a fresh and light-hearted look at prison life from the inmate's perspective. It's about four likeable losers and their adventures while doing time. Breaking every prison rule and convict code alike Flatrock Jake 8-Ball and Chapo are at odds with everyone.They're out of luck with literally no place to go.
