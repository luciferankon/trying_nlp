After an obscure indie sci-fi film explodes into a worldwide blockbuster overnight the film's young lead actor Thomas Emmeles must rapidly readjust to his new found fame. Lack of privacy a distant family and a greedy agent make the hours following Thomas' meteoric rise to fame chaotic and full of debate. In the harrowing face of his success Thomas must find faith in his abilities and the sincerity of the people who admire him if he ever wants to retain his identity and pursue his passion.
