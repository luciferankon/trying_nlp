Narrated by Matthew Broderick. We follow the family of USS Arizona survivor Raymond Haerry on a 5000 mile journey as they return to Pearl Harbor to place their father's ashes back aboard the great battleship. Ray Haerry Sr. passed away in September of 2016. In April of 2017 the Haerry family had their father/grandfather's ashes interred on the Arizona so he can once again be with the over 1000 shipmates who lost their lives on December 7 1941 when Pearl Harbor was attacked plunging America into World War II.
