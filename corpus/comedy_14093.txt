After performing the Rite of Penance Jim is startled when a chair flies across the room. He reaches out to his Priest Papa Gumpta Goomp. Papa deduces a demon is haunting Jim and calls for a cleansing. During the exorcism we get a glimpse of Jim's past. Jim a former atheist became religious when Papa rescued him from a cycle of addiction and self-abuse. The exorcism concludes with Papa violating Jim with an ice cream cone. Jim asks his sister Charlene to have a private investigator look into Papa's past. Upon discovering Jim's betrayal Papa traps Jim inside a magical snow globe for seven years. When Jim finally emerges he accepts his duty to his Father and Lord. Wearing a homemade bomb under his trench coat Jim walks into a Cold Stone Creamery and detonates the device.
