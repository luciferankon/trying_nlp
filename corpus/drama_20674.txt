Frustrated by his promiscuous wife and haunted by memories of an abusive stepfather Chad's self-esteem is at an all-time low. A psychiatrist attempts to help rid him of his feelings of inadequacy but Chad soon realizes that only he himself can bring about a change for the better. Of course nothing worthwhile is ever easy... To leave the past behind take charge of his life and become a more dedicated husband and father he'll need to face certain challenges and confrontations head on - ready or not!
