The Linda Show LA is a comedic web series appropriately named after its star Linda. She is an excitable overly emotional fame-obsessed aspiring socialite who makes social and political commentary...accidentally. Her sheltered nature becomes visible on her dating advice vlog when her farcical guests push her out of her comfort zone. She tackles sexism racism sexuality and more while exploring the city of LA. She is a strong female character though sometimes unknowingly offensive who will win you over with her big heart.
