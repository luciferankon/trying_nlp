Furio Moroni is out of prison six years in which he lost everything including his son. But now it's time to start over with what he's got left: his 50 years the diabetes that obliges him to inject daily doses of insulin and the money from the last hold-up. And then there's Uno the gigantic dog his son left behind. Irene the woman he was with before he went in. When Uno is stolen for the dogfights of East Europe Furio must save the dog if he is to save himself or die in the attempt.
