News Desk is a surreal episodic comedy about two ex lawyers Kandy and Adam Cho who have started a news show. Kandy dealing with her own set of flaws is determined to use her news anchor status to confront issues in social justice that the legal corporate world has failed to address. Meanwhile Adam who has been unsuccessful in getting Kandy to commit to him with his overzealous advances is trying every single avenue to win her over and get back at her at the same time leading to the love hate relationship between the two.
