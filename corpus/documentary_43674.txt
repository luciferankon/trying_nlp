For half a century Tibet has been under the shadow of Chinese rule. Despite Chinese attempts to oppress all glimpses of Tibetan culture Tibetans have tenaciously maintained their heritage. This film told through the story of one Tibetan monk documents the firm grasp Tibetans still hold on their culture.
