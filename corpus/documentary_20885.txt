HowardFilms follows up the hit tarpon mini-series Chasing Silver with the unexpected sequel: Chasing Silver: Location X. The one-hour special takes three accomplished anglers (from the Westernmost Southernmost and Easternmost points in North America and joins them all in a secret fishery for the trip of a lifetime. The anonymous guide remains masked throughout the journey - to protect his and the fishery's identity - and the anglers don blindfolds and sign agreements to protect the location. Viewers will see (usually wary) tarpon eat a flies within a rod-length of the boat and behave as if they are trained to perform. Anglers and guide coordinate for personal fish of a lifetime. The cinematography continues the HowardFilms film-style tradition - and the angling action includes things never seen on TV before! And things that may never be seen again...
