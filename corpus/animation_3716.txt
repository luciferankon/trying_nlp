The hub of world economy Sam City... Found here is the famous economics institute the MBA School. It's well known for training outstanding economics specialists. 4 kids enter the school this year. Summer a mischievous economics genius. Yoony the proud but warm hearted daughter-of-a-millionaire. Luey the soft-hearted artist. And Toto the smart and haughty bully. Our four heroes team up and use their economic knowledges to fight against the Akeron Enterprise a menacing threat monopolizing Sam City's economy.
