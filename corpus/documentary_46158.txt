A look at the California wine industry. With insight and commentary from many members of the wine world - including Two Buck Chuck's Fred Franzia champion racecar driver and winery owner Mario Andretti award winning winemakers Bob Foley of Pride Mountain Vineyards Daniel Barron of Silver Oak Cellars and Randy Pitts of Harvest Moon Estate Winery along with many others. This documentary shows that that much more than just grapes go into the bottle.
