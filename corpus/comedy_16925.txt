The story of two families living at opposite ends of the freeway. Bess Denyar is a doctor with a posh mother Margaret an architect husband Danny Bright and twin 13-year-olds at a private school Oscar and Edwina. When Bess finds out that she is adopted she is stunned but even more so when she meets her birth parents Wayne and Julie Wheeler. She also discovers that she has three siblings: Amber Kayne and Brianna. The 'bogan' Wheelers head up a drag racing team in the outer suburbs and are thrilled to discover the daughter they thought they had lost.
