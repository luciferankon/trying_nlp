Egle Petazzoni is a middle-aged postwoman and loves to wander the streets of Bologna by bike. It would be a postwoman like many others but two small details make she special: not only she was Lucio Dalla postwoman  but she opened some of the letters addressed to him - What she discovered is that some of the characters from Dalla's songs have decided to write to the Italian Singer: Anna and Marco Futura Meri Luis and many others had their secrets and wanted to reveal them to Lucio. Egle Petazzoni reading the letters finds out all their secrets.
