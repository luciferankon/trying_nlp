HOOD HOOPS is a raw glimpse into the mind motivation and personalities of a true cross-section of street basketball. While presenting intimate and candid reflections from some of the games' most notable faces (i.e. NBA Players Coaches and Legends) HOOD HOOPS goes further to introduce the audience to outstanding street players like "Main Events" "Skip to my Lou" or the "Bone Collector." This documentary features summer league highlights from New York to Los Angeles and contains interviews with Baron Davis Steve Francis Alonzo Mourning Paul Pierce Patrick Ewing and many others. It contains new music from the UN Sadat X and the "Draft Picks" a super compilation of raw MC's.
