The beloved "Boys Of Summer" are back in this critically-acclaimed National PBS Special Presentation. This film traces the history of the Brooklyn Dodgers from their beginnings in the late 1800s to the day they left Brooklyn in 1957. Special guest stars include: DUKE SNIDER CLEM LABINE CAMPANELLA CARL ERSKINE YOGI BERRA WILLIE MAYS PHIL RIZZUTO and many more.
