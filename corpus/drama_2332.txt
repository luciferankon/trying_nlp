Claudia Garancy has had the misfortune to lose her husband in an accident. Since then she has transferred her affection and tenderness to her daughter Josapha. Thanks to her motherly love her lucidity and her patient vigilance she has tried to guide little Josapha on the path of happiness. So when Claudia finds out that her daughter now a young lady secretly sings in a night-club she is alarmed. To her indeed being an artist represents the worst hindrance to happiness being...
