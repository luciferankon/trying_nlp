How to Sell a Film: (3rd Time Lucky) is the incredible little film that nearly didn't get made but did because cult film-makers Seumas Next and Stephan Kern never gave up secured an international Film Festival World Premiere for "How 2 Make a Film (Billion $ Weekend)" got the cinema to sell out touched a nerve with the audience and sold it in the lobby of the theatre. Frustrated at being unable to showcase their "cinema verite" masterpieces "How to Make a Film" and it's sequel "How 2 Make a Film (Billion $ Weekend)" Executive Producers Seumas Next and Stephan Kern finally unlock their passport to instant Hollywood success.
