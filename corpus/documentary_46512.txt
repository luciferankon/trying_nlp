On August 11th 1877 Dimiter Petkov lost his left hand in the battle on Mount Shipka in the RussoTurkish War. At the time he was an 18-year-old illiterate from Dobrudja who wanted only to be liberated. Three decades later already a Prime Minister he was assassinated in the center of Sofia. Slave Born is a documentary about one of the first builders of modern Bulgaria about his ideas dreams illusions and disappointments.
