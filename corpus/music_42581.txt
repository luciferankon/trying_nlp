At a gala event in 2010 Neil Young is recognised as the musician of the year by MusiCares. At the event many recognised musos paid tribute to the decades of service Neil has given to the music industry by performing some of his fantastic hits. Artists include Keith Urban CSN Norah Jones Dave Matthews James Taylor Sir Elton John Lady Antebellum and many others.
