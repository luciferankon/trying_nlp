The Crew of the Freighter Antaries detect a distress beacon and are contract bound to render assistance. On arrival they find a military ship with a renegade killer android and a single survivor with a deadly secret. Aware that all their lives are in danger the crew take immediate action to distance themselves from the stricken ship. However the danger is already aboard and begins to kill. The crew know they are outclassed and have little hope for survival as even their own android seems to be turning against them. No mater how desperate the steps they take are it seems nothing will stop this menace from picking them off one by one. In the end the remaining crew decide to take the ultimate risk to save not only themselves but hundreds on the nearby colony.
