Sebastiăo's two-decade quest is to use badminton a sport he never played to transform the kids in a humble favela in Rio de Janeiro. Kids loved the sport from the beginning but were crushed in tournaments. Using love sweat and the innovative training technique of samba dancing to teach badminton Sebastiăo imagined and created a community of champions family and dreams. The competitive yet tender relationships of young players and the qualifying of players for the 2016 Olympic Games reveal that even the most unlikely of dreams can become reality.
