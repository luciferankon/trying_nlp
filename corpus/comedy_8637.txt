Un-Natural Selection follows Joe and Sarah two recently single 30/40 something's as they stumble through the confusing & intimidating world of internet dating. They seek advice from their small circle of friends on how to present themselves online rules about coffee dates sexting strategic touching etc. Each character has a very different take on how to pursue the opposite sex. But Joe & Sarah must ultimately find a way to be true to themselves while going outside their comfort zones to find that special someone.
