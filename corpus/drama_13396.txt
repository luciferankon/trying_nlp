Happy Baby is a movie based on the novel of the same name by Stephen Elliott and is the story of Theo once an orphan in the New York foster care system now a grown man living in California. He returns to New York to reconnect with the love of his life. An abused former orphan living in San Francisco returns to New York to reconnect with the love of his life and understand how his past has shaped his future. It's a semi-autobiographical work based on the book of the same name originally published by McSweeney's.
