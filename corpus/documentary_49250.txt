Broadcast on Israel's Channel 2 for Rabin Memorial Remembrance Day this is a story of a town square-but not just any square. It captures the history of the Kings of Israel Square known as Rabin Square. This large concrete area has become an arena for Israel's national drama. The political demonstrations and historical celebrations that have taken place on this spot reflect the heartbeat of Israel. When a controversial plan to convert the square into a parking lot is discovered the relevance and importance of the square are highlighted.
