A diverse group of New Orleans artists and musicians struggle to find meaning in their lives work and relationships one year after Katrina. A nuanced view of the city and its people it shows the changing landscape of New Orleans as it has never been seen before dispelling the stereotypes of Bourbon Street and Twenties' jazz to reveal the tragic defiant joyful city many call 'the Soul of America.' A young musician tries to eke out a living selling worthless real estate. A dying woman constructs a secret oasis in the ruins of an abandoned home. A single mom's schemes to find love and money put her daughter in danger and a failed writer can't stop fantasizing about his dentist. Together these creative malcontents will do their best to bring life back to New Orleans one year after Hurricane Katrina.
