10 Marias: One Year Later is a one-hour documentary special revisiting the beloved real-person cast of the hit CBC series How Do You Solve a Problem Like Maria? One year after Elicia McKenzie was crowned the winner of the coveted role of Maria von Trapp in the Toronto production of The Sound of Music we visit her runner up and understudy Janna Polzin and many of the other finalists and find out how participating in this popular reality series changed their lives. We also examine the evolving relationship between reality television and theatre.
