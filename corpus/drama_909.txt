A Family On Edge is about two brothers Michael and Gabriel Bonds who were thick as thieves growing up in Atlanta who protected each other by any means necessary. When a fatal mistake separates the siblings their rock-solid relationship is forever changed. Shocking secrets from past and present create intensifying tension between the brothers who find themselves faced with a perilous choice resulting in A Family On Edge. This highly realistic urban drama is layered with unpredictable twists and turns that appeal to an intergenerational audience.
