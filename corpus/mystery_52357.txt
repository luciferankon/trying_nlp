Patrick is a young man hunted by dark visions which soon turn to reality; David is a police officer open to the idea that such premonitions may be true while his partner George a corrupt and violent policeman is eager to pin the violent crimes on Patrick. Professor K supports David's idea that Patrick is an innocent man with an extraordinary ability to see into the future; while Tina is the girlfriend David tries to distance himself from in order to protect her from the dark world he had started to be confronted with.
