Set in 1952 a time of confusion and fanaticism Ivan's naive make-believe story about guns found in a cave unintentionally betrays his closest family friends. Meanwhile he falls in love with Aleksandra the daughter of a partisan facing persecution and defends her in front of the class. It is 1952 time of repressions detention camps silence and fear. Even in times like that people hope help and love each other especially the children with their candor and spontaneity. Unfortunately the propaganda machine threatens to brainwash them. Ivan is smart artistic and recites Communist poetry with pathos. He cannot sense that he can harm even his closest people. After all he will take the road of truth before the cruel times have deformed his character.
