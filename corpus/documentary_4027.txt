On Feb. 4 1999 four New York City Police officers killed Amadou Diallo on his own doorstep in a hail of 41 bullets. Jesse Thyne an American Peace Corps volunteer who lived and worked with Amadou's family in his home village in Africa died there less than a year after Amadou's shooting. "Death of Two Sons" examines the political personal and spiritual implications of these two deaths. "Death of Two Sons" tells the story of Amadou Diallo the West African immigrant shot 41 times by four New York City police officers in 1999 and Jesse Thyne an American Peace Corps Volunteer who was living and working with Amadou's family in his home village in Guinea. Jesse himself was killed in Guinea less than a year after the Diallo shooting. This film explores the political personal and spiritual implications of their deaths.
