After graduating from Kaohsiung Normal University Wang Cheng-chung teacher was designated to teach in a rural - Nantou County Zhongliao Township Shuangwen Primary school which was lacking in educational resources. He was prepared to leave after the end of internship but met with the Taiwan 921 earthquake. When the students cried and asked him: "Teacher will you be coming back?" Wang Cheng-chung teacher was deeply inspired by the students and decided to stay with the school which was lacking in resources for the education of rural children and efforts.
