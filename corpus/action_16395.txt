A fisherman asks Gordon Freeman to eradicate the Combine's hold on the town of St. Olga a seaside town. Gordon takes out the soldiers guarding the headcrab launcher inside an abandoned church disables the launcher and then destroys two helicopters before riding a basket down to the fisherman again. As Gordon approaches the fisherman he invites Gordon to a dinner of leeches in celebration but before he can get further he starts to fade away with the fisherman remarking "You're getting a little fuzzy around the edges! Well I guess you've got better places to be."
