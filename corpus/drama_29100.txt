Jackson Price is a high-powered attorney and rising star campaign manager for a future US Senator. Until a scandalous affair ruins his career and Price loses it all. At the bottom of his fall from grace Price learns he has life threatening cancer and will have to fight to save his life. Abandoned and all alone Price is befriended by a cranky retired pastor named Earl from deep in the North Carolina countryside. As Price battles against the cancer trying to steal his future Pastor Earl races against time to help Jackson find redemption for his past.
