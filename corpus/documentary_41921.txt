Descending from a bloodline of musical excellence with the piano as his mastered instrument Chuchito Valdes has returned to his roots for both introspection and inspiration after the 14th Annual Latin Grammy Awards. His next goal - to develop and deliver a collection of musical compositions that enthusiasts around the world will enjoy and value. His unique ability to enable others to contribute their maximum musical genius during recording sessions emanates from a sincere level of confidence that a genuine collaboration of interests will produce the best possible product. While it is not clear where his journey will end Chuchito however does know where it started ... Precisely where the critical elements of his music were born.
