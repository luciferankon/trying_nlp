It follows the adventures of a group of Humans led by Meyer (Federico Crea) an explorer who has decided to form a team to search for new lands in which to revive the human civilization now dispersed in the growing forests. During exploration the team led by Aaron (Steven Gold) and consisting by Cora (Rebecca Romano) Lars (Luca Creta) and Emma (Lara Piccinelli) meet a group of Druids Nihmč (Karina Stadč) Gareth (Alberto Diaco) and Rheya (Constanza Angeletti). Druids are creatures that live in the forests with extraordinary abilities which put in place thanks to runes symbols unknown to humans and which are part of the life of the Druids. The first meeting between the two civilizations will be unfriendly; Human and Druids will fight for their cause with the help of an ancient prophecy that might bring back the peace.
