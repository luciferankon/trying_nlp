This movie is written by the famous author Yaddana Pudi Sulochana Rani's novel vijetha. As the name implicates strange relationship is about two college mates Madhav ( ANR) and Sandhya (Vanisri) an arrogant rich girl who is insulted by Madhav in the college and seeks to take revenge by pretending to love him. Madhav after knowing he is fooled by her kidnaps her and rapes her. As a consequence she gets pregnant. Her father kills himself and her brother becomes a cripple and she becomes a pauper and leaves her child in the orphanage. Madhav who returns from away collects and child and how he later makes up with her is the story.
