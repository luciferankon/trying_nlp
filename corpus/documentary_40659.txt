Bass Clef Bliss is a true story about a young man at the intersection of Autism hope and music. As a young boy his speech disappears and his sensitivity to sound triggers frequent tantrums. His music therapist introduces him to the trombone. With the help of caring professionals he makes a slow and steady progress in his ability to function in the world. Years later his high school music teacher discovers he has perfect pitch. Today his talent opens doors for him to play with orchestras theater productions jazz ensembles and his church choir.
