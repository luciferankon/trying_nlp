Love Exchange is a romantic journey of a culturally opposite couple -Siddharth Sathe and Shanoo Kapoor. They meet at their work place become good friends and ultimately fall in love. Things are fine till they decide to get married. Sid and Shanoo belong to two different cultural backgrounds of India. Sid a Marathi boy and Shanoo a Punjabigirl.Coming from different backgrounds little did they know that their love would face a cultural tornado ahead. Before they could confront their love in front of their parents the parents are all set to separate the love birds due to the cultural clash. Mr. Sathe (Sid's father) and Mr. Kapoor (Shanoo's father) get together as a team and intentionally set a condition for them to stay at each others house for 3 months to have a better understanding of the culture. To be together for life there lies a tough battle ahead for the couple. For it is easy to fight and rebel but much harder to convince. Will Sid and Shanoo's love for each other sustain the battles? Will they manage to convince their parents and make it to their wedding?
