After the director of a student talent show gets ill an young eccentric Duncan is more than eager to take over. Taking over the writing directing and starring in this production is no challenge for him. Dealing with everyone's personalities as Duncan finds is. After weeks of rehearsal and with not many people in the audience Duncan faces his biggest challenge yet...a possible failure of his big dream. This family comedy short is a story of friends supporting each each other through thick and thin despite their personalities. All is not well at the state of OCMD. After an illness sidelines a student director Duncan quickly steps in to take charge. With personalities as extreme as his doubt begins to creep in and threatens to squash his odd creation before it has a chance to be seen.
