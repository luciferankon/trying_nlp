A seasoned LAPD narcotics detective is brought up on charges by the FBI for dispensing his own unusual and illegal form of justice. When the charges don't stick the FBI special agent sets up a sting to ensure the corrupt detective goes down. A female undercover unwittingly becomes their pawn and payback overshadows legitimate motives making each of them - and us - tackle the moral question: Is skirting the law acceptable if it brings an end to horrendous crimes and keeps the public safe from heinous human beings?
