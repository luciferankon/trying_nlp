Back home after work Lucie realizes that Colinou her 5-month kitten is nowhere to be seen. She panics and phones a girlfriend so that she helps her find her beloved pet. In the meantime the young woman comes across a letter from her ex-companion with whom she broke up rather abruptly some time earlier. Its content is alarming: her ex tells her in substance that he has hidden Colinou somewhere INSIDE the apartment and she has to find him - before it is too Late. Crying her eyes out Lucie looks everywhere but in vain even with the assistance of her friend. At a time the latter decides to watch TV but the set does not work. To see what is happening the two women remove its housing and discover a package with Velpeau bandage tied around. Motionless as it is they fear the worst.
