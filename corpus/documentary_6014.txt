Micheal 'Eyedea' Larsen was a freestyle battle rapping champion a guitar player a break dancer lead singer of a punk band a poet a philosopher and a supportive friend to many. He passed away on October 16th 2010. We lost something very special and The World Has No Eyedea. Official Selection Minneapolis-St. Paul International Film Festival 2016. Official Selection New York City Hip Hop Film Festival 2016. Winner Platinum Award Spotlight Documentary Awards 2016
