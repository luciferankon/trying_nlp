Travis Nick and (Travis's Uncle) Armen use an online hospitality network couchers.org to find and stay with willing strangers offering up their couches. By doing so Travis and Nick eliminate their monthly requirement to pay rent and Armen a famous sex therapist can jump in on the adventures. Also caught in the mix is Shelby their mutual friend and Travis's crush. Together the Couchers stay with a new host every week and always find themselves entangled in new unexpected situations.
