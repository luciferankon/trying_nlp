Jor and Gerda is a loving couple that lives together in Copenhagen. Jor is trying to make a breakthrough as a writer without any luck so far. His struggles have caused Gerda to provide for them by selling sexual favors on the street. Jor is filled with resentment towards Gerda and because of what she has to do to provide food for the two. One day though their lives change completely when the small city boy Morgan comes walking into their lives.
