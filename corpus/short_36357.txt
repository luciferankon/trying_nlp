We Who Die is a noir senior thesis short film that portrays one man's fight against deceit betrayal and double crossing that takes place in Present Day Portland Oregon. It is the story of an underground fighter Charlie who has plans to leave town with his married lover Evelyn after one last fight. Just when Charlie thinks everything is in his favor things take a turn for the worse. Moments before the fight Charlie is told he must take a dive but will his pride and honor let him? On June 14th at the Hollywood Theater be ready for a visually stunning entertaining and compelling noir short that will shock the audience.
