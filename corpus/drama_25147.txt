Todd (Matthew Zuk) leaves dirty dishes around the house. Anne (Mandi Christine Kerr) feels obligated to clean his messes. If this is going on weeks after she moves in with him what should she expect years down the road? An evening with his parents Rita (Patricia French) and Harvey (Bill Murphey) triggers self-discovery for Todd and Anne that could either improve or unravel their relationship. What will they learn about what to expect from each other and how will their pasts mold their relationship?
