Claire is flung back into the dating scene after breaking up with her long term boyfriend Nick. She is apprehensive about the rules expectations and implications that she has to navigate through on her journey as a newly single woman. When Claire makes a genuine connection that goes beyond one-night-stand sex with advertising manager Pat she looks to her friends to help establish where she stands in the relationship. As this non-committal non-relationship continues Claire is forced to confront where she draws the line. 600 Bottles of Wine looks at the anti relationship what dating is really like in an era where no one really says what they mean or what they want.
