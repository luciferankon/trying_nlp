Christian a high-school gym teacher has been spoiled since childhood. Safely nestled with a security of a rich father he passes the days by drowning in drunken debauchery with his three best friends Ryan Matthews Bree Pierson and Michelle Corren aka "Skid-Marks." Meanwhile beneath the surface an acute sense of mid-life crisis begins to set in causing Christian's behavior to grow increasingly extreme and erratic. As the prospect of redemption looms just out of reach he assures it by mistakenly sleeping with an underage girl.
