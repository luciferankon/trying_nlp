Our motorcycle riding hero is introduced to the gritty side of India. Guy has a lot of followers in India and after getting a hint through social media they meet up with him in New Delihi to first help him buy a motorcycle that he intends to ride to a motorcycle race. But first he has to get there. Guy decides that his first leg will be taken by train. So we follow his exploits in getting train tickets and loading his 500 cc Royal Enfield onto the train.
