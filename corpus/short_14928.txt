The Australian Pop-Punk Band "The Playbook" have been involved in the Melbourne music scene for the past five years and have a well developed relationship with their fans. However following the departure of their old lead singer David Parker and the arrival of their new singer Laura D'Urbano a documentary crew decided to find out how this change affected not only the relationship with their fans but the dynamics of the band. Along the way former art director of the Australian music festival "Soundwave" Patrick Fox discusses the obstacles bands must face and the evolution of the Melbourne music scene.
