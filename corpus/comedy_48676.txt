Match Made in Radio is a new dramady webseries about a couple who meet for the first time at their wedding after being matched up in a radio contest to marry a complete stranger and given a free wedding. Brian the husband works as a stock broker. Michaela the wife works as a school teacher. Both individuals are desperate. They have tried speed dating internet dating sites singles bars and dating phone services and have failed to meet anyone as they're young age dwindles. Now after being chosen and carefully matched up on a radio game show they have agreed to be matched up and marry a complete strange. The series will focus on the couple getting to know one another as they live together for the first time in the same home.
