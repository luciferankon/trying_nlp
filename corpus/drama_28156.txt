Seventeen-year-old Jasper "Jazz" Johnson the son of a high-powered New York City attorney and celebrity life coach has it all but his privileged life is turned upside down when a beautiful young girl from the "wrong" side of the tracks accuses him of brutal assault. Jazz's trouble with the law further strains his parents' marriage as their proverbial golden child becomes the poster boy for teen dating violence. Jazz's dad is confident his money and power will get his son off the hook and into an Ivy League college but his mom is not convinced.
