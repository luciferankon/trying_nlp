The character has reached a point of reckoning which causes her to question the relevance of her life after fifty years. She has be a lover mother loyal friend and successful business woman but in reviewing the chapters of her life through her personal journal she cannot find satisfaction in her decisions involving love. Her timid demeanor and submissiveness towards her lovers has wounded her seemingly beyond repair. As she delves into her relationships with others she comes to a revelation that will have a life or death impact on her.
