George has been plodding miserably though life and his parents aren't making things any easier. His mother and father are a little...off...and caring for them has become a losing battle. When George turns to MediCorp-the family's health insurance company-for help his awful life becomes pure agony. Less than successful in his efforts to garner support from the compassion-less corporation George becomes desperate. In a final foolhardy gambit he faces off with MediCorp's eccentric CEO. But when he finally makes his stand George discovers that he is a mere plaything in the corporate giant's surreal endgame.
