Luna has just gotten her first job as a secretary for a major company in Jakarta. She's got her own desk new good friends and a seemingly friendly boss. There's only one problem. Ever since Luna started working for the firm she's been having horrifying visions and she's certain that something happened on the 13th floor of the building. However everyone in the building claims that there isn't a thirteenth floor there's not even a button for it in the elevator so how could this be? As Luna seeks help from her understanding boyfriend she discovers that her hunch may have been right but she has no idea what terrors are ahead of her...
