This is an intimate documentary about hope courage freedom and the ability to reach for and obtain your dreams. This inspiring feature shines a light on the power of the Cuban spirit its wonderful people natural beauty and historical significance. "Run Across Cuba" follows Alexis Garcia as he runs more than 1000 miles from the eastern most tip to the western most end of Cuba. Alexis covers the entire length of the Country he fled more than 23 years ago on a kayak. This epic journey brings Alexis back to Cuba. Travel along with Alexis as he literally runs across the entire island overcoming obstacles & reconnecting with the people and the land.
