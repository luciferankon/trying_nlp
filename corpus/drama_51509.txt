The world is startled by her arrival: while some raise her to the title of Diva others will stop at nothing to see her fail. Her success catapulted her to unstoppable fame a fortune that will compensate all previous heartaches and become the stellar element to help her thrive and become the most influential Latin artist of all times. But above all she is a ticking time bomb. ready to destroy her own ambitions the moment she feels she can't fend for herself. "Dolores" is the story of a perfectly imperfect mother and father of five children the neighborhood butterfly that one day transforms into the Diva of the Band the life of the eternal Jenny and her crew.
