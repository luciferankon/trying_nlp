Callie an 8 year old little girl parents are shot by an intruder. She is forced into foster care and traps herself into living in her own little world. Her new foster home has many animals. However she doesn't care for any of them. Callie and her foster mom goes to a nearby farm here Callie runs into this huge wild untamed Palomino horse. Callie bonds with the horse right off. Through the bond with the horse Callie comes out of her own little world. Just as she begins adapting to her surroundings tragic is among her again.
