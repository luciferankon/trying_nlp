A beautiful black-haired woman is plagued by nightmares where she dreams she's the victim of a burglar who strips binds and gags the sleepy woman. When she awakens she discovers her dreams were only too real. In the second story a shapely brunette courier sent to pick up a rare film collection is grabbed by a thief who plans on stealing the movies. When a perky blonde secretary tries to interfere she's quickly bound and gagged alongside the courier.
