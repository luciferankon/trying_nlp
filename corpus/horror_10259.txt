It is a tale of a deaf teen named Ally and her family whose lives are turned upside down when a bat colony is attacked by a parasitic insect near their New Jersey home. The bats morph into a deadly species known as Vesps that grow quickly and begin attacking humans. Ally has heightened senses due to her blindness making her able to feel when the creatures are close and come to the conclusion that the slightest noise attracts them in large numbers. She and her family take to the woods to live in complete silence as they hatch a survival plan.
