Alice is married to a poor and abusive man and has started an affair with Peter a wealthy and gentle lover who would do anything for her. Meeting at their secret rendez-vouz he finally convinces her to leave her husband and run away together to far-away lands but in the midst of their joy her husband barges in... armed. Running for their lives Alice and Peter try to escape but a sinister plot slowly unravels that proves in film noir nothing is as it appears.
