Filmed in June 2004 in Burnley England this concert video showcases three magnificent artists -- Bernie Marsden Mick Moody and Neil Murray -- original members of the classic heavy metal band Whitesnake. Special features include an exclusive Marsden-Moody performance of the songs "Trouble" and "Till the Day I Die" as well as an interview with Moody that reveals the group's varied influences such as the Allman Brothers and Lynyrd Skynyrd.
