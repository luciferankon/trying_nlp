Reeve is a reluctant hero caught in a deadly trap set by a psychopath known as the "Nightingale" where he will be killed for his past mistakes and misdeeds. Reeve discovers Ed a computer analyst who has been forced to work for the Nightingale or his wife will be killed. In order to survive Reeve must awaken his ninja abilities and fight his way through the deadly psychopaths to find Ed before the "Good guys" do which results in a shocking realization.
