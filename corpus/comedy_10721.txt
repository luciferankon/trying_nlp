Maxine and Shae have been best friends since grade school so when Maxine accidentally kills her boyfriend she naturally asks Shae to help her cover up the murder. After multiple failed attempts the two decide to bring in a professional to clean up their mess but he turns out to be nothing like they were expecting. Maxine and Shae will have to make it through the day by avoiding the police fending off nosy neighbors and surviving a wrathful mobster all while pushing their friendship to its maximum limits.
