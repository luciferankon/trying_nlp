For Fallen Worlds tells the story of a man who finally turns over a new leaf. The Dreamer sensitive and mysterious sick with nostalgia a wanderer in a black overcoat reaches his catharsis in the middle of a cornfield in Northern Serbia. "For Fallen Worlds" is about the long recuperation of the wounds aroused by passion. It is the story of a man who cries and finds the courage to stop. It is about short conversations which cure us of eternities of solitude.
