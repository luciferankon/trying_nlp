In this show Maria Bethânia recreates four decades of songs with the composers more often present in her career: Chico Buarque (Quem te viu quem te vę - You have been doing nicely; Olhos nos olhos - Looking at each other's eyes; Teresinha) Caetano Veloso (Oraçăo ao tempo - Prayer to time; Reconvexo - Reconvex; Nossos momentos - Our moments) Gonzaguinha (O que é o que é? - What is it?) Dona Ivone Lara (Nos combates desta vida - In combats of life) Roberto Mendes (Iluminada - To become illuminated; Estranho rapaz - Strange lad) Rosinha de Valença (Usina de prata - Silver mill)
