In a special division of the CIA a rogue agent Jake Toll comes out of hiding after 10 years to find and kill one man Spaights Burgh. But what he doesn't know is that his country wants him dead and he will be forced to face two threats in this highly-thought provoking film called "The Cry". Told in a black and white noir style high in intellect and precise with adventure - And this will be the end of espionage.
