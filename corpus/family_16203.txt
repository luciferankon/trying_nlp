Inspired by the heartwarming real life story of an American family. Joy Collins is stricken with a very rare virus and has brought her to the brink of death. Her doctors are at the ready to save her and she is now waiting for a heart transplant. Her your son Gabe has gathered the family together of pray for her recovery with the help of the spirit of his late grandfather. A Christmas tale about faith family and the true meaning of the holiday season.
