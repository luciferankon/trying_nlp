A sexy cheerleader finds her school's nurse bound and gagged in her office. When she attempts to untie her a crook clamps a chloroformed rag over her face. When she recovers she finds herself roped and gagged by her kidnapper. In the second story a college coed decides to make what she thinks is easy money by becoming a bondage model. The photographer who hired her isn't what he pretends to be and soon the model finds herself to be a helpless captive.
