Killer Teens is a one-hour crime series exploring the frightening phenomenon of murder committed by teenagers. Brutal and senseless as it may seem teenage murder is in fact the result of very real motivations-passion revenge jealousy greed even curiosity-and is all the more shocking because it is perpetrated by our youth. Killer Teens immerses viewers into America's most terrifying and unimaginable teenage crimes while providing essential understanding to the most important question of all: why are they killing?
