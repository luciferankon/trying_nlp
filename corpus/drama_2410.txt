Film based on a Award Winning Novel by Luis Arambilet: El secreto de Neguri (Alfaguara 2005). The film is a combination (Documentary/Drama) a recount of the story origins accompanied by two short films based on the original plot. Armando Neguri is a musician that discovers a secret passage under the kitchen sink. The passage opens to a mysterious parallel universe where Neguri can observe the neighbors using sophisticated visors.
