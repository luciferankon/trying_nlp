Following Dr. Emily Dosset and her team of medical professionals at the onset of a pilot program at LAC USC Medical Center focused on treating women with any of the seven diagnosed PMADs previously known as postpartum depression. The documentary film will follow three women currently under the care of this remarkable team of doctors. The PMAD Project was founded by Stephanie Wiand as a means to shed light on this debilitating yet very treatable condition. The PMAD Project will provide education via a series of videos and a feature film 'Attached to Hope' that will expand on this most important topic.
