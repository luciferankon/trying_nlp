It's Christmas Eve and the three best friends Chris Dane and Kevin have decided to host their very own Christmas Special with hopes of meeting the one and only Santa Claus himself. The guys are determined to make this the most special special ever so that Santa won't be able to resist making a personal appearance. They put on a Christmas Spectacular featuring a slew of celebrity guests musical performances and holiday cheer to make this the Bestest Christmas Ever! But will their special be special enough for Santa?
