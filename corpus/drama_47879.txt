This is a story about a well cultured people living in coastal village of Africa which is believed to be the birth place of the song AMAZING GRACE and the market place where slave trade was transacted. The African legacy is kept alive and archived by a philosophical paint and writer KISUH (BILLINGS SIWILA) who makes paintings and portraits of African and African- American . KISUH is the first born son of the Chief. But when the chief of the village dies will his second son-SIWILA (CHIA STANLEY KISUH) whose main focus is education inherit the throne without finishing his university degree?
