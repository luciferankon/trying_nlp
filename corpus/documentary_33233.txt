U R Here - Eight Artists in Regina is a broadcast documentary created by 4th-year students in the Department of Film at the University of Regina in collaboration with Zima Junction Productions. The artists featured in U R Here include: Singer/Songwriter Belle Plaine Printmaker Michelle Brownridge Dancer/Choreographer Heather Cameron Visual Artist Zachari Logan Poet Cassidy McFadzean Rapper Pimpton Playwright/Director Joey Tremblay Filmmaker Janine Windolph.
