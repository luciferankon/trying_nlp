Peter was one of the best computer hackers out there until his sister was caught in the crossfire. Blaming himself Peter walked away from everything that he knew and hid in a bottle. Jodie his handler has been on the trail of Alson Carlisle a terrorist. Alson has slipped through the hands of every three digit organization out there. He is about to launch his attack only no one has a clue on what they may be. Jodie has no choice but to pull Peter back into the fold. Peter wants no part in coming back but the chance to avenge his sister's death lights a fire under him.
