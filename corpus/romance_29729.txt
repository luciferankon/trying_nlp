The One and The Other... AKA...'L'Une et L'Autre' captures a world driven by passion and intrigues the audience to ask the question 'how far would you go for love?'. This seductive setting follows a man who is desperately in love with two women who share his affections equally. As both women eventually tire of not being the one the game of female manipulation begins. Though he has never lied to either both feel betrayed and in their insecure moments each demand of their lover to meet the other woman. Genuinely aware he has no choice and that his life is about to change he gives in and allows fate to take its' course.
