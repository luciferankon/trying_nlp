John is recovering from a bipolar episode where he experienced a messiah complex. He witnesses Bill a shipping magnate commit a crime. This creates a chain reaction as John enters a "manic state". John meets Elijah and Father Luke while John's ex-girlfriend and news reporter Sandra along with several of John's friends become entangled in the action as John flees from enemies and himself. SOLATIUM is a story of redemption a psychological thriller with themes of mental illness and spirituality. With 25 characters and 34 locations across Duluth the making of this movie was as manic as the content.
