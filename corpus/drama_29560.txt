Maham a working woman falls in love with Umer and later gets married to him. Subsequently she discovers that the man who had been stalking her is actually Umer's brother Bhaktyar. Umer disowns Maham and divorces her after his family questions her character instead of accusing Bhaktyar. What follows later is a series of events in quest of Maham's revenge on Umer his family and Bhaktyar whom she marries in the course.
