A successful career woman is under the gun to find a husband. Katrina (Faralyna Idris) is a successful woman but has an overbearing mother Puan Kamariah (Maria Tunku Sabri) who gives Katrina a deadline to find a husband or she will have to marry Haris (Anding Indrawani) the son of a friend. Determined to find a man on her own terms Katrina enlists the help of her friend Shai (Janna Nick) and Miss Pooja (Susan Lankester) the owner of a matchmaking agency.
