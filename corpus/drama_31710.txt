Ernst Lange has recently inherited his deceased father's business. Ernst lives a quiet life. But one evening he visits a restaurant and meet the cabaret artist Marcella. He falls in love immediately. Marcella and Ernst marry. Later Ernst introduces Marcella to one of his friends Raul Jones. Now Raul falls in love with Marcella. Ernst find out about their love affair and run into the two on a train station. Raul controls Marcella since she is drugged by him.
