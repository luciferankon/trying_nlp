The story of a fifteen-year old girl who finds herself referred to a re-education centre as a result of her love affair. She is desperate to love but this is denied her. Despite all the restrictions imposed on her she is determined to live her life to the full. The story of 15-year-old Ela who is sent to a re-education center. She yearns for love but is not allowed to love. Despite all the restrictions she decides to live her life to the full. However life is not so much what we want but what we get to live. Sometimes it is suffering and when we hit rock bottom we have to believe in miracles.
