On a bleak and misty night Jean Jean a soul-sick and tormented spectator watches a macabre show in a dingy cabaret. On stage the pianist and mistress of ceremonies known as the Priestess accompanies a cast of ravaged characters as they perform a tragicomic routine that evokes both the world of dreams and the chaos of contemporary society. A musical show consisting of disjointed cabaret acts Cabaret Neiges Noires offers a potent mix of satire and fantasy. Featuring burlesque song and dance performed brilliantly on stage as well as a dreamlike ballet which unfolds in settings both real and imaginary Cabaret Neiges Noires explores the varying intertwined destinies of its characters.
