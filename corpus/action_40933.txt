A young man on his way home from a night out is attacked and bitten by a vampire but fends off the attack. He is saved by two passing dark strangers. They bring him in to their fold and enlighten him in to the new dark world he has now been born into. While struggling to reestablish his former existence he now lives a double life. He meets a beautiful girl whom he starts a relationship thus inviting danger to his door whereby both worlds will collide.
