A vigilante spy by the name of Ace Jayson (Sky Storm) is on a mission to bust the Violent Ducks' latest ring leader Darius Kaleb (Lawrence Hinson III) until he partners with hero Reptile (Lawrence Hinson III). Now it's a race against time and the law to find Darius Kaleb and wipe him from the picture before the United States falls under the rule of the Violent Ducks. Featuring talents like Lavon Vonla as Big Cass and more this movie is the perfect balance of action and satirical comedy.
