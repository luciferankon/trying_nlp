A table set for three people where only two are having breakfast. A father who seeks refuge in his work and a daughter who makes up legends about the sea to face the grief for her mother who is no longer there. On a lonely island Aymara a 5 year old girl and her father and humble carpenter who restores old boats accompany each other along the solitary road to accepting the loss of a loved one. However one day Aymara decides to take her own path.
