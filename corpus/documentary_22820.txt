Vin Garbutt is an English folk singer songwriter guitarist penny whistle player and inspiration. This DVD tells his story from his beginnings growing up in the industrial heartland of Grangetown in Middlesbrough to his 40+ years touring the globe bringing joy and laughter to thousands of his devoted fans. The man is a phenomenon and his story is about as inspiring as it gets. This DVD is a must see documentary about one of the most endearing men on the planet.
