Welcome to Bowling GreenKentucky - home to the National Corvette Museum The Historic Railpark and Train Museum and headquarters for the Fruit of the Loom underwear and clothing company...just to name a few places of note. It's also known for its King-of-RedNeck-Real Estate...Edgar Mills otherwise known as Big EZ. This larger-than-life swaggering businessman owns most of the real estate in town including a liquor store a Laundromat and a whole heap of homes in varying degrees of condition.
