Kevin is a small town outcast born on the wrong side of the tracks. His dad Troy is the city dump operator a drunk that has been slapped with a restraining order by his wife Karen who lives in his house with the kids. Kevin's greater goal in life is to obtain higher education via a running scholarship in a town where hockey is the only sport that counts. Life is tough and his only income comes from helping his dad at the dump. Enter Phallen a new student at his high school for whom Kevin falls head over heels. In order to get the girl and get out of town he will have to overcome his dad his town but more importantly himself.
