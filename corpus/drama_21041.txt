Trapped Minds is a 4-episode psychological thriller mini-series filled with deception broken vows retribution and murder. The show revolves around Derek Shi ('Chris Chih-Cheng Lee' (qv)) a married man who has grown tired of his marriage with his beautiful wife Melanie Gan ('Ann Kok' (qv)). He decides the only way out of his marriage is through the cold-blooded murder of his wife.
