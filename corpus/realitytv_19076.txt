GetClose2 Maria Tailor is an Enterpire Original Reality Web-series that follows Dutch Fashion Designer and Urban Mom Maria Tailor as she deals with the tension of both being a high profile celebrity as well as a caring mother. Each episode features a Maria either at work running her own fashion label or at home with her family. Apart from herself many of Marias' celeb friends take the audience behind the scenes of catwalk shows high society parties and unexpected socialite encounters.
