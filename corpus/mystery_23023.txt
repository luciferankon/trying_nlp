A new chapter of the cold-blooded saga transports us to the New York of the late 1950s. The brutal murder of a rich lawyer's widow is not what it seems. Laura James a reporter with the 'Daily News' was investigating an ordinary murder case when it all went to hell. The deeper she digs the darker it gets. It seems that the roots of the case are buried somewhere in the history of WWII. Are you brave enough to venture into the chilling night full of horror? Well are you?
