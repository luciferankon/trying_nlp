Poshter Girl takes you on a mad roller coaster ride of a quaint little village in Maharashtra - Tekawde which is ill-famous for female infanticide and as a result there are no girls left within the village. When the question of boys' marriage is on heat a beautiful and Intelligent girl enters the scenario and changes everything. This is the story of the girl Rupali and her chosen 5 candidates who would do anything to win her heart. Female infanticide is at large in Tekawde village since last 15 years that neighboring villages also fear to marry their daughters in Tekawde hence there are no females left in the villageRupali a beautiful girl arrives in village and that's where every young men in village dreams of marrying her.
