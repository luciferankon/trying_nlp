More than a decade has passed since film director Nick Price has won his last Oscar Award. He has been through a dry patch and it's time for a come back to win another Oscar. With wild Africa as his backdrop he shoots a found footage film called Awesome like Albert. Albert Young a devoted gamer and his friend Elizabeth Scott are given an opportunity to travel to Africa and investigate Rhino poaching in Botswana. Instead of Rhino poaching they stumble across a strange battle. They capture the entire battle on camera and immediately find themselves spotted and hunted by mercenaries. Liz is taken hostage and Albert sets out on a rescue mission to rescue her whilst documenting the entire adventure as he progresses. Director Nick Price has the time of his life while making the movie in front and behind the camera and with some added luck he wins his Oscar.
