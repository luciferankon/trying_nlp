The true story of how the Fukushima 50 took on the daunting task of bringing the Fukushima Daichi nuclear power plant back from the brink of complete disaster and averted a meltdown of unprecedented proportions saving an entire region and countless lives. How the bravery honor and self sacrifice in taking on this suicide mission united a people and gave hope and pride to a nation crippled by one of the most powerful earthquakes and resulting Tsunamis in recorded history. Researching the background of these heroes along with a detail of their mission and impact utilizing recorded video accounts along with new footage and interviews.
