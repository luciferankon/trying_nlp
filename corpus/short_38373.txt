Miss Laika and her band The Fabulous Gramophones are about to have the time of their life. They will perform a concerto on a special occasion : the last ceremony in honour of the "arranged death" of the rich and famous music lover : Eddy D. Dexter. Every single details of this ceremony has been meticulously manage by M. Dexter's butler : Didier Cafard. Unfortunatley a sabotage has been commited and the band shouldn't be able to perform their music in time. Will Miss Laika be able to save the day of Mister Dexter's death. The show must go on!
