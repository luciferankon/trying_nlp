Gustavo (43 years old) comes back to the town where he lived his childhood hoping to find essential answers for his life. He recognizes important persons from his past and is rejected by his family He found the huge cement plant close to the humble town landscape clearly remembers and reflects in someway his interior conflict. He walks to what it was the grandfather`s lands feeling nostalgia and finding a sad but beautiful place. He goes to the cemetery where his mother lies. Finally he reconciles with his family.
