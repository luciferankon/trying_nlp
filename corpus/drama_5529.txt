A German ammunition transport has been blown up. The members of a combat cell hurry to get away. Only one of them the fair-haired man manages to get through the police cordon. He soon reaches the safe house and drops into the bed. Suddenly he confronts by the Turtledove a thief who has taken refuge in the same garret. They both have the same social background. The tense hours spent waiting for the dawn draw them together. The fair-haired man ideas touch a responsive chord in the Turtledove. When the police surround the house the Turtledove experiences a change of heart. He decides to share the dangerous life of his new friend. However the fair-haired man is shot dead. A cry of anguish echoes from the heartless walls of the neighborhood: "Is there anyone there? They have killed a man can you hear me?"
