On his way to the internship of a lifetime young linguistic genius (Malik) is diverted to Memphis TN when he encounters an adolescent child (Alex) whom convinces him that he's a victim of an abduction in progress. Deciding to aid the child Malik is catapulted into a deadly cat and mouse game with (Micheals) a renowned agent with the FBI. While flooded with questions of his own Malik must convince not only himself that he's done the right thing but also Agent Michaels that there is much more to the story than the words written in his case file. Otherwise it could mean both his and the life of the child.
