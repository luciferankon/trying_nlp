Oyee is the story of Krishna and Swetha two people on seemingly different tracks. Swetha yearns for her family while Krishna is trying to break away from his due to differences and the love of a woman Gayatri. A chance meeting and unfortunate circumstances place Swetha in Krishna's world and chaos ensues as he tries to convince his family that she is not the one he is in love with. What is keeping Swetha at Krishna's house? What will happen when Gayatri arrives? What will the family make of all of this drama?
