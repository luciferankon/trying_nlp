A taut thriller set in steamy New Orleans the story revolves around a female FBI forensic agent called in to assist the New Orleans P.D. in profiling a vicious serial killer. The agent who has just returned from Bosnia where she helped identify the remains of entire male bloodlines wiped-out during the horrors of ethnic cleansing for the International Criminal Court is stunned to discover that killer is wiping out her entire female blood line...and that she's next.
