Maria is an 18-college girl daughter of Mexican immigrants from the Bronx NY. Maria's life was never easy as she had to live with her violent father mother mother's schizophrenic husband and 2 sisters. The family moved between houses and shelters and never really had hope for a better future. When Maria started to go to high school she went thru difficulties like depression cutting her self social isolation luckily she started to write poems. The movie open the door to a world of the girl that against all odds keep the faith no family support not easy life only poems to keep her over the surface. The images from present time of Maria in college and Maria in her parent's house at the Bronx weaved with the past stories and the artistic storyteller - the poems as a way to express her past.
