After his girlfriend gives him an antique watch for his birthday Brad Sims finds himself caught up in a world turned upside down. The German mafia are so interested in finding the treasure full of historic artifacts that was smuggled out of Germany during Hitler's reign that they are set on finding this watch. And once they find out Emily has given it to Brad they use Brad's roommate Karl to help kidnap her and hold her for ransom. Brad then sets out to not only save Emily but find out what kind of secrets the watch really holds.
