A fierce dust storm blankets a treacherous stretch of desert as two mysterious strangers approach a campsite. They cross paths with Caleb Williams a humble man changed forever seven years earlier when his wife was brutally murdered. He is now a legendary hunter of men known only as the "Tailor". They join up with him as he travels to a small town outside of Yuma to help a Preacher whose town is under siege from something far more evil then just men...
