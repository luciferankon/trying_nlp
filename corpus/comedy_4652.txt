A series of 1/2 hour comedies shown as possible pilots to new series. Three of them were successful; Our Man in Canberra which had three episodes made but never shown but then was given a second chance with new episodes under the title _"Our Man in the Company" (1973)_ (qv)  _"A Nice Day at the Office" (1972)_ (qv) and Scattergood which was made into a series called _"Scattergood: Friend of All" (1975)_ (qv).
