Chinese adventurer Jesse Chang doesn't believe in the stories her father Jian tells her of dragons until they discover the remains of an actual dragon frozen in the mountains of Tibet. The culmination of Jian's lifelong search is also a career-making opportunity for Jesse's twin brother Jack a geneticist in America who sets to work unlocking the dragon DNA's potential. But when a rogue American general learns of the discovery and kidnaps Jack Jesse is forced to use Jack's untested formula on herself unleashing the ancient powers of the dragon. The twins must face a squadron of mutated soldiers to stop abuse of the dragon DNA and save themselves.
