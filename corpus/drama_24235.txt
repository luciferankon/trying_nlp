A widow and her daughter together with their fellow villagers commence the journey by boat towards Sagar Mela a religious fare which is conducted annually. They face a few hardships on the way however reaches their destination safely. The mother and the daughter share an extremely loving relationship which is further enhanced when the Batashi the daughter catches pneumonia and falls extremely sick at the pilgrimage. In spite of the doctor trying his best Batashi dies leaving her mother to continue the journey of the pilgrimage and therefore life.
