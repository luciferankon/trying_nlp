Travel around the world with Vivi as she sets out to find the whimsical world of Whirligig - a fantastical place where imagination reigns supreme. Come join Vivi and her adorable pals Backpack and Flashy as she imagines a series of enchanting adventures which come to life within the pages of her scrapbook. In each episode the trio is whisked away on a magical journey full of colorful fun new friends and valuable life lessons.
