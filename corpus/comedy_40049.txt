The decade is the 1970's the city is New York. The streets are over-run with pimps pushers and thieves. Even the police are powerless against the merciless crime boss Tubby Large. With no options left Sgt Blocker recruits the Fat Cats off the streets to fight crime as undercover agents. This action packed premise is the driving force behind the television phenomenon Fat Cats. Fat Cats - the movie presents 'the pilot' episode of this fictional series as well as a present day mocumentary look at the cultural impact this 1970's action series still has on American pop-culture. Filled with funk music black leather and exploding el Dorados Fat Cats is a celebration of the Americana that surrounded the biggest television show the world never actually saw!
