Though humankind has the same sort of sense they can be differentiated by their lack of external experience. This story details about the problem faced by the Protagonist against a Tribe who is in lack of wisdom due to the dearth of external experience. A wildlife Photographer saw the tribe in the middle of the Forest when he tried to take a photo of him tribe would have misunderstood about the camera as the weapon and defends against him with his Bow and arrow. Then the tribe follows him and walked along with him because he is curious to know about the new weapon (camera). After sometime photographer called the tribe for HELP when got trapped into leg snare (which is for the animals) Tribe offered his weapon (bow and arrow) and took out his camera (Doing the bartering mode) and walks away.
