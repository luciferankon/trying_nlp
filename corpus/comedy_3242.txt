The film centres around a TV channel executive the host and the executive producer who have a TV reality show; a competition broadcasting the day in the life of a middle class patriarch Nana. Nana is a retired lecturer who lives happily with his family members. He agrees to be part of the show very reluctantly only on the insistence of his young granddaughter who wants him to win the crown though Nana is totally against the reality circus. The TV reality show team arrive at Nana's palatial bungalow with their camera equipment. But strange happenings take place in Nana's life beyond his comprehension. Who is behind these strange chain of events? Does Nana win the crown for his granddaughter? What is Nana's family's reaction about this reality show?
