As the Defender of the Universe Kosmic Karl (Andrew Barrus) is ready to take on the world's problems in his tights and cape. If only he had super powers! Of course getting his memory back and falling in love would be nice too. Having lost his memory Kosmic Karl spends his time with his state appointed psychiatrist. Unaware of any real super powers his spare time is spent in acts of unsuccessful heroism resulting in a series of humorous failures encounters with police and a less than completely successful escape from a mental asylum. Through his successes and failures he finds love only to lose it and later come to the conclusion that the worlds problems are too big for one person to solve. A delightfully funny comedy suitable for the whole family.
