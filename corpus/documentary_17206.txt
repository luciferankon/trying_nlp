A look at the life and rise to stardom of Male Soprano Krassimir Avramov. A superstar in Bulgaria The film follows Avramov's life as he records his epic album "Popera" in West Hollywood (2006) . The film follows the Bulgarian mime and opera singer all the way from his childhood beginnings to a spectacular live performance of his album at the Kodak Theatre in 2006 as well as The LA Music Awards where he won star of the year.
