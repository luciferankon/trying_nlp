In Long Beach California school bus driver Perma Jean Pritchett inquires about a hit man to eliminate her husband Charles a minister at a local church. But when police uncover the plot and inform the preacher he agrees to help them trap his wife allowing detectives to photograph him "playing dead" in a downtown parking lot. Then they bring Pritchett to the scene - and videotape her reactions. "This is going to sound really cold and it hurts" Versie Jackson a 59-year-old grandmother tells an undercover detective in a Tavares Florida hotel room "but there's nothing I can do about it." As investigators listen from an adjoining room they're shocked as Jackson talks about murdering a number of witnesses against her son in a child molesting case. Among the targets: Jackson's ten-year-old granddaughter.
