Leonid Parfyonov shot a film for the anniversary of Galina Volchek chief director and artistic director of the Moscow "Sovremennik" theater. The author of the film says that he "made an attempt to explain himself and the audience what the word "modernity " means to this woman who has an almost supernatural sense of the new. The basis of the film is several interviews with Volchek as well as shooting in the sidelines of the theater.
