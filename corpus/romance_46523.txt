An intense love story. City's most successful barrister is Kunal Maitra. Swati comes and joins Kunal's solicitor firm. She belongs to a learned family. Over the course of time Swati falls in love with Kunal and marries him. Barrister Kunal Maitra was a womanizer. This not only made Swati hate Kunal but she gradually started hating all men. Whilst leading a monotonous life a young guy Sarit enters Swati's life. Sarit comes to visit Kunal Maitra at his residence in regards to some dispute about his fathers business where he meets Swati.
