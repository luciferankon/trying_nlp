This documentary follows the rise of a promising young singer songwriter as she meets with record executives plays showcases and hones her craft. Over a three year period Sarah logs video diary entries that chronicle her feelings dealing with homophobia self image and dreams of a record deal. As a child Sarah often wondered why she felt like a boy in a girl's body. Candid conversations with Sarah and her teenage friends give the viewer an intimate glimpse into the experience of coming out as a gay teenager. The viewer is shown the upside of pursuing one's dreams with the footage of Sarah's big break opening for international recording star K'naan as well as an even bigger show at an outdoor festival performing for a huge crowd. There are also many low points in Sarah's career throughout the three year span that make her question if she has made the right choices. This is a film about hope and possibility but also about redefining success when life doesn't give you everything you hoped for.
