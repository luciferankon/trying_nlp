Martina is a young girl who loves dancing and goes by a nick name Shakira. Martina is leaving a foster home in Eastern Slovakia as she has reached adulthood now she plans to join her older boy friend Roman in Prague. Before she leaves she promises to pick up her best friend Jana once Jana reaches 18 years as well. The film follows Martina's journey and we will learn that the life waiting outside is not going to be easy at all.
