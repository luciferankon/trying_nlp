It is not just in the Middle East that political activism is flourishing. 'Pockets of Resistance' is the attempt by the Danish journalist Ralf Christensen to identify the new creative and peaceful forms of revolt that prosper at the fringes of established society. From COP 15 demonstrators in Copenhagen to fierce confrontations in front of the Bolivian parliament and interactive political street theatre for Nepal's oppressed - Christensen's film is filled with inspiring 'glocal' mini-reflections of Arab revolutions
