Three people just before their 40th birthday want to change their lives: Judith a former stewardess is a single mother who lives in a secluded residential community. She earns her money with boring homework and telephone market research. After her friend has left her Ulrike announces in the youth office and starts training as a masseuse and beautician. Jochen an occasional jumper is about to pull people across the table as an insurance representative and hopes for his own flat.
