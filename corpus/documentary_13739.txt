A journey to discover the life and career of Vittorio Mezzogiorno one of the most important actors of the Italian theater and cinema of the last thirty years. A tale lived through the voice of his daughter Joan and the testimonies of those who knew him. Anecdotes photos archive footage and original music by Pino Daniele for an intense and emotional portrait dedicated to an artist of our time but especially the story of a man a husband a father and a friend.
