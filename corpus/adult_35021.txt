Six costumed cuties are securely bound gagged and tormented. In the first episode a detective goes undercover as a maid but who cover is blown and she winds up bound gagged and exposed by the crook she was investigating. In other stories a dirndl-dressed barmaid is roped and gagged in a tavern a policewoman rashly tries to tackle a crook single-handed and winds up handcuffed and tape-gagged a tall young woman who aspires to be a bondage dominant finds herself ball-gagged and roped a college student fantasizes of being a helpless damsel-in-distress an a snooty equestrienne gets her comeuppance.
