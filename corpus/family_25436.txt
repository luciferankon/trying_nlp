The show was aired from 20 September 1959 till 1967. It was hosted by Claude Kirchner with puppet "clownie" showing such movies as science fiction movies such as "Godzilla" "The Incredible Shrinking Man" and film comedies like Laurel & Hardy's "Pardon Us" "Pack Up Your Troubles" "Abbott & Costello Meet Frankenstein" and "The Three Stooges in Have Rocket Will Travel".
