This film consists of 5 retired Players sitting at the Round Table kicking GAME AGAINST GAME. For those of you unfamiliar with the Round Table this is a table meeting among Players to clear up misunderstandings about the Life. This is live UNCUT Unrated footage filmed in Las Vegas Nevada in 2009. Magic Don Juan is the "Chairman of the Board" for all of the young Players across the country. Gangsta Brown is the "Master of the Game" for International Players all over the world. Kenny Red was the 1st Player to bring the trophy home from Chicago to Las Vegas to the Bay Area. Diesel O'Aces is the "Voice of Choice" for all Players around the country. Johnny Dollar is the O.G. International Player a.k.a "Long Hair". This is a Double Disc DVD Parts I & II of GAME AGAINST GAME.
