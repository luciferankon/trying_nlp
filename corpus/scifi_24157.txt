THE CRYPTO-HISTORIANS is a Victorian Era Adventure set in a sub-genre of science-fiction that is often referred to as Steampunk. In a world where the stories of H.G. Wells are all based on fact: a hit man from the future an inventor from the present and a hero from the past must join forces to stop the "War of the Worlds" from becoming a reality. Utilizing the time travel secrets of H.G. Wells and Nikola Tesla they must transport themselves to the 1890s and change the course of history. Along the way they enlist the aid of some of history's most dynamic figures and prove the truth behind the motto of "The Crypto-Historians" - "The future is in our hands!"
