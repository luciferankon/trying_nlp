Packed with disc golf action Clubless tells the Montango legend that has only been whispered around disc golf courses. . .until now. Disc golf is life to Aaron and Noz. So when their favorite neighborhood course is threatened to be taken over by ruthless disc golf champion Zach Penner they cannot stand for it. Their peaceful existence takes a nasty turn as they must take a stand against the best in the world before everything they've come to know disappears. And before it's all over the world will finally know. . . Who is Victor Montango?
