Princess Turandot has been playing the role of "an unapproachable virgin" in Sofia. Sitting on a bench she polishes her battered cardboard heart with a shoe brush. A pasteboard magpie is perched on her head. Charlie Chaplin appears. The princess starts flirting with him and then lulls him to sleep on her bosom. Jackie Kugan and a globe-trotter arrive on bicycles. They seduce the princess and she is unfaithful to the sleeping Charlie Chaplin. Realizing this he fights a duel with the globe-trotter and kills him. Jackie Kugan commits suicide.
