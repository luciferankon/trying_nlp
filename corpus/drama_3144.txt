Ardiel a petty thief of Montenegrin origin attempts a robbery with an accomplice in a Rome suburb condominium. The shot goes empty but during the escape the young man crosses on the stairs a girl: Alice. A look between the two seems to be enough to seal their fate. From that moment they try each in his own way each with its own purpose. He in particular groped back for the theft this time in the next building. But a new meeting between the two most significant will open the way for a twist. The life of the building in the heart of the Roman suburbs is the environment from which the characters come to life in a tangle of suspended loves marked by the impossibility. In a world that seems to no longer recognize his creatures freedom of choice. But if love is really impossible thing still remains to be changed? It is love itself against all the conveniences to embody the last chance to change.
