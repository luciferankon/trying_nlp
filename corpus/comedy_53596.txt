Icchi comes over to Keigo's house and the first thing he says is "lend me your car." Borrowing Keigo's mom's car without permission Icchi tries to pick up girls one after another but is rejected each time. As the two friends go for a drive together aimlessly their car is stolen and they confront a gang of delinquent thieves. Keigo ends up getting taken hostage and out of desperation Icchi attacks a police box and steals a patrol car. He discovers a clue to locate his friend but finds him cruelly beaten and wrapped in a plastic sheet.
