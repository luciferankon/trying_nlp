Murat is known as a tough guy he is the friend of the good and the weak and the enemy of the evil. One day he saves Nazli the daughter of the gendarme chief Hasmet Pasa from Sabri the bandit. Nazli's father being the gendarme chief is after Murat for arresting him. As his men chase him Murat he hides in Nazli's room and she saves him. In the meantime Sabri the bandits is caught. He tells Hasmet Pasa that he will catch and kill Murat and in exchange Hasmet Pasa should pardon him. Sabri is dishonest he blames Murat for all his misdeeds and even abducts Hasmet Pasa. Murat saves the Pasa from Sabri. Pasa gets to know that Murat is a good person and gives his consent to Nazli and Murat's marriage.
