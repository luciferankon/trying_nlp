610 follows Connor part of an MTF Special Maz Hatters unit responding to an infection stress call deep inside a Foundation quarantined zone in open air Russia. The mission goes horribly wrong and you find yourself stuck inside an overrun underground facility sprawling with infected. Your goal is to try and find a way out; choosing to side with The Foundation or your own morals or continue to dig deeper to find the dark secret hidden in the depths of the facility.
