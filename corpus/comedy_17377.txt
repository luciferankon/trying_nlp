Lance King one of the most under-rated filmmakers of his generation is on a mission. Tired of developing art house erotica movies Lance and his crew must complete one final adult masterpiece before taking the independent film world by storm. In this documentary-style journey we meet his muse Heidi Lee as she struggles to balance work in the adult film industry and her new boyfriend. His loyal cinematographer and audio tech and even aspiring performer Kurt Lampton proving big things can come in small packages. Ultimately Lance must decide what will define his career as a filmmaker: his indie film roots or his genre bending adult classics. A hilarious view of the life of a filmmaker. Lance King wants to make mainstream films but has been forced financially to do what comes natural to him making erotic films.
