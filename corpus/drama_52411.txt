A honest head master (Bimal) lived in a village with his family. Circumstances made him a candidate of election. As a result MLA (Bibhash Chakborty) of that area became his enemy. Because the MLA understood that the popularity of the head master would make him the winner and the headmaster will eventually become the chief minister. Present chief minister joined with MLA. They blamed the headmaster in every corner but they failed. Mother of a dearest people died for political affair a few days before vote. Head minister tried his best to relieve people from abdominal deceases. He decided that he would not fight in election and he spoke his mind in a meeting a few days before the vote. Present chief minister was also present in that meeting. As head master removed himself from election Chief minister changed his nature and took decision to serve the nation properly.
