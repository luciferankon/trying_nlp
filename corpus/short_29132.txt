THE WINDOWS is Daniel Strehlau's first fiction short film. The film depicts a young writer sitting in front of his typewriter struggling with a story staring out the window. He perceives people in interrelated situations in various emotional states. The viewer sees love solitude tragedy and death. At the end of the film we discover that what they've just seen is a fiction in the mind of the writer. The first Daniel Strehlau's fiction short based on his own screenplay and his own storyboard from 1993.
