Ava McMaster has such a crush on Jane Dawson but cannot create a coherent sentence around her. That's where Charlie Bakersfield comes in. A guardian angel sent to help Ava McMaster because her friends Billy and Clooney don't seem to be as helpful as she would like. Does Ava get the girl? Is there anything in it for Charlie? Will Billy and Clooney stop arguing about pickles? All to be found out.
