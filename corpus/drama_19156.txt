Raktim Bose is struggling to publish his first novel from the most renowned publishing house in Kolkata. He encounters its influential editor - a man hated by many aspiring writers in the literary circuit of this city. The editor makes him linger on false hopes. Some of these underground writers try to convince Raktim he must eliminate this vicious editor for the sake of Bengali literature. Gradually Raktim starts believing in them. Raktim's novel talks about its protagonist Ayurved Sinha who narrates the story of Bidisha Roy his love interest. A novel he claims as a work of fiction. Imon a free going beautiful girl finds a manuscript of the novel and ends up entering into the world of Raktim & Ayurvend Sinha. The fine line between reality & fiction gets blurred. Does the author gives birth to a story or the story itself writes down the author?
