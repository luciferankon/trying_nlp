A story full of humor about love dreams and a role of music therapy in the lives of the disabled. The film protagonists are the participants of the Occupational Therapy Workshop in Swietochlowice. They form a band called 'Tolerancja' which gave more than two hundred concerts and released three original albums within two years of its existence. Their everyday emotions joys and concerns become an opportunity to peep into the world which is normally unavailable to us.
