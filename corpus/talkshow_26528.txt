The Great Northern Creative Show is a magazine show programme running concurrently with The Great Northern Creative Festival. Broadcast on That's Lancashire (Freeview Channel 7) weeknights from Monday 6th November at 7:00pm it features topical stories and studio guests. Various reporters also assist with subject-specific presenting both in the studio and on location or through filmed segments. It is produced by The Great Northern Creative Festival team and is recorded in the studios of the UCLan Media Factory in Preston Lancashire.
