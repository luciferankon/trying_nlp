The Book Of Mormon First National Tour launches in Denver at the Ellie Caulkins Opera House this month. This week we have the first installment of Edenapos;s visit with the creative team and some of the cast. Colorado natives Matt Stone and Trey Parker discuss creating their first Broadway musical and launching the tour. Gavin Creel stars as Elder Price. He talks about his first visit to Denver a new appreciation of touring and reveals his thoughts about taking on a role originated by a close friend.
