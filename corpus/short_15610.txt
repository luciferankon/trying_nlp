Directorial debut from actor/artist Tori Montgomery "Annaliese" is a short black and white movie filmed in silent-era mode with 2 main characters Annaliese and George Kacey who ends up as her victim. Filmed on location in Atlanta GA it features the talents of Tori Montgomery and Bill Pacer with Kevin L. Powers as cinematographer original score by Evan Hodges and edited by T.C. Morgan. A mysterious woman appears to a man and claims she is a vampire bitten by an ancestor of his 600 years ago. He invites her into his home then promptly tells her to leave. She later enters his house seduces him and bites him in the neck. He awakens the next day with an appetite for blood.
