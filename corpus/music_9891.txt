Rebel Invazion provides a look into the riotous but talented world of Cleveland based rap artists and urban models. It follows the antics of World Star Hip-Hop Vixen and Straight Stunting Cover Girl Beautiful the Beast nationally featured rap artist Mall Tsunami as well as the sexual escapades of J.O. The film is full of a variety of performances including Grammy & American Music Award winning rappers Bone Thugs-n-Harmony.
