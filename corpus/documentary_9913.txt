Shakuhachi (the Japanese traditional bamboo flute) and Skateboarding. They seem to have nothing to do with each other. But they actually have many things in common and also share the same problem. This movie disclose the hidden connections between those two cultures through the interviews with skaters and shakuhachi players including the world famous pros such as Silas Baxter-Neal Ryan Sheckler Pontus Alv Gou Miyagi Atsuya Okuda Kiku Day Alcvin Ramos and more. As the movie goes viewers will soon learn that the problem is not just for Shakuhachi or Skateboarding. It relates to all of us living in this modern commercialized world.
