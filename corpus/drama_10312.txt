On 30th July 1997 at Thredbo Australia 18 people where buried alive by a landslide. One of those men Stuart Diver managed to stay alive. With the rescuers working tireless to find survivors Diver was trying to let them know that he was alive buried under the rubble. After a massive 65 hours in the freezing temperatures wearing only his under wear Stuart Diver was the only person to be pulled from the rubble.
