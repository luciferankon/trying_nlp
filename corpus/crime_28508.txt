When Mordecai a hard hitting striker for AC Milan gets accused of taking bribes and setting up a "Red Card" penalty that turns the tide of a Fifa finals match we learn two things. One is never underestimate the corruption in football and the depths that it goes to. Two is never underestimate a man disrespected And torn away from his livelihood and his lives greatest passion. Mordecai hell bent on proving his innocence will do so at any costs and no one will stop him.
