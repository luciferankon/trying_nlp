Sophia Snow 22 is the poster child for nouveau riche chic with grandiose plans to climb the social ladder in a new city. She knows the quickest way to popularity is through the things that money can buy -- the latest technology the hottest car and the trendiest clothes. When her father refuses to give her a blank check she bolts out of his downtown condo to find herself on her own for the first time. She immediately goes against the grain out of sync with the world around her. The paths she chooses plunge her into chaos at every turn. Will she sync or swim? Only time will tell...
