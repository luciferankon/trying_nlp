Adelheid and Kornelius are married for 53 years. They are struggling to stay together as long as possible. By excessive sport mental fitness and self-mortification the couple tries to escape the death. But both are realists. The symbiotic "WE" cannot go on forever. Kornelius emergency-surgery in far Spain showed the fragility of life. A new plan is needed. So Adelheid and Kornelius are preparing themselves being widows one day.
