Pets Practice is the original family orientated TV show that first aired in Australia in 2013. Throughout thirteen entertaining episodes Dr Kevin visits many fascinating places where dedicated people are doing their bit to improve the lives of animals all over Australia. Dr Kevin has a lot of insight to share into some of our pets most common but perhaps not well known illnesses and ailments. Meanwhile get to know Strider Pirate Emmy Alphie and Buddy five puppies who start out a mischievous bunch but by the end of the series are fully trained (by experienced dog behaviorist Chris Loverseed) and ready to face the world.
