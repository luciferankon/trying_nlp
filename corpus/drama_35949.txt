Goodly is the story of a brave young girl Goodly who comes to the city of Kolkata from village to work as a house maid. She saves the family she works with from thieves. She also saves the life of a girl when her house catches fire. Goodly and her friends are kidnapped one day by men who want to bring about reformation in the society and protests against it. But they resort to violence and illegal ways to achieve their purpose. The children make them realize their mistake. They finally surrender. Goodly gets awarded by the President for her bravery and becomes a responsible Indian citizen who protests against any kind of wrong.
