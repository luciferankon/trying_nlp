Dr. Davin Beall is arrested for his participation in a medical scheme. He is brought before renowned lawyer Callahan Marks who agrees to hear his story before he goes to court. But when this interview turns out to be a plot designed by Dr. Beall and his boss SIR one must overcome their fears in order to survive. Includes twists turns and a cliff hanger ending although this is only part one of a longer film of the same name.
