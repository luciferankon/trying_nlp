Four friends from college: Karan Mahi Mathur Monty and Aarti love to play pranks on people which involves randomly selecting their telephone number from the directory and call them on the sly. But when they get in contact with Raj; they will find that their lives are not the same anymore. Mahi Mathur and some of her friends decide to play telephone tricks by randomly picking a number through the thick Bombay Telephones Directory. One day Mahi randomly picks Raj's phone number talks to him for a few minutes apologizes for the wrong number and then asks him to meet her at a nearby restaurant little realizing that this will start a chain of events that will catapult her life to great uncertainties attraction and distrust.
