This is the story of Kimmy Spitzer a lovable idiot who manages to ruin a backyard birthday party and work on her tan at the same time. Kimmy forgot to book the DJ and has to decide whether to fess up to her uptight best friend. The audience chooses the plot - do you save the party or save the friendship? Along the journey Kimmy gets help and hindrances from fellow Owotoites such as: Sandy Munch the owner of Muncher's Cove popcorn store her best friend's lusty mom the lecherous DJ Tune Bandit and Stephanie Crote the richest meanest girl in town.
