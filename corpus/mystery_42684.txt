Michael and Gloria Dalton are a married couple who live in Canton Maryland. Michael is a former film producer and Gloria is a former model who run a successful Real Estate Agency in Canton Maryland. When a family members murder turns into a cold case the couple takes it upon themselves to solve the crime. The balancing act of running a business dealing with the competition and pressure from the local police challenges the couple to act quickly. Soon the Baltimore Police department isn't too happy with what Michael and Gloria are uncovering. The local news and social media also take notice of the cold case that is revealing a connection to the Baltimore Police department.
