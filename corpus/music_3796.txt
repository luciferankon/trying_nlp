Power passion and politics collide in Carlisle Floyd's dynamic setting of All the King's Men Robert Penn Warren's classic story of ambition betrayal and violent tragedy. In Floyd's immediately engaging and beautifully paced opera the great demagogue of America's political fiction Willie Stark springs to life as an unforgettable stage character. Willie's meteoric and soul-searching rise from grassroots politician to powerful and ruthless state governor is captured in words and music that are rousing moving and haunting. With personalities and events that could be ripped from today's headlines LSU Opera's new Willie Stark brings fresh excitement to a quintessentially American masterpiece.
