Created as an authentic answer to the skewed narratives of Muslim Women that are presented in the current media The Muslim Women Series gives a true voice to the many Muslim women who do not see themselves represented honestly and positively in film and television. This is an ADVENTURE SERIES! The audience gets a chance to immerse themselves into the life of each subject as well as gets to see their surroundings and local environment which bred them. Each woman is pursuant of a goal Kamilah is on a mission to release her first solo album and Maria is on a mission to become a professional Muay Thai kick-boxer.
