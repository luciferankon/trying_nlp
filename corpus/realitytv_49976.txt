To see a different side of fame you have to think out of the box - literally. "Shoebox Sessions" digs up the most revealing mementos of a celebrity's past to shed light on who they are today. From embarrassing teenage photos to love-sick song lyrics our mystery box of personal history is always full of surprising and insightful revelations. Whether the box lifts the lid on laughter or tears every item is a turning point in a life's journey.
