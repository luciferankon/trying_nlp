Brad Mayfield (Kurt Angle) is a vicious criminal who takes pleasure in kidnapping assault and deranged murder. Officer Dan Burk is trying to stop his lust for blood while the stakes of the game quickly rise because of Burk's romantic involvement with a beautiful woman. She is Mayfield's next victim. When Burk's girlfriend and his daughter are kidnapped the case goes from just a job to a race against time to save his family.
