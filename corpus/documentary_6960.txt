The world's most famous gem has graced the presence of emperors kings and commoners alike. It survived a revolution and was the prized bounty in one of history's boldest and bizarre thefts. Legendary Hope diamond from its roots in 1600s India to its journey to America at the turn of the 20th century. This film debunks many of the myths surrounding the Hope Diamond and explores the cursed legacy that has been spun around the world's most mysterious and intriguing icon.
