Delerium has enchanted concert audiences worldwide with its unique blend of Electropop Sacred and World Music adorned with shimmering female vocals. Filmed in October 2008 at Washington D.C.s award-winning Nightclub 9.30 with additional footage from Atlanta West Palm Beach and Montreal Epiphany captures the ethereal essence of a Delerium performance realized as paintings in motion. Includes the hit song Silence. Produced and directed by Nasty Byte. Also contains the bonus short film Delerius a behind-the-scenes romp with the band includes interviews and fan footage.
