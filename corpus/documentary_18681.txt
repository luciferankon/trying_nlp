Eye of the Beholder is a provocative witty and entertaining inquiry into what we find beautiful and why. Series creator and host Serena Yang takes viewers on an adventure through the world's most fascinating cultures to explore the aesthetic tastes identities and sensualities of communities around the world. Every culture has its own definition of what's attractive. But as the world gets smaller our ideas about what's beautiful should get larger. "Eye of the Beholder" is a unique study of the world's diverse cultural expression and an empowering celebration of beauty in all of its forms.
