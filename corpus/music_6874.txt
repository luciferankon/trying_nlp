An experimental staged performance of Vivaldi's "Four Seasons" (1725) and Jean-Féry Rebel's ballet "Les Éléments" (1737) by the "Akademie für Alte Musik" Berlin under the leadership of concertmasters Clemens-Maria Nuszbaumer and Georg Kallweit featuring violinist Midori Seiler and choreographer Juan Kruz Diaz de Garaio Esnaola.
