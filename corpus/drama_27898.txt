Shooka a spirited hardworking girl from an Iranian mountain village is to be married in an arranged marriage. Shokrollah her neighbor with Down syndrome has been head over heels in love with Shooka since they were children and so Shokrollah's father buys his son's way into a marriage deal with Shooka's father. Enter Rafie the tailor and his daydreaming assistant Jalil who falls for Shooka after overhearing her pour her heart out to her family. Determined to be with the bride to be despite her impending wedding Jalil follows her over the mountain trails and invents ingenious ways to delay the wedding.
