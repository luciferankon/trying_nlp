John Loos: Too Big For This World is an original sketch comedy series for the ex-football player drag queens out there. Think Netflix's Characters showcases but for John Loos a large muscular LGBTQIA actor who did not get a Netflix Characters showcase. From Sia's wig to Kellyanne Conway's mind to the catastrophic ramifications of jorts Loos finds the absurd in the simple peccadillos of everyday life and heightens it to an 11.
