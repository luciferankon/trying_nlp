In 2005 Coach Joe Ellis made sports headlines when his Surry County Virginia Boys Basketball team won the first State Championship. Unfortunately he lost his battle with colon cancer 8 weeks later. "Going Out a Champion" the Coach Joe Ellis Story is a Cathy Irby Durant film based on the novel written by Doris M. Ellis. A host of Hollywood a-list stars will be cast in this much anticipated film. Coming to theaters in 2015.
