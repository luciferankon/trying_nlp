In this short documentary we hear the sounds of one night the sounds of different opinions smells colors and shadows. We see also the images of Crimea. They are emotionally different from the sounds that we hear. It happens to be the night of the Eurovision song contest. It continues to be the land of prosperity and battles. This short documentary paints the schizophrenic atmosphere that rules in Crimea of today. The picture and sound bring us flashes with different images and noises that represent the reality of 2 million people living on this infamous peninsula. Crimea knows all kinds of weather including the political weather all kinds of cultures. It was the land of ancient Greek democrats a rich land of prosperity.
