I'm going to Graceland is the story of Elvis Erin McAllister a teenage mother raised on a farm in Mississippi who in order to escape a dark terrible family secret runs away with her toddler son Joshua to New York City and earns the chance of a lifetime in a fellowship at a prestigious performing arts college. In the classroom the artistically talented Elvis meets a brilliant but also tormented dysfunctional professor Simon LeClaire (Yet to be cast) who relentlessly questions her core beliefs pushing her to reveal her shocking past or lose it all.
