What happens when The Jackson Decker Band a mega-popular 80's super group reunites for a blow-out comeback concert tour and bring their five teen and twenty-something kids along for the ride? Generations collide egos clash boundaries are tested and unsettled scores resurface with a vengeance as the aging rockstars try to relive their glory days. But for their kids spending the entire summer on a rock 'n' roll tour is the opportunity of a lifetime. The burning questions of love sex and all the regular coming-of-age issues are heightened by living in tight quarters on a tour bus and rolling into a wild new city every few days.
