Harlan county Kentucky has a sordid history of conflict corruption murder and mayhem. For many years Harlan was practically a war zone. This helped earn it the name Bloody Harlan. All those turbulent times and dark energy has seemed to have an effect on the area. One such area is the old Harlan county Jail house. The jail house has a checkered reputation and was at the center of much of this turmoil. It housed every type of criminal you can imagine from the common thief to murders. If you committed a crime in Harlan you had to pass through the Harlan county jail system. It appears all this negative energy has made the jail a hub for unexplained activity. The Crypto Files team takes a look at the past and the claims of unexplained activity. Are there Imprisoned Spirits still trapped in the jail? We intend to find out.
