The story of two people poles apart who cannot live with each other or without each other. Pratham Mittal (Hiten)  an arrogant young man who marries Gauri Aggarwal (Gauri)to take revenge on her and make her life hell. Gauri being who she is egoistically takes on the challenge. Will these two people keep there fights going or eventually realize that what they're doing is wrong and against the sacred marriage vows or will they live with each other and realize that they cannot live without each other and they are meant to be? It was destiny that brought them together... but will they realize it before its too late?
