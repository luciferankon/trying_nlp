Carnaby Street Undressed is a story of fashion music and eye opening insights into London's Carnaby Street in its 1960s heyday told by the owners of the fashion shops that defined the iconic Street and the popular musicians who were part of the "the scene". Carnaby Street Undressed is a story of fashion music and eye opening insights into London's Carnaby Street in its 1960s heyday told by the owners of the fashion shops that defined the iconic Street and the popular musicians who were part of the "the scene". This includes The Who lead sing Roger Daltrey Donovan Frank Allen from the Searchers Gary Leeds from The Walker Brothers and Peter Noone from Herman's Hermits.
