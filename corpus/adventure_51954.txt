Shrimpemon is about a peaceful world where everything is solved through battling Shrimp monsters these are entirely different from normal shrimps but are treated with love and great respect. Normally everyone's goals are to catch as much as possible and complete their S.J.D. (Shrimp Journal Device) as others are to train their Shrimpemon to their highest ability and compete in 3 championships making them the best in that region. Follow Shrilly in his adventure to become the best!
