A young brilliant cancer researcher is stricken with stage 4 Cancer. It is terminal. In her quest to find a cure she uncovers information on a "miracle" cure created by a renegade Doctor in the island of Puerto Rico using an enzyme located in a remote area called "The Devil's Lagoon". What begins as a journey to find a cure - turns into a thrilling adventure in the rain forest where only the ones with a "pure heart" can survive.
