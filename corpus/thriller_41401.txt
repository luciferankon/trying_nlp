Three years following the murders of several prominent Freemasons the young and newly appointed Grandmaster Jericho Beck struggles in his campaign for US Senator against rumors of Masonic Conspiracies from the fiery and capable Victoria Stann. His frustrations turn suspect as a series of attempts on Stann's life throw the worldwide ancient Order and Beck himself into suspicion. FBI Agent Odessa Lee and her team delve into the investigation to find the shadows and the figures lurking in them more complex than they ever thought possible.
