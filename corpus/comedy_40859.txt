A mob underling is supposed to complete a job and report back his boss. When he reports to the boss that he was unable to complete the job the Boss tells him he's going to be hit. The Underling explains to the boss the target had something that changed his mind on completing the job and gives the boss a bag of "Cool Ranch" Doritos that he took from that subject. The Mob Boss takes a chip and eats it The boss laughs and everything is okay.
