The events that unfold in "Wait! Where the hell is E.T.? P.S. Look out for the ugly plastic doll" take place in a surreal playground and will leave you with a couple of questions: Is it only happening in the mind of one person? Is it hallucination or is it just reality? Who the hell is Arthur Bottom? You will have to find out yourself! And don't forget to look out for that ugly plastic doll!
