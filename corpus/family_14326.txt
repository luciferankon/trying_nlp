Ishwar Sakshi is a Hindi drama starring Ankita Srivastava Sandeep Bhansali Chandan Madan and S.M. Zaheer. A father (Zaheer) promises to marry off his daughter (Srivastava) to a young man (Madan) with the statue of Lord Vishnu (Bhansali) as the witness. Later he goes back on his word and Madan appeals to the Lord for help.
