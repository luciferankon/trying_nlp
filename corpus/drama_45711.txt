Gretchen Ann ( Gladys Walton ) runs away from her foster parents but is sheltered first by Bill Kelley ( William Robert Daly ) a train brakeman then by elderly oilman Pete Sebastian ( Edward Jobson ). After Gretchen keeps Sebastian from being duped by a medium he sends her to a fashionable school asking that she agree to marry him when she returns. She accepts the condition but Sebastian releases her from the commitment when he realizes that Gretchen loves John Kelley.
