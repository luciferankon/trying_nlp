A couple isolated on a farm in South Africa cut off from the world both physically and emotionally pass their time reenacting their horrific past- role playing in an attempt to erase it. A stranger pays them a visit only to become part of the madness. African Gothic brings to life the story of Frikkie and Sussie caught in the unresolved grief of witnessing a horrific crime. Having experienced a traumatic childhood shaped by abusive parents they re-enact their childhood traumas on their isolated South African farm - role playing in the hope that through these games they will free themselves from their nightmares. The line between fantasy and reality are blurred when a stranger threatens to unearth their dark secrets.
