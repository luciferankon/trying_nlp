Federico Bruno Pirro and Benny are married. The first three have beautiful wifes Benny instead is married to a ugly woman. Ornella is Bruno's wife she is very jealous and maintains that all men are deceitful granted the opportunity while Claudine Federico's wife maintains the contrary point of view. So they bet that Tosca a former dancer will succeed in seducing Federico. Troubles for everyone. While their husbands are on a hunting trip the wives of a clerk of his boss of a doctor and of a friend of theirs discuss their faithfulness. Claudine is the most gullible and bets on her husband's love. Her friends decide to put him to trial.
