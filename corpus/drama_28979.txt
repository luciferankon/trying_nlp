When Jess marries the man of her dreams life seems perfect. But behind the charming face he presents to the world her husband hides a dark secret. Gradually Jess realises she is being subjected not to obvious violent physical abuse but purposeful subtle insidious mental and emotional abuse. Unless she acts she will be plunged into a dark unending nightmare and will lose everything. She must find the inner strength to take a stand against a threat that has found its way into her heart and soul - the man she loves.
