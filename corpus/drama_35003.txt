Sgt. Kevin Hill is a 22 year-old US Army Ranger currently serving overseas as part of an international peacekeeping mission. When his unit is ambushed by a group of insurgents Kevin is among those who are missing during a dangerous urban uprising. Julie Hill returns home from her first year of college to spend the summer with her dad. Guilty that she has not spoken with him since she left Julie hopes to make a change. Unfortunately with Kevin missing Gene is not his old self. Concern begins to wear him down but he is determined to see his son safe at home.
