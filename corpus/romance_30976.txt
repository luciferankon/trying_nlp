When Fleur arrives on her brother Hugo's doorstep in Melbourne only to find that he has run away to their hometown of Byron Bay Hugo's flatmate Jude is expected to look after her. But Fleur soon finds out from Jude's ex-girlfriend Madison that he is the one who needs looking after. Fleur's need for sexual attention and Jude's need to be adored draws them both together then pulls them apart putting love to the test in this contemporary Australian romantic comedy with a bitter-sweet heart.
