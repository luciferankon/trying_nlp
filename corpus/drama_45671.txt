Efe becomes a victim of a murder that was accompanied by an accident with his father. Efe who lost the accident lost the memory. Years later Efe who learns that he is the son of a wealthy family communicates with his twin brother Mete. But the people who learn that Efe lives are going after Efe to finish the half-finished job. But instead of Efe they shoot his twin brother Mete. Efe tries to replace Mete and take revenge on both his father and his twin brother.
