In the early 80's a game was created but never released... A video game no one was meant to find... A video game no one was meant to play... But in 2012 two YouTube game reviewers go on an adventure to find the greatest game ever created! Johnny Millenium and Rob Man must face an evil corporation two deadly assassins one Internet troll and the surprising and shocking Mr. Fufu. Finally the popular YouTube show Happy Console Gamer bursts on to the scene with this exciting action-adventure comedy. Will Johnny and Rob be able to find and weild the greatest game ever made?
