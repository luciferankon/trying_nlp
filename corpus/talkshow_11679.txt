American Entrepreneur is a business TV show that gives its audience a glimpse into the lives of prominent business leaders and their stories of corporate success. In a 30-minute television interview serial entrepreneur Michael A. Vadini uncovers the accomplishments hardships and lessons learned from the founders CEOs and Presidents of some of America's most tenacious companies. Season one focuses on a diverse group of entrepreneurs including a number of women and minority business owners and a broad spectrum of industries.
