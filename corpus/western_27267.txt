Cartwright's racket is to sell a ranch and then have Mason and his men drive the ranchers away so he can resell it. If they want their money back he gives it to them and then has them killed. Jack arrives and learns that Mason and his men are the culprits but that they have a boss. He suspects Cartwright and sets trap to expose him. Following the Civil War Jack Ridgely an ex-Union Army captain comes to Julesburg with his sidekick "Fuzzy" Glass to investigate the murder of his father the manager of Cartwright's Stage & Freight Company. One there he discovers that a land-selling scheme is being operated by local land agent Mason who sells property to settlers and then has his henchmen run them out of the territory. Jack suspects that Slade the man who replaced his father may be the secret leader behind the gang. Jack and Ross Haddon organize a band of vigilantes to bring law-and-order to the territory and at the same time find the person guilty of his father's murder.
