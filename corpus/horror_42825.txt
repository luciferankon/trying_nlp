The mysterious action packed horror thriller Nefarious will leave you jumping out of your seat. We follow the Tanner's who just moved into a new home in the country after the passing of their father. They are on a trail of discovery opening up the tale of a family who once lived in the gorgeous farmhouse in which they just moved too and the chilling story of what happened to them. What might happen to them next? This story leaves you wondering whose the culprit for all their misfortune. Is it the strange old lady next door? Is it the mother? Is it the brother? A friend? And what will happen next?
