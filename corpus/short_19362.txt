This video series in national distribution with FILM IDEAS is designed to help young adults deal with potentially serious situations in their daily lives. These dramatic recreations are balanced with advice from experts on topics from abuse and violence to alcohol. Includes all 7 episodes Hooked Trigger Happy Dead Drunk Touchy Situation Undesignated Driver and Emotional Wreck. (Aka The Trigger Series 1996) Created and directed by Brian McCulley written by the cast. (US)
