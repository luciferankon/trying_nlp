Game Over is a story of a Con Girl - Bold and Beautiful Smart and Intelligent; but that's not enough to play the Game of Life - Game of Gamble - Sometimes Game's Master Planner becomes Victim of the Game. She played the game - But now the game is playing with her. Her every step in the game leads her to a mystery where every question turns into the puzzle; her every efforts to get rid of the game opens the door of next game. On every step to finish the game takes her to the next level. To do the GAME OVER is a challenge and who will do it is a puzzle.
