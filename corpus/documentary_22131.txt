The notion of man's best friend being more than such may be unimaginable to some but to an increasing number of dog owners throughout the world they are so much more. Fly High: Story of the Disc Dog will explore the history of the rapidly growing sport of Canine Flying Disc in the canine competition world growth that has surpassed the likes of Agility and Obedience. The film will chronicle the history of this young sport and follow the progress of dogs and handlers as they strive to become world-class athletes.
