Kenny Lewis and Randy Jenereaux natives to Pittsburgh venture off onto a nationwide tour shooting 50 music videos all across America. The documentary shows the hard work put in to make this dream a reality living life on the road through the good and bad and highlights the different genres of music that they worked with. Although this was a dream come true for the two guys there were repercussions that couldn't be avoided while on the road. Unfortunately it would affect Kenny's life forever. The guys want to show you that no matter who you are or where you are in life that you can achieve anything if you are willing to work for it. This is truly one inspiring documentary from two normal everyday guys.
