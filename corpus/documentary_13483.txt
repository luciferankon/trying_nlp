Narda a 28 year old student from Bolivia leaves the country to conceal her pregnancy. Despite all difficulties she decides to continue her studies in the future. In order to fulfill her ambition and to support herself and her little daughter abroad she finds employment providing care to a rich Argentinian old man. In the last days of his life she becomes the most caring and the closest person for him. Soon however Narda will have to make more of important decisions in her life and once again find her own place in the society.
