The Nutcracker is the story of Clara a young girl who receives a nutcracker doll as a Christmas present from her godfather. Through magic the doll comes to life in her dreams and takes her on a magical journey. The Nutcracker Prince rescues Clara from the Mouse King her would be kidnapper and takes Clara to the enchanted land of the Snow Queen "Snowflakes and Candyland." There she meets the Sugar Plum Fairy and the beautiful Waltzing Flowers. Clara awakens from her dream on Christmas Day. Don't miss this classic brought to life by the world-famous figure skating team of Randy Gardner and Tai Babilonia and many other stars.
