Most houses contain our secrets within it's walls. Most homes are a safe place for us to live. This is not one of those homes. In this home the walls hold more than just secrets. In this home the walls hold the souls of the dead. In this home once you move in you never move out. Mike M Burke and Mystic Night Pictures presents a movie where strange nightmares keeps you awake at night and strange noises in the house keeps you in fear all day. Move in if you must but don't expect to ever move out. The house won't let you.
