One of the background- magazines on different days of the week all aired by the Flemish public broadcast VRT at a late hour (hence the common first part of the title Dutch for 'late edition') in this case devoted to the political life at the Belgian (federal nation) and Flemish (constituent state) levels not limited to the day-to-day news events but trying to look deeper or simply concentrating on some of the political actors jargon institutions etc. as such.
