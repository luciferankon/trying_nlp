Elsa is thirty eight years old. For the last five years she has been the main lawyer for a large oil holding company. The other thirty three years of her life have been working towards achieving this position. She is at the top of her career and the next step is board of directors possibly moving to London should the take over of several international companies takes place. The gold medal the red diploma degree another red diploma ... an incomplete list of successes at "Universities". Her career was fast and bright but she never managed to have children. Then problems begin creeping up on her.
