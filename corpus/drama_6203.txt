Off all the law offices in Korea why did he walk into hers? Han Min Gook (Lee Sung Jae) is the CEO of Independent Financial Group and is considered a genius fund manager. He was married for six years to top actress Lee Ae Ri (Han Eun Jung) but Min Gook's scandalous affairs led Ae Ri to divorce him. After the divorce Ae Ri suddenly hires hotshot attorney Byun Hyuk (Ryu Soo Young) and sues Min Gook for a $100 million settlement.
