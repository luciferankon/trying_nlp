Ryan Greenspan Oliver Lang Alex Fraige and Yosh Rau lead a collection of teenagers who revolutionized professional paintball in the late 1990s and early 2000s. Turning pro in 2001 as San Diego Dynasty the team would go on a half-decade domination never seen before. Until the unthinkable happened and Oliver Lang the face of pro paintball left the team in 2006 to join their bitter rivals. Five years later Lang rejoins the team as they prepare to kick-off the 2011 PSP Pro Paintball Season at the Texas Open. As the team sets its sites on its 50th tournament win-an unmatched feet on the sport-will Lang's return push them up the mountain are will hard feelings harbored over the last 5 years prove you can't go home again?
