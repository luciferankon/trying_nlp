When do you consider mistake a sin? Do we need to look into a person's intention or simply acts? This story revolves around a smart student named Guia Sison (Julie Ann Fortich) who is moving heaven earth and hell just to reach her goal. Guia happened to be Lydia's (Boots Anson-Roa) favorite student and Larry's (Eddie Rodriguez) lover and tutor who teaches her the sanctity of love-making. At the end lessons were learned and purposed reached it's triumph. Then the flower regained its honor and scent.
