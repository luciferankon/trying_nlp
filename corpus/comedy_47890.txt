There's nothing Curtis loves more than playing the role-playing game Swords and Swordsmen with his friends. Unfortunately life and expanding families have made getting together for these games harder and harder with each passing day. It's been six months since the last and just when it looks like they'll finally be able to continue their heart-stopping fantasy adventure... Curtis receives a knock on his door from his neighbor Cynthia. She's cute. She's strong-willed. And she wants to know what Curtis is doing that evening... 12 Sided Die is the stylish new comedy series about friends fantasy and romance and what happens when the three collide.
