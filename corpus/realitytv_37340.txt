Three celebrity artists travel the country to discover incredible raw musical talents. These lucky few are then tutored and refined to be the best they can be. They then face the ultimate challenge: performing alone on stage. Three separate audiences representing three different age groups get to vote but they cannot see the contestants. When more than half of any age group signals that they like what they hear the audience pod rotates and the singer is revealed. The catch is - all 3 audiences must approve if the singer is to progress in the competition. True Talent is all that matters and it is rewarded with a major cash prize.
