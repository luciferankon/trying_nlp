Early morning two gunshots break the peace of Yongzhou city. Li Pingyuan president of the largest corporation in Yongzhou is badly injured and his bodyguard is shot to death by a gunman. When Yongzhou police promptly moves to the scene for investigation Sun Haibin an entrepreneur whose business is based on a crime organization is also shot to death. At the same time a dumped body is found and reported. Several murders in a row shock the Yongzhou administration and provincial leadership in Ministry of Public Security. Therefore Yan Haidong captain of the provincial criminal investigation detachment is appointed to lead the investigation and a special investigation team of competent police is set up. Confrontation between justice and evil begins...
