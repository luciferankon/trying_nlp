The Play Button follows the story of a man named Jim. Jim goes to work everyday to an office job he hates and comes home to a dream he feels he cannot reach either due to his fear or his doubt in his ability. Jim's dream and passion is to create on Youtube. Youtube gives him the ability to create the ability to show and the ability to share. The one thing keeping him from accomplishing his dreams is his fear. Everything changes when Jim is faced with a choice. The choice to follow his passion and take the risk or to keep it safe and continue doing something he hates.
