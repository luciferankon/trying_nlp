Omari Lawrence an ex gang chief and community activist wants nothing more than to enjoy his new found freedom. But Omari's joy is short lived when everything he loves is snatched from him by ruthless thugs. With no way out no one to trust and everything to lose Omari is left with no other option than to go back to the streets. The closer Omari gets the stronger the scent of corruption and the more he must rely on violence as a solution. Only time will tell if Omari can salvage everything he lives for without succumbing to the beast of the streets and time is something he doesn't have.
