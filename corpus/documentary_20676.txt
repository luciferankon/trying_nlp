When man gets short of his life basics or fundamental human basics it's obvious one has to go an extra mile to survive just as it's in Nansana town council with it's residents vs water scarcity. But the question is has Uganda neglected the lives of its children living in Nansana Town Council? "I Need a Drop" there fore is Uganda's full length documentary drama that focuses on how residents of Nansana Town council over mine the existing natural water sources to bridge the gap of water scarcity in their area. Its major focus is on children as the most vulnerable group in this cause.
