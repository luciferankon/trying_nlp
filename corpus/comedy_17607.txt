Josie is a woman who is 8 months pregnant and living in Silverlake California. She is an actress with a rocker husband and a cute dog. Josie is just trying to find her way with her new found beach ball belly. She still goes on auditions that she might not be right for anymore. She is trying to fit into her new role so she meets with Doulas to find the one that will fit with her hip life style. And meets with mom friends to get some not so condescending advice. And of course there is the classic montage of her life.
