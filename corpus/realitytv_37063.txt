Four very successful women from Holland share the secret to their success with all its ups and downs living in glitter and glamourous Hollywood. Myrthe picking up a new life in Hollywood after her divorce. Conchita Leeflang raising her daughter among all the richness in Beverly Hills running 2 businesses singing and acting career while still keeping a stable upbringing for her child. Yolanda Hadid having a lavish wedding with record producer David Foster and raising 3 children while also running an interior design business. Princess Inge is raising her little Prince and all her dogs while trying to find Mr. Right.
