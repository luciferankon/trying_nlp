IT IS A STORY OF A BOY SACHIN WHO IS VERY KIND HEARTED AND A GOOD PLAYER OF CRICKET. SACHIN TENDULKAR IS HIS HERO. HE WANT TO GAIN NAME AND FAME LIKE SACHIN TENDULKAR. BUT ONE DAY HE MEET WITH AN ACCIDENT AND GOT PARALISED. ALL HIS HOPES SHATTERS. THEN HIS SCHOOL COACH AND CLASS MATES HELPS HIM AND BRING HIM IN MAIN STREAM.
