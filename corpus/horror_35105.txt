Over the years evil has existed in many forms. It has plagued humanity for various reasons. It has died in many ways. And now it has finally been resurrected in "The Murderous Rampage of Malachi Stitch". Gregory Miner (Michael Centi) is a young doctor struggling to clear himself of a murder he swears he did not commit. As he begins to collect evidence to prove his innocence he also finds himself fighting the many demons from his past. However Gregory soon comes face to face with his past; a terrifying abomination that simply refuses to die. It soon becomes all too clear that the evil he once thought only existed in his fevered mind has now materialized as a very real nightmare. With the help of his longtime partner Robert Craven (Ben Eckstrom)  Gregory attempts to end the slayings and clear his name once and for all. But as the body count rises and the sins of the past resurface it's obvious that no one is safe. Malachi Stitch has finally come home and your mind and soul are his to take.
