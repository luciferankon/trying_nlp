On the eve of the opening of the 215th Summer Exhibition of the Royal Academy London Chris Kelly host a program dedicated to one of the most popular art events of the year. Sir Hugh Casson president of the Royal Academy together with academics Peter Blake and Allen Jones reveal the problems of selecting and hanging a gallery. The program featured actress Jane Asher as well as musicians Ian Dury and Lynsey De Paul as special guests and they select some of their favorite works from the 1983 exhibition.
