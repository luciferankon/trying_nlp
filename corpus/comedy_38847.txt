Preston Prescott (Preston Corbell) had it all figured out the job of his dreams and a bright future to look forward to. That all change's in the blink of an eye and Preston scrambles to find his new place in life. Fellow Preston and his sister Ezra (Chelsea Corbell) as he puts his dreams on reserve and looks at the many opportunities ahead all while on his way to "Going Back" to what he left behind many years ago.
