Die Pfotenbande discovers the world of pets for kids through the eyes of our hosts Emma (7) Lilli (12) and Luna (13). The three girls are daughters of the popular German actor Til Schweiger. What do I have to know if I get a puppy? How do I have to feed my baby guinea pigs? What can I do If my pet gets lost? The Schweiger girls answer all questions everyone wants to know about pets and animals.
