This is the story about a person born during the time of WW2 evacuation and who quickly turned from a modest Jewish boy Joseph into a cruel and cynical crowned thief ruthless to everyone. The person who once lost forgot traded his present name by becoming simply a Baron... Streets of modern city are changed into wooden pavements of a provincial town in the beginning of 50th high society parties are turned into gangsters' meetings Mediterranean landscapes - into dirty railway stations of Russian suburbs detective's office of Stalin times - into a high security prison of 70th.
