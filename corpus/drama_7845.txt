When Jack (Brandon Gallagher) is faced with the decision to go to rehab or lose his girlfriend Roxy (Brooke Yerrington) he surrenders himself to rehab. Unknowingly it introduces him to a whole new world that he had never imagined. Meeting Benny (Jae McCain) and Cassy (Lindsay Kent ) in rehab sends him down a spiral of sex drugs and murder. Can he find himself and through love come out of it all unscathed?
