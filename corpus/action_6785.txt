The story focuses on an angry mob boss who is ripped off by his own people so in retaliation Mr. Scorello gets even with those who have wronged him the only way he knows best... by killing them! Mr. Scorello calls in a professional hit man to clean house. A violent bloodbath of revenge follows as Max Stryker delivers a brand of justice that is best served hot.. with lead that is! Prepare for an action adventure you won't soon forget.
