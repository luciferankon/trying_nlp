Home maker Charlie Kern lives in an old school bus. Inspired to create unique low footprint mobile homes Charlie began converting buses into dream homes and hasn't looked back. Filmmaker Anna Darrah gets an inside look at how Charlie keeps his clients off the grid and on the road! This is the fourth episode of Home Makers a new Zillow series profiling visionaries who design and build unique homes. Each episode is tailored to its subject by a different filmmaker offering fresh perspectives and an emphasis on creativity.
