The billion-dollar world of horse breeding often breeds a side order of betrayal. Billy Stone is revered for his good nature and skills with raising both horses and horse hands alike. Suddenly the man who appears to have everything has his moral fiber challenged beyond compare when a fire takes almost everything he holds dear. Unable to escape his innate gift of keen observation Billy must do his own problem solving when his life crashes down on him with the most unexpected wake up call he could ever answer.
