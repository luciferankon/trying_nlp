Girl's Blood is not your typical Japapese horror it is more of a genuine action with all the contradictory emotional cues of contemporary Japanese culture."Girl's Blood" is an unofficial fighting league where both amateur and quite skilled female fighters battle in various ways for an adoring audience. In the movie an above-board fighting organization threatens to expose the illegal nature of the Girl's Blood club. However the Ando group have committed various infringements of their own and the president of Girl's Blood blackmails them into a competition. Winner becomes pro league and the loser will be exposed and disbanded.
