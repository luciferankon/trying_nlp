An ex-wife hooked on uncontrolled consumerism strives to rob her ex-husband and his nice new wife of all of their earnings in this black comedy. However the ex-wife has hooked up with a millionaire and lays around her mansion all day while her struggling ex-husband lives in abject poverty in a ramshackle apartment. Finally getting fed up they decide to hire a hit-man to do her in. Of course the hit man is a bungler.
