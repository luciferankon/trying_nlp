UFC 200: It's Time is a one-hour TV special promoting the UFC's biggest and most impressive milestone to date. During the course of the show we go inside the lives and training camps of eight of the top fighters in the world and explain the stakes of each fight. Jon Jones and Daniel Cormier are set to settle the score in their much anticipated rematch. Former UFC champion and WWE superstar Brock Lesnar returns after a five-year layoff to face fan favorite Mark Hunt. Jose Aldo and Frankie Edgar battle over the interim featherweight title in Conor McGregor's absence. And Miesha Tate looks to defend her belt for the first time against hungry Brazilian challenger Amanda Nunes. UFC 200: It's Time will get you caught up during fight week so you are set on July 9th for UFC 200 live on pay-per-view.
