In this one-hour special cameras follow the cases of three babies with serious medical challenges and the dream team of specialists fighting to save their lives. After the babies' conditions are diagnosed in utero delicate pre-birth procedures and their subsequent deliveries are documented at Hollywood Presbyterian Medical Center. Immediately after birth the documentary team follows the urgent transports of two of the newborns to Children's Hospital Los Angeles and chronicles their stabilization and the life-and-death surgeries their survival requires.
