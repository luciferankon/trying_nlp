Since the dragons have disappeared magic's vanishing. Hope is raised as Sunelf Marween finds an abandoned dragon egg and little Nugur hatches. If dragons get native in Bayala again and all elves are reconciled Bayala can be saved. Princess Surah and her companions are facing an adventurous journey which leads the elves far away to the Dragon Mountains and to Surahs stepsister Nuray and the dangerous Shadowqueen Ophira.
