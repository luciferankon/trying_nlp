Following the accidental death of her husband Andréa gets her family together in "La Reverdie"the family home. They all come Mme Berthier Andrea's mother; Mony Andréa's sister; Odile and Louise Andréa's daughters; Didier Odile's ten-year-old son ... They are there to decide if they will keep or sell the house. Memories melancholy but also love and violence await them.
