Savage Destiny is a sci-fi drama adapted from the Turning Wheel by Philip K Dick. The story is set in Detroit in a dystopian future where the East has prevailed over a world governed by a hierarchical religious society the Arm who have developed a technology that allows you to see your next reincarnation. Sung-wu's daughter Sana is dying of the plague and he realizes he's doomed to also die before he has a chance to cleanse his karma unless he allows some outlaws to manufacture in some illegal drugs that could save his life and that of everyone else.
