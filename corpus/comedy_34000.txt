Sleepyhead has a dream in which he plays chess with a mysterious stranger. The loser has to make a feature film that does not indoctrinate in any one culture in anyway. After losing the game in his dream an awakened Sleepyhead and his smart aleck friend Blanco the Robot go into a mind-bending adventure to not only figure out what cultural indoctrination is all about but what kind of movie they will make without it....that is if they can.
