Nine year-old Chance Watson is lying cheating stealing and flunking out of school. Then one night a wonderful magical sheepdog appears and promises to make his dreams come true if he can follow "The Rules." Soon with the help of his talking sometimes invisible dog Chance has changed his life and the lives of many of the students at his school. But there are those who are out to stop him and prove his furry friend is not real just a figment his imagination. Even Chance begins to wonder: is he crazy?
