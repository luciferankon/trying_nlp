Difficult tale of poor struggling South Carolinian mother & daughter who each face painful choices with their resolve and pride. Bone the eldest daughter and Anney her tired mother grow both closer and farther apart: Anney sees Glen as her last chance. Anney a poor single mother from the rural South struggles to raise her young daughter Bone. They seem to find happiness when Anney marries a kindly man named Lyle who treats Bone like his own child. However after Lyle is killed in an automobile accident Anney remarries to the hotheaded Glen who abuses Bone physically and sexually and begins to tear the once tight-knit family apart.
