The story revolves around the three main casts: Mamboy (Johnny Delgado) owner of a boarding house in Malate. A jack-of-all-trades master of none and a sports buff wannabe; DJ Blue/Tong (Christopher de Leon) who's forever at odds with his cousin Mamboy. He's a happy-go-lucky forever a bachelor; while Edgie (Edgar Mortiz) dad of Tetet (Angel Locsin) owner of Laff-Laffan Bar and a boarder of Mamboy. Their wacky world becomes wackier when Rina (Pops Fernandez) enters the picture.
