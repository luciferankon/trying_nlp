In a galaxy a group of aliens are sent on a secret mission to save their planet from a black hole upon arriving at their destination an asteroid collides with their vessel causing the crew to crash land on Earth. The aliens live undetected for three decades until one day an old man and his dog find their home and take their book. Their only chance at getting home resides within the book but the aliens cannot read. With the help of two friends they read the book and find a legendary treasure map. Will they pull off the heist? Can they fix their ship take down the Space Pirate and save the galaxy? All while the Agency searches for the downed vessel.
