Three best friends Zoki Bruno and Leon are set to become the best rap artists in the country they spend their days creating rhymes and practicing their songs. Bruno even wins the rap battle to become the best free-styler in Slovenia and suddenly the path to their first concert is wide open. Their hearts beat hip hop until Bruno meets a beautiful violinist Nina and Zoki ends up in a hospital after a fight. Which love will prevail love for hip hop love for a girl or is it possible to have it all?
