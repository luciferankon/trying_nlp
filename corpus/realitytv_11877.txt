Paper Dolls is a reality show that chronicles the cryptic amusement that surrounds the lives of these six women. This unique cast of women has challenged the traditional notions of what it means to be on top - and through the course of their tribulations and successful careers - have evolved into more than highly reputable hair stylists and makeup artists. Each Paper Doll is rediscovering her creative passion for music writing their community and for life; and attempting to avoid anything that will jeopardize their rise to new successes. The show will premiere by showcasing each Paper Doll's career transition and philanthropic mission. Fire In The Skye Entertainment has created the reality show Paper Dolls about a group of independent women and their independent lifestyles in the Bay Area. Paper Dolls are: - Six (6) women who are in their 30s-40s and native to Bay Area California. - Aspire to change their communities in a positive way. - Long time friends and celebrity makeup artists and hair stylists. - On the brink of a career transition. - Ambitious tenacious boisterous and outspoken women. - Each possess varying talents and are ardently passionate about their creative crafts. - Individually each woman is successful and driven but together they become a force of change.
