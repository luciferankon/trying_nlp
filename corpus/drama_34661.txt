The movie tells about the issues on losing distinct nomad culture customs as well as traditional values of Mongolia which transferred from socialist system into open and democratic society in 1990 and developing with world in this globalization era. Tuvshin a young horseman who lived in the steppe raising livestock is naturally talented for singing and his passion to sing led him to Ulaanbaatar the capital city of Mongolia. The movie tells the contradictions of urbanization and nomadic Mongolians losing their nature behavior and ethics through the life of the horseman who inherited nomadic culture and adapting the natural conditions with seasonal migration. The main theme of the movie is to address issue on the danger for nomadic civilization the world invaluable heritage in this globalization era.
