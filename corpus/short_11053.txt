An adaptation of Kafka's "A Country's Doctor". Dr. Anton is called to a little boy for whom he must make out a death certificate. Happy to escape the patients in his practice he is taken to a mansion. There he meets the bizarre family of his patient: the importunate sister trying to distract him from his work by whiskey and music the ignorant mother the senile grandfather laughing in his wheelchair and finally the little boy who seems neither dead nor ill. Angry the doctor is about to leave the mansion when he suddenly discovers a festering wound.
