For office worker Hal Norman every day is the same and reality couldn't be more boring. When he loses his job and finds himself deep in debt he decides to go in for paid medical testing to make some easy money...or so he thinks. Suddenly he finds his world turned upside-down as he develops strange abilities making him a target for shadowy forces until he finds a safe refuge in the hidden district of Improbabilia. Hal joined by others like him must make the choice to either flee or stand against those who would destroy them...but is it all just a drug-induced hallucination?
