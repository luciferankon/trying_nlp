Julián finds love and a reason for living in the last place imaginable: the Dominican Republic's Najayo Prison. His romance with fellow prisoner Yanelly must develop through sign language and without the knowledge of dozens of guards. Julián finds love and a purpose to living in the last place he imagined: Najayo prison in the Dominican Republic.
