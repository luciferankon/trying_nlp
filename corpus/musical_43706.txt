Located under the deck of an transoceanic liner Vladan Vuckovic and his band ALHEMIA take us to a musical cruising through cities of Europe and America. In each city to which is dedicated an original music theme the musicians meet the characters who are part of its own spirit: knights painters fishermen miners - THE DIARY OF A LONG SAIL is some kind of a travel book which through connection of music water fire and dance has a tendency to a multiethnical connecting of our planet.
