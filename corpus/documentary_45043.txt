Exposing the truth about Michael Jackson's most controversial aspects of his life. This documentary covers the last 17 years of his life. Focusing on the child molestation accusations in 1993 and the criminal charges in 2005. This DVD explains why the jury found him innocent of all 14 counts chronicles his activities since his acquittal from 2005 until his untimely death. Since the King of Pop's death was so unexpected some released DVD's will end at his March 5 2009 announcement at London's O2 arena and other include an epilogue on his death.
