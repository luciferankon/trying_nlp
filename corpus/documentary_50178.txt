What impression does the Umbrella Movement give you? Troubles? Politics? Conflicts? On this road described as "almost a riot" facing oppression and attacks from all sides student occupiers shouted for "Genuine Universal Suffrage". Where there are people there is love. Other than the restless students a group of Christians stepped into the illegally occupied sites themselves with a mission in response to God's calling. They shared the joy and sadness with the protesters overcoming fear and not forgetful of their initial cause. They served supported protested remaining to the very end dutifully testifying for God in this era.
