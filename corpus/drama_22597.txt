A story of Vasudha Raisinghania a cheerful loving and intelligent girl who is the doting daughter of Dhirendra Raisinghania a powerful and manipulative media baron who runs a newspaper called Dainik Darpan. Vasudha soon discovers her father's manipulative ways but remains loyal to him despite the revelations and takes care of not only his professional interests but also his personal well-being. However complications arise when Vasudha despite being in love cannot openly pursue a relationship with Sushant who is the Chief Crime Reporter in Dainik Darpan since her father disapproves of the relationship.
