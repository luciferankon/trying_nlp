Mother Goose introduces the world to her first group of therapy patients including such famous Nursery Rhymes as Jack and Jill Cat (Hey Diddle Diddle) Widow (Itsy Bitsy Spider) Downey (Three Blind Mice) and Hunny (Humpty Dumpty). However the group is introduced to two new members John-John a *very* young leprechaun with indecent exposure problems and Sally a 'tongue twister'. Watch as your childhood favorites take on new persona's and deal with the scariest thing of them all. The real world.
