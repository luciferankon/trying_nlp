With Sophie no two voyages are alike. Born blind however armed with optimism and a genuine desire for adventure Sophie always accompanied by her trusted Dalmatian guide dog Pongo she sets out to discover the world that we live in through her senses and the eyes of her travelling companions. In the same way we will travel alongside with the intrepid journalist in her journey to the four corners of the world in order to explore the different cultures mingle with the everyday people and share their activities in this unique and unexpected travel guide. Ultimately as we participate in Sophie's sensational voyage we will re-evaluate the way we relate to others question the way we perceive our environment and above all embrace life's diversity through a different perspective.
