Get2gether' is a romantic comedy about a childhood friendship that turns into love when the boy and the girl meet again as adults. Rik (Rahul Banerjee) initially hates Diya (Priyanka Banerjee) but later falls in love with her after he recognises her as his childhood friend. But there's a storm to come as Diya already has a long-standing boyfriend. Game on for Rik.
