Embark on a sinister travelogue taking you to haunted places few would suspect exist. Armed with video cameras and hiking bags two Ghost Hunters explore the grandest oddest and most haunted buildings to ever exist in Europe. Surrounded by darkness and abandoned for centuries; these most haunted houses are still standing and beckoning those brave enough to enter. However their ghosts don't like to be disturbed. From the safety of your home embark on a journey into these remote and long abandoned mid-nineteenth century structures that come alive as paranormal experts encounter apparitions spirits phantoms destructive poltergeist manifestations and a bevy of bizarre occurrences. All of this is caught on camera along with incredible and unexplained phenomena. Experience firsthand the most eerie and unforgettable mansions ever to have been built with hauntingly beautiful footage accompanied by truly frightening historical information.
