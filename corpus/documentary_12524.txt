Behind a veil a young girl is hidden in plain sight. She is Elizabeth Smart taken from her home in the middle of the night on June 5 2002 and incredibly 9 months later found alive. Although stories like this don't happen that often when they do they rarely have a happy ending. Eight years after they took her her kidnappers finally face justice. What happened to Elizabeth Smart during her captivity? How did she survive? And how did her family cope with the unthinkable.-
