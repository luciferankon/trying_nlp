Bngvel (Turn) is a real-life story of Phally a Cambodian man struggling against the odds to be a good husband and father. Never being able to provide enough for his card-playing wife he heads to the big city in search of better employment opportunities only to find the more he earns the more he spends. Numbing out with beer and girls he comes to a point of crisis that shakes him up and he becomes determined to make amends.
