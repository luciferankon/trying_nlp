The documentary focuses on internal and external struggles of local news media members specifically WAFB Channel 9 in Baton Rouge during the city's most trying time all while the world is watching. Topics discussed includes breaking the news of the shooting of Alton Sterling releasing cell phone footage the outbreak of protests and the militarization of police all while remaining unbiased and moral in reporting. The audience will get a firsthand perspective from WAFB's news directors anchors and producers as these events unfold.
