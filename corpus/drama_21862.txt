Rupra is on a mission to fulfill his duty to the RAF during World War II. He is thousands of miles away from the life he knows with his family in India. He is a fearless fighter pilot that's not afraid to die to protect the country. But ill feelings start arising against Rupra within the ranks when he proves his extraordinary patriotism for England amidst the chaos of war. Can Rupra take on the might of the most formidable air force in Europe the Luftwaffe in the air as well as the mental torment of life on the ground amongst his fellow comrades.
