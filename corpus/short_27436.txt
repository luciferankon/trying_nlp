Kobra is a special extended part of the films "The New World Order: Art Thriller Christ Rituals" "The Hidden Messages of World War 3" "Red Dreams" "Christ is Coming" and "Christ Premonitions." All films musicals aim to the 2nd Coming of Jesus Christ and the battle between the Anti-Christ and Christ. Kobra is a character played by Omer Pasha in a black cloak that calls upon the Anti-Christ to hurry up and come so Jesus will come and this drama shall begin. And that the power of Christ is invincible.
