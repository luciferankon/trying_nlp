Valentina Director of International Affairs for Interpol Mexico decides to lay low when her career has taken her close to retirement. Secretary of State calls her up with a secret mission to save Mexico's image towards the world on how other nations view Mexico as an escape goat for European criminals. She is given no choice but to accept the offer. Valentina's passive political-office career could turn into an crime-chase action filled roller coaster ride full of betrayal that not only puts her career at risk but everything she's ever believed in.
