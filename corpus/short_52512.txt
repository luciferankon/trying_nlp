Harrod Blank who introduced the world to the the art car with his moveable epic "Wild Wheels" is back with an in-depth look at the personalities behind these multi-wheeled works of art. This time he focuses on Gene Pool's "Grass Bus" Harry Sperl's "Hamburger Harley" Hyler Bracey's "Bighorn" and his own two vehicles "Oh My God!" and the one of a kind "Camera Van." Aired on TBS. 29 minutes.
