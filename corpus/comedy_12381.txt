The story is all about Club X formed by various kinds of men from different careers and status; a snapshot camera man an straight-arrow student who actually owns a porn website a guy addicted to phone sex...even a famous psychiatrist. All of them spend their free time in the club enjoying pornography. Among other things they try to secretly obtain photos of female celebrities. While the police are trying to track down the club and their behavior in X-Circle they are totally clueless until the prettiest and sexiest agent sacrifices herself to be both the prey and the decoy.
