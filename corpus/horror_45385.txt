Inspired by the films of Dario Argento this modern American giallo tells of a young woman who suffers from vivid nightmares about an unknown gloved maniac sexually mutilating male victims. It turns out her dreams are real and it's up to her to use her visions to unlock the identity of the Killer. Set to a pulsating soundtrack by Velvet Acid Christ. She wears stylish black leather gloves and wields an assortment of sharp and blunt weaponry. She is the Gloved Murderess and the city is her catwalk to deliver stunning visual carnage. The dreamer. The pervert. The psychic. The lesbian. The flatfoot. All will collide with the metropolitan nightmare.
