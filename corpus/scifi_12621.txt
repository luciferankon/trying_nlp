An Australian boy "Lan' takes a trip to the USA to see his father's scientific invention during his arrival he receives tragic news saying that he has lost his father. A USB gets given to him from a secret CIA agent with the name Dr. Light Lan then proceeds to head to the nearest hotel to find out what it is he received. He then soon discovers that the world is going to be under attack by the evil Dr. Wiley. His father explains what he has to do in order to carry out the mission to save earth so Lan volunteers to combat the mechanoid minions of a mad scientist.
