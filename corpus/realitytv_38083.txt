LA XLARGE is an eclectic program mix that features major events concerts and club life that takes place in the LA area. LA XLARGE features social and cultural events in and around Los Angeles the show plugs into what's new and hot in music comedy film and fashion. LA XLARGE is dedicated to presenting the lifestyle of LA to people in a big way! From the guys and girls that hit the books at Southern California schools from the sexy club goers to the concert going fanatics the business movers and shakers and all the larger than life celebrities we encounter all around this great city.
