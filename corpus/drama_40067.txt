It was with much difficulty that Ai-Ching ('Cyndi Wang' (qv)) was able to save enough money and arrive in her dream place Paris. As a result she was extremely happy about this. At the same time Tzu-Hao ('Leon Jay Williams' (qv)) is also in Paris receiving an international fashion award the grand prize. They both got into a big argument at a small artistic shop over a small wooden horse that they both fell in love with. In the end with the owner's consent Ai-Ching is able to obtain the wooden horse.
