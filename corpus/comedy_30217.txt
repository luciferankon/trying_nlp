During a boring session of a scientific council Mr. Radev a reader at the University decides to leave the urban civilization and start a new life. On the road a mysterious car tire that takes him to a mountain village completely forgotten by people with only one inhabitant overtakes him. They - the reader and the peasant try to conform to each other. The magic of novelty slowly loses its charm. Only a miracle is able to save their closeness -and they find a map to a buried treasure.
