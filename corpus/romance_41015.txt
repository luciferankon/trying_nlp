Dan's stuck. He lives a mundane existence working a mediocre job that he doesn't care much about. He yearns for a relationship but has no idea how to go about talking to a woman. He's a decent man but lord knows he could use a little help. When God singles him out one night to share some discouraging news about the state of the world Dan turns his life completely upside down in a desperate quest for true love. His job family and preconceptions are all tossed aside in exchange for a series of chaotic dates with a variety of women in a mad 24-hour dash to find his soul mate. But is it all real or just the results of a fried food nightmare?
