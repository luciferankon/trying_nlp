Be it cup cakes eclairs bavarois or biscuits all three bakers must bake the same thing under the watchful eye of a master patissier. But the difference is in the detail - and they must bring their own particular flair style and interpretation to the recipe if they want to win the day. The ultimate proof of any pudding - or cake is in the eating - the end result must not only look good it's got to taste heavenly as well. And now it's left to the general public to determine the winner.
