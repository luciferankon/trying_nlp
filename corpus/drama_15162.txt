Chashman the character is played by the Actress Farah Shah Chashman when young was adopted by Mahagul character played by the actress "Naveed Shehzad". Mahagul has two sons elder one is Shahgul her step son played by Asad and her own younger child Shahnoor character played by Ahsen Khan. Chashman grew up with Shahgul all her life hence developed a sweet understanding which eventually took its form in a love relationship. Mahagul as mother was very happy and they accept the decision of both by set up their engagement. The younger son Shahnoor lives abroad for his studies and comes back as he hear the words about brother's engagement but things takes another path when Shahnoor finds himself in love with Chashman.
