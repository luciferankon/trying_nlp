The movie is about Doctor John Garth who has been convicted of the murder of an elderly friend and is awaiting his execution on death row. He is given permission to continue his experimentation on a serum that will stop the aging process using the blood from other criminals. Without the knowledge of the prison authorities he tests the serum on himself. Unfortunately the serum is turning him into a Jekyll & Hyde. Garth has his death sentence commuted to life in prison and is later pardoned and is released from prison .....
