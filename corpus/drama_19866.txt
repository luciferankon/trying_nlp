Auto rickshaw is a story that revolves around an Auto driver who takes part in illegal auto races in the city. He loses his closest friend in an accident in one of the races he took part. Now he wants to quit racing because it reminds him of his late friend. But a local don who has invested lot of money on him for the race wants him to drive races forever and threatens to kill him if he doesn't. Now that the auto driver has no choice other than racing against his own will meets three men who escaped a mental asylum and wants to get out of the country. The three men promise him that they will get him out of his problem if he helps them to get out of the country and they propose a plan of robbing a bank for their financial problems. The Auto Driver accepts and goes with the wind. What happens then is the Plot of Auto Rickshaw.
