This is a Biopic of a handicapped Irs officer Ajay Singh who is presently deputed as Deputy commissioner of income tax at ranchi (Jharkhand). First time in Indian film history the same Irs officer replayed his own role as Ajab Singh in this Biopic based on real events of his life. Azab Singh born in a poor Bpl farmer family. In an accident he lost his eyes and ears and became partially blind and deaf due to costly medical treatment which his parents did not afford. Azab Singh was genious and his father always encourage him to do best in life.
