Father Ev (Father Everette Trebtoske) retired but much loved pastor of St. Mary parish in Wimberley Texas talks about the important memorable meaningful and humorous events of his life. From his days as a star football player his calling his work at Boys Town Cristo Rey and finally as pastor of St. Mary in Wimberley Texas he takes you through his life's adventures and escapades. For those who knew and remember him this is a memorable journey into his amazing past that you will enjoy and treasure. For everyone else it is the insightful journey of a child saving a candle and finding his calling leading to a life of service and beyond. (Interview filmed in September of 2010 in Wimberley Texas.)
