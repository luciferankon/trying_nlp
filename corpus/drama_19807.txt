Payne Stewart was more than just a professional golfer who won eleven PGA Tour events three major championships including his final win at the U.S. Open just a few months before his untimely death at the age of 42. Payne's spirit personality and the transformation of his heart transcended the sport of golf. This motion picture "True Payne" tells the story of a man powerfully choosing to come to terms with his purpose his faith and the legacy that would be left for his family and loved ones.
