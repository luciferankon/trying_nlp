On the day of a full moon Kyle and Dahlia decide to take a camping trip to Mako Island as a goodbye to summer. But they accidentally land in the moon pool of the island which changes their lives forever. The force of the moon turned Kyle into a merman and Dahlia into a mermaid. Anytime they touch water they grow a tail. The brother and sister must stick together to get used to the recent developments that have hit them.
