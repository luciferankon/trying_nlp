Four orphans; Anna (Jette Verheugen) Sven (Rafael Polman) Daan (Mees Hofman) and Timo (Lukas Nieuweboer) live on the street and need to steel to survive. One day Daan finds a villa. A white villa. The orphans move there with the thought being alone but it appears that they're not the only people.... That's how they get into the claws of a humiliated drug dealer.
