IdoliDisciplina kicme Elektricni orgazam Laki pinvini Piloti Ekatarina Velika all line up to show their specific music styles in a one of a kind gathering of the best of the New Wave and punk music in Belgrade and Serbia in the 1980's. The legendary Belgrade Student Cultural Center is the venue hosting a gathering of the most popular and prolific rock bands of the Yugoslav New Wave movement. In this highly rated TV special Duca Markovic a young radio personality takes the audiences through the music that revolutionized Serbian and Yugoslav youth culture.
