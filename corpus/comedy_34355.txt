Fortysomething adolescent Simon has an unwelcome visitor. Talia his lawyer ex-wife has come to inform him that it's time to grow up and take responsibility for their teenage daughter Arwen. That or she'll be contacting the Child Support Agency. Which leaves him only a week to seduce his brand new lodger Isabel. And Arwen is supposed to be having her room. Then there's the usual problem of Krystof Mr. Morality kicking off about it all. Only disaster can result. It does.
