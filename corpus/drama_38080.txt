Maheen (Sana Askari) faces many challenges in life due to her love marriage with Faizan (Imran Aslam later played by Asad Siddiqui). Faizan a psychologically disturbed man tortures every woman he marries. In the past he has married 4 other women and harmed them physically/mentally. He plans to do the same with Maheen. Soon after with the help of her sixth sense Maheen begins to realize that Faizan's family and past has been impacted with black magic. Suspense occurs when Maheen begins to see ghosts of the dead. She must find out Faizan's past and the hidden truths.
