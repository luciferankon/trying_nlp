Lethal Cargo covers the fleeing of refugees from war regions in leaky boats towards safe havens - Migration the spilling of toxic waste into the depths of the ocean during goods transportation and in our two most recent episodes the illegal handling of drugs and weapons across the Mediterranean. Drugs are of course the trail blazer of all trafficking across the Mediterranean. A 4-part series featuring the following 45 minute episode: Migrants Toxic Waste Weapons Drugs.
