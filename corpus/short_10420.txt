Based on Leslea Newman's story "A Letter to Harvey Milk" this award-winning short examines the sensitive issues that surface when Harry Weinberg an older Jewish man enrolls in a creative writing class taught by a lesbian teacher. For one particular assignment Harry writes a letter to his former customer and friend the slain political activist Harvey Milk. The letter unleashes a series of events which impel Marvin Harry's best friend to painfully recall and reveal a tragic incident that occurred in a concentration camp forty years earlier.
