Cinna is 31 years old and works as a show performer and actress. Suddenly one day she suffer a stroke a clot in the brain. After the surgery she wakes up with paralysis in the entire right side and aphasia. She can not speak read or write. Cinna refuses to be broken and takes up the fight against the disease. The road turns out to be longer than she imagined and does not lead back - but to something entirely new.
