Series will give an intimate heart-warming and sometimes shocking look into the lives of moms and daughters who are anything but ordinary. Each episode will take an emotional journey inside the minds of two teenage girls ages 12-18 who are struggling to create healthy relationships with their dysfunctional mothers who are consumed with an obsession such as plastic surgery weight shopping working out or partying. Driven to their breaking points and put in the unthinkable position of taking on the role of the parent these teenagers are demanding a change. Through tough love dedication and the help of a therapist these pairs will try to pull themselves back from the brink of disaster and maintain a healthy and loving mother/daughter relationship. The families fight to repair an already broken relationship while experiencing anger heartache love reconciliation and change.
