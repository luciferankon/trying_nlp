After her father's death Hatsumi moved to this small town with her lawyer mother. Hatsumi joined the marathon team in school with her best friend Mari. Being irritated with her mother Hatsumi felt lonely in this new life. One day Hatsumi met Ryutaro a boy working for waste paper recovery. Soon they became close to each other. However one day Ryutaro sexually attacked Hatsumi suddenly. Hatsumi's mother made it a rape case and these two young lovers could not see each other. Hatsumi was suffering from her broken heart. She could not forgive Ryutaro but at the same time she was eager to see him. Drowning in mixed-up feelings Hatsumi made a surprising decision to her future...
