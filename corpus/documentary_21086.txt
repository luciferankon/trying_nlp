The documentary Gentilly Or Not To Be is a reflection about the state of the Quebec' nuclear power plant Gentilly-2. The film is asking the following question to the defenders and the detractors of the nuclear: beyond the risk of accidents and the problems caused by the storage of nuclear waste what are the impacts of the nuclear on human health?
