Aminul Haque a middle class government employee lives with his wife Latifa and two children. He is very much honest in his work though his co-workers inspires him to adopt dishonesty. His dream is to buy a land in Dhaka for his residence where he would live happily with his family. He searches news related land in newspapers. He finds one by the side of Meghna river but it goes under water during rainy season. Thus he losses hope and return home with sorrowful hearts and fever. By this time police comes to his house in search of his son Sohel who hijacked briefcase of a passer-by. He cannot take this shock. Finally he gets his desired land but forever.
