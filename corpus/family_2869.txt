Christmas Eve. The city houses streets and the whole atmosphere is lit up in colorful lights for the season. Alex Greg Kevin Sara - and all children of the world are making their wishes for Santa. Dores write her wish to be able to skate again one day and pursue her dream of artistic skating and her father takes the letter and goes out of the house to escape his deep sorrow for his daughter. James enters in their church and prays for a miracle to save their daughter knowingly that reality looks very grim. Christmas morning under the tree Santa has brought their gifts making them very Happy kids .... The houses and the whole city are being covered by a slow falling white snow...... Six months later we see Dores skating again and her wish come true.
