The sexiest star of Japanese cinema and one of the most popular genre actresses in Asia Kirara Asuka leads as the beautiful savior in shining armor in this action packed Adventure Dynamo! Supporting cast include Rina Akiyama Rakuto Tochihara and Mitsuki Koga among many other hot young talents. The action scenes are choreographed by Takanori Shibahara an internationally renowned Action Director of "Wild Stunts" fame.
