A lovely bride and her equally beautiful bridesmaids are in for a shock when their limousine arrives to transport them to the wedding. A woman bent on avenging herself upon the bride's family has captured the real driver and left her bound and gagged. Soon she and her henchman have the three gorgeous girls roped and tightly gagged and ready to be whisked away to points unknown. In a second tale two women are captured bound and gagged. When they attempt to escape the thug guarding them forces them to strip each other then binds them even tighter than before.
