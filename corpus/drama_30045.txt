This family-friendly movie is about love faith and redemption. The movie has a Latin flavor but its subject matter cloaked in the Christmas spirit is universal. Beautiful and alone Eva feels a spark of romantic interest when she meets Tito a widower struggling with the loss of a wife the loss of hope and the loss of his faith. Tito is consumed by the pressures of caring for his children and trying to keep a failing business above water. His growing bitterness overshadows the best parts of his character and drives him to actions that will only add disgrace to his woes. Without a friend left on earth nothing short of a miracle can relieve Titos crushing and solitary burdens. However miracles have been known to happen...
