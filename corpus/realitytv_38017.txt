Internationally renowned former 3x boxing lightweight champion of the world Tony "The Tiger" Lopez lets us take a look at his current life as a bail bondsman. Segments of real time action as he pursues criminals that have skipped bail with his team of bounty hunters. Both dramatic and light hearted the show is rich with real life characters; celebrities everyday citizens friends and family. It conveys a portrait of a man with compassion for the people yet a warrior's heart when the heat is on.
