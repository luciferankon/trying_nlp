From a bedroom window a voyeur watches the people who live and pass through the corner of 6th and Market st. in San Francisco's grittiest neighborhood- The Tenderloin. Shot in true guerrilla style over the years of 1998 and 1999 it pre-dates reality television and simply presents reality. A woven cloth of short stories that tell themselves the absence of a narrator and with only music and the sounds of the street- the traditional documentary format is turned on it's head leaving the viewer to interpret for themselves...
