Eric Shepherd (Mike Stanley) comes to realize that the doxital drug he has been injected with causes a series of bizarre side effects. His longtime friend Laura leaves Eric unable to confront him regarding the truth behind the doxital. Meanwhile doxital patients escape from the Mt. Clemens Hospital for the criminally insane. These patients have only one thing on their minds...Human Flesh! Dead is Dead Again was made with public domain footage from the films "Messiah of Evil" "Silent Night Bloody Night" and "Panic". New footage was shot and the outcome is a disturbing gore wrenching tale of "Old School Horror".
