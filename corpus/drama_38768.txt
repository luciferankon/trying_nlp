Cranky old Al Kaplan cons his estranged grandson Nate to join him on a road trip. As members of Al's senior posse join them Nate is appalled to learn that the secret purpose of the journey is to kidnap a Nazi war criminal who has been hiding in the U. S. Nate is unwittingly caught in the middle of this vigilante bid for justice. Al Kaplan and his gang of wisecracking Holocaust Survivors plot to kidnap the Nazi war criminal before he is deported back to Lithuania. Nate Al's estranged grandson is unwittingly lured into the plot - and his relationship with his grandfather begins.
