The year is 2027 and Britain is not as we knew it ruled over by a oppressive far-right government with the figurehead of a puppet King. In this country there is nothing but division. Jane Sullivan a state journalist who is tired of the rigged press is contacted by the leader of an underground guerrilla fighter group the elusive "Plantagenet" with the offer of a story an offer she can't resist. As her story unfolds she finds a group of young men and women who have put their lives on hold to fight for what they believe is right but the question posed to them all is what their doing right?
