This one hour special tells the powerful story of three men who received the Medal of Honor America's highest award for valor: Chief Master Sergeant Richard Etchberger Staff Sergeant Robert Miller and Staff Sergeant Salvatore Giunta. A Father a Son and a Husband these men are the true definition of heroes. Two of them died as they had lived: caring for those closest to them. While SSG Giunta the first living recipient of the MoH in 40 years was only trying to save his best friend.
