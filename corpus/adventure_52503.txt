An airplane carrying policeman and gangsters crash-lands in a jungle where they discover a white girl and her father lost twelve years previously in another plane crash. The father and daughter are living quietly with the natives but are eager to get back to civilization since the girl has a large estate and fortune she can claim. She makes a deal with the pilot to return for her after she has shown him how to escape by the river. A tribal medicine-man and the head gangster attempt to prevent the pilot's escape. An airplane carrying a pair of police officers escorting gangsters to prison crash-lands in the African jungle. They are rescued from an attack by savage natives by a white woman who appears out of nowhere and it turns out she is a lost heiress who herself crash-landed in the jungle years before.
