Dr. Neelkanth and his wife Arundhati are living in his native village since the last 5 years. Their son Bundoo is studying in a boarding school in Pune. Having born in a rich family money was never an issue. After successfully practicing for 2 years in Mumbai Dr.Neelkanth decided to go to the village for the better of the rural people much to the annoyance of his wife. One day he gets a visit from one Shekhar Mirajkar a sales executive from a tractor firm. A bewildered Arundhati is shocked to see Shekhar her ex lover.
