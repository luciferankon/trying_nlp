In Spring is a masterpiece of Ukrainian cinema avant-garde a non-fiction film made by Mikhail Kaufman Dziga Vertov's brother and co-author along the lines of the avant-gardist theory of Ťcine-eyeť. The film shows Kyiv in 1929 almost unknown today. Pictures of wakening city its resurging life resonate with lyrical views of reviving nature. Kaufman's attentive camera dwells deliberately on smiling faces of children lyrically depicting a declaration of love to Kyiv. In In Spring Kaufman used the method of Ťhidden camerať for the first time.
