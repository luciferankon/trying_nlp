An Abridged Series created by YaroShien "How To Be An Adventurer" follows the exploits of Bell Cranel and his goddess/advisor/fuck-buddy Hestia as they attempt to make a name for themselves as a Familia in the cutthroat business of Dungeon Crawling in the city of Orario. However while he may face formidable foes in the form of frightening monsters in the Dungeon Bell's most persistent voracious adversaries are instead the various women who all seem to want to jump his bones.
