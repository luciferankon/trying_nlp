A young woman refuses to conform to her times. He Tianxin ('Na-ra Jang' (qv)) is smart and sassy and aspires for more than what society tells her women should be. Dressed like a boy Tianxin wants to become a doctor. When she meets Emperor Zhu Li ('TAE' (qv)) and the handsome Zhang Zhesan ('Hao Gao (I)' (qv)) they both fall in love with the unconventional young woman. What will become of this unusual love triangle? _"Unruly qiao" (2011)_ (qv) is a 2011 Chinese drama series. It is a spin-off of the 2005 drama _"Diao man gong zhu" (2005)_ (qv).
