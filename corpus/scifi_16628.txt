In an alternate reality a series of intelligence failures and corruption has left The United States and Russia in the clutches of the Cold War. To counter the failures the governments have created special agencies to deal with these domestic threats. Dryden an agent of the secret police for the USA is in charge of hunting down traitors. When he unknowingly executes a scientist working on a secret government project called Sky Dome - a project that will end the deadlock in mutually assured destruction in favor of the USA Dryden finds himself trapped in a game of cloak and dagger with not only the Russians but also those inside his own country.
