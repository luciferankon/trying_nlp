Gamer nerd Yukio meets Yanagi and learns about his plans to stream his suicide live online. Although confused about the situation Yukio agrees to help Yanagi. On broadcast day Yanagi murders Sekiguchi before heading home to begin streaming the broadcast. During the final conversation with his viewers personal information about Yanagi gets leaked. Yukio watching at home rushes off towards Yanagi's place. While being ridiculed by those who are watching Yanagi hangs himself at the scheduled time.When Yukio arrives all that awaits is Yanagi's hanging body and the comments of the viewers trying to avoid responsibility.
