In the small hamlet of Pushington Downs Edward the Less is assigned by the grand wizard Primatene to destroy a cursed token before it falls into the hands of the Evil One though this doesn't really fit in with Edward's plans to get out and see the world. Aided by his faithful servant Soapwort McFuggletoes and the [not so] Noble One Edward is begrudgingly sent off on his quest to destroy the token while trying to evade the evil Bus Riders and trying not to feel too much like "Lord of the Rings."
