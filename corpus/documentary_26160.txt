Animation is everywhere. From Asian anime to American cartoons. Even animation from dutch soil has become booming as of late. Cartoons can't be ignored anymore. For some people it's nostalgia and for other people it's a way to lose themselves into a fantasy world filled with humor and wonderful stories. Sync takes you behind the scenes of this world. Together with voice actors directors engineers translators and studio management Sync will let you peek into the world of dutch localization the impact it has on our society and the love that's being put into it.
