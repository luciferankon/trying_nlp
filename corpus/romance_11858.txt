STAN MANN (STEVEN CHASE) a new money multi-millionaire whose life consists of ladies gambling and booze lives at a 5 STAR HOTEL where the Staff tend to his every need. Known as "STAN THE MAN" for his extreme generosity he gets mixed up in a casino gambling scheme with Russian Mobsters and in one fateful bet STAN loses everything. Planning to drown his sorrows in a bottle STAN is critically wounded in a liquor store robbery where he takes the bullet intended for the store clerk KRISTI (ANNE LEIGHTON). STAN has a near death experience in the ER when a slightly resentful ANGEL gives STAN a chance to change his ways and redeem himself by finding his one true love within 30 days.
