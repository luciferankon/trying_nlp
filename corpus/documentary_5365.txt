Acclaimed author expedition leader and chef Andrew Dwyer takes you on an adventure like no other in the Campfire Chronicles. You will discover wild and remote locations where Andrew will meet the locals encounter unique and surprising plants and wildlife uncover curious and quirky histories and cook up a feast on the campfire using cast iron and coals. You'll see country never before seen on television and all filmed in stunning High Definition on the sensational Red camera. The documentary is accompanied by a moving and innovative soundtrack composed and performed by acclaimed musician Charlie Owen. From the mountains to the high seas and the deserts Campfire Chronicles is a unique new television experience!
