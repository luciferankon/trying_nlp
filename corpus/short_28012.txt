Set in downtown Tucson and the Tortolita Mountains in the beautiful Sonoran Desert "The Tortolita Twelve" is a unique and lyrical statement about cyber-bullying. The film follows twelve teenagers some bullied some bullies some just going along; all dealing with the fallout from online events that have forever changed their lives.
