The boy by the name of Ural having argued with the brother goes on hundred steps to depth of the well-known Bashkir cave. The boys even didn't assume in what this dispute can pour out! In the ancient world where Ural got the treacherous Shulgan reigns who is the brother of Ural-batyr from the Bashkir epos of the same name. Having passed serious tests Ural comprehends the true price of friendship and fidelity reveals a secret of rock paintings on walls of an ancient cave.
