A retellening of the controversial story of the 90s hip-hop rap music record label Death Row as depicted through the eyes of rappers Calvin "Snoop Dogg" Broadus Ricardo "Kurupt" Brown and Delmar "Daz Dillinger" Arnaud. The film discusses working conditions under Marion "Suge" Knight and the releases of the critically acclaimed albums 1993's Doggystyle 1996's Tha Doggfather and 1995's Dogg Food.
