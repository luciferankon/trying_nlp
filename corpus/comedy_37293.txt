Three struggling friends find a scandalous video that could finally lift them from working class stiffs. The star of the recoding is not going to lay down his future political career depends on retrieving the video and eliminating any evidence of the indiscretion. A private detective a former pimp and an angry ex-convict are only the obvious obstacles our boys must overcome. Lucky for them they have the aid of army veteran Uncle Miguel. It's our three Janitors against an entire cast of obstacles and hurdles. Watch as the comedy ensues. It could be the biggest of "come-ups" or the final call. There are laughs as well as danger around every curve and every plan of our bumbling janitors.
