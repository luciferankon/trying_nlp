Set in 1958 A Group of aliens crash land on earth and is discovered by the son of an food company called Monet foods. Monet Foods colectes the transmitter used to call more aliens in the blink of an eye to earth. They capture them and inject them with chemicals then use thier meat for food. After the kidnapping of thier pet alien snoogans and finding out the horrors of Monet two greaser chicks Annie and Pinkie try to stop monet foods before the aliens decide to destroy earth for the horrors they caused thier race.
