The series chronicles the miraculous journey of a small church formed by worship leaders and musicians on the gulf of Mexico in to a supernatural movement Worship United is a diverse group of worship leaders singers and musicians coming together to worship God. This incredible vision was birthed in Ladell Cole in 2012 as he called on worship leaders and minstrels from different churches across the Tampa Bay Florida area to Unite together in Worship thus forming Worship United. The sound is very diverse ranging from Hillsongs Gospel and Caribbean this Kingdom sound connects with any audience and ethnic group. Worship United has become a movement in the city of Tampa and across the country. The ultimate goal is to create an atmosphere of worship that will cause lives to be changed hearts to be transformed and people to be refreshed in the presence of God.
