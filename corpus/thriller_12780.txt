Young author Simon Knight writes his debut novel in hopes of reconnecting with his mother who is fast losing her memory. While the book cannot bring her back it becomes a huge success with the public. But when the novel inspires a horrible crime in real life Simon falls into ignominy as quickly as he rose to fame. Overwhelmed by guilt and fear Simon takes refuge in a mysterious old hotel where he hopes to escape the spotlight and write his comeback book. But this is no ordinary hotel. Here dreaming and waking begin to blur and the boundaries between fiction and reality fall away.
