Unique recording from a unique show with Scandinavia's biggest rock band - EUROPE. In this semi acoustic concert accompanied with a string quartet Europe performs songs from their career together with songs from bands that have inspired them over the years. The show was broadcast live over the Internet making this evening a very memorable milestone for the band that they decided to release this show on DVD and CD. The picture was restored into 16:9 and the sound mixed into 5.1 DTS.
