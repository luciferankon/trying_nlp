In this bawdy newsroom comedy middle aged swingers Morty and Lydia team up with their grown kids Donnie and Sara to launch a streaming news network out of their basement but with no audience and becoming desperate they enlist their crew of misfit anchors to host a new lineup of oddball shows including Truth or Shock Judge Inga Toe to Toe and Home Cooked Shopping.
