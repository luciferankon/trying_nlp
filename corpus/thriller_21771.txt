Alex Prager is a rising star of the British Foreign Office. Dedicated and driven yet scarred by events in her past she seems destined for a stellar career. But when Alex learns of the sudden death of her estranged twin sister everything she once knew is called into question. Sensing things are not as they seem Alex begins to investigate and is soon thrown into a brutal world of corruption terrorism and murder. But a chilling discovery regarding the involvement of the British Government causes events to take an even more terrifying turn forcing Alex to confront truths more shocking than she could ever have imagined and putting her own life on the line.
