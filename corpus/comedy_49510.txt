'Skippy's Basement' is a Fandom-oriented show that will offer news commentaries sketches and guest interviews. The show is hosted by Thaddeus J. 'Skippy' Kozlowskowiecz and Ralphie 'The Wrench' Robbaccio a couple of life-long nerdy fanboy types from Brooklyn NY. Produced for Geektastic Nebula by TurboMandala Entertainment and Skippy's Mother Mrs. Kozlowskowiecz (Hey it's her house her rules...).
