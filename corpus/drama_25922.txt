Phillip Chandler was once the premier adult film actor but today he is a man drowning in an unforgiving past an indifferent present and an uncertain future. The offer of a comeback video and a blossoming relationship with a loving woman give Phillip hope for a fruitful life again... until the past beckons for retribution and Phillip must make the crucial decision to move on with his new life or settle old scores that will change everyone's lives forever.
