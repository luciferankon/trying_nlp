HOA is a comedy/drama based in a small California housing development in the desert. The residents are manipulated by a self-serving Home Owners Association board. A retired government hit man (Max) attempts to reintegrate into society in his retirement years. He becomes a member of this HOA but takes his responsibilities a little too seriously. Jocelyn an ex-FBI agent now a Park Ranger and single mom is also on the HOA board. There is a weekly murder of an HOA offender who has committed a crime against civility and society by violating one of the Covenants Conditions and Restrictions (CC&R's).
