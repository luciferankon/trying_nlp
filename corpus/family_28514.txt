After a year-long historic 54000 kilometer globe-trotting journey to collect signatures from such superstars as Jackie Chan Hugh Jackman Priscilla Presley and many many others the STARS WITH HEARTS International Celebrity T-Shirt Relay reached the zenith of its journey at The Magical Scarecrows "Let's Make Magic for a Day" event of 2009 to benefit children touched by terminal illnesses. While Australian and international superstars such as Barry Crocker Joe Bugner and Leo Sayer put on a variety performance spectacular Auntie Lynn donated Magical Scarecrows outfits books and DVDs for the children. Witness the final signatures being added at the climax of this historic journey for Variety the children's charity.
