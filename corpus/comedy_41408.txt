When a package goes missing from his route deliveryman Ned Whiteman begins his quest to track it down only to find that the contents included an enchanted killer yeti hand. He must team up with Judy Terracotta security guard for life in order to capture it before it attacks the entire town. Meanwhile the original intended recipient of the package Hilary Montegnya engages in a series of diabolical schemes as she anxiously awaits the yeti hand's arrival.
