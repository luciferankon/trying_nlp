Ohitika is a young Oglala Lakota man from the Pine Ridge Reservation. When tragedy struck his family his idea of justice lost its balance. In his pursuit of justice he sought to push all of the negativity out of his own spirit and accidentally created a menacing Gnaskinyan spirit which only he can see. Ohitika fights daily to destroy the Gnaskinyan but will the Lakota teachings of balance change his warlike approach?
