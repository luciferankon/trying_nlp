Late Night at the Plaza is a comedic and eclectic talk show that showcases a collection of national talent encompassing everything from the host's week to week bits to stand up comedy gorgeous dance numbers and everything in between - all put together on one stage live bi-weekly with an 80 minute run time all at the Plaza Theater in Calgary Alberta. After a half decade (5 seasons) of production Late Night at the Plaza has garnered a sizeable cult following in Alberta and looks to continue building on that. Calgary AB every other Wednesday night at the Plaza Theatre.
