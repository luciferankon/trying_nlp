The students lead themselves to a world full of colors emotions music visions dreams and hopes. By occupying their school and according to the general situation in Greece in 2006 the students are demanding the obvious for better infrastructures at schools for advanced lessons and teachers to develop their knowledge continuously in an educated frame which quenching concerns and opens horizons. As a team and individually with seriousness consistency and humor they contribute to a better education system in the future having lived a unique and unforgettable experience that brings them closer to one another. The students came closer with each other which did not have the opportunity to live through the competitive educational system. That was when they felt love for their school.
