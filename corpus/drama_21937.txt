Soccer coach Gao Bo is coaching a women's team in Japan which brings back memories of a similar job he had in China. He decides to return to China to reorganize his old team. This appears to be a quasi-remake of Woman Basketball Player No. 9 (1957). The young and energetic coach Gao Bo is coaching a women soccer team at a Japanese club he often thinks of a provincial women soccer team he once coached in China. He is determined to reorganize the women soccer team therefore he returns to China. With the full support of Zhui Yin the director of Hongfeng Electric Appliances Factory Gao Bo makes a solemn pledge to the Sports Commission: to win the Championship of National League within three years..
