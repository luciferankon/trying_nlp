Consentidos Estrellas is a children's magazine inspired by the wonderful imaginative world of children which will be taken home by the talent of 10 mini reporters and animators along with Osman Aray and a fantastic ballet. Through segments and musicals this space gives all children the opportunity to recreate a world full of fun entertainment color and information. This program is inspired by all the fantasies of the children of the house where they as protagonists will have the opportunity to animate sing dance imitate act have fun and learn healthily.
