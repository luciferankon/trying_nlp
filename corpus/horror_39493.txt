Ghost Eyes concerns a female hair stylist (Chen Szu-chia) who is seduced by the vampiric ghost of a former optometrist (Szu Wei). Using supernatural contact lenses to control her mind he gradually drains her life essence as she is forced to find new victims until tries to make a stand and rid herself of this terror once and for all.
