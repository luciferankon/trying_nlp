In the pilot episode instructors Toni and Jason announce that they want to train to become professional Dancesport competitors forcing studio owner Brian and his partner Kyle to contemplate expanding the studio to help them. Brian also decides to hire new trainees Bobby and Jessica. Chelsea Brian's dance partner and her husband Thomas help keep everyone balanced. Bobby has to decide how to come out to his father Peter who is the Associate Pastor of their church and his mother Sarah who is well-known in the community. Each is faced with the decision to play it safe or step outside of their comfort zone and follow their heart.
