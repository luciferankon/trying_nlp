The series premieres with Zane Lamprey and his drinking buddies Steve McKenna and Marc Ryan visiting their first city on the Drinking Made Easy Tour Phoenix Arizona. Zane takes in a flight at Four Peaks Brewery makes a monster prickly pear margarita at Aunt Chilada's and learns how to make illegal tequila. Meanwhile Marc drinks dog beer at a "Barkmitvah" and Steve feeds his legend at Gilligan's Bar with 5lbs. of tater tots and a ranch dressing bomb.
