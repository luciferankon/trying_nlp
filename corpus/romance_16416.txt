Many older people associate most beautiful memories of one of their favorite holiday destinations: the sea. But with progressive dementia these impressions fade more and more. This film is designed specifically for people with dementia. Thus it is now possible to experience the beauty of the coast and sea in the TV and to remember old times. The film follows a pair at a eventful day at the seaside: in the morning in the port with a trip to a picturesque mill while sunbathing in a beach chair while walking through wide dunes to the lighthouse with a classic tea time and the ride on horseback on beach to the atmospheric sunset at sea. Without dialogue and without close-ups of faces the film offers enough space for personal memories. With emphasizes peaceful imagery and high color contrasts accompanied by harmonious soothing music and the sounds of nature is an experience of a special kind of the film.
