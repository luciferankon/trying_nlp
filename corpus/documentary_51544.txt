No one has ever clearly explained to women what happens to their body throughout their life. The prevention of certain conditions and changes which affect us remains limited. Women no Pause is a documentary which shows women above the age of 45 from all over the world. In Japan the country with the longest life-expectancy through to France Spain the Equator and all the way to Tanzania we will meet mothers wives working women housewives professionals grandmothers and gynaecologists who above all want to be seen as WOMEN.
