Diagnosed with an extremely rare form of cancer that doctors said he had "a million to one" chance of contracting with the only strong odds being that he wouldn't survive "Million to One" is an intense documentary that follows the story of Matt Burnsworth and his seemingly never ending battle with a rare disease and the tough luck that life can sometimes throw at you. We hope that when you see this story it will change the way you look at the world. You will be inspired end up feeling warm about life and believe that when hope is part of the equation - nothing is impossible - no matter what the odds!
