The heart of the matter has been lost for a long time. The greatest talkmaster of all times has the lovely variety and strength of language destroyed. He talks a lot and says nothing. Florian the gardener cultivates the language's variety in his garden. He starts searching for the heart of the matter. He realizes the nothingness behind the talkmaster's seemingly powerful slogans. The battle over the last word takes its course. A silent movie in Dolby Surround.
