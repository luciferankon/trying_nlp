Having fallen out with his girlfriend Dolorčs Juanito leaves for Mexico where he becomes a famous matador.There he meets a Viennese singer Fanny Miller who falls for him so much so that she intercepts the letters Dolorčs keeps on sending him. In desperation Dolorčs devotes all her time and efforts to dance and she becomes the celebrated dancer Estrellita. After a series of misunderstandings and ups and downs the sun of Andalusia will finally reunite the two lovebirds.
