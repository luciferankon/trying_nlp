Papa Roach: Live & Murderous in Chicago is the first DVD released by Californian rock group Papa Roach. It was released in the United States on November 22 2005 - and features the band performing live at "The Vic Theater" in Chicago Illinois. The program captures alt-metal rockers Pap Roach thrashing away at their most intense during an exhilarating Chicago concert bringing down the house with an energetic 75-minute set.
