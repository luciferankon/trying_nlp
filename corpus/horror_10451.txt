Action-packed sequel to THE EVILMAKER. A family member of the victim in part 1 tries to figure out what happened at that cabin in the woods not long ago. Before you know it the body count begins and a supernatural being makes its presence known... A Secluded House... Unexplained Dissapearances... Kathy is a troubled psychic whose younger gothic sister Rachel vanishes. As the search for her sister begins Kathy is thrust into a world of darkness and sex crazed demons. All worldly barriers are shattered when the path to survival is marked with corpses and stolen souls. As insatiable evils seek to destroy Kathy her tortured fight from within ignites her search for answers and her sister.
