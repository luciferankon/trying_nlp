Being shot in nine countries and exploring the havoc cancer wreaks globally "Finding Match" traces the battle to capture the final summit in cancer cure. Spearheaded by VP Joe Biden the Cancer MoonShot 2020 Program aims to win the war on cancer and turn it into a manageable chronic illness. While current "possible" cures find matches in chemotherapy cocktails stem cell transplants and other therapies the Cancer MoonShot aims for patients to find a curative match in immunotherapy. Fifty years later will America's 2020 moonshot be as successful as 1969?
