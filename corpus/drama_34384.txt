In 1995 Axel a lawyer working in former Yugoslavia adopts his lover's daughter Stanka. Years later Bosnian journalist Osman the son of victims killed by Stanka's father a Serbian army officer investigates and finds the couple. When Axel dies Stanka in mourning continues her life journey with Osman and then disappears. Osman eventually finds Stanka in Belgrade at the trial of her father Dragan indicted for war crimes.
