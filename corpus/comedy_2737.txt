After a few years of separation a young Russian girl Olga comes to New York to reunite with her boyfriend Sasha who defected from his ballroom dance team at the International competition. Sasha lives with his American girlfriend Susan whom he tells that Olga a circus artist is his cousin. The situation becomes quite uncomfortable: Olga who doesn't speak English is jealous Sasha is confused and Susan is suspicious. She demands to place Olga somewhere else. Thus start Olga's adventures in the US.
