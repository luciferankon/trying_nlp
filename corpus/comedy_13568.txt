After a heart-wrenching breakup Oscar Sanchez was left reeling into a cocoon to live a life without meaning. Now 9 years later he finds himself teaching history at a school he hates with people he doesn't care to know. In order to relieve himself from his painful yesteryear Oscar must liberate himself into the present by cutting his ties to the past. Sometimes it's easier said than done. Sometimes one needs a little guidance. Sometimes it's just a matter of time.
