Five couples who met at an antenatal class tell their "warts-and-all" honest funny highly emotional stories of pregnancy birth and the first few weeks. This is everything you wanted to know about birth but were afraid to ask! By parents for parents REAL BIRTH STORIES is not an instruction manual or a text book. These personal honest stories scale the highs of lows of having a baby. The stories chart every step of life's most incredible journey from conceiving to becoming a new parent. Honest funny heartfelt and surprising these stories will help expectant parents prepare for the amazing journey ahead.
