A short film that follows a young man throughout his day and chronicles the various pressures and judgments put upon him by the modern world. From the expectations to match physical standards to the expectations around intelligence style and social capabilities Guy is unable to come to terms and fit into the fast pace of the world around him. It isn't until he breaks down that he is able to sync with the rest of the world and by then it is too late for him to see that by doing so he disappears into it.
