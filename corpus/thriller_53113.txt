Businessman Jim Pennie and his wife Karin have a happy life with their daughter Annie. However on the eve of Annie's 12th birthday she begins having nightmares. These nightmares reveal dark information from the family's past. A past that Jim had until now kept hidden from the rest of the family. Ten years earlier Jim Karin's brother Paul Graham and their strange friend 'Solo' committed a crime. Although they had good intentions it had deadly consequences posing the question - how far would you go to protect your family?
