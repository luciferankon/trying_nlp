Meet El Oso (the Bear) one of the founders of Casino- the dance that launched salsa. A simple man whose joie de vivre delights Oso travels through Havana regaling us with tales of his youth when Havana's exclusive clubs were white only forcing him to dance on the streets. After rock n' roll and the revolution intervene Oso and his circle of friends choreograph a new history felt today across the globe-in Mongolia Macedonia Melbourne Malmo Munich and Malaysia--and that's only the M's. One of the first U.S.-Cuba co-productions The WHEEL OF LIFE spins international relations onto the dance floor.
