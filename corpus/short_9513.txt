Under His Roof is a short psychological thriller that follows Christina Marshall a college sophomore on a visit to the woodland cabin shared by Meryl her older sister and James Meryl's frustrated novelist boyfriend. What begins as a friendly check in to see how Meryl's adjusting to country life takes an unsettling turn after James makes a sexually aggressive pass at Christina. Blinded by James' manipulation Meryl refuses to see him for what he truly is and Christina must take drastic action to save them both from his clutches.
