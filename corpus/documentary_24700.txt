Documentary about a traditional parade that goes marching the streets of Ipanema in Rio de Janeiro during the Carnival called Banda de Ipanema (Band from Ipanema) and the man who created it in 1966 Albino Pinheiro. Albino was sort of a cultural promoter father of the series of Brazilian pop music shows at affordable prices called "6 e Meia" (Six and a half).
