Mr. Olympia is one of the most significant and well-known international bodybuilding competitions held under the auspices of the International Federation of Bodybuilding (IFBB) from September 18 to 21 2014 in Las Vegas Nevada USA on the Orleans Arena. This was the 50th on the account competition of "Mr. Olympia". The prize fund amounted to 710 000 US dollars. The winner was the American bodybuilder Phil Heath becoming a four-time winner of the competition.
