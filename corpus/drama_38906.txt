In 1982 Las Vegas the mafia grip on the casinos between the Chicago outfit and other Midwestern mobs is held together by a fragile truce. Now a new player emerges as a powerful New York crime family tries to make its way west. The murder of one its members at a Vegas nightclub sends shock waves nationwide and the ramifications are a full scale war unless a summit in Miami keeps the peace. Chicago's top enforcer and his showgirl girlfriend are the prime suspects and missing but the motive is unclear. The only form of help is the FBI and its crack team of agents who are there to stop the mob war from happening
