Ram (Viju Khote) and Shaam (Anand Abhyankar) are rich partners in business who have an unusual habit of betting with each other for insignificant reasons. According to Ram a human tends to behave as per the situation around him whereas Shaam believes that no matter what the situation is it cannot change the core of human's nature. The rich duos challenge each other for a mere bet of ten rupees and decide to have a real-life experience of human tendency. They create a situation where their honest successful employee Vaibhav (Sachin Pilgaonkar) is forced to see the world of poverty while a laid back beggar named Chandu (Ashok Saraf) gets to live the life of a rich businessman. Their lives take drastic turns where Vaibhav realizes the true value of humanity while Chandu realizes the lack of humanity in the high society. Chandu and Vaibhav later learn that Ram and Shaam are spoiling their lives for a mere bet. They decide to even the scores by teaching Ram and Shaam a lesson. Will the rich duos realize their mistake?
