Leera the soulmate is an upcoming Indian space adventure love story film directed by Sumnash Sri Kaljai produced by Usha Kaljai under the banner of Kaljaimovies . Leera Kaljai and Mehul Adwani in the lead role . Movie poster was released on 17 January 2017 . The film teaser and trailer was released in media on 9 February 2017. The film is scheduled for release 2017.
