Shortly after the discovery at Gran Sasso and CERN where neutrinos were recorded to be traveling faster than the speed of light an investigative team of journalists set out to meet the man who pioneered much of the research. Since the revelation leading physicist Professor Harry Gordon has released papers detailing his theories on time travel and how he believes it not only to be possible but within grasp in the very near future. Naturally the claims have caused much debate within the scientific community. The documentary makers sought to find out about his theories and see what makes Professor Gordon tick yet when they arrived they're a different story unfolded before them...
