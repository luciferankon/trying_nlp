Eleven thieves and con-artists spend their time stealing auto parts and other petty robberies but when several of them are detained for a brawl during a soccer match they decide to do scams to gather the money to pay the bail then when the whole gang is back together they decide to do one final big hit steal the jewels of a mysterious woman. A gang on eleven small-time thieves (which also form an amateur soccer team)  plan a big score their plan is to gain access to a rich and mysterious woman's house in order to steal her jewelry but when one of the members start having second thought the whole heist is put on jeopardy.
