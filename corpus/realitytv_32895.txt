The Hollywood Hunter is a reality based television show capturing the life of Freddy Harteis and his celebrity wife Jeannie Mai (Style Network's "How Do I Look"). Living in Hollywood Freddy pursues his passion while doing his best to educate a city culture that lacks understanding and acceptance for hunting. While hunting around the world for Big Game Animals Freddy takes time to showcase all the good that hunters do. He uses unique conservation methods partners with charities and helps to feed those in need. As the Hollywood Hunter his goal is to be one voice that represents what millions of hunters do.
