A Mother is losing her grown son to a rare form of cancer and as she sits in the hospital with him day in and day out they remember together their past. fun times laughter hard time etc. The son goes through horrible treatments to try to keep him alive at the mother's plea.. but he wants to let go and let god and only does this so she can have some time with him. he feels that maybe he can save her if she sees that he is not afraid.. as he dies his only concern is his love for his mom and to save her so that she can have a fulfilled next chapter of her life and not be alone.
