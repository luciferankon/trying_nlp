The story takes place primarily in contemporary (2014) Los Angeles. It's the perfect place for Von Parker a hypocritical televangelist and spiritual leader to kings and king makers and the perfect place to tell the story of his rise fall and the ultimate redemption of his soul. The series which will encompass four (4) to five (5) seasons will chronicle Von Parker's life at the Pinnacle of his career (first three or four seasons) Von Parker's Fall from Grace (first half of the final season) and ultimately Von Parker's Redemption (final half of the final season).
