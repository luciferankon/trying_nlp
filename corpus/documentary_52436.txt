An enlightening and energetic documentary showcasing the diversity of the American people. The filmmakers two brothers drove from Los Angeles to New York City with five questions in hand interviewing ordinary Americans not ordinarily seen in film or on television. Conquering desert heat in the Southwest traversing the breadth of Texas and surviving the Gulf Coast's tropical storm Isadore we see the heart of the American people. Fall in love with America again not just for its scenic beauty but for the millions of unique voices that make up one people.
