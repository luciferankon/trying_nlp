An inspirational and triumphant film about life and its unpredictable nature; a story about how what you experience makes you who you are. It's about understanding that you are the creator of your own life and of your own reality. A talented and recently graduated young actor Kevin has everything he ever dreamed of: a part in a feature film money great friends and love which he finds in the beautiful Merrill. Yet one day something happens by pure coincidence and changes everything in his life. Nobody can control what is to happen; we can only make a choice about what we will do about it . 'Coincidence doesn't exist'' is a compelling and touching film for everyone that will change your perception of life and happiness. Its a must see.
