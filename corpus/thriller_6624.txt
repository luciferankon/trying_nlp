An mysterious accident is the trigger to an inside war for Sando an enigmatic man far away from the ordinary who tries to save himself of the self-destruction that is tortured from his antagonists (archetypes) of his sub-conscience. A fight that tries to moderate the Intermediary and which will be strengthen by Judith an psychologist with whom Sando is involved in a romantic relationship. Envy Lies and Death convey in the search for power making unstable Sando's dreamed order. Everyone within his reach will be in danger.
