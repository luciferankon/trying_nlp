Sexual Suicide is an adult fiction based novel set in a small town in Indiana. After a horrific car accident the effect on three young beautiful girls turns out for the worst. As the gates of hell open up in their mind the temptation to live breath and kill become more of a reality when they awake from a terrible nightmare. A feud awakens when the sisters become torn apart; fighting between heaven and hell angels and demons and reality and imagination. The battle to fight away from the evil seems to die down while becoming possessed and provoked by the devil himself.
