This one-hour drama is set in motion when a neo-Nazi skinhead endures a traumatic event so massive that it forces him to abandon his klan whom he also holds responsible. With few options he discovers the only way to obtain forgiveness and redemption is to join an investigative biracial-led group THE JUSTICE HOUSE which causes him to come head-to-head with the wrath of his brethren and other extremist groups. The ultimate journey follows the struggles of a man who must confront his ingrained demons as he fights to achieve the impossible - to learn to love those he intensely hates.
