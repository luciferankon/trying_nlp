Though together for less than 2 years Buffalo Springfield led by Neil Young Stephen Stills and Richie Furay released 3 seminal albums. With an original sound drawing from rock folk blues and country influences they were determined to chart their own course and compromise for no-one and in so doing created a timeless body of work. As Buffalo Springfield (Revisited) founding members Bruce Palmer (bass) and Dewey Martin (drums) assembled this stellar set of talented musicians and took to the road to expand on the legacy. Experience this great performance which includes the band's biggest hit For What It's Worth and 12 other songs one of which has never before been released on record: A Thousand Dreams.
