Enter the real lives of Ashley Haglund and David Patino a couple living and working in Morristown New Jersey. David a professional photographer and Ashley a professional cinematographer spend their days at work and at play. Watch their adventures with their good friend Elyse Grosman and their dog Muddy. This is a reality show based in Morristown NJ that I am creating about my life. Episodes will be released on a weekly basis. I am creating this all with a Sony small hand held HD camera which I take with me everywhere.
