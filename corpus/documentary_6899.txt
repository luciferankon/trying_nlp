Mainstream Source kicks off our Legends Among Us series with a full-feature documentary pilot exploring the life and career of legendary trumpeter Steve Madaio. In his own words Steve describes his experiences from performing onstage at Woodstock with the Butterfield Blues Band through his years of performing recording arranging for and touring with iconic performers Stevie Wonder The Rolling Stones John Lennon Earth Wind and Fire Barbra Streisand James Taylor Neil Diamond Madonna Eric Clapton and virtually hundreds of others. This is a must-see for lovers of popular music in the last half-century.
