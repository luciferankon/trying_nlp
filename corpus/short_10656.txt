Same Drugs is a timeless coming of age story. Relationships are complicated road maps and each leaves it's unique mark on one's character. In Same Drugs pieces of a free-spirited youth are folded packed and carried along the road of life by a girl and boy. Truth lies in embracing the happy thoughts of their childhood. The two realize there is infinite beauty in every stage of life untold change and growth. Life in itself is charmed. Although their youth may have past at what point did they forget how to fly.
