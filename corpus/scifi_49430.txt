Inspired by NBC's drama 'Heroes' 'Zodiac' is an experimental prequel produced by students at the College of Charleston. It follows a fraternity of empaths led by one immortal Morgan Dread (Michelle Iannantuono) whose paths entwine with a young Isaac Mendez (Alexander Weinberg) due to their abilities. Meanwhile Isaac's friend Roscoe (Alexander Parfenoff) is trying to find his place as he discovers his own empathetic powers and is offered a position as the Zodiac's newest member.
