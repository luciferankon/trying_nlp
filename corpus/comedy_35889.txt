In a city full of dreamers romantics and famewhores some people in LA just want to make rent. Do Better is the story of three roommates trying to figure their lives out at an age where their peers are settling into careers getting married and buying homes. If there's such thing as a third life crisis Zora Nadia and Kiki are living it; working dead-end jobs staying in a crowded apartment and hoping for so much more. While so many are out here doing the most these three are just trying to do better.
