'Jreamwriter's - BENT' is a domestic violence awareness film project based on characters and situations taken from the novel The Rainbow through the Eyes of a Closet Homosexual's Wife by Jreamwriter. The film focuses on the lead character Kyleena who portrays an emotionally psychologically physically and spiritually abused woman of bi-racial beauty ethnicity and poor parental conditioning. The film shows how Kyleena finds herself in a third marriage with a man Alex who has advertised himself to be a straight and holy man of God. Only as the story progresses and Alex's deceptive and debased character becomes more and more transparent Kyleena finds that her spirituality marriage family friendships dreams views of homosexuality and sanity are all put to the test.
