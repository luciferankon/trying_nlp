Sammy & The Salve Boy is an odd-ball comedic webisode series about a pair of odd couple roommates (Mark and Sammy) who receive a slave boy from a craigslist post. Mark is an uptight responsible and hard working car salesmen while Sammy is an unemployed slacker. Our story picks up as Sammy's girlfriend Jane breaks up with him. Sammy now single and heartbroken turns to the craigslist personals to find himself a new girl. While surfing the personals Sammy responds to an ad to "TRAIN MY SLAVE BOY". Minutes later a dominatrix is at his door with the slave boy for Sammy to "train". Sammy finds a new companion in the slave who he has named "Ivan" and uses his company as away to get over his ex-girlfriend.
