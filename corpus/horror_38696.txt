In the midst of the bland suburbia of Australia a dark force is growing. An ancient evil that has terrorized the world for millennia has awoken. The old stories told to frighten naughty children hold a dark truth. Detectives Michael McMorrow and Caitlin Mendez find themselves confronted by a terrifying force of preternatural origin. Don't sleep alone. Don't look under your bed. Don't sin or you may fall victim to Babaroga.
