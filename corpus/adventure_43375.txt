Young Tarcisio finds himself in Malta following suggestions from American businessmen and Vatican officials. On the research for work to finance his living expenses he finds and researches the Fungus of Malta at the Nation's highest point - Top of the Hill. He tries out the drug and finds that it effects his brain's left lobe. Tarcisio begins to see visions of Malta's adventurous past and future understand different languages enlightening his understanding of Malta and its history. He becomes quite a personality on the Island often consulted by major politicians.
