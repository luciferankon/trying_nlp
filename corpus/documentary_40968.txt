Different Abilities is the story of unlikely opportunity. It's the journey of a disgruntled actress and her partner as they learn life lessons taught by Helen Keller the Texas Lions camp and 5 junior high students. The film begins with actress Mollie Milligan and her business partner Brian Fabian searching for a film making opportunity. An unusual opportunity presents itself and the couple begins a two year adventure. First stop a camp for special needs children in Kerrville Texas. This quickly evolves into traveling to the homes of 5 special needs children across the state of Texas. Each child's life reveals the obvious and clarifies the confusing.
