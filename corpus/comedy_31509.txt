Ana Falconi is the general manager of a factory she is a widow with a son. She decides to invent her perfect man to share all of her dreams and desires. A group of shareholders attempts to edge her out of power and to lead the company into fraudulent bankruptcy but she negotiates with an Italian group in order to save it from going bust. When the Italian envoy Piero Bellini arrives in Buenos Aires Ana discovers he is physically identical to the man she made up. At first she is shocked (especially when she meets his wife) but soon the two work together happily and are able to find just what they want and need from each other.
