In "Mother of the Bride" mother and daughter battle over the planning of a wedding. Lindsay the bride-to-be wants a modern unconventional and fun wedding. Joann the mother of the bride wants a by-the-book conservative traditional wedding. Whether it's choosing a dress picking a venue making the guest list or planning the rehearsal dinner.... everything is a battle. And when mom doesn't get her way will she ruin the wedding? Who will win this battle of the wills?
