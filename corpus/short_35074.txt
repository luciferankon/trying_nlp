Two former friends from the same barrio meet as adults and find that they could not be any more different. Rafael a compassionate public defender remembers where he came from and seeks to help the disenfranchised. In contrast Miguel who now likes to be known as "Mike" is a high-profile defense attorney driven by ambition greed and ego. When the elevator they're sharing stalls this Latin "odd couple" is forced to face each other and confront their differences.
