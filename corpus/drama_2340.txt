Chand Miah earns his bread and butter by singing in the street with his daughter Ayesha. Ayesha's mother Ambia wants her daughter to go to school but Ayesha loves to sing with her father. One day some foreigners come to the village where the father-daughter duo sing songs and gets popularity. But Ayesha gets sick few days later and also Chand Miah's popularity diminishes with the invasion of foreign culture.
