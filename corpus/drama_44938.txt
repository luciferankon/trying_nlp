Tradition still abides in the ADK - the Adirondacks region of upstate New York - as nine friends gather for their annual all-night hog roast even as their community crumbles around them. But the arrival of Carter a slick rock 'n' roller from the City will expose the simmering tensions within the group and ignite an age-old feud.
