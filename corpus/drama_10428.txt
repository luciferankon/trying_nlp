Agioupa an unruly orphan girl sleeps in the fields choosing the company of the birds to her fellow villagers. Thus she is often raped by the peasants. A doctor who feels pity for her offers her a place to stay but Agioupa leaves when she finds out that he is in love with another woman. Two peasants who want to rape her get into a fight and one of them is killed. The peasants consider her a witch and lynch her.
