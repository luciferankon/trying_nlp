Dallas(Kibwe Dorsey) is a bar owner who lives a life of street crime. After spending years in prison for killing a man over his then girlfriend and hardened by his time in prison this once nice guy has his life forever changed when the daughter he never knew existed walks into his bar 16 years after being told by her mother that her father is indeed alive. As events begin to unfold Dallas his daughter Laila(Kiara Dorsey) and his entire crew are affected. As Dallas decides to change his life everything begins to go wrong as he faces life and death opposition from a detective an enemy and a his own crew. Dallas has to decide whether to let go of a life of crime or fight for the life he never knew he could have or wanted.
