The story takes place in a hamlet called Ramadurga. A tantrik makes life hell for the villagers by looting their valuables raping womenand killing them. Jeevan (Shivarajkumar)comes to the hamlet to know his 'Janma rahasya' (mystery of his birth). To his shock he comes to know that he was the grandson of Bhajarangi (Shivarajkumar) who fought against the tantrik and saved the villagers from his harassment. The story takes many interesting turns and twists & he defeats the tantric.
