'DOODLE BABY' is a collection of 21 bright and colorful animated cartoons for infants and toddlers! Watch as these delightful cartoons take shape on the screen; Your child will smile from ear to ear as a basic line drawing develops and transforms into full-color animation in front of their very eyes! Featuring various animals shapes and characters including birds a train a little squeaking mouse and a trumpeting baby elephant this DVD is full of adorable cartoons that will 'Doodle' their way into your hearts! Set to playful instrumental background music the cartoons are broken into short segments making it easy for young children to follow and enjoy.
