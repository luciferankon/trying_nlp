After her ten year volatile marriage Kate Saunder's world takes a dramatic turnthat will change her life forever. After the death of her Aunt kate and her eight year old daughter Sophie become very wealthy overnight. Kate knowing she can't trust her husband turns to the only other person in her life her best friend. Alan Saunders's Kate's husband is a shadow of his former self becoming more paranoid by the day he seems to enjoy inflicting mental torture violence and abuse on his wife which has escalated since losing his job with the government as a camera surveilance technician. Kate's story unravels to reveal a web of lies deceipt and even murder after being ground down by her husband for so long will kate find the strength to get through all the madness that is now become her life!
