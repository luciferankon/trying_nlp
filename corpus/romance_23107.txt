Dr. Pundalik a bachelor and an eye specialist also acts as a counselor to his students along with his assistant cum man Friday Prakash. He shares a love hate relationship with Dr. Purva Purandare his childhood friend who coincidentally also has her clinic opposite his. One day at his farmhouse he meets Mr. Bogus who sells him a herb claiming that anyone who uses it in the form of eye drops will fall in love with the first person he sets his eyes on. What follows is a series of romantic mishaps and goof ups packed with superlative performances laced with crackling punch lines making this a laugh a minute romantic musical entertainer.
