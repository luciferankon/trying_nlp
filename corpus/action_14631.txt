Raajveer is an army man whose relationship with Amarjeet his dad hit rock-bottom ever since his brother died. He returns home for his dads 60th birthday celebration hoping to patch-up with him. On the same day his other brother uncovers the evil plot of Rastogi Construction which would leave thousands of families homeless and is kidnapped by Rastogi's goon. Story revolves around following questions. Will Raajveer be able to rescue his brother? Will Amarjeet forgive Raajveer? What is the faith of residents of Shivaji Nagar?
