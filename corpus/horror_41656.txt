Luisa thinks that she lives under the shadow of her parents. She has the felling that all her life has been designed by them. Neither her promising career and support of her boyfriend Ruben seem to remove that idea from her head. Also some horrific events of her childhood are going to return to her life with devastating force bringing more distress and grief... And maybe murder. Luisa's going to have to deal with a situation that completely gets over her. Because how you defend yourself when your worst enemy is... yourself?
