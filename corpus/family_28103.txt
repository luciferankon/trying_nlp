Van Je Familie Moet Je Het Hebben is about Jeroen de Groot and his unusual family. The family is loaded with money and they deeply enjoy the benefits of the rich life except for Jeroen. Jeroen is clearly not into this luxurious lifestyle and this results into serious tensions. In the movie this situation is getting so tense that Jeroen makes a harsh decision: he leaves his family and goes out on an unforgettable journey. During this journey he discovers that his family isn't that bad at all.
