After a catastrophic global event has ravaged the world two survivors emerge from a fallout shelter deep beneath the earth. Radiation permeates the shattered landscape after the bombs were dropped and with nothing but their biohazard suits to prevent an agonizing death and without food or water it becomes a nightmare race against time to locate other survivors. But as their journey progresses it becomes apparent that another threat has emerged into this new world something malevolent that stalks the land when the sun goes down.
