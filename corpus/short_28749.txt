Palermo is a neighborhood of the city of Buenos Aires. With more than 100 years of history the neighborhood was transforming even to acquiring his current configuration. A neighborhood with his particularities and contrasts. The zoo the monuments the limits the architecture everything in constant change; in occasions annulling his own history. In experimental key intensive images document not only the picturesque and daily thing but also situations that hide themselves. The past and the present of a bizarre neighborhood.
