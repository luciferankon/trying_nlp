Kim 32 married part of Hong Kong's Independent Commission Against Corruption is investigating Ho Chi; Kim goes to Shenzhen City hoping to catch Ho bribing customs officials. In Shenzhen Kim parties with his young mistress a call girl named Mandy and wakes to find her dead. The mainland police lock him up without counsel. In transport however he manages to break free and escape to Hong Kong where he's pursued by the implacable Officer Pai who cares not if Kim is guilty or innocent: Pai does his duty. Now a fugitive Kim discovers Mandy had ties to Ho. Kim with his long-suffering wife's help and with Pai closing in seeks proof Ho framed him. Is there any gleam of hope?
