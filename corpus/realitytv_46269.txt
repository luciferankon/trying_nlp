Two hosts introduce the program -a Chinese girl and an American guy who is fluent in Mandarin. Their fun rapport gives the show a lighthearted feel. A segment called "Culture Shock" follows the lives of young people in both the U.S. and in China showing where they live where they go to school what they do for fun etc. In true magazine fashion each program features additional segments focusing on trends advances in technology and current entertainment news. Shot as a co-production in China and the U.S. the series takes a colorful look at the interest of modern day youth on opposite sides of the world.
