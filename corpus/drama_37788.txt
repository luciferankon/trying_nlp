The film features a Girl named Siddhartika who after being molested in her early childhood was put up for adoption. Times have changed and she is currently living with her "RICH PARENTS" and has developed a socially isolated identity. She loves to smoke up Ganja/Hash joints as well as drink alcohol. The film covers her evolution as situations force her to enter a slum only to be challenged by a set of highly optimistic children totally opposite of her cynical character.
