Charts the struggles of ex-prostitute and self styled Mother Theresa to the working girls of Las Vegas Annie Lobert as she builds her "Hookers for Jesus" ministry. With a set that goes beyond the bright lights of the strip to the prisons juvenile detention centers churches and sleazy clubs and with a cast that includes teenage hookers cops pastors and pimps "Hookers for Jesus" takes the audience into a world that they have only glimpsed fictional versions of in movies confounding expectations and stereotypes at each turn.
