You went to a liberal arts college. You majored in English or Sociology or Art History. You have a mountain of debt and no marketable skills. You took your graduation money and moved to New York City with only your dreams of grandeur your parents' HBOgo password and your two best friends. Now what? That's exactly what Sarah Jessica and Parker are trying to figure out. No one quite prepares you for life as a twenty-something. The best you can do is hold on for dear life keep a bottle of wine handy and try to figure out how to adult.
