When she wasn't stuffing her face with lizard meat or playing push the boulder with her big sister Cavegirl was picking the fleas from her Mother. By the time she was 11 Cavegirl realized she was different. Much to the anger of the tribe it was obvious for all to see she was ahead of her peers in the evolutionary scale.
