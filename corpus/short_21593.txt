After a disastrous road trip planned in haste and fueled by hopeless love jaded and recently separated exes Ray and Christine stop off at a truck stop diner in search of directions for the quickest way home. Against the warnings made by local law enforcement about taking any road but the one they seek they decide to ignore this "small town fodder" and test their fate. Once on it this God-forsaken path will lead them to discover more than they ever bargained for about themselves each other and what it means to love eternally when even death is but a passing mile marker on a road that never ends...
