Plagued with constant warfare disease and political social and religious turmoil the world as we know it ended in 2019. Many turned to cannibalism to survive in the world dying off due to famine. One man treks the wastelands to track down the group of cannibals who took everything from him. With dangers lurking at every turn his character and will to survive is tested beyond limits.
