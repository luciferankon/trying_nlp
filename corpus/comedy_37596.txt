This is a sitcom about a cat named Swaggapuss and his struggles in being it the real world. Swaggapuss is strongly influenced by his friend Chips who he gets into crazy adventures with. Swaggapuss gets kicked out of the house after his mother Mommapuss finally has had her last straw with his laziness. Swaggapuss convinces his cousin R.L. to let him move in with him. R.L. agrees under one condition that Swaggapuss keeps a job. The only issue is his opposition Cleroy who is always in competition with Swaggapuss for his spot. This show contains a mix of live action mixed with candid moments.
