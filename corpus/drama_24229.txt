In south of Iran Farough who has a garage is not letting Sohrab to has his own job. They fight each other and in the fight Farough dies of an heart attack. His brothers are going after Sohrab to take revenge. Sohrab flees with his truck to a cafe. There he meets a pregnant woman Fatemeh and decides to take her to the city. The cafe's owner boy too comes with him but in the way their troubles begin.
