With increasing debate about education parents are looking for a better option than outdated curriculum and standardized testing. "Montessori Rising" shines a light on how Montessori education works to create intellectually curious and independent problem solvers. The film includes comments from 21st century education experts including Tony Wagner and Sir Ken Robinson as it examines how Montessori education prepares children to succeed in our fast paced ever-evolving 21st Century economy.
