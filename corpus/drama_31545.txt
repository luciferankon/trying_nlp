On the coast of a foreign land Sophia and her son wander their only property a red suitcase and only obstruction to find a way to eat and a place to sleep. But who are they? Are they fleeing from a distant memory or are they themselves a memory from years ago in someone else's mind? What happens when past visions and future facts collide? Inspired by a real entry in a forgotten diary Paragraph treads the delicate line between storytelling and memory.
