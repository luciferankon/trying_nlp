Feeling betrayed by his mentor and boss Lucas Ogtel (Tennyson Harris) seeks to shed his dark past when he meets and falls for the captivating and ambitious Erika (Melan Perez). But his former boss Dez (Jeremy C. Y. Butler) is determined to keep Lucas under his control and he quickly discovers that Dez's power over him is far-reaching and the fight to free himself of Dez's corruption will test the very depths of Lucas's soul.
