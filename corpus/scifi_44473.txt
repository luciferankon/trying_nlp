In 2021 Earth has colonised Mars and has discovered basic extra-terrestrial life on Neptune. However little do Earth's people know that they too have been discovered by an advanced highly intelligent life form. When Earth is attacked it's militaries and governments taken by surprise defences broken communications lost countries stand alone against these invaders. In Britain Prime Minister Elsworth finds himself in a situation he could never have prepared for and now he must lead his country in its greatest hour of need. Elsewhere Dr Landvik a leading scientist of the National Space Observation Centre (NSOC) tries to find out everything he can about the aliens and see if a form of communication can be re-established. Meanwhile four friends Alice Scott Stephanie and Ryan desperately try to escape the attack on their home city but with Earth being overrun chances of survival are decreasing every second.
