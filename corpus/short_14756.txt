12 year-old Charlie lives in a small town in the mid-west. In addition to his misunderstood autism Charlie's development has been inhibited by his obsessive and coddling mother. Anxiety-ridden and unable to sleep Charlie resorts to following the advice of a hippie spiritual healer who urges him to eliminate the biggest obstacle in his life. Little to the healer's knowledge he has just planted the seed in Charlie's mind that will blossom into tragedy.
