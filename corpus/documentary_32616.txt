Daniel Lanois was born in Hull Quebec. Sept. 19 1951. His family moved to Hamilton Ont. in 1963. In 1974 he and his brother Bob built and operated the Grant Avenue Studio where he produced records by acts such as Martha and the Muffins Parachute Club Raffi and Ian Tyson. In 1989 he released his first solo album "Acadie". He also produced "Oh Mercy" by Bob Dylan and "Yellow Moon" by The Neville Bros. during the same year.
