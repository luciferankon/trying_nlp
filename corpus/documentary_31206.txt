June 4 1947 Casanova Staffora a rural village in Pavia Province. We are in Italy 2 years after WWII and the young (7 years old) Angela Volpini met a Lady while she was up on the hill with her friends. The beautiful Lady tell to Angela "I coming to teach you the way to be happy in this World" After that day she become the protagonist of extraordinary mystical experience which took place from 1947 to 1956. The documentary describe as a child was catapulted to the headlines and the attention of multitudes of people and faithful and like the World could be better thanks this experience of freedom happiness and creativity that is shared in the community of Nova Cana in the same rural village after almost 70 years.
