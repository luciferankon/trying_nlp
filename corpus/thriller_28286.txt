In the streets of wasteland deep in LA a 10 year cold case involving the serial slaying of 33 prostitutes spanning 3 weeks in 1983 resurfaces as bodies begin to pile with the same MO as at large "skid row slayer". Detective Elizabeth (Liz) Abson partners with Detective Dexter (Dex) Abby to chase the case back down the rabbit hole. With contrasting personalities and clashing investigative methodologies this love hate relationship filled with dark similarities seeps with sexual tension and a chemistry almost too perfect or full of toxicity.
