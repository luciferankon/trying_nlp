Interior decorator Stephanie Dorsey (Becky Mullen) and contractor Adam (Bobby Johnston) seem to be the perfect couple living in the perfect neighborhood. Beneath the face their marriage is crumbling when Adam enters into an affair. Tempted with wandering eyes of her own Stephanie has another -- life-threatening -- problem to deal with: one by one her friends fall victim to a brutal attacker and she's convinced she's next on his hit list.
