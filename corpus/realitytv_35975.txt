Lynda and Jools Topp continue their culinary journey around New Zealand meeting passionate food producers home cooks and lovers of life. In this factual series with heart the Topp Twins celebrate inspiring Kiwis who are living their dream and bring us tales from the countryside through the universal pleasure of food and love. The Topps' gift for spontaneity and wit combined with a deep love for the land and its people delivers emotionally satisfying stories that are life-affirming humorous and inspiring.
