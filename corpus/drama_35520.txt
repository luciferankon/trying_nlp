Klo is a young vivacious fourteen year old girl who is about to turn fifteen.+++ She feels that she already has all of the responsibilities of an adult but she feels one thing is missing. After all she already takes care of her father she cleans the house she cooks irons and has read the books of the greatest authors in the Latin world.+++As the most significant birthday of a Latina approaches she feels she is ready to take that next huge step towards adulthood; lose her virginity.+++ Antes De Las Once is a story about three adolescents as they address the universal challenges of growing up in a time where drugs sexual promiscuity delinquency and suicide seem to run rampant.+++Parents so caught up in their own day to day lives are unaware of the challenges their kids are going through and often neglect their children resulting in dangerous life altering situations for everyone.
