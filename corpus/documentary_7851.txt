The film reveals the everyday moments of the lives of ordinary people living in Moscow and the Moscow region. The characters at first glance are not connected. However in reality they are closely intertwined storylines - happiness and the meaning of life faith and friendship money and work humanity and sensuality. The fleeting course of life as the water flows washing the stones in the river that passes through each of the characters. Despite the nervousness and greed of modern life the picture is permeated with optimism kindness altruism and compassion.
