A story of a newlywed couple named Neecy and Terrell. Two months before the wedding Neecy was raped by a man that was HIV positive. After the wedding Terrell and Neecy could not consummate their marriage as they wait for test results for Neecy. In the midst of waiting Terrell finds out that being married is tough but marriage with no sex from his wife could be worse. What will he do? Waiting becomes a weight that may be too much to bear.
