Five bondage vignettes: in the first a superheroine and a pretty wannabe are victimized by the school's sinister professor; in the second a pretty blonde gets more excitement than she wants when she begs her boyfriend to bind her to a chair; in the third a innocent (and lovely) brunette becomes entangled in the dangerous world of espionage; in the fourth a boss demands to know why his ace advertising exec always includes bondage in her artwork; and in the fifth a beautiful spy and her innocent roommate are captured by an enemy agent.
