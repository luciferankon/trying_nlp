A sometimes nostalgic sometimes shocking re-examination of what really happened in the Reagan era the film shows how everything from classic TV show's like "Family Ties" to the political rhetoric of Oliver North still frame the way we think about major issues today. "Back to our Future" argues that today's events have not only been shaped by the 1980's but in many ways mirror the decade. With political instability in the Middle East volatile gas prices and cultural wars raging "Back to our Future" proves why as a country sometimes we must look back in order to move forward.
