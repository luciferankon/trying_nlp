During her childhood painting allowed Skye to escape from her world in the small rain forest town. After leaving school she journeys to Brisbane Queensland to attend university to further her love of art. Her artistic talent grows and Skye attempts to sell her paintings through a gallery. An art dealer continues to sell her paintings to pressure Skye from revealing his sexual advances. She leaves Australia and heads to Paris France where she falls in love with another artist; who soon becomes jealous of her success.
