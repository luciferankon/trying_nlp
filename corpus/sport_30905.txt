What began as a single publication is now an outdoors multimedia company serving fishing and hunting enthusiasts in the upper Midwest with information on both local and regional waters and woods. The MidWest Outdoors family includes; MidWest Outdoors Magazine MidWest Outdoors TV MidWest Outdoors On-Line Fishing Facts Magazine as well as a variety of special sections and sports show programs. Midwest Outdoors TV first aired in 1985 on WCIU in Chicago and has since grown to include 30+ local regional and nationwide channels.
