METRO follows the lives of 6 young women in NYC as they strive to succeed in their respective fields. Tina is a disenchanted model who is having an identity crisis she relies on Amber a stylist whom she has just met. Chikako is Japanese and infatuated with Tina. Anke is a German girl who gets conned through the internet and has no where to stay. She is taken in by Lila who wants to use her.
