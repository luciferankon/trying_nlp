Suzie (Fortunate Tumukunde) is a newly wedded house wife though still youthful she faces a hard life when her sister in law Angie (Sharon Mutetsi) is brought into her home sick. She suffers from paralysis but soon her illness worsens and goes on to torture her in-law both physically and psychologically. Despite all this Suzie's husband Andrew (Rodeny Dhikusooka) is not at all bothered and given the fact that he is away most of the time he finds it hard to believe. Now the situation gets even more deadly and dangerous a life is lost then another jeopardized and blame is cast onto someone.
