In the wake of the 2016 Presidential Campaign and the Women's March a new wave of female empowerment has spread across America striving to not only achieve equality for women but also for men minority groups the physically and mentally disabled the LGBT community and more. However throughout history there has been a long-standing disparity within the movement. 'She Persisted' explores for the mainstream feminist movement has often overlooked minority voices in the fear of class and race issues dominating gender issues and adopting an Intersectional approach would help bridge those gaps.
