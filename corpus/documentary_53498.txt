Featuring Maylene and the Sons of Disaster Bury Your Dead Misery Signals Haste the Day and many more. - This film is an eye opening ear crushing experience. It includes 22 incredible live performances by artists such as Drop Dead Gorgeous Thumbscrew and August Burns Red. It features high quality audio and video hilarious interviews tons of music videos and a few hidden gems. Shot mostly in the Atlanta area these packed shows filmed from every angle remind you why nothing compares to a live show. These videos are filled with incredible breakdowns powerful blastbeats and emotional choruses that are executed with incredible timing and accented with original effects that will leave you begging for more.
