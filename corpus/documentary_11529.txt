UNIQUE PERCEPTIONS is an inspiring film series about Artists who redefine the term 'Disability'. This visually stunning docudrama follows some of the world's greatest artists that are either mentally or physically disabled. The objective of this unique film series is to explore the inner world of these extraordinary individuals. Learning about their art and approach towards the reality of life. This is an effort to experience their passion for art and witness their triumph through many challenges. The documentary ends with a conclusion that we are all a great force of energy..a spark...a cell...a spirit or just a star in the Universe... ...we are beyond just a physical being and we are all connected.
