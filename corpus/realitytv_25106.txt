Welcome to "POLKA-holics!" - a unique and hilarious docu-series about two small town Polish families - the Socha's and the Lasecki's - and the crazy and colorful polka-centric world they live and play in. The show's setting takes place in Green Bay WI where everyone lives and breathes boohya bratwursts and beer. It's a magical land where trips to Lambeau Field are like visiting the Emerald City the yellow brick road is made of golden cheese and the state dance is the Polka. There's no place like home don't 'cha know.
