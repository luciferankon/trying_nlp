Lili Pascal Fabian Amina and Sultan. Five teenagers from Munich Germany. Between still being a kid and coming of age they struggle for their place in society. At IMAL the International Munich Art Lab which offers professional qualification to young adults from different social and cultural background through artistic projects the five teens have the opportunity to develop and stage their own musical together with 20 like-minded people. All of them dream of a life on stage. Will their dream come true? Or will every-day life get in the way?
