Few know the story of how the U.S. Navy SEALs (Sea Air and Land) became the renowned warriors of today. Without them much of world history would have been written differently from the beaches of Normandy to the Pacific theater Korea Vietnam Iraq and Afghanistan. This Documentary explains the story of the U.S. Navy SEALs and how they came to be from their Frogman and UDT (Underwater Demolition Teams) ancestors to their making in 1962 and to present day. Former Navy SEALs and UDTs are interviewed and third stories are told from all eras of Naval Special Warfare.
