Amy is a model and admitted diva enjoying the good life in Tampa Florida. While she loves her family she's embarrassed by their redneck ways - not to mention traumatized that she grew up eating her own pets! When Amy realizes by denying her roots she's hurting the people she cares about most she nervously agrees to a reunion with her hardcore hunting mud wrestling prank obsessed relatives. Can the family convince Amy to embrace her past and confront her fears? Regardless of the guns fireworks and pyrotechnics this is undoubtedly the most explosive episode of "Redneck Rehab" yet!
