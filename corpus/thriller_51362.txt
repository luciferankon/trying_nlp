Silvia is a young and innocent psychiatrist that is about to celebrate her bachelorette party. That night she gets abused by a stranger on a bathroom. From that day on a blue skin patch starts growing on her neck and she finds out that she's been infected with a fungus that only grows on corpses. Trapped on a net full of secrets Silvia will do her best to find her mysterious attacker and to uncover the truth behind her disease all of this while trying not to lose the trust of her boyfriend.
