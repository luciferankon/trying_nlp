Unable to define themselves as real or believable prototypes of humanity the 15 characters trapped in this Plato's cavern can only repeat actions actions without a specific sense or control of their behavior. In this moment in history the Subject looses direction subjugated by his own creations. What is the cultural product they will leave to the next generation of survivors? Who are these people? What are they doing? Will they resist to the temptation to become images themselves?
