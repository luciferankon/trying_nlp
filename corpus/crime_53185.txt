THE 4TH LEVEL- Race Matheson possesses such a high level of consciousness that he is suddenly catapulted into two separate realities: a writer with a loving wife and son in one reality and a ruthless drug dealer in the other. Unable to control the shifts Race's writer self has to figure out why this is happening to him and stop it before the hit on his drug dealer self can be carried out causing an inter-dimensional reaction that will kill them both.
