1984 Washington State Cheryl Barnett a mother of three is force to move her kids in with her parents after an ugly divorce. In the beginning the family have a difficult time fitting in around the 'Podunk' town of Shelton. After several months the kids settle in making new friends but soon stumble across the towns well kept secret mainly considered Indian folklore. The Barnett family will eventually be forced to do anything and everything to keep their discovery under wraps from the rest of the world but not without the help of some unlikely heroes.
