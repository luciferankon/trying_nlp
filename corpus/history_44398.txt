Born in to a poor Irish family in rural Australia Les Darcy soon rises to be the greatest boxer in the world. Yet once the First World War exploded the man that the whole of Australia and the United States had become besotted by finds himself torn torn between the duty he felt he owes his family and the duty many felt they owed their country... Les decides to continue fighting for his family lifting them out of poverty but heavy consequences follow for this beloved Blacksmith Boxer
