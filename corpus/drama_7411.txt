Life becomes a distant blur in a matter of seconds... When Gina finds herself divorced and alone at the age of 40 her world starts to crumble. She begins barreling down a self-destructive path one bad choice at a time. On the brink of madness her only hope is to let go of her past and learn one of life's cruelest yet greatest lessons: forgiveness. Only then will she realize the "happily ever after" she has been searching for has been inside of her all along. With the support and coaxing of her life-long confidant Makenzie and the wisdom of a deaf artist Tulio she initiates the steps to getting her life back on track. In the aftermath of a tragedy she is forced to make one final choice; her greatest of all. Silent Choices takes us on an emotional roller coaster of tragedy and triumph of loss and hope of bondage and freedom.
