SCENE ONE: A man who lost his fiancée and was in depression found a tape of his fiancée from his fiancée's friend. The words in the tape are encouraging and the man realized that he should face up his life bravely as his fiancée did. SCENE TWO: A girl received a tape that contained her dead friend's words. The words in the tape were encouraging except the last part which contained hysteria and hopelessness. Having observed the depressed mode of her friend's fiancé she erased the last part of the tape and gave it to him.
