Hank Williams is the undisputed "King of Country Music." Singer...Songwriter...Legend...Hank Williams started a tradition which is still being followed today. This program traces Hank Williams' incredible life story through rare film clips and revealing interviews with his friends and fellow performers such as Roy Acuff Minnie Pearl and Chet Atkins. Included are performances of many of Hank's greatest songs by today's top country music recording artists who also tell how Hank Williams inspired their career. Without a doubt there never was or ever will be another country star of such magnitude and influence as Hank Williams. Featuring Hank Williams Jr. Willie Nelson Randy Travis Waylon Jennings Kris Kristofferson Emmylou Harris Ricky Skaggs Dwight Yoakum and many others!
