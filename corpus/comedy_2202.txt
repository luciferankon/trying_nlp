The Virgin Intervention is a web based comedy series that follows Valerie Hudson a 25-year-old woman living in Sydney. She has great friends a caring family and her career trajectory as a marketing manager is going swimmingly. Valerie is just where she wants to be. Except for the fact that she's still a virgin. When her well-kept secret is outed during a drinking game her best friends are mortified and decide to host an intervention. Valerie insists it isn't a big deal but to everyone else it's a hugely embarrassing problem that has to be fixed. Their goal: to convince Valerie it's time to have sex. She's not religious or waiting until marriage so why is she waiting?
