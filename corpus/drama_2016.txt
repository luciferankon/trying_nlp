Joey and Alice have hardly enough to keep their heads above water. Alice's wealthy parents disapproved of their daughter marrying Joey but after a while they had to admit that together with Emily their 6 year old little girl they were a happy family. After Alice's sudden dead Joey takes care of Emily the best he can but doesn't manage too well. After losing his job he reluctantly agrees with Emily going to live with her grand parents. But Emily isn't happy. When Joey finally realizes this he fights back... When an aneurism kills Alice Joey is left to look after their daughter Emily on his own. Her maternal grandparents don't feel he is capable and fight to get custody.
