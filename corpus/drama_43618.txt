Follows a French Canadian woman going to jail for 7 years for pushing her father down the stairs. As the story unfolds we get to see how this all came about and the reasons behind it. The story follows the woman in jail as well as her family on the outside. The main character is a Quebecois woman who is sentenced to seven years in prison for the attempted murder of her father. The series shows her involvement with her family and a special friend and their interactions with the prison staff. It also develops stories of some of her fellow prisoners and shows some of the relationships between prisoners and between prisoners guards and other prison staff.
