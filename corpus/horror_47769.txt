The award-winning "Dark Realm" is the unique combination of Ron Fitzgerald's dark theatrical Gothic Illusion performances artistically blended with a Horror film narrative. It tells the story of a Master Illusionist inspired by the Occult who may actually be in league with evil forces to assure his success. His deadly secret may also bring destruction for him his lover Raven and everyone else in his macabre show while his manager Price pushes him t continue performing as illusion and reality collide.
