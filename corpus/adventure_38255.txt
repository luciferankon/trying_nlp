Gonul is a famous singer her boss Zekeriya is after her money. He gets an assassin Mehmet. Mehmet accepts the task only to get the money for the operation of Uncle Riza's grandchild. He introduces himself alias Ertugrul and flirts with Gonul. They fall in love with each other. As Gonul wants him to give all her money Mehmet confesses everything. Zekeriya is still after the money he abducts Mehmet. Gonul prepares the ransom money. Mehmet escapes he saves Gonul from being killed but he cannot save Uncle Riza. The police catches Zekeriya. Mehmet and Gonul start a happy new life.
