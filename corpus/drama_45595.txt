Jonathan is a Pop Culture Nerd who has a heart of gold but has difficulty relating to women to combat this he seeks services with prostitutes whether it's in their homes or in legalised brothels. Through a series of events he meets Louise. A drug addicted Escort who is heavily influenced by Celebrity Culture. As the two social outcasts are drawn together for all the wrong reasons Jonathan makes it his mission to help Louise understand the error of her ways and to provide her with the kind of love and affection that has been missing for most of her life.
