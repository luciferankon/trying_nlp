Wine and food and many other joys that define the "good life." The Best of Wine and Food was a 30-minute television series that was a visual journey through not only the world of wine and food but of the other joys that define the good life. Each of the 26 episodes provided tips on the finer things in life and includes ways to entertain taste buds traveling tips through the various wine country sides the artistry of well-known chefs as well as a behind the scenes views of great restaurants and restaurateurs.
