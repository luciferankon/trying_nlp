The Real Cannabis Culture is a short documentary featuring some of the industries most credible influencers. Centered around the Emerald Cup the largest outdoor cannabis competition in the world we take a deeper look into the medicinal and advocacy aspects of the culture. Large artists in the industry such as Collie Buddz and Beats Antique share their perspectives on cannabis and their positions on legalization. Dr Bonni Goldstein specialized cannabis physician and Medical Director of Canna-Centers explains the proven benefits of medicinal marijuana. While giving context to the factual attributes of the plant we also tap into the emotive value of the marijuana movement from the perspectives of those within the community.
