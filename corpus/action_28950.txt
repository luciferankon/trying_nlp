Out of control follows actress and martial-arts guru Lucy Lin (Cecilia Cheung) as her visit to a film festival in Berlin is marred by the unwanted appearance of her ex-fiance Interpol agent Tom Young (T.O.P.). Young is hot on the trail of renowned cyber-criminal Bennet Kayser (Michael Trevino) who's planning a deadly attack on the day of Lin's premiere. As Kayser unleashes a virus that allows him complete control over the fleet of stretch-limousines heading to the red carpet Lucy finds herself at the villain's mercy - her hopes of survival pinned on the man who left her at the altar all those years ago.
