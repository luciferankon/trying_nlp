The Vita 4sm is a Rock N'Roll Band made up of fruits and vegetables. The Vita 4sm Pepper CJ Eggy Plant and Broc want your kids to be healthy strong and active! Through colorful lively entertainment and jamm'n tunes the Vita 4sm will get your kids up out and moving. The Vita 4sm Healthy Lifestyle for Kids Program is designed to educate children and families on the importance of a healthy lifestyle which is essential in obtaining optimal health. The goal is to help combat our nation's obesity epidemic in children.
