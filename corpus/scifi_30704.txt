Doctor Blo has traveled to earth on a microwave beam. Only something has gone horribly wrong with the new transport technology. Due to a space warp occurring simultaneously with a microwave accident a transformation of existence between two life forms replaces an earthling with Doctor Blo. The Baytaz hostile aliens from another dimension have tracked Doctor Blo from his last physical form in order to steal this new technology and use it for their own diabolical scheme. They pounce on him with all their robotic fury and he is taken prisoner. On the other end of the universe a hapless space traveler is engulfed in events beyond comprehension. Pedro is just a businessman it is up to Veronica whose own planet Zitor is in peril to convince Pedro of his destiny. Only Pedro and Veronica two life forms against and army of robots can save Doctor Blo and with him planet earth!
