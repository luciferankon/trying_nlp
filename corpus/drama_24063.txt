Junichi is a scout recruiting girls for work in hostess bars. He's been at the job for seven years but he's too softhearted to be a real success. Nanami who is a music teacher at a junior high school wishes that something would happen to change the course of her ordinary life. Junichi and Nanami have a disastrous first meeting. By the fourth time they bump into each other Nanami begins to see Junichi as the man who just might save her from a mediocre life. Although it goes against everything that her upbringing has taught her Nanami decides to give him a chance.
