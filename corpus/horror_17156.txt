A direct follow up to Rotten to the Flesh: Trapped in a jail cell Derek begins to negotiate with Hank to figure out why he would stoop so low. Not giving Derek the answers Hank leaves and goes to his office to find out that his right hand vampire Neil has been having an affair with his sister. Concerned for her husband Beth has a terrible feeling that she will never see Derek ever again. Planning a rescue mission their spy tells them that Derek is in prison. Bringing Derek to his office Hank finally tells the human why he's undermining the province and the government. Now knowing the truth Derek laughs for he too has a deadly secret.
