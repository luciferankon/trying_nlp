Time Drop is a story about a father who risks his life with a controversial drop back in time to save his daughter from becoming a victim of a serial killer. When heroic fireman James McCormick's daughter is murdered by the crazed serial killer Grundy Knox he teams up with his eccentric scientist friend Nick to drop back in time to save her life. When he goes back too far and saves a previous victims life it changes the course of time and becomes a race against time itself to stop the killer and keep his daughter alive.
