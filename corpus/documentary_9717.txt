Escaping the heat of a northern summer Simon and his companions head across the globe to the beautifully rugged and pristine back country slopes of New Zealand. There they encounter typical kiwi hospitality adrenaline-pumping heli-rides ski plane landings onto precarious mountain tops and some of the most spectacular and humbling mountain scenery they have had the privilege to experience. These five breathtaking episodes guarantee to get your heart rate going and will inspire you to pack your bags for a remarkable adventure down under.
