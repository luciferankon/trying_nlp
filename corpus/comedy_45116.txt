Best friends share everything but can they share a man? Anita and Lisa have been best friends since 5th grade. So when Lisa suffers a lost Anita welcomes her her weed smoking cousin and crazy brother Max into her home. But when Lisa finds out Anita is dating someone from her recent past it causes all kinds of baby daddy hysterity throughout the neighborhood . Is it sloppy seconds or true love? Find out in Prince E. Mayes-Sullivan's hysterical new stage play musical "My Best Friend's Man"
