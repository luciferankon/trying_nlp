Pop Channel is a satire program information and intellectual cynicism which airs every Saturday on Top Channel. The structure of Pop Channel refers to a newsroom-of a genuine informative television which reported the news in international national and local according to a classical division between sections of political economic and social news edition. Pop Channel reports mainly for events related to actuality which may be real or fictitious. The characters are also included in the report real or fictional depending on context information. The program parodies typical situation of a direct connection informational edition breaking news and special correspondence.
