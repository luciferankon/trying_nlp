This documentary treats movie fans to a behind-the-scenes look at the making of the 1978 version of The Prince and the Pauper (also known as Crossed Swords) a film adaptation of Mark Twain's classic novel about child of royalty and a child of poverty who are forced by circumstance to trade places. Includes interviews with the cast and crew who share some of their experiences with working on the movie and discuss some of the efforts that it required.
