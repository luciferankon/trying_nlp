The Mr. Dependable Aaditya Shah who loves his tuxedo as much as his piano in his seemingly perfect world is not even aware that he is looking for his imperfect companion. The adventure sports lover and early morning hater RJ Antara is the most loved voice of the city. This mad hatter has no idea about the insane adventure coming her way. The kitli-ni-cha lover and Mr. jo-baka genius Sagar has fallen in love 23 times or at least he thinks so. But this time it's true love and he's not ready for it. So what happens when the imperfect the mad and the genius come together?
