In this action packed futuristic drama Jimmy and Sam are forced to throw blows with hit-men hired by renowned mastermind criminal and stepsister Araceli. Left with no other alternative but to run for their lives Jimmy and Sam learn their fate comes at the high price of love and death. Despite their hatred for each other the cosmos have something else planned for this hard-hitting couple whose futures are uncertain from a disease that is about to wipe out mankind. "Save the world if you can but make sure you save yourselves" is the couple's cut-throat mentality.
