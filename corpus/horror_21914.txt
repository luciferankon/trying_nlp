Six friends go out to the middle of nowhere on a camping trip they just want to have one last bash before their summer break is over and they have to return to their separate schools. Their worst nightmare's come to life though when two psychotic brothers enter their camp wanting nothing more than to have the time of their lives as they brutally assault and torment them all one by one. The only hope for them is to hide as long as they can and pray they make it through the night.
