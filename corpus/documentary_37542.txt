Banal scenes from the preparations for and during the course of a freetechno party regularly alternate with a frenetic sequences of events from Argentine history to produce a hypnotic and frightening trip. On the one hand is the aesthetic of the music video and its ability to capture the state of entropy across time by using audiovisual repetition and playing with pacing color and brightness. On the other hand is an effect-free record of real life specifically the freetechno subculture whose non-aggressive nature contrasts with the destruction and chaos that rule the surrounding world.
