10 great punk rock classics presented karaoke video style... with a punk rock twist. 2 versions of each song/video one with guest star vocalists and the other with no vocals for your karaoke pleasure. Also included is a live performance from the band "Punk Rock Karaoke" featuring Greg Hetson (Bad Religion/Circle Jerks) Eric Melvin (NOFX) Derek O'Brien (Social Distortion/Agent Orange) & Steve Soto (Adolescents/Agent Orange) with guests from the audience on lead vocals.
