What happens when a man who has it all becomes entangled in a web of lies deceit and infidelity? Michael Davenport's life is great until his wife becomes withdrawn and isolated taking with her their intimacy. Michael's resolve is tested one day when he meets Denise James a beautiful woman who is all too willing to give him the passion he is missing at home. However Michael will soon learn that all it takes is for one mistake to turn his perfect world upside down. Brought together by lust their lives will be forever changed by one dark deadly secret that will bind them all together forever.
