Animation and live action turn the Book of Genesis into a learning adventure for your children! In ONCE UPON THE BEGINNING Livingstone the Lion tells the story of an ancient book filled with true stories of its own such as creation and the flood. When Lockwood wanders from his flock his guardian sheepdog Dublin is worried. Fortunately the lamb runs into Livingstone a wise lion who tells Lockwood about a mysterious and ancient book. The lamb hears how two children named Alex and Tiffy discover the book and learn about the creation of the world a great flood a city named Babel and...but then the book stops. So begins this exciting ongoing adventure that mixes animation and live action to draw kids into the world of the Bible.
