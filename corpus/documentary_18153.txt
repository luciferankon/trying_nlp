The 14 Days Project is an evolving multi-location series of events that combine stirring visual art through subject selection portrait stills multiple narratives and richly textured video and sound material across geographical locations. Collaborative works by rising artists The project also known as the Face of Freedom involves an interplay among three environments: stills film and narrative where borders are permeated new relationships developed and the expressive power of the blended mediums made visible and cohesive. The content focuses on people foremost places and most importantly the human spirit.
