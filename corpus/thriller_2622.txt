On February 14th 2007 a website titled "MangledAngels.com" launched that showed a diary of a serial killer named "The Prophet" or "Angel Maker" along with pictures and video of beaten tortured and murdered women. Despite the FBI's cyber crime division's attempts to locate and shut down the site it stayed active till January 28th 2008 when it finally went dark without warning. During that year of terror 12 women including an FBI Cyber Agent were beaten tortured and murdered. ~It is unknown what happened to the killer.
