Portail Sciences a popular science show is shown on the Télévision Rive-Sud channel (TVRS channel 9 Vidéotron). In Portail Sciences science become accessible to everyone! At each episode Myriam Kessiby explore a theme in different angles: meeting with quebecois researchers simplification of recent studies and the craziness of Dr. Nad; everything is put on to discovery. Information always recent and credible presented with humor and no pretension. Come with us into the... Portal!
