In 6 episodes of 26 minutes each this documentary film approaches fashion education through the portrait of four students over the course of their final year at major French design schools. The narrative principle throughout this crucial final year of school is to juxtapose the paths and the choices of each students on the cusp of his or her professional life. The film looks at the wide variety of careers for which they are destined simultaneously providing a whole new perspective on the vast panorama of Ťfashionť and the garment industry.
