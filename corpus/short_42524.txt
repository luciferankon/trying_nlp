'Ice Factor' is an ice hockey program established in 2005 to assist schools in their efforts to keep 'at risk' students from leaving education prematurely. Designed to help all manner of people within low socio-economic school districts it has proven highly valuable to young Indigenous Australians who are faced with additional challenges in their youth. Providing just a small glimpse into some of these young adults lives this short documentary is a tribute to these amazing individuals the sport of ice hockey and the Ice Factor program.
