Rosehill Dwelling follows 5 teenagers into a vacant home said to have once been owned by a family that had fallen victim to a local murder-suicide 8 years prior. Taking place on Halloween of 1998 Kai Seiko Jo Guy and Dude find more than they bargained for when they break into the town's abandoned home. As the night goes on the truth behind the murder-suicide unravels and the teens find that the abandoned home may not be so abandoned after all.
