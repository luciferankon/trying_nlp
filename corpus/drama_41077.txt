When a nine year old boy's obsession to become invisible gets out of hand his family decides to intervene. Although it seems to solve the immediate problem their tactic pushes the boy further into a world of his imagination. Adding to his confusion is the arrival of his teenage cousin who seems to have a secret of her own. It is an eventful summer vacation where each one's fantasy world would collide with reality and leave them scarred. 'The Summer of Miracles' is an experiment in subtlety trying to create realistic drama by limiting exposition and emotional manipulation.
