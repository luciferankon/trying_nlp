Rick and Roddy are twin brothers and in the early 90's were two of the biggest males strippers in the world. Fast forward 20 years and you will find 2 men who have pissed away their fortunes fame and now must find a way to reclaim the spotlight except this time they are forced to do it 'plus size'. A Mockumentary about a twin brother male stripper duo whose popularity in the early 90's has waned. Today they're trying to make a comeback but now they're a bit on the large side. After a falling out with Roddy will Rick be able to reach for his dreams and succeed? Maybe with a little help from some new friends?
