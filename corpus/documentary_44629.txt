What factors lead to the outmigration of young talent from North Dakota and what will entice more of our best and brightest to stay? Prairie Public Television's new locally produced documentary Building Our Future examines the issues and presents options for stemming the tide. Narrator writer and producer Matt Olien traveles across North Dakota to speak with young people political leaders and native North Dakotans who have returned to the state and he visits communities that are successfully dealing with the the program of outmigration.
