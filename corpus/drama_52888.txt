Red Notice is a Film Annex original web series inspired by Dante Alighieri's timeless classic The Inferno. Though not a literal adaptation Red Notice explores the relationship between Dante and Virgil in a modern setting and creates a world of sin in the heart of Gotham. Throughout the series our two main characters Margot and Vicky see the dark side of New York City that only night crawlers and adventure seekers get to see. After all New York City is closest to the home of the seven deadly sins!
