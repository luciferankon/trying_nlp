Ms. Cableknit is determined to get her students into gear and "make that audience roar with excitement!" However she meets her demise when a mysterious and unsuspecting villain sabotages the show! Meanwhile Prissy and her "Momager" Dina-Lynn are determined to make Prissy an over-night star only to have a bit-part actress steal the spotlight! Sarah is a film actress preparing in her dressing room for the opening night of a stage play. Constantly being interrupted and having to deflect questions about her personal life we see how she uses the very strife she tries to conceal to get into character.
