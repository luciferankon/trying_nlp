Combining the instrumental influence of Ray Brown and Charles Mingus and the vocal styling of Sarah Vaughan and Ella Fitzgerald jazz bassist and vocalist Kristin Korb has created a sound all her own. This concert was filmed in early 2005 at Vienna's famous jazz club Porgy Bess and features a truly magically performance by Korb. The film also includes insightful artist interviews interwoven with the live footage making it the complete package for Korb fans worldwide.
