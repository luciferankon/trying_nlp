Marijana's life revolves around her family whether she likes it or not. They live on top of one another in a tiny apartment driving one another crazy. Then her controlling father has a stroke and is left completely bedridden and Marijana takes his place as head of the clan. Soon she is working two jobs to keep everything afloat while her mother and disabled brother do their best to scupper the ship. Driven to the edge Marijana finds comfort in seedy sex with random strangers; and this taste of freedom leaves her wanting more. But now that she has finally found freedom what's she meant to do with it? When Marijana's dominant father falls seriously ill she takes over his role as the head of the family taking care of her irresponsible mother and mentally disabled older brother. The new power constellation allows her to explore her sexuality and her inner strength and gives her a taste of freedom.
