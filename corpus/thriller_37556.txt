A man suffering from Insomnia gets introduced to pills that allow him to cultivate and experience lucid dreams that could continue with interruptions too. With each real-life character that has caused an impact in him getting a parallel in his dream the distinction between reality & dream thins out and that consumes him. The final act on how he gets out of it brings the curtains down with a wonderful little perspective brought to note. Vicky (Siddharth) is an insomniac torch boy in a local theatre. One day Vicky meets a stranger who introduces him to a special tablet Lucia which gives him the much needed sleep and also an invincible dream where he plays a reigning star. In both dream and reality Vicky meets a girl Divya (Deepa Sannidhi) who changes his life and she is the reason for his present coma condition.
