When a mysterious young woman drifts into the small town of Somerset passions flare up as her hypnotic persona pierces through a tight-knit group of friends all at critical crossroads in their lives. Over the course of a turbulent summer bonds are tested loyalties are questioned and relationships are shattered as the entangled friends are forced to confront hard truths about themselves and each other. Ultimately they all must choose to forge ahead through their confusion down the bumpy road of adulthood and into the wild unknown future.
