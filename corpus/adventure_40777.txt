Seven beautiful ladies run afoul of villains brandishing chloroformed soaked rags leaving them bound gagged and helpless. In the first vignette a beautiful blonde is abducted and chloroformed. In the next tale a woman tries an unusual tactic to get her roommate to quit smoking. After a beautiful woman disses her the angry woman whips out her trusty chloroform and fondles the trussed unconscious and formerly mouthy lady. An editor accuses a novelist of plagiarizing her latest book and becomes the victim of a bondage-laden revenge plot. Next a lovely paparazza finds the tables turned when her quarry starts videotaping the photographer now bound gagged and naked. Finally a sweet young thing has a argument with her boyfriend and finds herself naked and hogtied.
