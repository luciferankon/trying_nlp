Sudarshan is a music teacher who lives with his daughter Sanika in a beautiful house far away from city life. Besides his work and being a single parent he is often found lost in the fond memories of his wife who died when Sanika was a child. Suddenly his health deteriorates due to abnormal wakefulness. His loss of ability to concentrate makes him irritable followed with depleting memory. Sanika finds it difficult to connect with her father who is unable to talk until she finds a secret put away in a wooden trunk.
