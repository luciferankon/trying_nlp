Sangam is the story of a young girl named Ganga who is ambitious and believes in following her father's dreams and giving the best to her family. Her parents are her support pillars who unconditionally under any circumstance stand by her and her dreams and she moves ahead to fulfill them. But life makes her put the interests of others before her own aspirations and she emerges heroic. Her father dies and the villain marries Ganga.
