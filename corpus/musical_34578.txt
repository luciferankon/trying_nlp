Preoccupied with personal matters club owner and former singing sensation of the "Diamondettes" Geneva Branch (Loretta Devine) has allowed her nephew Taylor (Christian Keyes) to co-manage her popular rhythm and blues club The Soul Cafe. Visited through the years by many national r&b stars The Soul Cafe has been the "it" club for the past 3 decades. In the midst of the crazy day-to-day operations problems arise that force Geneva and Taylor to make some difficult faith-testing life decisions that will impact the viability of the club and test many relationships within The Soul Cafe.
