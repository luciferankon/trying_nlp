Meet Alex and Alice McNally: part-time cops full-time twins. After their father the former Chief of Police died in the line of duty the twins were thrown into the force to fulfill his last dying wish. Underqualified and overconfident they are only permitted to work weekends. They are assigned "special police tasks" which often get them "in way over their heads."
