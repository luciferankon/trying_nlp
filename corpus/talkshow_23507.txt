Life and Style with Zara delves into the depths of spirituality and diversity through the voices of unique and inspiring individuals from all walks of life. Watch as host and creator Zara Durrani takes you on a modern journey of empowerment and spiritual healing by covering issues and topics often ignored by conventional entertainment shows. Some of our guests include Breast Cancer Survivor and Motivational Speaker Bif Naked Environmentalist Severn Suzuki Lama Marut and Yogi Will Blunderfield.
