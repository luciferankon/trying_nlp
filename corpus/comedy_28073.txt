Sam Hudson has been a DJ at Radio Uno the country's largest radio station for longer than she cares to remember and the botox just isn't doing the trick anymore. Sam struggles to stay hip and relevant to keep her position as Radio Uno's 'Voice of New Music' dealing with muddy festivals debauched rockstars and killer pasties along the way. When her longtime producer jumps ship to work with the up and coming newcomer Lorn Sam needs the help of naive young intern Hannah to grab the top job.
