Richard Singleton (Chris June) travels to Atlanta from New York City to surprise his brother Franklyn (Greg Lakhan) and sister - n - law Monika (Arianna Ennis). Things don't go as planned for Richard when he finds out that his estranged brother and wife does not welcome him with open arms. Richard goes to the local bar to relieve some stress when he encounters Atlanta native Rish Utley (Thomas Mackey). Richard loses his memory and replies on the assistance from Rish to help him navigate through the ATL on an evening they'll both never forget.
