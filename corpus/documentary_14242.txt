Rickshaw Wars is the documentary feature on the Parisian pedicab community and their war against capitalism Americanization of Europe gypsies and each other. Our film features an eccentric and often controversial cannon of characters on their journey to achieve economic salvation and ideological freedom. With political tensions in Europe at an all time high the purpose of this documentary is to examine the real life outcomes of austerity measures in France. Paris offers a unique and hauntingly beautiful backdrop to this story and the pedicab community found at the heart of it.
