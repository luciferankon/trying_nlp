Hustler Club Las Vegas The Show is a 30 minute unscripted series set in the exciting city of Las Vegas at the worlds only Erotic Events Center. The $40 million events center is the first of its kind boasting a beautiful rooftop venue FUSO showcasing an unbelievable view of the city six bars 3 stages and a 40' dancer pole. The show is centered around the main staff and creators of the Center in Las Vegas. It follows Harry and Jason Mohney (father and son) as they guide a team of misfits into the first team of Erotic events center executives. They travel extensively on the companies private jet Vu Air One to many clubs around the United States and abroad. Father and son do not always see eye to eye but manage to get the job done creating the largest and greatest club chain in the world.
