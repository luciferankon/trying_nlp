1997 - An old notable provincial family gets together in the sumptuous family castle. Each one of them accepted Hélčne's invitation for lunch to talk about the property's renovations but the debate becomes violent when she tells her two brothers that they can no longer receive any grants. The two men refuse to pay and ask Hélčne to find a solution. She then meets a certain Thomas Texier an attractive thirty-year-old man full of energy and attention who quickly established himself as essential. In love with old stones keen on France's History fascinated by the great families he found a solution for Hélčne. When the rest of the family meets him everyone falls under his spell. However behind his kindness hides a manipulator with no scruples able to make a whole family become paranoid.
