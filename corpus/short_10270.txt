Greg is an ill-fated wall painter burdened with the task of covering graffiti with his goo-drenched roller brush. On this his day of reckoning he notices that the instant his brush strokes cover any given image on the wall it appears in real life as if by magic first the balloon seller then the basketball teen followed by the motorcyclist and the mysterious suitcase man. But in the end could it be greed or dripping bad luck that leads Greg to his demise?
