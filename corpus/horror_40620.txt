Eight friends reunite for what should have been an unforgettable great weekend. But when six of the friends arrive they find out not everything is what it should be. From Co-Directors Shawn M. Bretton and Christopher Michel comes an all new tale of terror located in the heart of The Doom Trails. As the urban legend of Dead Man's Bridge unravels before their eyes friends disappear bodies are found and one question will be answered: If you scream in the middle of the woods and nobody is there to hear you will it really help?
