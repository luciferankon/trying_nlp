The cargo ship Repubblica del Brasile sails a circular route between Italy and West Africa. Trough intimate interviews and peculiar visuals the film deepens what leads sailors to their life against the backdrop of unseen European and African scenarios. From the master who's in charge of a 27 Italians and Filipinos crew to the cook from the chief engineer to cadets and ordinary seamen anyone contributes to take a deeper view on such a peculiar life across states time zones day night sun and storms. Furthermore the film discloses inner moments of reflections on man his needs thoughts and feelings. Wide Blue Delivery combines vastness and dark spaces constraints and freedom it's made of air and iron of human wisdom and fragilities.
