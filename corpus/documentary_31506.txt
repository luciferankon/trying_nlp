June 1977 a baby Orca was found in distress near Naniamo British Columbia. Two weeks later she was befriended by Bill Davis who began feeding her by hand in the wild. She would become known as Miracle since she survived a harrowing rescue a gunshot wound and rampant infection. She went on to star at Sealand of the Pacific Victoria British Columbia. But five years later she would die under a cloud of controversy. This is her story.
