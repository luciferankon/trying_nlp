From Wrexham to Wuhan is a 55-minute documentary following the adventures of four British bands on music tours across China as part of the UK Now festival. Commissioned by the British Council in association with Split Works the film was shot over 50 days between July 2012 and February 2013 and features the bands Gallops (Wales) And So I Watch You From Afar (Northern Ireland) The Fence Collective (Scotland) and Jamie Woon (England).
