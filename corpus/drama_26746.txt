Bonoshree' is the story which explores the complexities of relationships. Bonoshree is a doctor's wife. Bonoshree gets frustrated when her husband who is busy with his professional life cannot spend enough time with her. She befriends Arijit a fan of her singing. Gradually Bonoshree understands the seriousness of her husband's profession. She rejects Arijit's proposal who then attempts suicide. Finally all complexities are sorted out.
