16 year old Jessica Burns has a secret that she's afraid to share with anyone - except her best friend Brian Slater. For the past year she's been victimized by another girl - her former friend Avery Keller one of South Brookdale High School's most popular and beautiful students. What can you do when the world sees the image of a person but not the reality? With Brian's help and a hidden digital camera the evidence of Avery's relentless harassment is captured and finally exposed-bringing both girls and their families face to face with the truth. A very powerful story exploiting the reality of bullying from both sides. Shows the potential harm that can be done but explains how it can start. A must watch for anyone being bullied or even a person bullying someone else.
