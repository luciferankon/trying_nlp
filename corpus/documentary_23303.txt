This documentary covers Touchstone Theater's staging of Cervantes classic novel "Don Quixote" as a part theater part parade - "theatricade." With Don Quixote resurrected once more on the streets of Bethlehem we plunge our arms "all the way up to the elbows" in this thing he calls adventure. An adventure not only to behead giants and defeat knights but to unite the community of Bethlehem beyond the separations of north and south rich and poor Anglo and Latino which divide it.
