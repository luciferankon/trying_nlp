Lalu Seth is a gangster who carries on his nefarious activities without any fear of the police. Then one day his men's cross paths with a young man named Gautam Hiraskar a violent confrontation ensues - and Lalu Seth decides to teach Gautam a lesson and entraps him in a never-ending web of deceit and deception which results in Gautam running for his life - not only from Lalu Seth's hoodlum's but also from the police.
