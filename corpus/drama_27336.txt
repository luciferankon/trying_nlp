Lee Ga-Young is a young fashion student who has just been accepted into a fashion school in New York City but her guardian Madam Jo Soon-Hee is determined to make sure that she doesn't go. Fashion designer Kang Young-Gul runs his own shop called Young Girl and is behind on debts to a local gangster. Jung Jae-Hyuk the heir to the fashion empire J Fashion goes over to America to study before he takes over the company.
