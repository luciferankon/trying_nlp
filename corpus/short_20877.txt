National TV commercial for Kellogg's Eggo Waf-fulls which have strawberry flavored-filled jelly in the Eggo. This TV commercial started first aired on numerous TV networks including Disney and Nickelodeon in Winter of 2000 and stopped airing around Late Spring/Early Summer 2001. Eggo Waf-fulls were popular in the early 2000's but were discontinued around 2008.
