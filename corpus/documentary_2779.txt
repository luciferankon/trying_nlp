Just before the trial of Klaus Barbie who is charged with crimes against humanity this documentary reveals the events about the case. It refers also to main actors of the case: Jean Moulin Jacques Verges Serge Klarsfeld Pierre Lamothe Henri Nogueres Frederic Dugoujon Henri Amouroux Edgar Faure Marc Aron Raymond Aubrac Lise Lesevre Mario Blardone Simone Lagrange Julien Favet Rene; Wucher Elie Nahmias. Lucie Aubrac and Joe Nordmann.
