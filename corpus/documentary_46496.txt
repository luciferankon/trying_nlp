Second of two documentaries about Pedro Cano in which two meetings with students of Fine Arts for the exchange of ideas on the subject of working process of the Murcian artist in his Travel Notebooks are recorded. In this second documentary meeting with students it takes place in the Pedro Cano Foundation (Blanca Spain). The documentaries are understood in the innovation project "Del Natural" that directs and coordinates Carmen Castillo and produces TvUM in collaboration with the Innovation Unit of the University of Murcia.
