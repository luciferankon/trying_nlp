A portrait of a man condemned to die on Texas Death Row filmed in the Polunsky Unit where all Texas Death Row inmates are housed. Duane Buck prisoner 999231 in an exclusive interview talks openly and candidly about his childhood his pain his faith his regrets and his desire to be forgiven by his victims. Buck's case is one of the most famous and high-profile death row cases in the USA because of perceived racial bias during his trial. Buck's case has been taken up by the NAACP and is one of the few death penalty cases to be accepted and reviewed by the Supreme Court of the United States.
