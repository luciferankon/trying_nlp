Sam and Max are two young men with potential... potential to Do Whatever. Unfulfilled in his job working at a law firm Sam comes up with a brilliant alternative career path-- to do whatever. What is Do Whatever exactly? It's all in the company's name. If you need something anything done they're your guys. Do Whatever finds Sam and Max in hilarious and unpredictable situations. But just as the business is taking off things get complicated by Helmi Max's sweet online girlfriend who lives in Finland. As their long distance relationship deepens Sam fears it could mark the end for their adventures in whatever.
