When a perky member of extraterrestrial royalty is abducted by a masked intruder a secret government agency dispatches its top agent to investigate her disappearance. Before long she's grabbed by another hooded villain becoming yet another securely bound and gagged captive. Meanwhile an alliance of superheroines assigns their own champion to rescue the innocent victims but she falls victim to a sinister clone and winds up unmasked roped and muzzled with a vibrator secured where it will do the most good while the villains make their escape.
