Produced by TN2 in partnership with the Government the Preferred Broadcast of the French is more than just entertainment it is a program of public utility: In order to reduce prison overcrowding randomly selected prisoners are regularly transferred to a 3000-hectare reserve. Prisoners of common law prisoners of conscience and political prisoners are thus serving their sentences in the open air left to their own devices. But to preserve this ecosystem of a new kind beatings soon became indispensable. Accompanied by a professional cameraman 5 candidates from various backgrounds will have 5 days to regulate the fauna of SAFARI PARK by shooting down as many prisoners as possible. Will they succeed in coming to an understanding and forming a homogeneous and effective group? Will they come out of this dangerous hunt in a hostile environment? You will know by following the SAFARI PARK on TN2 and the social network partners.
