A series big on haircuts and bold personalities each Tease episode will feature two up-and-coming hair stylists as they face-off in an outrageous Olympics-style tournament. The aspiring stylists will put their skills to the ultimate tress test in front of a panel of expert judges as they compete for a chance at the supreme challenge - a one-on-one battle with a high-profile master stylist. Should they surpass the expertise of a hair pro their photo will hang on the Tease hair wall of fame and they will receive the "silver scissors" the crowning symbol of their styling victory.
