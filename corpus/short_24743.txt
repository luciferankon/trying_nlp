Svetlana one of the world's top neuroscientists has dedicated her life to finding the cure for the quickest spreading virus on the planet: Zombism. Secluded from human contact in a lab in northern Siberia she has little practice with human interaction yet she desperately dreams she could find a man who loves her for the wonder that is her brain. That dream however is turned on its head when the good-looking charming Vanya stumbles into her lab and wants her brain in a much more literal sense than she ever intended.
