In the dusty little town of Furlough in Texas an animal is slaughtering the cattle and the locals. When the teenager Tommy is killed their friends Anna Furlough her Mexican-American boyfriend Miguel Gonzalez Jill Gillespie and Rosie finds that a Mexican werewolf Chupacabra is the killer and they plot a plan to kill the beast. In a dusty Texas border town mired by racial unrest a series of gruesome and bloody killings heightens tensions; but only four high school students know the truth - the killings are the work of the mythical Mexican Werewolf Chupacabra. While the adults of the town blame and accuse each other the four teenagers two whites and two Mexican Americans band together to try to kill the beast and save their town.
