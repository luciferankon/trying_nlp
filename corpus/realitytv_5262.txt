Follow luxury stager Meridith Baer as she and her team completely redecorate homes listed for sale in Los Angeles' high-end real estate market. In hopes of getting her client's homes to sell quickly at top-dollar list prices she and her team pull from her company's incredible 135000-square-foot warehouse space - a decorator's dream - full of furniture accessories and art. Watch as these homes go from raw spaces to completely transformed glorious showplaces and learn tips on how to stage your own home to sell.
