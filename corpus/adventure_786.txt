Fatih Sultan Mehmet finds out about the turmoil in Mora. The Greek Kani Pasa blames the vizier but Kara Murat tells the Sultan That Kani Pasa wants to take the vizier's position. Fatih Sultan Mehmet gets furious and issues an edict to kill Kara Murat. Murat goes to Mora to protect the governor Tugrul Bey. However The Greek Pasa's brother Davut kills Tugrul Bey. Murat takes his revenge. He mixes amongst the people of Mora and helps them. Murat meets Zeynep daughter of the ex-governor and they fall in love. Letters showing the slander of the Greek brothers are found. When Davut imprisons Zeynep Murat fights for her and saves her. Murat takes the letters to Fatih. Fatih understands that Kara Mrat was right from the beginning.
