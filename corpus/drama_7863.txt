The Christ is born to the Virgin Mary. King Herod goes to persecute Him but they exodus to Egypt. Lost Years later Jesus returns and is baptized by John the Baptist. However when John is beheaded by King Herod's son at the behest of Salome Jesus accelerates the evangelism. Concerned for their own position Pharisees then conspire to have Him crucified by the Romans but Christ rises on the third day and His Gospel continues forth forevermore.
