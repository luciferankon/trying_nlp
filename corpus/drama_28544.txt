Sudetenland May 1945. A group of retreating German soldiers capture a group of young workers totally deployed in a chemical factory. They wants to use them as hostages during their progress to the American line. Along with them to hide in a nearby mansion which soon becomes a place of last fight which is in the minds and in the turbulent backdrop of war Czechoslovakia shortly before the liberation. Psychological probe as well as intimate drama of a small castle in the middle of the wall is a mosaic fate mapping short stories attitudes and thoughts of individual characters on both sides of the barricades.
