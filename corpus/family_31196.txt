Gino Pestino is one of the leading gunrunners in the state of Texas. After Gino's wife Cha Cha hires a new cook behind his back Gino is faced with the challenge of accepting the new cook as both his personal life and illegal business dealings collide. In the meantime a dashing ATF Agent named Mike Montoya begins to investigate Gino in the hopes of bringing him down in revenge for the death of his partner and in the process becomes romantically entwined with Cha Cha. Now with everything at stake Gino faces his most difficult challenge yet survival.
