A complicated series of sound and imagery piece together the thoughts and emotions of one man as he attempts to find his purpose. The entirety of the silent film makes excellent use of an intricate soundtrack to portray feelings that cannot be reproduced by anything but music. This narrative challenges the audience to use their senses on a higher level than they may have thought possible as they view the character in the film do just that. Director Harley Nerheim-Chereck from Portland Oregon wrote the accompanying music as Pre-Kinetic Blueprint alongside the filming of Field Notes.
