A new horror film from writer/director Kelly D. Weaver. It's been 5 years since the disappearance of Raven Fleming and her friends. Family members and siblings are looking for them. They get a tip that a known psychic and ghost hunter Calvin Granger might be able to help find them. As a side job Calvin helps the police on missing persons cases. On this night the family of the missing are going to find out that somethings are better left alone.
