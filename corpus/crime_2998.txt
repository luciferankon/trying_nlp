Based on the book 'Dunpeckham' London Boy is an autobiographical account of one man's humble upbringing in Peckham in the 1960's and 1970's. It follows the gritty and honest account of author Johnny Mack during the early part of his life. From impoverished beginnings Johnny is soon lured into a life of crime alcohol and drug abuse whilst slowly carving a reputation for himself in London's violent underbelly. A family man Johnny's loyalties are tested to the limit between that of his wife and children and the sinister 'faces' of the criminal fraternity. See Londonboy.tv or London Boy UK on Facebook.
