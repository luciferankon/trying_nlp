Keloid 16 in maturity and human perception is actually 115 years old. He was born into a long line of mentally and physically superior humans. His abilities enable him to speak via telepathy teleport practice telekinesis and control electricity at least those are the abilities of which he's aware. At his side his mother Marielle. She is a strong willed overprotective figure who would protect her son with her last breadth. Together they find a way to survive while being haunted.
