Troubled detective Nick Ferguson has a near fatal run-in with vicious serial killer Teddy who's been terrorizing Los Angeles by murdering young women and removing their organs. After receiving a new heart and recovering in the hospital Ferguson continues his obsessive search for Teddy. However Ferguson has now acquired psychic abilities that enable him to have horrific visions of Teddy's victims as they're being tormented and murdered. A psychotic man hunts down young women then kill them and sell their organs on the black market. Detective Nick Stevens has been chasing this killer for years and has to overcome this personal demons in order to conquer this serial killer.
