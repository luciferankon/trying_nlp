Anastasia is an aristocrat and very wealthy. She lives in a castle and has many acres of forests and lands and spends a lot of time riding on her favorite horse Talabar. As an adventurous independent woman she is used to travel alone. One day she is enjoying a wonderful ride when suddenly a terrible storm starts. She seeks protection in an old castle. The thunder makes the walls shake. Lightnings send their bright light through the halls. The fearless Anastasia begins to create a cozy place in front of an old chimney. But Anastasia is not yet aware of what is happening very close to her. The black lady leaves her coffin. Her son is also on his way. Their goal is the abandoned castle. Floating slowly towards Anastasia. The creatures of the night are desperately waiting for the encounter with that beautiful aristocrat.
