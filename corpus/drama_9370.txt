After a crisis in his own life after the collapse of his marriage and business Oskars the former fan of extreme sports is the author of the most popular national blog on divorce. He examines reasons of the marriage breaks often starting relationships with his female interviewees and even more often infuriating his sources of information with the open and sharp reflection of their life stories. Oskars also wants to renew the relationship with his 10-year old son who influences by Oskar's ex-wife hates his father. Oskar's female boss tries to use him for her own good while Marta who Oskars meets by accident while stopping her to commit suicide brings into his life a sparkling feeling as Oskars falls in love with her. But this relationship is not happened to succeed either.
