The Infinite Crisis Experience is a live interactive reality show centered on the progress and the lives of 6 exceptional Infinite Crisis players-MrFanatiq Neostormx Impurrvious DennisTheTall JenFo and Rabies-as they gather week after week to train in a high-stakes high-intensity push to become professionally competitive at Infinite Crisis Developed by Turbine "Infinite Crisis" is a free-to-play MOBA PC game that delivers a heart-pounding competitive player vs. player battle experience set in DC Comics' legendary Multiverse. It puts players in control of beloved heroes in twisted incarnations such as Gaslight Batman and Atomic Wonder Woman playing in league with well-known characters including Superman The Joker Green Lantern and The Flash. Each week our team will play with viewers online as part of their training. Think you can take them on? Think you can take them DOWN?
