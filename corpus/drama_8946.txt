When the Noralty oil company ignores warnings about the dangers in the Colombian border area eight of its employees (Canadians and one US Navajo) working on a pipeline in Ecuador are kidnapped by the well-organized guerrilla group which demands a $20000000 ransom refused by the concern and forces them deep in the Amazonian jungle where they hide from the army. Forty long days of marching trough this green hell gets the hostages in such bad shape that they must finally be brought into an insurgent camp. The Canadian embassy skeptic except for one RMCP sergeant can only negotiate about the price...
