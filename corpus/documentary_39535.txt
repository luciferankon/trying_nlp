Interviews include among others Rabbi Joseph Potasnik Executive Vice President of the New York Board of Rabbis and Host of WABC Radio's long-running "Religion on the Line"; Rabbi Ismar Schorsch Chancellor Emeritus of The Jewish Theological Seminary; and Rabbi David Ellenson President of Hebrew Union College-Jewish Institute of Religion. A round table discussion between Orthodox Conservative and Reform Rabbis highlights the range of acceptance of the Abayudaya conversion. In addition current photos from the Abayudaya community underline the importance of support by Jews from around the world.
