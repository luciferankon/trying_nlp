On the one hand GROWTH CAN DANCE deals with power relationships and hierarchy in corporate spaces and organizations but on the other hand a senior manager is perched on a boom lift eating chocolate biscuits while her assistant managers play with backhoe and truck toys... So maybe GROWTH CAN DANCE seeks to explore something else... Maybe GROWTH CAN DANCE is a dream -- a dream where the pace of work could be slowed down a dream where a conference call could become a sensual experience a dream where gender differences would disappear and where power relationships would be disabled...
