A film Documentary into the World of the Paranormal featuring North Texas Ghost Society and their travels across the state of Texas and neighboring states showcasing the paranormal activity they have encountered. This is a full behind the scenes unscripted documentary showing the life of a Paranormal Team and the sometimes bizarre and shocking Contact they have received. Follow along as they try to find answers to the evidence they have collected and to understand the how what when where and why of the Paranormal events experienced.
