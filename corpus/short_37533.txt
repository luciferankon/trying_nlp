A count has decided to marry. When his former lovers hear of this they decide to kidnap him from the office of the Justice of Peace and bring him to a manson. He is able to escape and returns to his bride's house. Meanwhile a bum has told her where he is and she is off to the mansion. When the groom doesn't get a response to his shoutings he tries to enter the house through the window but is arrested the same happens to the bride and the bum when they try to enter.
