The memories of the bullying that Tony went through as child and their affects on his adult character have never fully left him. Through his passion for music and story writing he starts making a feature film about it but before he finishes it he decides to track down the lead bully to get her point of view. The meeting proves difficult but uncovers information that shocks him and changes his views about the bully he'd held for years forever. Though the bully's actioned had harmed him they harmed her even more.
