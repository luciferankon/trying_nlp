Mysterious Midwest is a paranormal documentary series produced by Nick Evert and Paul Beaver. The series premiered in the Fall of 2009 on Halloween weekend. The program showcases first hand accounts and investigations of paranormal bizarre and or inexplicable events. Mysterious Midwest works with The Munroe Falls Paranormal Society on many of the episodes. The paranormal team provides evidence analysis for the documentary aspect of the show and for the reality aspect of the show the team becomes the focus during their overnight investigations of supposed haunted locations. Mysterious Midwest offers genuine paranormal chronicles and evidence and leaves the conclusion open ended and to the discretion of the viewer
