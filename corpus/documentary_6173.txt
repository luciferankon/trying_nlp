Meet Your Farmer is a series of short films about farms in Maine. Produced for Maine Farmland Trust an organization that works to preserve farm land in Maine for farming use the films offer a glimpse at the many different types of farms in the state. From the potato harvest in Aroostook County to the innovations of a seventh-generation farmer Downeast to the struggles of a dairy farmer in Western Maine the short films remind viewers that farming is more than just a historical feature of Maine; farming in Maine is alive and well.
