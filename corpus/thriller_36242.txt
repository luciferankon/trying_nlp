100 Degree Celsius is a Malayalam Thriller written and directed by Rakesh Gopan. A women-centric movie starring Shwetha Menon Meghna Raj Bhama Ananya and Haritha. Based on a real-life incident the movie revolves around the lives of five women - a housewife banker IT professional TV reporter and a college student. The movie takes a turn when the lives of all the 5 women turn upside down.
