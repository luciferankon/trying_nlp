Miles To Surf is a gritty unvarnished look at the extreme measures to which surfers will go if they can't get to the beach. This documentary by long-time surfer James Fulbright follows the history and evolution of board sports. From skateboards to wakeboards Fulbright talks to the inventors the discoverers the hard core junkies for 'the buzz of standing sideways' that is a common bond for surfers around the world.
