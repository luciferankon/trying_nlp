A masterpiece made by MS Farooki. Faisol is a student of an engineering school. His good friend Sathi loves him. However he likes a young and attractive teacher Fahmida who lives in separation from her husband who takes classes in the same engineering school. The story is about this triangle and their interaction with the surrounding environment of characterless teachers over enthusiastic relatives and gossiping friends. A true masterpiece depicting the ever changing ecology of urban life.
