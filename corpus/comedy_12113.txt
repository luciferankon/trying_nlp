Set in the early '90s two awkward aspiring musicians team up with a slimy small-time music manager to form the world's most influential boy band you've never heard of: The Alphabet Boyz. A fast-paced "Spinal Tap meets The Office" '90s mockumentary intercut with present-day pop star faux interviews The Alphabet Boyz chronicles a motley crew of questionable talent who miraculously fail their way to the top of music history.
