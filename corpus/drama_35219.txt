Set in the last days of a dying logging town Christian (Schneider) returns to his family home for his father Henry's (Rush) wedding. Reconnecting with his childhood friend Oliver (Leslie) and Oliver's family wife Charlotte (Otto) and daughter Hedvig (Young) he unearths a long-buried secret. As he tries to right the wrongs of the past his actions threaten to shatter the lives of those he left behind years before. The wedding of Henry owner of a logging company in demise will bring back to his hometown his estranged and troubled son Christian for the festive event. However even though everything appears to be untouched by time Oliver his beloved friend since childhood seems to be unable to make him feel like home and pick up where they left off. Eventually the day of the joyous event arrives and everything is in order for the festivities nevertheless Christian's volatile temperament will be the catalyst to everyone's lives forcing a life-altering epilogue.
