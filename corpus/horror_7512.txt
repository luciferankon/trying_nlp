The small town of Raelin KY has always had a bit of mystery behind it. But recently teenagers have started coming up missing bringing it to the attention of the FBI. When an untested rookie is teamed with a grizzled veteran to tackle the case they soon discover that nothing could have prepared them for the myth of The Fifth Hollar. With the help of the sheriff's office and local residents the 2 Special Agents attempt to discover the truth behind this old coal mine town while chasing down the legend of the "Soul Taker".
