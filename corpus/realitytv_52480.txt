A reality tv series which followed the fortunes of 5 "wannabe" celebrities as they attempt to break into the celebrity world of their choice: from tv to film glamour modelling to photoshoots. It looked into their lives in great detail from life at home to life at work and their attempts to be famous. In this episode Sam gets an audition to be a vampire and gets told to audition for Superman instead. Afterwards the cameraman gets mugged live outside the Arndale centre but Sam manages to restrain the muggers and they ran off.
