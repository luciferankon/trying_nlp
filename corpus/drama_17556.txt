Ryan a small time criminal on the run after a diamond heist gone wrong is caught breaking into a cottage by its owner Mrs Potter; a bitter unsociable and self isolated old lady who has just been given a month to live. Although from very different worlds the two realise they can help each other and agree to spend the month together; it's a hasty decision that turns into the most extraordinary month of both their lives. A month that changes everything...
