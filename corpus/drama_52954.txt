Comprised of four interconnected chapters in the first chapter we see four young men undertaking a shady and risky venture for a huge sum of money when one among them faces a crisis. Chapter two is about a middle-aged man boarding a bus with a huge sum of money anxious and suspicious of his fellow passengers. In the third chapter six youngsters set out on a journey a mission to register the marriage of two their friends who have eloped. The final chapter shifts focus to a mother watching over her son in the hospital waiting for her husband to come as time and her hopes slip away.
