Hustle follows the adventures of some artistic foreigners in LA. Los Angeles isn't like any other city in the world - the freak flag waves high in a town where Botox is cheaper than soy lattes. From renting an apartment that doesn't come with roaches ants or boas included to living with kleptomaniac roommates or brain-dead Croation prostitutes and the yoga-obsessed gym enthusiasts Hustle covers everything you need to know before moving to the city of Angels.
