After divorce the filmmaker's sister moved to the United States where she married again and subordinated everything to her new job. To her entirely reorganized life however enters her teenage daughter from the first marriage arriving from China to live with her. Two women who lived apart for a long time now learn to cohabit under one roof. Mutual misunderstanding leads to conflicts in which the director himself decides to intervene. In conversation and soft details the intimacy of his camera reveals cultural differences of the two big words: the USA and China.
