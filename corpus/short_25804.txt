This Short entails several stories intertwined  and these 4 strangers are so addicted to their Mobile Devices so consumed by the need to be connected to the electronic world that they miss out on some amazing opportunities both professionally at Work and personally with family members. This story follows distracted people living full time distracted lives never really truly focused on anything other then their Data Plans. This Short give examples of how they lost touch with the reality of their own lives. These People are "Connected to the Pod".
