The story of two women who at first seem to have nothing in common: the young and restless Clairy and the older dispirited but devious Danae. They live in adjoining apartments. Through a series of unpleasant and painful events they will get to know each other and come closer. They are not so different after all: two people in a crowded city frustrated and obsessed. As the film progresses events tax the two women's mental resources and makes them turn to each other for help. But though at first each one is looking to use the other in order to alleviate her own anxiety in the end following a dark and dramatic climax their masks will fall and their souls will meet.
