Lucas Bank has always dreamed of becoming a cop but his applications to the force keep getting rejected. After returning home one Monday morning from posting yet another application Lucas finds his house has been broken into and his world has been flipped upside down literally. When the police don't show up Lucas realizes that he needs to take matters into his own hands. Fuelled by his contempt towards the police department for not letting him in and his newfound rush being involved in a real life crime Lucas is forced to become the neighborhood vigilante.
