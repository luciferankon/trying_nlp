Seeking is a webseries born out of real life dating stories collected from slews of New Yorkers eager to share their woes. Seeking follows Ella as she is dared by her roommates Nate and Isaac to give absurd and undatable men a chance in the NYC singles scene. Along the way she must put up with morning people married men civil war re-enactors stalkers and ultimately needs to decide just how much nuttiness she is ready to put up with for the sake of companionship. Seeking is spreading the word about what sadly dating is really like in NYC.
