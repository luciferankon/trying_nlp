A seemingly random series of events is taking place in which people in desperate circumstances are receiving cash gifts anonymously. Bank officials trace the source to one elderly mans account but a group of young people is doing the actual distributing. The FBI and Wall Street officials are investigating the activities suspecting various evil motives. Meanwhile the cashing is spreading out across the country impacting more and more people. As federal agents tighten the net the group is forced to become more imaginative. Arrest and trial become inevitable; will the forces of greed dominate? Or will generosity win them over?
