A visionary unlike anyone else and narrated from Tony "Big Tuna" Accardo's Point of view this series will follow the birth of the Chicago Mob as young Al Capone begins his ventures into bootlegging and becoming the most notorious Mob Boss in Chicago. From his start as Capone's best friend /Bodyguard and Enforcer Accardo's role in the Chicago mob sees him navigate through prohibition to helping elect Kennedy Ventures in Las Vegas and beyond.
