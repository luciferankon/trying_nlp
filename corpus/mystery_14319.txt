The mystery begins when Leo (Alahay Abraham) comes back from vacation and reunites with his high school friends Mark (Ashwin Jagdish) and Lisa (Tiffany Medeiros). Everything seems to be fine until Leo encounters a haunted spirit. Lisa advices him that he is dealing with something different therefore he should get assistance from Michael Lake (Ammar Abraham) who can easily deal with supernatural and he is the only one that can solve a mystery behind this haunted spirit.
