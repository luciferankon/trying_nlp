Nuclear Family begins the story of a young couple John and Lynn who are trying to survive in the woods after what appears to be a nuclear holocaust. They stay on the move with their 11-year-old daughter Pauline... all the while searching for their missing 8-year-old son Grant. Meanwhile present society has disintegrated and the family must stay one step ahead of the Berserkers: lawless wild brutal men who rapepillage and destroy as a way of life. Shot as a pilot presentation for a TV series the story will take this family and the others they meet along the way in their search for Grant and to avoid the Berserkers to discoveries beyond the existing reality.
