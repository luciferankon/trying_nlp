Tim Harrison is twenty-one and living life to the full. His family is strong his friends are there at the best of times and his girlfriend is forever. But what would happen if for no explainable reason all of this was taken? What if everyone you knew and loved suddenly turned you away a stranger? Happiness and security comes through those we love. Their compassion shields us from the nightmares that linger just beyond the shadows. Losing everything in an instant is bound to test even the strongest of souls.
