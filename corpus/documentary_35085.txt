The Road To Success is the first ever film biography of this hugely successful band and will delight their millions of dedicated fans. It includes interviews with the band themselves and with close friends colleagues family members teachers journalists and others. Rare footage of Nickelback much previously unseen and from all stages of their career is included throughout the program. The film also includes location shoots from their hometowns and from other areas connected to their story many previously unseen photographs from the band members earliest days footage of the only Nickelback tribute band.
