The movie is all about Vettrivel (Sathyaraj) a rich merchant in Coimbatore. His son Sakthivel (Sibiraj) helps his father in business and comes across Nikitha and falls in love. Meanwhile Vettrivel gets his daughter married to a rich family in the village. Unfortunately they happen to be a gang of terrorists. Meanwhile Vettrivel opposes his son's love. The rest is all but how Sakthivel brings an end to the terrorists and also succeeds in holdingthe hands of Nikitha.
