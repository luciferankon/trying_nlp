Alexandria Egypt. To save the once largest Jewish community in the world from being shut down Ben an ultraorthodox Brooklyn Jew visiting Jerusalem is sent out to be the desperately needed tenth man for the ceremonies of Pesach. Having missed his plane and been kicked out of the bus in the Sinai desert his last hope is Adel a grumpy Bedouin looking for his camel. When their car breaks down it becomes a matter of their very survival.
