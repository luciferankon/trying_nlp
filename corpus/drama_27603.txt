A popular novelist suffering from writer's block is an unforgivable crime from the perspective of crazy fans. Meng Ke Huai (Johnny Lu) is a famous romance novelist who suffers from writer's block and can't finish his new book because he no longer believes in love. After a flight from Taipei to Kaohsiung an obsessive fan kidnaps Ke Huai along with an innocent bystander flight attendant Li Xiao Yang (Janet Tsai) and forces him to finish his long-awaited novel.
