The movie is about the lovers who constantly try to search something else from each other. It begins when the man breaks up with the woman. Their lives are monotonous lonely and restless. Min-su is depressed with Eun-suk who wants to leave him. He meets his friend for consolation. Eun-suk is also depressed but it's because of another man Sang-huk whom she loves. But she finds out her friend Mee-hee is going to marry Sang-huk and suddenly Eun-suk makes her mind for something.
