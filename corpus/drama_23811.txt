Henry Williams only wants one thing in life: a way to provide for his family. After months of unemployment in an unstable economy he seems to have found a golden opportunity to be self employed through a man named Linus Gartner an employer who promises him more money than he's ever made in his life. Overtime through broken promises and delays the lack of income puts a strain on his family. Therefore Alicia Henry's wife who is pregnant with his third child has differences with Henry about his new employment. The plot thickens when Linus attempts to skip town without paying leaving Henry nothing to show for his work. This pushes Henry over the edge and he finds himself on a mission to find Linus and put an end to his life while contemplating his own wife and kids lives then ultimately taking his own.
