USSR. 1978. Extraordinarily gifted engineer the passionate inventor Alexey Barannikov was exiled from Moscow because of political reasons doomed for a miserable life of a driver in a deep province. But as an educated one - he became a personal driver for a young Mikail Gorbachev. Two years the best Soviet anti-counterfeiters detective and KGB special agent desperately tried to catch "the international gang" or "NATO spy saboteurs." They were suspecting those hypothetical "high-end equipped professionals" in the attempt of destabilization of the Soviet economy before the Olympic games by the endless flood of fake money that were made better than real. But the mysterious genius always was a step ahead. Since the beginning Alexey Barannicov was a personal driver of his persecutors.
