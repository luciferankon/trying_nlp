Filmmaker Gary Lester's Fuzzy Little People offers an unflinching look at the plight of shelter animals in America while offering insights into the hallmarks of responsible pet ownership. The film also provides an overview of the many attractive animals presently in shelters awaiting a caring family and loving home. Featuring Alicia Emerson DVM and Kate Thomas along with animal stars Don Pablo Chihuhua Bandit T. Siamese and Leo T. Cat.
