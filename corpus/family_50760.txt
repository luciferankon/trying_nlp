It's a creative and exciting series where artists can practice and showcase their talent and skills. For example Actors can perform monologues scenes and improvisational skits; Comedians can perform comedy skits; Singers can sing A Capella or to music; Bands can Jam Drummers Keyboard players percussionists or guitarists can perform solos; Dancers can Dance; Martial Artists can spar or do fight scenes; Sketch Artists can draw; Painters can paint...
