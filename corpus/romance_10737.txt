These 3 good-looking brothers were ambivalent about love and marriage until fate decided to intervene. Are they ready to step into what would be the next stage of their lives? The tumultuous relationships continuously question Chen-Wei Chen-Hao and Chen-Kai's resolution. Is it better to stay with the one true love or to keep their options open? Can they work out what they want when faced with the 3 women who turned their worlds upside down? Get ready for a wild ride.
