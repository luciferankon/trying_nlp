Hamlet's Mouse-trap is an elegant mystery thriller which as the title suggests is in the tradition of Shakespeare's Hamlet. This is the story about Maria a young noblewoman and direct descendant of the House of Romanov whose mother was murdered during a supposed robbery in their home. Maria has set out to discover why her mother was murdered and by whom but she must also decide whether she will commit an act that would betray her faith her family's legacy and her mother's memory.
