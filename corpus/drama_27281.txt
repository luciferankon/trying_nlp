Two good police officers are face with an unfortunate adversity when they are dispatched to an active armed robbery scene. As the officers try's to talk the suspect down they receive fire from the man and fire's back. One of the receives a non life threatening injury during the fire fight. In the midst of trading shots the suspect is killed by the officers. The result of that intense night the two officers are indicted and put on trial for neglegant homicide due to an innocent bystander getting killed by a stray bullet.
