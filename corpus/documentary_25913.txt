Flex Wheeler - a four-time Arnold Classic champion - is largely regarded as the Uncrowned Mr. Olympia. Featuring previously unseen archival footage of bodybuilding's most admired and respected champions this film provides unprecedented access into the life of an enigmatic figure. Flex shares his life-long battle with depression low-esteem and suicidal thoughts despite his many victories in the public eye. Flex Wheeler's journey is narrated by his contemporaries Ronnie Coleman Shawn Ray Dorian Yates and Kevin Levrone; and industry insiders including Peter McGough Charles Glass Chad Nicholls and Tony Doherty as they 'weigh in' on the unparalleled impact Wheeler has made on the sport that has become synonymous with his name.
