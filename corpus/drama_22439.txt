The attractive Dutch student Eileen teaches at a school in Bogota when she is confronted with the imbalances of the Colombian society. Her idealism ensures that she refuses to accept the status quo and slowly Eileen's actions against injustice become an activist character. Than Eileen is introduced to the FARC the largest rebel movement in South America. She takes up arms and is involved in armed incidents and kidnapping. Her FARC membership is hard to accept by the home front  but Eileen regards this as the only right way. Story based on the Dutch FARC member Tanja Nijmeijer.
