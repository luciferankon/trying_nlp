Based on the popular MMO Star Trek Online by Cryptic Studios Star Trek: Durandal follows the crew of the USS Durandal on its first mission set just before the main story of the game. Species 8472 known as the Undine have infiltrated the major powers of the Alpha Quadrant to the point where no one can be trusted. Seeking new ways to detect Undine infiltrators Admiral Quinn assigns Captain Allerka with the mission to take the Durandal to a specific location to recruit the aid of an old nemesis.
