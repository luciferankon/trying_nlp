A teenage boy named Simon was on his way home when he found a wounded man on the side walk. Upon trying to assist the poor soul for medical needs a car came speeding towards both Simon and the injured man. After a series of blackouts and glimpses of a padded cell and a mysterious man in a white coat Simon awakes in an alleyway not certain how he survived the car accident with no injuries or wounds to bear. He tries to resume his walk home only to end up encountering various monsters along the way and brief encounters of a stalker. Is Simon loosing his mind or is he roaming around in a living nightmare he can't get out of?
