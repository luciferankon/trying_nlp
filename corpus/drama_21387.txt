Hollywood. 1937. The Golden Age of Cinema. Six women who come to Hollywood with the dream of becoming famous find themselves working as stand-ins to the glamorous stars of the era. They gather at their local watering hole for a birthday celebration where they share their hopes and dreams with the bartender Jack the ringmaster of the "stand-in" circus. During the course of one evening a new stand-in is introduced to the group instigating a turn of events which will change their lives forever.
