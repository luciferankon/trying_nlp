The Gettysburg Story brings alive America's greatest battle in a fresh dynamic way. Cutting-edge technologies including aerial drone cinematography helicopter mounted cameras motion control time-lapse and 3D animated maps bring the stories of Lincoln Lee Meade and others to a new generation. Filmmaker Jake Boritt's documentary is based on the best-selling Battlefield Auto Tour. The Gettysburg Story is narrated by acclaimed actor Stephen Lang (Avatar Gettysburg Gods & Generals). Unprecedented access to the Gettysburg National Military Park brings never before seen imagery of the exact ground where events changed the course of history. You'll see Gettysburg - a unique timeless American place - as it's never been seen before.
