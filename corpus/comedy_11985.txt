In this Flemish version of "America's Funniest Home Videos" actor-writer-comedian Frank Dingenen (in the last seasons singer Bart Van den Bossche) enhances with commentaries which also provide the binding element the comical value of numerous short amateur videos mostly accidental some staged send in by the public at large often grouped by subjects such as kids animals clumsy handymen weddings and other parties domestic/sports/traffic... accidents etc.
