Valentina and Sebastiăo are two young men controlled by a principal who forces them to steal precious jewels through disguises. The mysterious man who submits them to such crimes is known as "El Amo" and to convince them uses death threats as a resource. After several days they are wanted by the police and although they hate each other they plan to pretend to be siblings and take refuge in Săo Bartolomeu College belonging to Augusto Ponce de León and Francisca Campos.
