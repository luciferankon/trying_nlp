A best friend cheater movie. Pablo Steel is a failing internet talk show host with an eccentric group of characters that revolve around his delusion talk show world. Pablo is in an existential crisis questioning the value of spending so much time with an obsessed number one fan. His old best friend also his ex band leader quit the show and is making derogatory Facebook videos about him. Meanwhile his right hand man Lou Green who does movie reviews is questioning his serious Shakespearean acting commitment ever since he met the unbelievably talented improviser from France named Rober LeRoux. When Pablo decides he wants to be best friends with Rober and ditch his number one fan feelings are hurt and trouble ensues.
