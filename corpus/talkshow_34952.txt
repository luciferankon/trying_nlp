Meet Rike an 11-Grader from British Columbia's Interior. Her passion is rodeo. She has 6 horses at home and trains every day to win a saddle at the BC High School Rodeo Finals. If she wins she could make it to the nationals and receive a scholarship from a university in Colorado. (This segment shot by Josias Tschanz and crew). Other segments: A visit to North Vancouver's Grouse Mountain and a conversation with Alan Franey and Volker Goetze at the Vancouver International Film Festival 2012 (by Mass Abedi).
