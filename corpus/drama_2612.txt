A police called Poyraz Karayel gets prison sentence because of an allegation and loses custody of his son. After his penalty he becomes a cab driver with the help of his old chief Mumtaz. But also he has an another job: following Bahri Umman who is the head of one of the biggest mafia families of Istanbul. One day Poyraz fails to get his son while he was driving his cab and the incident ends in police. Meanwhile he meets Aysegul.
