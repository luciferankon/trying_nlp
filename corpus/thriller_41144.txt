In a society where the rich the poor believers and non believers collide our teen's build up their values trying to make their way through life. After a trip to the beach their circumstances change for the worst. Temptation masks itself showing up in every corner and planting doubt fear and anger. One by one they start fighting an inner battle against the evil spirits in their lives. Their weaknesses and cause of inept parenting influences their outcome. Will they have the strength values morality to fight temptation? Will their circumstances push them into devilry? Find out! The Summoned ... not as innocent as you may think.
