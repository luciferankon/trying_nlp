In 'The Price of Heaven' filmmaker Jack Janssen follows a potential top football player in a year where there's much at stake. Pablo is 13 and a great football talent but the question is will he succeed? He plays for the Amsterdam football club DWS where football stars as Frank Rijkaard and Ruud Gullit also started their careers. Pablo's trainer thinks Pablo could be better then Gullit. Ajax PSV and Feyenoord want Pablo for their youth teams. But also at school Pablo has to show what he is capable of.
