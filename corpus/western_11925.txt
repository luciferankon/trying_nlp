A gunslinger named Jack Swipp rescues a Mexican man named Pacho. In order to repay the debt Pacho dubbed 'Amigo' by Jack joins him to repay the debt. As Jack is a constant thorn in the side of 'Amigo' Amigo has convinced him that Gorda means 'Courageous Hero'as the Gun Fighter travels the lands explaining to people he is the mighty 'Gorda'. Whether the Gorda is good or bad you really never know until the finally come upon a town in which they must make the decision to do the right thing. Morality comes into play with a bit of insanity.
