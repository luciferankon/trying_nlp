Repercussions follow the life of Andre Lemon Harrison a young ambitious African-American male who was raised by his single mother Tracey. He and his Caucasian girlfriend Sabrina whom he has been with since high school have dealt with the pressures of being a biracial couple but through their trials and tribulations have managed to overcome the social stigma. Now while striving toward his Bachelor's degree in Journalism Andre is forced to face his own personal demons which may change his perspective on family friends and even his relationship. Andre's various stresses threaten the elements of his life that he holds most dear. As the series progresses; social issues such as infidelity substance abuse sexuality suicide poverty crime abortion wealth and mental health issues will be addressed by way of a multidimensional character study that will examine and unravel the lives of these diverse set of characters.
