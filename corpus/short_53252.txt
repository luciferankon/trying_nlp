This short film is the story of an ordinary business man named Joe as told from the point of view of his heart. The heart acts as observer and commentator explaining how every action in Joe's life does affects him. Unfortunately Joe doesn't take very good care of himself so his eating habits his lack of exercise his smoking habit and his high stress levels all (as the heart explains to the audience) create more work for the heart and problems that may become serious bodily health problems...all of which lead to Joe's finally suffering a heart attack. However Joe is hospitalized in time recovers and with the help of both his doctor and wife begins a more sensible lifestyle which will ensure that his heart will continue to function properly for a long time to come.
