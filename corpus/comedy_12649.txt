Prathi (Prathi Srinivasan based on herself) an Indian girl in her early twenties is a college-dropout trying to figure out her life. While handcuffed by her kinky boyfriend Ryan (Ben Hyde) the fire alarm goes off and he abandons her chained to the radiator. Distraught and rejected Prathi is looking for answers. Instead she gets her traditional mother Padma (Pooya Mohseni).
