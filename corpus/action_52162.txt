On the eve of his big comeback fight Middleweight boxing champion Cole 'fists of' Steele won the match but lost everything. Three years later with the help of Noise a wily girl from the rundown streets where the ex-champion has found himself he uncovers a way of clawing his life back. But first he'll need to punch his way through the city's corrupt underbelly and inadvertently earn himself the moniker of the Pugilist.
