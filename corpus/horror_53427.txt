Hellions Rise is a new web series from the minds of Matthew B. Moore Brett Mullen and The Drive-In Film Series. The series follows the ancient magician Merlin and his band specialist as they try to hunt down the daughters of Lilith and put an end to thousands of years of demonic cruelty. An ancient battle fought in the modern world Hellions Rise delivers myth magic violence gore comedy boobies and all of the other awesomeness you would expect from writer/director Matthew B. Moore.
