Ann Williams secretary to eccentric drama critic Frederick Skeates is persuaded to alter a ruinous review of Shakespearean actor Edmund Davey by Davey's wife Barbara. Davey's 'Othello' becomes a hit and Ann even though fired by Skeates becomes a fan of Davey and starts to fall for him much to the jealousy of her boyfriend Tommy. At the prospect of involvement in an adulterous triangle Ann recoils; but despite her resolution the characters' love lives become ever more tangled and a real-life tragedy of Othello looms...
