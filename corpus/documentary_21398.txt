The Maccabiah Games is the third largest sporting event in the world. Nearly 10000 athletes competed in the 2009 Maccabiah from over 70 different countries. JLTV is the exclusive Television provider of the Maccabiah Games in the US Canada and all other countries outside of Israel. JLTV understands the importance and impact of the Maccabiah Games and is committed to broadcasting the Games to as many viewers as possible.
