The" Diamond Knight" series is centered on a young gifted woman name "Dia" with amazing super abilities. "Dia" has been trying to find her purpose and origin in a foreign world (earth) since youth. The world she lives in has evolved greatly including new kinds of criminals; which new types of heroes would need to step-up to the occasion to help make the world a little safer as a vigilante. "Dia's" life is nowhere near being easy or perfect as she becomes known as "Diamond Knight" in this science fiction drama series.
