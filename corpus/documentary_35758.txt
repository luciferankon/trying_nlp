This film was shot in a small village of the Omo Valley in southern Ethiopia together with the Karo tribe. The Omo Valley is a site where tribes villages are reduced to human zoos to increment the national tourism industry; big manufacturing corporation such as H&M have land-grabbed most of the indigenous territory for cotton field plantation; the Omo river is being closed by the biggest Dam of Africa leaving two hundred thousand people with out access to water. We went there and after meeting with one tribe we asked them to imagine their future in a cinematic way this basically was the script. We weren't that surprise when they came up with a pop futuristic idea of themselves creating a syncretism between tradition and modernity. The result is a Docu/Mocu film about one of the last african tribe
