Also called Candy House this drama revolves around Taro's (OdagiriJoe) candy shop named "Sakuraya" and the lives of the four people who frequent this place as the shop despite turning in no profit holds treasured memories of their childhood days and all the guys including Taro spends their days chatting about the mundane topics and just being content with no aspiration. The story is simple and about day to day life of these characters and each episode is a lesson about life and happiness.
