On New Years eve of the year 2000 the paths of eight persons cross in an unexpectable and dramatic way. A businessman who weavers between his wife and his mistress faces an extortion of extremes. An unemployed man becomes a hired killer to escape from his misery. A child wanders the streets alone trying to survive. Is the dawning of the millennium the beginning of a new epoque or is it that behind all of the glittering celebrations people are experiencing a different reality?
