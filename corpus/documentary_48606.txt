The dwindling numbers of the Southern Sea Otter now commands the attention of USGS and Monterey Bay Aquarium scientists. Watch as the scientists conduct their annual otter census. So far the news is not good. The scientists capture track and observe otters in their central California near shore habitat. These cute and cuddly predators are what is known as a keystone species. Their presence in the coastal ecosystem determines the success and failure of many species below.
