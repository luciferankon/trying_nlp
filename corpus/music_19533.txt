With a selected international all-star line up featuring God Module System Syn Spetsnaz Icon of Coil and Mexican hard-floor gods Hocico these shows were a must for any real Electro enthusiast. It's a ninety minute montage of the greatest hits of all involved acts captured with multiple cameras and presented by Hamburg's own Crazy-Clip-TV-team. There is a detailed behind the scenes feature. It also features part of a show of electro-therapists KiEw who appeared as a special guest at the Hamburg-concert.
