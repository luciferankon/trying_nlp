We live in a world remade by a ubiquitous involvement with illuminated screens and handheld wireless computers called smartphones. At the heart of this new world is something we call "the desire to share". LIFE AFTER DIGITAL is a startling look at the unintended consequences of this world without privacy-from revenge porn cyberbullying and trolling attacks to a new generation of facial recognition technologies that effectively turn the face of every social media user into a barcode.
