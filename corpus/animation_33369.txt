A young girl named Nubbin (played by Elizabeth Daily) who is eager to go to school and learn wakes up one morning and walks into a world of talking animals vegetables and letters known as Tucked Away. She quickly learns that this hidden treasure is a vacationing place for all LETTERS from all over the world then spending each day searching for ways to help protect and keep Tucked Away hidden from Sammy The Snake and the rest of the world in this hybrid combination of animation and education from CW Studios.
