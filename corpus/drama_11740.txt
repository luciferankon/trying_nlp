Prospective art student Haneul has suffered throughout high school at the sadistic hands of Chiu the school bully. Everything changes as Haneul becomes a senior and Myungho an old friend and new ally transfers to his school. With the aid of Myungho Haneul rids himself of his tormentor expresses himself through comics makes new friends and for the first time finds love. His happiness is short lived however as its foundations begin to crumble. Chiu hatches plans of revenge against Myungho. Green-eyed Minsik lusts after Myungho's girlfriend. And they all pounce on one easy mark namely Haneul. What follows is an emotional rollercoaster of unexpected twists and turns. After finding salvation will Haneul be able to break free of his own shell and finally soar?
