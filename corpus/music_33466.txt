Legendary Moody Blues singer/composer/guitarist Justin Hayward performs a magical concert at Atlanta's famed Buckhead Theatre before an enthusiastic sold out audience highlighted by numerous solo hits and Moody Blues classics including Tuesday Afternoon and Nights In White Satin. Joining him on stage are Moody Blues regulars keyboardist Alan Hewitt and vocalist Julie Ragins along with guitar virtuoso Mike Dawes. The DVD also features an hour long bonus documentary On the Road to Love which captures the behind-the-scenes activities from Justin's 2013 US tour.
