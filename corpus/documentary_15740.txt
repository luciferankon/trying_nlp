Terry Wiles was first featured in 1979 in _"BBC2 Play of the Week" (1977) {On Giant's Shoulders (#2.12)}_ (qv) and told the story of his early years. A victim of Thalidomide he was adopted and with his new family emigrated to New Zealand. This documentary tells of the fight Terry now in his 30s and his wife have had since they married. Ostracised by their families and recipients of hate mail they have been married for eight years.
