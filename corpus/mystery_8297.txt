Police Inspector Anand (Sanjeev Kumar) and Karuna (Sulakshana Pandit) are in love. They meet with the approval of their elders and are married. Shortly after the marriage there is a homicide and the clues point toward Karuna. Anand disbelieves these but noticing a change in Karuna starts to actively pursue these clues. The rest of the family including Anand's dad a Judge by profession side with the daughter-in-law and Anand is left to pursue this against his family's wishes.
