A Maverick Social worker who is used to doing the impossible for children and their families finds herself with the ugliness of child murder by a mother and her boyfriend. A three state chase puts them both behind bars. Now a hunt begins to find the father. Will he be ready to be a father? Will the children want to be with him? What was it like for them in foster care? Find out the struggles everyone must fight to once again make a family when nobody thought they could.
