Highlights of the James Bond franchise with emphasis on the women of 007 including interviews by David Giammarco with Maud Adams Lana Wood Halle Berry and James Bond producer Barbara Broccoli as well as Pierce Brosnan and David Giammarco's perspective. The special coincided with the release of the 40th anniversary James Bond film "Die Another Day" and David Giammarco's brand new anthology book "For Your Eyes Only: Behind the Scenes of the James Bond Films".
