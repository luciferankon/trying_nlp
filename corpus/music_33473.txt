For seven days in summer Reggae lovers around the world tune in to The Greatest Reggae Show on Earth held annually in Montego Bay Jamaica. Red Stripe Reggae Sumfest features top artists Abijah Anthony B George nooks Baby Cham Wayne Marshall Tony Rebel Culture Bounty Killa Capelton Elephant Man Ninjaman Rayvon Damion Marley with special guests Ja Rule and Ashanti.
