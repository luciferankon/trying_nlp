In a small suburban town a group of juvenile gangsters known as "Urban Storm" rule the night. They will do anything and everything to get what they want. Even murder. Jack Martin (Korey Austin) a juvenile ex-convict with a mysterious past is released from prison. He reunites with a few old friends only to run into "Urban Storm" the deadliest juvenile gang in the world. After witnessing a murder Jack is forced to survive the terrors of the streets where "Urban Storm" rules the night. He and his friends have no choice but to lay low. They can't go to the police nor can they go home. Their only choice is survival. Pure and simple. Their numbers severely drop as one by one they are targets for gang hits. Will anyone survive?
