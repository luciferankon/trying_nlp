The Caravan film follows an eclectic group of international carriage drivers and horse riders as they journey across the American south from California to Florida over 5 months and 2500 miles. This epic journey weaves it's way through iconic landscapes mountains vast deserts pine forests Bayou's and back roads of America. The film explores the complexities of group dynamics the relationship we share with our horses in this modern world and the surprising connections made along the route by this group of relative strangers united by their love of the horse.
