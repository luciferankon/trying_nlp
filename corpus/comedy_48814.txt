Join the crew of a once famous online agency as they try and rebuild their shattered business after their Creative Director (Johan Joffemar) was embroiled in a scandal that drew national headlines. Johan was once the most sought after creative mind in the digital world. With a knowledge and flair far surpassing anything anyone has ever seen (or can seemingly understand) Johan along with the ever-manic agency owner Jimmy Dasher and the relentlessly pessimistic salesman Bill Bhoy built a digital agency few others could touch amongst the hype and hysteria of the Internet gold rush.
