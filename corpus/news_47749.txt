To-Day is a daily 12-minutes entertainment TV show. TO-DAY aims to bring a general audience the characters and historical events that have shaped our world. TO-DAY addresses these facts taking as departure point "every day of the year". From the 1st January to 31st of December two presenters introduce us to historical relevant event that occurred on that specific date: the World War II peace treaties began the coffee was invented Abdel Nasser took power in Egypt Daguerre revealed his first daguerreotype Omar Sharif was born the first transplant was successfully made etc. "History Told day by Day"
