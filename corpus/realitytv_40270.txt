As a how-to show dedicated exclusively to trucks 4x4s and SUVs Truck U finds monster-truck expert Matt Steele and master Drag Racer Bruno Massel involved in hands-on projects crucial to serious offroading and truck performance. Ideal for reaching the flourishing truck 4x4 SUV sport-compact truck and offroad audiences. Bruno Massel is an accomplished television host and professional drag racer on the NHRA national tour. He has hosted programs on TNN SPEED ESPN2 Spike Oxygen and Discovery. In addition to Truck U Bruno currently hosts NHRA's Xplod Sport Compact Drag Racing Series and Xplod Stylefest Oxygen's Fitness Insider and BBC / Discovery's Top Gear.
