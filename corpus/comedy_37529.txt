Kiss Me Gordon is a comedy about the imbalance and dichotomy of marital comfort and sexual desire. It is the story of Gordon a middle aged married man who becomes obsessed with Judy a 20-something sexpot hot mess. Gordon although married to Tanya a loyal beautiful wife thinks that a clandestine affair with Judy can save him from his boring domestic existence and reassert his manhood and virility. When he offers Judy $2000 to meet him in a hotel room in Hollywood it sets the stage for hilarious disaster.
