This documentary delves into such horror and sci-fi subjects as vampires make-up and special effects low budget B-movies from the 1950s to the 1990s comedy and horror and women in horror. It also has tributes to Forrest J Ackerman The Night of the Living Dead and William Castle's career as well as an "ingredients of horror" sequence presented by Elvira and a trivia quiz at the end. In between the segments is host George Hamilton with commentary and interviews with filmmakers on what they do and what horror means to them.
