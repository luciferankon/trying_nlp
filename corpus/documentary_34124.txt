John Featherstone a two time national coach of the year and mentor to Steve Sarkisian and Dave Canales spent the last 30 years of his life giving kids a second chance. For many of the players it's their ticket to a college degree. For others the dream is to follow the footsteps of 60 others from the school who made it to the NFL. While the team is stacked with talent and expectations going into the 2012 season what happens next surprises everyone. Playing in one of the most competitive conferences in the nation the odds are stacked heavily against them yet some still find a way to rise to the top.
