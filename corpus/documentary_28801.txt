A strong and touching story about a man and his family fighting deadly brain cancer. A man in his prime learns that he has an aggressive brain tumor. Practically the diagnosis means a death sentence. In this documentary we follow him his wife and their children closely while the family travel s trough this hard road filled with love from diagnosis to death. The film portrays the entire process in a very personal and naked manner and shows us what this family goes through when one of them has to face the death. Cancer could be called a national disease. Everyone knows someone who has suffered from cancer. Although many can be cured today A cancer diagnosis reminds us always that we are mortal. " Until cancer do us part" is a highly touching inspiring and timeless documentary that can be relevant to anyone. The film shows us that even in the midst of the darkest tragedy the lust for life fighting spirit and love will still remain.
