'Take Us Home' A movie by Luk Wyns Tim Slegers and Bob Dejongh. For over 2 years Royal Antwerp Football Club was followed closely by a professional film crew of die hard fans. Because of a series of extremely improbable events they witnessed one of the best stories in sports ever. This is the amazing adventure of Royal Antwerp Football Club the first and oldest club of Belgium. After decades of glory the club relegated to second division where it stayed mid-table for over 12 years fighting for survival. The only thing keeping the club alive was the massive legion of loyal and flamboyant fans. Experience the hope despair grief and happiness as the team seizes the final opportunity for a miraculous comeback.
