When Monette a traveling salesman going through a mid-life crisis picks up a mute hitcher one night little does he know he is about to make a life-altering decision that will forever shatter the lives of those around them. Embittered by his wife's affair and an embezzlement scandal he is unwittingly involved in the salesman unveils his thoughts to his deaf-and-dumb passenger and in doing so sets a series of events into motion which will push him to the brink of sanity. Exploring themes of impotency identity and inefficacy through the prism of morality and religion their actions that night will leave a path of destruction in their wake that no-one can ever forget.
