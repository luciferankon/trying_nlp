A puppeteer whose career has been affected by the invasion of films computer games and internet is fired and his show is closed down. He meets a fortune-teller whom casts a spell on him so whenever he is put down or bullied he would shrink. By the the time he is shrunk to the size of his puppets and no longer able to support his family he embarks on a mission to grow back to his original size. Through meeting Nokhodi [little pea] the legendary character of an Iranian folk tale his destiny takes a different turn.
