A high school mascot raises bloody hell on a Midwestern town after being brought to life by a disenchanted teenager. When outcast student Selena Kelly resorts to black magic to achieve revenge on those who mistreated her Nixon High suddenly has the scariest mascot around. As the body count rises Selena realizes her plans are hitting a little too close to home and that certain family secrets may be revealed. Unfortunately the only person who can bring an end to the terror is the same person who caused it in the first place.
