Two friends Logan and Slack move out to Hollywood to "make it". But when one of them decides to spend all of their rent money on a horrible idea they are faced with possible eviction and rushing to find roommates to pay for their apartment. Now instead of making it they have to bum it on their own couch live with interesting characters and find a new way to live their dreams. From naked people trying to share the same bed with Logan a drama queen muscle bound commercial actor making the apartment into his own meditation spa and a soap opera Latina "cougar" turning ever conversation in to a melodramatic even how will these two buddies pull through?
