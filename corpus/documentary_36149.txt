A feature-length documentary following the middle and high school-aged members of Off the Curb an afterschool hip-hop dance troupe as they struggle through adolescence committing themselves to living a "no drugs and no violence" lifestyle. Off the Curb was founded by Jackie Henderson ~ a dancer choreographer and fitness instructor from Newport Rhode Island ~ in 1992. Her own personal struggles are revealed as she mentors the group through her dedicated guidance and choreography uplifting them and helping them to succeed. Chosen via a highly selective audition and interview process due to limited funding the members of Off the Curb learn how to overcome disadvantages and setbacks in life through the art of dance.
