Tobias is on a mission to date as often and as many as he can. The thrill the adrenaline the anxiety has become an addiction and something he can't go without. He has even momentarily quit his job as a herbalist/acupuncturist whilst he entertains this addiction. His obsession for needling his face is all part of his beauty regime to stay looking young. His best mate Penny on the other hand is date-phobic and she settles for her monotonous job as a nurse ignoring her talent as a singer and most of the time swiping left on 'NEXT... ' dating application. In this momentary madness of date mania life still goes on and trials and tribulations play out. Tobias' dog Vagina going missing Lucy announcing she is pregnant Lucy announcing she is not pregnant Tobias preparing for fatherhood Tobias preparing for a break down Penny announcing she is pregnant Penny announcing she loves Tobias Tobias' many interesting dates from NEXT... and Vagina miraculously coming home are all mysterious occurrences of their momentary experiences. But which are more exciting? Momentary experiences of the date addict or of the date phobic? You decide! NEXT... is a six part web series that follows the adventures of Tobias (a date addict) and his best friend Penny (a date phobic) and is set in Melbourne Australia. The title refers to a dating app both Tobias and Penny are constantly using in the series. Quirky and entertaining NEXT... the web series will have you reaching for your iPhone to see who is online after watching each episode. A real highlight is the soundtrack performed by Michael Meeking and his band.
