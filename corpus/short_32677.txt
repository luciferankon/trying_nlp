Fix It follows Jason and Time two recent college grads. They like all young twenty somethings have to come to terms with life after college. They also have to come to terms with how their lifelong friendship will have to change as one of them plans to move to another coast. As if that wasn't enough the pair then discover a piece of alien technology that has fallen out of the sky. Tim seeing this as an opportunity to keep Jason in his life takes on the task of trying to fix it. The pair is then launched into a race against that puts their friendship (and maybe their future) in peril.
