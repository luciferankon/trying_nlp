A year after the events of Grey Room in small town in Florida a young woman with special powers discovers she is the target of demonic creatures. After seeking the help of a local physic a team from the Vatican is sent in to both protect her and also figure out what the source of this evil really wants with her. For this is only the beginning of a greater war that is to come and it all started with one birth; the birth of the Anti-Christ: Susan Wilcox.
