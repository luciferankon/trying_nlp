CIAO is not a conventional short film. Dare to step inside the mind of the 'Girl' where euphoria anguish and whispers co-exist in an uneasy high-wire act. Every moment is now a new beginning. Experience the catharsis the eruption of emotion coming in bursts and trickles. Follow her signs. Enter her inner world. Feel the shape of her universe and don't be surprised if you realize that you've been there before. With a raw and cutting-edge style that daringly defies story-telling conventions its vibrant unbridled energy pulses in the same contradictory rhythms as the 'Girl's' heart; frenetic and halting urgent and delayed intense and searching. It will leave you with sensation of life's eternal renewal and a curiosity to delve deeper into its universal internal enigma.
