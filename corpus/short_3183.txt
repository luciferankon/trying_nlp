A young adult couple going for a morning jog find themselves thrust into an amber alert situation that neither of them expected. Blaire a 25-year-old Grad student is determined to save the young girl despite the danger he might face. His girlfriend Jessenia who puts her brain before her actions isn't too thrilled but refuses to leave her boyfriend's side. They both take on the responsibility of pursuing the kidnapper and save the life of Avery Johnson a 7-year-old little girl.
