After serving three years in prison for embezzlement a pretty ex-con is determined to avenge herself upon her two partners who left her holding the bag while she spent time in the slammer. Determined to recover the missing loot the crook grabs her former partners and ropes and gags them until they agree to tell her where the money is hidden. The second story is a home invasion tale where two young beauties are bound gagged and held at gunpoint by a vicious captor.
