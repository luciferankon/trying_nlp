The Devil Walks Among Us is a glance into the world of Nashville's hot rod low brow art music and kustom kulture scene. From pinups to pinstripes the film explores Nashville's subculture including the Redneck Rumble Muddy Roots Voodoo Hoodoo Burlesque Off Broadway Kustom Thrills Tattoo Confederate Customs Lucky Box Guitars and more while bringing awareness to the Nikki Burgess case a pinup model artist and mom who mysteriously vanished on May 23 2014.
