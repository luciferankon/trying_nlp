Raissa Chaves - a young psychologist - in her childhood meets Mary Cotton an old horror legend that is held in the interiors of a school bathroom where a blonde young woman is imprisoned. And when she's invoked she appears to take those who call her. Nowadays this legend is known as 'the bathroom blonde' . Years passed by and new rumors began to appear of a presence in Raissa's old school's bathroom. So she goes back to her childhood scary memories to finally unravel the mystery of Mary Cotton.
