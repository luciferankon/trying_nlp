Kiran(Naresh) is a manager in a shop of electronics. Staff members tease him for his shyness towards girls. To prove them wrong he urgently marries a girl (Anitha Reddy). But his inexperience frightens him about the possibility of his failure while celebrating first night with his bride. To over-come this fear he seeks his secretary Margaret (Manochitra)'s help. Every attempt is met with some obstacle. After many attempts he regrets his cheating attempts. However he thanks Margaret for helping him overcome his shyness. Meanwhile a stranger begins to blackmail Kiran about his involvement with Margaret. How Kiran overcomes these and celebrates the happy night forms the story.
