This is an introduction to novelist and filmmaker Stephen Pytak his characters and his fiction. Pytak is known for writing a series of novels about a low-rent mercenary known as "The .40 Caliber Mouse." His third book "The Wild Damned" gave his readers new thrills by introducing the seductive urban explorer "Stacia Rose" and the sinister mass murderer "The Carrion Crow." Hosted by actress Jenna McBreen this 55 minute documentary offers a visual introduction to those characters. It also includes insights from fans and critics.
