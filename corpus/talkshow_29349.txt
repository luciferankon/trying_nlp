Holly Lemyre is a free spirited young feminist born and raised in the Bay Area. Finding herself entranced with Nevada County Holly moved to Grass Valley in August of 2012 to begin her education. Holly is a full time student president of the reproductive health club C.H.O.I.C.E.S. at Sierra College NCC and runs a sex health blog Sex Digest in her spare time. Cyber-trafficking is the story of a 14 year old girl who found herself victim to a form of sexual exploitation she had never known existed. This first hand account by Holly is an important glimpse into a world wide problem and serves as an important precautionary tale to young men and women everywhere.
