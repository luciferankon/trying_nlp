2006 evokes a certain life condition in theory a possible result of a terrorism-filled future. Some of the sick aspects of this difficult period are displayed without any restraint by showing the powers of the mass media as well as the negative limiting connection that ties the man to the few objects of his surroundings thus conveying the full reality of the situation. In this way the spectator is brought to suffer the same deep sense of isolation claustrophobia and mental confusion that the protagonist experiences.
