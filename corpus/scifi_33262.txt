Alice has always had memory gaps for this reason she is now a scientist with a strong resolution to find a solution against all kind of neuronal degenerative diseases. Sadly her experiments are never successful and she has no other option than exceed all moral legal and ethical limits trying to achieve her purposes and survive. Alice finds in a childhood friend Ulysses the best support and conditions to go forward with her experimentations. Ulysses suffers the Savant Syndrome which dramatically brings the subject to never forget things or experiences. They accidentally will discover memory and time are in strange way connected. As a result of this fact their experiments become something unexpected and never imagined before.
