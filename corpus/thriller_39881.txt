Julia Miles was your average "Youtuber" who fell victim to the twisted game known as Project Piecemeal. On the exterior Project Piecemeal appeared to be to way for the troubled youth to engage in their dark desires but no one knew this game was controlled by a malicious group called The Brotherhood. When Julia is found dead her uncle Edgar Miles decides he will not rest until he uncovers the truth of the Brotherhood and Julia's death. Edgar begins hunting down the Brotherhood one-by-one with the help from his tech-savvy friend Carlos. As he inches closer to the secrets of the Brotherhood his life and those around him becomes in danger.
