Follows a story of love. Nea has not seen her sister Jasmin for nearly a decade since their father Larry took her and left Nea and her mother Elena for the wealthy Yolanda. It also follows the story of the Ynares clan on how patriarch Roden and matriarch Susana deal with their sons the intelligent career-driven Ivan who is engaged to Karri a woman of acceptable social bearing and the care free Oliver. It also follows on Oliver and Jasmin's cats-and-dogs attitude on each other on Ivan as he meets Nea Yolanda's maltreatment of Jasmin and on how Nea's sunny disposition in life will change forever.
