Immanuel works for a publishing house that is counting its days. The publishing house is owned by Joseph whom Immanuel treats like his father. Debts force Joseph to leave behind his business. But Joseph recommends Immanuel to a job interview of an insurance company. Though Immanuel lacks the domain knowledge through his selling prowess he gets the job. He gets a new boss Jeevan. Jeevan is ambitious professional. He sets up a professional challenge for Immanuel . Now its Immanuel turn to stick to morality when it comes to business.
