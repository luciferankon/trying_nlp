For the Love of You an African-American love film explores the dynamics of a rocky relationship between Tai a career driven woman and Cordell her aspiring music artist boyfriend of three years. After years of supporting his aspirations and demanding his affection her hope for matrimonial bliss becomes a nightmare. Their lives come to a crossroad when the ultimate betrayal is revealed. Amidst obstacles of a broken heart Tai is comforted by the encouraging words of her assistant and best friend Cherie who urges her to leave her grief with Cordell and find new love. A chance meeting at a get-together proves that love cannot be found - it simply arrives. Tai meets Brandon - a hopeless romantic looking to complete his life with the woman of his dreams. As the two develop a relationship Tai struggles to believe that love could be found the second time around.
