Set in 2005 in the city of Los AngelesCalifornia a new police district is about to start up. The entire station is composed of new recruits fresh out of the academy. This movie follows four recruits specifically Squad 56. Fresh out of the academy and one ticket under their belt they are about to have the biggest case of their lives. One of the biggest drug kingpins is in town and he's leaving carnage in his path. It's up to squad 56 to stop him before all of L.A is in turmoil. But what they don't realize is the new station wasn't built to protect the public. It was built to guard 500 million worth of narcotics that belong to the west side. Their lives have just gotten harder.
