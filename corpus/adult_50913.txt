Several former college students decide to have a class reunion to renew old friendships. Unfortunately one of their old classmates is now a prominent politician who needs to find an incriminating photograph thought to be in the possession of one of the attendees. One-by-one the girls are grabbed gagged and roped so the man can search for the embarrassing evidence. In the second story a pair of bikini-clad babes are nabbed at a beach tied and gagged and marched through the woods to a van that's stationed to whisk them away to an unknown destination.
