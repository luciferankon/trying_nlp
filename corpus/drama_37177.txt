A struggling artist Michael falls in love with a beautiful New York debutante only to be rejected by her family. He returns home but clings on to the hope of seeing her again. Three years later they meet in London when Caroline arrives with her new millionaire fiance and invites Michael to her wedding. On the eve of the wedding however their luck changes as Michael inherits a fortune whilst Hamilton loses his in a banking crisis. Now that their situation is reversed Michael joins the Bridal Party in a last minute bid to win Caroline back. But who wins? Bridal Party is a classic story as passionate as 'The Great Gatsby' written with good humor and honesty it laughs at the charade of High Society marriages. Set in lavish locations with heart-breaking wedding songs it will have you laughing and crying down the aisle. Love wins in the end.
