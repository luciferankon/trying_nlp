Ryan is having dreams that seem to come true no matter what he does. As he explains it to a Psychiatrist it comes to light that the dreams are violent and even deadly. In an attempt to find normalcy he gets involved with a bartender named Carissa. Just when it looks like he is free of his violent dreams he has one of her. They agree that together they can work it out only to find that Ryan doesn't know the whole truth that can destroy them both.
