An evil emperor discovers a powerful crystal egg that covers entire world in darkness. The ruler the Repitilias vows to destroy anyone who gets in his way and starts war with the race of Squirrelings. He was so evil that the crystal cracked a single shard was lost according to the prophecy the only one who can save the world and heal the egg is someone of a pure heart. Join Red the last of the Squirrelings who must stop the evil emperor and save the world from darkness.
