GT Racer is the spectacular innovative cinema verite; style documentary series that looks and feels like feature film. It is following international historic racing. Once again Alexander Davidis is pushing the envelope by shooting his latest production straight to chip in HD video. This round his crew utilized the ultra shallow depth of field of latest Digital SLR HD cameras as well as digital in-car HD cameras. As a further novelty this production was entirely financed privately by racers and fans of the show. Features cars: Pre-War Bentley Mini Lotus Bizzarrini Cobra Corvette Mercury Cyclone McLaren Lola MIB Williams FI
