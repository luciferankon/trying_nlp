From the inner city streets of Baltimore Maryland Octavius J. Johnson a.k.a O'Lord is a zealous teen who embarks on an emotional journey of self-discovery as he balances his academics family and budding career as an entertainer. "O'Lord! (You Can't Serve Two Masters)" is an original unscripted television series which documents the life and journey of Octavius J.'s transition into manhood while on the road to Hollywood. When his artistry is skewed by family discord and his devotion to church he becomes rebelliously consumed with his high school sweetheart. At the front door of Hollywood will he turn his back on it all...or will he walk through the doors to become the next sensational teen entertainer? "O'Lord!" is an inspiring story full of charm laughter and testimonies of perseverance.
