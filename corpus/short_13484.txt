For Star Wars fans camping out for 10 days is more than just a bid for a good seat. Hilarious quirky and bittersweet experiences of Star Wars fans' last camp-out in line reveal their passion for Star Wars goes beyond the films impacting their families friends and communities. Filmmakers Suzanne Joe Kai and Marissa Becker chronicle fans at the first movie May 26 1977 to the 3D release Feb 10 2012.
