About a month ago Limei's best friend Yiting died in a car accident. She didn't believe that this accident was just a coincidence and tried to find evidence for that. But suddenly Limei disappeared without a trace. Her brother Darren is on his way to find out why she disappeared and follows every clue he got. Shockingly he had to find out that his sister has been kidnapped and he realizes that everything is connected to a company in Taiwan that is working on an evil project together with a German company.
