Marcelo makes a living as a painter in a Buenos Aires square with other street artists. Rejected and lonely as a result of his family's break up he devotes himself to his girlfriend and dreams of a happy future together with her. She however has other plans which along with Marcelo's discovery of the reasons for his family's separation create increasing conflicts between them and make Marcelo face reality more clearly. Marcelo is a young artist who makes a living painting in a square with other streets artist. He feels terribly abandoned and lonesome because his family has completely fallen apart. Perhaps that is the reason why he dedicates all his energies to creating a strong relationship with his girlfriend. Reality opens his eyes violently perhaps permitting him to see the future more clearly...
