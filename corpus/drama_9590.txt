Gerreo lives with a sense of worthlessness and solitude everyday of his life. One day 'Star' comes suddenly to his apartment. 'Star' is nothing but a plain Star but it changes everything for him. He may become a little fool. Things are not quite the same anymore. There is a better part about it. It may be because now he is more easygoing he starts to get along with the people around him. he makes friends has his girlfriend and works hard. The feeling of alienation begins to fade away without him understanding the reason why. 'Star' is not just a 'Star' now...
