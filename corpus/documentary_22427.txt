Costa Rica Hawaii Hong Kong New-Zealand... Through the prism of a minority - lesbians - this film questions the influence of the environment on the individual. Twelve women give their account and point us back to this interrogation : what freedom in the construction of our lives? Shot in Costa Rica Hawaii Hong Kong and New Zealand Reflections offers an intimate and complex portrayal of twelve women. Through the prism of a sexual minority the film raises the question of the influence of the environment on individuals.
