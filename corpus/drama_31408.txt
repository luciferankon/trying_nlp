In an ever changing climate of the black male many differences have evolved so why do all these men still face so many similar problems. One such black man finds himself facing this single culture mentality head on from the exact men that have been pushed together by such ideologies. When our lead is near a mental collapse and about to lose the job his education afforded him a concerned employer sees his uniqueness and decides to assist him with his challenge. She takes him to an unconventional hypnotherapist to retrace his steps and make sense of these on going misconceptions and confusions that have fractured his reality and left him unable to keep it all together.
