Jake Narang of Vile Films meet with fine artist and tattooist to the stars Henry Hate. Starting out life in Orange County LA under religious family values Hate's homosexuality clashed decisively with his upbringing and after leaving the army he sought refuge in Queercore: a vibrant and unabashed gay punk scene. Now Hate runs Prick Tattoo in London achieving notoriety through his tattoos with high profile clients including the late Amy Winehouse and Alexander McQueen.
