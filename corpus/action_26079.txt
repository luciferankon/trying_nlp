Gitekomba is a young guy whose life is being ruined by illicit brew He is married to Njambi who always tries to conceal her husband's weakness. Gitekomba's mother supports him financially to help him be away from Njambi who she blames for the lack of a grandchild. One day Gitekomba needs to choose between money food and drink a beautiful girl and illicit brew. He chooses unwisely. Njambi decides to find an ex-boyfriend so that she can get a child to save their marriage.
