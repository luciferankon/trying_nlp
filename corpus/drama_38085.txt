A New York City High School coming of age tale about the tumultuous and spontaneous journey of eclectic New York City High schoolers via the prism of music high school lifestyle. The show falls under comedy/drama and reveals what life is life for the teenagers at MAPA High School (Multimedia and Performing Arts HS) and all the obstacles the students deal with on a daily basis as they express their creative talents and grow into adulthood. The show also dives into common issues today's high school students and the young go through.
