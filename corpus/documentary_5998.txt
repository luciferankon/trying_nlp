A entertaining eight-part (which can be watched separately or all together) look at the making of the film. The parts consist of 'Journey to the Big Screen'; 'Recruiting the Troops'; 'Ender in Zero-G'; 'Battle School Revealed'; 'The Mind Game'; 'Behind Enemy Lines'; 'The Alien World'; and 'Ender's Mission Complete'. They explore various subjects like adapting the book for the screen casting and some of the convoluted wire work required for the zero gravity scenes.
