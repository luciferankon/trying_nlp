TV Guide Network counts down 25 of Hollywood's Greatest Love Affairs of All Time. From Bogie and Bacall to Brangelina they are the legendary romances that have captivated the world. Discover the love stories of your favorite celebrity couples including the larger-than-life pairing of on-and-off love birds Elizabeth Taylor and Richard Burton as well Hollywood's most prominent power couples like Lucille Ball & Desi Arnaz and Tom Hanks & Rita Wilson.
