Kgonta Bo 79 is one of the last shamans of the Kalahari San Bushmen. In turn village healer soothsayer and storyteller this delightful great-grandfather takes us on a unique adventure. Invited by his friend Lucinda Backwell a South African anthropologist to help identify and describe in click language an incomparable collection of 3500 San artifacts the shaman's ancestral knowledge also sheds a new light on a 40000 year old mystery and our universal past.
