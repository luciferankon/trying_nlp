Follows one of the most prestigious and wealthy families on the Upper East Side who have built their entire fortune on the business of diamonds. Due to a clause the grandfather placed in his will only his granddaughters are set to inherit his massive fortune these are the heiresses. After the death of their cousin the remaining heiresses try to figure out if the family curse is real or not and if not who killed their cousin and more importantly which one of them is next on the hit list?
