Daniel and Rebecca have been together for five months and Daniel has had enough of Rebecca's incessant drinking. Daniel turns to his ex-girlfriend Rose for help with his situation. Rose's sister Marlene having recently come out of an abusive marriage does her best to encourage her sister to leave Daniel to his own issues and just worry about herself and her six year old daughter Karina. Marlene her fifteen year old daughter Lilly Marlene's new boyfriend Charlie and some other family members are all gathered for Karina's seventh birthday party. Rose is setting up the party games and there is a knock on the door. Daniel shows up unexpectedly. Will Rose wake up and see the reality of the situation before it's too late or will she forever be "Blindly Bound?"
