When Katie is at a real low after her dog suddenly dies she decides to take a walk but she's so distracted by her grief that she almost steps out in front of a moving car. Someone grabs her shoulder pulling her back to the sidewalk. This is the first moment that Angela the annoyingly perky and klutzy angel enters her life. When Katie goes to a friend's house "spooky stuff" starts to happen like a mug crashing to the floor by itself. Angela helps Katie out of predicaments figures out and solves Katie's constant bad luck and eventually becomes her closest friend and companion - who still occasionally knocks things off shelves at the wrong moment.
