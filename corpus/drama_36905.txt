Can a good friend be an equally good lover? Ke You-Rong ('Kimi Hsia' (qv)) fell in love with Wan Hong-Zhi ('Nicholas Teo' (qv)) when they attended university together but he liked her friend Yi-Xue ('Amily Lou' (qv)). Putting her feelings for him in check You-Rong decided to set up Hong-Zhi with Yi-Xue and resigned herself to just being best friends with Hong-Zhi. Years later even after Hong-Zhi and Yi-Xue broke up You-Rong has maintained the firm line of friendship with Hong-Zhi.
