A Psychology professor with a deep passion for psychedelic studies tries to heal his war-veteran brother who suffers from extreme PTSD using Ayahuasca. When his billionaire best friend that runs the world's most popular social media app finds out about the upcoming ceremony he demands to be included along with the professor's little sister who's a stripper that dances to support her family after their dad is killed in a car accident from someone texting and driving. After the ceremony their revelations on consciousness unleash more than anyone had imagined especially the millions of people on the social media app who get their privacy exchanged for involuntary public transparency.
