Ever try leave the house without your Smart Phone? Drive without Google Maps? Try a restaurant without reading Yelp? Ever worry if you're computer or devices are being hacked? We're living in a data/digital dependent world. In the near future everyone becomes dependent on digital devices i.e. computers mobile phones apps. This makes Arknight Industries the most powerful corporation in history. Not everyone is on digital. There's a few that reject it the Moto Nostra a group of motocross riders led by the renegade A-Hole X. Through their underground events they perform crazy dangerous stunts that excite the masses. Slowly they lead an uprising that empower the masses to embrace "analog". This makes them a threat to corporations and governments who rather have the citizens hooked on digital because that makes them easier to manipulate and control.
