Caleb escapes into his graphic novel to help him block out the traumatic memory of his father. His method works until he adds a new character that begins to wreak havoc on his comic world and life. As the world falls apart the memories of his father reemerge and the pain that comes with them. Caleb must stop this new menace and finally face the memories that have plagued him for so long if he ever wants to live a normal life. A dark and brooding tale in the vein of Sin City in which Caleb a young artist struggles to overcome his troubled past by sketching a comic book world in his graphic novels a world that only he could pull himself into. Soon enough Caleb finds himself unable to distinguish between what is fantasy and what is reality in the real world forcing him to wage war against his own creation Seth his antagonist character.
