Hardened and guilt-ridden Oren is a young huntsman who exists on the pages of a storybook. When his brother's life is in danger he springs into action attempting to keep his family safe. On his journey however he inadvertently falls through a portal out of his book and into the real world! Lost and confused he teams up with a young librarian named Lucy to try to find a way home. As time goes on however certain feelings develop and Oren must choose...does he return home to his family or does he remain in the real world with the girl who brought him back to life?
