The beloved characters of Sir Arthur Conan Doyle come to life as puppets! In this entertaining Japanese series Sherlock Holmes (voiced by Kouichi Yamadera) is ever the authority-defying troublemaker at Beeton School a boarding school in London. John H. Watson (voiced by Wataru Takagi) transfers to the school and becomes roommates with Holmes in room 221B at Baker House one of the houses at the school.
