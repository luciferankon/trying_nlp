Every parent wants the best for their kids and Shahzad is no exception. Ever since his wife died he's been trying to keep his two kids Salma and Hassan on track. Salma is growing up quickly and Shahzad wants to make sure she's set up with the right guy to settle down with It's a promise he made his wife and part of what he considers his duty as a dad. But what does Salma want? Unbeknown to Shahzad she's caught up in a whirlwind romance with charismatic charmer Imi. Salma knows Imi is not what her dad is expecting but can she find a way to make everyone happy? A hard-hitting drama with a devastating finale Murdered by My Father is a story about the power and the limits of love in communities where 'honour' means everything. Widower Shahzad promised his late wife he would do his best for his children Hassan and Salma especially to find a suitable husband for Salma in his work-mate the dull Haroon. Salma however has fallen for the more charismatic Imi and neglects her studies to be with him. After she has asked her father to allow her to choose her own husband an angry Haroon humiliates Shahzad in front of their colleagues and Shahzad eventually finds out about his daughter's secret lover. Salma runs away from home to be with Imi and Shahzad catches up with her but there are no happy endings for any of them.
