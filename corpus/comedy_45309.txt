This is a film about a father who is recently laid off at a company and becomes the head of the household of two kids. Meanwhile his wife takes on the heavy task of earning the income for the family. The film offers the audience a look at the serious side from a husband that wants to re-connect with his children figure out his path in life and encourage his wife to "bring home the bacon". Meanwhile the humorous side comes from his interactions with his kids and his friends. It's a rather dark comedy with an upbeat feel at the end. Comparable to: "Mr. Mom" and "Little Miss Sunshine"
