Featuring interviews with numerous friends and family of the celebrity couple and leading Royal Family experts Secrets of the Royal Nursery will go behind-the-scenes of the monarchy revealing developments of what the Duke and Duchess of Cambridge (and the world) should expect over the coming months before the birth of what assuredly will be the world's most famous celebrity baby the future King or Queen of the United Kingdom. The special also will chronicle Catherine and William's marriage leading up to her pregnancy; the intense global attention it immediately generated and how it has started to affect the Royal Family fashion and the media; and provide a detailed look at the history of Royal babies and potential names of the their first child.
