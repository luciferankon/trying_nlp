When her fiancé skips the wedding to cover the antics of an inebriated Congressman and his co-ed cohorts Addie Kent has a meltdown. But try to strangle your fiancé on live TV and everybody freaks out. Even when it's the nation's most obscure political talk show host Frank Mann. Now banished to court-ordered community service the hot-headed former producer finds herself in a special kind of hell - public access.
