Paul Shah is an actor best known for his role in the daytime soap 'Faraway Close.' Frustrated at the limited opportunities afforded to him on the show Shah travels to Cyprus to perform as his hero - Sammy Davis Junior. A comic blend of fact and fiction suntans and swing 'I Gotta Be Me' follows Shah and the cast of Andrea Morrelli's Rat Pack tribute show on a five date tour of the island's bars and amphitheatres.
