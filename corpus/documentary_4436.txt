Filmed all around the country this film celebrating Oz events and the many different Oz festivals that have been enjoyed by many over the years. Along the way hear comments from different people that have attended events. We will travel to the Chesterton Wizard of Oz festival Chittenango's Oz-Stravananza festival and even other events like the Oztoberfest in Kansas and others. We take a look at some of these talented folks both historians impressionist among others by way of both video/audio clips - many which have been submitted by fans and others.
