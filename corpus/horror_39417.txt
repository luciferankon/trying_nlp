A Thakur mistakenly kills a Dharma a religious man by burning his hut in front of his mother and brother Shankar seeing the dangerous situation of angry villagers and his deteriorating health he sends his son Suraj in town to Vakil along with his servant Shamboo.Years later Suraj is grown up and in love with Rachna they both get married and against everyone's wish decide to visit their palace in village while on their way the face many horrifying incidents.Shankar who comes to know that Suraj has arrived in the palace he goes to kill him but before he could harm him he gets killed by an ugly monster who stays besides the palace and just one door separating him from the world.
