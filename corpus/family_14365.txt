Ruger is nothing special. He's the runt of his litter the "baby" and his littermates - especially his sole sister - never let him forget it. It only gets worse when their litter-mates are no longer there to provide a buffer between him and the cocky bully Layla. Winter's challenges help him find find his feet while his new role as big brother gives him some much needed self-worth. He cultivates close bonds with his younger siblings the tightest surprisingly with the brother that shares Layla's mean streak. But when Cian starts sniffing around the daughter of a rival pack Ruger will have to shake off his insecurities to keep his family from shattering forever.
