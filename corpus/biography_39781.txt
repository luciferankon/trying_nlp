The American Mohamet limited-series is as stylistically moody dramatically enthralling deeply intelligent and beautifully flawed as our main character. The very real drama of a man accepting the fate of his last days while he reflects on what he's experienced throughout his years fills in the gaps of understanding how a man could do what he's done. Each episode takes the viewer along for each climactic step toward Joseph's inevitable death; all while flashing back (through reflection and shared reminiscence) to the many moments and personalities throughout his life pertinent to his impending destiny. The gritty conditions of life in the 1800's frame Joseph Smith and the rich cast of characters around him with the harsh settings and implausible events resulting in public controversy sexual scandal treason murder a run for the presidency and an enduring American religion.
