When Big Syke gets released from jail he finds his objective in finding a capable 9-5 wasn't a reality then reverts back to the life of an outlaw. When getting reacquainted with his childhood friends who are still active gang members he finds the only realistic option in surviving is by taking chances in the fast life. While being chased by detectives and recapping his passed days with his late friends 'Mental Illness' 'Big Kato' and '2Pac Shakur' Big Syke's plans on becoming the Last Man Standing becomes the only mission.
