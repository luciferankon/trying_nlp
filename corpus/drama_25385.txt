Diego Borges (Benício) is a famous movie star living the best moment of his life and career. He marries his fiancée Laura (Guinle) the same day he finishes filming his last movie. Soon he finds out she has had an affair with his best friend Guilherme (Ciocler). However Diego dies before the opening of his great movie. At the day of the opening Laura and Guilherme are surprised at how close their lives were portrayed in the screen.
