One (1) out of four (4) women have encountered some kind of domestic violence in their lifetime. Even when they are out of the environment they can still have some lingering effects of their past experiences such as low self esteem fear of building new connections and relationships doubts about trusting their own decisions and having ruminating thoughts about the past. This presentation is specifically designed to help Hypnotherapists to work with people who have put the abusive environment behind them and are ready to move forward into loving fulfilling and safe relationships.
