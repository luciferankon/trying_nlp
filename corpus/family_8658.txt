Meet Professor Crunch and the Daffies. An unexpected experiment goes haywire and ZAP the Daffies are brought to our world from Daffieland. They must learn about our world and as they do so will you. Never before has Science been so easy to learn and to love. Join Professor Crunch Chloe Urchin and the Daffies on this high-energy musical adventure into the fascinating world of science. With over 1 hour of entertainment and 12 memorable songs your child will learn about: the Solar System Scientific Organization & Classification the Great Minds of Science & Exploration and a whole lot more!
