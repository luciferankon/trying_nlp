SPOILER: Rupa is a village girl who lives with her brother Abul. She was married to Ashik a doctor who died by sinking boat on their marriage night. She losses her sense as well as her memory after hearing the news. Few days later Doctor Masud lookalike of Ashik comes to the village and Rupa thinks him as her husband. Masud suggests to take her to his professor for treatment. Abul and Rupa goes there and stays in a Hotel where Masud visits everyday. Masud's wife Parul somehow knows this. Now it is time to persuade Parul about Rupa's condition and Rupa about her disease.
