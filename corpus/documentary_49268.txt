A new documentary on a revolutionary Irish breakthrough for the treatment of hunger and malnutrition in developing countries. Every night one in six people in the world go to bed hungry. In the face of this disturbing statistic One in Six is a story of hope and success. The documentary tells the story of Community-based Therapeutic Care (CTC) an innovative approach to dealing with severe hunger and malnutrition in developing countries that is revolutionising its early detection and treatment and in the process is empowering families local communities government institutions and even local economies.
