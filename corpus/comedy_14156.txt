A wacky European cosmetic surgeon infamous for his unique creations of beauty is pursued to India by a pair of obsessed malpractice detectives. The Doc with his assistant Theo is hired to further beautify the daughter of an eccentric Bombay family so that she can win a series of beauty pageants. The Doc becomes an overnight sensation but he still has to outwit his pursuers. Featuring a truly International cast and a brilliantly unique musical score the Genius of Beauty is an outrageous brand of humor and multiracial flavoring.
