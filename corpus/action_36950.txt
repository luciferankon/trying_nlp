Set a young contract killer winds up on the wrong end of her own employers after she makes a deadly mistake. Now the most deadly killers on the planet are bearing down on her. Set at the advice of the man who trained her embarks on a journey into the desert trailed by those killers. On her way she finds companionship from a young idealistic black sheep named Samuel who against the will of his protective sister Dana joins Set's journey to find redemption for her soul and above all else survive to see the end. After making a Deadly Mistake Set a young contract killer finds herself number one on the most wanted list of some of the most deadly killers on the planet and embarks on a race against time and hired murderers to correct her mistake find redemption for her soul and above all else survive.
