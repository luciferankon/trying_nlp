A two part Interview by Kelly Kierans with Henry Williams for Averity TV. Henry is an Irish-Africian Community leader in Galway Ireland and also a taxi driver in the Galway having arrived from Nigeria over ten years ago with his young family. He is a remarkable man a former international soccer player with his home country he is a powerful spokesman for his community and is especially involved in football coaching the new Irish. Henry is also very involved in his church and community projects in the city but acknowledges that racism is growing in the city. None more so than with other taxi drivers.
