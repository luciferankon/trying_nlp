Lee Ann a troubled and pregnant teenager whose existence revolves in and around downtown Los Angeles a world of desolate streets and run-down tenements a million miles from the sunny city most of us know agrees to sell her child to a middle-aged couple against the wishes of her artist boyfriend only to discover the couple has a secret even more troubling than her own. Lee Ann is a free spirit who refuses to be tied down by any job or relationship. The one constant in her life is Angelo her sometimes roommate who she'll always return to. Angelo in love with Lee Ann deals as best he can with her unpredictability and accepts her into his apartment after she disappears for months at a time. One of these times she returns pregnant. Lee Ann convinces Angelo to find a buyer for her baby. But the parents-to-be hide a dark secret. Will Lee Ann and Angelo find happiness? Or will Lee Ann's demons get the better of her?
