He may be small because he is just a kid but Ajiboy is not just any kid. Quick-witted and smart-mouthed he is entrusted by his father Fauzan to manage and care for Malacca's Water Theme Park for a short while as he has matters to attend to. However Ajiboy's abilities are doubted by Cumi and Mat Saja the park's supervisors who love to take advantage of their positions by giving freebies away to women. When Ajiboy finds out he decides to punish the two by demoting them to menial cleaning tasks which prompts them to commit a betrayal. As if he does not have his hands full already Ajiboy has to find a wife for his father in order to fulfill his deceased mother's wishes.
