A poor and flamboyant fisherman Zafeiris Kossis who was at the prompting of uncle Minas singer in Athens maintains his mother and his sister's widow's family. In the center where he sings Renata the daughter of the millionaire big publisher Derku and editor of one of his newspapers is visiting him. At their first meeting they strongly argue because of a girl's article in the newspaper and his own response. When they happen to meet again in Hydra where they know better a strong and mutual feeling develops between them.
