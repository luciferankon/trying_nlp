Esperanza a young woman with an extremely pessimistic outlook on life returns to work on Monday morning after a weekend of binge drinking. Realizing she's in no shape to work her co-workers intervene with the aim of convincing her to change her ways. But Esperanza is having none of it. Stark and bitter in tone this film offers a glimpse into the perspective of those for whom everyday life has turned into a senseless farce and an unbearable burden.
