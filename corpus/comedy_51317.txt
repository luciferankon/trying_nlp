Drag Realty is a comedy series starring Ruby Dickulous. Ruby a snarky drag queen Realtor and her assistant Porsche work as hard as they party. From shopping drinking and clubbing their way through social circles and hot spots while closing million dollar listings. While attending an elite party they wind up running into Ruby's nemesis Sharon. who once shared a love interest back in the day. Ruby was the slutty crazy pill popping party girl. Sharon was the pearl-wearing PTA Mom type. Their former flame chose Sharon. Jealousy kept them enemies but business kept them close. Their untimely meeting causes friction until they find themselves with more in common after Sharon's husband decides to leave her.
