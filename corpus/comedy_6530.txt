Tim is a white collar telemarketer who has just been dumped by his girlfriend. The pain from the break up hit him so hard that he will now use it as leverage to change his entire life - and then some. He learns how to dance builds up his body and ultimately becomes a stripper who works at a gay bar and does bachelorette parties for extra money. However one particular bachelorette party will become more of a surprise for Tim than its party-goers.
