Tom is in his own journey to battle Ankylosing Spondylitis among other health issues during his pursuit to get healthier and stronger. In his journey he demonstrates his every day life in the gym and encourages others to find their own path for Diet and Fitness in the form of a motivational Q&A talk show. He preaches the fundamentals of Calories (CICO) Flexible Dieting and Progressive Overload for training. There is no one pathway for health and fitness and Tom helps educate his viewers on how to identify which path will work best for your life.
