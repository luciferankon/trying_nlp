Jon is an artist stuck in a corporate job. Depending on the choices he makes he will escape his dissatisfying life or be trapped forever. The Passenger takes place over three different stories starting at the same moment in time. It explores how people influence each other and impact each others lives. After descending into the underground night scene in Detroit - encountering figures of mythical proportions - Drugs Sex and a whole world of Art - Will Jon become an artist and experience his dreams coming true or remain a prisoner of corporate America?
