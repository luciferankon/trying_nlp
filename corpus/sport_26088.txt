Mohan is a teacher as destined by life after his budding sports career is halted by injury. In his love-life now he faces a rather difficult challenge: one that he can compete in by no fair means. Having dealt with being forced to stay away from the game of Cricket that he is so passionate about he now faces the prospect of losing the girl he loves. The gentleman is torn. He now also faces the daunting task of teaching a motley bunch of kids half from his hostel and the other half an adventurous cricket club that calls themselves the Suntaragali (Whirlwind) Superstars the virtues of non-violence fair-play and a sportive spirit. Unintentionally he is in the midst of their squabble that takes the form of a cricket match. Does he win the match and make amends with Nikhila his sweetheart?
