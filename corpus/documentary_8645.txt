The Gondolier prepares his gondola for various trips of love vows and promises along the Marina del Rey Channel around Mother's Beach. His girlfriend and True Love Silvana comes in her red dress for this special occasion of filming. The documentary crew boards the boat with cameras director/hostess and dreams of the sea. Along the way a bottle is found in the waters! Oh no the Gondolier says these waters are getting polluted! He asks one of the passengers of the gondola to collect it. It turns out that it's a love note "Will You Marry Me" type from one hopeful lover to another. The gondolier John's girlfriend Silvana hugs and kisses him saying Yes.
