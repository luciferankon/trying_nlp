Trevor a simple pig farmer wants more adventure in his life. Tired of just feeding pigs Trevor wishes for more. Little does he know that his adventure is just around the corner! In a world full of elves wizards and trolls. Trevor embarks on a journey to track down his grandfather's killer. By his side he has Auralie; an elf who deals swift justice to those who call themselves evil. Along with a unfortunate Wizard named Turpin who just wants to prove he has what it takes to be a true Wizard. This fun adventurous series is packed full of laughs and excitement!
