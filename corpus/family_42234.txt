In the series premiere 'Marilu Henner' (qv) prepares special crepes with sausage bacon and sweet and savory toppings for her sons who are home from college; and makes a lunch of spicy salmon on a spinach-arugula salad for her friend radio host 'Leeza Gibbons' (qv). Also: a family potluck barbecue is served on the deck which includes turkey and Portobello burgers; and Marilu shares the secret to her perfect guacamole.
