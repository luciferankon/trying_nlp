A young couple Tomio and Madoka decide to get married after graduate school. Madoka convinces Tomio to visit a fortune teller to have their future foretold. Tomio is immediately attracted to the seductive psychic and returns to her late one night. When he comes to his senses and rejects her out of guilt and tries to leave she puts a terrifying curse on him. Now Tomio roams the streets trying to literally keep his head on straight! This is a chilling surreal and blood-dripping tale of black magic and the terrifying price of infidelity.
