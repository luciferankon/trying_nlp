Manchester Holdings is owned by the mysterious William Manchester who is unwillingly influenced by the power of Daphne Bright a gifted astrologer.At the helm of his conglomerate of twelve companies Manchester has placed the determined talented and brilliant Natacha De La Tour. Powerfulbright executive women are running each of the twelve companies under the authority of Natacha. They were handpicked by William Manchester according to their impressive resumes and business acumen. Manchester Holdings is attacked on every front by Sir Robin Maclaren; head of the power-hungry MCL Group. Natacha will have to with the help of her 11 CEOsface fight and stop him.
