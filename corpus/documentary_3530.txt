June 2014 Benoit falls gravely ill the diagnostic is not good : high index of suspicion for malignancy of prostate lungs skin and bone cancer. The doctors recommend immediate chemo and radiation treatments he's not going to make it past the holidays but Benoit refuses everything and decides to live the rest of his life to the fullest. He drinks more beer smokes all the cigarettes and weed he can get his hands on and dedicates his time to his passion : forging metal. Three years later against all odds Benoit is still living his life to the fullest. Produced and directed by Demian Fuica this documentary follows Benoit's unique path in life.
