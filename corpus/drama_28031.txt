A young lady picked up a hitch-hiker in her car took him in to her house as a Good Samaritan. The hitch-hiker raped the young lady and drowned her plea for help by raising the volume of her music box curious neighbours did nothing to address the unusual situation. The young lady got pregnant and religious leaders began to condemn her choosing not to understand the peculiarity of her situation. They passed judgement on her as if they are without sin.
