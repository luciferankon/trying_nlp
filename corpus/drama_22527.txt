In a small and secluded corner of the vast universe where thick forests and extend the majestic mountains of the Ala-Too unfolds the story of a young man named Arzy. He was deprived of the possibility of birth hear and speak. Despite the fact that nature is cheated of his hearing and speech he never felt vulnerable. His rebellious spirit is not broken - no ordeal no evil mockery peers. Arzy fallen in love with his neighbor Tansulu but fate separates them at a time. Always he sees the prophetic dream where a wise man comes and points him the way. This dream further strengthens his belief that he and his beloved finally be together. The way to conquer the world starting from the village. Arzy left a mark he had a son Aytegin long-awaited child from his dreams. He is world famous musician he hears and speaks the language of music.
