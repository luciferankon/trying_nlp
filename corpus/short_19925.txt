Sheila Green a hired killer one of the best in the biz is pregnant. Every since daddy let her hold an AK-47 she's been captivated by the kill. One more job and then off to an exotic island to wipe the slate clean - her commitment to her unborn baby. But Sheila hits a pothole on the road to paradise when she leaves a witness to her final kill. She must erase this "evil eye" but when all is not what it seems to be the stakes skyrocket. The prodigious killer faces the choice of defying her boss or killing again.
