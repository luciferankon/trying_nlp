Born from war comes the origin of modern terrors. In 1865 a disgraced Union doctor joins forces with a gang of AWOL Confederates to reclaim riches from the mysterious profiteer who bilked them all. But their mission proves disastrous upon encountering a terrifying breed of the undead: ones that can think. The key to survival lies within exploiting the doctor's personal demons.
