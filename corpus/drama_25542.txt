A cautionary drama centering around a group of ambitious and head-strong young adults developing a controversial and risk-taking documentary project that can either break or make their career in the film industry. This 10-episode narrative series will deal with how a group of aspiring filmmakers will face controversy betrayal threats deception heart-break and plenty of risk revealing the behind the scenes life of a film crew that is struggling to make it into the industry. Some will fall some will rise but they will all struggle trying to make a name for themselves while they eagerly reach for Western heights.
