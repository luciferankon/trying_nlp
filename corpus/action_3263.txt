Hand picked by international security Steven Marshall a professional bodyguard takes on a six month assignment to a secret diamond mining facility deep in the heart of the Vietnamese jungle. Much to his wealthy families disapproval Steven along with Paul Edwards a bodyguard chosen by the U.S Embassy are to protect their client Milan at all costs whilst she comes and goes to oversee the weighing of the quarried diamonds. Milan is the beautiful daughter of a suspected Chinese war lord whom deals in the illegal arms trade and is therefore prone to kidnapping and terrorism attempts.
