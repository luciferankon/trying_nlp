I Can Dance! is a feature-length story about the power of dance to transform children's lives in American public schools. It is the story of 4 tough-talking elementary school kids who have never danced before. In this deeply inspiring film the children who are at first shy resistant apprehensive and even hostile open up to the discipline grace and joyful power of dance. They learn to speak the universal language of rhythm and soul transcending and expressing their unique life circumstances.
