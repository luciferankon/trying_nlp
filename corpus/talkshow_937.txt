Ziva Vrazka is a Morning and Night Show Simultaneously for the Bulgarian Community-a Morning Show in Bulgaria and Europe and a Night Show in USA and Canada. The English Translation is "Live Connection"-the Idea is The Show to be a "live connection" between Bulgarians from All Over The World and Those in Bulgaria. A dynamic Mix of News Opinions Comments about Popular Events and Inspiring Stories-It is a Show about People who Search Try Build and Succeed.
