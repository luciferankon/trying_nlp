The plot revolves around Fatima who is troubled by a vision of a mysterious person since her childhood. After 22 years of psychological problems she wants to confront the vision. The film follows her search for answers where she discovers a connection between the A'ali burial mounds and the haunting vision. It is among the ominous burial mounds that the plot unfolds and by chance brings about a meeting of Fatima and her ex-husband with the other main characters of the film. They are rebellious teenagers Khalid and Amin who are involved in drugs and Khalid's older brother Ahmed who is trying to stop them throwing their lives away. This is not just a thriller but a story of a group of individuals finding themselves in a dark situation with a mysterious presence that is trying to make them confront their problems.
