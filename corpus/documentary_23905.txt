Director Patrick Hewlett follows a brilliant but unsigned rock n roll band for 9 months on tour through Europe America Mexico and The Bahamas in a last ditch attempt to make it big. The band must overcome dodgy promoters money struggles band fights cancelled shows and their own egos until a chance encounter in The Bahamas promises to change everything. Could this be the big break they've been waiting for? Or is it finally time to kiss goodbye to their dreams? With stunning locations featuring a host of larger than life characters By All Means: A Rock n Roll Story is a documentary about dreams love friendships sacrifice and most importantly the power of music.
