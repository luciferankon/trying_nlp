A secret society of financiers known as the Q begin to wage war on each other for control of the world monetary system. Over time Q factions begin to emerge with new forms of currency to gain dominion over the world monetary system. As each Q faction arises with a new form of currency another opposing faction arises to exterminate it. In the midst of the chaotic births and deaths of the Q fiat currencies a hacker group known as the Zeits decides to create a new digital crypto currency by the people for the people that can operate independent of the Q.
