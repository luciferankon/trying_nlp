Angel McGee knows everything about women... Max Rosenberg has never had a woman... and Jackie is still looking for the man of her dreams... They may have nothing in common but they're all about to get entangled in a bizarre mystery of murder in NYC... Max Rosenberg is a Wall Street file clerk and walking contradiction - an uptight free spirit a disorganized obsessive-compulsive and a faithful womanizer. While Max buries himself in the lives of his eccentric "friends" who are all searching for love a serial killer spreads terror through the streets of New York. As Max's best friend and polar opposite Angel(a slick dread-locked love-machine) schools Max in the ways of the fairer sex his other friend Slim Jim falls victim to the "Slasher". But Max proves that nerds can be heroes when he "risks it all" to end the killer's reign of terror.
