May 6th 1937 The LZ 129 Hindenburg made its first successful flight from Frankfurt Germany to Lakehurst Naval Air-station in America. This historical event changed the course of history and gave birth to the age of the airship. Country's all over the world were in compilation to see who could build the biggest best and fastest airship. City's now took to the sky floating high above the earth and the only people that live on its surface are farmers and people who live with animals. We follow Morgan her mysterious metal pocket dragon and her group of privateers as they travel the globe with their routine Rum delivery's. Morgans dream is to continue her grandfathers work by building a machine that takes energy from the air. The only thing is she is not the only one looking for this technology and the pirates of skull island will kill for it.
