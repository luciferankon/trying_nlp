A television Documentary that portrayed rapper/actor T.I.'s farewell going away to prison event held in Sean Combs a.k.a. Diddy's mansion in Miami's elite "Star island" neighborhood. On March 27 2009 U.S. District Judge Charles A. Pannell Jr. sentenced T.I. to one year and one day in prison and ordered to pay $100300 for federal weapons charges. T.I. had his sentence reduced from a maximum 10 years and a $250000 fine with a plea bargain. The program showcased celebrity interviews and commentaries from his best friends in the industry along with a touching in depth interview with iconic hip hop figure MC Serch.
