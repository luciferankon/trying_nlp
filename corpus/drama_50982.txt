Azad comes to Sweden by mistake. In order to blend in with the family that hides him he has to pretend that he is nine years old. Suddenly he's quite alone in a new country and doesn't understand the language. But with the help of a spaced-out hot dog vendor the coolest guy in school and world champion Kajsa Bergqvist maybe he can meet his real family again. Azad loves to do the high jump and holds Kajsa Bergqvist as his hero. Together with his brother Tigris he's separated from his family and ends up in a new country - Sweden. Even though they're getting new friends they never give up the hope of being reunited with their family.
