A story about a young man and his dog who go on a two week camping trip in the Rockies to get away from the everyday grind. Trouble strikes when his dog and only companion are lost while on a evening hike. How far will he go to save mans best friend? The dangers of the wilderness come to life as he fights to find his best friend and not get killed in the process. This action packed drama tugs at the heart strings of anyone who's ever had a dog and is sure to have you on the edge of your seat throughout.
