The story of Legendary hip hop/r&b/pop trio TLC. The group rose to fame in 1991 when they signed to LaFace Records releasing their multi-platinum debut. Their 1994 follow-up CrazySexyCool sold over 22 million copies and Fanmail sold over 10 million copies in 1999. This group experienced bankruptcy illness group fights and death.
