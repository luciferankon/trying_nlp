When God presents Nick Chandler a struggling middle-aged screenwriter with an offer he can't refuse hilarity ensues. How would Nick like to put his name on God's Girl an original modern new screenplay written in Heaven by William Shakespeare scored by Wolfgang Amadeus Mozart and with sets designed by Leonardo Da Vinci? In this uniquely and uproariously funny Hollywood tale what seems to be a slam dunk for Hollywood's next "Movie of the Year" turns upside down when the very reason for the Heavenly project is revealed. God knows a girl who HE figures with the right vehicle could become a star.
