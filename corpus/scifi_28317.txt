Using a robot the Priestess of Ku a Captain and his crew steal the priceless "Eyes of Hub" from a space vault and return the jewels to its original place causing the thieves who stole them to seek revenge. After falling for the robot the Captain seeks out human MOONIE who made the robot in her image. Looking for a new Captain she agrees to meet and hires him to command her ship. However a simple mission turns deadly after the murderous thieves catch up to the new Captain of the Thalia. Based on the book Moonie in Too Many Moons by Nicola Cuti.
