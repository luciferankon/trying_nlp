A Clockwork Orange meets Kurt Cobain in this dark-comedy thriller about music murder and the destruction of Generation X. An indie Grunge Noir fable on the loss of innocence and the perils of selling out X-GEN follows Kirk (or is it Kurt?) and his flannel wearing ex Tobi (Vail?) as they are brutally "co-opted" INto the cross-hairs of a menacing Boomer/Millennial conspiracy to ERASE their generation (X) which is sandwiched in between them... Come as you are.
