Film compilation from the heavy-metal label Earache Records combines the contents of both iCRUSHER 1 and 2. It contains music videos from artists such as "Parasite God" by Mortiis "Bury Your Dead" by The Haunted "Christbait Rising" by Godflesh "Nothing" by Society 1 and more. Also included are live performances from Napalm Death Carcass Cathedral and more. In addition it includes audio tracks from Rabies Caste December Wolves Insision and Ultraviolence.
