This documentary series travels to modern Vietnam to tell the stories of four different young Vietnamese people and their thoughts about the present and future of their country. One is a fashion designer with Western ideas about designs another is a police agent-turned-artist the third is a photographer with a taste for the aesthetics of the body and the last one is a DJ of techno music. They speak of the duality between Eastern belongings and Western inspirations and of the difficulties of living in a Communist regime.
