The sequel of the first television series 'Hollands Welvaren' that evolves around the workplace. In Hollands Welvaren II Michiel van Erp portrays passionate and driven Dutchmen at work. After all these years where do these people get their pleasure from? How do they overcome crises or risks? How do they stay passionate and inventive - whether they are self-employed or have gladly been working for a boss for years?
