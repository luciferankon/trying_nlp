The story shows a day in the 1990s of Gugu a little girl who is living in a relative's place in rural Wenzhou Zhejiang. She comes to understand the life of her father that does business away from home throughout the trip to the city center. At the end of the day how will she conceal the incidents during the day when facing her mother? This film demonstrates the unique childhood memories of children born in the ever-changing 90s Wenzhou through Gugu's eyes.
