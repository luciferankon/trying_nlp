While politicians in Washington debate the cost and role of healthcare in our country our nation's hospitals must find a way to continue serving patients. One such hospital is taking a novel approach to meet the needs of its underserved population. Central California's 'Community Regional Medical Center' is innovating by implementing a radical new system of patient management. If it succeeds it could revolutionize how hospitals manage the flow of patients with and without insurance. Featuring interviews with doctors patients and hospital staff this documentary explores the roots of the current healthcare problems and how one hospital is attempting to deal with them.
