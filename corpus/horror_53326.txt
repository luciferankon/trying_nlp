'Tales of Fear' is a six part anthology film styled after EC horror comics of the 1950s. The film includes a variety of tales: Teenagers in a graveyard at night realize it's not wise to disturb the dead. A nosey woman thinks her neighbor Norman is up to no good. The intentions of a child murderer with his latest catch are no match for child's play. Cellular reanimation was the doctor's goal going to pieces became the result. A coyote hunter discovers that it's not safe to hunt out of season. Bank robbers on the run seek refuge but what they find within the barn may be more permanent than what they're looking for.
