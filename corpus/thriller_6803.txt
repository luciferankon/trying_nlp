Tina Tudor (J. Cynthia Brooks) an exotic dancer finds her life in great danger when she's the sole witness to a murder. The murderers have tried to kill Tina before she can testify against them so two policemen are assigned to safeguard her. But one of the cops finds himself falling for Tina and the two begin an affair which not only compromises the officer's integrity but threatens to destroy Tina's relationship with her longtime boyfriend.
