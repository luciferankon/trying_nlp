In this touching story about love loss happiness and pain will two men of modest means be able to find true love among three women with magical powers? Wen Daren (Liu Chang) and Chi Feifan (Yang Jun Yu) are looking for love but only have themselves and their time to give a prospective love interest. What happens when they meet three unusual women - Yao Susu (Bai Liuxi) Yao Tianxin (Veronique You) and Lin Yiyi (Li Yan) - with super powers? Can Daren and Feifan help the women heal their past pains and learn the meaning of true love? "To Me Love" is a 2014 Chinese web drama directed by Zhang Lei.
