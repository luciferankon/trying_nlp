While a sleeping serpent the size of an island grows in the underground galleries of the city of Săo Luís its local inhabitants fight against surreal situations to survive in a brutal every-day life but they are unable to perceive the appalling conditions they live in. Reality and fantasy walk hand to hand in this history that originates a movement: the Solrealismo (Sunrealism).
