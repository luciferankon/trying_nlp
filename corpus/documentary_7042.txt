Long dismissed as a mere fortune-telling method Tarot can be a tool to help us understanding ourselves others and the world around us. This video takes a 21st century approach to learning Tarot. It acts as a visual guide that covers everything you need to know in order to become a Tarot reader.You can learn how to tap into your subconscious and use the clues in the cards to develop your intuition leading you to greater awareness and enlightenment. It is simply about learning to see the patterns of your life as revealed in the turn of a card. Topics covered include choosing the right deck to performing your first reading and much more.
