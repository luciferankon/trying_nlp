Five friends decide to spend their college spring break in Florida and fly down to Miami in their small private plane. While circling the the airport waiting to land they fly into 'The Bermuda Triangle" and suddenly find themselves on a island invested with zombies and only way to escape the island. But tempers rise between the group as they panic and struggle to understand each other. They meet another man on the island who has his own problems. They must learn to trust each other to survive.
