Jester'Z Improv Comedy Live Vol 1 is a family friendly show featuring improv style theater games stand-up comedy and improvisational music performed in front of a live audience. Featuring scene-based short-form Jester'Z tackle everything popular including current events pop culture and historical hot topics. Great fun for all ages. Starring Matt Meese (from Studio C and Saints and Soldiers: The Void) and hosted by Jeff Rawls.
