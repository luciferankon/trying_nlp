Billo is accosted by Dilawar and his stooge Naag. She is rescued by Zaildaar (village chief) Jora Singh who injures Dilawar in the process. His embarrassment is too great and he commits suicide in front of his brother Zaildaar Ranjot Singh vowing him to kill Jora and marry Billo. Ranjot slaughters Billo's family and comes after Jora for revenge. But Billo moves into Jora's home for sanctuary and they are to be married.
