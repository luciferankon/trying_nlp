Dancin' On Air is Philadelphia's longest running dance/reality TV show where the areas most talented and attractive people get to dance sing and show their stuff. The legendary show is now the basis for a Reality Television Series produced by Relativity Television ("Catfish") for the Fuse Television Network seen in 171 million homes.
