Amnesiac Agent Edward Hallow awakens from a five-year absence and goes on a mission to rediscover who he really is. Set against the backdrop of a futuristic town known as Meta City 'Amnesique' is a psychedelic musical caught between a surreal dream and a bleak reality. The question is whose dream and reality is this? 'Mais cette fois je me rappellerai tout'. (But this time I will remember everything).
