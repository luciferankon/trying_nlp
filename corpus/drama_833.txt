A coming of age story about love loss and revenge centers around two teenage friends Adam ('Vladimír Polívka' (qv)) and Marek ('Jan Cina' (qv)) whose aimless lives in a small town are suddenly disrupted by the appearance of Anna ('Johana Matousková' (qv)) the troubled daughter of a rich and influential local businessman. Initially her free spirit energizes Adam but soon he finds himself thrown into a spiraling chain of events. His innocence is about to be abruptly replaced with the adult emotions of guilt fear and revenge.
