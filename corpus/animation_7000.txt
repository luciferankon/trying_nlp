A peaceful kitchen is turned on its head when its guardian is tragically damaged and sent to the foundry to be RECYCLED. BARTON a diabolical FISH KNIFE seizes the opportunity to take the kitchen and plots to rid it of its inferior REGULAR SET. The REGULAR SETS only hope is JOHNNY an abandoned CANTEEN SPOON. JOHNNY will need to summon all of his courage and embark on a treacherous journey to rescue the guardian before BARTON can complete his evil plan.
