A primitive subclass tradesman who is shipwrecked for MONTHS in space finds purpose to his life in the form of revenge when his company blatantly fails to rescue him. As a result he destroys every one of his internal barriers to greatness acquiring talents and unlocking the unimaginable potential of the human mind without ever losing his primitive base simplistic identity. Both hero and villain Gully Foyle represents the terrifying magnitude of consequence and drive leaving a wake of destruction in his single-minded pursuit while simultaneously sending the human race barreling down the terrifying gauntlet of progress.
