Sharlene Vs. was an MTV News Canada music travel series that took viewers beyond where the tourists go. In each city Sharlene met artists of all kinds who took her under their wing showing off unique off-the-beaten-track spots special to them and the personal stories behind each location. Cities explored in the series included Copenhagen Hong Kong Austin Houston Lyon London Isle of Wight Reykjavik and Lisbon and aired in prime-time on MTV Canada.
