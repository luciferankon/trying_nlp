Warrior Moms - 6 women on a mission for CHANGE. Beyond the glitz and glamor of Hollywood this film is an unpredictable mother's journey full of extreme highs and spirit breaking lows as 6 famous women risk it all to embark on a mission to dispel the myths of the "A" word by changing the narrative changing legislation and redefining the meaning of Motherhood -- all the way to the White House. Starring: Jacqueline Laurita (RHONJ) Dawn Neufeld (former VH1 star of Football Wives) Jodi Gomes (Exec Producer and National Advocate) Areva Martin (Atty and CNN Regular on Dr. Drew/Anderson 360) and Maura West (2012 and2015 Best Actress Daytime Emmy Winner)
