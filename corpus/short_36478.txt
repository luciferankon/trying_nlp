Mohamed & Amir are colleague and friends. They are both entwined in a psychological war between them. Amir an introvert is bad at expressing his feelings and Mohamed takes full advantage of it. Mohamed humiliates Amir continuously and makes sure that Amir's self esteem is completely shattered. Amir cant express his anger and his rage piles up to a point where he decides to kill Mohamed. But how is he going to kill him?
