From producer and director Chuck Rayner comes the TV series "Heroes of The Street" based on real life paramedic events. This riveting drama will take you on an adrenaline fueled ride as you follow these unsung heroes who save lives everyday. Audiences will experience the hope that paramedics give to the sick and injured as they deal with the stress of the job. This will be a feel good show that everyone can relate to and have a better understanding of the paramedic's emotions on the job as well as their personal lives. This is sure to be a fantastic show that will make you laugh cry and cheer for your heroes!
