7 years ago the world watched in horror as the NYPD was put on trial for the shooting of Sean Bell and Amadou Diallo. Among chants of "No Justice!" "No Peace!" the chants outside the courtroom rocked the world but was justice served? In this sequel to "If I Die Tonight" the cameras follow the next seven years of these cases presenting both sides to each story in an effort to find the truth and culminating in the trials of the officers involved. How far has our country really come?
