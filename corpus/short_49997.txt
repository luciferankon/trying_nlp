Diagnosed at 19 years old with schizoaffective disorder - which has been described as the 'unhappy marriage of bipolar and schizophrenia' - Kevin Nolan began writing music to regain some of the freedom he'd lost due to his illness. 'Hum' is an intimate and music-fueled tour of Kevin's world.
