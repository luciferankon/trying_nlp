Julie is a gentle peaceful woman who would never hurt a fly. She would probably cry if she saw a fly get hurt. Julie is a poet painter and lover of art. Always with her trademark smile Julie loves the earth and all that grows from it. But darkness creeps into Julie's beautiful world. When things go bad and life gets dark through her darkness her love prevails. This film is a creative mind-bending drama with strong acting and a surreal storyline. This is the feature film debut from writer/director Eric Mattson.
