What is the price of power? The third film in the landmark documentary series on leadership 'The Price of Kings: Oscar Arias' takes an in depth look at the life of the little known ex-President of Costa Rica a man who won the Nobel Peace Prize in 1987 for his tireless work to achieve an end to violence and conflict in South America through diplomacy and disarmament. Serving as President of Costa Rica on two occasions Oscar Arias' eight years in power came at a personal cost. The film is built around revealing interviews with Oscar Arias and the people who knew him best; his comrades confidants and his adversaries to reveal the sacrifices made and the price paid for bringing peace to his people.
