One Boy's Dream follows the inspirational story of Matt Perkins an ordinary down to earth guy as he builds his much loved Honda CB500 up to a competitive standard. In this film Mat aims to take his bike to three of Britain's greatest circuits with the hope of someday getting into racing. One Boy's Dream was created to show how you can get the most out of your motorcycle on a realistic budget and that even a basic bike can be competitive. With a little money and a lot of hard work it's possible to create something truly special.
