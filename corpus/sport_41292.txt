UFC 217 held at Madison Square Gardens New York on November 4 2017 featuring 3 title fights on the main card including Georges St Pierre returning from a 4 year hiatus against the reigning middleweight champion Michael Bisping as the main event for the UFC Middleweight Title. Other bouts included: Cody Garbrandt vs TJ Dillashaw bantamweight title bout. Rose Namajunas vs Joanna Jedrejczyk strawweight title bout. Stephen Thompson vs Jorge Masvidal welterweight bout. Paulo Borrachinha vs Johny Hendricks middleweight bout.
