Ted's gambling problem is taking a toll on his relationship his finances and his sanity. He owes a lot of money that he can't pay back and he's at the end of his rope. Out of desperation Ted makes a deal with some shady characters for a large sum of money. All he has to do is follow 3 simple rules. But once the rules are broken his girlfriend disappears people begin to follow him and strange phone calls coming from his own home number drive him into paranoia. Ted must own up to his debts and pay the ultimate price.
