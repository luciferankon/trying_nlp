Blasternaut and his robotic friend Spot are about to embark on a journey across space but they find that their ship's engine isn't working. Blasternaut beams Spot out for repairs while he goes below deck to check on the Polytronic Combustion Regulator. Spot is abducted by the Trash Alien. When Blasternaut comes back up Spot is missing. He contacts the Galactic Commander and she sends him on a mission to rescue Spot.
