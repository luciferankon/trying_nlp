Alma a secular Jewish lady stars in a popular Israeli TV show. She's immediately attracted to Ben a new roommate who's aloof mercurial and also secular. As he starts getting drawn to the life of faith by a rabbi he tries to persuade Alma who has become his new girlfriend to also consider it. After setting this stage the TV series explores Alma's skepticism about the religious life her interactions with those who practice it and whether the couple should remain secular pursue a life of faith together or break up. Several people tug her in different directions: her boyfriend a Jewish man who lost his faith the rabbi his enthusiastic followers and her skeptical parents. How can Alma's and Ben's fragile romance possibly survive these forces?
