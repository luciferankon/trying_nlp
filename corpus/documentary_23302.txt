This is a different film about the Romanians a film in which they do not whine and do not beg. The Romanians from the village of Orizare Sliven region and from the village of Marchevo -situated some 300 km away in Gotze Delchev region are trying to take a control over their fate regardless of the hardships encountered. Taking the challenges that modern life faces them with they are trying to achieve their goal - to squeeze out of ocean of desperation without losing face.
