Kali Amar Ma' is the story of Lakshmi who loses her mother right after her birth. But Goddess Kali becomes her mother and protects her from every problem. Mother Kali protects her from her step-mother. Goddess kali also helps her get married. Problems again come into Lakshmi's life when her husband's life is at risk. But her devotion and prayers bring him back from the jaws of death.
