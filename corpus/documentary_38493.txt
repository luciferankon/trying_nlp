A different way to look at the meaning of war through the bond between Franco Nerozzi a former war journalist and free soldier who turned himself into a volunteer of the human rights and Nerdah Mya colonel of the Karen National Liberation Army an ethnic group which lives in the jungle of Burma. Along with two medics Franco crosses illegally the border between Thailand and Burma to bring aids to the Karen people while Nerdah son of the national hero is morally obligated to fight for the freedom of his people against the Burmese Military regime.
