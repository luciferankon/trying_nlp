The year is 6 B.C. Many years had passed since Daniel's prophecies and most had forgotten the prophecies of a coming messiah. Mankind had strayed away from God's way. Evil was prevailing. It was now time. God will send his son to cleanse mankind of their sins. The prophecy was about to be fulfilled. Journey back to Holy Land and witness three epic events of New Testament- Nativity Baptism & Three Temptations. Embark on an amazing visual treat!
