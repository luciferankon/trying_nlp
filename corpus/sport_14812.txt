Episode One of USL Breakaway will feature in part a look at the Super Y-League ODP players who visited the West Ham Academy as part of USL's partnership with the EPL club an interview with USL Executive Vice President and COO Tim Holt and a look at the double duty role that Colin Clarke plays in Puerto Rico as head coach of the USL-1 Islanders and the national team. Also in the show will be a USL-2 fan feature a look at the Chicago Fire PDL program and the Women's Soccer Celebration in Colorado.
