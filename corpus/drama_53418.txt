Renee Eaves inspirational true life story of her fight for justice against a corrupt Queensland police force. After being crowned Miss Bikini World she catches the ominous eye of a local police officer who's attentions over the next four years turn into a sinister case of stalking. Renee's life is torn apart and sent plunging into a downward spiral left homeless with a young child fighting against what seems an impossible foe a cancer scare ensues and just at her darkest hour she decides to push back. Renee turns her pain and anguish into determination and in a David-and-Goliath-style quest for justice she successfully self-represents herself in court against Australia's top legal counsel and in doing so this single mother became the first Australian woman to successfully sue the police force for damages and overturn a 100 year legal precedent.
