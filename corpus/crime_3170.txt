Fabrice Lombard has neither the means nor the looks to attract the female species. Whether it's on the billboards in the street on commercials on TV or walking down the Avenue des Champs-Élysées everywhere he looks he sees beautiful women he could never approach. Constant humiliation from society and a long drift into solitude are slowly turning his murder fantasies into an irrepressible impulse and Fabrice Lombard into a serial killer...
