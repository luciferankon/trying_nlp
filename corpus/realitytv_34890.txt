M2D's Livin' the Dream is a new outdoor program that combines the lives and dreams of Sparky a professional Firefighter his team and viewers. Join M2D as we rip across North America hunting fishing and having a great time while the cameras roll. When tearing it up on the road the team enjoys visiting with local firefighters for lunch good company and tales about the big one that got away. Most segments will have cooking tips from firehouse kitchens across North America. Its fun crazy exciting and its REAL! From the big ones that get way to the big ones that go on the wall Sparky and team bring out the true camaraderie of fellow hunters fisherman and firefighters! Join Team M2D as we make our dreams come true!
