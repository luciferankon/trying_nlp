Craig and Jane Hamilton-Parker a husband and wife psychic team together with their two daughters as well as extended aunts uncles grandmother and great grandmother are Britain's only psychic family. They're the stars of Our Psychic Family which sees them meet Angie Best former wife of football legend George Best for a personal reading - as well as giving advice from beyond the grave to Eighties pop star Toyah Wilcox. Our Psychic Family features everything from haunted houses to psychic pet clinics from a psychic inspired dating event to a search for a psychic apprentice plus the pressures of putting on their own theatre event.
