Mr. Raja who experienced his mother death due to poverty become rich through hard working. He believes that only money can bring happiness in life. Alam his friend believes love is the ultimate happiness and loves Nila. Raja also likes Nila and wants her as his wife. But NIla dislikes him for his pride and decides to marry Alam. Alam had an accident on marriage day and Nila goes to Raja for help. Raja's belief regarding money deepens. Will this belief diminish?
