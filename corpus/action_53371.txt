There is a race to control the middle eastern power grid and the US has come under fire being accused of eliminating several top Arab nation officials during a secret summit meeting. Back in the US a group of professionals within their respective fields are being hired by a billionaire to help thwart an imminent attack on American soil with the help of technologies never before seen. This is a story about who or what really controls who and the outcome will certainly set the way of the future. Technology as we know it will never be the same.
