'Purged' is a comedy about three colleagues who have been forced to work their time off in the café; of the afterlife. Fed up with their punishment of one million hours and the bureaucracy of the system that's keeping them there two of the café; employee's decide to take action and hold Jesus hostage to God. Meanwhile God sits in a boardroom watching on in horror. With no other choice God picks up the phone and gives Satan a call to try to work out a plan to get back at these two miscreants.
