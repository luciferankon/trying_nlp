Racing drivers and Fifth Gear presenter Tiff Needell takes you inside the world of the high speed crash for a power sport extravaganza of mad moments mistakes and mayhem. Motorcycles Offshore Powerboats Rally cross JetSki Motocross Formula 1 Powerboats and Super trucks are just some of the amazing machines you will see at the max in this film. Featuring exclusive footage of a fatal crash involving Hellbender identical sister car to Vampire the 300 mph dragster that nearly killed Richard Hammond. Plus join Vicki Butler-Henderson as she experiences racing an offshore powerboat and having to escape from an upturned submerged cabin. Plus a unique DVD extra feature will take a series of high speed thrill rides round some of the greatest circuits on some of the most powerful bikes in the world.
