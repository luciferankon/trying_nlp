Sureyya Cahit lost her family in the earthquake in Adapazari 1967. Sureyya who was in love with Cemal had to move in her aunt's house in Ankara. Cemal visits Sureyya frequently but one day he is arrested and went to prison. They had to become seperated from each other. After years passed Sureyya's daughter will fall in love with Cemal's son and former lovers path will cross again.
