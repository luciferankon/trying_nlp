How do you define something that by definition has no rules and won't easily be defined? That's exactly what "OnCreativity" sets out to do. OnCreativity is a series of short informal interviews with a diverse range of designers artists musicians animators educators etc. It explores a single topic: Creativity. With the differing points of view expressed by each person in their own words OnCreativity hopes to inspire confuse provoke and delight. From Academy Award-nominated filmmaker Bill Plympton to original Mad Man George Lois internationally known graffiti artist (and founder of KRINK) KR to world-renowned graphic designer Milton Glaser OnCreativity asks a specific set of questions to a wide range of people and looks for patterns. Each person's story and relationship to creativity is unique all of their stories and observations are intimate and insightful.
