We The Voters presents the subjects of democracy elections and U.S. governance in a new accessible non-partisan format. 20 viral films are designed to inspires and activates an audience of millions. The project incorporates real characters dynamic storylines and celebrity influencers into films apps and games that demystify how the government works and motivate Americans to seize the power of their vote. Initially launched during the 2016 Presidential race the project continues to encourage voters to make informed choices by providing context for the urgent issues unfolding across America.
