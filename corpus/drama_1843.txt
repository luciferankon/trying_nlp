A coming of age romantic underdog drama about Morris Dwann a millionaire terrified by emotional connections who drifts through a systematic routine of job interviews searching for an understanding of his workaholic father. Forced to take a job Morris will have to ultimately accept himself and those around him in order to defy his father and discover what life is really about.
