As the talented but silent conductor of the Bravo Youth Orchestra Takt you must save the Concert Hall from destruction. With your conducting skills you must impress the people of Bravo Town with your classical musical performance. Aid with the help of a mysterious fairy Symphony this is your only chance to make the people of Bravo Town like classical music. Along with this you will recruit various characters from their performance to join you with your music until you reach the Concert Hall for the final showdown to joyfulness of classical music. Will you succeed?
