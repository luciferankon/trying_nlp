Chuck works at a dead-end job and plays video games all the time. He mainly ignores the wants and desires of his girlfriend Rosie. But when she gets pregnant Chuck refuses to change and make their life better. But then Rosie walks out on him Chuck loses his job and Chuck's friends ignore him. Having lost it all and hitting rock bottom Chuck must fight for his life to get back everything he holds dear. This adventure takes him to battling drug dealers hippie protesters mad scientists insane occult leaders and the Devil himself.
