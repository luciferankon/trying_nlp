Bad girl Allison brings British online date Darren to the house party but becomes convinced she must break up with him because she thinks he's too nice and she can't be "herself" - nasty and unpleasant - around him. Unbeknownst to Allison Darren is terrorizing her friends with his somewhat maniacal behaviour. Allison concocts plans to show Darren how much of a bad girl she really is.
