This is a story about a man named Adam that is a victim of today's society.He is subjected to many obstacles that outbalances his life.The same obstacles that many people face today in modern big cities. Some of these obstacles include financial difficulties being part of a broken family and stress and anxiety.Ultimatelyhe looses his house to the bank causing his marriage to fail. Eventuallyhe decides to move out the cityin a very beautiful forest.There he meets a man that helps him find the answers within himself.The power of nature and its beauty starts to affect Adam's mind.He develops a very peaceful state of mind which ultimately balances his life back.He finds his inner peace.He reaches the light on the other side of the tunnel.
