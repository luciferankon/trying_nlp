Set in a future in which robots are as ubiquitous as pets are today and if you don't make your robot loan payments your mechanical best friend will be repossessed by Lee a repo agent who refuses to see robots as sentient individuals and has built up large debts betting on robo-cockfights. Lee's latest repo job takes him to the doorstep of Mike and Max the former a man who has lost everything in a unspecified tragedy and the latter an adorable companion robot who would do anything for his master. Together the two men and the little robot will end up taking down the repo business entirely.
