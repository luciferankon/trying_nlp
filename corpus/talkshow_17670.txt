Ariana Grande took a break from ruling the charts to drop by MTV's iconic Times Square studio for "Total Ariana Live" an all-Ariana themed 30-minute "TRL" special hosted by Sway Calloway. The pop superstar performed an intimate stripped down version of her #1 smash hit "Problem" AND debuted her brand new single "Break Free." On top of it all she also announced her upcoming VMA performance and went Broadway style on a hip-hop jam.
