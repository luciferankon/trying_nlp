A short fictive story about the lighthouse keeper Thorleif who lives alone in a lighthouse on an island in Norway. His biggest desire is to get a new social life but his current life situation is an obstacle for himself: He's living alone on an isolated island the lighthouse needs maintenance he's getting older and he hasn't had any human friend for a long time. Suddenly one day he gets a letter from the Norwegian Coastal Authority. They've started planning the automation of lighthouses around in Norway which means he's being let go. Thorleif sees new hope for a good life at the end of his days. But what about his connection to the lighthouse?
