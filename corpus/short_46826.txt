'Ikhtiyaar' is a film about a small town girl in India named Shaihnaz. It is a love story based on Polygamy. This film shows inner conflict of Shaihnaz who has seen her mother suffer throughout her life because of indifferent behavior of her father. Shaihnaz runs away from her father's house to her aunt's place in Delhi and meets the love of her life 'Aarav'. When a ray of happiness just comes in her life a truth is revealed to her and her life comes back to square one.
