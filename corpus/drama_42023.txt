The story Follows Reid Morrison a down and out musician who tries to escape his hometown mistakes and start over in New Orleans. Thinking the mistakes he has made will never be forgiven he attempts to seclude himself from anything worth while. After landing a job as the lead singer of a local bar's band Reid meets a group of people; including the girl of his dreams. They teach him that the only person he needs forgiveness from is himself. When his dark past comes back to haunt him and threatens to ruin everything he has worked so hard to build Reid must quickly decide what is more important: his addiction or his soulmate.
