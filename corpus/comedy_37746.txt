Can sisterhood survive the "Hollywood Machine"? Denise Monica Kenya and Roxy are about to find out. Four women who came to the city with big dreams are waking up to a huge nightmare...There might not be enough room in this town for all of them. As they hustle through LA LA land mastering the art of the audition the industry party and the callback it becomes evident the ladies might have to master something else...letting go of each other. Good Luck!
