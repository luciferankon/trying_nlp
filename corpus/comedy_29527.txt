Nicole Leduc a coed and three of her friends prepare for one weekend of play and love with four "beautiful" men fellow students from her university. The men arrive but George and Mariette Leduc (parents of Nicole) and Junior (her brother) arrive home early which complicates matters leading to funny situations. Nicole and her friends carry on their loving over the weekend and the film climaxes with an orgy of love. A light hearted look at the joy in life of young Quebecers today.
