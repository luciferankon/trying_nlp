Prepare for a fun-filled emotional journey with 52 young musicians from a variety of backgrounds. Though they have no formal training the Louisville Leopard Percussionists come together with the shared desire to learn how to play music and perform as a group under the leadership of their founder and teacher Diane Downs. This delightful film - the final installment in HBO's acclaimed 'The Music In Me' specials - profiles the young members of the Leopards as they embark on a trip from their Kentucky home to the Big Apple!
