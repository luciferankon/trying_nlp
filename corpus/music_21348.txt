In each episode Red Beard scours the Southern California music scene to find just the hottest up and coming rock acts anywhere. Then he invites them to come do a private invitation only concert in his North Hollywood studio. Then with the studio audience comprised of invited guests industry insiders and some selected band fans the show begins. Red always invites some of his Rock Star friends along for the journey. After the band performs Red Beard interviews his Rock Star guests that come to see the in studio band. One thing you can be sure of it is a good time. Come kick it with one of L.A.'s coolest music lovers!
