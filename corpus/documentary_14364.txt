Biography of the late author/actor/filmmaker 'Kenneth Anger (I)' (qv) covering his early life as a child actor up through his career as an author ("Hollywood Babylon") and avant-garde filmmaker (_Scorpio Rising (1963)_ (qv) _Invocation of My Demon Brother (1969)_ (qv)). Included are interviews with friends and colleagues and archival footage of Anger as well as clips from some of his films.
