Bret 19 yearns to leave his Connecticut hometown and head off to college. He figures summer will be fun with his buddies but as the days progress the situations he finds himself in spiral out of control. He has run-ins with the police problems with his girlfriend involving another girl and a revenge attack he never should have involved himself in.
