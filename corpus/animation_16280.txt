From the information highways of cyberspace hails Twipsy a cyber messenger who manages the world's emails and data traveling from end to ends of the World Wide Web to deliver them to the right people online. People like 13-year old Nick 8-year old Lissie and friend Albert who experience the joys and thrill of the World Wide Web. While Twipsy flees from shady villains willing to corrupt the data he sends Nick accidentally ends up whisked away into the virtual realm of cyberspace and arriving face to face with Twipsy. Thrills and adventure ensue between the cyber world and the real world.
