Two Con Artist from the Southside of Chicago decide to play the rocks con on an unsuspecting jewelry store owner named Simon Balagula. Upon finding out that the South African Red diamonds are fake Simon has a severe heart attack. As fate would have it Balagula turns out to be the uncle of Russian Mafia Captain named Vor the Brigadier. Fearing the 14 day murder contract on their head the con-artist make an escape to Atlanta hoping to hide out. But when Vor sends the sexually sadistic contract killer Butcherknife Brown down south to serve out the hit their con-skills truly get put to the test in a deadly rush to pay 2 million dollars in restitution. The fears and consequences of the con life are explored when Two Con-Artist from the Southside of Chicago Hustle the Russian Mafia. Their street skills are put to the test when they are given 14 days to pay 2 Million dollars in restitution.
