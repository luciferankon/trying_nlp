In this documentary about low-budget filmmaking in upstate New York you'll learn how affordable digital-video technology has changed the lives of the artists behind action flicks monster movies nonfiction stories and comedies. "Every Pixel Tells a Story" introduces viewers to a wide range of independent filmmakers all of whom prove that with a little ingenuity access to the right technology and plenty of tenacity filmmakers can still practice their craft 3000 miles from Hollywood. In fact "Every Pixel Tells a Story" is an example of what can be accomplished on digital video. Producer-director Peter Hanson shot and edited the movie in a matter of weeks using a camcorder a computer editing system and a $30 microphone from Radio Shack all while spending a fraction of what the documentary would have cost had it been shot on film.
