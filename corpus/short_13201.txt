Enrico recently immigrated from Rome and finds himself working in a small Italian bakery under the strict supervision of his boss. He soon meets Marzia the boss's daughter and the attraction is instant. One night Marzia returns to the bakery to find Enrico with another woman and a nude one at that. She quickly discovers Enrico's passion for decorating the female body and after some convincing from Enrico she returns to the bakery one night to see his sensual art form with her own eyes.
