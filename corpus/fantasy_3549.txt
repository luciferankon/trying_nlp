Take Everything is a movie series which gives a futuristic look at a group of 5 partners belonging to a secret society known as "Original Chamber" that specialize in high level heists. The group consists of descendants of notorious robbery factions that existed dating back to the early Egyptian civilization. The group's current mission is to rob the world's largest and most secure casino located in Las Vegas Nevada without being caught. Take Everything set in 2109 is the ultimate fusion of organized crime and futuristic technology.
