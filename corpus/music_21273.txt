Bongo Boy Rock n' Roll TV (BBRR TV) is a media outlet that gives indie outlets of all genres an opportunity to have their music videos seen in thousands of homes across the country. Produced by music connoisseur and Bongo Boy Records founder Monique Grimme and musician Gar Francis of The Doughboys the New Jersey-based company has expanded to nearly two dozen syndicated stations. Whereas other cable access shows place artists in a rotation within a limited viewing area BBRR TV places their artists on a six days per week rotation in Manhattan Nashville Los Alamos and other metropolitan areas.
