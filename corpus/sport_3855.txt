Hosts Adem Martin del Campo and Austin Wesolowski show you all the steps to turn a stock truck into a capable off-road vehicle. Prerunners are a style of truck that is bred from off-road racing where long travel suspension is added to stock trucks to pre-run the race course. Prerunners have crossed over from desert racing into a popular way of customizing any truck - they can be driven on the streets and off-road. Prerunners used to be seen only in the desert Southwest but they're capability off-road has taken them everywhere - no matter the terrain. With step-by-step fabrication bolt-on modifications and cool tools FullDroopTV sets to educate and entertain fans of off-road sports.
