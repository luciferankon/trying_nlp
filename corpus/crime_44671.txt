After double crossing local gangster Jack Crooks abusive husband Lionel knows that the net is closing in on him. Henchmen Eddie Bell and Percival Sullivan have got him in their sights and it's only a matter of time before they 'turn his lights out' for good! His long suffering wife Peaches also has her mind set on his demise and with the help of her friend and the weird and eccentric Dr Henrick Oldcorn she sets the wheels in motion for his eradication. Lionel knows that his days are numbered but he isn't about to hang about to see who gets him first and he quickly plans his escape. Disgusting man Lionel has a problem. His long suffering wife wants rid and after double crossing a local gangster he finds himself in a position that is detrimental to his well being. He tries to plan his escape will he get away in time?
