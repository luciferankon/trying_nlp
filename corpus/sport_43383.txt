Ahead of the 2015 Capital One Cup final Tottenham Hotspur vs Chelsea Spurs TV broadcast a live broadcast on their official Facebook page and tottenhamhotspur.com to the millions of fans around the world. The Matchday Show was presented by Daniel van der Molen and co-hosted by Ledley King the last player to captain Spurs to Wembley glory in 2008 and features a host of engaging build-up content. Highlights include interviews with Chairman Daniel Levy members of the 2008 League Cup-winning team such as Jonathan Woodgate and Jermaine Jenas celebrity fans and current players including an exclusive with popular striker Harry Kane. Ledley King now a Club Ambassador will also answer questions sent in by fans via Facebook on air during the show.
