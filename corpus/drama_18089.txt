Gabriel goes back to his hometown to pay a short visit to his mother. While he's there he has a minor accident and breaks his arm. The doctor puts a cast on it and tells him that if he wants to avoid surgery he should stay in town to avoid traveling until his arm has started to heal. So Gabriel must stay in town longer than expected. While he's there he runs into Camilo a boy he went to school with. They haven't seen each other in ten years.
