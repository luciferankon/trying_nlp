This is the story of Jesus as never told before. This epic story spans twenty centuries. Beginning with the birth of his mother Mary onto the miraculous birth of Jesus himself the story follows Jesus through his ministry his miracles his betrayal and his lifting up to Heaven by God. The story takes us to the future with the return of Jesus and his final earth-shaking conflict with the Anti-Christ. It is an historical drama chronicling one of the most inspiring and fascinating figures in human history.
