From the creators of "Young Blood: Evil Intentions" comes an epic Sci-Fi adventure you don't want to miss. When a small town is plagued by killer cicadas for them it seems hopeless. After two dimwitted servicemen spray the cicadas with chemicals mistaken for pesticide these little buggers mutate to an uncontrollable size. Soon the planet is in peril as these colossal cicadas effortlessly demolish buildings and crush poor citizens. It seems there is only one man who can stop them before it's too late. Will he be able to save the day? Or will the world fall victim to the "Invasion of the Killer Cicadas"?
