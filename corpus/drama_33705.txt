An English governess marries a complete stranger while he is on his deathbed. When he dies she inherits his estate but is soon involved with some French spies working for Napoleon. Where should her loyalties lie? Helena an impoverished formerly wealthy and privileged member of the upper class arrives at the wrong manor where she thought she would become a governess. She is almost immediately whisked away by Lord Carlyon cousin of the owner to an inn where the real owner (his cousin) is dying after being stabbed by Carlyon's brother Nick. He convinces Helena to marry the dying cousin to save Nick from hanging. Upon inheritance of the house she discovers her dead husband had been utilizing his estate as the base for smuggling in French goods and government information to Napoleon...
