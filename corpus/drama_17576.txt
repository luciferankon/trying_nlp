Long widowed mother dedicated her life to upbringing of her daughter Renata. After Renata graduated from high school her mother decides to find love and gets married again but Renata does not approve mother's choice. "New Dad" gives his stepdaughter not quite fatherly looks but mother doesn't want to notice it. Renata leaves home and goes to her best friend Malika unaware that Malika is seeing Renata's stepfather. Where can one find support when vices prevail over intimates? This is a story of how power money and hatred break people's lives.
