College dropout Kudo was approached with a job opportunity overseas by chance. He accepts the opportunity to get out of his days in a rut but becomes involved in a crime caused by an old friend Yano whom Kudo reunites with by coincidence. Kudo and Yano must run away from their chasers. Can they leave the town safely? From moratorium to getaway. Switching between comedy and tragedy at an accelerated pace. This is a youthful action-packed film.
