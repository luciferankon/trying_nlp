James is a guy with no future desperately seeking a possible career. He stumbles upon a popular local band Afterlife and proposes the creation of a documentary. With the help of his friend Bryan they spend a few days together trying to put on film what the band is able to capture with music. Everything is starting to look up for everyone involved . . . and that's when everything goes wrong. As James Bryan and the band discover dreams rarely come true and life is only what you make of it.
