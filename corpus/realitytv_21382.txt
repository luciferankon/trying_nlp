Psychic Challenge is a reality show in which 10 people pretending to have supernatural abilities are put to series of challenges to examine their actual skills. The competitors will have to cope with variety of tasks to prove that their success is due to a talent not to ingenuity luck or just guessing. The talent of the competitors is supervised by jury made up of 3 "skeptics". They watch closely and analyze how the clairvoyants manage the challenges. The world-famous illusionist Astor a professional psychologist and a former examining magistrate are the people who decide which of the clairvoyants must leave the show. The original TV format is English and by now it is realized with great success in England Israel Holland Russia Ukraine Germany and USA. The show was first broadcasted in 2006. The license-owner is the British company RDF Rights. The Group have produced a version of the show for the Romanian market.
