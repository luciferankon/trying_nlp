Yo & Mo are two ladies living and loving in London. Both are fast approaching thirty and the reality of being left on the shelf is becoming just that a reality. Determined not to grow old as lonely spinsters with just themselves and a cat for company they decide to go on an all out assault to find their future husbands. For Yo & Mo marriage is for life therefore they are determined to make sure its with the right guy even if it means meeting all the wrong ones!
