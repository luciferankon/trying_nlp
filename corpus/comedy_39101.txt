A wobbly street vendor ('Noel 'Ungga' Ayala' (qv)) an ignorant probinsyano ('Rene Requiestas' (qv)) and an unemployed loafer ('Jimmy Santos (I)' (qv)) meet in a small-time restaurant run by Mama L ('Vangie Labalan' (qv)). Realizing they all have hungry stomachs they hang on to each other. After several attempts in looking for a job they finally sees a solution to their problems the lottery.
