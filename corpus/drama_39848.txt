Rose Davis (Caitlin Blair Thistle) has low expectations when she arrives in the small town of Brookdale New Hampshire. That is until she meets Morgan Thompson (Grady Justice) and discovers a mysterious bond they share - one that goes far beyond their physical attraction. Morgan must teach Rose how to cope with a curse that has plagued Brookdale and "The Clear" for hundreds of years. To avoid hurting the people around them the teens will do their best to handle this curse with caution and awareness. Due to a crazed curmudgeon named Prescott (Eric Eastman) their lives will be turned upside down. As suspicions rise and the appearance of a full moon illuminates their true identities Rose and Morgan must decide whether they are willing to sacrifice their innocence in order to survive. Their love for one another will forever change "The CLEAR".
