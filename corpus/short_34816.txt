The story of Tony and Paula two college buddies who lose touch when they enter the "professional" world. After years of hard work and little progress Tony decides to strike out on his own and start a business and Paula comes along for the ride. But it soon becomes clear that not only is Paula the brains of the operation - she is also falling for Tony a big problem considering her pending marriage to another man. As they pitch investor after investor Tony shows his incompetence and Paula shows her feelings and they begin to ponder the possibilities of what could be - success for him and love for her.
