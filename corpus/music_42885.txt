Choreographer to the stars Tina Landon and her top dancers take you through a complete studio dance session (breaking down the steps) of some of Tina's most iconic and award-winning music video choreography she created for such artists as Jennifer Lopez Shakira Britney Spears Samantha Mumba and many more. DVD also features interviews with Tina and on-camera tributes to Tina Landon from these recording stars.
