The owner of a pawn shop David Woolfson is a banker of last resort. A grouchy old guy with a mushy heart Jewish immigrant from South Africa he's been running the store for nearly 16 years. Not that he needs to. It's just that if he was stuck at home he would go mad. Then one day Chris Hoard ex-convict and psychopath shows up and offers his help. For free. An unlikely friendship develops. However Chris has his own luggage to carry and it is wearing him down.
