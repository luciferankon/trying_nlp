Natalie (Elizabeth Wagner) is a young woman who is running out of money and so she turns to her friend Denise (Lynn Wolf) for help and advice. Denise introduces her to the sleazy but well paid world of naughty lap-dancing - but goes too far drugging Natalie and making it look like they are lesbian lovers. Natalie's world suddenly slips into a spiral of decline where she endures perverted and degrading acts placing her very life at risk.
