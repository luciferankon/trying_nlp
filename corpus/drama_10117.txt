The hardworking gardener Qurban is a married man at his mid-thirties. All he cares about is earning the money to get rid of his rubbish truck and buying a new one. When his employer's son shows up with two girls at the house he works at Qurban gets dragged into their romance unintentionally. Qurban's desire for observation leads to obsession. Hindered by his superego he is left on a limbo.
