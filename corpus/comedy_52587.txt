Case Study explores people's views and opinions of the realities of our relationships such as race relations disease and illness hetero- and homo-sexual relationships loneliness dating sex marriage etc. While Case Study provides an abundance of laughs it is also wrought with emotional twists. Insightful and educational Case Study allows its audience to be the proverbial "fly on the wall" peering into the character's real lives. Viewers also hear directly from the characters as they respond in camera to probing interview questions concerning their own personal relationships. Every audience member views Case Study with a true sense of "I can relate!"
