In the morning of December the 7th 1988 a shift of the Arabic- and Eurasian-tectonic plate were the reason for one of the most devastating earthquakes of the last hundred years. On the basis of lifetime-witnesses reliving the day of the earthquake - visually reconstructed  the haunting images of archive-footage original documentations and latest results of research Dust and Ashes looks back on and reappraise the short- and long-range consequences for the people and environment.
