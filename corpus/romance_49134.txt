Manolis is a fisherman living in Crete. After his break-up with his girlfriend he decides to go to Mykonos and work there in order to forget what happened and make more money to help his sister and her kids. Lina is a youngcoldrich woman and daughter of a shipowner. She is in Mykonos on a cruise with her sloop. Manolis and Lina meet each other for the first time when he tries to sell some fishes to her and they have a big fight. Everything in their lives will change after that.
