I Promise is Pocket Parables' powerful mini-series based on the Tribulation Period spoken of in the book of Revelation. In this 7 part mini-series the drama begins as a massive disappearance has occurred on the earth. New scientific evidence produced by the new charismatic Presidential World Leader Julius Roman tries to disprove the resurrection of Jesus Christ. The faith of those who remain are challenged as they come face to face with the reality that their loved ones always warned them about.
