Two American filmmakers document an Indian guru's trip to the United States. How does one man capture the hearts of countless others and what exactly is his message? East meets West in this eye-opening journey through the minds of an esteemed spiritual leader and his devotees. Two American filmmakers document the visit of an esteemed Indian guru Dada J.P. Vaswani to the United States. Throughout the two-week journey the filmmakers capture how one man captivates the hearts of countless others. A few questions still remain below the surface though: What exactly is Dada's message and why do those who come into contact with him gravitate toward him so strongly? East meets West in this eye-opening journey through the minds of a beloved spiritual leader and his loyal devotees.
