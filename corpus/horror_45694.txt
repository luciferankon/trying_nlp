This film is a supernatural thriller / horror about a college fraternity president who after his frat house is banned from on-campus activities takes his pledges out to undergo their final night of initiation on a remote piece of property he recently inherited after the death of his father. Unaware of a long-buried family scandal associated with the land he unwittingly unearths a deadly supernatural force and what was supposed to be a "pledge night" of innocent fun turns into a nightmare of unrelenting horror.
