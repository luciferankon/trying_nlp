Abhishek and the marriage (WT) is a documentary movie about the young Indian ABHISHEK (37) who does not want to get married and for his family that is a huge problem. Abhishek is designing between tradition and Western modernity an alternative existence by the beach for Western and Indian tourists he builds up a holiday resort in Goa. This he wants to expand also in the Himalayas. If he succeeds in spite of corruption and Mafia to preserve his company and if he is not yet married his girlfriend especially the pressure on him is stronger by the family.
