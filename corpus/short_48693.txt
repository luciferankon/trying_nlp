Toilet is about two brothers' (EDDY and ERIC) whose similar upbringing helps them to unite and thus share each other's fate caning. This entire psychological surreal drama takes place in one day as MOTHER pursuits Eddy for failing his exams after coming out of hiding from the toilet with the cane. Eric elder brother returns home and dashes into the toilet as soon as he could. We find out how the toilet becomes a secret place where secrets are revealed.
