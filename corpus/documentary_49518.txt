In Nairobi Kenya four twentysomethings drop out of university to form an art collective creating a counter narrative to the lives expected of them. Little did they know they would end up becoming one of Kenya's top bands and ride a wave of international acclaim. How do they deal with it? They go to Space. Borrowing on a post-modern cultural mishmash of Sun Ra Kung Fu movies Hip Hop and a thousand other bits of detritus "Just A Band" paints a truer picture of urban Africa than you can imagine.
