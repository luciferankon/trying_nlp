After being sexually harassed by her employer and blacklisted by his jealous wife Jehane sells herself as an indentured servant and survives rape to arrive loathing all men at Edward's - a survivor of the 1622 Massacre. When Edward and his native friend protect her during a native attack she battles her hatred and tries not to judge all men for what a few have done. As Edward's romantic interest grows Jehane's sexual harassment fears rise and her rape-related post traumatic stress disorder peaks. Jehane almost too late realizes that the biggest threats to her life are not men and natives but the jealous women of Edward's past who've been manipulating her.
