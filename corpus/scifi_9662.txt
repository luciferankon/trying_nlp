A science fiction thriller that follows the life of Asher Frost a young born again Christian who finds himself kidnapped by an underground alien conspiracy group along with eight other strangers. Only 7 of them including Asher survive. They learn that they are carrying an alien pathogen that could wipe out not just the human species but other alien species as well. With limited time left Asher and company have to find a cure or face the possibility of being responsible for ending all life on earth.
