Michael Decuir Jacob Zumo and Julien Troullier are three roommates that all share a passion for art and painting. Upon walking into their home you are immediately hit from all angles with oil paintings watercolor sketches pen and ink drawings and more each with their own unique style specific to each artist. This documentary explores the motivations inspirations thoughts and feelings of what it's like being an artist in Baton Rouge from each artist's perspective how living with two others influences each of their creative drives as well as taking an intimate look at much of their work. This film is a mixture of poetry art and music from some of the best Baton Rouge has to offer all coming from one residence. You never know what kind of creativity could be brewing right next door to you.
