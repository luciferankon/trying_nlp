"Doppelt Geschleckt Hält Besser": Petra and the Marion are working as Au Pairs in Switserland. While Marion is away visiting her aunt Petra has a sexual encounter with the couple they are staying with. "Lady Ficker": A posh hunting party is introduced to Mrs. Denning. An orgy ensues in which the butler and maid are allowed to join in. "Biz Zum Letzten Tropfen": Julia visits her Uncle Pennings where aunt Gelinde is throwing a party filled with sex.
