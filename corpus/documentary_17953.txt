Directed by Michael Lynch Sons of the Sea took 6 modern twenty-something Qatari's on a watery adventure to re-discover their roots. Activities included learning to sail an antique Dhow diving for pearls using traditional methods and SCUBA diving on the reefs rigs and wrecks of Qatar. Sons of the Sea follows a team of young Qatari adventurers as they attempt to live the lives of their grandfathers and sail a one hundred year old pearl merchants dhow across the Arabian Sea. Freediving for pearls navigating by stars exploring mysterious ship wrecks they endure the rewards and the challenges of a life at sea where their survival depends only upon themselves.
