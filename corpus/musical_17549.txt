Jermaine Jones is a singer song-writer performer & musical entrepreneur. His smooth bass vocals are captured best when singing R&B Neosoul Jazz & Gospel. Jermaine is a veteran performer having sung in major venues with his latest accomplishment American Idol Top 12. Jermaine NEW single "All Around The World" written & produced by Shavoni Entertainment was released on July 19 2012. Jermaine is also a vocal instructor at "Highest Praise Performing Arts Center" in Atco NJ. and has made his awesome gift known in various Churches and Arenas throughout the East & West Coast of the United States. Jermaine is now in the process of recording his debut CD which will be released in the Spring of 2013.
