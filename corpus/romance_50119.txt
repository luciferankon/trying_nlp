Kyung Tae (Lee Dong Wook) is cold-blooded and perceived to be a good-for-nothing guy. To take care of his dead friend's older sister Moon Sook (Park Joo Mi) and nephew Kang Koo Kyung Tae goes to Ganggu Harbor in Yeongdeok South Korea. He takes care of Kang Koo who is cautious around the unfamiliar Kyung Tae and Kyung Tae falls in love with the ill Moon Sook.
