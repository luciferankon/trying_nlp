Time Is Money is the first movie directed by 'Chin-Jung Kang' (qv). It revolves around the story of Chen Mo (played by 'Chin-Jung Kang' (qv)) who is one of the second generation of rich family. Due to his addiction in gambling he lost everything in which his dad has passed over and this infuriates his old man. His dad left him a painting by painter Chu Zhong Tian (played by 'Mark Lee (X)' (qv)) and it is only worth $100000 Taiwan currency which is not enough to pay off his debt.
