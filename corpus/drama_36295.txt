Hsiao-Chien is a ten-year-old boy nicknamed Beatle. His best buddies are Lady Bug and Bee. Three of them hang out all day and continue to chat on MSN when they go home. One day when Beatle is late for school as usual at the subway station he runs into Red Snail a girl who carries a red schoolbag. Red Snail winks at him. After that day Beatle begins to search for the trails of Red Snail trying to find out why she winked at him.
