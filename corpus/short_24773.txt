The documentary Niche explores the many intricacies of current shark conservation measures. The film is a compilation of personal footage stock footage and interviews with professionals from many backgrounds. Niche is intended to first establish the importance of sharks to the survival of marine ecosystems followed by a unique glance into the complexities of the challenges in current shark conservation measures. What sets Niche apart from other shark conservation films is the representation of diverse perspectives. Bringing the voices of commercial fishermen scientists and conservationists all to the table in order to discuss the future of shark conservation.
