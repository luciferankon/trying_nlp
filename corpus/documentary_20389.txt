This film follows 12-year-old Steven Aghakhani and his father Armik as they race supercars at speeds upwards of 160mph. Steven races in his McLaren 650S against some of the world's fastest supercars while his father coaches and mentors him. This film takes you beyond the internet hype of Steven the 12-year-old racing million dollar sports cars and introduces you to a father who wants nothing but the best for his son and a son who wants nothing more than to make his dad proud.
