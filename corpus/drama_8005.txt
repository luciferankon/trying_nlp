Strawberry Lane is story of Clarissa Dorina Jacq and Lupe that form a friendship in a detention center called "Bahay Bagong Pangarap". Together these four girls will inspire each other to pursue their dreams and to remain hopeful no matter what life has in store for them. Witness how their lives eventually turn out to be intertwined as the story progresses. Discover what awaits the three girls in their journey to a new life a new beginning with their new family and friends.
