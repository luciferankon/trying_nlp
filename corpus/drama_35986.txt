This is a story about 2 persons meeting from different walks of life come together. They eventually fall in love with each other and when the million dollar question is raised - "marriage" the boy decides against it and suggests they should live together first. (Live in relationships are not common in India and is in fact shunned by society). The girl is stunned but eventually with lot of reluctance agrees. The rest of the story is about the difficulties and harassment they face together from parents society friends etc - and how they handle it and what happens of it.
