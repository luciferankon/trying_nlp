Cheetay follows the often ill fated but always funny misadventures of three friends (Adeel Jaleel and Shakeel). Shakeel is a wheeler dealer always looking to make a fast buck. His failed ventures always taking a toll on his faithful wife Hunya and their son. Jaleel is sub-human and is probably the missing link. His antics are a constant source of misery for his fiery hot tempered wife Sana. Adeel is a disgraced TV chef with an eye for the ladies. His philandering ways often land him into trouble. The object of his affection is Dr. Saba Much to her disdain.
