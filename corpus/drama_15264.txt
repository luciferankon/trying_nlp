The story of the women of Vietnam. As a follow-up film to The Battle of Ripcord (2013) we take an intimate look into the lives of those left behind outcast from their friends families and communities as the world moved on. The women of Vietnam were in waiting while their husbands brothers sons and fathers fault and died in a distant land. During a time of social revolution the civil rights movement and uncertainty these brave women could do nothing but wait pray and hope that they would see their loved ones again. This is their story.
