When roommates Cat (Alexa Berry) Markie (Brianna Endrina) and Jo (Jessica O'Brian) find themselves struggling through a collective "Quarter-Life" crisis they decide that starting an Adult Call Service is the perfect "means to an end." After all no one has to know about it so what could possibly go wrong? As the girls dive deeper into their inexperienced business venture the lines been professional and personal begin to blur leaving them to ponder where they begin and their on-phone alter-egos' end?
