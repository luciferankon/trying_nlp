The Store Manager is used to ignoring the many complaints filed by his customers. However the Manager has met his match in Mrs. Moffat who sneaks into his office and demands to be heard. Beginning with an unnatural animal attack she witnessed as a child every trauma in Mrs. Moffat's life appears to be directly linked to the Store. The Manager tries to placate her as the daily operations of the Store run out of control but Mrs. Moffat intends to raise a good old fashioned stink...
