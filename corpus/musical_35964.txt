Summer is an experienced local singer having played the gamut of Los Angeles indie music venues for years. She's had her share of let downs; playing empty venues and believing the false promises of music executives. Her younger cousin Michelle has just moved to LA straight from college. With her undeniable amount of talent almost instantly Michelle receives accolades for her musicianship. As the girls struggle to find their "sound"  and what it means to be an artist they are inevitably pitted against each other in their quest to find their place in the music industry.
