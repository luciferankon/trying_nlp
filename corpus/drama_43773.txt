Change... that's what Marty is about to experience. After meeting Samara Marty leaves his bohemian life behind as he falls deeply in love with her. As a result of her parent's death Samara is suffering from temporary amnesia. Will she be the same person that fell in love with Marty when she recovers? Meanwhile Denise Samara's best friend is amidst a love triangle. Matt her old boyfriend wants to get back with her not knowing that she's secretly involved with his own brother Steven. When you think you have it all figured out life shows you different. It's always meant to be.
