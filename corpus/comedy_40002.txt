A chance investment opportunity forces Gareth Green home to cash in on his dad's vintage car. His father the self-proclaimed "Bearcat" guilts Gareth into letting him join in on the road trip back to San Francisco. On this trip of a lifetime Gareth is forced to come to terms with his irresponsible Dad his troubled upbringing and their tumultuous relationship. However Bearcat has other things in mind for their travels like forcing his lame ass son to learn how to enjoy his life. This is an uplifting comedic tale about getting past the disappointment of your previous situations and accepting the people around you for who they really are.
