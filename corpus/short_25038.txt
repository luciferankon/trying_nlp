Blessed with the power of super strength but cursed with the limited ability to only use it on things with handles HandleSmasher is one of the silliest superheroes to be covered by the hit show "Worlds Worst Heroes." Follow HandleSmasher's adventure through C-List hero obscurity as he tries desperately to help those in need only to consistently fall short. When the stakes are raised will he be able to rise to the occasion? Will he be able to uncover the dark secret behind the very show that's following him?
