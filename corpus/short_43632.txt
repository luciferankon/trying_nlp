The Cross is a short film about father Tony a priest that is battling with his faith over seeking revenge for his brother Alberto's murder. He is torn between the love of the church and the love of His brother and struggles with the teaching of the bible and is tested by his faith. Marlon a notorious Jamaican gangster and his gang are determined to track down something valuable that belongs to him and goes on a brutal path of destruction to get back what was once his. The question is will father Tony do what's right within the eyes of God or revenge his brothers murder ?
