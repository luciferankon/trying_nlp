This film follows the stories of 3 women: newlywed Shante (Blair Sharne) and best friends Charlotte (Courney Banks) and Kim (JoAnna Rhambo). Married to Deacon Hunter Sharne is considered the "Church Cheerleader." She is always upbeat and ready to mingle until allegations surface about her husband and another woman. Will she lose her faith and give up? Meanwhile after 10 years of friendship Kim and Charlotte's relationship will be tested when Kim's ex-husband resurfaces looking to put their family back together and leaving Charlotte alone to figure out her own situation. This story is the TRUE definition of "things aren't always what they seem..."
