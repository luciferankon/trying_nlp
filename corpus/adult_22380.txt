Jonah is a closeted gay man attending a bible college. When he catches the hypocritical homophobic school dean in a compromising situation he runs away. Jonah's father however is a powerful Senator who oversees federal funding for the college. The dean must find and return Jonah to the school or there will be financial repercussions. Meanwhile Jonah has been taken under the wing of Currie an atheistic street hustler who only believes in sex for money. Can the virgin save the hooker or vice versa?
