Nicole is a struggling writer who misses her ex boyfriend; Chris is a frustrated studio musician who misses his band; Mary is a married yoga instructor who misses some excitement; they're best friends since high school and they get together to celebrate the wedding of another high school BFFE Julia. Nicole brings some motivational books and a notepad Chris his guitar and a bottle of Jack Mary brings Ted her workaholic husband and a pregnancy test. It's all fun and games until Adam Nicole's ex arrives with his new girlfriend the exotic young and clever Veronica and the Pandora's box is opened. Now Nicole wants Adam back Chris wants to get drunk and laid and Mary confesses she's having an affair. While everything goes sour their personal dramas must be put on hold when tragedy hits and the wedding is suddenly cancelled.
