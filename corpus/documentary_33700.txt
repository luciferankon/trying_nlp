Down in Suntown: San Diego's Homeless is a documentary from director/producer Corey B. Stevens and host/producer Michael Potter that explores different opinions and viewpoints on homeless life and issues pertaining to drug/alcohol abuse mental illness police interaction possible solutions and some of the help available to the homeless in San Diego CA. Corey and Michael take you deep inside to present riveting candid interviews with homeless persons in their environment owners of non-profit organizations attorneys church leaders concerned citizens and more. This film is sure to be an eye opener for many and is intended to help bring awareness and discussion about an important issue that affects all of us.
