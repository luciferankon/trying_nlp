Fearing he will become a government informant the mob decides to knock off its former boss when he goes into the hospital for an operation. They plant several hit-women as nurses with orders to make his murder look like an accident. Agent N and Sabrina are charged with protecting the former mob boss and capture a bevy of nurses to ensure his safety. Soon the would-be killers escape and our heroines are in their clutches. Will truth and right prevail?
