It is 1910. Maia an orphan can't wait to meet her long lost relatives in Manaus in the heart of the Amazon. She travels with Minty her fierce-looking governess. She dreams of a loving family in an exciting jungle to replace her dead parents. Instead she discovers two vicious & spiteful cousins who hate the jungle and refuse to go outside. But the wonders of the rainforest more than make up for her horrible new family. And when Maia meets a mysterious boy who lives alone on the shores of the Amazon she begins a thrilling journey to the heart of a wonderful new world as they try to escape from the boy's pursuers from England.
