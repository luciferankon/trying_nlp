Kinare is an emotional poetic depiction on screen this is an innocent love story on a big social canvas set up in coastal area. The story based on realistic characters which give natural outcome as and when story unfolds. The movie gives high importance to the locations in lieu with the characters depicted in the film. Overall this is a yet another experimental film which is fresh in all aspects. The movie attracts every segment of audience especially family goers. Kinare is a wholesome package of entertainment tagged to reality.
