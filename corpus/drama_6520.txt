Susanne Danbury ( Katherine MacDonald ) and Walter Gaylord ( Nigel Barrie ) the man she loves are among the weekend guests of her publisher Hugh Bemis ( Charles Clary ) and his wife Agatha ( Mona Kingsley ) who also loves Walter. While enacting a play Susanne shoots Bemis with a gun she thought to be unloaded. Investigation reveals that Martin Tancray ( Charles Gerrard ) intended the bullet for Gaylord and Susanne is happily exonerated.
