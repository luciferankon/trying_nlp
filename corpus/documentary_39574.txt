Emotionally retarded? Manic-depressive? Sexually deviant? Lewis Carroll JM Barrie Edward Lear and Mark Twain were also brilliant launching a Golden Age of kid's lit. Today there are new icons JK Rowling Lemony Snicket Eoin Colfer Philip Pullman... Captain Underpants? Shut Up and Write Me! is a documentary about writing for kids. Would be writer AJ De Aquila studies the old masters and attempts his own masterpiece goaded by two daemons a stuck-up Moose played by Rick Roberts and a cynical Pigfish. Dishing out advice are authors and experts including Richard Scrimger Lisa Pijuan and Robert Munsch; Roald Dahl offers words of wisdom from beyond the grave.
