When two worlds collide three prophetic brothers born to bring balance to the world must come together to reunite their family in order to save the universe. Kai born and raised by his single mother; and oblivious to his destiny is caught in between these worlds where he learns of his newly found powers and his newly found brothers Avery and Micah. Now these brothers must solve the mystery of their individual pasts and receive their destiny before the evil Mistress Jayde plagues their home.
