Todd Craig is an outcast of society - blamed for an accident that brought the township of Little River to its knees. But when two young girls are found hiding in his barn Todd accepts a responsibility that goes far beyond protecting two children from a modern world they've never before experienced. An ancient enemy is descending upon the Little River Township and only Todd holds the key to defeating them. But will Todd's neighbors allow him the freedom to fight for the town's survival? Before he can gain the trust of the citizens of Little River Todd must first learn the power of forgiveness and come to terms with a new reality of who his neighbors are and who are his enemies.
