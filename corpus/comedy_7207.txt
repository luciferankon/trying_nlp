Tilly von Broeckhuijse enjoys her quiet life. She inherited a large sum of money from her father. The easy life ends when her evil twin-sister Emma von Broeckhuijse enters her life after having spent 26 years in Australia. Emma claims a part of the heritage which is not hers. A wild chase starts. Finally Tily ends up in the house of her neighbor Joke. When Tilly thinks Emma is gone she returns...
