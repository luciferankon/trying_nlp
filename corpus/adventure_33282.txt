Several stories are featured in this film. In the first a home owner goes over the line when two inept burglars invade her house. In the second a psychiatrist and her patient explore bondage as part of the treatment program. In the third three beautiful ladies are bound and gagged while the villain searches for a secret formula. In the fourth a buxom brunette is menaced by a bondage crazed gorilla. In the last two beautiful superheroines discover there's a limit to their powers when they are tied up together.
