Clyde is stopped from committing suicide by his friend Pope. Clyde cannot get as high as he feels he could. After deep therapy he realizes that he used to get drugs whilst in the womb and decides to go back to the town where he was born to find his mother's family and rediscover the drugs that used to get him high. On their way to the south of their country they meet Muffin a girl who's on the contrary trying to run away from her past.
