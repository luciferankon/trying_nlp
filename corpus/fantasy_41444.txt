Aranya stays in North Calcutta reluctantly goes to work in a small company doing meaningless chores just to keep his family happy and. Aranya believes it is his job to protect everyone from evil. He does wonder at times... why... others cant understand his mission and his powers and why they cant believe that he is special... but he knows... no... he believes that they will all be thankful one day. Aranya has a soul mate Deb with whom he grew up. They went to the same school and were inseparable all through their childhood. But Deb was a boy wonder and grew to be a man of amazing talents. He sailed through his studies was a natural athlete was there in school functions and was a darling of teachers parents and friends alike. He was effortlessly the center of attraction everywhere. College took them onto separate journeys and thereafter their lives were tangential. Life for him... is to achieve... more... relentlessly. It is story of a single fateful day when two best friends who are very different from each othersee their world changes...forever.
