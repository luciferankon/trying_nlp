Rookie TV reporter Kyle Rohr is out for the "Big Scoop" when he meets the daughter of a known mobster while she is drunk stumbling on the streets of downtown. Kyle and his sidekick cameraman Greg Street hatch a plan to use her to get information on her father the mysterious "Mr Smith" and turn that information into the scoop they've been waiting for. The plan unravels quickly when Kyle and Greg realize that the bodyguards of the Mobsters daughter Tricia are hot on their trail and they soon discover that the truth of who's behind the biggest crime spree in Cleveland history is more sinister than they thought.
