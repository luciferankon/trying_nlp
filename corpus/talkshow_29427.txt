The A.V. Club Hosted By John Teti air Thursdays at 9pm on Fusion TV. The irreverent Chicago-based series features the editorial brains behind the taste-making entertainment website AVClub.com sharing their distinct perspective on movies music television and more. Recent topics have included a fall TV preview food trends The Price is Right cord cutting and the viewing habits of TV watchers roles for women in Hollywood entertainment awards Twitter the polarizing effects of the series "Girls" the 'Black TV' renaissance and more. Each week Teti dives into another corner of pop culture covering everything from The Oscars to Oscar the Grouch. The series is produced by Onion Studios with Alicia Haywood MaryBeth Searls and James Fleischel serving as executive producers. This marks the first series from one of Fusion Media Group's digital brands to premiere on FUSION TV.
