A story of brotherhood and hope... The great Turkish poet of equality and freedom Nazim Hikmet (1902-1963) had visited Cuba in 1961 due to the invitation of his friend and colleague Cuban poet Nicholas Guillen. After the attempt of the invasion of Bay of Pigs of the USA Nazim went to Cuba for solidarity and also to observe the enthusiasm of the Cuban Revolution. Through the testimonies of Cuban intellectuals whom had met with Nazim in this joyous voyage and through the witnesses of the Revolution's history and actuality the film discovers how the Cuban Revolution influenced Nazim and how Nazim influenced the Cuban intellectuals.
