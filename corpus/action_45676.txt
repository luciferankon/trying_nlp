Martial art experts Darus Richmond and Quinn Richmond display extraordinary knowledge and skill in this video on "COMBAT CONCEPTS". Unlike many martial arts training video "Combat Concepts" DOES NOT focus on ANY one particular style and/or system... NOR does it focus on flashy movements. The goal of "Combat Concepts" is to insure that EVERYONE from the novice to the advanced martial artist can understand how to effectively handle COMBAT and LIFE threatening situations. From their home in Southern California experts Darus Richmond and Quinn Richmond have help to place a new light on how to handle combat situations.
