You are a senior in high school along with your two best friends Naomi Patterson and Suzu Cappini. You are also the granddaughter of Harold Anderson the CEO and founder of Anderson Toys a toy company sworn to give children amazing products and give a large part of their profit to charity. One day you are called home to attend your grandfather's funeral. Afterwards you learn that you have inherited his large estate. After deciding to move in the moment you walk through the door of your new home you see five injured yet very handsome men on the lobby floor. You find out that they are Incubi demons who consume and use sexual energy of humans to survive'. With nowhere to go out of sympathy you offer your home as refuge. What will happen as the Incubi get comfy? Why exactly did they come the human world? ...Will you WANT them to leave?
