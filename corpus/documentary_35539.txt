Millions of Americans are at a crossroads. We're faced with doing more of the same but wanting a different outcome. We want to be happier healthier and more at peace with ourselves. We want joy and love. But now for the hard part. Are these desires and wishes compatible with our actions and choices? This program explores how to make positive changes so that we can achieve more of what we want out of life. Why do smart people do stupid things? What creates the limits to what you can achieve? Do you believe that the limitations of the past will become the limitations of the present and future? How can we make better decisions? How do we create empowering beliefs and banish disempowering ones?
