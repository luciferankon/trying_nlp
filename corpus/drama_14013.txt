A handsome young writer William who's combating writer's block with nowhere to go . William's best friend Max finds him at the park one day and eagerly divulges a discovery he's made. It seems there's secret Bordello hidden deep in the Mojave Desert and Max is begging to hunt for it. As the two travel through the desert down forgotten dirt roads William ponders the fabled women of the "Desert Rose" an old brothel with stunning and willing women. Suffering from writer's block attractive young writer William agrees to help his friend Max search for the fabled bordello that lies hidden somewhere in the Mojave Desert. The two men's fantasies of what they might find grow ever more explicit and the temperature rises as they draw nearer to the house's reputedly beautiful and talented women.
