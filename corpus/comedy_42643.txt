Living It Up tells the story of a bus driver who is on the verge of committing suicide when a man offers him some friendly advice - borrow 100 million pesetas from the Mafia and do everything he has ever dreamed of before ending his life. While spending the money he falls in love with Mexican waitress and realizes now he has something to live for. The only problem is that now he has to find a way to pay back the $1 million he owes to the Mafia! A man borrows 100 million pesetas to blow on a final round of merrymaking before committing suicide falls for a Mexican waitress and finds he has something to live for but having spent the money now has to deal with loan sharks.
