After her family moves to the city Chloe finds her new school to be a scary place where she doesn't fit in. When her father tells her the Bible story of Jonah Chloe uses her imagination to bring the adventure to life complete with a nervous prophet rival gangs of Ninevites who happen to be amazing dancers a hearty band of Spanish mariners a troubled King and Queen a pair of lovable fish named Humphrey and Chum and a trio of singing crustaceans called the "Crabelles." As Chloe likens the story to her own life she discovers there are no misfits in God's plan.
