The Boy on the Bus is a magical realism fable in the vein of The Fisher King. It is an uplifting story meant to inspire in a world where everyone is looking for something to believe in. It is a true celebration of our wonderful melting pot city of Los Angeles and America. A young man's life unravels when he falls for a mysterious girl who takes him on an Urban Holy Grail adventure to save her life. This mythological love story is set in the modern immigrant working class of present day L.A. and bus-line 549. It is a road trip that never leaves LA. THE BOY ON THE BUS is a magical realism fable an uplifting story about people's search for meaning and one young man's search for himself.
