Unlikely competitive partners Dani and Charlie Miami Dade State Attorney Investigators land in surprising predicaments in the search for conniving criminals from luxurious Miami to exotic Morocco. Based on true events and with some of the most beautiful location scenes in the world... "Miami Uncovered" while a serious crime drama is filled with hilarious and unusual circumstances as well as an unique investigator assignment and detail making this story stand out from the other buddy detective police movies that have been produced. The lead detective Dani is fortunate that she has her senior partner always looking out for her. Never before has this true story been told which uncovers the intermingling and lifestyles of certain Miami families running from the cops.
