Based on a comic book by writer Matthew McLean Ninety follows two scientist who both share a passion for preservation of our future although through completely different means. Andalus a Malthusian believes that the planet can not sustain all of humanity and in order for the planet and humanity to survive Ninety percent must perish. Haven uses technology to give the planet and humanity a fighting chance. Haven has been able to augment the Ninety percent of the foreign organisms in our bodies to given individuals abilities. Will we be the Ninety percent to perish or can the Ninety percent in each of us be enough to save us.
