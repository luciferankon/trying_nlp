The dull life of a retired tailor completely changes when a filmmaker comes to make a documentary about him. Without intention the old tailor falls in an unspoken love with the beautiful filmmaker until the time he decides to give an end to his feelings... In his old-fashioned shop all alone with the sunny memories of a bygone past Iordanis a retired silver-haired tailor sees his business wither away bereft of work or friends. Under those circumstances an unexpected visit from an aspiring filmmaker the fresh and kind-hearted Maria will inspire a new meaning in the tailor's life as Maria intends to shoot a documentary pivoting around the quiet artisan and his dying art. Now as if by magic a ray of light begins to shine in his once gloomy shop however Iordanis knows well that their honest and cordial relationship will be short-lived and by the end of the shooting separation will be inevitable.
