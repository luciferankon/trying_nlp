An industry that has sustained a town for over 100 years announces that it is closing its doors. A family that has been the cornerstone of the community. has given up. The only thing that can save the dying little town... IS AN UNSPEAKABLE TRAGEDY. From Chip Rossetti the writer and director of FATHERS THE ACCIDENTAL MISSIONARY and THE BORROWED CHRISTMAS comes a faith based adventure into the HEART of a family and the CORE of a mountain. 94 FEET.
