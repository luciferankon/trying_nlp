The World's Best Selling Mystery Novel is Coming to the PC! Agatha Christie's world-famous novel And Then There Were None is brought to the PC with all of its original baffling suspense! 10 people strangers to each other are all invited to a lavish estate on an isolated island. Through a recording their mysterious host accuses each of his 'guests' of murder and proceeds to exact 'justice.' Tension mounts as one-by-one the number of people are reduced through the ingenious plotting of the unseen killer. Prepare yourself for an investigative thriller like never before! You play Patrick Narracott the driver of a boat who takes ten guests to the Shipwreck Island for a weekend gathering. Finding yourself stranded when your boat is scuttled you take part in a murder investigation but the host begins murdering them one by one. It is up to you as the odd man out to find a way off the island and find the murderous host.
