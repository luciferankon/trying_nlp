Waterfront is a spoof of the legendary Elia Kazan flick On the Waterfront with Marlon Brando. The legendary "coulda been a contender" scene has been changed it to be a scene between two deaf brothers. One brother Charlie is part of a shady gang; the other Terry wants to work as a BSL teacher at the RNID. Terry's interview at the RNID is approaching and he has high hopes. But Charlie has other ideas...
