The evil ninja lord Quan Chi Hooker plans to terminate all living ninjas in the world. In order to do this successfully he must create his very own secret ninja army. When ninjas starts to drop dead all over the place it gets the attention of chief inspector Gordon of the HK police department. He must solve the case of the dying ninjas... Meanwhile Quan Chi Hooker finds out that in order to succeed with his evil plan he must also terminate his own ninja army and himself ... Something he didn't think about when he made up his evil plan.
