Holly Davis is struggling in therapy after the death of her twin sister Haley. Her therapist Jennifer Campbell Ph.D. suggests Holly try a new form of bereavement therapy. Jonathan Knightley a representative of Restore Industries promises that Holly will be able to speak with Haley. Has the company found a way to contact the dead? Or is it all a mind game achieved through a high-tech version of a magician's sleight of hand? Will Holly learn what she needs to accept her sister's death?
