Ultimate Mountain Biking released in 1989 is an advanced training video for the serious mountain bike rider. Pro riders Tinker Juarez Jim Deaton Cindy Whitehead Glen Adams and others share their training riding and nutritional tips and techniques. The video features California mountain scenery original music score race footage and pro interviews. This video earned several awards including a Telly Houston International Film and TV Festival Award Ben Franklin Award and International Film and TV Festival of New York Award.
