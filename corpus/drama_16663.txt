The House On Cuckoo Lane is the rarest underground horror movie of all time. Some people claim it never existed. Those who believe of its existence claim it is the strangest and most unsettling underground film of all time. Legend has it the film was found in the very house where it was shot. This is the film every video collector wants. For film geek Shaun Costello The House On Cuckoo Lane is an obsession. In his quest to acquire the legendary movie he scours the video underground consulting dangerous traders. But as Shaun will soon discover the thrill of the hunt is often greater than the reward. This short film presents a fictional narrative framework based around the legendary House On Cuckoo Lane videotape.
