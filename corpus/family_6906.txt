Based on the true story of Regret the first filly to win the Kentucky Derby. Regret was considered a mistake from the night she was born. Her owners thought they needed a stallion to get them into the winner's circle but with the help of her faithful canine companion Lucky Regret lives down her name and becomes a legendary champion.
