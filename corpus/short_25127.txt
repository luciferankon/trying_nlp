Prophecies of doom disaster and political catastrophe envisioned by some of the world's most famous psychics between the 1960's the year 2001 are conjured up through 3D-animation industrial films text and historical footage -- the sum of which combine to form a visually stunning meditation on the forces that guide us into a dark paranoid and uncertain future. Soothsayer reconsiders yesterday's daunting and sometimes whimsical predictions for the future after they've been outpaced by time.
